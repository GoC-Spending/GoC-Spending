Company Profile


In 2000, the Sicame group implanted an industry in Canada with the purchasing of the old Alcan division of accessories and connectors with the contribution and business activities done previously with other factories of the group. The new entity is called Cicame Energie Inc and is based in Saint-Hubert on the south shore of Montreal. Cicame 's first goal is to supply the North american utilities with connectors and accessories used in aerial and underground networks. Our strenght is to develop connectors according to client specification.
Cicame Energie recommends complete solutions in dead-end and joint connections for high tension (765kV) and low tension (600V and less) for all cable types (ACSR, AAC, AAAC, ACSS, CU,...).

In January 2008, Cicame Energie bought INDUSTRIES POLTEC in Montréal-Nord

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Splices - dead ends - stockbridge dampers - Live line working and safety equipment
Cicame Energie is providing complete solutions for dead ends and splicing on transmission lines. We are also providing technical support and products for vibration damping solutions on transmission lines.
Another panel of our activities is to provide safety equipment to line workers for: live line tooling, personnal grounding equipment, insulated gloves, hot sticks.

Product Name:
Mechanical and Crimped connectors LV/MV
Traditionnal mechanical connectors or insulation piercing connectors, as well as crimped connectors in Aluminum and/or Copper for use in Low or Medium Voltage

Service Name:
Training on electrical safety
Cicame Energie is recognized by Quebec governement as a training center in electrical safety. Cicame Energie is specializing in trainings on safety for medium and high voltage.
