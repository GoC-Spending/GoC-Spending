Company Profile


Dendoff Springs Ltd was established in British Columbia, Canada in 1906. The company manufactures all types of Coil and Leaf Springs, both hot and cold wound, including Compression, Extension, Torsion, Wire forms, and Flat stampings. Sizes range from .010" diameter wire to 2.50" alloy bar stock. Dendoff stocks a wide selection of spring wires and alloy bar stock, including stainless steels, inconel, music wire, phosphour bronze, oil tempered, chrome silicon, & other varied specialty wire materials.

Production includes flat Leaf Springs for Cars, Trucks, Trailers, Off-Highway Trucks, Locomotives and Rail cars. Capacity includes both Full-Elliptic and Semi-Elliptic Leaf Springs, U-bolts, and Heat Treated Bolts from 3/8" to 1.50" diameter bar.
Services include Heat Treating, Shot Peening, and Stress Relieving.

Industries served include: Mining, Logging, Sawmill, Construction, Aggregate, Road Building, Agriculture, Railroad, Food Equipment, Marine, Vibrating Conveyors, Oil field, Hydraulics, Automotive and wide array of Original Equipment Manufacturers.
Export shipping worldwide, Dendoff Springs can assist you with all your spring requirements.
