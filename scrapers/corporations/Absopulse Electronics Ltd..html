Company Profile


ABSOPULSE Electronics Ltd is an original equipment manufacturer (OEM) specializing in the design and manufacture of custom and semi-custom switch-mode power conversion products for the railway, automotive, industrial automation, power utilities, mining, marine and telecommunications sectors.

The company’s product lines include industrial and railway quality DC-DC power converters, AC-DC power supplies, rectifiers, battery chargers, DC-AC sine-wave inverters, phase and frequency converters, DC-input dimmable LCD display back-light inverters, DC-output UPS systems and complete power systems in 19" and 23" racks.

ABSOPULSE's rugged power supplies are designed and built to last for an operational life of up to 30 years in demanding environments.

We can meet almost any customer requirement within an output power range of a few watts and up to 30kW. A continuous research and design program ensures that we meet the latest requirements of the power supply market.

ABSOPULSE’s customers include small and medium size enterprises as well as the largest corporations in the world. We work directly with these companies. We also have strong representation in North America, South America, the UK, Europe, Middle East, Australia and New Zealand, in addition to Canada.

Our quality procedures have been approved by international approval agencies as well as our large corporate customers.

To ensure our customers can ship their end-products world-wide, in-house certification is available for CSA, UL and other approvals.

ABSOPULSE is a BABT (equivalent to ISO9002) approved manufacturer and it is NATO listed.

ABSOPULSE is located on the outskirts of Ottawa, in an 83,000sq foot custom-built production plant and warehousing facility. Since inception in 1982, the company has grown steadily, and undergone several phases of expansion. ABSOPULSE Electronics Ltd is a privately held, wholly Canadian owned company.
