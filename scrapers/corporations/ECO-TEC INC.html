Products/Services: Filtration, reverse osmosis, ion exchange equipment for water treatment; chemical and metal recovery systems


Company Description: Eco-Tec designs and builds water treatment, and recovery systems which purify water and help to solve environment problems around the world. These systems are used to remove suspended and dissolved solids from water, reclaim "heavy" metals such as copper, nickel, and chrome, purify the acids that are used in metal treatment processes, or remove contaminants from amines used in hydrocarbon processing systems.


Power generation, pharmaceutical production, electronics manufacturing, oilfields, mining, electroplating, aluminum anodizing, and steel finishing are examples of markets that use Eco-Tec systems.


Utilizing an advanced ion exchange process called Recoflo®, complete recovery systems often include other technologies which Eco-Tec has either developed or licensed from around the world. In addition to recovery applications, Recoflo is used for water purification, particularly where large volumes or high purity are important. The high efficiency Recoflo® process reduces capital and operating costs.


Incorporated in 1970, Eco-Tec Limited has developed a broad range of products based on the Recoflo® ion exchange process. Eco-Tec exports to over 50 countries including the U.S., Europe, Japan, the Far East and India.

Locations:
Eco-Tec Inc. - Located in Pickering, Ontario, Canada, this central location houses engineering design, manufacturing, sales, customer service and support staff. The development of new products is based on the latest separation technologies, using comprehensive analytical laboratory and pilot plant facilities. Eco-Tec Inc. serves the Americas and the Far East.

Eco-Tec Solutions located in India specifies systems to meet the needs of the industrial growth and economy in India.

Prosep Technologies Limited - From its U.K. location, Prosep specifies systems to meet the needs of its European and Middle Eastern clients.

Eco-Tec systems are used in a wide variety of industries including power generation, pharmaceutical, electronics, mining, plating, steel (stainless, tinplate, electrogalvanized) finishing, aluminum (architectural, coil, lithoplate, aerospace) finishing, pulp mills, and mineral (hydrometallurgical) processing. Typical examples of Eco-Tec's abilities include recover/purification of mineral acids and concentration/separation of dissolved metal salts.


Recoflo® water demineralization systems are used in the power generation, electronics, automotive and chemical industries, mining (produced water) and anywhere else that high purity water is essential.


Over 2,000 systems have commissioned in nearly 60 countries around the world. While Recoflo ion exchange is the heart of the most systems, Eco-Tec employs chemical processes such as evaporation, membrane crystallization, electro-winning and micro-filtration to offer complete solutions. Treatability studies, installation services, on-site supervision, training and a customer service hotline available 24 hours a day are all part of Eco-Tec's offering.


Eco-Tec has been recognized for its achievements with a number of awards including the Canada Award for Business Excellence , recognized as of Canada's 50 Best Managed Private Companies, and has received the Global Traders Award. Eco-Tec is ISO 9001 registered.

Product Name:
APU® Acid Purification Unit
Solutions consisting of inorganic acids (sulphuric, hydrochloric, phosphoric, nitric) and dissolved metals (iron, nickel, copper, aluminum, etc.) are often discarded by industry. The cost of replacing these acid or salt solutions is significant, yet the high cost of waste treatment is often a more important consideration. The Acid Purification Unit (APU®) separates the metals from the acids, for reuse, using only water. No other chemicals and virtually no energy are required. The acid-metal separation process is now justified in a large number of applications that were not previously considered.

The benefits are:
~reduced acid consumption
~reduced waste treatment
~reduced labour costs
~reduced production downtime
~more uniform production quality; increased energy efficiency
~low operating, capital and installation costs; compact size
~full automation and high reliability.

Maintenance costs are minimized through the use of corrosion-resistant components.

Product Name:
Recoflo® Compressed-Bed Ion Exchange
Recoflo® is one of the most significant advances in ion exchange technology in the last two decades.

How does it work?
Ions in solution are exchanged for other ions that are contained in synthetic ion exchange resins. By passing the solution through columns of cation and anion exchange resins, all the ions can be exchanged, leaving only hydrogen and hydroxyl, which combine, leaving pure water. Alternatively, by suitable choice of ion exchange resins, a selected portion of the ions can be removed.

What are the benefits?
The system's many advantages include reduced equipment size, lower capital and operating costs and superior performance.

Product Name:
ChromaPur
The Eco-Tec Chromic Acid Purification (ChromaPur) systems improve plating performance with advanced ion exchange technology. During the chrome plating process, there is only a small amount of the acid plated. The balance is lost to rinses and fume scrubbers.

The ChromaPur uses Eco-Tec's advanced ion exchange technology to remove harmful contaminants, leaving clean, effective chromic acid for recycling. The contaminants left in the waste stream are easily treated in a waste treatment system.

Product Name:
NickelPur
The Eco-Tec Nickel Recovery System uses deionized water from rinsing, avoiding recovery of tap water contaminants. It does not recover brighteners from rinses and rejects sodium from the rinse water. It recovers nickel salt in the correct sulfate/chloride ratio and removes acid from the recovered nickel salts to ensure the bath pH is not affected. The system is designed to ensure all nickel can be recycled without any long term effects on the plating bath.


Product Name:
MicroPur
The Eco-Tec MicroPur is a version of the APU® designed in a range of sizes to accommodate the small to mid-sized pickler.
With consistent bath chemistry, pickling performance is assured. With metals at low levels, further improvements are realized in cooler running temperatures and reduced generation of sludge and fumes.

The APU® uses a resin sorption process and simple water wash to recover acid. The net result is a virtually continuous process for acid purification.




Product Name:
CER Caustic Etch Recovery System
Etching or chemical milling with caustic soda is often the greatest operating expense in aluminum finishing.

Eco-Tec's Caustic Etch Recovery System, CER, continuously crystallizes and removes dissolved aluminum from caustic soda etchants used in processing aluminum and the caustic is returned to the etching/milling tanks for reuse.


Product Name:
DPU
Low levels of dissolved aluminum is critical to a quality process. Eco-Tec's DPU with its innovative regenerant recovery method and ability to keep dissolved aluminum at a low level ensures an excellent return on investment for the lithoplate and capacitor foile manufacturing industries. It reduces acid and waste neutralization costs, allows consistent chemistry composition for consistent quality.

Product Name:
PDP System
The performance of recovery boilers declines as they become plugged. Treatment of the electrostatic precipitator (ESP) dust, where chlorides are concentrated, removes the chlorides before it is returned to the Kraft cycle. This can reduce deposit formation and plugging. The Eco-Tec Precipitator Dust Purification PDP system reduces boiler plugging by controlling chloride in Kraft Pulping Liquors. It is based on a simple process which allows for the separation of dissolved salts by contacting with a resin, similar to the ion exchange process.

Product Name:
AmiPur
Controlling the impurities in gas treatment units at refineries, natural gas processing and syngas plants.

The Eco-Tec AmiPur system, using Recoflo® ion exchange technology, continuously removes Heat Stable Salts from amine solutions. Continuous removal of HSS allows for consistent performance of amine units under optimal conditions with minimal attention. Low HSS cotent has shown to dramatically reduce corrosion, thereby extending the life of process equipment and reducing maintenance and filter replacement requirements.



Product Name:
BgPur Biogas Purification
The BgPur is designed to reduce sulfur dioxide emissions, equipment corrosion, and fouling by removing hydrogen sulfide (H2S) from biogas. Eco-Tec's patented H2S purification system offers cost savings, and greater energy recovery for use in multiple applications



Product Name:
Recoflo® Demineralizer
The Recoflo® Water Demineralizer is able to produce higher purity water without the need of a mixed bed polisher. In many cases, it is able to produce water with a conductivity as low as 0.1 microseimens/cm with less than 0.010 mg/L of silica using only the cation and anion beds.

Product Name:
Recoflo® TriFlo
The Recoflo® TriFlo Demineralizer produces the very high purity water (i.e. conductivity of less than 0.1 microseimens/cm) often required to feed critical and super critical boilers. It can do this economically and simply in a single process unit from feedwaters containing up to 450 ppm of total dissolved solids. Ultrapure water can be achieved by simply adding an additional cation resin bed as a polisher after the usual two-bed demineralizer to remove the contaminating sodium ions. The TriFlo Demineralizer can provide solutions for applications requiring high purity water in the range of 10 - 17.5 megohm resistivity.



Product Name:
Spectrum Micro Media Filter
The Spectrum Micro Media Filter is able to achieve its high level of performance by using two particulate media layers, and a patented backwash process. An upper layer of coarse media allows for an extended service cycle by providing depth filtration of large particles while a lower layer of very dense micro media provides surface filtration of smaller particles.

The Spectrum Micro Media Filter can handle water with a suspended solids concentration in excess of 100 mg/L and provides a filtrate turbidity of less than 0.1 NTU. The Spectrum Micro Media Filter provides excellent pre-filtration for packed bed ion exchange or reverse osmosis.



Product Name:
Eco-RO
Combining the Recoflo® ion exchange technology with reverse osmosis equals low cost mixed bed quality from high TDS feed waters without mixed bed complications. The patented system delivers high purity water and generates less waste than comparable RO/mixed bed and double pass RO systems. The system utilizes regenerant recycling therefore simplifying waste treatment equipment.

Product Name:
Recoflo® Water Softener
Recoflo® Water Softeners outperform conventional systems, reduce operating costs up to 40%, use less brine and water for regeneration and deliver less than 0.1 mg/L total hardness. Lower regenerant use means less waste is generated and the compact design makes it ideal for retrofit applications. The shorter cycle times eliminates biological fouling in the resin bed and subsequent release of bacteria in the product water making it ideal for pretreating a reverse osmosis system.



Product Name:
RecoPur® CBM System
Increase production by eliminating Coal Bed Methane produced water issues with Eco-Tec's RecoPur® CBM Water Treatment System. This system is specifically configured to economically purify and recycle produced water that exceeds agricultural and environmental standards.

Why Use RecoPur® CBM System?

Quantity of Produced Water
The RecoPur® CBM System provides a continuous, online process for handling large volumes of water from multiple wells producing in excess of 50,000 barrels per day.

Quality of Produced Water
The RecoPur® CBM System can purify the water to SAR levels of less than 3 exceeding purification standards for agricultural and other uses.
