Service Name:
Lean Manufacturing and Kaizen Coaching and Implementation
At CITC we understand how challenging it might be for many companies to dedicate their internal resources to identify opportunities for change.
Our team of seasoned professionals with diverse skill set and background dedicated to take a lead and provide scalable, cost effective and timely solutions for your business. Our mutually beneficial cooperation enables &#8203;your growth and competitive advantage on a markets you serve.
We apply many tools and methodologies to conduct thorough examination of your processes, and identify opportunities for productivity improvement. We work in collaboration with your staff to get to the roots in our S.W.O.T. analysis.

1. Our seasoned professionals has a proven record & many years of experience of delivering tangible results to SME and Global companies.
2. We will apply unbiased S.W.O.T. analysis, and together with you will develop and execute growth plan for your company.
3. We identify opportunities for improvement, explore new markets for you and develop strategic planning of your sustainable growth.
4. As a partner of yours we will support your vision and work collaboratively on achieving your goals.
5. We strive helping our clients to improve productivity by finding novel solutions how to eliminate wasteful activities and innovate your operations processes.
6. We promote robust communication to create a good functional alliance with your team. Effective communication is a key to any project success!
7. Our people are dedicated GO-TO integrated leaders, who came from the same industry where you are.
8. We will not interfere trying to change your mindset and a culture, but we will make a great afford to inspire your employees,
like we are, about the Kaizen culture. We will equip your people with comprehensive Lean Manufacturing tool set and knowledge
base, so when we leave your facilities they will drive the improvements continuously.
9. We engage metrics and performance measurement to truck the process. Our impact is easy to measure, and process is
transparent and suited to company's specific needs.
10. We work hard to amplify and accelerate innovations at your company by leading your process development and product
commercialization. We provide access to academic (R&D), marketing, finance and manufacturing talent & experts, and
facilities to embrace your business success.
