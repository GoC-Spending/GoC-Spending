Company Profile


Cascades Boxboard Group is the largest coated recycled boxboard producer in Canada. Our presence is felt internationally: we have three boxboard mills in Canada, two in France, and one each in the United States, Germany and Sweden.

Over 4,300 Cascades Boxboard Group employees contribute to our success, and Cascades Inc. is proud to have been named one of the 100 best employers in Canada for three years running. Cascades Boxboard Group combines a passion for quality with a love for the environment and an inexhaustible interest in innovation.

As a world-class leader in boxboard and boxboard coatings, we deliver a variety of products designed to meet our customers needs, and have our own research and development department to keep up with their ideas and requirements. We provide value for our clients money: great boxboard at great prices, with precision and creativity as part of our value-added package.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Packaging products / Folding cartons
FOLDING CARTONS FOR PRODUCTS IN THE FOLLOWING AREAS:

FOOD:
As you walk down the aisles of the average supermarket, close to
one-third of the paperboard packages you see are produced by
Cascades Boxboard Inc. Cartons for cookies, crackers, cereals,
potato chips, cheeses, pretzels - whether national brands or
neighbourhood names.

DAIRY:
Cascades Boxboard Inc. services North American dairies with
millions of ice cream cartons every year, and our range of
structural desighs include the Tuck-Tite carton with a zipper
opening, the standard lock-end carton and the cylindrical 2
pieces Convocan style.

BEVERAGES AND DISTILLED PRODUCTS:
With our many years of serving the brewing, distilling and
soft-drink industries, we have developed the expertise to
effectively match form with function to produce packages that
both market your product and ensure safe transport to and from
its point of purchase. Included in the full line of beverage
packages is our patented "Unipak" carton - (the Rolls Royce of
Canadian one-dozen beer packages), the 6-pak basket carrier, the
12-can dispenser carton and the elegant liquor cartons printed
and embossed on metallized film or foil.

HOUSEHOLD:
Detergents, soaps, facial tissues, napkins, kitchen cleaners,
food wraps... the list goes on and on.

COSMETICS AND HEALTH CARE:
In the very demanding field of personal care products and
cosmetics packaging, Cascades Boxboard Inc. offers the skills
and technology for the production of sophisticated cartons that
measure up to the most critical marketing specifications.

OTHER CONSUMER GOODS:
From hardware, film and foil wrap to fast foods and tools, this
market segment covers a vast range of packaging needs.
