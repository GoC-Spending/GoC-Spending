Company Profile


Manufacturer, distributor, retailer. A natural range of Face, Bath and Body Care products, formulated only with the highest quality natural and organic, ingredients from renewable, sustainable and equitable sources here in Canada and around the world blending the latest technologies with ancient therapies. The aim of this line is to leave a very small imprint on the planet ecosystem but have a huge impact on the physical and emotional well-being of its users.

Opès LAUTUS Therapy

Cleansing products for the body, hair and face

Wild Canadian Seaweed and Loofah Soap 140 g
Handcrafted in the traditional way, our soaps are made by the conversion of fatty acids from palm, coconut and cabbage oils. A unique blend of glycerine and seaweed extracts resulting in a rich, mild and light soap. Exfoliate using the Loofah side and stimulate your skin. Tones and invigorates, remineralises and moisturises and promotes radiant skin. Use daily with our Magic Soap Holder exclusive to Opès.

Opès offers you a new way to keep your soap dry and free of bacteria. No more, messy, slimy dish soap which promotes bacteria and diminishes the lifespan of your soap. Easy to install, no tools required our Magic Soap Holder will not only give you a bacteria free soap it will also save your soap from dissolving rapidly. In sum it could save you up to 40 % of your annual purchase of bar soap in addition to protecting you from inheriting unwanted bacteria on your skin and no more, hard to clean soap build-ups.

Shampoo with Wild Canadian Seaweed and Dead Sea Minerals 200 ml
Our pH balanced, extra mild Seaweed Lustre Shampoo is enriched with wild Canadian seaweed extracts. Saturated with minerals and trace elements it contains marine elements beneficial for people with dry damaged hair and is specially formulated for people suffering from psoriasis on the scalp. Restores and hydrates dry hair and sensitive scalp, adds brilliance and shine and improves moisture balance and appearance. Can be used daily.


Loofah Products
Top quality Egyptian Loofah accessories provide soft and efficient skin exfoliation. Gloves, back strap, natural loofah sponge and other shapes of accessories fit for every body parts. 100% natural and biodegradable, the vegetal loofah fibres provide deep cleansing and invigorating effect on the skin. Its exfoliating virtues have been known since antiquity. It removes dead skin particles, opens pores, stimulates blood circulation and offers a light lymphatic drainage. Beauty salons and SPAS appreciate and demand this quality.
