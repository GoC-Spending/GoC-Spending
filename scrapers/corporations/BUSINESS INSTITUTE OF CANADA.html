Technology profile

Dr. Raina's Business Tools, Models, Game based Teaching and learning tools are extensively used for coaching.

These Business Tools have been developed to bring cost efficiencies in an organisation since these act as a catalyst for generating in-house cost reduction and business prosperity ideas. Dr. Rainas Low Cost Software provides products and services that help students, professionals, entrepreneurs, businessmen in cost management and business improvement processes in purchases, marketing, manufacturing, administration, personnel functioning, financial planning and budgeting, inventories, strategic management, project implementation, marketing planning, product development, business planning, benchmarking, process re-engineering, six sigma and TQM techniques, factory flow processes, product mix and create long term values by offering qualitative goods and services at low cost.

These low cost business tools include strategic and operational executive planning, business analysis, sales and marketing dashboard, workfloor operations, quality management, motivation, team building, identifying leadership requirements, analyzing and solving problems, financial models, competitiveness, negotiating, modelling the competition, and training. Our online courses teach what each business tool and model is all about, how it can be used, where and when it is useful, and what some of the common pitfalls one comes across in businesses.

To stay in business, an organisation must at least recover its cost, i.e, cost of production, administration, marketing, distribution and use of assets. The problem can be worst if the organisation can not recover its variable cost, that situation would mean that more the production, more losses the organisation will incur.

Cost management, which is the key to survival and growth in the present global cut-throat competition and spiralling input costs, requires adequate timely focus rather than initiating action in panic. Market leaders are pursuing cost management as a strategic imperative to stay ahead of competition. No matter how strong the commitment to quality, or how low the rate of rejection of products, or how consistent the level of services, winning and holding customers around the world will be impossible unless price is also the lowest. Hence we need cost management tools.
