Company Profile


EMS SATCOM provides its customers with more efficient mobile communications resources when and where they need them. With its alliances and partners, the company has excelled at delivering products that provide critical resources to users in the air, on land and at sea. The company is a wholly owned division of Atlanta-based EMS Technologies Inc. (NASDAQ:ELMG), and is a pioneer in the development of satellite-based communications equipment. Its products and services enable users with real-time mobile broadband voice, data and videoconferencing capabilities virtually anywhere in the world. Located in Ottawa, Canada, EMS SATCOM employs over 230 people and has a development lab in Tewkesbury, U.K.

The principal focus of EMS SATCOM is business and military aviation, but it has leveraged its expertise in satellite-based communications equipment to address the land mobile and maritime segments as well. Since 1978, the company has also been deeply involved in the Search and Rescue (SAR) field developing both equipment and software for the management of SAR operations that has saved more than 40,000 lives.

Allied with Inmarsat, the world leader in mobile satellite communications, the companys products include terminals, antennas and radomes, and cabin network equipment. Its customers are front-runners in a diverse range of industry segments.

SARVIEW Information Management System

Product Name:

Product Name:
Radomes
Tail mount SATCOM antenna radomes are available for the

Product Name:
GeoLUT 600 Geostationary Local User Terminal
GEOLUT-600 from Honeywell Global Tracking is a local user terminal (LUT) that porcesses 406 MHz distress beacon alerts over geo-stationary satellites, and provides rapid notification to SAR authorities worldwide.

Product Name:
LeoLUT 600 Advanced Technology Local User Terminal
LEOLUT-600 is a local user terminal that processes 406 MHz distress beacon alerts over Low Earth Orbit (LEO) satellites and automatically provides notification to SAR authorities worldwide.

Product Name:
SARMaster Rescue Co-ordination Center
SARMaster is an advanced search & rescue (SAR) incident management system that provides rescue coordination centers (RCCs) around the world with a complete view of SAR data, including emergency beacon locations, information about local SAR resources (e.g. vessels, helicopters and personnel) as well as extensive SAR planning tools used to coordinate all SAR efforts in cohesive fashion.

Product Name:
AMT-1800
The AMT-1800 IGA is designed to bring ARINC 781 functionality to the smallest Class 7 form factor on the market. The phased array technology maintains performance at low look angles and meets the most stringent Passive Intermodulation (PIM) Swiftbroadband requirements. The AMT-1800 IGA operates over the extended L-band frequency range to support operation with the new Inmarsat Alphasat satellite.

Product Name:
AMT-3800
L-band fuselage mount Class 6 high intermediate gain phased array antenna for Inmarsat single and multi-channel connectivity.
eNfusion AMT-3800 is the next generation High-Gain Antenna that will offer affordable broadband voice and data solutions to general aviation, air transport and military operations.


Product Name:
Aspire 100
As part of the Aspire™ family of products, the Aspire 100 provides multiple channels of voice and a low data rate channel over the global Iridium Satellite network. The system utilizes the Low-gain Iridium Antenna to offer in-flight connectivity, and is installed on the fuselage. The Aspire 100 system is ideal for small to mid-sized aircraft.

Product Name:
Aspire 200
The Aspire 200 is Honeywell’s smallest Inmarsat SwiftBroadband system enabling simultaneous voice and data connectivity at rates up to 650kbps on all classes of aircraft. The Aspire 200 system can be installed with any of the Honeywell antennas to provide all classes of SwiftBroadband service (Class 15-LGA; Class 7 – IGA; Class 6 – HGA).

Product Name:
HSD-400
The eNfusion® HSD-400 High-speed Data Transceiver offers four channels of Swift64 Service or two channels of SwiftBroadband service over the Inmarsat I-4 satellite network as part of a complete Satellite Communications system.

Product Name:
HSD-440
The eNfusion® HSD-440 High-speed Data Transceiver offers both SwiftBroadband high-speed data services (over Inmarsat I-4 Satellites) for the cabin and safety services (ACARS, Aero H+ over I-3 Satellites) for the cockpit in one 8 MCU form factor.

Product Name:
HD-710
The HD-710 terminal is a upgrade to the Inmarsat Swift 64 and SwiftBroadband services. The HD-710 offers two channels of Swift64 (64Kbps/channel) or two channels of SwiftBroadband (432 Kbps/channel), in addition to a 60 Watt linear High Power Amplifier.

Product Name:
CNX-200
The CNX-200 is an airborne data router and WiFi AP that includes hardware based network acceleration and compression to reduce airtime costs.

Product Name:
CCU-100
The CCU-100 is an aircraft-certified networking device for multi-port routing, a Wi-Fi access point and PBX for cabin telephony.

Product Name:
CCU-200
The CCU-200 provides network and telephony connectivity to multiple cabin users in combination with multiple Satellite Communications systems.

Product Name:
AMT-50 Multi Cahnnel Aero H/H+ Antenna
The AMT-50 Multi Channel Aero-H/H+ Antenna is the world's smallest, lightest and most affordable Satcom Antenna ever designed and marketed for use with Inmarsat ARINC 741 Satcom Avionics.
