Company Profile


Acquired Intelligence Inc specializes in the development of knowledge-based products and services and also develops web-based medical and financial applications.

Its expert system product (ACQUIRE) is in use in over 40 countries around the world. ACQUIRE applications serve a broad range of people, from computer naive one-time users to expert peers using applications on a daily basis.

The myACQUIRE subscription service (www.myacquire.com) allows quick, cost-effective deployment of ACQUIRE web-based applications.

Technology profile

Our main product (ACQUIRE) is a development system for building
and maintaining expert systems. It includes a knowledge
acquisition system that follows a step-by-step methodology for
building knowledge-bases, a built-in inference engine, and a software development kit (SDK) to integrate knowledge-bases with other software applications.

Our consulting services offer both knowledge-based and standard
software application development. In addition to knowledge-
based technologies, we also have extensive database and web-
based skills gained through experience integrating expert
systems with a wide variety of other software applications.

We often use our myACQUIRE subscription service to deploy web-based applications due to its flexibility, cost-effectiveness, and ease of maintenance.
