Company Profile


RCS Energy Services is an industry leader in Integrated Project Management, engineering and other related field services, offering comprehensive services and support to civil, mining, oil and gas, and other industries around the world. We help companies apply new technologies for creative problem solving and efficient project management, while at the same time overseeing regulatory compliance and health and safety.

RCS is proud to offer certified professional services in all areas of project management. Our team is fully equipped to partner closely with you for comprehensive project management, administrative support, and technological problem solving to keep your project on track.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Portable Steam Generation
The RCS line of rental and rental purchase steam generators do not require, piles, engineered surface locations and or any other type of pre-foundation work.



Application Use of RCS Portable Steam Generation

- Warm-up phase of new wells
- Temporary steam for shutdowns and repairs of existing facilities
- Pilot steam facilities
- SAGD Primary
- Cyclical steaming application
- Remote test wells


Financial Benefits

- Flexible monthly rental charges with Rent to purchase options available
- NO capital outlay


Operational and Engineering Support

- Full operational support can be supplied
- Heavy oil Technicians with 10-15 years experience
- Reservoir support
- Engineer support


Specifications for a Typical RCS 25mmBtu:

- 220m3-270m3/day total H20 at 80% steam quality
- Capability to run a steam separator to achieve 95% steam quality or higher
- 3-1 turn down for warm up phase
- Dual fuel, diesel or natural gas
- 3-5 days set-up time required
- 36000kg to 55000kg gives the ability to move in during 75% road ban condition with proper equipment


Water Treatment:

- Complete dual drain softener package, remote water treatment not required
- Compact design to fit in the same building as OTSG
- Heavy oil treatment for pressure or atmospheric treating processes


Our equipment can be custom manufactured to your project requirements.

Service Name:
Integrated Project Management
RCS Energy Services is an industry leader in Integrated Project Management services. Our combined expertise and nimble infrastructure ensure a quick start, professional management, and timely completion of your mining, oil and gas, water, and civil projects – no matter the size.

Our team combines multiple engineering and field disciplines into one cohesive operating unit, ready to manage each stage of a project in less time than our competitors.

Our domestic and international network of resources and best-in-class support services enable us to leverage strong relationships with local stakeholders and ensure that with RCS in charge, your projects are completed safely, within budget, while adhering to strict regulatory controls.

When RCS works as the prime contractors on a project, we us our proprietary software, Integrated Project Manager, to manage all aspects of your operations: safety, land, surveying, construction, drilling, completions, facilities, infrastructure, and personnel.

Integrated Project Management services:

- Prime contractor to manage all operations
- Project budgeting and cost tracking
- Regulatory compliance and monitoring
- Risk management and mitigation
- Environmental compliance and solutions
- General liability insurance protection for your entire project

Service Name:
Enhanced Oil Recovery
RCS Energy Services offers professional experience and advanced solutions for enhanced oil recovery and reservoir stimulation. New and depleting reservoirs sometimes require enhanced recovery schemes to achieve their maximum potential and ensure general field economics.

RCS evaluates and implements a variety of reservoir recovery enhancements and has the ability to develop custom recovery schemes for specific reservoirs. No matter what solutions we use, you can be assured the methods we use will be carried out safely, within budget, and will adhere to strict regulatory controls.



Enhanced oil recovery services:

- Well optimization
- Chemical and thermal design and implementation for heavy oil projects
- Polymer floods design and implementation
- Production optimization
- Old well recovery

Service Name:
Environment
RCS Energy Services employs a staff with diverse environmental experience to ensure your projects conform to all regulatory requirements. Our primary goal is that your projects are conducted safely, within budget, while adhering to strict regulatory controls.

Engage us at the beginning of a project to identify environmental hazards early in the planning process, so we can develop a global environmental strategy.

When a project is already in process, RCS is available to step in, assess any existing hazards, and implement short-term solutions and long-term planning.

We also help companies with pre-land acquisition, well before a project is established. RCS develops inventory modeling and maps out a course for their entire project.



Environmental services:

- Pre-application scouting and access management to ensure the most cost effective solutions possible, diminishing environmental impact
- LANDmanager: RCS’s proprietary software for Land Management Design
- Utilization of proprietary software and digital mapping systems for Land inventory monitoring reclamation managment
- Long-term reclamation planning
- Pre-reclamation phase 1-3 assessments, leases, pads, roads, sumps, mines, water ways, buildings, and facilities
- Reclamation field execution, including the completion of any type of site, facility, leases, roads, pits, sumps
- Sump searches and pre-application testing
- Environmental impact assessments, both pre- and post-project
- Waste and water management
- Water tracking and usage

Our Environmental team is housed under Boreal Land Services.



Service Name:
Product Optimization & Equipment Solutions
RCS and its principles have worked around the world for 25 years evaluating projects and providing optimization solutions. We ensure the economic viability of your project by sending industry specialists to first evaluate your requirements. We then provide unique solutions either through training, the addition of highly specialized personnel, and/or customized design of purpose-built equipment to guarantee completion and optimization of your project.



RCS and its team can evaluate your project and provide the following services to support your project:

- Budgeting
- Purpose-built, designed and manufactured equipment suited specifically to your project
- Downhole Design & Engineered Drilling, and completions solutions
- Technical Personnel Solutions: experts to fill operational gaps or provide onsite training in specialized fields of operations


Whether you are analyzing the feasibility of a new project or require optimization of an existing project, count on RCS to provide state of the art customized personnel & equipment solutions.

Service Name:
Regulatory
RCS Energy Services regulatory services ensure your project is in full compliance with all regulations from start to finish. We employ skilled regulatory professionals with extensive experience, who can identify potential problems, offer efficient solutions, and streamline the successful completion of your project.



Regulatory services include:

- Federal and provincial well licenses, permits, and audit packages
- Compliance monitoring
- Annual reporting
- Waste management design solutions, including applications, manifesting, reporting, and tracking
- Injection, disposal, and water source permits


Our Regulatory team is housed under Boreal Land Services.

Service Name:
Safety
RCS Energy Services maintains a team of highly qualified professionals for all your safety requirements, and we can provide you staff with NCSO, CRSP, COR, and Enform certifications. Applying quality control measures can significantly reduce the occurrence of workplace incidents and accidents. Achieving your goals with the highest level of safety is one of our core responsibilities, and our professional team streamlines this process to create an efficient and safe workplace.



Safety services in your office environment:

- SAFETYmanager is a proprietary software that manages databases, complete field safety, investigation, personnel, and audit forms from the office certifications, tickets, licenses, drug testing, office and field personnel, all relevant forms.
- Health and safety program development
- Program implementation and maintenance specific to you company’s needs or accreditations, such as COR (certificate of recognition)
- COR certified internal and external auditors
- Preparation for industry or COR certified health and safety audits
- ISN & Avetta (formerly PICS) monitoring & reporting
- Mobile training services and online safety training courses
- On-site training center in Calgary
- Emergency response planning and testing
- Digitized safety forms – along with comprehensive data management of all forms


Safety services on-site and in the field:

- Comprehensive health and safety program monitoring, managing both on-site auditing and reporting
- Field COR audits & reporting
- Contractor personnel & equipment evaluations and pre-project audits
- An entire team of on-site safety personnel to supply complete field project safety and management
- Supplemental on-site safety personnel and management to augment your existing team
- Security personnel for construction, oil and gas, mining and hydroelectric projects
- Safety equipment rentals
- Mobile extensions and additions to SAFETYmanager, our proprietary software, as part of our comprehensive data management capabilities
– digitized safety forms for tablets, computers, and all mobile devices


Our safety management team is housed under Integrated Safety Management Services.

Service Name:
Joint Ventures Engaging Aboriginal Communities
RCS Energy Services is proud to support opportunities for Aboriginal communities and individuals through joint ventures. RCS assists Aboriginal communities by supplying support services to enter a wide variety of industries.

We prioritize growth opportunities for Aboriginally owned businesses through sustainable joint ventures between industry and the communities of interest.

Our team has extensive experience dealing with the unique challenges of working with Aboriginal communities that have minimal exposure to industry.


Services assisting Aboriginal communities and individuals:

- Identification of essential goods and services required for success in each industry sector, and opportunities to fulfill those needs
- Access to joint ventures or partnerships to supply essential goods and services, with priority given to local private sector partners
- Contract negotiations for goods and services with the many industry sectors
- Determination of the appropriate corporate vehicles for business ventures or partnerships
- Legal advice on commercial and taxation issues associated with business ventures or partnerships
- Negotiation of contracts with potential partners
- Finalization and execution of business ventures in the identified priority areas
- Identification of employment and training opportunities and development of required certification delivery mechanisms
- Establishment of First Nations monitoring, evaluation and audit systems
- Defining traditional land use areas
