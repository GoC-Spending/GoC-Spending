Company Profile


Editarians offers a complete range of professional editing and proofreading services. Since 2002, we have been consumed with establishing industry best practices and delivering wow to our business, nonprofit, and academic clients. Clients love our quick turn-around, on-time delivery, affordable pricing, and friendly approach. Our secure process safeguards your data, and we back everything up with a rock solid, 100% satisfaction guarantee.

We edit just about everything: ad copies, admission essays and letters, annual reports, APA style and Chicago style papers and assignments, articles, brochures, corporate style guides, curriculum vitae, journal articles, letters, manuals, marketing collateral, newsletters, presentations, press releases, proposals, reports, resumes, self-published books, theses and dissertations, training manuals, user guides, and website content.

Let us bring our experience, critical analysis, and impeccable standards to your important documents. You will be wowed!

My name is Richard Eaton and recently I successfully completed the requirements for a Masters Degree in Leadership from Royal Roads University. A significant part of the graduation requirements involves the completion of a major project papera thesis. Royal Roads demands a very high standard of writing, and is meticulous about learner adherence to the American Psychological Associations (APA) writing style standards. As a busy professional with a large business and growing family, I had very little time to spare and could not afford to go through any lengthy rework processes if I wanted to graduate on time. This need for first time approval by the Royal Roads Ethics Committee left me with little margin for error, and I needed to find a high quality editor who could respond to my needs quickly and efficiently.

On the advice of Royal Roads faculty member David Reagan, I retained the services of Karen and Erin with Editarians. I found Karen and Erin to be an excellent editing team who, at all times, provided me with high quality, timely advice regarding APA style and writing quality. Because I was forced to do much of my writing on weekends and evenings, I was particularly impressed by Karen's willingness and ability to respond at any time of the day or night to the latest draft of my paper, resulting in the completion of my paper well ahead of schedule. I highly valued Karen's insights and advice, and seldom found the need to second guess any input she had for me regarding my paper. I also found her friendly and very encouraging, which was of critical importance in helping me to stay motivated throughout a very challenging personal learning experience, complicated by ample opportunities for self-doubt. Even more amazing is the fact that she accomplished all this without even having an opportunity to meet with me face-to-face at any time during my major project. In many ways, Karen helped to provide the leadership I needed to complete a challenging leadership focused project, and was consequently a good role model for me.

I have no hesitation in recommending Editarians to any other learner seeking a high quality editor who is also an excellent leader.

R. H. Eaton, MA
Founding Partner
Berlin, Eaton & Associates Ltd.
