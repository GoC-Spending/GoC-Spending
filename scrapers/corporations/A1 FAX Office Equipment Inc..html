Company Profile


Office equipment sales & service. Since 1978. Certified Dragon NaturallySpeaking word recognition reseller. Authorized dealer for Xerox colour printers, Philips digital dictation systems, Grundig digital dictation systems, Olympus digital dictation systems. Panasonic, Lexmark, HP digital printers/copiers.

Service most brands of copiers, printers, fax machines, paper shredders, dictation systems. On site service (Ottawa area only).
