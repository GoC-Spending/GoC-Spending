Success stories:

Robert Bob Mason has 35 years management and consulting experience in strategic and business planning, international business management, new product and service development, and project management.

Currently he is a management consultant, providing business planning, opportunity evaluations and operational implementation expertise to MMM Group International Inc. - an engineering consulting firm involved in a diverse range of assignments including planning, project management and structural, mechanical, electrical, transportation, traffic, municipal, landscape and environmental engineering design.

He is also providing strategic guidance to senior managers in Health Canada relating to the implementation of the department`s Cost Recovery Initiative - a program to change fees charged to drug and medical device manufacturers and distributors for licensing and marketing their products in Canada.

Bob was an executive for a number of years with a telecom high-technology firm, directing its marketing strategy, product management, pricing, communications and sales support activities,
and was instrumental in its evolution from start-up, through buy-out to public entity.

As product manager with Canadas major telecommunications carrier, he also negotiated and implemented international settlement arrangements with foreign companies to expand and manage calling card and reverse charge (collect) services in North America and overseas. He has also led multi-disciplinary teams and spearheaded the development and take-to-market efforts of several multi-million dollar, business-critical projects.

Bob has worked for a number of clients in both the private and public sector, and has developed excellent relationships with officials and executives within many Canadian federal government departments.
