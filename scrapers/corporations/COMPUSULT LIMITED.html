Company Profile


Compusult is a Canadian-owned Information Technology (IT) and electronics company that has been in operation since 1985. Our primary focus is geospatial technologies to meet defence, aerospace, and government needs. We are also extensively experienced in design and development of custom and commercial software and electronics; systems integration; and secure data centre operations and hosting services, especially for Web-based applications. We are also a leading designer and exporter of products for persons with disabilities. Our Canadian offices are in Mount Pearl, NL; Halifax, NS; and Ottawa, ON. Since 2007, we have alos operated a U.S. subsidiary, Compusult Systems, Inc. (CSI) in Reston, VA.

Our flagship product, Web Enterprise Suite (WES), is an integrated suite of software applications, based on open standards, for efficient, cost-effective creation of Web-based geospatial portals for centralized data discovery, collaboration, sharing, management and interoperability. These portals are deployed for applications in Defense, Homeland Security, Situational Awareness, Sensor Data Access and Management, Environmental Monitoring and Management, Emergency Management and Response, Public Health and Safety, and Resource Management. WES, an established Commercial Off-The-Shelf (COTS) product, is the most comprehensive and cost-effective geospatial portal solution available today. Our broad range of WES clients include the U.S. Department of Defense (DoD), major DoD Prime Contractors, the Canadian Department of National Defence (DND), NASA, the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS), and Natural Resources Canada (NRCan).

Compusult has been exporting our products and services internationally since 1987. Our primary market is the USA. We also export to Japan, Korea, Australia, Norway, and the EU, and anticipate future sales of WES and associated services to government and defence clients in Latin America.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Web Enterprise Suite
Web Enterprise Suite

Provides integrated data mining and data warehouse in a single,
complete soulution. Web Enterprise Suite helps customers
discover, analyze and distribute information, increasing
effectiveness and, from a business perspective, makes firms more
competitive in their marketplaces.

With Web Enterprise Suite you can have easy acccess to digital
catalogues, data archives, and decision analysis tools. All
information can be consolidated into one seamless WAN or LAN
integrating database, GIS, imagery, data analysis and Internet
technologies regardless of platform or data format.

Product Name:
Automated Monitoring Systems
Responder, DigiCite and Vindicator are automated monitoring
systems that allow healthcare and other facilities operators to
improve existing services, while maintaining and even reducing
costs. All of Compusult's automated monitoring systems
supplement the efforts of daytime staff, support reduced staff at
nights and on weekends, and provide improved working conditions
for all personnel.
Each system offers generally unobtrusive installation and
integration with current facilities equipment, such as alarm
systems, electronic displays, telephone consoles, switchboards,
etc.
Where workplace accessibility is an issue, Compusult leads the
way. Responder, DigiCite and Vindicator incorporate
state-of-the-art technology that enables blind and visually
impaired individuals to independently work in security,
switchboard, and other monitoring and response-related
environments, including:
 hospitals, medical centers, and other healthcare facilities;
 military bases;
 manufacturing and physical plants, factories, utilities, and
refineries;
 vessel control rooms;
 government and corporate facilities; and,
 any organization requiring enhanced automated monitoring and
response systems.
Customized Responder, DigiCite and Vindicator systems are
currently installed at medical centers and air force bases across
the United States.


Product Name:
FasseTrack
FasseTrack is a powerful, multi-user asset management system for
electronic tracking of fixed assets within each area of your
operations. Assets are uniquely identified by user-assigned tag
numbers, which may be generated on bar code labels for
subsequent scanning by bar code readers and portable data
collection terminals. FasseTrack keeps you abreast of the status
of every asset in one or many organizations.

FasseTrack is also a flexible and user-friendly system. It
simplifies identifying, locating, and tracking all assets, from
computers to general office equipment to furniture.

FasseTrack can also be used with FasseTrack PDA, our mobile
asset tracking system.

Product Name:
Assistive Technology for Persons with Disabilities
Compusult Makes the Workplace Accessible to Employees with
Disabilities

Since 1993, Compusult Limited has provided Assistive Technology
products and services to make the workplace accessible to
employees with disabilities. Our products provide access to
computers, telephones, alarm panels, electronic indicators and
displays, bar coded items, Web sites, databases, and broadcast
and spoken information.

Service Name:
Procurement Opportunity and Delivery System (PODS)
PODS is the Procurement Opportunity and Delivery System.

It is your one-stop, comprehensive source for Canadian, and
indeed, world-wide tender opportunities. PODS is an interactive
opportunity network which can supply Canadian business with
timely information on tenders which match your unique subscriber
profile. It's easy-to-use World Wide Web interface will provide
you with powerful opportunity searching capability and a wealth
of additional information on bidding requirements of the various
tendering agencies.

The PODS provides automated bidmatching services to companies
across Canada, especially with respect to tenders issued by the
Governments. PODS uses state-of-the-art data communications and
software technologies to address current and prospective
opportunities for the on-line access to procurement information.

For subscription or additional information, please visit: www.pods.net.
