Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Label Printer Applicators
IBC is the supplier of printer applicators, label applicators and packaging machinery for nearly every industry known to the world. We specialize in accurate high speed applicators of labels for every product including lumber, bottles, boxes, cans, pallets, consumer goods and fruits. Whether your printing labels for individual items or for bundled pallets IBC has a cost effective solution.

Product Name:
Bar Code Scanners
Bar code scanners are integral to inventory management in shipping and receiving, point of sale systems or in manufacturing processes to keep track of work-in-process. IBC has wide range of options for every application. Wireless, bluetooth or corded; let us find the right option for your application.

Product Name:
Mobile Computers & Tablets
International Bar Coding offers a broad range of data collection, mobile computing, RFID with wired and wireless connectivity. Looking for PDA, tablet, gun style, or vehicle mounted; IBC has a mobile computer for your application. With brands like Motion, Lenovo, HP, Motorola, Symbol, and DLI; IBC has the tools for your needs.

Product Name:
Point of Sale (POS) & Mobile Point of Sale (MPOS)
International Bar Coding carries a full line of point of sale peripherals including receipt printers, bar code scanners, weigh scales, MSR's, pole displays, touchscreens, computers, cash drawers and the software to make it all work together. We're now one of the leaders on the West Coast for Mobile Point of Sale (MPOS.)

Product Name:
Industrial Label & Receipt Printers
Our barcode printer product line includes direct thermal and thermal transfer printers, OEM print engines and printer accessories. Whether your printing labels, receipts, or invoices; we'll have the printer for you at the best price.

Product Name:
RFID Products
Only IBC fully delivers on the promise of scalable RFID systems. IBC offers the unmatched combination of a complete suite of RFID product and services, two decades of RFID experience and industry leading partners.

Product Name:
Software
IBC can simplify access to information, improve organizational agility, streamline integration with a wide range of software applications, even for the highly specialized industries and organizations.

Product Name:
Tags & Labels
IBC's selection of label and tag materials meets the requirements of most applications and budgets. Our labels and tags include paper and synthetic label and tag materials. Every year we produce upwards of 500,000,000 tags and labels every year.
Thousands of different custom colors available.
UL/cUL and CSA approved materials.

Service Name:
Bar Coding Systems Design and Implementation
IBC, International Bar Coding Systems & Consulting Inc is an inventory management, data collection, labeling, bar coding and RFID solution provider. IBC has the most advanced technology in Label & Tag printers, Label Applicators, Printer Applicators, inkjet coding systems, hand held portable computers and laser scanners.

Service Name:
Computer Programming
IBC supplies user friendly data collection software to help you keep track of your work in process, inventory and shipments. No matter what product you make or sell we can help you keep Score! IBC can enable you to verify inventory, collect data and generate required documents for shipping, verify accuracy of order fulfillment and track product movement through production to keep track of work in progress.

Service Name:
Hardware Design and Mechanical Engineering
IBC has been designing and constructing the world best printer applicators since 1993. Our world renowned PA1000 printer applicator is the fastest and most accurate machine in North America. Our engineers are constantly studying the newest technology to improve our speeds and accuracy standards. Let IBC quote you on any specific label applicator, printer applicator or packaging machinery.

Service Name:
Installation, Servicing, and Systems Maintenance
The technical crew at IBC has installed and maintained our systems on six continents and are fully aware of the importance of quick service and maintenance turn around times. Many of the companys we do business with are 24/7 businesses that demand constant up time. We are prepared to be your partner in up keeping the systems we install with as little as yearly visits or as extensive attention as monthly preventative maintenance calls and 24 hour on call support. We will NOT leave you hanging!

Service Name:
Remote IT & On-Site Support
Todays small-medium businesses are overloaded with technology that wasnt available 50 years ago. Your business is filled with critical computer hardware, software, networking equipment and peripherals needing to operate and communicate flawlessly to support your busy customers. At IBC we let you focus on being you and putting your best foot forward, while we sort out your technology issues. Dont be bogged down by the stress and expense of hiring Information Technology (IT) services, let IBC be your on call IT support, always there when you need us.
