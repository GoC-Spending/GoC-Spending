Company Profile


For over half a century, DFP (Dover Finishing Products) Inc. has supplied the industry and consumers with premier quality touch-up products for furniture and finished wood. Our Classic and Production Fill Sticks use the finest waxes, dyes and pigments to ensure colour consistency and reliable fills time after time. Our Touch-Up Markers along with the trend setting Pro Finish line of spray finishes and toners are all made with pre-catalyzed formulations that allow for excellent compatibility over existing finishes as well as formidable resistance to yellowing and abrasion.

The Dover Finishing System Classic and Production Fill Sticks offer the finest wax repair available. The high grade waxes yield a semi-hard stick that is simply rubbed into the damaged area, the excess wiped off and what's left is a beautiful repair that costs a fraction of refinishing.

Our Pump and Factory Touch-Up Markers are fast-drying, easy-to-use tools that are a must in any repair or manufacturing operation. The Pump's rich pigments allow worn edges, scuffs, nicks and scratches to virtually disappear restoring wood pieces to their original beauty. The high solid content also makes it an excellent veneer and laminate repair solution. The Factory Touch-Up Marker is fast becoming the industry standard in production-line applications involving colour restoration across various types of finishes and in any case where a fast-drying touch-up is a must.

Without a doubt, the line of Pro Finish Spray Finishes and Toners are the professional's choice when rich tones and clarity are required in enhancing the beauty of the wood. From clears and wood tones to black and white opaque finishes, no job is too tough for these top quality finishes.

And now, with our line of Trade Secret aftermarket touch up products for the consumer, retailers have an excellent new revenue source and can guarantee that their clients are using only the best products available for wood and leather furniture.



DFP. Inspired touch up solutions.
