Company Profile


Mission
To provide major corporations and organizations
with value-added commercial printing,
folding packaging and related services.



Operating Highlights

- Commercial printer since 1982, began packaging in 1987
- 45% of sales from commercial customers, 55% from folding packaging customers
- Main customers in Windsor-to-Montreal corridor
- History of careful, steady growth
- Competitive edge: perfectionist approach to quality, service, reliability
- Employs 95 people
- Finest equipment available:
Macintosh and Agfa-based front-end
Heidelberg presses,
Stahl and MBO folders,
Mueller-Martini stitcher-trimmer,
Bobst die-cutters and folder-gluers,
- ISO 9000 registered since 1998; version 9001:2000 registered in February 2002
- Average annual sales increase of 10% since 1991
- References available on request

Technology profile

Pre-press

- Several Macintosh PowerPC's loaded with the latest versions of page composition, color management, trapping and conversion software. Although we ordinarily work in QuarkXpress, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop, we have the capabilities to use any Mac or PC file format.
- DAT, DVD, CD, JAZ, ZIP, Optical drives, Internet connection (ADSL)
- Agfa Apogee 3.0 Printdrive, Pilot, Quickstrip workflow software
- Agfa Galileo Platesetter (35" x 45" plate size)
- Scancorp Othello (drum) imagesetter with Ultra 1 RIP (20" x 251/2" image area)
- Agfa Sherpa 43" digital proofer
- Fuji 950 film processor and Fuji 850 plate processor
- 3M Matchprint II and Color Key proofing systems
- NuArc platemaker
- ScanMate 4000 Drum Scanner
- Umax UC1200SE Flatbed Scanner
- Graphtec CAD/CAM sample cutting machine and CIMEX software
- Several other digital proofers.

Offset Presses

- Heidelberg six-color CD Speedmaster with coater (40" x 28" sheet size)
- Heidelberg five-color Speedmaster with coater (40" x 28" sheet size)
- Heidelberg two-color Speedmaster (40" x 28" sheet size)
- Heidelberg two-color GTO (20" x 14" sheet size)
- Heidelberg one-color Gesti (10" x 8" sheet size)
- AB Dick one-color (18" x 11" sheet size)

Bindery

- Stahl folder (40" x 30" sheet size)
- Stahl folder (26" x 20" sheet size)
- MBO folder (40" x 30" sheet size)
- Mueller-Martini 335 stitcher-trimmer (8-pocket)
- Polar-Mohr cutter (50" mouth)
- Shrink-wrap, round-corner, multi-hole, Bostitch machines

Folding Packaging

- Two Bobst 1260 die-cutters (49"x 35" sheet sizes)
- Two Bobst Media 68 straight-line gluers with Handipak, autotaping attachments
- Heidelberg Windmills (13"x18" and 12"x15")
- Heidelberg Platen (24"x36")

Shipping

- International 28' 44,000-lb. truck
- Navistar 18' 7,000-lb. Truck
