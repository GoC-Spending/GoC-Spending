Company Profile


Since 1989, HRSG has worked with a range of industries to define talent needs, address skill deficiencies, and improve
individual and organizational performance. We are a recognized leader in the field of competency-based talent management,
and our flagship software, CompetencyCore, is being used by organizations around the world, to facilitate the more
effective application of competencies across the talent management lifecycle. Clients include global corporations and small
or midsized organizations operating in sectors such as logistics, finance, accounting, technology, HSE, HR, manufacturing,
sales and marketing, and many more. For more information, please visit www.hrsg.ca.
