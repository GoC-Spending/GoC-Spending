Company Profile


The Food Safety Market is owned and operated by Freeborn & Associates. Our company has been in business since 1997. We provide training and development of training materials for the foodservice and hospitality industry.

Our company has provided services to most of Canada's top foodservice companies from independent operations to chains and franchises.

We have been recognized with awards from the Foodservice Consultants Society International. You will find articles penned by the founder, Kevin Freeborn in top industry publications.

Since our beginning we have certified tens of thousands of food handlers in food safety and thousands of trainers.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Online food safety training
What is it?
NFSTP online enrollment keys are special codes that can be used to enter the online training. Within 2 business days of purchasing these enrollment keys you will get an email with instructions on how to distribute them to your employees or students.

Who should purchase this?
Foodservice operators who want to have their employees take online training but want to pay for the training on their employees behalf.

Schools, colleges and universities that want their students to take the online training. Often bookstores at these institutions will purchase the keys.

Supervisors and Persons in Charge of Food Safety (PICS) for Tim Hortons restaurants.

Service Name:
Classroom food safety training workbooks
What is this?
This package includes a copy of the NFSTP Student Workbook and a prepaid Exam answer form.
This product is perfect for school bookstores. Also ideal for training agencies who wish to spend their training budget before the end of the fiscal year end.

Student Workbook
The NFSTP Student Workbook is used during the training as a training aid that permits the student to follow along during the training and take notes and complete quizzes to reinforce the learning. It is the perfect compliment to NFSTP's PowerPoint.

-139 full colour pages
- Review quizzes for each lesson
- Lot's of images to reinforce concepts
- Easy to read and follow during training

Scantron Answer Form

The Scantron form is for students who are completing the National Food Safety Training Program certification exam. This is for paper exams only (this is not required for online exams). Students fill in their name and contact information and then record the answer to each exam question on the Scantron form. Their trainer then collects the completed forms and returns them to the National Food Safety Training Program administration offices for marking.

Who should purchase this?
Certified NFSTP trainers should purchase the Workbook and Scantron form for their students. This includes private trainers, college and university instructors or corporate trainers. Purchasing the Stident Workbook and Scantron answer form together saves you money (purchasing them separately is slightly more expensive).

Students taking the NFSTP at school can also purchase this package for their courses. Check with your school's bookstore as they may carry it as well.

Important note: Trainers will have to order the exam booklets separately.

What we do

The world is continuously changing. Our job is
to know how to manage that change, identify
opportunities for your business and transfer our
knowledge so that our clients benefit.

Freeborn & Associates will work with your
organization to develop a "customized" solution
that works. To ensure this we follow a process,
a series of steps, that set and manage
expectations for each client.

1. The initial meeting with our clients gives
them an opportunity to get to know us and for us
to know them. At this meeting we identify what
the client can expect from us and explain what
commitment we need from the client.

2. A preliminary 'diagnosis' of our client's
needs is next. We get at the root causes of
their challenges and identify some possible
solutions. This is when our client can look at
some options.

3. Preparing a proposal and agreeing on a
process, how the project is going to be managed,
is required before moving forward.

4. Straightforward, practical issues such as the
communication process with the client and how
time will be allocated during the assignment.
This includes things like how much time will be
spent on research, face to face meetings,
documentation and reporting. We make sure our
clients are in agreement with our methodology
before we start to conduct the assignment.

4. Fact finding using information from the
company, the industry or the marketplace, helps
us provide a more precise diagnosis of the
client's needs. We analyze the information and
provide feedback to our client.

5. Developing an action plan that will work is
the next step. You want proposals that are
results oriented and realistic. We will provide
ideas for solutions; evaluate the alternatives
and present our recommendations.

6. Successful implementation requires us to
support our clients through coaching,
facilitation, training and developing the client
team. We are available to provide hands-on
assistance throughout the implementation of your
plan.

7. Evaluation and follow-up are critical to the
measurement of success for each project. A final
report will include the changes we have observed
and cite the feedback from the participants.
Using this information we can look forward to
next steps that will keep your company on a path
of continuous improvement.

Freeborn & Associates follows this process with
all of our engagements so that our clients know
what to expect and get what they've been promised.
