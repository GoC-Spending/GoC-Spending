Company Profile


A Profile of Our Financial Services:

In today's fast paced and incresingly global business
environment, organizations must look to foreign markets for
opportunities to enhance their growth and profitability. The
emergence of a global market place for financing projects is
driving many of these firms to seek out established contacts that
will facilitate their initives.

Assessing international funding markets is a substantial
challenge for any organization. With each foreign contact, they
can face different requirements, protocol, procedures and
regulations. Researching foreign financial sources, making
appropriate contacts, understanding the business culture and
environment, requires a great deal of time, energy and resources.
Most companies find it is extremely difficult to find the means
to develop and implement an international fund locating strategy
within the context of their managerial resources.

Assessment Systems Corporation, through the experience of their
management team and associations with individuals and
organizations, possesses the ability to introduce their clients
to capital enhancement programs as one of the models to
facilitate financing. The increased activity in the international
investment arena has created new opportunities for organizations
all over the world. We have positioned ourselves to capitalize on
this trend. Organizations looking to finance their growth must
have a well developed business strategy. Whether the organization
is a going concern or "start up", foreign funding sources require
detailed background to assess the value of a plan. Funding
sources are diligent in examining the quality of proposals
presented. All assumptions about a company's ability and their
management team will undergo tests through careful due diligence.
Assessment Systems Corporation facilitates the process by
identifying weaknesses in business plans and making
recommendations for improvement.

Once satisfied, Assessment Systems Corporation will locate or
develop a customized approach for a funding program to facilitate
the specific requirements for an organization.

A Profile of Our Information technology Consulting:

Our services have been designed to address each of the main
functional areas within an Information Systems organization. Our
consultant's knowledge and experience has been applied in the
following areas:

Project Management, with an average experience of over 25 years
working with clients executives to define business technology
goals and objectives, conduct work flow analyses, ROI, undertake
business process re-engineering in a manner to support new
technology infrastructure, as well as, the typical task and
activity management associated with applications development.

Systems Engineers, a highly skilled and specialized group of
technical consultants, to select, design and implement the
technical environment to support the client's information systems
plans and application's portfolio.

Systems Development specialists, using the most current tools and
technologies in the industry, we have tailored and installed
package solutions and developed custom systems to address client
application requirements. We have significant experience with IBM
mainframes, LINUX, UNIX, NT, WINDOWS implementations supporting
various client server models.

Network Support Specialists, provide consultation and hands-on
problem solving on all communications related issues by
providing direction in network hardware and software,
configuration of architecture, programming, installtion and
on-going support.

Internet engineering and Design, more currently we have provided
training and consulting on a broad variety of Internet services:
custom web site design and implementation with hosting services,
e-commerce consulting for the development of solutions from
catalog software to credit card authorization and payment
processing, web enabling existing applications and web
applications design for specific commerce with content.

Facilities Mangement Personnel, assume complete responsibility
for a specific position or function, such as technical support
manager or computer operations and administration.

As a potential service provider, we would be able to individually
contract any of the above skills and if requested position an
organization to outsource this activity.
