Company Profile


Computer Room Services Corporation (CRSC) has set precedence by providing professional, knowledgeable service to every client. Established in 1983, we have built our reputation on providing professional services, maintenance and design solutions that enhance your business continuity. We have designed and built more than 300 facilities, ranging from wiring closets to data centres, across Canada; spanning all major sectors including government, institutional, industrial, and commercial.

Availability
With many companies today relying on 24/7 continuous uptime, it is important to partner with a company that has the experience and expertise to design and build a highly reliable infrastructure to support mission critical applications. At CRSC, we design and build facilities with high quality, redundant equipment to ensure reliability. To maintain a high level of availability, CRSC has developed maintenance programs to ensure that all equipment is functioning properly, and to identify any potential threats before they happen. CRSC offers 24/7 remote monitoring services and employs factory trained field service technicians to provide emergency service and comprehensive preventative maintenance inspections that cover the entire facility.

Efficiency
Data centres consume considerable amounts of energy, resulting in high operating costs and impacting our environment. By analyzing your needs, CRSC is able to design an efficient infrastructure by utilizing high efficiency, modular equipment including scalable Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems, Computer Room Air Conditioning (CRAC) units complete with free cooling and EC fans, Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) measurement and verification tools. CRSC employs Leadership in Environmental and Energy Design (LEED) accredited data centre designers who apply green building practices to critical physical infrastructure design, with the potential of contributing 1 Innovative Design point.

Value
One solution will not work for every business, and therefore we consider each client individually to determine the most effective plan of action. This plan of action is set in to motion by the CRSC team from conception to completion and beyond. We have an experienced team of data centre designers, application engineers, project managers, field service technicians and audit specialists to assess, design, and deliver reliable and efficient infrastructures on time and on budget. Adding to this value is our long-term relationship with many manufacturers, allowing us to purchase direct - maximizing cost savings which is then passed on to you.

Service Name:
Design Build Maintain - critical physical infrastructures
Computer Room Services Corporation (CRSC) is a leader in the design, build, and maintenance of all types of mission critical physical infrastructures, including data centres. Established in 1983, CRSC provides professional services, maintenance and equipment for all components of the data centre infrastructure. The CRSC team will supply, install, commission, and maintain any and all equipment within the facility including HVAC, uninterruptible power supply (UPS) systems, racks, generators, environmental monitoring systems, fire detection and suppression systems, security, raised flooring, and structured cabling systems. There are more than 300 CRSC designed and constructed facilities across Canada; spanning all major sectors including government, institutional, industrial, and commercial. CRSC employs 25 people in Ontario.
Extensive experience with all components of data centre infrastructure is behind the success of CRSC and a key component for client loyalty. Technology is constantly evolving and todays data centres and server rooms must be designed and built to respond to these changes. CRSC has developed a system to achieve this with every project. Growth strategies, long term planning, and the ever evolving technology landscape is factored in to all recommendations and solutions. A CRSC facility is designed, built, and maintained to last with high availability, efficiency and density.
The CRSC commitment to the total life cycle of the project ensures a long-term partnership with clients to design and build an optimal facility and continue to effectively maintain all post-implementation aspects.
CRSC employs factory trained service technicians specialized in all components of the power stream and are equipped with the most advanced power quality testing and diagnostic equipment. CRSC maintenance services include generator systems, automatic transfer switches, HVAC systems, transformers, UPS systems, power distribution units, monitoring, fire detection and suppression systems, branch circuit and facility management services. Proactive maintenance is key in determining potential faults before they impact a data centres and result in negative implications on the facility.
The proactive approach to designing and maintaining data centres has been proven to provide clients with confidence in the reliability of their facility with continuous uptime.

Technology profile

CRSC began as an equipment supplier, and through market observations and analysis, evolved in to an organization providing professional services for the design and build of mission critical infrastructures, maintenance of equipment within the power stream, and distributor of several lines of quality products.
CRSC is one of the first companies to bring together all aspects of the critical physical infrastructure power stream, another factor separating CRSC from the competition. The majority of the industry competition focuses on one business segment (ie. equipment supply or design only). CRSC is viewed as industry experts because of their experience and knowledge in all aspects of the IT infrastructure.
