Company Profile


Bolloré Logistics Canada is an international freight forwarding and logistics company established in Canada since 1976 with its head office in Montreal and offices in Quebec city, Toronto, Winnipeg, Vancouver, and Calgary.

Bolloré Logistics specializes in the worldwide transportation and handling of cargoes on turn-key projects as well as of Canadian and foreign goods and equipment on a door-to-door basis for both exports and imports that are shipped by air, ocean, and land.

Bolloré Logistics Canada also has a Transborder division which specializes in the door-to-door surface and air transportation of cargoes to and from the United States and Mexico as well as an in-house Customs Brokerage Division.

Through its affiliation to the Bolloré Group, Bolloré Logistics Canada offers you direct access to a
network of approximately 600 offices around the world in 105 countries.

The Bolloré Group is one of the world's premier international freight forwarding and logistics companies. Our expertise is based on a team of highly qualified and experienced personnel and our staff is well versed in the management and coordination of projects requiring the handling and transportation of equipment and materials.

The members of our multi-disciplinary team will pool the knowledge and experience acquired from previous projects to
ensure that your cargo is transported and delivered within the specified time frame and in the most professional manner.

Bolloré Logistics Canada is actively involved in projects that call for marshalling, crating and transporting a wide range of materials and equipment from Canadian and foreign suppliers to job sites throughout the world. These activities involve monitoring deliveries,handling, export packing , preparing and presenting consular and shipping documentation ,reserving shipping space, chartering on short or long term basis, and inland transport coordination from the port of entry to the job site including customs formalities. We also provide reliable worldwide tracking systems and performance reports.
Bolloré Logistics Canada's services are fully ISO 9001:2000 compliant.

Visit us at our website : www.bollore-logistics.com
