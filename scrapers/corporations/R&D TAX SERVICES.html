Company Profile



SPECIALISTS IN THE SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH AND EXPERIMENTAL DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

R&D Tax Services is a bottom-line oriented group of corporate tax specialists, with 30 years combined experience in SR&ED claim submissions. A wide range of experience with both large and small companies allows our firm to select the submission strategy that is best for yours. We have made successful claims for every industrial sector from Automotive Technology to Water Treatment.

Tax changes are included in each federal and provincial budget and the number of court decisions and government rulings affecting compliance is increasing. R&D legislation is complex and it is often difficult to know if your company's development activities are eligible or what costs you may claim for. Complicated filing requirements and the Canada Revenue Agency's subjective claim review process make the task even more daunting for companies without the manpower and experience to devote to it.

Many of our clients are companies who have tried, with varying degrees of frustration or failure, to claim on their own. More and more, businesses are turning to R&D Tax Services to minimize the frustration and maximize the returns through professional SR&ED claim strategies.

BENEFITS YOUR COMPANY WILL RECEIVE

Professional Experience

R&D Tax Services' team of qualified accountants, engineers, and technical writers are the ideal combination to support your SR&ED claim. Together our technical team and accounting team have filed over 700 successful SR&ED claims with the CRA.

Minimal Interruption

We operate with very little interruption to your staff. We gather the relevant information through one or two brief consultations which are scheduled for your convenience.

Dedicated Staff

R&D Tax Services is focused in its approach to SR&ED claims. Our staff writers, accountants and consultants deal exclusively with the SR&ED program to ensure that your claim will be dealt with thoroughly and efficiently.

No-Risk Engagement

Our fees are based on a percentage of funds actually recovered. You are invoiced only after funds are in hand. This allows us to provide our services on a no-risk basis. If you used an accounting firm, their fees could actually be more than the amount you recover.

Confidentiality

All financial and technical information received by our team for the preparation of your SR&ED claim will be kept in the strictest confidence.

A Positive Bottom Line Impact

In most cases, our services result in a government cheque issued to our client. This is money you may be entitled to, but would otherwise not receive without excessive time and frustration on your part.

Success stories:

R&D Tax Services has successfully prepared over 700 refund claims in every field of industry. Here are some select examples of companies that have benefited from our services:

SOFTWARE

R&D Tax Services approached a large publicly traded company, which had never filed for SR&ED tax credits and reviewed the ongoing development of their software. The company was unsure if their development work qualified for SR&ED refunds and was too busy to have their staff spend time on a project with an uncertain outcome. R&D Tax Services has now successfully filed for close to $1,000,000 in tax credits for the company.

MANUFACTURING

A manufacturing company filed a SR&ED refund claim on their own and after a series of lengthy and frustrating audits had their SR&ED refund claim reduced to less than 10% of the original amount filed by the company. By leveraging our wealth of experience, R&D Tax Services filed a subsequent SR&ED refund claim for the company and had 100% of the SR&ED refund claim approved and refunded in less than 3 months.

ENGINEERING

R&D Tax Services approached an engineering firm and reviewed the development projects that they were involved in with their clients. The engineering firm thought that their work did not qualify for SR&ED refunds because they were paid by their client. R&D Tax Services was able to obtain multiple SR&ED refunds for the engineering firm. Since then this engineering firm has referred several of their clients to R&D Tax Services for their SR&ED refund claims.

SOFTWARE

An accounting firm was unsure about the scientific eligibility of one of their software clients and called R&D Tax Services to determine if their client's work qualified for SR&ED refunds. R&D Tax Services reviewed the company's activities, filed a SR&ED refund claim, and successfully recovered a refund of $75,000 for one year. R&D Tax Services is now working with the accounting firm on the preparation of a second SR&ED refund claim for the client.

AQUACULTURE

R&D Tax Services approached a fish farm to review the various development projects that they were involved in. The company had not filed for SR&ED refunds because they were told that their work did not qualify for SR&ED refunds. R&D Tax Services has now successfully filed for SR&ED refunds of over $500,000 for this company.
