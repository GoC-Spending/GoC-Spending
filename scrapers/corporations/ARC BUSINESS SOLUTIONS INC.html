Company Profile


ARC Business Solutions Inc. is an established, growing and customer-oriented information technology solution provider with proven abilities to incorporate strategy, creativity and technical aspects into business solutions. ARC operates across North America and has offices in Edmonton, Calgary and Toronto.

Core business verticals are: Professional IT Services, Enterprise Content Management and Managed Network. With exceptional capabilities, ARCs team of dedicated professionals is committed to delivering high quality information technology solutions to meet client-specific business challenges.

ARC possesses the practical and applied experience necessary to deliver value. Our experience includes successful assignments in both the public and private sectors: Power and Utilities, Oil and Gas, Government, Manufacturing, Banking, Insurance, Telecommunications and Healthcare.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Captiva InputAccel
Captiva is the leading provider of document capture software and enterprise input management solutions. These solutions are designed to optimize the delivery of mission-critical information into an organization, transforming paper and electronic forms and documents into accurate, valuable data that can be stored and quickly accessed to speed daily operations, reduce costs, improve customer relations and meet ongoing compliance initiatives.

Captiva has developed a comprehensive suite of document capture software and input management solutions that meet the needs of organizations large and small and can readily scale from one department to an entire, global distributed enterprise. These solutions range from toolkits that enable organizations to develop their own custom input management solutions, to off-the-shelf solutions that can be immediately deployedto customizable enterprise solutions that readily integrate with ECM, ERP and legacy systems, among others.


Product Name:
Documentum
The EMC Documentum family helps you manage all types of content across multiple departments within a single repository. With a unified repository, various groups can easily share and reuse their content with other areas of the business that would benefit from access to this valuable information. Our product family also allows your business to share its content safely with outside organizations, including partners, vendors, and customers.

Product Name:
IBM / FileNet
FileNet helps organizations make better decisions faster by managing the content and processes that drive their business. Many organizations have taken advantage of IBM/FileNet ECM and BPM solutions for help in managing their mission-critical content and processes.

Product Name:
Prodagio (Imagitek)
Prodagio Software applications for accounts payable, case management, contracts management and self-service are engineered to automate and streamline complex business processes. Each is designed with embedded best practices and user-configurable business rules; Prodagio fits your business model instead of forcing you to adapt to a rigid structure normally found in most business applications. Prodagios off-the-shelf applications leverage your existing ERP investments and provide full and complete visibility into your total business process.

Prodagio provides the value of a custom solution without the risk, expense and time involved in deployment.

Proven for more than 10 years in the world's largest corporations, Imagitek solutions bring intelligence that enables our customers to eliminate guesswork, present comprehensive information within one system, and ultimately reduce operational costs.

The Prodagio Business Suite is made of several business applications and one monitoring tool that cover the following areas:

Accounts Payable processing
Case Management
Contract and legal document management
Self-Service

Service Name:
IT Professional Services
Your business needs are assessed, understood, analyzed and transformed into effective, efficient and sustainable information technology solutions.

Systems Delivery: ARC provides professional services spanning the entire software development lifecycleProject Management, IT Architecture Consulting, Design, Construction, System Testing & Quality Assurance, Education/Training and Post-Implementation Technical Support Services.

Management Consulting: ARCs strategic and operations consulting practice assists organizations through analysis of existing business issues and development of future plans. ARC provides experienced consultants, best practices, proven methodologies and analytical techniques, effective change management and people-oriented coaching skills.


Service Name:
Enterprise Content Management
ARC is an award-winning, recognized leader in the design, implementation and deployment of successful Enterprise Content Management, Business Process Management and Collaboration solutions.

ARCs experience and solutions address the entire continuum of capture/input, document management and delivery (e.g., web content, information rights management), and optimization of business processes, enabling organizations to manage their content, streamline workflow and shorten process cycle times.
ARC possesses certifications and applied experience in EMC Captiva (InputAccel, Dispatcher), EMC Documentum and IBM/FileNet, along with other related technologies (Oracle, Java, .NET, SharePoint).
ARC has earned its reputation by designing and implementing practical solutions that have allowed its clients to realize real world benefits encompassing sound content management practices, compliance with legislative requirements, improved access to information, accelerated business processes, improved operational flexibility, enhanced customer service, compliance for electronic records as documentary evidence, reliable records management practices and reduced costs (e.g., filing, storage).


Service Name:
Managed Services
Managed Services is a monthly service, founded on industry best-practices, that provides real-time monitoring of networks and connected devices.

Through secure remote access, knowledgeable ARC personnel are available to support your internal help desk functions, as well as to participate in regular planning sessions. These services help take care of day-to-day network issues and allow your IT team to focus on longer-term issues and confronting your core business.

Core benefits are: predictable and affordable monthly costs, continuous, real-time network monitoring, maximum stability and availability of your network, reduces risk of outages and network failures, prevents day-to-day issue before they occur, and enables proactive strategic planning for your business.
