Technology profile

DESIGNLORE® is licensed Mechanical Engineering and ID (industrial Design) service provider located in Toronto.

We specialize in medical and consumer product development and enclosure & electronics packaging development including RF and EMI/EMC shielding and thermal management, and development of precision electromechanical devices with design of mechanisms. Our strength is mechanical engineering and ability to engineer solutions even for the toughest applications..

From your Idea to Finished Product - DESIGNLORE® provides complete set of engineering, Industrial and Mechanical Design, R&D, CAD/CAE services, including testing, prototyping, support to manufacturing, and other professional engineering and product design consulting services.

DESIGNLORE® offers either as complete Turnkey Product Development solutions or as customized product design and development service packages, both to small companies and entrepreneurs as well as to large corporations. Depending on clients specific needs we also offer customized Industrial Design and Engineering services including prototyping and support to manufacturing.

At DESIGNLORE® all services are provided by licensed and certified engineers and designers. We are structured as "design studio", and we do not have sales or HR people and associated costs.
