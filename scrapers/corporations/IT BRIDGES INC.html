About IT Bridges

IT Bridges is a leading Information and Communication Technology “ICT” vendor servicing the GTA with leading edge technology and a comprehensive portfolio. IT Bridges was formed in 2002 and through strategic partnerships and market placement has grown from strength to strength.

About IT Bridges

We currently service customers in the Small and Medium Enterprise (SME) market. Our name says it all as we offer a BRIDGE for IT solutions, from IT Architecture, hardware procurement, Implementation to maintenance and technical support.

Our IT services architecture is designed by our top Management and Senior Systems Engineers to ensure multiple models and thought processes. We procure hardware from accredited suppliers thus ensuring that clients benefit from long term flexibility and the best possible investment protection.

When selecting Software we offer clients freedom of choice through ensuring integration, efficiency and flexibility, therefore ensuring reduction in total cost of ownership (TCO) while also supporting seamless integration of current and new business processes. Our service division is fully integrated into our operations with a dedicated focus on servicing clients with industry leading solutions.

Our passion and the reason we exist is to help businesses grow. If technology isn’t working, it is stopping the organization from getting things done to service clients, close sales and allow staff to access the required data anytime anywhere.

Our Values

Over the last 12 years we have designed, built, supported and improved a comprehensive range of IT systems and solved every type of problem out there, with clients of all sizes and in many industries. We’ve tackled big and some not so big projects but the premise was always based on our core values:

In-depth Study client’s needs and plan for enhancements / upgrades.
Act with integrity.
Provide high-quality, skilled support.
Take Initiative.
Use our partnerships, alliances to leverage cost effective solutions.
Prevent problems, rather than put out fires, by providing expert service, and quality equipment.
Improve and innovate continuously.

Mission Statement

To provide medium sized companies with a personal and complete IT Services, Solutions, Consulting, Designing, Implementation and Support.

Let IT Bridges be your service and solution provider for all your IT needs.

Unique applications:

Consulting Services

IT Consulting Services has now taken center stage, transforming customer experience, business operations and the way we work together. To maximize the opportunities presented by the digital revolution, companies need to align business IT and security with strategy, using digital solutions and intelligence as an enabler for innovation and growth.

Consulting Services

As an independent firm, we find the solution that is right for your business – we don’t sell you the product that is right for us. We combine our specializations and the knowledge of our most experienced people into a team that works well with you and for you, giving you the insight you need and challenging your approach to create exceptional IT solutions with lasting impact.

We help organizations make the best use of information technology to achieve their business objectives in the most comprehensive manner.

Each business is unique and has its own IT requirements. While larger organizations meet these requirements through an in-house team, small and mid-sized businesses (SME) largely depend on IT consultants to provide valuable advice and recommendations at an affordable cost.

IT Bridges is one of the leading IT Consulting Services providers in the GTA that has been helping SMEs meet their daily or periodical IT requirements most cost-effectively. Our IT consulting services are designed to help you deal with technical challenges and give you a distinctive edge over your competitors.

Depending on your requirements, we can provide external advice, recommendations as well as extend our special expertise to manage temporary projects, where hiring of permanent employees is not required.
We See Things that You May Have Overlooked.

Whether you have a technical background or not, there are a number of factors that you can miss while managing and maintaining your IT infrastructure. Moreover, organizations that cannot afford to have an in-house team of IT experts are also at a risk of taking wrong decision. IT Bridges’ consultants have the experience and the expertise to provide answers to the questions related to your IT infrastructure. Our specialized expertise in estimating, overseeing, and managing IT systems makes us the best choice for advising you on various matters related to information technology. We are capable of providing you with a fresh perspective to a problem, along with more objective advice and recommendations, to help you take better decisions.
We Solve Problems Faster and Better.

Key / Major clients:

Managed Services

Unlike the old “break-fix” IT Support model, our Managed Services align the interest of the service provider “in this case, IT Bridges” with those of the client – namely you. Both parties desire for the client’s network to be absolutely stable – because they both lose money when anything goes wrong. In the event of downtime, you lose productivity, while we lose our most precious resource – technical support
Managed Servicestime. As a result of this alignment, we are incentivised to work harder and smarter to ensure that your network remains 100% stable.

You pay a fixed monthly fee for us to keep your network up and running, no matter what.
There is no limit to the number of hours you can use.
We are incentivised to keep your network as stable as possible!
We do all of the proactive maintenance we can to achieve optimal network stability.
In order to achieve that, we’ve made significant investment in the latest network management systems.
You enjoy the benefits of enterprise-level automation and monitoring systems.
Our response times are backed by a comprehensive SLA (Service Level Agreement).
Monthly IT servicing on your company network, workstations and notebooks.
Track, report and resolve all IT problems as they arise.
Security management, ensuring all software is up to date.
Ensure your systems are all virus/spyware/malware free.
Reduce downtime; foresee problems before it impacts your business.

Network Administration

IT Bridges engineers are highly skilled in designing, installing and supporting complex and disparate networking architectures with the following network integration services provided.

Network Administration

To protect against outages, IT Bridges can design a comprehensive disaster recovery plan to protect your assets, your reputation… even your existence. You’ll be able to mitigate disaster risks to your computer systems, assure the recovery and availability of your mission-critical systems, and preserve valuable information assets with minimal interruption to customer service.

IT Bridges can design, implement, support and maintain network infrastructure to connect your servers, desktop and mobile devices, point of sale systems and other IT and communications infrastructure. Connecting People

Testimonial:

Like many companies over the past 25 years, my husband and I decided to incorporate computers to make work easier and more productive but in the process we learned that we also needed to have someone to take care of the frequent problems that can be encountered with computers. Over the years we tried many different computer repair services, many were unreliable and some downright dishonest, often we felt as if it was a make work project at our expense, so we would move on to another such company until we finally had enough and did not trust any of them.
Word of mouth has always been the best advertising for any company and one day when I was complaining to a friend about computers and the so called service providers, she told me of her friend a computer engineer. She arranged for us to meet Abdel and his company IT Bridges, from that day on we never looked back, we finally met someone with "HONESTY and INTEGRITY". When our computers needed a clean-up or maintenance, Abdel was there and when our system needed to be replaced, he was there to take care of it for us and guide us through the most effective but economical ways to go. We learned a lot including the importance of having our own server. Abdel would always advise us of the best approach both for efficiency and cost and we learned to trust him and his judgement, as he would never steer us wrong or charge us unjustly. Thank you Abdel and IT Bridges for coming into our lives when you did, we only wish it had been years earlier.
