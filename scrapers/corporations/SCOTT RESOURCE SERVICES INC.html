Company Profile


Primary areas of expertise are in fisheries, wildlife and
vegetation. SRS has provided professional services to more than
900 clients in government, industry and the private sector on
over 1500 projects. Affiliations for Jim Scott (President)
include: R.P.Bio., A.Sc.T., Canadian Soc. of Environmental
Biologists, American Fisheries Soc. member and past executive
(1991-1992), Canadian Water Resources Assoc. member and
Northwest Floodplain Management Assoc. member.

Forestry
 Watershed Restoration Program projects
 Stream classification assessments as required by B.C.s
Forest Practices Code
 1:20,000 Reconnaissance Fish and Fish Habitat Inventory
Projects including Quality Assurance Monitoring
 Baseline inventory to support forest development plans
 Environmental monitoring and fish salvage services for
the forestry sector

Fisheries Management
 Sport, commercial and subsistence fishery surveys
 Habitat evaluation/classification, escapement/standing
stock estimation and lake surveys
 Impact assessments and mitigation/compensation designs
 Fish habitat rehabilitation

Wildlife and Vegetation Assessment/Management
 Bio-inventories of vegetation and wildlife
 Impact assessments and mitigation/compensation designs
 Bioengineering
 Wildlife control structures

Infrastructure Projects
 Environmental services related to pipeline installation
and maintenance, road widening, realignments and/or extensions,
bridge and culvert installation/replacements, and railway
extension/expansion
 Services have been provided to federal, provincial and
municipal governments, utility companies, rail companies and
private contractors

Development (Residential/Industrial/Commercial)
 Environmental impact assessments to support rezoning,
subdivision and development permit applications
 Agency liaison and environmental approvals expedition
 Impact mitigation and habitat compensation plans
 Erosion and sediment control plans
 Environmental monitoring

Aggregate Industry
 Biological impact assessments to support applications
for existing and proposed quarries
 Erosion and sediment control plans and monitoring for
quarries and floodplain extraction
 Regulatory agency liaison and negotiations

Scott Resource Services Inc. has played an integral role in
projects which have won environmental design awards. We are
proud of our reputation as a leader in design and construction
of habitat rehabilitation and compensation works. Examples of
projects that have won awards are presented below.
Columbia Bitulithic Ltd.
This project involved relocating a degraded 1.3 km section of
Pepin Creek. Our involvement included design of the new creek
channel, approval expedition, agency liaison and construction
supervision.

Awards Received
 Ministry of Energy and Mines and the Mining
Association of British Columbia Outstanding Reclamation
Achievement award for 1999 in the Sand and Gravel Category
 Aggregate Producers Association of British
Columbia Reclamation Commendation for 1999
 Abbotsford Chamber of Commerce monthly
environmental recognition award for October 1999
 Abbotsford Chamber of Commerce Annual
Environmental Recognition Award for 1999
 The project has also received positive
recognition from local government, Ministry tours, school tours
and stewardship groups

City of Abbotsford
This project included relocation of two tributaries to
Marshall Creek in order to consolidate an otherwise
undevelopable area for a transit garage. Our involvement
included design of the new creek channel, approval expedition,
agency liaison and construction supervision. This project
resulted in 6300 m2 of new fish habitat.

Award Received
 Federation of Canadian Municipalities-CH2M Hill
Sustainable Community Award for 2001 in the water category
 Inclusion in the FCM Municipal Governments and
Sustainable Communities: A Best Practices Guide
