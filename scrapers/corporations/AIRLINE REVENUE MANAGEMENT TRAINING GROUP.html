Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Revenue management training and consulting in the travel-related industry
A company dedicated to revenue management and pricing, our expertise comes from the practical experience gained in the daily application of revenue management and pricing principles, faced with the challenges and opportunities of companies operating on the many levels of service from global to regional, from traditional to low-fare, and also value-based product offerings.

We have established a team of highly qualified subject matter experts who have solid and practical experience. Our own training includes process innovation, project management, and six sigma.

We can assist you by creating long-term profitable solutions like revenue management services, coaching, on-going support, workshops, recurrent training, and sharing of best-demonstrated practices.

Success stories:

Our customers have engaged rmexpertise.com for advisory, training, or innovative solution design in our various areas of expertise. Some of these companies have benefited by training their entire staff at their own in-house courses custom designed and delivered to meet their training needs. Others have retained us to provide the leadership and facilitation of innovation system design to meet future business models.
