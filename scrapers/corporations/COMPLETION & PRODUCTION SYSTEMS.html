Company Profile


Alpine Oil Services Corporation is a Canadian-Based, Oil and Gas
Service, Product Engineering and OEM Original Equipment
Manufacturing Company, providing specialized technology to the
North American and International petroleum exploration,
drilling, workover/completion and production industries.
Alpine conducts leading edge engineering research and
development programs inhouse. The resulting OEM proprietary
products for the energy sector are then utilized within the
service divisions of the company, ALPINE TESTERS & TELEMETRY,
QUINN TESTERS, ALPINE WIRELINE, EXCELL LOGGING & PERFORATING,
ALPINE SUBSURFACE ELECTRONICS, ALPINE PRODUCTION TESTING &
UNDERBALANCED DRILLING, TECHCORP INDUSTRIES and ALPINE PRODUCT
SALES & SERVICES. With a proven track record, Onshore and
Offshore, Alpine is actively involved in the sale and rental of
equipment world-wide through authorized agents and marketing
distributors. In addition, Alpine also pursues transfer of
technology agreements and the licensing of its proprietary
design files. Alpine and/or subsidiaries are members in good
standing of the Petroleum Services Association of Canada
(PSAC)and the Canadian Association of Drilling Engineers (CADE).

Product Name:
Underbalanced Drilling Product Sales
Alpine Manufactures a complete line of Underbalanced Drilling
Recovery Systems and Surface / Downhole Flow Control Systems
including the Techcorp RPM System 1500, 3000 & 5000 models
Rotating Blow Out Preventers, Wireline Retrievable Drill Pipe
Float Valves and the Techcorp Underbalanced Drilling Deployment
Valve (UBD Valve). Alpine 1 Piece Underbalanced Drilling Combo
Drill Packs are the most compact underbalanced drilling systems
available. These modular systems package the Transfer Pump,
Sparging Pump, Solids Pump, Multi sided Choke Manifold and the
Dual Samplers into a single skid mounted package. These units
are compatible with any of the Alpine Combo P-Tanks and provide
the benefit of expedited rig-up and reduced transportation
requirements. Alpine 2 Piece Underbalanced Drilling Drill Packs
offer the operator all the same systems as the 1 Piece Combo
Packs, however the Pump system and Choke manifold / Sampling
systems are separated into two separate skid systems. This
allows for sizing of the choke manifold system to the specific
requirements of the operation. These systems also allow an
extended operator work space. Alpine 3 Piece, Ultra High Volume,
Underbalanced Drilling Drill Packs utilize a dual Separator / P-
Tank configuration. A large 500 bbl 45 ft x 8 ft p-tank is used
to provided high volume capabilities while a 150 bbl separator
provides all instrumentation and is packaged with the pump
system and sampling system. Alpine Combo P-Tanks have full 3
Phase Separation capabilities and can be used during both
underbalanced drilling operations or during production testing
operations. These systems can be either skid mounted or trailer
mounted and can be either horizontal or vertical type systems.
Alpine manufactures a variety of Combo Hydraulic Flare Stack /
Pipe Skids for underbalanced drilling systems, these units are
usually skid mounted with all piping, include auxiliary flare
lines and are equipped with electronic igniters. Alpine OSPACK
Real Time Flow Reporting Software integrates a custom designed
real time flow reporting system with a 25 channel data
acquisition system. This allows continual monitoring of all
critical functions of the underbalanced drilling operations and
provides real time feed back to the drilling engineer. Constant
production test data is developed while drilling. Alpine
Underbalanced Drilling Lease Office Command Centers, both skid
and trailer mounted, these mobile offices are each equipped with
a Real Time Daniel's Flow computer and with a 25 channel data
acquisition system feeding Alpine's custom designed OSDACK and
AMDAR software systems. A full line of high pressure Alpine
Fully Automated 3 Phase Test Separators for both domestic use
and export sales. These systems are fully tailored to meet
customer requirements. Alpine fabricates both gate valve and
plug valve choke manifold systems using third party valves and
chokes. Alpine manufactures both direct and indirect, steam and
gas fired line heaters for production testing. These systems can
be either skid or trailer mounted. AMDAR is Alpine's custom
designed, Windows based field mode production testing, data
logging and reporting software. This software package was
developed to allow fast, accurate data reporting during
production tests. The Alpine Fast Separator system is a very
compact high pressure Vortex separation system developed to
meet the future requirements in International and offshore
underbalanced drilling systems. This unit allows for higher flow
rate production without the need for huge P-Tank separation
systems. Alpine designs and manufactures custom designed early
production systems for export sales. These systems are designed
as modular systems to allow the flexibility of adding modules to
meet field production requirements.

system and sampling system. Alpine Combo P-Tanks have full 3
Phase Separation capabilities and can be used during both
underbalanced drilling operations or during production testing
operations. These systems can be either skid mounted or trailer
mounted and can be either horizontal or vertical type systems.

Pipe Skids for underbalanced drilling systems, these units are
usually skid mounted with all piping, include auxiliary flare
lines and are equipped with electronic igniters. Alpine OSPACK
Real Time Flow Reporting Software integrates a custom designed
real time flow reporting system with a 25 channel data
acquisition system. This allows continual monitoring of all
critical functions of the underbalanced drilling operations and
provides real time feed back to the drilling engineer. Constant
production test data is developed while drilling. Alpine
Underbalanced Drilling Lease Office Command Centers, both skid
and trailer mounted, these mobile offices are each equipped with
a Real Time Daniel's Flow computer and with a 25 channel data
acquisition system feeding Alpine's custom designed OSDACK and
AMDAR software systems. A full line of high pressure Alpine
Fully Automated 3 Phase Test Separators for both domestic use
and export sales. These systems are fully tailored to meet
customer requirements. Alpine fabricates both gate valve and
plug valve choke manifold systems using third party valves and
chokes. Alpine manufactures both direct and indirect, steam and
gas fired line heaters for production testing. These systems can
be either skid or trailer mounted. AMDAR is Alpine's custom
designed, Windows based field mode production testing, data
logging and reporting software. This software package was
developed to allow fast, accurate data reporting during
production tests. The Alpine Fast Separator system is a very
compact high pressure Vortex separation system developed to
meet the future requirements in International and offshore

systems. Alpine designs and manufactures custom designed early
production systems for export sales. These systems are designed
