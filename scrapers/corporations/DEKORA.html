Service Name:
Home Staging Services Vancouver and Calgary
Since our start in the spring of 2003, we've been the innovators and industry leaders of home staging in Canada. We've staged thousands of homes and work with individual home owners, investors, top realtors, builders and developers. And we've been the subject of the award-winning HGTV series, The Stagers, which aired for two seasons across Canada and the US.

We draw on the expertise of our diverse team of talented designers, stylists and other professionals to meet the unique needs of each client and property. We specialize in the enhancement of residential properties for sale, and we're committed to helping our clients maximize the selling price and speed the sale of their homes.

From space-challenged urban condos to palatial estate homes and everything in between, we work with every type of property. In our consultation process, we determine your unique needs and budget, and tailor the services to fit.

Using a proven approach, our staging experts evaluate each home inside and out, and recommend the strategies that will create the most engaging presentation ranging from lighting, furniture choice and placement, art and finishing touches to landscaping and exterior enhancements.

We draw on our extensive inventory of furniture and accessories in our warehouses, choosing from our traditional, transitional, modernist, contemporary and emerging trend collections and continually sourced new items, and using original gallery artworks to complete the picture.

We offer comprehensive staging services and can take care of everything needed to get a home ready to sell. Or if you prefer to do it yourself, we can provide the guidance you need. We can also coordinate almost every kind of service that may be needed to prepare your home for the market, from painting, repairs and maintenance to landscaping.
