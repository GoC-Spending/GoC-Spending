Company Profile


Jer-B-Syl inc. is a plastic baskets and trays manufacturer used for the retail of produces (fruits and vegetables). A new division named I Kkwit also manufactures ashtrays and urban furniture. Please read the following informations.

Good Day!





I Kkwit has now become much more than its 5000 ashtrays and the thousands of other products of incomparable strength and quality, sold in 10 countries to date. Without being pretentious, we are becoming THE standard of many small, medium and large cities and businesses in Canada and in the world. Now that we have proven ourselves, we are launching a full line of indoor and outdoor furniture and believe me the quality is just as unequalled.



Please allow me to send you therefore the all new virtual I Kkwit catalogue including price lists. Please note that all our products are available in 400 colours! And are made in Chambly, Quebec-Canada and, thus providing jobs to our people at home!

Please consult our enclosed catalogue. For more information visit our website www.ikkwit.com

BENCHES AND PIK-NIK , BISTRO AND TERRACE TABLES: Photos, price list and technical information (specs) of our 13 models of benches: single, double, single with backrest, double with backrest, picnic, bistro and terrace tables. See photos on http://www.ikkwit.com/Photos/Benches.aspx


BOLLARDS: Photos, price list and technical information (specs) of our 2 models of bollards (changeable if necessary). See photos on http://www.ikkwit.com/Photos/Bollards.aspx
The range of protective bollards came about out of necessity. Because of their shape, several customers already asked us if our ashtrays were bollards. So there you have it. In order to match my products, you now find a complete range of bollards to protect your public spaces. They can be installed without having to dig to anchor them. Available in 4 or 6 inches in diameter.



FENCES: You will see on our website that we have used the bollards to make a Fence by using stainless steel cables, the result is fantastic! See photos 18 to 28 on: http://www.ikkwit.com/Photos/Bollards.aspx


BICYCLE RACKS: RacK@BiKesMD: A new revolutionary bicycle rack made in Quebec!
(Name and trademark, designs, worldwide registration, patent pending).

I guarantee you that you will be impressed with the ingenuity of the unique and absolutely incredible design of our 8 models. We are increasing the amount of bicycles per linear foot and diminish their entanglement there where the whole world entangles scratches and breaks their bicycles by taking too much space for parking! Isnt that wonderful? No more tangled handlebars and broken cables! The support as well as the grounds is easy to maintain. Since it is raised, its concept and unique design allow fitting more bicycles in less space. Moreover, the part that holds the bicycles can be detachable, if we want, allowing for snow removal between the two poles. You should really see it!



Discover our range of bicycle rack model Andréa which will facilitate storage and parking of your bicycles on the floor. And the new model Vert-i-Kal* for storage on the wall. Photos on our web site by clicking on: http://www.ikkwit.com/Photos/Bicycleracks.aspx



* Model Vert-i-Kal: This is a wall mounted bicycle rack for indoor parking, where the bicycle is free standing! It can be put in series on the wall, on a single column and/or several in series on the same column like a Christmas tree. A pure ecstasy of design, the ease-of use when parking the bicycle, its efficiency and above all its a space saver.



SUPPORTS TO STACK EMPTY WINDSHIELD WASHER CONTAINERS: RacK@WasHMD


No more overflowing garbage bins, thanks to this innovative new product created by I Kkwit. The base is fixed to the ground and the pole is removed to be emptied in the recycling bin. The system is easy to handle and it locks to prevent theft. The 6 feet high pole contains 17 empty containers!! With its affordable price, you will want one per pump and one close to your entrance!



Contact us today to reserve yours as they fly quickly! (Not the empty containers because the RacK@WasHMD is designed to prevent them from flying away.)



Available in 400 colours, like all our products, they can be painted in the colours of your banner!

Another original and improved idea of I Kkwit!
