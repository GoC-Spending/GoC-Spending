Company Profile


HDC Human Development Consultants Ltd. develops customized
technical training programs and competency-based performance
management systems. The goal is to improve corporate, job, and
employee performance by optimizing the use of assets (including
people) and reducing the risk of incurring losses. These
programs ensure that employees can do their work safely and
effectively, and can make decisions in the company's best
interest.

Services  

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Self-instructional training courses/kits for purchase
These competency-based self-instructional courses/training kits cover a variety of areas including technical, safety, and loss control and can be purchased from HDC's web site at www.hdc.ca. The content can be accessed on computers or printed. The courses/kits are practical and useful because users and manufacturers have provided input as to the content of the courses/kits. These courses include self-instructional modules, job aids, and applicable self-tests, knowledge tests, exercises, and procedures.

Product Name:
The Exemplary Worker
HDC has published a series of seven books on The Exemplary Worker. All of the books focus on using thinking strategies to work safely, effectively, and efficiently, identify critical issues, and solve problems. Workers and supervisors refine their own thinking strategies and learn new ones. Improved job performance can contribute to increased job satisfaction and promotions.

Service Name:
Develop competency-based training programs
Most of these customized programs are targeted to industry and are designed to ensure employees can do their jobs safely and effectively. The goals are to improve corporate, job, and employee performance. For many organizations, improved performance results in increased profit.

Service Name:
Develop reference manuals
A systems approach is used to design and develop reference manuals so that the content is practical and useful.

Service Name:
Develop competency-based performance management systems
These customized competency-based performance management systems apply technical training strategies and often incorporate pay-and-progression and career development schemes. All of these systems are administered by the customer.

Service Name:
Technical Writing
Technical writing is one of the requirements of consultants to develop our customers' products. HDC has a comprehensive, internal training program for all consultants so that they can provide quality services to our customers. HDC's internal training profile for consultants can be viewed and downloaded from our web site at www.hdc.ab.ca.

Service Name:
SafeThink
This training course is for operators, tradespeople, and supervisors to use a structured critical thinking strategy to identify and predict hazardous situations to prevent illness and injury. For more information, click on the following URL:

http://www.safethink.ca
