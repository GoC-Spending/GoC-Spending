Company Profile


"We Entertain the World by Creating the Absolute Best Mascots! Period." is more than a mission statement, we live it every day. Street Characters is the leading supplier of durable, highly active mascot characters to Professional Sports (NFL, MLB, NHL, MLS), scores of Colleges and Universities, Minor League Sports, as well as Corporations and Community Groups.

Clients include: The San Jose Sharks (NHL), Nebraska Cornhuskers,
National Hockey League, Calgary Olympic Development Association,
Arizona Cardinals, Detroit Lions, Philadelphia
Eagles (NFL), Tampa Bay Devil Rays, Minnesota Twins (MLB)
Travelodge hotels. Based in Calgary, Alberta, Street Characters
was founded in 1986 by Glenn Street, co-creator of the NHL's
first mascot ``Harvey The Hound`` of the Calgary Flames. Glenn
also performed as Harvey for many years. Street Characters was
born out of Glenn's inability to obtain an adequate costume
supplier. Glenn's experience as a professional mascot performer
gives the company a Worldwide reputation in the production of
durable, functional ``User friendly`` costumes. Other services
include expert training, audition management, consulting, and
additional training aids such as administrators manuals, and
performer training videos.

For more information contact Aubrey Fishman, Director of Marketing
Toll free at 1-888-MASCOTS (1-888-627-2687) or (403) 250-8232 ext. 25 or
by fax at 403-250-3846.
