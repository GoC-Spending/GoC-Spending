Company Profile


DST is a firm of consulting engineers, scientists and contractors
specializing in environmental and civil engineering related
work.
Northern Ontario Business Award 1995, 2006, 2011.
Consulting Engineers of Ontario Award of Excellence for Environmentally Friendly Slope Stabilization, 2011
Consulting Engineers of Ontario Award of Excellence for Environmentally Friendly Ground Improvement Works, 2010
Member of Innovators Alliance, Ontario's leading growth firms.
Extensive experience in the forest, mining and railway / highway
transportation, energy and insurance sectors.
CIVIL AND GEOTECHNICAL ENGINEERING: dams, highways, ports, tailings dams, retaining walls, ground stabilization,
foundations, shoring, terrain studies, construction quality
assurance.
ENVIRONMENTAL SERVICES: planning and feasibility studies,
ecosystem studies, site assessments, remediation design,
risk assessments, waste utilization, site characterization, indoor air quality, site cleanups, permitting, industrial hygiene and occupational hygiene support
CONTRACTORS - FOUNDATIONS: micropiles, shoring, harbours,
underpinning, grouting, erosion control, soil nailing.
CONTRACTORS - REMEDIATION: contaminated soil and sediment
clean-up technologies, bioremediation, in situ remediation
technologies, barrier technologies, sustainability, waste reduction
CONTRACTORS - DRILLING: geotechnical and environmental,
piezo cone penetrometer, dilatometer, pressuremeter; trenchless technologies, directional drilling, horizontal drains, sewer and water services
CONTRACTORS - GROUND STABILIZATION: Soil Nail and Root Technology (SNART) for railway and highway embankments and landslide stabilization.
