Company Profile


Eng. and manufacturing laboratory blown film, cast film,
ribbon film lines. Production pelletizing equipment, extruders,
mixing equipment, consulting for plastics or plastics recycling
industry worldwide. Compounding equipment for plastics. Anti
sapstain spray and dip turnkey system for spraying lumber. Food
grade equipment mixing and blending.
