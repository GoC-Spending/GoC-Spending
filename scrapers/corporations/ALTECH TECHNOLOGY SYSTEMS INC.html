Company Profile


ALTECH Technology Systems Inc., as part of The ALTECH Group, is
an engineering design, manufacturing and installation company
providing pollution prevention, minimization and treatment
equipment to industry. We design and install proprietary
technology solutions for wastewater, hazardous waste management,
recycling systems and contaminated soil systems; conduct bench
scale treatability tests and design commercially available
treatment systems for industrial laundry, textile and dying
plants, food, meat, and dairy processing, chemical
manufacturing; pulp and paper manufacturing, petroleum refining,
electroplating operations, water recycling and management in the
photo processing industry; installation of fixed base thermal
desorber and/or soil washing for contaminated soils; sludge
drying and solidification system for waste management.

Because new technology systems require a high degree of
professional engineering and technical ability we maintain our
own research and development capability. With our manufacturing
space, we can run demonstrations to satisfy the customer. As
well, with other resources in The ALTECH Group, we can perform
technical studies to evaluate customer situations to ensure a
properly tailored solution.

MARKETING STRATEGY

Generally sales methods for our equipment are based on
maintaining environmental compliance and cost effective savings
when compared to the cost of alternatives. Priority markets
include, most important, the United States. Other priorities
include Mexico, Central America, South America, Europe, Asia,
India. We would be very interested in establishing joint
ventures and/or informing other kinds of strategic alliances
with foreign firms.

ALTECH Technology Systems Inc. designs, develops and
manufactures
technological solutions for environmental problems. We have
technology applications in the waste, water air and soil
remediation fields. With our research and development
capability, we are constantly evolving new applications.

ALTECH engineers and scientists provide innovative environmental
solutions identifying complex technical problems and developing
reliable, cost effective pollution abatement and/or recycling
alternatives. ALTECH delivers on the concepts of pollution
prevention and waste minimization are practical, efficient, and
cost effective, providing attractive paybacks. Applications
include:

Industrial Waste Water
- photo processing industry
- plating and metal finishing
- printing industry
- pulp and paper manufacturing
- chemical manufacturing
- petroleum refining
- food and/or meat processing
- paint and pigment industry
- dairy industry
- industrial and consumer adhesives
- industrial laundry

Waste Management
- hazardous waste separations, recycling, treatment and cleanups
- sludge separations, solidification and drying
- recycling technologies

Soil Remediation
- soil vapour extraction equipment
- pump and treat systems
- soil washing
- thermal desorption
- bioremediation

Air Management
- thermal oxidation units
- water scrubbing units

Product Name:
Environmental Products and Services
Product Name:
Pollution prevention and water recycling by using membrane technology effectively upstream in the production process.
Cost effective use of water requires innovation and a creative approach with a detailed understanding of the industry process under review. Coupled with accurate knowledge of the chemistry involved, a recycling system can be designed and tailored to the specific problems and objectives of the situation. Through ALTECH's 12 year history, we have gained extensive experience in the industrial sectors of food, dairy, beverage, wine/beer/spirits, cheese, meat processing, chemical/petrochemical, petroleum, photoprocessing, printing, industrial laundry, textiles and dying, plating and metal finishing, pulp and paper, paint and pigment, and industrial and consumer adhesives. This approach provides good business alternatives with reliable, operator friendly equipment.


ALTECH designs and manufactures package water recycling technologies consistent with pollution prevention and treating contaminant situations at the source. Treatment options concentrate on membrane technologies from reverse osmosis, nanofiltration, ultrafiltration, to microfiltration. ALTECH delivers a full service including water/wastewater characterization to fully understand the water volumes and quality to be recycled. Membrane equipment modules are easily expanded and installed. Equipment solutions come complete with automated controls, operations manuals and commissioning service, training, and maintenance and service support.
