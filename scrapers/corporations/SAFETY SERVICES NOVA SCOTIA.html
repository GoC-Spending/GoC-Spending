Company Profile


Safety Services NS has been in the forefront of safety for 60 years. Always recognized as experts in all areas of safety and especially all safety training, we now offer consulting and business services in:
- ergonomics
- quality management and ISO 9001
- OHS management with ISO 45001 and CAN/CSA Z1000
- project management for all types of projects
- workplace testing and evaluations for noise, indoor air quality and other contaminants
- chemical management, MSDS/SDS management and writing safety data sheets
- OHS policy and program development and implementation

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Training
Development and/or delivery of all types of training, specializing in safety training. We offer standard, pre-prepared courses plus custom courses designed specifically for your organization's needs. We can deliver courses at our facility or yours - or even over the Internet!

Service Name:
Project management
People don't plan to fail. More often, they fail to plan! Our project management experts can help you plan projects of all types properly and comprehensively, and even manage the implementation of the project.
Following the latest Project Management Book of Knowledge, we offer:
- initial planning, including initial budgets, scopes, work breakdown structures and Gantt charts
- project risk and issue management
- ongoing budget and scope management
- stakeholder analyses and relationship management.

We deliver projects on time, on budget and on quality.

Service Name:
Quality management
In today's world, ongoing success requires a comprehensive quality management system. Regardless of your organization's size, our quality experts can help you design and implement a quality management system that is fully compliant with the latest ISO 9001 standard.

Service Name:
Occupational health and safety management systems
Workplace safety is a critical part of working in today's business world. And as with all critical functions, occupational health and safety works best if it is supported by a comprehensive OH&S management system.

Working to the latest ISO 45001 and CAN/CSA Z1000 standards, we can work with you to design and implement an OH&S management system that will support your managers, your supervisors and your staff, and promote injury reduction across your organization.

Service Name:
WHMIS and chemical/MSDS/SDS management
The WHMIS laws changed in 2015. The change presents an opportunity for you to update the way you manage chemicals in your workplaces.

We offer a full range of WHMIS training, from the most basic forms to the most advanced. In addition, we will manage your MSDSs or SDSs for you, ensuring they are always up-to-date and available to your staff either on your own servers or on secure websites created just for your organization. For those of you who manufacture products either for in-house use or outside sale, we will even create detailed and comprehensive safety data sheets for you.

Service Name:
Ergonomics
Ergonomic injuries are the most common and most expensive of injuries. Our certified ergonomists offer a full range of services from customized training to workstation evaluations to the design of full-blown ergonomic programs.

Service Name:
Air testing / IAQ / Noise testing
We offer a full range of services to test for airborne contaminants such as organic vapours, lead and asbestos, indoor air quality and noise.

Service Name:
Asbestos testing and management plans
Our certified health and safety professionals have dealt with asbestos in almost all of its forms. We offer a full range of services from:
- sampling and analysis at certified laboratories
- staff training either for awareness or advanced "Working with asbestos"
- working with you develop Asbestos Management Plans that meet all legal requirements.
