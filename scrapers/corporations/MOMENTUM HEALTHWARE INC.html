Company Profile


Founded in 1995, Momentum Healthware specializes in the development, delivery, integration and support of seamless information technology software for the healthcare industry. Its solutions serve the information systems requirements of small to medium-sized hospitals, the continuum of long-term care facilities, and the home care sector in Canada; and the continuum of long-term care organizations in the U.S.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Convergence Platform
The Convergence Platform has been designed using Service Orientated Architecture (SOA) principles, where the business objects are independent or loosely coupled, and where business objects are separate from User Interface. Combined with extensive use of XML, Web Services, and SOAP (Simple Object Access Protocol) results in a system that is highly adaptable, configurable, and scalable.

Momentum has worked with large Government and Provincial agencies since our inception in 1995. Since that time, we have grown and nurtured these long-standing relationships. Today, Provincial and Government-led roll-outs account for the majority of our install-base. The success of these relationships and installations is a testament of Momentum’s ability to provide strategic and scalable solutions to meet the wide ranging needs within a complex National/Provincial-led health system.

The Momentum Convergence Platform was designed to accommodate the needs of the organization instead of the organization accommodating the needs of the software. The system comes with hundreds of configurable reference data options in addition to a large number of application workflow options. Organizations requiring custom development can be accommodated easily and all without code split - which can be very costly to an organization.

Product Name:
Assessment Management
Our Assessment Management System helps administer standardized assessment tools for the continuum of healthcare. We are a licensed interRAI vender, and offer the full interRAI assessment suite; we also accommodate and support any other comprehensive and configurable assessments that work with your existing system.

Given the flexibility of Momentum’s
Assessment Management System, we have
the ability to incorporate assessment tools
specific to the needs of your organization -
whether pre existing or new; these
client-specific assessments can be fully
integrated within our software platform,
and form an integral part of your clinical documentation.

Additionally, interRAI assessments, have been a core part
of all Momentum applications since our
inception in 1995. All currently published
interRAI instruments are available in the
Convergence Platform.

Product Name:
RTLS In Motion
The Momentum Real-Time Location System (RTLS) Platform improves operational performance by providing healthcare organizations with a single system capable of locating patients, residents, staff, equipment, and inventory via information derived through a variety of wireless locating technologies. Today, real-time location technologies consisting primarily of tags, sensors, and software provide the ability to locate people, assets, inventory, and specimens instantly and reliably. The true value, however, lies in the ability to go beyond basic locating of items. The Momentum RTLS Platform enables healthcare organizations to leverage location data to maximum benefit, from simple location of resources to true performance management. Whether deployed as a wireless nurse call and staff duress system and/or a full ED and OR workflow solution, the system allows healthcare organizations to leverage their real-time location infrastructure for maximum value and benefit. The software has been designed to provide a comprehensive solution from day one, while offering unlimited flexibility to expand the solution over time.
