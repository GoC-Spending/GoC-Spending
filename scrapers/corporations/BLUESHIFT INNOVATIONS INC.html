Technology profile

BlueShift Innovations Inc. was founded in 2003 on the first principles of customer-focused and growth-driven entrepreneurism by a small group of software developers with roots in Computing Science and Engineering. The founding members had a unique vision for developing customized and convergent IT products and services intended to aggressively leverage its customers' operations in today's Global economy. Its priorities are to provide Enterprise Support Solutions that significantly increase business efficiency and productivity of its Corporate and Independent clients.

Its headquarters are located in one of Canada's fastest growing business capitals Edmonton, Alberta. It is proudly known as the "Smart City" for its business-savvy leadership, high standard of living, cosmopolitan population with a work hard - play hard attitude, and world-renowned scientific achievements that have improved the quality of life around the world. In fact, the University of Alberta is now the hub for leading R&D in microelectronics with Canada's 1st National Institute for Nanotechnology (NINT).

MISSION

BlueShift Innovations Inc. takes care of the systems that take care of your business. Our primary mission is to develop IT solutions that help our customers achieve their business objectives. Your business is our business. We deliver the integration of web-based services and custom software applications for your business using the latest technologies in the market today. They are specifically designed to satisfy today's growing B2B (business-to-business) and B2C (business-to-customer) requirements as well as anticipate future needs. The expected results are rapid deployment, high performance, cost effective solutions that optimize operations, and increased sales revenue.

CONSULTING

BlueShift Innovations Inc. provides the technical perspective necessary for strategic planning, business process engineering and next generation systems architecture. Each of our clients receive personal one-on-one consultation intended to isolate business requirements and develop intelligent solutions. This involves prioritizing features with the customer's budget in mind.

SYSTEMS INTEGRATION

BlueShift Innovations Inc. provides software development, Internet solutions, and services. These products and services include integrating services and developing custom-designed solutions that meet our clients needs, while providing innovative project development using the latest technologies in the market today.

Internet services include website development, hosting solutions, and project management and development. It is important to note that the application of computer technologies is fundamental to the company, rather than developing the technologies themselves. These product technologies will be designed, developed, and maintained in the best interest of BlueShift Innovations Inc.

BlueShift Innovations Inc. provides a broad scope of IT products and services tailored specifically for its independent and corporate clients' business needs. They include but are not limited to the following:


building Standard and Enterprise Applications for
Corporate Enterprise Applications: B2B, B2C, and the new BlueShift Innovations Inc. standard known as business-to-patient, or B2P
Healthcare System
Educational System
Research Firms and Organizations
Industry Specific (customization and automation)


developing Web-based Services for
Application and Web-based hosting
Website development (design to implementation)
Customized web-based applications
Deployment and integration services
In a nutshell, BlueShift Innovations Inc. is an Application Service Provider, a Web Service Provider, and a customized software development centre.

BLUESHIFT INNOVATIONS TEAM

BlueShift Innovations Inc. consists of a highly aggressive and gifted team of computer professionals with Engineering degrees and Computer Science backgrounds. Our team brings a collection of technical expertise from some of industries foremost leaders including those in the microelectronics, telecommunications, power and utilities industry, oil and gas industry, medical research institutions, and biomedical engineering sectors.
