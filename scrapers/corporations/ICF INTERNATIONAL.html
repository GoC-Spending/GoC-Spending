Company Profile


As one of Canada's leading environment and energy management
consulting firms, Marbek develops solutions to the resource and
environmental challenges confronting private and public sector
organizations, in Canada and across 30 countries internationally.

Established in 1983, Marbek offers clients multi-disciplinary
expertise and experience. Our consulting services are innovative
and responsive to client needs; and are provided with an
uncompromising commitment to quality.

Marbek's clients include: federal, provincial and municipal
government departments; energy utilities and companies; industry
and industry associations; research agencies; development banks;
and non-governmental organizations.

Company Description
Established in 1983, Marbek Resource Consultants provides consulting services in the interrelated fields of energy and the environment. Marbek has contributed effectively to environmental and energy management goals through a diverse range of domestic and international assignments. The firm's principals have been involved since the early 1970s in the conceptualization, implementation and evaluation of a host of energy efficiency and environmental policy and program initiatives for all levels of government. Through more than a decade of utility and private sector experience in Canada and internationally, they have contributed extensively to many DSM planning and analytical techniques used today.
Marbek is managed by five principals, with support from a multi-disciplinary team of in-house professionals with expertise in a variety of fields including natural resource management, law, economics, engineering, environmental sciences, and computer science. They have also established relationships with a network of organizations and individuals with complementary skills. Marbek has a proven ability in building project teams that are specifically tailored to the needs of each project.

Product Name:
Environmental Products and Services
Product Name:
Services to help improve energy efficiency and find alternative energy sources
Marbek Resource Consultants can provide a full range of services to help improve energy efficiency and find alternative energy sources including:

Analysis of energy-environment linkages;
End-use load forecasting;
Energy audits and building simulation modelling;
Energy technology profiles and assessments;
Load monitoring and savings verification;
Product and building energy performance standards and labelling;
Technical and economic analysis of energy management options;
Profiles of residential, commercial, industrial and transportation energy use;
Developing country technology transfer and capacity building;

Marbek also provides policy analysis and program planning for alternative transportation fuels and advanced technology vehicles and general advice on energy policies and programs.

Product Name:
Consulting services on a wide range of approaches and tools for the design and implementation of EMS and sub-systems
Marbek Resource Consultants provides a full range of environmental management services. Our approach is patterned on the ISO 14000 model but focusses on practical implementation strategies and integration with existing business planning and information systems.

Services include:

Review of environmental aspects;
Design of responsibility and accountability mechanisms;
Design of strategic and business planning systems;
Development of action plans;
Conduct of baseline studies;
Development of environmental performance measures and indicators;
Risk assessment;
Emergency planning;
EMS audits;
EMS training;
Capacity building in developing countries.

Product Name:
Consulting services on a wide range of approaches and tools for the assessment of environmental and socio-economic impacts of programs
Marbek Resource Consultants provides a full range of environmental disciplines including engineering, economics, law, ecology and communications. Using this expertise, the firm develops impact maps to attribute outcomes to programs and evaluates in quantitative or qualitative terms the implications of those impacts on businesses, individuals and society as a whole.

Specific services include:

Development of evaluation plans and frameworks;
Methodology development;
On-going monitoring of impacts;
Ex-post and ex-ante cost benefit analysis;
Peer reviews Stakeholder interviews;
Economic évaluation R&D impact evaluation;
Developing country capacity building.

Product Name:
Consulting services on a wide range of approaches and tools for measuring organizational and societal progress on environmental issues
Marbek Resource Consultants provides a full range of environmental disciplines including engineering, economics, law, ecology and communications. Using this expertise, the firm conducts data gathering, prepares issue papers, background papers and discussion papers and studies to develop and implement environmental measurement frameworks.

Services include:

Issue analysis (including science and technology assessments);
Development of indicator frameworks;
Identification of environmental aspects;
Identification of indicators and metrics;
Design of information collection and management systems;
Analysis of decision-making incentives;
Cost estimates Development of implementation plans;
Development of accounting systems.

Service Name:
Life-Cycle Management

of life-cycle assessment methods for products and services.

Climate Change Analysis/Modelling

Service Name:
Environment and Energy Communications
Marbek provides environmental communication services that
include: product conceptualization; research and analysis;
writing of manuals, brochures, booklets, reports, research
and issue papers; translation of technical and semi-technical
concepts into non-technical language; editing; and liaison with
translators, designers, and printers.

Service Name:
Environmental Policy and Program Development
Marbek prepares strategies, business plans, and workplans and
assists with contracting and monitoring of environmental services
for public and private-sector clients. Specific services include:
issue analysis (including science and technology assessments);
analysis of alternatives to regulation; analysis of cost and
benefits; development and analysis of opportunities and barriers;
public and stakeholder consultations; drafting of legislation,
regulations and policy documents; program design and
implementation; preparation of contract documents and
administration of contracts.

Service Name:
Environmental Management Systems and Environmental Indicators
Marbek provides a full range of environmental management
services. Our approach is patterned on the ISO 14000 model but
focusses on practical implementation strategies and integration
with existing business planning and information systems. Services
include: review of environmental aspects; design of
responsibility and accountability mechanisms; design of strategic
and business planning systems; development of action plans;
conduct of baselines studies; development of environmental
performance measures and sustainable development indicators; risk
assessment; emergency
planning; EMS audits and training.

Service Name:
Environmental Assessment
Marbek develops, evaluates and implements environmental
assessment policies, programs, and guidelines. One
particular area of expertise in impact assessment is Strategic
Environmental Assessment (SEA) - EA for policy and programs.

Service Name:
International Sustainable Development
Marbek's international practice offers interdisciplinary services
to meet the sustainable development needs of developing
countries. Marbek implements long-term development assistance
projects with a focus on capacity development and productive
sector modernization. Aspects of this practice include:
institutional development planning and training; managing
technical and financial support programs; facilitating strategic
alliances between Canadian and developing country business;
carrying out environment and energy program monitoring and
evaluation; and undertaking technical, financial, economic and
market analysis of energy management and GHG reduction measures.
