Company Profile


Manufacturer offering integrated services such as welding, machining and mechanical assembly, servicing North America market in various industries: energy, petroleum and gas, iron & steel, hydroelectric, pulp & paper, and others where the equipment is critical in terms of complexity and intellectual property.
Certification: ASME Section VIII, Div I (U-Stamp), NB R-Stamp, ISO 9001, CWB W47-1 Div. 2 and Non-Destructive Testing-ONGC-Level II Certified inspectors
