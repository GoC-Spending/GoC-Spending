Company Profile


Cowan Dynamics, established in 1957 is headquartered in Montreal and is a provider of engineered solutions for fluid power, pneumatic and control applications for domestic and international markets.
The Company specializes designs and manufactures standard and special purpose hydraulic and pneumtaic actuators and process control pannels for a wide variety of industry sectors.



Head office and manufacturing: Montreal, Quebec
Sales office: Oakville, Ontario


REGISTERED TO ISO 9001-2000
REGISTERED UNDER THE CONTROLLED
GOODS PROGRAM (CGP)



PNEUMATICS AND FLUID POWER GROUP.
This division specializes in the design and manufacturing of high
quality pneumatic and hydraulic cylinders and control systems for industries such as: Pulp & Paper, Steel Mills, Aluminum smelters, Oil and gas and OEMs of heavy machinery.

The company's strength lays in its ability to work closely with
customers to develop solutions for particular fluid power and process control applications.
The engineering and sales staff draws from many years of
experience to develop practical and cost effective solutions.
