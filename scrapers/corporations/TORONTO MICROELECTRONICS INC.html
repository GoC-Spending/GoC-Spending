Company Profile


Toronto MicroElectronics Inc. (TME) is an industrial computer
design and manufacturing company, located in Toronto, Ontario,
Canada. The company is involved in custom product design, as well
as design and manufacturing of ISA, EISA and PCI based
passive-backplane industrial single board computers.

TME is an independent, privately held corporation, and is owner
managed. It was established in 1986 and incorporated in 1988. The
company began business in 1986. Products designed by TME include
Automatic Remote Tracking Systems, RF Remote Security Systems,
and Industrial Single Board Computers. The company currently
designs and manufactures their own line of passive-backplane
based industrial PC products, including ISA 386SX, 486DX2-66,
486DX4-100 EISA 486SX, EISA 486DX, ISA/PCI 486DX/DX4, ISA/PCI
Pentium (tm). All TME products are designed in-house by TME, and
are manufactured under TME supervision in North America.
Manufacturing of the product is supervised by TME at a
subcontractor facility in Toronto, Canada.

TME leads the Industrial Single Board Computer market by being
the first to market with products such as 386SX product with
integral FLASH Solid State Drive, 486 ISA Board with SCSI-2, and
Pentium PCI with and integrated SVGA CRT/Flat panel Interface
using the Intel Triton Chip set. More recently TME has produced
much smaller and more embeddable CPU cards for the purposes of
embedding in space critical applications.

TME products are in use around the world in such applications as
flight simulators, medical
test equipment, data acquisition systems, shop floor control,
ruggedized military systems, high-end portable workstations. and
telecommunications switching equipment to name a few.

TME specializes in sales to OEM customers. TME supports OEM
requirements with custom designs, custom modifications to
standard products, detailed engineering co-operation and OEM
level quality control procedures.

The marketing of TME products is through a network of
Distributors, Manufacturers Representatives and direct sales to
end-users, integrators, OEM's and large corporations. Aggressive
product promotion has included participation in trade shows such
as COMDEX Fall, CeBit and the Real Time shows.

Key Personnel include:
President and Engineering Manager - Quang Nguyen,
Vice-President of Sales and Marketing - Peter Nelson P.Eng.
Software Manager - Ralph Birt.

TME's Mission: TME provides high-quality, cost-effective,
innovative embedded computer solutions for the OEM customer. We
take pride in our commitment to technological leadership and
dependable service.

Dependable Technology: TME has once again combined a high
level of integration with up to date Micro-processor technology
for use in its PC Compatible Single-Board Computers. Utilizing
the 386SX and 486 SLC processor families, TME has created a cost
effective and space efficient solution for many embedded
applications. On-board peripheral support includes a 3 Mbyte
Flash disk with TME's unique Flash File system whose TSR uses
less than 1 Kbyte of Main memory. Combined with an on-board Flat
panel/CRT controller, the 2001 SBC provides an excellent platform
for mobile, Kiosk, intelligent terminal process control flight
simulator, Telecomm switching, ruggedized military and other
applications.

Dependable Support: TME is a Toronto based firm
specializing in embedded PC technology
since 1988. Using in-house technical support combined with a

Product Name:
C-MORE: Computer Monitors Optimized for Reduced Eyestrain
Features:
- 2 - 4 Display Monitors
- One monitor for Full motion video
- One monitor for information
- Up to 64 monitors can be connected to one computer
- Each monitor can display its own information
- Supports Windows 95, Windows NT, OS/2, X-Windows

- Supports resolution of 640 x 480 to 1208 x 1024
- Independent Graphic Display Memory for each Video Port

- Up to 4MBytes of Display Memory for each Video Port
- Supports ISA/PCI Bus
- Video Conferencing
- Education
- Video Wall
- Information Kiosk
- Desktop Publishing

Applications:
- Process Control
- Building Automation
- Financial & Stock Analysis

This exciting new product from TME provides the
workstation user with a low cost solution for the problem of
monitor squeeze. C-MORE is a multiple display interface product
allowing several monitors to be connected to a single workstation
under Windows NT, Windows 95, OS/2 or X-Windows. The user can
expand several windows to occupy multiple monitors. No longer
does the user have to juggle several applications on one screen.
With C-MORE a virtual window of up to 32K x 32K pixel resolution
is possible. The applications are limitless.

CAD engineers can view schematics and layouts
simultaneously. Financial analysts can easily compare predicted
results with actual results. These are only two of the many
applications for C-MORE. Eliminate
the eyestrain of trying to view multiple applications on one
monitor and get C-MORE!
