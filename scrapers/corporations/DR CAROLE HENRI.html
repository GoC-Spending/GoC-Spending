ENVRIONMENTAL HEALTH SAFETY CONSULTING

Dr Carole Henri raises Environmental Health and Safety, quality for the 21st Century.
- We have over 126 years of combined experience in Environmental Health Safety Consulting;
- We are the first company to introduce “Environmental Home Inspection” in Ontario
- We are also the first company to introduce “Cold Flash Sterilization” in Ontario and Canada
- We are also the first company to introduce “Infrared and Thermo Imaging” to the education for “First Nation/Metis of Ontario and Quebec”

Dr Carole Henri’s Vision is to advance the performance, environmental, health and safe of international workplaces and obtain exemplary partnerships. Dr Carole Henri facilitates corporate leaders/ stakeholders with an Accredited Environment Health Safety Professional to ensure due diligence is served. Heightening attention in these regards will increase proficiency, securing a competitive economy and improve capital investments within the all Construction, Institutional, Educational, Industrial, Hospitality, Food, Manufacturing, and Mining sector. Vigilance to this fundamental business function provides widespread benefits to all concerned.

Success stories:

On the project site at Lear Corp Windsor Ontario Plant, MOL had put a work stoppage on the gate before the Demolition contractors and sub-contractors had even arrived.

One of the workers had seen my work performance and had past my name along to them. After receiving a phone call from all the contractors involved, I had a demolition meeting with all the owners.

I explained my call to MOL and stated the reasons and then the contractors stated their story, hence after meeting with the MOL on site, within minutes not only was the work stoppage had been lifted but I had reclassified the intensity of the work involved.

Between all the contractors I managed to save them over a total of millions of dollards

Time and time and again I have saved companies stress, work stoppage but most of all I have saved their profit margins
