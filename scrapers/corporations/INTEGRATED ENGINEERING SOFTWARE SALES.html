Company Profile


Integrated Engineering Software is a research and development
corporation specializing in the development and marketing of
Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) 2D/3D Field Analysis software for electromagnetic, microwave, particle trajectory, thermal, and structrual design.

Integrateds programs are recognized as one of the most accurate and advanced algorithms available. Our software has been proven to reduce pre-design, prototyping, and product maintenance costs, as well as improve time-to-market, productivity, and product quality.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
COULOMB
COULOMB: Is an advanced 3-dimensional electrostatic field solver
using the BEM, which offers unprecedented opportunities for
design efficiency and reliability. It is typically used used by
customers who specialize in the design and analysis of equipment
and components such as: insulators, bushings, grounding
electrodes; voltage gradients around high voltage shields, power
transmission lines and telecommunication cables.

Product Name:
ELECTRO
ELECTRO: Is a fully integrated 2D/RS Computer-Aided Engineering
package that is based on the Boundary Element Method (BEM). The
BEM helps to provide smooth, reliable results and accurate
solutions to complex problems. ELECTRO is typically used by
customers who specialize in the design and analysis of electrical
equipment and components such as: insulators, bushings, grounding electrodes; voltage gradients around high voltage shields; power transmission lines; telecommunication cables; microstrip and
integrated circuits; and parts and assemblies subject to electrical fields.



Product Name:
MAGNETO
MAGNETO: Is designed to quickly steamline the problem solving
process. It is a fully integrated 2D/RS Computer Aided
Engineering software package. MAGNETO is typically used by customers who specialize in the design and analysis of magnetic equipment and components such as: permanent magnet assemblies; recording heads and reclosures; electric (AC/DC) motors; magnetic shielding; cyclotrons, solenoids and transformers; magnetizing fixtures; and circuit breakers.

Product Name:
AMPERES
AMPERES: A 3D magnetic feild solver for the design and
analysis of magnetic equipment and components, which incorporate
linear, non-linear, and permanent magnet materials including:
Circuit breakers, recording heads and reclosures, electric AC/DC
motors, magnetic shielding, cyclotrons, solenoids, transformers,
permanent magnet assemblies, sensors and magnetic
instrumentation.

Product Name:
OERSTED
OERSTED: A 2D/RS time-harmonic eddy current feild solver for
magnetic design problems that require large open field analysis,
exact modeling of boundaries and in applications where dealing
with small skin depths are critical. Oersted is typically used for the design and analysis of electric/electronic equipment and components such as: MRI, non-destructive testing systems, bus bars, charging fixtures, induction heating coils, magnetic recording heads, magnetic shielding, induction motors, coils and transformers.

Product Name:
CASPOC
CASPOC: A system simulator designed for the simulation of power
electronics and electrical drives. It is used in the design and
simulation of complex power and control devices and systems such
as: rectifiers, DC/AC convertors, resonant convertors, power
engineering, induction machines, vector controls, mechanics,
active filters, harmonics, DC machines and stepper motors.

Product Name:
FARADAY
FARADAY: A 3D time-harmonic eddy current field solver for
applications requiring large open region analysis, exact
modeling of boundaries and problems where dealing with small
skin depths are critical. Faraday is used in the design and
analysis of magnetic equipment and components such as: MRI, non-
destructive testing systems, bus bars, charging fixtures,
induction heating coils, induction motors, magnetic recording
heads, magnetic shielding, coils and transformers.

Product Name:
LORENTZ
LORENTZ: A 2D/3D electromagnetic analysis software tool for
applications requiring design and analysis of charged particles
that incorporate electrostatic and magnetostatic components such
as: CRT and X-ray tubes, high energy particles in nuclear
applications, cyclotrons, klystrons, accelerators, ion
implantation, electron lithography, disk sputtering, electron
guns, mass spectrometers, magnetrons and high voltage corona
effects.

Product Name:
SINGULA
SINGULA: A 3D high-frequency electromagnetic analysis software
for components including: wire antennas, surface antennas,
dielectric antennas, EMC/EMI interactions, various current
sources and electromagnetic effects on human bodies.
