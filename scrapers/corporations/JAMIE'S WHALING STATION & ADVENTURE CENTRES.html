Company Profile


Started in 1982, Jamie's Whaling Station provides Whale
Watching, Bear Watching, Sea Lion Tours, Hot Springs Cove tours,
Kayaking tours, Harbour and specialty tours, scenic flights and more. Whale Watch tours are provided on one of four 29 foot Rigid Hull Inflatables and two 65 foot sight seeing vessels. Our larger
vessels are Coast Guard Certified for 46 and 49 passengers each,
and include such amenities as heated indoor and outdoor seating,
washroom, snack and liquor bars. In addition to Whale
Watching, we operate Hot Springs Cove Day tours on our 45 foot
heated cabin cruiser. This vessel also has a washroom, complete
compliment of safety equipment, and is Coast Guard certified for
12 passengers. We also offer harbour and specialty tours, hiking tours, kayaking and scenic flights. All of our vessels leave from Jamie's Whaling Wharf in Tofino or our Ucluelet Station. We have free parking for over 100 vehicles, including Motor Homes and Tour buses. Jamie's Whaling Station Gift Shops offer an eclectic collection of Gifts and Souvenirs.We also have an office in Ucluelet at the other end of Esowista Penninsula, offering whale watching and other tours in Barkley Sounds on either our 65 foot 'Lady Selkirk' or a 29 foot Rigid Hull Inflatable (RHIB)

We are members of Tourism Vancouver Island, Tourism Victoria,
Tourism Nanaimo, Tourism Vancouver, as well as many other
Chambers of Commerce including Tofino-Long Beach.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Whale Watching from Tofino
Canada's original Gray Whale Tour Company, Jamie's Whaling
Station and Adventure Centres offer quality educational and fun
tours in Clayoquot Sound from Tofino, and the Broken Group
Islands and Barkley Sound out of Ucluelet. Whale Watch from the
West Coasts only large, certified, 65 foot (20 meter), 49
passenger vessels, Lady Selkirk or Leviathan II, or aboard one of our 4 purpose-built, 29 foot (9 meter), 12 pax Rigid Hull Inflatables (zodiacs).
Bear Watching, Sea Lion Tours, Hot Springs Adventures, Harbour
and Specialty Tours, Meares Island Hiking, Guided
Kayak paddles, Scenic flights  you want adventure  we have it. We guarantee Whale, Bear and Sea Lion Sightings March 1 through October! Since 1982, top wholesalers and agents have been using Jamies service as we use our own vessels and trained guides - no sublets - to ensure a consistently high quality product for their guests. We are the only company in town that owns its property and docks and offer a unique Gift Shop with over 100 FREE PARKING sites for motor homes and cars. We can accommodate groups up to 156.

Service Name:
Hot Spring Cove Boat Day Trip
Join us aboard our 35 foot Cabin Cruiser for a magical cruise through Clayoquot Sound to Hot Springs Cove. Once at Hot Springs, we will leave you there for three hours, plenty of time to walk the one mile boardwalk through the Ancient Rainforest to the Hot Springs themselves, soak for an hour and a half and meander back to the dock. You will need to bring a towel, bathing suit, lunch, and something to drink. You should also bring good walking shoes for rainforest trail, and water shoes for the Hot Springs. On our way back to Tofino, we will stop at the Gray Whales feeding grounds and do some Whale Watching, although the Whale watching on this tour is not guaranteed, due to weather and whale conditions.This tour operates March 1 - October 31 and is booked
through Jamie's Whaling Station in Tofino.

Service Name:
Kayaking Tours
Nature! As close up as you can get. Paddle over tidal pools
teeming with mussels, anemone, starfish, crab, and more. Lift
your heard to discover you're being watched by the bold, cold
staring eyes of a bald eagle. Blue herons, or perhaps an otter
or seal will become curious about you, plus many other species of
birds. Meares Island explores the ancient rainforest, 1000
years old. Tours are for all levels of paddlers from novice to
experienced. Please contact Jamie's Whaling Station for more
information.

Service Name:
Bear Watching Tofino
Join us as we cruise through Clayoquot Sounds inner waters in
search of the bashful Black Bear as they search the low tidal
beaches in search of their favourite delicacy, the small Rock
Crab. Tours depart Daily April 1 through October. Departure
times vary with Low Tide Schedules. Contact our office for
these departure times.

Service Name:
Whale Watch in Barkley Sound
Our office at 168 Fraser Lane, on the waterfront in downtown Ucluelet, invites you to experience the same quality tours in Barkley Sound as we have been providing since 1982 in Tofino. Whale Watching on the "lady Selkirk" a 65 foot Coast Guard Certified vessel that carries 46 passengers, or experience the thrill of one of our 29 foot State of the Art 'Rigid Hull Inflatables' (Zodiac -Style). Cruiser Suits for warmth and flotation provided on RHIB's.

Service Name:
Hot Springs Sea To Sky Tour
Join Jamie's for a Sea to Sky Hot Springs Adventure! Boat up to Hot Springs Cove and fly back by Float Plane, or Fly up then Boat back. Included is all the same great tour as our regular Hot Springs Boat Tour. Check with our office for departure times. Tour runs March 15 through October.

Service Name:
Lennard Light Roundabout year round tour
This tour will be on our 26 foot 'Lifeboat' style vessel and
guests will be suited up in our zodiac cruiser suits to deal
with winter weather.

Join us on a 2 hr. voyage to the rugged outer islands of
Tofino's harbour entrance. Navigating around Lennard Island, we
pass directly under it's famous light tower and into a beautiful
tidal lagoon where Harbour Seals reside. On our journey home,
we parallel the beaches back to Tofino and investigate the inner
islands and passages that define the harbour.
The spectacular view of Clayoquot, the village, sea life,
eagles, and their nests will provide excellent photo
opportunities.
During times of inclement weather, we will cruise to Lemmens
Inlet within Meares Island, which was declared an Aboriginal
Park in 1988. Surrounded by the Pristine Rainforest, we
checkout where Captain John Gray built Fort Defiance in 1791 and
learn how his actions there affected Canadian and American
history.
Being on a major migratory route, this trip also offers
excellent bird watching. Often a phenomenon of nature occurs
here where untold millions of Jellyfish congregate, truly an
amazing sight in the crystal clear water. Don't forget to bring
your camera.


Service Name:
Meares Island 'Big Tree Trail'
A short boat ride from Tofino deposits you on the shores of
Meares Island where you will be left on your own to wonder the
trail to the largest western Red Cedar in the world, in addition
to other wonders in this primal rain forest. You will be picked
up again in about two hours.

Service Name:
Bear Watching Ucluelet
Join us as we cruise through Barkley Sounds inner waters in
search of the bashful Black Bear as they search the low tidal
beaches in search of their favourite delicacy, the small Rock
Crab. Tours depart Daily June 15th - September 15th. Departure
times vary with Low Tide Schedules. Contact our office for
these departure times.

Technology profile

Our fleet of eight vessels consists of 2 65 foot, 47 and 49
passenger, Coast Guard Certified Passenger Vessels. These
vessels have on board washroom, snack and liquor bar, heated
indoor seating and three outdoor viewing decks. We also have
five 12 passenger, 29 foot Rigid Hull Iflatables (RHIB's), and a
12 passenger 35 foot cruiser. All 12 passenger vessels are also
Coast Guard Inspected. One of our 65 foot vessels,
the "Leviathan II" was specifically
designed for whale watching and features two Glass Bottom
viewing ports.
