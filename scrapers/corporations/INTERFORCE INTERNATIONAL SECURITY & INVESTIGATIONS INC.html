Company Profile


Interforce International Security & Investigations Inc. is a security guard and patrol service and private investigations provider providing security guard services, concierge security guard services, construction security guard services, and mobile security guards services in the greater Toronto area. Our private investigations division provides investigations services for corporations to combat WSIB fraud, embezzlement, insurance companies to combat insurance fraud, infidelity cases, and legal support for law firms. and We promote safer communities by implementing innovative technologies currently utilized in today's police and military forces to make our men and women faster, smarter, and more effective. If you are looking for civilian, data, property protection, or private investigator services for your business or community, trust Interforce International Security & Investigations as your top choice for protection and covert surveillance needs.

Not only is Interforce International the most innovative security guard and private investigations agency in North America, we are also cost effective. With the implementation of the HST in Ontario, security guard and private investigator services have become more unaffordable. Safety and justice is a necessity, not a privilege or a luxury, and we will work diligently with our clients to develop the perfect mix of innovate and cost effective security guard and investigations solutions to suit every budget.

Technology profile

Director of Operations Kien Hoang has announced that Interforce International (commonly known for its robust technological innovations) will offer biometric fingerprint identification to all of its clients in need of controlled access and quick identification. "This type of technology will likely appeal to situations where you have contractors signing in and out all the time or to act as a tenants database." says Luis Fuentes, Customer Relations Manager for the company. By scanning a person's thumb, the scanner immediately identifies the individual's name. A complete biography with comprehensive information including a photo of the individual is then accessed on the company's database computer. In situations where tenants are locked out of their apartment and do not have their identification on them, a biometric fingerprint scanner can be very useful in granting access for individuals without verifying traditional identification documents such as a drivers license or permanent resident card. Clients who wish to keep track of visitors and contractors entering and leaving the building will also benefit from our biometric fingerprint scanners. And for the "bad guys:" Repeat offenders are quickly identified without verifying identification, which gives the security guards on duty instant access to crucial information in relation to the offender's past violations and to prevent mistaken identity. In fact, one out of ten people questioned about their activity will lie about their identity. With the use of biometric scanners, margin for error is more then 1 in a million chances.

Interforce International is the currently the only law enforcement agency next to the Milwaukee Police Department to implement biometric fingerprint scanners in their operations to better aid our security guards for quick and positive identification purposes. For high risk assignments, Interforce International will provide every client with a portable hand held biometric fingerprint scanner.

Biometric fingerprint scanner is now available to all clients (with the exception of mobile) on a 2-3 year term with 24 hour coverage.

Unique applications:

We are the first security agency in North America to implement biometric scanning and facial recognition technology for identifying repeat offenders and for attendance keeping purposes who frequent the premises as well as residents who are locked out and are unable to identify themselves. The only law enforcement agency to implement biometric technology to aid their operations before Interforce International is the Milluwake Police Department.

We are the only security agency in Ontario that offers a hybrid of physical and remote security. What does this mean for you? It means that you can enjoy the physical presence of our highly trained security guards in combination with remote monitoring sensors that will be placed in key remote areas of concern on your property. That way, we can monitor your property from various angles which are not possible from traditional security. For example, a security guard makes a patrol in the parking garage. A thief who has been watching the security guard's movement has noticed that the security guard makes a patrol every two hours. Right after the security guard is done monitoring the garage, the thief commits the crime and is gone long before the security guard returns. But because Interforce International had placed a motion sensor/ infrared camera in the garage, that sensor would of paged the security guard immediately to return due to suspicious activity and the crime would of been thwarted.

Key / Major clients:

Although the lists of our clients is confidential, we have clients from warehouses, construction firms, school boards, and professional associations.

Success stories:

One of our clients who contracted Interforce International to secure their warehouses from theft have benefited from our perimeter security services which involves wireless infrared sensors to monitor the perimeter of the facility. Because of this, any intruder would be detected immediately and prosecuted.
