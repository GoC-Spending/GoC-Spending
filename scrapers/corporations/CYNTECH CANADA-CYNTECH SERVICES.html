Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Pipeline Anchor System
Cyntech Corporation designs and manufactures several types of
anchor systems which are used for stabilization and buoyancy
control of large diameter pipelines. Our pipeline anchor
systems may be used in both onshore and offshore applications.
Cyntech also provides installation equipment and site
supervision.

Product Name:
Helical Piles
Cyntech designs and manufactures helical piles for use as foundations for structures such as pipe supports, storage tanks, process equipment, buildings, electrical transmission towers, wind towers and solar projects,

Product Name:
Solar Support System
Cyntech designs, manufactures and installs single axis and dual axis solar support systems. Our products are engineered and manufactured in Canada for harsh weather conditions encountered in northern climates. Cyntech's system is a complete ground to panel system which is fully integrated with Cyntech's helical pile foundation system.

Service Name:
Storage Tank Repair
Cyntech Corporation provides a full range of repair services
related to petroleum storage tanks including:
- tank engineering & inspection
- tank lifting and levelling
- tank relocation
- repair or replacement of tank foundations
- repair or replacement of tank floors, roofs and shell sections
- installation or replacement of tank nozzles and appurtenances
- installation of release prevention barriers and leak detection
systems

International capital projects:

Corporate Expertise:
-----------------------
Cyntech Corporation is a leading manufacturer of pipeline
anchor systems for stabilization and buoyancy control of
large diameter pipelines. Cost savings of up to 80% can be
achieved by using Cyntech's anchor systems rather than the
traditional methods of pipeline buoyancy control. Where
required, we also provide installation equipment and
assistance.

Cyntech is actively pursuing international pipeline
projects in South America, Europe and the Far East.
