Company Profile


The Pathway to Sustainability  Flooring that Lasts Founded in 1989 on the principal of environmental stewardship, DINOFLEX® Group LP manufactures indoor and outdoor surfaces by using post-consumer ground rubber. DINOFLEX® helps to reduce the amount of tires piling up in our landfills and in the past 10 years alone, DINOFLEX® has consumed over 1.8 million recycled tires. As members of CaGBC and USGBC, DINOFLEX® products can contribute towards LEED credits for construction products. Our recycled rubber flooring products are in compliance with rigorous indoor air quality emissions requirements and meet the criteria to earn FloorScore® and CHPS certification. Our products are environmentally friendly, exceptionally durable, shock absorbent, slip resistant, stain resistant and fall well below the static propensity requirements giving DINOFLEX® flooring an edge within the flooring industry. The long lasting surfaces will keep their original luster for years with minimal maintenance. We work closely with architects and designers to create visual impacting colors for both our indoor and outdoor products: Evolution Floor Tiles, Stride Fitness Tiles, Sport Mat Flooring, PlayTiles®  Playground Tiles, NuVista® Exterior Tiles, and CushionWalk® PAVERS. Were proud to have our products used around the world in museums, libraries, schools, hospitals, offices, retail outlets, apartment complexes, ski resorts, arenas, recreational facilities and so much more! DINOFLEX®  Recycled Rubber Surfacing Specialists!

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Cushion Walk® Pavers
For use on decks, walkways, patios, rooftops, seniors facilities and more, this rubber tile manufactured by DINOFLEX® comes in interlocking and non-interlocking applications. The tile is made using a combination of recycled rubber and EPDM. The tiles can be made smooth or with a brick design pattern and combined with the benefits of being slip resistant, water permeable, noise reducing, spike resistant, stain resistant, impact absorbing, durable and low maintenance, make an attractive addition to any outdoor project.

Product Name:
Evolution Tile
DINOFLEX® offers a commercial flooring line of recycled rubber tiles called EVOLUTION. Available in an array of colours, the slip resistant, stain resistant, durable and noise reducing flooring is perfect for schools, offices, museums, libraries, retail spaces, seniors homes, health care facilities and more! With the choice of our non-glue interlocking, the tiles can be moved or flipped over in areas of high traffic, doubling the life of the floor. This product meets FloorScore® and CHPS certification and helps qualify a project for LEED credits.

Product Name:
Playground Safety Tiles
Since 1989 DINOFLEX® has been producing a playground safety tile that meets ASTM standards based on varying fall heights. Available in many colours, this tile is slip resistant, low maintenance, durable and impact resistant. It's a perfect solution for ensuring our children play on a surface that is both safe and fun!

Product Name:
Sport Mat Tile
Made using recycled rubber, this floor tile is used in high traffic areas including ski resorts, recreation facilities, offices, commercial applications, water treatment facilities, locker rooms, schools, ice arenas, pro shops and more! With several colours to choose from, these tiles can be applied using a glue down application, or with our interlocking method which allows the tiles to be moved and/or flipped over increasing the life span of the floor. DINOFLEX® submits its products to stringent third party testing to achieve FloorScore® and CHPS certification. The Sport Mat is a low maintenance product - it never needs to be stripped or waxed. It's stain resistant, slip resistant, sound absorbing, and contributes to LEED credits for projects.

Product Name:
NuVista® Tiles
DINOFLEX® continues to support the recycling process by regrinding its post manufacturing rubber materials through our ReGrind Program. The NuVista® Tile is one product that is made using a combination of this regrind and recycled rubber and EPDM. This outdoor tile is used on walkways, patios, decks, seniors facilities, apartments and more to increase the aesthetics and safety of the area. NuVista® requires minimal maintenance, is interlocking, durable, slip resistant, stain resistant and sound absorbing. The NuVista® tile comes in an assortment of colours and can help contribute in achieving LEED credits on projects.

Product Name:
Dinomats®
The Dinomat® is an extremely durable, tough, non-porous, non-skid mat that can withstand heavy industrial and commercial use. It will resist tearing, cutting, freezing, and reduces noise levels. Ice does not stick to it and it can be permanently laid on wood, metal, tile, and concrete surfaces.
The Dinomat® will enhance any ice arenas decor and is amongst the most durable rubber flooring products on the market today. The 100% recycled Dinomat® provides protection in the penalty and player boxes and other high pivot zones while lobbies and dressing rooms can be accented with our colored Sport Mat Flooring.
The Dinomat® also offers farm animals a healthy, natural environment. DINOMAT® protects the floors while decreasing vibrations and reducing noise. Whether by reducing bedding use, improving air quality, minimizing cleaning time or making your horses more comfortable, good quality flooring will pay for itself.

Product Name:
Stride Fitness Tiles
Designed for areas of higher impact within a work out facility, mainly weight lifting areas, Stride Fitness Tiles are slip resistant, sound absorbing, impact absorbing, low maintenance and stain resistant. They are FloorScore® and CHPS certified and can contribute to LEED credits for building projects.

Product Name:
Park Right Wheel Chocks
Used with vehicles, trailers and RV's to keep them from rolling, these 100% recycled rubber wheel chocks are weather resistant and skid resistant. They are designed to fit commercial and heavy use vehicles. They come standard with a poly rope handle.

Product Name:
Park Right Parking Curbs
These weather resistant, lightweight, 100% recycled rubber parking curbs can be adhered or bolt fastened to walls for vertical installation. the Park Right Parking Curbs can be installed by one person due to being 1/10th the weight of concrete. The curbs are resistant to UV, moisture, oil and temperature variations. They are maintenance free and flexible, allowing conformity to road surfaces. These curbs are used for municipal parking lots, public parking facilities, private parking facilities and indoor parking. They come in 3ft and 6ft sizes.

Product Name:
Park Right Tongue Chocks
Used for camper jacks, utility trailers, light equipment trailers, boats and rv jacks, the Park Right Tongue chocks are made of 100% recycled rubber. They are designed with a convenient poly rope handle, and a drain hole in the centre of each chock. The chocks are skid resistant and act like a brake. They help to prevent unlevel set ups and sinking into the ground.

Product Name:
Tree/Post Ring
DINOFLEX® Tree/Post Rings provide a permanent, clean appearance with a professional landscaped look to your yard and are virtually maintenance free. The Tree/Post Rings contain up to 90% of recycled rubber. They stay in place and won't scatter, blow away or decompose. They also protect the tree from mower and trimmer damage. Best of all, the Tree/Post Ring controls weed growth while allowing water and nutrients in, keeping your trees happy and healthy. Use the Tree/Post Rings for trees and shrubs, fence posts, fire hydrants, lamp posts, mailboxes, street signs and telephone poles.
