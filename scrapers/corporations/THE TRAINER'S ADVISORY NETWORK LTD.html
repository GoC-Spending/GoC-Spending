Company Profile

Our Certified Technicians can go to you and work with your technician to install the product or just go online to help your technician install the licenses to the server and workstations.

5 - GoldMine Admin Training (Customization)
Our Certified Trainer's will work with the Administrator of GoldMine to customize all aspects and secure the Database

6 - GoldMine User Training
Our Certified Training Consultants will work with the Administrator of GoldMine to plan what it is the users need to work successfully with GoldMine. A TRG (Trainer's Reference Guide) will be created to specifically meet the needs of each user or group within an organization. They will bring 12 years of CRM experience to their training.

Service Name:
7 - Online Training - Ongoing
With the use of www.gotomeeting.com, we now incorporate our distance learning into the ongoing training we provide to our clients. This is non-intrusive training that meets the needs of each caller.

Service Name:
8 - QuoteWerks for GoldMine
Our Technician and Trainers work with clients to ensure they have the ability to Quote their clients while having each quote interact with GoldMine. A match made in heavean for our current customers.

Service Name:
9 - MasterMine Software (Reports for Excel)
This unique product allows all data in GoldMine to be pulled into an Excel pivot table and managed through this process. The interesting function is that the product will also allow for revisions to GoldMine through the pivot table. Works incredibly well.
