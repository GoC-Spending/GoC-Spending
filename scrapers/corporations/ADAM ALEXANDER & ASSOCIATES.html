Company Profile


Adam Alexander & Associates is a professional Human Resources management and Executive Recruitment consulting practice offering specialized services, practical solutions and measurable results to clients in a variety of Human Resource Management areas, including Executive Search. Our clients include entrepreneurial businesses in software development, financial services, natural resources, manufacturing, retail, First Nations organizations and professional business services.
We also work extensively with associations and community service organizations. We are certified (CHRP) by the British Columbia Human Resources Management Association.

Adam Alexander & Associates combine a broad range of human resources management principles with several years of solid management and consulting experience to produce recommendations and optimum solutions that are practical and unique to the clients needs. Our services are designed to help meet the challenges faced in the competitive and rapidly changing business world. We take pride in developing solutions that work and giving personalized service to our clients.
Making the most effective use of the people in the organization will result in improved customer service, greater productivity, increased quality of work and a better bottom line.

Adam Alexander & Associates mission is to provide the highest quality service and results possible to meet our clients human resources.

Service Name:
Executive Recruitment
In todays competitive business environment new talent is often the key to an organizations success. Attracting the top talent to your organization is critical to your ongoing success. Your decisions can have critical short and long- term repercussions for your organization. Marketing the opportunity your organization has to offer is a time consuming process that requires extensive knowledge and experience.

Adam Alexander and Associates executive and professional search process will ensure that you make the right hiring decision  timely and cost effectively. We will ensure that there is an excellent fit between the organizations needs and those of the individuals you select. We will provide you with objective support and counsel throughout the search process. We will work closely with you throughout the assignment so you can be confident in the candidate you ultimately select. Adam Alexander & Associates utilizes a results orientated approach in addressing your executive search and recruitment needs.


Service Name:
Human Resources Advisory Services
The Human Resources Advisory service is designed to assist small to medium sized businesses identity and address organizational, management, employee and performance issues. Many organizations do not have the capacity of dedicated human resources management staff, or the time, knowledge and flexibility to consider human resources management activities as efficiently as they would desire; yet are being bogged down by internal issues. Managing human capital is important; however, it cannot be allowed to take your focus off the primary goal of addressing customer needs, building stronger customer relationships and the organizations bottom line.

Adam Alexander & Associates can provide your organization with the same professional expertise as the Human Resources departments in larger organizations, at a fraction of the cost.


Service Name:
Occupational Health & Safety Programs
Successful businesses realize that health and safety is more than a legal obligation. Workplace injuries and illness can be devastating for any business. When a worker suffers an injury or illness, there is a personal loss that cant be measured in dollars. For your business, that injury can mean the loss or absence of a valued employee with specialized knowledge and skills.

Consider the costs associated with a workplace injury:
Additional training of new employees or temporary help
Damage to property, equipment or product
Downtime for other workers
Valuable time to complete related paper work
Increased WCB premiums
Lower morale and productivity
Disruption in production scheduling
Prosecution and fines
Impact on the workers life
Protecting workers from injury and illness isnt just the law; its also the right thing to do. Informed employers realize that health and safety is good business, and that safety pays in more ways than one. A safe business is usually a successful business. Many businesses, large and small, have found that the quality of their product(s) improve with a proper safety program in place. Like quality, safety has to start at the top with management leadership and commitment. Developing and maintaining an effective OH&S program, with high standards of performance will result in identifying workplace hazards and reducing workplace injuries and illness.
