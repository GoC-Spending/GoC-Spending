Company Profile


BC Contacts Directory is a British Columbia, Canada business directory & company/contact/industry research software and database tool enabling you as a CASL (Canada Anti-Spam Legislation) compliant business developer to save time finding new business needs.

Find companies by industry, company name, street address, city, postal code, etc. Find contacts by industry, contact name, company name, title, email and phone number. Contact titles include Owner, Founder, President, VP, CEO, CFO, CIO, COO, CTO, Director of Marketing, Executive Director, General Manager, Marketing Manager, Sales Manager, etc.

What makes BC Contacts unique?
BC Contacts frees you from having to contact prospective clients or business partners at random so that you can instead concentrate on those predisposed to doing business with you.
