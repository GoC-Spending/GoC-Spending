Company Profile


Innotech was incorporated in 1992 exclusively for
the marketing of Embrace Air products. Test marketing was done
from 1989 to 92 when market demand deemed it necessary to focus
on Embrace Air products. Currently manufacture and assemble
the products at 83 Dufferin Street in Orillia.

Countries of export include the U.S.A., Hong Kong, Greece,
Lebanon, France & Great Britain. The products are recognized
by thousands of doctors, physical therapists, occupational
therapists, chiropractors & home care specialists.

Product Name:
In-Line Seating
In-Line Seating
The key to the relief and prevention of low back pain often
starts with proper seat support.

The In-Line Seating system has been designed with a rigid frame
and layered foam to cradle the user ad distribute weight evenly.
Countoured leg troughs and overflow edges reduce weight on bony
prominences. the rigid design prevents the buttocks from sinking
into soft seating, a situation that can result in back fatigue
and low back discomfort.
The In-Line Seating system can be used alone or can be attached
with velcro straps to the EmbraceAirPlus or EmbraceAirKing.
