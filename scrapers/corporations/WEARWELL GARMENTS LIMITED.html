Developing a custom designed, hassle-free Uniform Program and management for our customers is our main focus. WearWell currently offers a huge selection of styles that are manufactured right here in Canada. We can put together an excellent uniform or safety garment program from our existing line of corporate and service apparel. In addition, we partner with respected suppliers to ensure we can provide you with complete uniform coverage from head to toe.

If you need to start from scratch for your program or require specifically designed garments, we can do that for you too. The philosophy behind WearWell is to work with our customers to develop and manufacture the best possible uniform solution.

WearWell is pleased to bring you an exclusive line of high performance Flame Resistant apparel designed for work environments where risk of exposure to electric arc, flash fire and ferrous metal is present. Geliget Gear (Division of WearWell Garments Limited) offers superior protection, everyday comfort and incomparable value. But, most importantly, this work wear meets the standards for Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) and Flame Resistant (FR) clothing set out by the Canadian Standards Association for the electrical industry, the Canadian General Standards Board for the petrochemical industry and the European Union for ferrous metals.

Geliget Gear provides complete FR PPE wardrobe management services and the flexibility of stock, stock modification and custom FR apparel programs.
