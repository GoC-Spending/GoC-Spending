Company Profile


In 2009, OHM Medical's Mission was to supply and ensure AEDs are accessible to the public throughout Canada. As of 2014, like all companies, we have evolved.

"Ohm" in Merriam-Webster's Dictionary is defined as "a unit of measuring electrical resistance". In defibrillation, Ohm means "impedance". Therefore it is our mission to break down any resistance or impedance for those who want to learn. We break down any impedance to make sure our people get the best equipment in the market. Finally, we break down barriers so that our clients get the best high quality service everyone deserves. OHM Medical believes in sharing our knowledge throughout the world by providing Healthcare Education Resource Centres which are accessible to all. OHM Medical will go the extra mile to make sure those who want to learn will have access to it.

With the ultimate vision to help save lives, OHM Medical's vision is to instill the knowledge of early defibrillation, medical skills, and concepts to the general public and health care professionals, starting with healthcare professionals in British Columbia, then in Canada, and ultimately to the ends of the world.

As a Registered Training Affiliate with the Heart & Stroke Foundation of Canada (HSFC), International Trauma Life Support (ITLS), and Advanced HAZMAT Life Support (AHLS), OHM Medical specializes in teaching various courses created by these world class organizations. Our team of physicians, critical care nurses and paramedics bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the world of advanced life support education. Each instructor has an extensive background in providing critical care to pre-hospital and hospital settings.

Working with our Medical Director, we provide a customized Public Access Defibrillation (PAD) program which provides medical oversite/ directorship for any corporation or industry. Our PAD Program is the most comprehensive and functioning program in the market. Although, WORKSAFEBC does not require a medical directorship program in the workplace, our medical director is ready to be called on to review an incident when called upon.

Other services provided by OHM Medical are customized medical workshops and medical consultation.

OHM Medical is proud to be a member of the Richmond Chamber of Commerce. OHM Medical Training Services Inc. is also an Accredited Business with the Better Business Bureau of Canada.

Product Name:
Trucorp Airway Manikin
TruCorp have a professional commitment to medical best practice and patient wellbeing. TruCorp are taking medical training to new levels of realism through development of anatomically correct simulation systems.

Product Name:
Intubrite Laryngoscopes
Winner of the 2013 Journal of Emergency Medicine Services Hot Products Award, INTUBRITE laryngoscope blades and handles offers flexibility, durability, and portability at an affordable price. INTUBRITE manufactures its uniquely designed laryngoscope blades from surgical-grade, stainless steel. Unlike your typical fiber-optic or incandescent bulb, all laryngoscope blades are custom-fitted with a proprietary array of UV and White LED bulbs (black light and white light). This specific UV and LED wavelength is tuned to deliver precisely the correct illumination, providing a unique, anatomy-differentiating and glareless view.

Patented combination White/UV LED lighting produces optimized high definition viewing area, causing airway structures to be clearly visible and vocal cords to stand out. Reflectivity from fluids and tissue in airway is also dramatically reduced when compared to incandescent lighted blades, even fiber optics. Available in sizes MAC (0, 1, 2, 3, 3PLUS, 4), MILLER (00, 0, 1, 2, 3, 4) and PHILLIPS (1, 2)

Product Name:
Zoll Medical AED
ZOLL Medical Corporation, a leader in medical products and software solutions, helps responders manage, treat, and save lives in emergency rescues and in hospitals; outside the hospital while at work or home; in doctors' and dentists' offices and schools; in public places and on the battlefield. ZOLL's products contribute to managing patient care and saving lives, as well as increasing the efficiency of emergency medical, fire, and hospital operations around the globe. OHM Medical is a proud AED distributor for Zoll.

Service Name:
Heart and Stroke Foundation Training Centre
Registered Training Centre for the Heart and Stroke Foundation of Canada. OHM Medical Specializes in Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS), Advance Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) and Basic Life Support (BLS).

Service Name:
Advanced Hazmat Life Support
AHLS is offered in collaboration with the American Academy of Clinical Toxicology (AACT), and was developed by the Arizona Emergency Medicine Research Center (AEMRC), a Center of Excellence at The University of Arizona College of Medicine.

Each live course involves a board-certified toxicologist and physician and is taught by AHLS verified Instructors. The AHLS program also trains medical personnel to become AHLS Instructors who bring the course to their region.
