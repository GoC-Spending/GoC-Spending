Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Practicum Program
The Practicum Program offers practicums for students designed to gain professional experience in their area of expertise for university credit. Placements are tailored to university and candidates specific requirements taking into account specific interests. Placements are educationally focussed and are unpaid.

Service Name:
Hospitality Program
The Hospitality program offers youth between the ages of 19-30 to work and travel throughout Canada. Positions are in Canada's world famous hotel and resort communities. The program includes placement assistance, orientation, accommodation support, travel and access to our online community.

Service Name:
Ranchstay Program
The Ranchstay program provides youth between the ages of 19-30 the chance to live in rural areas of Canada in order to experience Canadian farm-life first hand for short stays of up to 6 weeks. Participants live with a host family and receive room and board during their entire stay.

Testimonial:

It is with pleasure to have worked with Internex and the numerous students we were able to place for a mutual beneficial arrangement. We are pleased that as soon as our company identified an intern your firm was able to find appropriate individuals who were interested and enthusiastic to work with us. As we look back over the years we can truly say that this program achieves its goals. - ScotiaMcleod

This is to thank you for providing our company, Underhill Geomatics Ltd., with unpaid yet highly motivated software engineering interns over the past year.
Our core business is providing land, topographic, and engineering surveys and geodata conversion services for local and international clients.  Since we've been doing this for nearly 100 years, we've amassed a huge number of topographic and legal survey plans.  Your interns have helped us build a fully working data management system, with great functionality and an excellent user interface, to help us manage those survey plans.
While not a mission-critical engineering tool, the system is of great benefit to our staff.  It is a great productivity tool as it can reduce the time spent searching for information on our survey plans from hours (searching paper and card records) to seconds (searching the database).
As you're probably aware, there are various soft benefits to the internships:
As current students of software engineering they bring to us fresh ideas and knowledge on computing technology.
Being in a geomatics engineering business environment they learn about the workings of another engineering discipline.
Foreign students returning home carry with them the goodwill and potential future international trade from a Canadian business.
Finally, we hope you can continue to provide us with similar high-quality interns in the months or years to come as we wish to expand our data management system to accommodate survey work orders, further improving our efficiency. - Underhill Geomatics

Im Anna from Australia. This testimonial is way overdue but I guess its not until now, a couple of months later that I can properly express how amazing my time in Canada was.
I lived in Vancouver for almost three months and it was unforgettable! I completed an internship with a sports museum, conducting research, collection management and helping set up exhibitions and events. The behind the scenes experience at the museum was invaluable and will hold me in good stead for a career in the heritage industry.
