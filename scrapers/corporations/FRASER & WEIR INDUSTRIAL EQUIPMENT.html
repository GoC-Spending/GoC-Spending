Company Profile


Fraser & Weir Industrial Equipment, a Canadian company based in Nova Scotia, is the Atlantic Canadian representative for national and inter-national manufacturers serving the industrial and commercial business sectors. Our mission is to provide the best possible service to our customers by effectively addressing their product needs through our own due diligence and by utilizing the professional resources available to us through the engineering and technical support groups provided by the manufacturers we represent.
Our manufacturers provide products ranging from tiny O Rings to 2000 HP Boilers; from electric motor controllers to huge seals and bearings for the propeller shafts of the largest sea going vessels afloat. Services are provided to industries as diverse as medical authorities, oil & gas and aerospace. Whatever our customers needs, Fraser & Weir Industrial Equipment is committed to providing the best possible service, whether it involves sourcing a unique product or arranging expert engineering assistance to resolve production or equipment design issues. The manufacturers we represent have the highest standards of quality and service, necessary to maintain maximum production levels with minimum downtime and the lowest possible maintenance costs.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Seals and Bearings
Trelleborg Sealing Solutions (Canada): For over 40 years, Trelleborg Sealing Solutions has provided complete sealing and bearing solutions for a broad variety of applications ranging from on deck machinery to diesel engines. Trelleborg has marine approvals which include Lloyds Register of Shipping, American Bureau of Shipping, Korean Register of shipping among others. Trelleborg also provides sealing solutions for the O & G industry from the smallest casing seals to the largest Drilling Riser Buoyancy Modules, as well as manufacturing solutions for non-standard customer requirements throughout the oil & gas markets.



Product Name:
World Wide Electric Motors
WWE is the largest importer of electric motors in the USA. They sell electric motors, Siemens motor controls, and shaft mount / worm gear reducers from an expansive national inventory to qualified dealers, distributors, and selected OEMs. All products come with a one year warranty or better and, as with our other manufactures, WWE provides excellent customer service to Fraser & Weir Industrial Equipment through their highly trained, highly experienced engineering and sales staff.



Product Name:
Amtech Drives, VFD Drives & Motor Soft Starters
Amtech Drives is in the Motion Control, Automation, Power Quality and Industrial Electronics business. Based in Doraville, GA, USA, Amtech provides complete motion control, automation & power quality system solutions as well as individual system components for the industrial motion control, automation & power quality market from applications engineering to hardware and software. For the oil and gas industry, Amtecs Adapt System for SRPs (Sucker Rod Pumps) offers the ultimate solution in SRP control. This unique system precisely estimates the Pump Off from Torque requirement of the unit by VFD.



Product Name:
Boilers, Blowdown Tanks, Condensate tanks
NovaTherm Boilers: A respected manufacturer in their own right of the Powerflex high pressure steam boiler and based in Longueuil, Quebec, NovaTherm is also associated with Victory Energy Operations, based in Collinsville, OK, USA, a manufacturer of large O and D Type watertube industrial boilers.



Product Name:
Infrared Explosion Proof Heaters, Heat tracing, Ovens
Scott Can Industries Ltd.
ScottCan manufactures 20 different sizes of THERMCAT catalytic gas infrared explosion proof heaters. Certified by appropriate authorities worldwide, these products are used extensively by the oil and gas industry to heat process equipment and instrumentation.
ScottCans AURORA heat tracing equipment is used in conjunction with the THERMCAT catalytic heaters to provide explosion proof heat tracing of pipes in hazardous areas.
ScottCan Also incorporates THERMCAT emitters into complete ovens for heating system kits. Supplied in conjunction with electronic controls and gas train, and with or without and enclosure, THERMCAT ovens provide a fast and economical solution in the finishing, leather tanning and glue drying industries.
ScottCans line of MOJAVE and SAHARA electric explosion proof heaters are used extensively in hazardous areas such as oil and gas drilling rigs and tar sands processing facilities.



Product Name:
WEG Electric Motors
In its industrial parks located in Brazil, WEG controls all production steps since the foundry works, stamping, up to enameling and packaging.
Initially manufacturing electric motors, WEG started to diversify during the 80's with the production of electronic components, industrial automation products, power and distribution transformers, liquid and powdered paints and insulating varnishes.
WEG supplies industrial electric motors, single phase motors, DC Motors, Low and High Voltage Motors.
WEG also supplies Variable Frequency Drives and Soft Starters

Product Name:
Large Pumps
Andritz / Ritz Pumps:
Supplies pumps for a variety of industries; water, oil & gas, pulp & paper, energy, hydro and others.
Supplier of single pumps or will design large pumping stations.
Single source supplier; development to model test; design to manufacturer; project management, site installation, service & training.
Four test loops in Austria & Switzerland where pumps and turbines are developed and tested.
www.andritz.com
