Company Profile


Alberta Tubular is a privately held company, founded in 1989, which supplies premium quality oil country tubular goods (OCTG), line pipe, polyethylene pipe and float equipment primarily to the oil and gas industry. As the largest, independent supplier of OCTG in Western Canada we are able to procure goods on the open market from our well-established supply network. Our manufacturing suppliers are strategically located across Canada and south of the border in Oklahoma providing us the depth required to compete internationally on price, volume and geographic location.

We are customer focused - customer driven to supply quality tubular products and services. Our customers have come to rely on our people and their extensive experience in the tubular industry. In working with each of our customers we are able to recommend and supply the appropriate products and services - whether for a major project or an ongoing supply of tubular products  and our industry connections allow us to provide our customers with materials beyond those we normally stock.

Our stocking points are strategically located throughout Alberta, British Columbia and Saskatchewan, Canada. Our shipping infrastructure provides quick, easy access to product and safe, expedient transport to any region in Canada and the US.

Alberta Tubular consistently delivers premium products and exceptional service to the oil & gas industry. We supply a full range of corrosion, insulation and mechanical coatings for line pipe and internal corrosion protective coatings for tubing. All our products are known in the industry for their high quality and consistent performance in the field and each is backed by our suppliers full guarantee and technical support.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
CASING - NEW
Alberta Tubular supplies casing in sizes 114.3mm (4 1/2") to 339.7mm (13 3/8") OD, ERW or seamless in grades J-55, K-55, N-80, H-40, L-80, Stel-80 and P-110. Our primary suppliers are Paragon Industries Inc., Lakeside Steel Corporation, Welded Tube of Canada and Evraz North America.

Product Name:
TUBING - NEW
Alberta Tubular supplies tubing in sizes 48.3mm (1.9") to 88.9mm (3.5") OD in grades J-55 and L-80. Internally coated tubing and specialty connections are also available.

Product Name:
TUBING - USED
Product Name:
FLOAT EQUIPMENT
Alberta Tubular supplies both Weatherford and TopCo float equipment from all of our stock yards.

Product Name:
LINE PIPE - STEEL
Alberta Tubular supplies ERW CSA Z245.1 line pipe. We carry inventories at both Shaw and Bayou coating facilities in sizes 60.3mm (2") through 323.9mm (12") grades 359 in a variety of wall thicknesses. We also have the ability to offer other grades, sizes larger than 12" as well as pipe manufactured to API or ASTM specifications on a project specific basis. Our primary suppliers for CSA material are Evraz North America, Evraz Camrose Works and TMK Ipsco.
