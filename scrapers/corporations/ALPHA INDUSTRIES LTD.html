Company Profile


Alpha Industries Ltd. represents numerous companies supplying specialty abrasion and corrosion resistant products, replacement parts, and liners for various applications to our customers in the oilsands, oil and gas, coal fired power, aggregate and cement, mining, and forestry industries. We also supply adhesives and sealant products for HVAC, transportation and other applications.
