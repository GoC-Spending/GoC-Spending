Company Profile


Exact Furniture is a manufacturer and distributor of high quality furniture products that are attractively designed and competitively priced. Our product line includes podiums, lecterns, Plasma TV stands, A/V carts, projector and multi-media carts for the videoconferencing, and A/V equipment for education and training markets. We also do custom designs and store fixture work, which includes custom mill work for store fixtures and educational markets.

For more details contact Jim Fulton at jimf@exactfurniture.com.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Furniture- Podiums, Lecterns, and TV stands
Our line of podiums, lecterns, and TV stands are made with and come in are standard or custom finishes. Built for durability, these stylish pieces are produced on the latest, state of the art equipment and assembled and finished with skilled labor, which results in a high quality fit and finish.

Product Name:
Multi-Media Cabinets
Our mobile cabinets are the perfect solution to house projectors, computers, DVD and VCR's. Some of our cabinets come with first surface mirrors that can be used to shorten projection distances to the screen.

Product Name:
A/V Racks
Our mobile A/V racks are available in various sizes and custom finishes. Some features include portable casters for easy mobility, adjustable/removable shelves, locking doors and more. Visit the Exact Furniture website for more details.

Product Name:
Credenza
Our durable and stylish credenza's allow you to store your electronic equipment. Options include shelving or rack rails, an overhanging top, ventilation grills and casters. These credenza's are suitable for any office or boardroom environment. For more options and details visit our website today.

Product Name:
Custom Millwork
Our custom millwork, made with melamine or laminate, is great for store fixtures, schools or offices.

Product Name:
Plasma Stands
Our plasma stands are available in various sizes for the office, classroom or meeting room. They come in our standard and custom finishes and provide a stylish and affordable accessory for your plasma display.

Product Name:
Wall units
We produce custom furniture wall units. These units are perfect for storing plasma/LCD TV's behind in a safe and secure manner.

Product Name:
Instructor Carts & Desks
Our Instructor carts and desks are portable and economical; great for the educational and office setting. With a variety of options, there is a great selection of instructor equipments that will suite your classroom/lecture hall or office environment. Visit the Exact Furniture site for more details.

Product Name:
Store Fixtures
We produce custom store fixtures for some of the largest retailers in Canada. Some of our clients include custom fixtures for Tim Hortons and Esso On-The-Run.
