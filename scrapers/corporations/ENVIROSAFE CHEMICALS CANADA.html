Company Profile


Envirosafe Chemicals Canada is an environmentally conscious
manufacturer of biodegradable products, microbial cultures for
hazardous waste, lagoons and manure degradation. Envirosafe Chemicals Canada Aersol Packaging, Private Labelling, specialty biodegradable chemicals for stripping, hydrocarbons and
paint removal. Biodegradable liquid Ice Melt with corrosion
protection. Envirosafe Chemicals Canada will work to improve industrial chemicals for a safer more environmentally sound future with the alternatives to toxic chemicals now being sold in the market place.


ENVIRONMENTALLY SOUND FORMULATIONS FOR YOUR CHEMICAL REQUIREMENTS
We are proud of our Superb Quality Environmental Products for Industry.
Our Manufacturing Plant is located in North Battleford, Saskatchewan,
Canada.

This message is from our president Mr. James R. (Jim) Davey.

It has been my mission to research and developed my own line of superb quality environmental products for industry. These uniquely designed products are now available to Corporate Companies to purchase and/or private label for resale within their own marketing programs.

The Specialty Chemical side of our business allows me to design (formulate), alter and manufacture new specialty chemical products for corporate entities to suit their needs in industry.

Envirosafe Chemicals Canada packaging department will package to suit your specifications.

We are pleased to offer a complete line of aerosol and bulk packaging.

Our company has a strong effective business base. We have worked with several companies Nationally and Internationally over the past fifteen years.

Strands, Northern Metallic, Regents (Easyway) Washington USA,
plus several other professional corporate giants.

We would be happy to service your needs. Call us today to formulate
a product for you or to purchase or private label one of our products.


Thank you, I look forward to doing business with you.
