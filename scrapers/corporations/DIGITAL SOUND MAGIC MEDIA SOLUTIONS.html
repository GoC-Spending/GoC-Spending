Company Profile


Digital Sound Magic Recording Studios Ltd. is a professional audio production, video production and digital media consulting service with over 20 years experience serving TV and film, corporate, and private clients around the world (Telus, Microsoft, NBC, Coca-Cola and more).

We operate out of Vancouver BC, Canada. (just blocks away from The Bridge Studios and Vancouver Film Studios).

Staffed with experienced recording engineers and producers, and armed with Source Connect and ISDN, clients from all over the world trust Digital Sound Magic for precise, high-quality recording, production, and unbeatable customer service.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Professional Audio Production
Our talented engineers are carefully chosen not just for their experience and technical skills, but also their incredible musicianship. And there are no egos here!

After 20 years in the biz, we've learned that the best performances come from providing the most comfortable and supportive atmosphere. We love working with all kinds of people - no matter what the project.

Whether you are recording a full band CD, film ADR, voice-overs, singer-songwriter productions, or even tape transfers and digital conversions, you will always get first-generation versions of your projects. The result? a clearer and cleaner sound on TV, radio, CD and film.

We can then master your CD with ISRC codes, proper track indexing, and furnish you with a master disc and track list printout. All you have to do send your masterpiece to the CD duplication plant.

Service Name:
Professional Video Production
Captivating videos that grab your audience's attention

Digital Sound Magic specializes in the development of powerful and creative, fully customized videos for your business. Always delivered in high definition, our video projects include:
Corporate messages
Not-for-profit videos
PSAs
Music videos
Industrial and training videos
Product demonstrations
Business profiles
Live event videography

We make life easy for you by offering complete in-house services, including script development, casting, filming, editing, music composition, adding stock footage, stock music, and motion graphics animation.

With the flexibility of filming at your office, our studio, or on location, we offer turn-key, "no-hassle" services that fit any budget.

World-Wide Webcasting

We supply fully produced webcasting and LiveStreaming services from anywhere on the globe.
Simply tell us your needs and we'll take care of the rest, including: crew supply, 1, 2, 3 or more live cameras with operators, all audio and live switching consoles for the professional hi-definition broadcast you deserve.
We can also archive all live video for later retrieval or DVD production.

Service Name:
Digital Media Consulting
Your Problem, Our Solution

Whatever the scope of your concern (whether looking to increase client interactivity online to remote webcasting via satellite data and video uplink), we can deliver tailored solutions and guide you along the path to success.
We offer digital media consulting services to private and public sector corporations world-wide.
Our responsibility to you is to help you make smarter, more-informed decisions that affect your company's efficiency and bottom line.

Introduce. Analyze. Report. Advise.

After more than 20 years of audio, video, and photographic production, we have the experience and the expertise to help you.
We don't offer esoteric or spurious ideas; what we offer is solid advice and proven methods and techniques that parallel the same high expectations you have on your company and yourself.

Service Name:
Voice Overs and Narration
We bring your message to broadcast quality life

If you need ADR, voice-overs, narration, demos, web audio and more, we can record and enhance your project with an all-inclusive, high quality production.
This includes studio production, recording, mixing, editing and providing professional voice talent from our exclusive roster.

Connect with your audience - with maximum impact.

After more than 20 years of recording voice-overs, ADR for films, commercials and animation, and narration for web videos, pod-casts, and radio ads, we have the experience and the expertise to help you stand out from the competition.
Couple this with our 30,000 sound effects and music library, and you have a completely professional recording that will engage listeners and create a lasting impression.
