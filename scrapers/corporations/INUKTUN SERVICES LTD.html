Company Profile


Inuktun Services Ltd. is a world leading designer and manufacturer of Modular Mobile Robotic Crawler Vehicles and Industrial Waterproof Video Cameras used for remote inspection of confined spaces and hazardous environments. Since 1989, Inuktun has built a reputation as the expert in remote controlled robotic transport, visual inspection equipment and instrumentation tooling. Reliability, efficiency, quality, and cost-effectiveness are emphasized in the design of all Inuktun products.

Technology profile

In addition to manufacturing and selling a complete line of standard equipment for a variety of remote robotic inspection applications, Inuktun also offers a complete range of services from engineering conceptual design through to project completion. Inuktun's highly qualified team of engineers, designers and machinists modify standard systems frequently to meet unique industry specific applications.

To ensure and maintain the companys high standard of quality, systems integration and testing are completed with in-house electronic and machine assembly staff. In addition, Inuktun regularly develops formal partnerships with other businesses and consultants to provide specialty engineering in accordance with design criteria. As a result, Inuktun is able to provide customers with innovative, high-quality equipment that is functionally reliable, easily operated, and cost-effective.

Unique applications:

All of Inuktuns systems are built on the foundation of modular design. Clients can select from a range of standard camera, crawler and modular controllers to create their own off-the-shelf, yet 'customized' solution.

Inuktuns systems can be used as a platform for the addition of manipulators, sensors, sonars, and any other desired products.

Not only are the product components modular, but the chassis have also been designed with flexible configurations allowing them to be used in multiple applications. All of the Versatrax systems can be easily converted from parallel to in-line configuration to fit within a variety of pipe sizes.

Key / Major clients:

Inuktun has an excellent track record in providing complex, custom, mobile robotic systems to clients such as NASA, the US Army Corps of Engineers, Ontario Power Generation (OPG), BC Hydro, GE Nuclear Energy, Hitachi, Beijing Municipal, Idaho National Laboratory (US Department of Energy), Atomic Energy Corporation of Canada, US Naval Research Lab, and many others.
