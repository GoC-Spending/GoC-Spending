Company Profile


With over 15 years experience in jack construction, three expansions in the last ten years, satisfied customer and loyal work partners, our belief is that we manufacture the best jacks in the industry. Shot, or tall, big or small, for every application, we build them all. Our jacks have a great reputation all over North America.

Produces 21 different jacks:
sidewind jacks
topwind jacks
custom-made jacks
trailer couplers.


Ironworks Inc. carries all parts, gears, bearing, bushing, threaded rod, nuts, pins, springs, handles, pushnuts, caps screws, and plates, for all model jacks.
