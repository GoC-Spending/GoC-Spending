Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Chinese consulting and manufacturing
Silk Ventures Group Ltd. is a leading specialist in doing
business within Mainland China. Our team of experts in Chinese
business aim to help your firm grow and expend your business by
successfully identifying in which aspect your firm could most
benefit from China.

We offer a full range of services, from initial market
consulting to full service product sourcing. Our comprehensive
scope of services aim to better get to know your firm so that we
can develop a sound understanding of your business, ensuring
your long term success both in China and abroad.
Our services are structured and priced in such a manner that we
are capable of accommodating any type of firm. From a start-up
SME to a large multinational, no matter the task we are equipped
to serve your needs.
Product Sourcing

Have a product that you want manufactured? Trying to cut your
existing manufacturing costs? Why not consider China as your
manufacturing center?

Silk Ventures Group Limited can help you take advantage of the
manufacturing facilities that exist in China.

Long Term production

If you have a product(s) that you would like to have
manufactured, and would like to cut your production costs, China
may be your best option. At Silk Ventures Group Limited we want
you to succeed and enjoy a long-term benefit from China. Our
full sourcing services, removes all your concerns about
international investment. We take full responsibility for
product design, production, assembly and overseas shipment
(based on your inventory management strategies).

We want to help you and we take the time to get to know who you
are. We want to maximize your investment in China. With that
in mind, our initial consultation with your managers, involves
getting to know as much about your business as we can. Once we
are comfortable working with your manager and understanding your
expectations, we then send our research team to the field. Our
feasibility studies identify all the local suppliers, facilities
and channels to maximize the quality of your final product.

Once we are confident that we have the best solution for your
manufacturing needs and you agree that these are ideal, we then
move to implement the project. Our team of experts will fully
run all aspects of your international endeavor, and in the end
the benefactor will be your organization as your expenses will
have dropped dramatically. Chinese sourcing helps free up your
factory space and reduces a number of burdens that your staff
would otherwise need to address. What&#8217;s more, using our
innovative solutions that eliminate fixed asset investment,
reduces your risk to a very low level. Our goal is to see your
firm&#8217;s long-term success in China.

Short term product Sourcing

Not looking for a long-term commitment? Only have some simple
one-time products that need to be produced? No Problem!

Our extensive networks of contacts of factory general managers,
ensures to you that we will identify the most suitable factory
for your outsourcing needs. Just tell us what product(s) you
require and the time frame you need them to be delivered. Upon
identifying the most suitable factory for your needs, we will
use our engineers to monitor and ensure that your products are
produced with the level of quality you expect.

We will work with your team to ensure a process management chain
that is not only cost effective, but is also delivered on a
timely basis. If you later require a more regular and on-going
long-term outsourcing commitment, we will develop a suitable
supply schedule via an international logistical strategy.
Our goal is to work with your firm to ensure that we cut your
manufacturing costs. No matter what are your expectations or
needs, we will give you our commitment of quality and excellent
service. We want to be your partner in China!

Market research

If you are looking for the most up-to date market trends or
require a very specific research project, Silk Ventures Group
(HK) Ltd. holds a team of experts that will give you the leading
market information that is exclusive to the needs of our
clients. Unlike other high priced competitors, our consultants
are more committed to getting the information that you need.
Our consulting reports provide detailed information at both the
micro and macro level.

We only recruit the most enthusiastic consultants who are
passionate about a career in Chinese market research. Our team
of young and experienced consultants thrive on new challenges
and welcome any information requests you may be looking for.
Typical available consulting projects include (but not limited
to):
1) Assessment of existing market trend
2) Performing competitor analysis
3) Market demand assessment
4) Determining existing competition and remaining available
market
5) Feasibility studies
6) Business development schedules
7) Business set-up, costs and opportunities
Advice and assistance on setting-up sales channel in China
Entering the Chinese market is often not a strait forward
business venture. Furthermore, numerous Chinese sectors require
you to have a domestically based joint venture partner if you
are looking to do business directly on the Mainland. Silk
Ventures Group Ltd. will work with your firm to ensure that we
set you up with the most reliable and suitable joint venture
partner in China. Selecting the wrong Chinese partner could
jeopardize the future of your firm both in China and at home.
If a market gap does exist for your product(s), we will help you
determine which market entry strategy would be most suitable for
your needs. We will help you:
1) Incorporate the firm (obtain necessary licenses)
2) Identification of potential business partner
3) Locate a suitable office to base the Chinese operations
4) Find a suitable distributor for the product both locally
and nationally
5) Set up a suitable national logistics strategy to ensure
that the product is distributed in a timely manner
6) Perform credit checks to ensure the credibility of the
Chinese buyers
7) Offer a suitable market entry strategy
8) Help to protect the client&#8217;s intellectual property
concerns
9) Identify business opportunities and current available
market share
10) Identify current market trends,
11) Existing problematic areas
12) Determine all costs associated with a Chinese
investment,
13) Potential return on investment
14) Action plan to be taken to enter the market.
