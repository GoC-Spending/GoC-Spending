Company Profile


Thomas Keeper consistently performs as one of Canada's top Realtors. He has been part of the top two real estate offices in the world, Re/max and Century 21. His stellar performance at Re/Max was recognized through inductions into the Re/Max Executive Club in 2004 and Re/Max Platinum Club in 2005. He was also named the "Top Producing Agent for the second half of 2005 according to the Real Estate News and became a member of Who's Who in Luxury Real Estate that same year.

Since starting TINK both his sales and exposure have increased dramatically. He is a member of the Board's Million Dollar Club and is among the top-selling realtors in Canada. He has been featured in such publications as Profiles of Success Magazine and he was also featured on an episode of HGTVs #1 Real Estate show, Buy Me.

He credits his success to his single-minded focus on giving his clients what they need and a business-like conduct that many respond to even during their first meeting. A thorough understanding of not only the inner workings of the market but also how to devise a marketing strategy to conform to its ever changing trends has also played a key role in his success. But it is his personal approach to clients that really wins them over.

What separates Thomas from the rest of the suit-and-tie crowd is his nuanced understanding of the industry his willingness to listen. Whatever your real estate needs, he has established a team of professionals that are the very best in their field. He credits this team, who with their stellar experience, assist him in related areas of each transaction. Thomas is recognized as a leader in the industry not only by his peers but most importantly, by his clients.
