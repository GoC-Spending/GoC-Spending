Company Profile


THE FRONTIER PROJECT MANAGEMENT DIFFERENCE

Frontier Project Management Ltd. is a privately owned Canadian engineering firm with a strong focus in Project Management. Since its inception in 1977, the company has grown steadily onto the worldwide stage from our roots in upstream, subsurface Oil & Gas disciplines and Civil & Construction. At Frontier Project Management Ltd. we understand the value that quality, time and costs mean to our customers. Our innovative solutions, quality control processes, efficiencies and ongoing improvements ensure that your objectives are continually being met. For your company, this translates into quality work, on time project management and efficient costing. Our strong entrepreneurial spirit drives us towards bringing the best solutions to each of your projects.

EXPERIENCE AND SUCCESS

For the past 35 years our solutions have been founded on a solid base of knowledge, experience and success. Frontier Project Management has the proven capability to deliver comprehensive and complete solutions  from great ideas to rewarding realities; whether its one well or a field development or a finished infrastructure.

QUALITY

Using Frontier Project Management means the quality is at a higher standard and is combined with more effective ways to deliver practical results. When you hire us, our quality assurance processes include: 1) in-depth research and learning about the particular operating area as well as its idiosyncrasies (e.g. hazards, potential operating difficulties and so on); 2) sound engineering and operational planning derived from our many years of experience; and, 3) a rigorous peer-review process for revealing areas that can be improved or may require greater attention; thereby avoiding critical and costly mistakes. The result is a quality process that accomplishes the outcome that you expect - without incident and regardless of complexities.

SAFETY

Our company Safety Program is recognized by the Certificate Of Recognition (COR) program governed by Alberta Employment, Immigration and Industry. We have been awarded this certification consecutively every year since 2003. Our commitment to our people, contractors, our customers people and all stakeholders ensures that we maintain healthy, safe and productive work environments.


OIL & GAS SERVICES

1. PRIME CONTRACTOR

Our interest and capability is to fulfill your unique requirements from planning and implementation to successful completion. Where necessary, we will adapt to changing environments in keeping to the objectives specified within the scope of work. As Prime Contractor, Frontier Project Management Ltd. maintains its own adequate insurance, safety manuals and Emergency Response Plan; an inclusive benefit for the
Operator.

Over the years we have developed great relationships within our network of government agencies, local and world class service companies. Our professionalism and reputation throughout this network is acknowledged and regarded for its ability to provide effective solutions. We have established an efficient administrative infrastructure for environmental, safety and financial reporting. We take the position of Prime Contractor seriously and accept full responsibility for addressing our customers objectives and meeting the requirements from all governing agencies, regulators and respective regulations.

2. PROJECT MANAGEMENT

The difference in rendering our services as Project Manager instead of Prime Contractor is that the full responsibility and liabilities related to all regulations remains with the Operator. We will provide planning, design, organization and coordination of the project in order to ensure that it meets the quality standards, costs and proposed timeframe within the clients established procedures, including safety and Emergency Response Plan.

3. UPSTREAM ENGINEERING

We have extensive knowledge and experience within national and international arenas for drilling, completions and workovers thereby allowing us to effectively design and implement your project  regardless of changing circumstances. We have abundant expertise in vertical, directional and horizontal technologies, multi-fracking, under-balanced, re-entry and multi-lateral projects.

4. FIELD OPTIMIZATION

Other services offered by Frontier Project Management Ltd. include oilfield evaluation and analysis for developing a sustainable and enduring exploitation plan. Our efforts yield incremental production, while extending the productive life of the reservoir. Frontier Project Management Ltd. delivers optimal solutions by selecting and utilizing the most effective, case-specific technologies; unbiased of brand.

5. WELL SITE MANAGEMENT

Our wellsite management services group utilizes only the best qualified personnel for planning, organizing and executing the program. Frontier Project Management Ltd. ensures that all of our personnel are current with industrys competency certifications. The cumulative decades of knowledge and experience held by our people ensures that your project is completed successfully.

CIVIL & CONSTRUCTION SERVICES

Frontier Project Management Ltd. supplies full time Construction Management services for all phases of your project. Frontier Project Management Ltd. is a member of the Calgary Construction Association. Our employees have been involved in the construction industry as general contractors and owner representatives. We have a collective experience of 75 years in construction.
We use a Construction Project Management System, eliminating the need for a General Contractor and resulting in a possible 10  12% reduction of the total construction budget. The involvement of the Construction Manager within the Design-Build team will ensure that quality standards are met to the owners requirements, as specified by the designer, and the execution of the project schedule is maintained.

Frontier Project Management Ltd. provides the following scope of services:

1. PRELIMINARY CONSTRUCTION & PROCUREMENT

We participate in the design for construction with the architectural team during the design phase to advise on efficient construct-ability and also with the scope and tender development, bid evaluations, budget formation, scheduling and writing contracts with sub-trades.

2. CONSTRUCTION

Frontier Project Management Ltd. acts as the Prime Contractor on behalf of the owner per Alberta OHS regulations to confirm that the owners specific safety procedures are followed, that a Site Specific Safety Management Plan is created and implemented, to conduct per-shift (daily) tailboard briefings to review safety matters. Frontier Project Management Ltd. will coordinate sub-contracted trades to ensure productivity and achieve the shortest possible overall project duration and minimize cross trade interference during construction execution. We set our focus also in monitoring project scheduled tasks and ensure the critical path tasks receive the highest priority. We effectively communicate project status with the owner and design team and also obtain, or advise on, necessary permitting.

3. PROJECT ACCOUNTABILITY

Frontier Project Management Ltd. will review invoices and authorize for payment, calculate progress payments and administer payables, track daily manpower, equipment resources and materials delivered to site. We also monitor change orders or directives.

4. POST CONSTRUCTION

Frontier Project Management Ltd. will administer deficiency list as well as warranty period, provide the project wrap-up and contract completion and build commissioning and hand-over.

INTERNATIONAL

Frontier Project Management has worked internationally since 1980, principally in the drilling and completion fields. Frontier Engineering has participated in projects in Malaysia, Vietnam, China, New Guinea, Australia, Pakistan, Greece, Jordan, Oman, Kenya, Botswana, Madagascar, Morocco, Bolivia, Ecuador, Venezuela, Colombia, Peru, Cuba and Trinidad.
