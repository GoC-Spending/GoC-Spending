Company Profile


Ecodyne Limited, one of the world's long-experienced water
technology companies, designs and manufactures water treating
equipment and cooling towers. Principal markets served by Ecodyne include the power generation, oil and gas, chemical, petrochemical, pulp and paper, mining and general manufacturing, as well as towns and municipalities. Combining a team of experienced engineers and project managers with financial strength through collaboration with affiliated companies, Ecodyne is able to undertake large projects and meet complex specifications. The codes and standards to which Ecodyne equipment is designed and constructed are ASME VIII, API,CSA Z299.3 & W59 and TAAPI. Ecodyne is also an ISO 9001 registered company.

Major export clients range from CSW/Phillips Cogen, Air Products, Exxon in the United States, Dow in The Netherlands, Marmara in Turkey, SADAF in Saudi Arabia, ADNOC in Abu Dhabi, Chittagong Urea Fertilizer Plant in Bangladesh, EGAT in Thailand, BP Kwinana in Australia, Methanex Chile Ltd. in Chile, and many more in over
30 countries.

COMPANY DESCRIPTION:


Established in 1958 and headquartered near Toronto, Canada, Ecodyne Limited is one of the world's long-experienced water technology companies, who designs and manufactures water treating equipment and cooling towers. The company is divided into two groups to serve the various markets: Ecodyne Water - water treating equipment; Ecodyne Cooling - cooling towers, upgrades and repairs. Ecodyne employs approximately 70 people in Canada and has sales representatives in Canada, the United States and United Kingdom.

A large team of experienced engineers and project managers enables Ecodyne to undertake large projects and meet complex specifications. Ecodyne's commissioning personnel start up or trouble shoot, at the client's request, the water and waste water treating plant and cooling towers and are able to resolve chemical, mechanical or electrical difficulties, including modification to PLC programs.

Ecodyne's drawings are prepared on AutoCAD 2007, CADworx Plant. An AutoCAD translator is available. Since control systems provided are PLC or DCS based Ecodyne has, in house, the capability to program the systems and creat graphics packages custom designed to our clients' individual needs.

Ecodyne's video conferencing equipment enables it to have timely, effective and comprehensive "face to face" communications with its customes and suppliers around the world.

Standards and Qualifications: Ecodyne meets the highest standards in all its operations. The codes and standards to which our equipment is designed and constructed are ISO 9001-2000, ASME VIII, API, C SA Z299.3 & W59 and TAPPI.

Technology Offered: Ecodyne is able to offer a full range of equipment either directly to a corporation or a consultant and welcomes the opportunity to discuss technology transfers, licensing and joint venture arrangements.

Financial Information: Sales are $30-$40 million per year, of which approximately one-half comes from export sales.

Geographic Marketing Activities: Ecodyne has exported equipment to more than 30 countries in the world i.e. United States, The Netherlands, Turkey, Saudi Arabia, Abu Dhabi, Bangladesh, Thailand, Australia, Chile, Malaysia.

Product Name:
Equipment for the treatment of water
Too numerous to list and describe, this company offers a wide array of modern treatment equipment, including:

Clarifiers: The Reactivator is designed for water clarification, removal of suspended solids and removal of organic colour. The equipment can also be used for cold lime softening of water. To increase capacity, Floc Barrier can be added.

Deaerating Heaters or Vacuum Deaerators: For the removal of oxygen and other dissolved gases from cold water systems.

Hot Process Softeners:To decrease hardness, or alkalinity, or to remove silica from water, at minimal cost.

Ion Exchange Equipment: Softeners, dealkalizers, demineralizers - each one designed to suit the purchaser's specific application.

Monoplants: Packaged water treatment systems incorporating coagulation, flocculation, and filtration to produce potable water.

Oil Coalescers: A unique oil coalescer treats oily waste streams, water from oil fields and contaminated condensate.

Specialized Ionizers: For uranium extraction, sugar deashing, etc.

Product Name:
Industrial process cooling towers.
Ecodyne is able to provide both counterflow and crossflow designs constructed of either wood or concrete incorporating the latest designs in air moving and heat transfer components including PVC film fills, wood and polypropylene splash fills.
