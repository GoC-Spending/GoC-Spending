Company Profile


ENSPEC a specialized environmental consulting firm located in southwestern Ontario. Our team has over 23 years of experience in the environmental sector as it relates to Designated Substances, Environmental Hygiene monitoring services and Indoor Air Quality investigations. We have sound technical skills for providing input into many different interdisciplinary projects.

ENSPEC staff has been involved with hazardous materials management at numerous industrial, commercial and residential settings throughout Ontario and has managed projects ranging from a few hundred dollars to over ten million dollars.

ENSPEC has a record of achieving project objectives in a safe, timely, and cost effective manner. We take a hands-on approach to everything we do to ensure successful project completion and have an unwavering commitment to safety. ENSPECs staff are experienced in dealing with workers and other stakeholders who may be affected by the hazardous materials management process, and are also sensitive to the issues involved in working in a management/union setting. The result is that you can be confident in the capability and veracity of ENSPEC to deal with any situation from a health, safety, financial and liability perspective.

The staff here at ENSPEC has extensive experience in developing and implementing comprehensive protocols and contractor specifications for a wide range of hazardous materials projects including Pre-Demolition and/or Pre-Construction surveys. Our successful track record has made ENSPEC a preferred technical expert in the environmental sector.

In addition to ENSPECs broad range of experience, we are recognized as a leading expert with issues associated with asbestos, designated substances and mould.

ENSPEC believes that every client, no matter how big or small, has the inherent right to receive the same quality services. This is our commitment to you.
