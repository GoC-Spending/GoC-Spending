Company Profile


Cemcorp Ltd. CONSULTING ENGINEERS are specialists in the design and construction of Food & Beverage Plants (Beer, Alcohol) and Cogeneration Plants. We are a multi-discipline team of engineers, constructors, financiers, covering all facets of business. An ISO 9001 registered firm.Services to the Process Industries, such as development of Business Plans, Feasibility Studies, to Project Management of complete production facilities.
PCbrew® and PCflow® are all Trademarks of Cemcorp Ltd. for PC-based computer control systems for the Process Technology industries.

Environmental Profile

CEMCORP was established in March, 1984, to provide process and equipment specialist consulting engineering services to the Industrial Process and Manufacturing fields, particularly the food processing, distillery, brewery and biotechnology chemical processing industries. Today the firm is an ISO 9001 registered, multi-discipline consortium of independent consulting engineers that provides, in addition to process and equipment design, a wide range of expertise including structural, electrical, controls systems, industrial Occupational Health & Safety and environmental and hazard control engineering to the process industry.


CEMCORP brings together on an "as required" basis a small group of highly qualified, internationally experienced professional consultants, engineers, manufacturers, contractors, and financiers. Because of the specialized proven technology, "know-how", experience and management skills of its retained team, CEMCORP can provide certain project services better than traditional sources of consulting engineers, "turnkey" manufacturers, and contractors. For projects ranging from translating single process performance requirements into detailed equipment specifications and drawings to capably managing a total capital project with an independent project management team, CEMCORP has the necessary design fabrication, manufacturing, erection and installation expertise together with current technology and people resources available.


CEMCORP specializes in translating process performance requirements into detailed equipment specifications and Computer-Assisted Design and Drafting (CADD) Services including shop fabrication drawings for users, consulting engineering firms, equipment manufacturers and contractors.


CEMCORP has completed over 400 Capital Projects worldwide where the detailed design and Project Management services were provided from Canada with the majority of the fabrication and construction obtained from local manufacturers and contractors.


Specialities available for Technology Transfer include: Fluids Technology Engineering Design (process, mechanical, electrical, chemical and utilities specifications); specialized processing equipment systems such as Heat exchangers to TEMA and ASME Unfired Pressure Vessel Codes, process piping systems, to 3A and ASME B 31 Pressure Piping Codes. For Breweries, Distilleries, and Food Processing plants, CEMCORP can provide Environmental and Effluent Treatment designs to recover alcohol from waste streams, along with the conversion of biomass to ethanol. Proprietary designs can be provided for Stem Jacketed kettles, reactors, mixers, vacuum pans, batch and continuous stills and evaporators, solvent recovery and extraction systems.

CEMCORP assists foreign manufacturers of Boilers, Pressure Vessels and Pressure fitting to obtain CRN for their imported equipment into Canada

Simultaneous saccharification, fermentation, and distillation technology for the conversion of biomass to refined ethanol.
A total turnkey design and system for Raw Material Separation and Cellulose Conversion using some of the raw material for enzyme production is implemented. Simultaneous Saccharification and Fermentation, Alcohol Recovery and Concentration is obtained through the continuous multi-column distillation of alcohol. Either a total turnkey system, or Technology Transfer can be provided from detailed process and equipment design to capably manage a total capital project with an independent project management team. Benefits are the conversion of disposal expenses into revenue earners.

Service Name:
CONSULTING ENGINEER - BUILDINGS-STRUCTURAL
Service Name:
Distillation and refining technology.
Alcohol that would otherwise be vented into the atmosphere or would require effluent treatment facilities can be recovered though a total turnkey design and system. This system both collects and refines waste beer through continuous multi-column distillation. The recovered alcohol can either be returned to the fermentation process for additional beer production or marketed as distilled neutral alcohol for blending with beverage spirits. By combining the alcohol recovery process with Immobilized Yeast, Simultaneous Saccharification and Fermentation Technology, total alcohol production can be increased. Either a total turnkey system or simply Technology Transfer can be provided, from detailed process and equipment design to capably managing a total capital project with an independent project management team.

Benefits of this process are the recovery of product to provide higher plant yield, increased revenues, and reduced effluent treatment and disposal costs.
