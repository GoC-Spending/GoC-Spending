Company Profile


Lifter is a Montreal Web Marketing Company serving Canadian companies with search engine optimization and pay per click management in both French and English. Our team of Montreal-based professional internet marketers will help you grow your business with bilingual search marketing services.

Lifter's dual expertise in SEO and PPC permits us to craft an online marketing strategy for our clients based on a combination of paid search marketing and organic search optimization. With PPC ads you can get immediate results in terms of new leads and new customers. The base of keyword data gleaned from research and experience will serve for both the paid search campaign and the search engine optimization process for your website.

Short term wins from Google Adwords and longer term results from organic search engine positioning are a winning combination for your search marketing strategy. Contact Lifter at 514-806-0402 to see what we can do for you. We are always pleased to offer a free Adwords audit or SEO assessment for your current online marketing campaigns and website.

Adwords PPC Management in Montreal
Lifter is a Montreal Adwords Management Company. Service Name:
Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Montreal
SEO Montreal Services from Lifter will enhance your web site's position in Google search results. Search engine optimization includes both on-site factors as well as external referencing and linking to build a quality SEO profile.

On-site actions for your site ranking on Google will include meta-data optimization to create a clear keyword focus for each of your primary site pages, as well as technical fixes to eliminate coding problems that may reduce your page rank or loading speed.

External factors include link-building and our external linking policy is to search out a small number of high-quality links that will definitely add to your web site's rank in the Google search algorithm. We entirely avoid any black hat techniques and any grey areas of SEO - as experience teaches us that short-cut methods will come back to hurt rankings in the long run.

Call us at 514-806-0402 to learn more about quality professional SEO services in Montreal, for your Canadian company. Thanks.
