Company Profile


M.S.M. Group happens to be the bridge connecting the employers and the employees for finding the right candidate on a specified job role. We have a huge database of diverse talents from which we pick the ones suiting the job role you provide. Picking the right candidate is no cake walk and we do it only after understanding the job role followed by checking with our database. In case we find the perfect fit, we refer him to the client or we recruit fresh talents to meet the demand. We have always managed to excel our clients' expectations in this regard.

We offer complete staffing solutions to our clients. Being your staffing partner we begin by sourcing the right people for your organization, training them when required, inducting them into your organization, monitoring their performance, evaluating their progress and conducting follow ups at regular intervals to ensure a successful placement.

We manage all the mandatory deductions from each paycheck dexterously. We also provide WSIB coverage and handle all administrative responsibilities in terms of human resource management. Staffing the right people for the right position at the right time is the key to success and we aid companies in achieving the same. Get in touch with us to know in details about our services.

The biggest advantage of choosing us as your partner is that we have a wide and diverse pool of talent ready to serve your business organization. Our talents will help in operating your business smoothly.

Service Name:
Employment Agency
M.S.M. Group is a placement agency based in Toronto. Specializing in skilled personnel and general labor staffing services, the group provides staff for full-time, part-time and temporary staff to the client companies. We believe that organizational success rests with the company's staff and hence right people must be placed in the right place. Thus, we serve as the link between employers and employees.

For Employers

Our team of dynamic, skilled and dedicated professionals is committed to provide you with the latest and most effective human resource solutions. Spotting the right candidates to fill in the vacant position quickly, reducing turn over, maximizing productivity and boosting company morale we do all for your organization.

We offer placements in various verticals which include IT, engineering, manufacturing, construction, accounting, transportation, mechanical and industrial sectors. We find suitably qualified candidates for positions which include the following:

§ Electricians
§ Baby Sitters & nannies
§ General labor
§ Receptionists
§ Office assistants
§ Accountants
§ IT specialists
§ Millwright
§ CNC operator
§ Skilled tradesmen
§ Machine operators
§ Truck drivers
§ Forklift operators
§ Mechanics
§
For Employees

M.S.M. Group knows and acknowledges the fact that finding the right job is not easy for job seekers. Moreover, one wrong move can ruin your career. Hence, we take you through a screening and interview process to analyze your strengths and weaknesses helping you to land the right job to grow professionally and fulfill your career goals. Enlisted below are our placement services:

1) Placing advertisements in renowned media
2) Proper screening and evaluation services
3)Formal presentation of offers from various organizations
3) Identifying determining job and talent fit
4) Guidance provided by our recruiters through the entire hiring process- from interview to placement.
