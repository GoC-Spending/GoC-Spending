Company Profile




The World's Leading Supplier of Essential Fatty Acids

Bioriginal delivers innovative essential fatty acid (EFA) solutions which are used around the world in nutraceuticals, functional foods, skin care products, cosmetics, animal feed, veterinary products and over-the-counter pharmaceuticals. Our distribution network spans six continents, with facilities in Canada, the United States, Europe and China.

Bioriginal provides EFAs including flax, evening primrose, borage (starflower), black currant, fish, hemp, echium, perilla and camelina as well as CLA. Delivery systems range from bulk oil and capsules, to finished, packaged products. In addition to the standard oil or seed formats, Bioriginal provides custom formulations, highly-concentrated EFA oils, water-soluble EFA powders, and a line of flax-based low net carb functional food products including BakOmega®, FibrOmega® and ProBioFlax.

Our vertical integration, strategic alliances, rigorous quality control and geographic presence contribute to our ability to guarantee a continuous supply of the cleanest, purest product on the market today. By incorporating research, sourcing, processing, marketing and distribution capabilities, we are able to manage the impact of many of the variables that determine success in todays competitive marketplace. Our focus is on developing strategic alliances and partnerships that help maintain our competitive edge and our lead position in the global marketplace.

Bioriginal was built on science, and continues to be a leader in EFA research. Our R&D team works closely with the scientific community, staying abreast of the latest research and participating in clinical trials. Our entire process actually begins in our growers fields, utilizing the knowledge and experience of our agronomists to ensure that only the best seeds advance into Bioriginals EFA products. Our specialized Bio-Elite® quality assurance process allows us to monitor production and quality to guarantee the safety, security and reliability of our products. Bioriginal has a sophisticated enterprise resource management system that ensures product traceability, and is registered with the FDA in compliance with the U.S. Bioterrorism Act.

Bioriginal was one the first companies in Canada to receive a site license from the Natural Health Products Directorate, certifying that our products are manufactured, packaged, and labeled according to GMP regulations. In addition to this certification, Bioriginal will continue to manufacture in compliance with the more stringent pharmaceutical-level GMPs. We offer organic products certified by Quality Assurance International (QAI), and endorsed as Kosher and Pareve. Bioriginal adheres to Proposition 65 standards, is certified by the USDA for organic production, ensures our fish oils are certified to be CRN compliant, and offers DSHEA compliant products. In addition, our oils are non-GMO and meet WHO Codex standards for edible oil safety. We test for pesticides and other contaminants and provide a Certificate of Analysis with every shipment. We have patents pending for proprietary manufacturing methods, as well as proprietary plant strains.

Bioriginal is the recipient of several awards recognizing business achievements and innovative approaches to product development. Bioriginal has also received the 2003 ABEX Business of the Year Award as well as the ABEX Export Award. The Saskatchewan Chamber of Commerces ABEX Awards honor outstanding achievements in business excellence. We were also named a Canadian Innovation Leader by the National Research Council in 2003, and presented with an ABEX Award for New Saskatchewan Product for the development of a new product process in 2002. In 1998, President and CEO Rick Kulow received the Ernst & Young Canadian National Entrepreneur of the Year Award for the Prairie Region, Agriculture and Food, and Bioriginal received an Ernst & Young Canadian National Entrepreneur of the Year Citation for Innovative Product Development that same year.

Our mission is to bring the benefits of EFAs to society, and we continue to seek long term, win-win relationships with companies sharing our vision. In addition to competitive pricing and superior products, we provide our customers with excellent technical, scientific and marketing support. Please visit our corporate website, www.bioriginal.com or our consumer website, www.fatsforhealth.com for more information about our company and products.
