Company Profile


Established in 1986 as Captain Copy Inc., Captain Printworks today is a privately held offset and digital printing company, employing 16 professionals and serving more than 2500 clients nationwide. With over 30 years of excellence, our clients range from small businesses to large, national and government institutes. We also provide printing and publishing services to copy centers, brokers and ad agencies.

Our printing shop not only provides digital, wide-format and offset printing services, but also, it has an engraving press in-house; one of a select few printers in Canada. We also have a multitude of letterpresses, with hot plate attachments that can hot foil stamp, emboss and die-cut. Having these equipments in-house gives us the control of the quality and also, hastens the timing of your printed material.

Service Name:
Letterpress
Letterpress printing is a method of printing where the text is indented into the printing surface, using a die; visually defining every character on the paper. It is a very popular techniques for business cards, invitations, postcards, books and many more.

Service Name:
Engraving Printing
Engraving printing can produce a very fine, raised three-dimensional detail on a printed piece; better than other printing methods. It is one of the most unique ways to put text and images on paper.

Use it to make a lasting impression; engraving adds elegance, distinctiveness and individuality to your business cards, engraved invitations, letterhead, fine arts, labels, gift cards, presentation folders or use it for security measures, such as: security cheques, latent images, micro text and designs, raised images and many more.

Service Name:
Large Format Printing
Large format printing can produce many type of prints, including posters, vinyl adhesives and banners, using a variety of textured papers. It uses water-based inks that is environmentally friendly and it is very durable with indoor and outdoor application.

Service Name:
Offset Printing
Offset printing, also known as offset lithography, uses a metal plate to transfer an inked impression onto the paper being printed, instead of directly being made on the paper. It allows for greater accuracy, consistency and exact color match, in comparison to digital printing.

We offer multiple press size options, on-site press approvals with color choices ranging from single to full color. Embossing, UV coating and Hot Foil Stamping are a few out of many finishing options available to cater to your vision, however challenging it may seem. Business cards, invitations, pamphlets, brochures, postcards, handouts, presentation folders are a few examples of items that can be offset printed.

Service Name:
Digital Printing
Digital printing is a method of printing from a digital-based image file, directly to a variety of medias. Digital printing eliminates the need for a plate which, is used in offset printing. Instead, it offers a faster printing service and printing-on-demand.

Digital printing services can be used for quick turn-around time for short-runs of business cards, postcards, pamphlets, brochures, labels, catalogues, magnets and many more.

Service Name:
Hot Foil Stamping
Hot Foil Stamping is a process that applies pigmented foil onto a solid surface using a heated plate. The contrast between the paper and the foil stamp will bring the finished piece to life, when the light is reflected on the foiled area. We have a wide range of foil colours, from glossy to matte, to metallic, marbled, vibrant, pearl holograms and clear foils.

We provide hot foil stamping services for letterheads, envelopes, business cards, invitations, labels, presentation folders, and much more.

Service Name:
Embossing & Debossing
It is an elegant process that refers to the impression of a design, decoration or lettering on a surface. A raised image is produced on paper by means of a die, and the application of pressure and heat. This is also commonly referred to as letterpress printing services.

Planning an emboss and deboss printing project starts at the design stage. The outcome will be decided by the choice of thickness of elements and their position in the overall layout. With over 2 decades of expertise, our staff will be glad to offer you free consultation on how to achieve the best effects.

Service Name:
Die Cutting
Die cutting is the process of using a die to trim paper, plastic and pressure-sensitive adhesives and foams into a desired shape.

Die-cutting printing draws attention to the 3D effect on paper and it adds character to it. It transforms a standard product from simple to stylish, once the design element is incorporated. Also, die-cutting printing service can add a decorative or a functional component to a print design, giving it a modern vintage-feel; make a thumb-hole for an envelope, slits for business card placement, or a notch for a folded piece.

Service Name:
Edge Painting
A simple yet sophisticated process of applying paint like colour to the edges of your finished products - be it business cards, wedding invitations, books, or journals.

Service Name:
Duplexing
Duplexing is a unique technique of layering together two or more sheets of paper into one thicker sheet for a increase impact. It produces a more premium, deluxe card that is high quality and thick.

You can use duplexing for coasters, wedding invitations, business cards, menus and more. It can also be combined with other finishing options such as die cut, hot foil, engraving, etc.



Service Name:
Perfect Binding
Perfect binding, also known as adhesive binding, is a method of bookbinding, where hot glue adhesive is used to attach the paper to the cover, at the spine. The finished item is quite durable, in comparison to other binding methods.

Perfect binding services are for making booklets, paper-back books, art books, portfolios, programs, magazines, directories, school yearbooks and manuals.

Service Name:
Lamination
Lamination is a material that has multiple layers, so that the composite material achieves improved strength, appearance and stability. Laminate is permanent and is applied by heat, pressure, or adhesives. Laminated printing services not only adds weight and texture to your printed materials, but also, it waterproofs for safekeeping.

Items can be laminated

- Business cards, invitation cards, greeting cards and postcards.
- Presentation folders, menus, pamphlets and brochure covers.
- Floor graphics; wall graphics, posters and outdoor signage.
- Photos, magazine pages, newspaper clips, photo ID cards.
- Promotional pieces and many more.

Service Name:
Crash Numbering
For all of your forms, tickets and other numbering projects, we handle letter numbering on both traditional Heidelberg Platen and Digital numbering
