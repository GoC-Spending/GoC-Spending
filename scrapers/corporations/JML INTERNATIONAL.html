Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
EEC Soil Stabilizer
I.E.E. Soil Stabilizer is presently in use in twenty three countries around the world both for road building and dust control in the mining industry. IEE lets you build more roads, more economically while attaining higher load bearing specifications, reduced permeability and reduced maintenance costs; often and most importantly, with far less imported materials!


Product Name:
ECO Enzyme Environmental Solutions
Eco-Enzyme is a fermented, liquid multi-enzyme produced in the United States. It has been approved for specific uses by the USDA. This includes its use as a general cleaner at federally inspected meat and poultry processing plants.

Since 1996 it has been tested and developed at our Costa Rican facility for a broad range of environmentally sensitive applications. These include sanitation, the elimination of malodor, flies, mosquitoes and their larvae as well as for the acceleration of all types of organic biodegradation. Despite our well documented effectiveness, Eco-Enzyme is non-chemical, not an insecticide nor a detergent.

Eco-Enzyme is a 21st century enzyme technology that is effective and economical. It is a safe, organic product composed entirely of food grade vegetable ingredients. There are no bacteria present in any of our products and they are factory certified free of salmonella.

Our product works in part due to the catalytic action of its proprietary enzyme cocktail. Eco-Enzyme functions best in aerobic situations (presence of Oxygen) though we have also demonstrated powerful odor elimination results in anaerobic systems (ask to see our Perdue University manure pit study). Eco-Enzyme eliminates odor by chemically altering ammonia, hydrogen sulfide and phenols. We are most effective in lowering the all important O.D.T. (Odor Detection Threshold), the most telling measure of odor as a nuisance.

Eco-Enzyme is organic and 100% biodegradable. Eco-Enzyme accelerates biodegradation in organic composting applications and significantly improves wastewater characteristics in many wastewater treatment applications.

It is non toxic and harmless to humans, marine life, flora and fauna. The conscientious application of Eco-Enzyme in chicken and pig farms has been demonstrated to improve animal health, increase productivity ratios and reduce the incidence of respiratory illness.
Eco-Enzyme works well within a wide range of pH and temperatures. It is non-hazardous, not explosive nor combustible. It causes no known allergic reactions and no special handling precautions are required for personnel applying our products


Product Name:
Anvil Wireless Communication Solutions
Anvil Technologies specializes in providing wireless solutions that are highly mobile, secure, and quickly deployable. Anvil's solutions are available to and used by private industry and government organizations. This communications infrastructure is the key to enabling access to live video and audio and realtime access to data applications.

Referred to as RECoN (Roaming Emergency Communications Network) for the Emergency Management sector and as ICoN (Infield Communications Network) for the non-emergency commercial organization, both solutions have been integrated using COTS (commercial off-the-shelf) and Open Systems components allowing for maximum flexibility.Wirelez

JML International is a Canadian company specialized to seek out and develop profitable business opportunities in Mexico by synergizing Global, technical expertise, and financial capabilities with the vast needs of Latin American business and industry, in a way that builds a solid foundation for growth, long term business relationships and profit.

The focus of JML and Vibra-Flo is to create, develop and manage strategic business alliances between ourselves and International companies to pursue projects and corporate joint ventures, and commercial relationships. At present, the objective is to take advantage of the petroleum boom that is foreseen in Mexico in the years to come.
