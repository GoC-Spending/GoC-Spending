Company Profile


A. F. Theriault & Son Ltd. is a Canadian boatyard based in Nova Scotia. Since 1938 we have built over 650 vessels of up to 150 feet (45 meters) for a wide range of uses and clients. These include fishing boats and passenger vessels, commercial workboats, fire/patrol/pilot boats, as well as luxury pleasure yachts. One of the largest private boatyards in Atlantic Canada, our modern and well equipped facility can produce monohull or catamaran vessels in fibreglass, steel, aluminum or advanced composites. We are fully qualified and equipped on-site, to build or repair all manner of boats, and our employees are experienced and highly professional.
