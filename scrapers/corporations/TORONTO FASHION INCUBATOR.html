Company Profile


The Toronto Fashion Incubator (TFI) is an award-winning, non-profit, small
business centre, uniquely tailored to serve the fashion industry.
By providing shared facilities in a stimulating environment,
offering studio rentals, managerial and office assistance, business mentoring,
promotional opportunities and entrepreneurial development at a
not-for-profit rate, TFI members are able to accelerate their
business growth and reduce the risk of failure.

Access to the Toronto Fashion Incubator's services and facilities
are available to members on two levels: Outreach and Resident.
Anyone can join the Outreach program simply by remitting an
application and annual membership fee of $140 + GST. The
Resident program is the TFI's premier membership program
available only to those who can demonstrate exemplary innovative
talent, business savvy and start-up financing. A 3-year business
plan and application must be remitted and approved before
acceptance into the program. Residents receive an on-site, 24/7 design
studio, unlimited mentoring time, access to production equipment (optional) and pay a monthly studio rental fee.
Leases are one year in length, renewable annually up to five years.
For studio rates, please contact us.

TFI members and alumni have won many awards recognizing their work including the City of
Toronto's Awards for Excellence in Fashion Design, Ryerson University's Alumni Award, Fashion Group
International's Eveleen Dollery Award, Yorkdale Shopping Centre's New Designer Discovery, Style magazine's New Designer of the
Year Award, TFI New Labels award, Mercedes-Benz Start-Up, CAFA and Hazelton Lanes New Designer Award.

The Toronto Fashion Incubator is widely respected within fashion
and business development circles and our entrepreneurial culture
is envied around the world. Cities such as New York, London, Paris, Milan, St.
Petersburg (Russia), Dublin, Capetown, Kobe, Chicago, Philadelphia, Washington DC, San Francisco
and Dallas have looked to the TFI as a model of success and
inspiration to start their own similar centres.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Small Business Assistance
TFI provides small business assistance to all types of fashion entrepreneurs. We offer in-house studio rentals, Outreach memberships, business mentoring, fashion business seminars, trade and consumer shows and access to an extensive resource
centre housing lists of industry specific information such as buyers, contractors, sample makers etc., examples of business plans
tailored to the fashion industry, and current and archived trend forecast publications.

Service Name:
Mentoring
Business mentoring is offered on a wide range of fashion, business and legal subjects from design, manufacturing and exporting to
business planning, intellectual property, branding, sourcing, media relations, sales, marketing and promotion. TFI also offers consultation services to cities and organizations looking to start their own fashion incubator.

Service Name:
Studio rentals
The TFI offers 10 in-house studios available to qualified and approved applicants. Studio rentals are rented at not-for-profit rates and include 24 hour access and security, 7 days per week, access to onsite facilities such as the TFI resource centre, meeting rooms, mentoring, networking events and promotional activities. Companies sub-leasing space within TFI are known as Residents and are automatically enrolled in the TFI Resident Program. Applications for the TFI Resident program can be obtained by calling (416) 971-7117. A three-year business plan, proof of financial readiness and samples of exemplar product are required to join the TFI Resident Program. Studios rent from $400 to $500 per month plus tax.

Service Name:
Marketing & Promotion
TFI members (Residents and Outreach) are promoted through the Incubator's extensive industry network including key contacts in
retail, media and government. Exclusive promotional opportunities such as fashion shows, competitions, networking events, editorials and export initiatives are extended to TFI members. In addition, TFI organizes various industry and consumer trade shows throughout the year to enable emerging Canadian brands an opportunity to sell to retailers and consumers.

Sector information

Key / Major clients:

Established in 1987 by the City of Toronto, TFI has helped many Canadian fashion entrepreneurs to launch, build and grow their fashion businesses. Some notable brands include David Dixon, Joeffer Caoc, Juma, Smythe, Todd Lynn, Pina Ferlisi, Antonio Azzuolo, Greta Constantine, Sid Neigum, Laura Siegel, Line Knitwear, Foxy Originals, Shay Lowe Jewellery, Jon De Porter Jewelry and RADLEY School Accessories.

Testimonial:

"It would not have been financially viable for me to launch my business if I wasn't a TFI Resident. The affordable rent, use of the sewing machines and great mentoring are all huge assets to my business. The connections that I made through TFI have allowed me to secure a major account which landed me my largest order to date."
-Sid Neigum

"The Toronto Fashion Incubator offers an extremely nurturing and positive environment for designers to launch and grow their brands. The contacts and relationships I've gained and fostered while being a Resident have been instrumental in building my accessories business and growing my sales."
-Erin Fitzpatrick, RADLEY School Accessories

International capital projects:

In 2013, TFI partnered with the Chicago Fashion Incubator to create an exchange program that would provide beneficial opportunities for their respective members. Activities included participation at Toronto and Chicago Fashion Weeks, exposure and introduction to retail buyers and industry VIP, media engagement and networking socials. In 2014, a similar initiative was held with the Philadelphia Fashion Incubator.
