Company Profile


ABBs advanced solutions combines state-of-the-art FTNIR analyzers with process and application knowledge to improve process performance, productivity, and safety. ABB Analytical provides simple, low-cost, high-performance, general-purpose FTIR spectrometers for research and routine infrared analysis in analytical chemistry labs as well as teaching labs. Also, the company offers FTNIR spectrometers for analysis application development by the user as well as turnkey analyzers for a large variety of analysis tasks. These instruments are employed in QA labs, at line near the process or on-line. They are capable of performing real-time analysis of the chemical composition and/or physical properties of samples or process streams. Our analyzers are used in the following industries: Academic, pharmaceutical, petroleum and biodiesel, semiconductors, remote sensing and aerospace, life sciences, semiconductors, chemicals and metallurgy.
