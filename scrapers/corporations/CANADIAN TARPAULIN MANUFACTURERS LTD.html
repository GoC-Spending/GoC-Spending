Company Profile


Canadian Tarpaulin is a private corporation which started
business in Saskatoon in 1980, manufacturing tarps and
protective coverings for any application. We offer stock sizes
and styles as well as custom fabrication, repair service, roll
goods and hardware sales. Our main trading area is Western
Canada and Northern and Mid-Western United States. Our primary
markets are fabric covered shelters, trucking, manufacturing,
lumber, mining and agriculture.

Key Customers
Our product is sold through many wholesale and retail
operations. We also sell to a large array of independent
businesses such as large trucking and trucking supply companies,
contractors and agricultural enterprises. In addition we service
lumber mills, the mining sector and oil and other resource
companies. We deal with many large original equipment
manufacturers who in turn incorporate our product into their
finished product. In the area of direct selling to the public,
we are primarily involved on the recreation end of the industry.
Servicing tent rentals, boat covers, golf carts, custom work, as
well as specialty items for the trucking industry such as winter
fronts. We also service a very diverse range of customers in the
shelter industry by supplying them with various covers and
products.


Facilities and Equipment
In industrial textiles, we have the best equipment and man power
to fill your needs. Our total facilities encompass more than
55,000 square feet. We use a variety of machines including a
rotary fabric welder, radio frequency welder, wedge welder and
hand-held hot air welders. We have in excess of 40 sewing
machines from 1 to 3 needle designs and various sized from 10:
to 40" arms. We utilize fabric cutting equipment capable of
efficiently cutting 100 or more layers of material at one time.
The installation of eyelets is done with automatic grommeting
machines. This allows us to produce large volume jobs and large
covers efficiently.

Quality Assurance
Canadian Tarpaulin was previously registered to ISO-9002. At
present we are not registered, but for the most part we do
operate under the ISO concept.

Mission Statement
Canadian Tarpaulin is recognized as a leader in the protective
covering industry. Constant refinement and innovation coupled
with the use of the finest quality materials and superior
construction methods has enabled us to exceed our customers
expectations.

Pledge of Quality
WHETHER OUR SERVICES are consummated through the terms of a
sophisticated agreement or a hand shake, OUR PLEDGE to our
customers is that, we will provide a superior product in terms
of quality, innovation and reliability at a price that reflects
honest value.

Guaranteed to go the distance.
