Company Profile

Service Name:

Our Customer's Success

Our passion is to help customers deliver operational improvements that significantly advance their business and help them reach their goals.

Through collaboration with our customers we seek to determine and understand issues and opportunities that are strategic to business improvement. We specialize in solving these issues and responding to opportunities using technology to make significant incremental improvements.

Methodologies

A methodical approach to addressing issues and opportunities can dramatically improve results. Capturing essential information, ideas, and documenting them in a clear and concise way enables effective team collaboration and maximizes use of team member’s skills and contributions. Working With Us

Our culture and core values focus on collaboration and team work in an entrepreneurial environment. We enjoy our work and enthusiastically take on challenges with our customers and suppliers.

Service Name:
Security - Information & Operations Scorecard
Every organization has finite resources to apply to information security. Understanding areas of risk and the organization's ability to monitor and mitigate risks is the key to developing an appropriate risk mitigation strategy.
People looking to compromise the security of an organization look for the easiest or weakest link in the security profile of an organization. A balanced, holistic, and layered approach is required to identify and mitigate risks. Information security is an ongoing series of processes, procedures, and practices working together.

The questions facing every organization relevant to information security are:
•Where is the organization vulnerable?
•How does an organization know what should be done?
•How often should it be done?
•What should not be done?
•Are the resources available?
•Does it make sense financially?

Data Perceptions has developed a formal approach to helping organizations arrive at the answer to these questions and to provide a roadmap on which to proceed in their risk mitigation effort.

Our Business Model

The Data Perceptions business model allows us to put together a team of professionals that best suits customer specific Technology & Operations (Tech & Ops) challenges.

Data Perceptions' focus is on combining technical and business expertise to help companies meet their operational business objectives through the use of technology. Our structure is ideally suited to collaborative strategic planning, design and implementation through the augmentation of client teams from a variety of sources.

We do this by starting with our core team of project strategists (enterprise architects), who meet with client stakeholders, analyze objectives and requirements, develop strategies and recommendations, and provide high level design and budgeting services. We enhance the team as required with job specific project managers, industry specialists, and key technical people drawn from our extensive talent community.

Over the past decade, we have strengthened this talent community by actively seeking out and connecting with organizations that augment our services and share our focus on providing exceptional value to the client. To ensure effective collaboration, we look for companies with strong ethics that demonstrate a good fit with our core values. We also target a 15-40% overlap in services knowledge which creates the foundation for strong teamwork and successful integration with client teams.

By working collaboratively with clients on project planning and implementation, client staff benefit from knowledge transfer and training during the project deployment. This allows operational teams to provide ongoing maintenance, support, and enhancements of the solution.

With this business model, we aim to provide the best suited personnel for each project, enabling us to finish on schedule, and with the expected results.

Distribution of employees:

Data Perceptions' consultant team of exceptional people is comprised of staff and subcontract associates. Staff consultants are project strategists, or enterprise architects with a variety of business and technical skills. Associates range from project strategist generalists to very niche specialists with technical or business knowledge. Data Perceptions' technology and operations consulting model utilizes a wide range of knowledge and experience.

Data Perceptions' core competency is crafting project teams to overcome business problems and deliver operational improvements through the use of technology. Our core values and guiding principles are essential in selecting entrepreneurial people with good attitudes and values, to form effective project teams, quickly and easily. Data Perceptions uses project strategists to guide and build project teams.

Project implementation teams usually involve project strategist(s), a project manager, Data Perceptions' subject matter experts, and the customer' workforce. Strategy development teams are led by two project strategists. Subject matter experts are brought in during problem definition and solution development. Technology strategy development can address a specific problem or development of an IT strategy road map of prioritized projects for the organization.

Although collaborative projects with a variety of personnel are typical, engagements requiring specific expertise, skills, or experience are available to fulfill customer needs.

Over a 20 year period, Data Perceptions' talent network has grown to hundreds of associates. We have consulting personnel in over 12 countries.

Every organization has finite resources to apply to information security. Understanding areas of risk and the organization's ability to monitor and mitigate risks is the key to developing an appropriate risk mitigation strategy.

Data Perceptions has developed a formal approach to helping organizations arrive at the answer to these questions and to provide a roadmap on which to proceed in their risk mitigation effort.

Our Business Model

The Data Perceptions business model allows us to put together a team of professionals that best suits customer specific Technology & Operations (Tech & Ops) challenges.

Data Perceptions' focus is on combining technical and business expertise to help companies meet their operational business objectives through the use of technology. Our structure is ideally suited to collaborative strategic planning, design and implementation through the augmentation of client teams from a variety of sources.

We do this by starting with our core team of project strategists (enterprise architects), who meet with client stakeholders, analyze objectives and requirements, develop strategies and recommendations, and provide high level design and budgeting services. We enhance the team as required with job specific project managers, industry specialists, and key technical people drawn from our extensive talent community.

Over the past decade, we have strengthened this talent community by actively seeking out and connecting with organizations that augment our services and share our focus on providing exceptional value to the client. To ensure effective collaboration, we look for companies with strong ethics that demonstrate a good fit with our core values. We also target a 15-40% overlap in services knowledge which creates the foundation for strong teamwork and successful integration with client teams.

By working collaboratively with clients on project planning and implementation, client staff benefit from knowledge transfer and training during the project deployment. This allows operational teams to provide ongoing maintenance, support, and enhancements of the solution.

With this business model, we aim to provide the best suited personnel for each project, enabling us to finish on schedule, and with the expected results.
