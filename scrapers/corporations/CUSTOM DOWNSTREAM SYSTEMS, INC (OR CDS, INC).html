Company Profile


CDS, Inc. is a manufacturer of custom downstream plastic extrusion machines delivering turnkey systems for most polymer or rubber applications including pipes, profiles, tubes, engineered plastics, automotive parts, custom products, and medical devices. Increase your productivity through custom machinery from cooling, sizing, to haul-offs, saws, cutters, combination units as well as texturizing, collection and auxiliary machines. Streamline your engineering for reliability and availability of your operations with CDS machines: Built Without Compromise. Visit www.cdsmachines.com.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Vacuum calibration units
CDSs vacuum calibrators ensure consistent cooling and calibration
of rigid profiles with a wide range of water and vacuum outlets. The
tooling rails and table size can be customized for many applications,
and an optional recirculation system or auxiliary vacuum tank provides additional flexibility.

Product Name:
Vacuum sizing tanks
A testament to CDS's flexible manufacturing capabilities are its vacuum sizing tanks, which cool and size post-extruded applications using flood, spray, or a combination of the two. Each compartment is sized, designed, and constructed to the customer's specifications, allowing maximum flexibility and control. Standard features of our vacuum sizing tanks include individually regulated vacuum valves and gauges, copper stand pipes and spray bars, and 3-axis precision tank movement.

Product Name:
Cooling tanks
CDSs extrusion cooling tanks are offered in a variety of sizes, and are made of non-ferrous materials to minimize rusting and water contamination. The tanks can be sectioned to allow for separate spraying and flooding capability. An optional recirculation system increases cooling efficiency and reduces the amount of waste water produced.

Product Name:
Air cooling tables
CDSs air tables cool plastic profiles by means of fully adjustable air blowers and
nozzles. The tables are solidly constructed and can be easily positioned to
match the height of the extruder and downstream line.

Product Name:
Belt pullers
Versatile as the applications they handle, CDS's belt pullers are your choice for pulling standard or custom profiles and small to medium-sized tubular products, such as garden hoses, irrigation pipes, and medical tubes. CDS's belt pullers are designed and manufactured with varying speed ranges, belt configurations, and belt materials to target your specific extrusion requirements.

Product Name:
Cleat pullers
Large rigid profiles need large rigid pullers to keep them moving along the extrusion line smoothly. CDSs Cleat Pullers have a robust platform designed to eliminate slippage and distortion of the extruded product, and can match the unique contour of the processors profile. The Cleat Puller is ideal for pulling window lineals, fencing and decking panels, and siding.

Product Name:
Pipe pullers
To accommodate the size and unique processing requirements of extruded pipes, CDS offers its brand of Pipe Pullers, which are designed to provide steady, precise pulling action for pipes of various materials, sizes, and hardness ratings.

Product Name:
Slab-Rod pullers
When belt or cleated pullers aren't enough to pull heavy polymer shapes, processors turn to CDS's Slab/Rod Haul-offs. With a pulling force of 100,000 Ibs, sizeable contact areas, and a heavy-duty support platform, CDS's Slab-Rod Haul-offs are designed to pull dense, industrial components such as solid or hollow rods and thick sheets (slabs).

Product Name:
Fly-knife & rotary cutters
Our fly-knife cutters are the ideal tube-cutting solution for variety of extruded applications, such as cable and wiring, hoses, medical tubing, and straws. These cutters are also suited for small, fleixble or rigid profiles, such as edgebands, weather-stripping, and plastic trim.

Product Name:
Traveling up-cut saws
CDS's traveling up-cut saws are ideal for cutting semi-rigid to rigid applications, such as window lineals, fencing panels, siding, railing and small to medium-sized tubular products. For greater cutting and speed-holding accuracy, these saws can be equipped with a brushless servo motor (CSS series), which also incorporates a built-in length controller. The table travel speed is self-adjusting and is designed to eliminate interference with the extrudates advancement.

Product Name:
Traveling cross-cut saws
CDSs traveling Cross-cut Saw is specifically designed to cut extra wide applications, such as decking boards and polymer slabs. Unlike the traveling up-cut saw, this unit executes lateral cuts to the application. Servo-driven models improve cutting accuracy and cut-to-length repeatability. Linear rails and bearings assure smooth table and blade travel. Features such as a solid base, table and blade guards, and cut-off switches make our cross-cut saws safe and dependable.

Product Name:
Planetary saws
CDS's servo-driven Planetary Saw is designed and manufactured to accurately cut pipes of various materials and diameters (O.D.) The unit's carbide or diamond-chip blade executes its incision around the pipes circumference while in synchronization with the extrusion line speed.

Product Name:
Embossing & brush-scouring
Create stunning wood grain impressions with CDSs Embossing stands, which embed a repeated, customer-specified pattern into extruded material. The speed can be set with a manual dial or a digital keypad for greater versatility. An oil-heating unit can be added for profiles that require heated rolls.

CDSs Brushing/scouring machines create a continuous grain-like pattern in post-extruded applications, such as wood-plastic composite (WPC) decking boards. A full enclosure and a vacuum blower ensure that dust and chip build-up is minimized, and the brushes are all easily accessible for quick replacement.

Product Name:
Collection & conveyor units
CDSs rugged and reliable pneumatic tilt tables can
quickly move material into a collection rack or bin for
easy pickup. The tilt action can be controlled via an
electronic timer, a mechanical limit switch, or a
photo-optic sensor.

The takeaway conveyor table moves light profiles off
the end of the downstream line, and deposits them
into catch bins using either compressed air bursts or
a mechanical arm. The ejection process is triggered
by a mechanical switch or photo-optic sensor. Table
width, conveyor speed, and bin type can be
customized according to the profile, plus an
optional overhead light allows added visibility.

Product Name:
The Combo Cooler
When one of our customers needed a flexible and reliable cooling solution for their extruded application, CDS was there to help. Designed to cool rubber automotive seals, this specially designed unit incorporates a hot tank, with mixing valves that blend plant chilled water with hot water from a reservoir with four immersion heaters under the tank to maintain a controlled temperature.

Product Name:
"Radius" Spray Cooling Tank
Designed to process extruded rubber gaskets, this specially configured Spray Cooling Tank has an inside radius of 72 inches and is one of several downstream units making up a complete plastic extrusion line. Measuring 16 x 16 inches in cross section, this tank also features 4 corner stainless steel bars with quick change spray nozzles, a closed loop water recirculation system, and a high-speed air stripper compartment.

Product Name:
Die tooling
Using advanced computer-assisted designs and EDM wiring technology, CDS can customize your die tooling to your plastic extrusion application. Email your requests to dietooling@cdsmachines.com

Product Name:
Medical High-Precision Vacuum Sizing Tank
Ideal for the precise shape requirements of medical extrusion tubing applications, the CDS Medical High-Precision Vacuum Sizing Tank provides efficient water cooling, while maintaining an enhanced heat transfer rate. Its stainless steel retractable water reservoirs, exterior controllable product support rollers and water level adjustment features maximize operator control and reduce downtime.

Product Name:
Medical Servo Belt Haul-Off/ Servo Fly-Knife Cutter Combination Unit
The CDS Medical Combination Servo Belt Haul-Off/Servo Fly-Knife Cutter unit combines precision cutting accuracy and durable pulling capabilities in an efficiently designed small footprint. Its accurate cut-to-length feature, X-Y cutter adjustment and easy blade replacement channel provides processors with a safe, efficient and accurate extrusion solution to the highly complex demands of medical extrusion tubing.

Product Name:
Medical Takeaway Conveyor
The CDS Medical Takeaway Conveyor effectively and efficiently collects extruded medical products. An easily adjustable frame, with enhanced front-mounted controls and the ability to make independent line speed adjustments reduces product collection costs, while increasing overall line productivity.

Service Name:
Service
At CDS, we have a tradition of backing great products with great service. We respond quickly and efficiently to all service-related requests, whether it's for a technician to repair your machine onsite or a replacement part to be sent overnight to your premises. service@cdsmachines.com
