Company Profile


Alliance Business Solutions offers online business management and coaching services that are designed to help businesses increase productivity and revenue generating potential.

Want to launch your business, but feeling overwhelmed by all that's required for a successful launch?

Suffering from the effects of S.O.S. - shiny object syndrome - and need help staying focused on your business vision?

Are you still trying to do everything by yourself, and experiencing frustration with the amount of time and energy you spend on non-core activities?

We can help you launch your business, stay motivated on your goals, and become more efficient so that you gain back valuable time which you could use to grow your business doing the things you enjoy most.

Contact us to find out more about the proven benefits of working with an Online Business Manager & Coach.

Do what you do best - and let Alliance take care of the rest.

Service Name:
Online Business Management
Alliance Business Solutions offers a diverse range of business support solutions including:

Business Management:
- Review and/or help develop key operating processes/procedures
- Develop client intake and follow-up strategies designed to optimize client interface
- Mentoring/Strategic planning sessions to identify actionable items that will help move your business forward

General Administration:
- Calendar and Email Management
- Client concierge and list management
- Assist identify/implement automated business tools to manage various systems
- Documentation: Word, PDFs, reports, PowerPoint presentations, Forms & Templates, Proposals, Newsletters
- Travel & Meeting Coordination

Social Media Management:
- Create/Optimize social media profiles: LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter
- Help identify keywords and essential messages
- Subscribe to predetermined targeted groups for valuable content contribution/exchange
- Email Marketing/Newsletter campaigns: format content/graphics; schedule broadcasts; promote to social media sites

HR/Recruitment Assistance

Service Name:
Business Coaching
Alliance Business Solutions is home to *Catapult Your Biz* - a coaching program designed to help entrepreneurs launch, focus on, and grow their businesses. With three distinct tiers in the program, entrepreneurs join the program at the stage that's right for them, and will be assisted through the following:

(a) the start-up phase of their business - ensure the best tool and resources are in place, and the right steps are taken to launch their venture;

(b) stay motivated by working with an accountability partner who will help them gain clarity and remain focused on their S.M.A.R.T goals - we'll work together to ensure you identify and set the right priorities to help you move your business forward; and

(c) grow their business through proven systems and strategic planning - we'll hone in on key priorities and help entrepreneurs implement measurable tactics to ensure successful results.

With regular communication and actionable steps, we'll help entrepreneurs defuse the overwhelm of running their business, and support them through their business development.
