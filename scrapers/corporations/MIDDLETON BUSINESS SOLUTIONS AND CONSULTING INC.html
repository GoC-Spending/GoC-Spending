Bank Reconciliation

The main benefit of a bank reconciliation is the controlling of
the most valuable asset (cash) of any firm, by bringing into
agreement the current cash account balance reported by the bank
and the cash balance reported by the depositor (your company)
immediately after the receipt of the monthly bank statement.

This benefit is achieved by verifying the transactions on the
monthly bank statement with the transactions in the cash ledger
account and analyzing any discrepancies for the possibility of
any errors or omissions by either the bank or the depositor
(your company).


Trial Balance

A trial balance ensures that the information in the general
ledger is accurate and that all transactions have been accounted
for. A trial balance proves that the ledger is in balance by
listing the account balances in a ledger.

Solution and Enterprise Architecture
The architecture services we provide include:
Ø Solution Blueprint
Ø Physical Architecture Deployment
Ø Gap Analysis/Business Needs Assessment
Ø Technology Option Study
Ø Architecture Review and Assessment
Ø Architecture Sub-system Design and Deployment
Ø Migration Planning
Solution Blueprint
A solution blueprint completes the first 4 rows of the Zachman
Enterprise Architecture Framework. The solution blueprint is
defined in the following stages:
Ø Define Business Requirements - This stage involves
identifying, validating and documenting the business
requirements including business goals and objectives, critical
success factors, constraints; risks and business processes.
Ø Define Architecture Requirements  This stage involves
identifying, validating and documenting the architecture
requirements including, but not limited to functional
requirements, quality requirements, available requirements and
architecture risks
Ø Design Logical Architecture  This stage involves
analyzing the business and architecture requirements to create
the logical architectures for the data, application, technical
and organizational architecture. Also migration planning is
performed to describe migrating the existing architecture to the
new architecture.

The solution blueprint:
Ø Aligns solution architecture with business vision
Ø Helps ensure ROI
Ø Minimizes risk by improving IT planning capabilities


Physical Architecture Deployment
The Physical Architecture Deployment services involve the
managing and implementing of the various architectures. This
service rollouts the architecture to supports the development
and implementation of the solution. The Physical Architecture
Deployment service helps in the realization of the solutions
ROI through the delivery of new business capabilities to the
organization
Gap Analysis/Business Needs Assessment
The Gap Analysis or Business Needs Assessment assess the
strengths and weaknesses in current business/IT plans by looking
at your organizations business strategy, technology strategy,
existing business capacities and existing technology
capacities.

The gap analysis/business needs assessment:
Ø Identifies any potentially inappropriate investment
Ø Provides a way to focus efforts on areas that provide
business benefit

Technology Option Study
The Technology Option Study reviews business and architecture
requirements and identifies potential technology components.
This service helps to ensure that the appropriate investment
decisions are made by looking at technology alternatives that
support the requirements.

Architecture Review and Assessment
The Architecture Review and Assessment service examines the
solution or enterprise architecture for completeness and
adherence to architecture best practices.

The architecture review and assessment:
Ø Locates areas not covered in clients plans
Ø Reduces risk during subsequent implementations

Architecture Sub-system Design and Deployment
The Architecture Sub-system design and deployment service
develops key components, such as data replication strategy, high
availability strategy, etc, of the architecture. This service
helps to ensure that show stopper issues are resolved quickly
with minimal impact to the schedule.

Migration Planning
The Migration Planning service develops and plans the migration
for the current solution architecture to the new solution
architecture.

The Migration Planning service:
Ø Allows budget and staff planning to begin in advance of
implementation cycles
Ø Reduces implementation risk by planning migration so
dependencies do not impact schedule
