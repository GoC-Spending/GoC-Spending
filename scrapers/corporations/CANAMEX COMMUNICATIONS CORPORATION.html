Company Profile


Founded in 1978, Canamex offers the TeraMessage Mobile PRIVATE Service for the Protection of Sensitive Corporate Mobile Information. Using end-to-end military-level encryption, the TeraMessage service provides a PRIVATE communication service for corporations who need to protect mobile information. Sender can view on computer or Smartphone if recipients are available to receive messages, BEFORE sending the message. Sender automatically receives read confirmation. Text and attachments are encrypted for maximum security. Send unlimited size messages. An optional master log contains the detailed record of all send and receives messages by computer and Smartphone users. This is not SMS or email. TeraMessage Mobile can be installed on any Smartphone serviced by any cellular company.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
QUIKPAGER 2400
QUIKPAGER is a professional, reliable Alphanumeric Paging device especially designed to be used in the Office, Hospitals,
Dispatch Centers and other businesses, to send private text messages to alphanumeric pagers and personal communication
devices. QUIKPAGER uses the telephone line to send messages to paging companies. QUIKPAGER is state-of-the-art technology for Instant Delivery of
Information and Message Management.


Product Name:
Wireless Pocket Pagers
Canamex will supply brand name POGSAG pagers with sales of QUIKPAGER Wireless to create an on-site paging system to suit
specific applications.

Product Name:
PageRouter for Emergency Response
For Emergency Response at factories, Hospitals, refineries, etc., PageRouter and QUIKPAGER Wireless transmit text messages to on-site pocket pagers for delivery in seconds. System can monitor machines or connect to automation factory monitoring equipment. It can also connect to Nurse Call systems in Hospitals or page messages from telephones, network computers or the Internet.

Product Name:
QUIKPAGER Wireless
QUIKPAGER Wireless is a self-contained, on-site Wireless Paging product used to send messages to local, on-site alphanumeric pagers.Type messages for instant transmission within a radius of 1 or 3 miles, depending on selected power and upon terrain conditions. Responding to voice prompts, use any touch-tone telephone to page people. Connect Nurse Call, Alarm and Monitoring Systems for instant paging.

Product Name:
QUIKSERVER
QUIKSERVER unit used to route messages to QUIKPAGER Wireless
from Nurse Call, Monitor and Alarm Systems, other QUIKPAGERs or
PCs. One TAP RS-232 output connects to QUIKPAGER Wireless. (3)
TAP RS-232 inputs simultaneously receive TAP messages from other
QUIKPAGER units, Nurse Call, Alarm and Monitoring systems.

Product Name:
On-site Paging wireless equipment
Wireless paging transmitter, paging controllers and pagers for on-site application. Send messages to pocket pagers from
touch-tone phones, network computers, the Internet or dedicated keyboards.

Service Name:
TeraMessage Mobile Private Service
TeraMessage Mobile PRIVATE Service protects your company by giving you total custody of sensitive corporate mobile information sent between phones and computers. Your sensitive mobile information always stays guarded in your company, instead of being stored in external public messaging servers where you lose control of its destiny. With TeraMessage, sensitive information travels protected on the Internet, where the end-to-end encryption key is automatically changing in a random pattern for each user. Executives simply install the TeraMessage app on their mobile device. As the Administrator of your PRIVATE TeraMessage service, you decide who belongs to the service and who can communicate with whom. TeraMessage is a PRIVATE service for Corporations that need to protect sensitive mobile information.
