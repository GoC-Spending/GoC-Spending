Company Profile


Pestend Pest Control Toronto offers professional 100% Money Back Guaranteed pest extermination and wildlife removal services in Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area. Pestend Pest Control employs only humane wildlife removal techniques and eco-friendly pest extermination services in Toronto. We specialize in bed bug extermination, raccoon removal, squirrel removal, skunk removal, wasp nest removal, bee hive removal, mouse extermination, rat extermination, bird removal, flee and tick extermination, cockroach extermination and any other pests commonly found in Ontario Canada.
