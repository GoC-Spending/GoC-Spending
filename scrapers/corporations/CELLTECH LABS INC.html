Company Profile


Compliance Testing and Engineering Lab. Provides Industry Canada, FCC and CENELEC SAR & EMC/RF Compliance testing, CE mark testing for Europe, as well as IC representative services pursuant to RSS-100 and DC-01(E). Services are provided to OEM's other EMC laboratories and RF engineering & design experts. Full product certification and Project management for worldwide certification filings. Facilities have Fully Anechoic Chamber, Screened rooms, SPEAG SAR DASY RF Exposure Measurement System, OATS. ISO 17025 Compliance testing.
