Company Profile


CPAS Systems Inc. is a leading global supplier of retirement systems technology. CPAS provides advanced administration software for plan sponsors, both single and multi-employer, as well as third party administrators and financial institutions. Designed for the Internet and for employee self-service, CPAS software is multi-language, multi-currency, and multi-jurisdictional in its design. It offers a complete solution for the administration of all forms of defined benefit and defined contribution pension and savings plans. CPAS Systems Inc. is the first pension administration software company, globally, to have been awarded registration to the ISO 9001:2000 standard.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
CPAS/dc
CPAS/dc offers financial institutions and plan sponsors a
complete solution for the administration of registered and non-
registered Defined Contribution and savings plans. The system is
designed to work in both "group" and "non-group" modes.



Product Name:
Creative Actuary
Creative Actuary: Creative Actuary is an annuity calculator. It
frees plan administrators from dependence on their actuaries to
calculate annuity values, optional forms and commuted values.

Creative PAGe: The Creative Pension Adjustment Generator
calculates PA values for employees T4 forms. It can handle any
type of pension formula and provides a full data interface to
payroll, human resource systems and CPAS.

Creative Benefits: Creative Benifits is a complete non-pension
benefits administration system. It uses the same technology as
CPAS and the two products can be integrated. Creative Benefits
produces full billing statements for insurance carriers, and
comprehensive annual benefit statements.

CPAS: The Creative Pension Administration System provides a
complete system to administer all types of pension plans. It
provides full record-keeping, benefit calculations in accordance
with the plan rules, legislation and the applicable actuarial
assumptions. It generates all required employee statements and
actuarial data.

CPAS/dc: provides to pension plan sponsors and third party
administrators the tools they need for day to day administration
of defined contribution pension plans. CPAS/dc tracks the
investments of each member and calculates their benefit
entitlement.

Technology profile

Employing an Oracle DBMS, the CPAS v5 Unified Platform supports the complete administration of Defined Benefit, and Defined Contribution pension plans. The same system can also administer various hybrid plan types, as well as insured benefits. Pensioner payroll and fund accounting are also provided, as is workflow and document management.

CPAS v5 allows users to have a single platform with multiple functional components. The application offers our customers access to their defined benefit, benefit premium tracking, group and or individual retirement savings, via one screen. Allowing an administrator to view all member records in conjunction with one another, from one easily accessible, and manageable screen.

The CPAS v5 Unified Platform is truly an Application Framework containing an Open Data Dictionary, Scripting Language, Rules generator and Data Migration and Cleansing Tools. The overall platform has been designed to support additional functional applications for a global market place. It can be defined to meet any customers specific requirements.
