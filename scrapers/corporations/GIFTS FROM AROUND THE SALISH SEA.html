Company Profile


Gifts From Around The Salish Sea - sourcing products that promote Coast Salish Cultural Designs. Based in British Columbia, products include the following: Artwork, Beads, Blankets, Books, Cedar Boxes, Clothing, Drums, Glass.
Jewellery, Masks, Wood, Metal Plaques & Promotional Products, Music. Red Cedar Designs encourages local shopping within the Salishan Seas' Territory. A portion of every sale will be used to give back to this Territory's Community.

Our "Old World" Series of glass is reflective of a time when the refining process was less than what it is today. Though our glass is of the highest quality we have created a service of glass which has many tiny bubbles to give it that "Old World" look. We guarantee that the bubbles do not effect the strength of the glass in any way, they only give it more character and warmth. We also feel that the little bubbles gives the glass more of that "Handmade" look, reflective of the time where all glass was handmade. If your looking for Designer Handmade Glass Tiles that have that handmade Old World feel, this line is for you.

Designer Handmade Glass.

They are: 2”x2” Glass Tiles that have rounded sides and corners. They are made out of handmade glass that is surprisingly

stronger than commercially made art glass.They can withstand shock from temperature changes hot/cold water.

They can be used for Back splashes, for Kitchen & Bathrooms, or simply used to accent kitchens maybe even used possibly for

drawers.

They are made In a glass kiln that require several firings. They are fired in a kiln at a temp of 1480degrees f.

The Firing cycle is about 8 hrs as part of the firing process they are Annealed which is similar to Tempering.

We, can make these in a multitude of colors

Your, welcome to search out other websites that feature similar type tiles a couple of things that separate us from other places is

the Quality and Craftsmanship of these Tiles. And, we are in a unique position to be able to manufacture these tiles at a far lower

price, than our competitors there tiles range anywhere from $22 – $75 each.


Our "Old World" Series of glass is reflective of a time when the refining process was less than what it is today. Though our glass is of the highest quality we have created a service of glass which has many tiny bubbles to give it that "Old World" look. We guarantee that the bubbles do not effect the strength of the glass in any way, they only give it more character and warmth. We also feel that the little bubbles gives the glass more of that "Handmade" look, reflective of the time where all glass was handmade. If your looking for Designer Handmade Glass Tiles that have that handmade Old World feel, this line is for you.
