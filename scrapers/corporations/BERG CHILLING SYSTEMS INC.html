Company Profile


Berg Chilling Systems designs, manufactures, installs and services industrial thermal process control systems for a broad range of industries and specialized applications around the world. Over the past 40 years, we have pumped, chilled, frozen and heated our way to the forefront of global thermal management solutions.

Our product range is as diverse as the markets and industries we serve. From our Toronto based facility, we custom design, manufacture, test and integrate a wide range of skid mounted industrial process refrigeration equipment, mechanical packages and fluid pumping systems for ease of installation and commissioning.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Air Cooled Refrigeration Units
Air Cooled Refrigeration Units
Berg's line of air cooled chillers are designed for reliable, long life in everyday or extreme environments.

Air Cooled Refrigeration Units start at 2 tons of refrigeration to any greater capacity necessary.

Features:
- Fully modular system. Major components can be located inside or outside work area to optimize efficiency, save floorspace.
- Pre-piped, pre-wired and factory tested eliminates the need for site assembly
- Fully packaged and quickly installed system means minimal or no downtime
- Reclaimed heat can be used to heat plant in winter months, reducing utility costs.

Product Name:
Blown Film Coolers
Blown Film Extrusion - The manufacture of plastic film for products such as shopping bags is achieved using a blown film line. This process is the same as a regular extrusion process up until the die. The die is an upright cylinder with a circular opening similar to a pipe die. The diameter can be a few centimetres to more than three metres across. The molten plastic is pulled upwards from the die by a pair of nip rolls high above the die (4 meters to 20 meters or more depending on the amount of cooling required).

Berg's blown film cooler is a specialized module for use with plastic blown film extrusion lines.

Features:
- Heavy gauge stainless steel to reduce corrosion and film stream contamination.
- Left and right hand part configured.
- Automatic temperature control
- Air Flow (SCFM): 500 - 4000
- Capacity (BTU/hr): 69K - 550K

Product Name:
Chilled on-Demand Water / Glycol Systems
Berg Chilling Systems can provide chilled on-demand water/glycol solutions for your food processing chilling equipment and for your sanitary ingredient water requirements.

Product Name:
Industrial Pumping Systems
Berg offers complete flexibility in pumping systems. Stainless steel reservoirs are mounted on a structural steel base with banding for additional long term structural support. Single and dual loop systems come standard with factory installed valved headers with, and pre-wired central control panels, which include: a NEMA 12 enclosures, 120 Volt single phase control voltage,starters and fusing.

Options available:
- Detachable pump / tank skids to facilitate transportation and installation Double ended tank sets
- Dedicated or combined standby pumping
- Variable frequency drives
- Mounted auxiliaries Chemical treatment and filtration
- Heat exchangers
- Custom configuration to meet specific requirements

Product Name:
Portable Chillers
All Berg process cooling chiller portable refrigeration units come with a liquid chiller circuit and a pump tank system for a complete solution. Berg portable chillers are available in water or air cooled, single or dual pump circuits with condensers remote or attached.

For use in small and / or dedicated applications. These self-contained units are available in: Indoor Air Cooled Indoor Air Cooled with Remote Outdoor Condenser Water Cooled (City Water).

Berg has packaged more high performance and convenience features into into our portable chillers to provide an exceptional and economic solution for your process needs. Plus, with our extensive list of available options, we can customize a chiller specific to your application:

- Process pumping to 90 psi
- Standby pumping
- Single or dual circuited refrigeration loops
- Non-Ferrous configurations
- Centrifugal blowers for heat reclaim
- Heat and low temperature designs
- Factory mounted disconnects

Sized from 1 to 40 refrigeration tons

Product Name:
Recreational Ice Rink Chillers
Berg's line of recreational ice chilling systems is specifically engineered for ice and curling rinks. Systems are factory assembled and tested to ensure highest level of quality.

Highlights -

- 100% Heat Recovery
- Highest System Efficiency
- HFC Refrigerants
- Reduced Refrigerant Charge
- Water or Air-Cooled Chillers
- Compact Footprint
- Central Controller for Multiple Component Systems
- Skid-Mounted Equipment
- Complete Pumping Stations
- Compressor (HP) 20 - 120 or More
- Cooling Capacity (Refrigeration tons) 15 to 300 or More
- Heating (BTU/hr) 270k - 846k

Scope of supply can include the complete ice making package including chiller, piping, ice rink floor etc.

Product Name:
Shell Ice Makers
The Berg packaged shell-ice maker is a highly efficient machine that provides unique fragments of finely divided ice for the protection of perishable food products in storage and in transit.

Berg Shell-Ice Makers are available air cooled or water cooled models. In addition to an air cooled model with a remote (rooftop) condenser, Berg offers two self- contained, packaged units - air cooled or water cooled - which require only the simple connection of suitable water and electrical supplies to produce ice. The water cooled models can be equipped with marine type condensers suitable for salt water operation. Standard air cooled models are equipped with copper tube-aluminium fin condensers.

Highlights:
- Only stainless steel or food grade plastic parts in contact with ice
- User selectable ice thickness
- Fresh or salt water ice

Applications:
- Meat & Poultry
- Fish
- Fruit
- Vegetable
- Juice freezing

Product Name:
Water Cooled Refrigeration Units
Berg Water cooled refrigeration units have a choice of semi-hermetic refrigeration compressors, scroll refrigeration compressors or screw refrigeration compressors with a double pump tank mounted with the chiller module, PLUS the advantage of being able to reclaim waste heat.

Features:
- Compressor (HP) 20 - 120 or higher
- Cooling Capacity (Refrigeration tons) 15 to 300 or higher

These modules are used where:
- Cooling tower water is available
- Integral pumping is required
- The inside air is either corrosive, extremely dusty or hot
- Supplemental heating is of no benefit
- The plant is air conditioned

Product Name:
Water Temperature Control Units
The BERG BTC Series Water Temperature Control Units are designed to maintain process water temperature to a desired set point.

TCUs are commonly used in the Plastics Industry to hold part tolerance and reduce scrap by maintaining consistent process temperatures (Injection Moulding, Thermal Forming, Blow Moulding and Extrusion Processes).

Features:
- Long-life, low-watt density incoloy heaters resist corrosion better than copper or stainless steel.
- High capacity pumps for turbulent flow.
- Removable top and side panels provide easy access.
- Bypass line protects heater and pump if process flow is shut off.
- Adjustable water supply pressure switch prevent operation at low supply pressure. Closed loop and negative pressure units available.

Service Name:
Custom Engineered Chilling Solutions
Berg Chilling Systems is a technologically advanced designer, manufacturer and installer of industrial refrigeration and cooling systems.

We maintain a large engineering department, staffed with specialists experienced in the design of refrigeration, fluid pumping and heat-transfer systems.

With equipment and systems operating in all corners of the globe, we support our customers with highly skilled international sales personnel able to assess your cooling needs, and an international service department that routinely dispatches highly trained technical personnel for commissioning and training services.

Putting the latest in 3D design technology to work for you creating sophisticated, high-impact communications. Berg offers one-stop, in-house engineering services - from initial ideas to a finished, packaged product.

We pride ourselves in our ability to package custom solutions for customers. Our product portfolio ranges from single compressor portable-type chillers to large multiple compressors, central refrigeration systems utilizing the latest technologies in environmentally friendly refrigerants.

Visit www.berg-group.com

Unique applications:

Temperature Control Units

Berg designed and built high-performance temperature control units with variable speed pumps and programmable logic controls that
accurately monitor and control widely varying temperatures, flow and pressure requirements in critical applications.

The Automotive Industry

An injection molding application in the automotive industry, where cooling water for moulds and hydraulics is piped to each machine
from a central mechanical room, maximizing floor space and efficiency.

Fiberglass Cooling Towers

A 730-ton capacity cooling tower system designed and built by Berg to cool a large welding system, cutting fluids and hydraulic equipment. The cooling system consists of multiple-cell cooling towers, with a pump-tank set inside the plant. Berg cooling towers enable users to take advantage of utility benefits by providing "free winter cooling", a feature available to all Berg customers located in cooler climates.

Ice Machines

Berg ice machines are available in two air-cooled formats and one water-cooled design, to meet the demands of applications in the food processing, construction and chemical industries. In addition, they offer the versatility of producing ice of varying thicknesses, from either salt water or fresh water, with no equipment modifications, simply a turn of a dial. Berg ice machines can also be customized to satisfy special requirements, such as plasma freezing. Components in contact with ice or water are made of stainless steel or food-grade plastic to meet stringent government and industry standards for health and hygiene.
