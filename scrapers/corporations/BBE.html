Company Profile


BBE is a 35 year old logistics and supply chain company specializing in supporting remote resource development projects in northern Canada. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of the NorTerra group of companies. NorTerra is equally owned by two aboriginal development corporations - the Inuvialuit Development Corporation (IDC) on behalf of the Inuvialuit of the Western Arctic and the Nunasi Corporation on behalf of the Inuit of Nunavut.

BBE provides ongoing supply chain support to some of the largest resource projects in northern Canada, as well as continually adding new clients who require the operational knowledge and experience BBE has gained over the past three decades operating in the Arctic.

BBE provides connection to Arctic Canada through transportation gateways in Alberta, Manitoba and Eastern Canada. BBE has the financial capacity to undertake the proposed work, and has the necessary facilities, equipment, partners and well-trained staff to fully deliver the required services.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Project Staffing
With a team of dedicated logistics experts, BBE is able to provide experienced contract personnel to our clients in variety of capacities and roles that may be required by a project. Our personnel are specialized in project logistics and understand the technical roles and support required to operate and manage projects in all conditions and all facets of logistics management.

BBE project staff is fully trained and safety accredited in all logistics support roles and will ensure maximum efficiencies in all work plans while minimizing project costs and associated risks.

From chartering aircraft through building ice runways to remote camp management and all project logistics management positions required, BBE is the logistics company to support your projects success.

Service Name:
Supply Chain Logistics
We focus on maximizing the efficiency of your supply chain through control of quality, control of cost and control of time. We specialize in efficient movement of supplies and personnel. Our experience and ability to understand your project requirements and timelines enables BBE to provide logistics management in:

planning,
sourcing,
transportation,
verification of orders and vendor receipt of shipments
strategic timing for delivery.

Freight receiving, storage, consolidation, and transport are controlled through BBEs network of transportation links. As the complexities and requirements of each project are unique, we actively communicate with our clients to develop a timeline that will ensure accuracy of shipments and the timeliness of deliverables. This project management includes verification of freight, warehousing through our supply chain networks, and delivery of freight to the locations as required and when required. BBE ensures the continuity of your projects operation through our cargo tracking system, management of freight consolidation and loading. Our intimate understanding of truck, airplane, container and rail capacity allows BBE to maximize payloads and optimize pricing for customers thus ensuring accurate transportation routing and timely delivery.


Service Name:
Procurement
Our unique network of service providers allows us to find cost effective and timely solutions for our customers. Whether the mode of transportation is Trucking, Air Freight, Air Charter, Marine Cargo, Rail, or Containerization  we have it covered! BBE has a team of personnel dedicated to the sourcing and procurement of goods and general basics including groceries and fuel.

Supplies:

BBE sources and delivers products for customers in the North. With one phone call, BBEs team will source everything from groceries, parts, equipment to household goods.

Transportation:

Whether its a hot shot, dedicated load, aircraft charter or consolidation, BBEs provides procurement of transportation services throughout North America and around the world.


Service Name:
Customs Clearance Facilitation
BBEs cargo services, located at the Edmonton International Airport, can facilitate customs clearance activities with your brokers or help you find one to meet the needs of your business.

BBE works with all major customs brokers operating in Alberta, so well help get the paperwork done and signed, speeding the customs clearance process

Service Name:
Freight Forwarding / Expediting
BBE subscribes to the fundamental principle of expediting: Deliver Anywhere  Efficiently and On Time. Our team of expediters works 24/7 to provide you with the best delivery solutions domestically and around the world. Whether working from our offices or on your project location, we provide solutions to machinery and equipment breakdowns, project essential equipment movement, special orders or hotshot services enabling our clients projects to return to full operation as quickly as possible. BBEs professional relationship with the best in their class vendors ensures all clients receive timely attention and excellent service. Most importantly, keeping our clients informed of cargo arrivals through long supply chains is what we do best.

Service Name:
Passenger and Baggage handling
BBE operates its own passenger terminal at the Yellowknife Airport and supplies resource clients with passenger check-in and baggage handling services that meets their needs. Highly trained staff understand client and air carrier requirements and government regulations associated with processing passengers and their baggage.
