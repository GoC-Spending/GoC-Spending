Company Profile


We manufacture, supply and install a wide variety of fabricated
products in stainless steel, titanium, aluminum, carbon steel
and other alloys. We supply stainless steel tanks, plain and
dimple jacketed, specialized machinery, conveyors, bins,
hoppers, pump bases, platform and stairs, modifications to
existing equipment etc.

Filor has experienced field crews that are available 7 days a
week to install stainless steel process piping, conveyors,
tanks, pumps and any ancillary equipment.

We stock stainless steel tubing and sanitary fittings for
immediate delivery.
Personnel with up to 30 years experience in this type of
business is your assurance of our ability and "Know How".

All our contracts are fully covered by Liability and Property
Damage Insurance.

In the past our services hae been used mostly by Pharmaceutical,
Cosmetic, Food, Beverage, Pulp and Paper, Distillery and
Chemical Industries with the following list being some of our
repetitive customers:

AMKCO International Pte. Ltd.
