Company Profile


CopperMoon is an award-winning, Aboriginal-owned communications company with a strong team and locations around BC. The CopperMoon team is very mobile and works for clients on-location.

We have a passion for working with First Nations communities and organizations to create strong, effective communications that assist them in achieving their goals. Today, CopperMoon is western Canada's only full-service Aboriginal communications agency, winning an inaugural BC Aboriginal Business Award.

Since inception, weve built a deep portfolio of successful projects for everyone, from individual First Nations to broad reaching government organizations. Each of us at CopperMoon has a vested interest in First Nations communities and culture.

We seek to build more capacity with every project we do. We'll hire local people, including youth, to help us wherever possible. As part of every project we work on, we train staff, and give them the tools so they can continue without us after a project is done. Our capacity building tools include our very easy to use website Content Management System, as well as Word templates that we create and logo usage guides.

A project is only as successful as the project management that drives it. In our experience, the better the project management the faster and more efficient a project reaches completion. At CopperMoon, we provide experienced and effective management of your project. We understand that you don't have time to micromanage, and we factor in the time to get the job done efficiently.

Key / Major clients:

Our past clients include:
The Federal and Provincial governments, Aboriginal Sports and Recreation, world famous artists Roy Henry Vickers and Robert Davidson, VanCity Credit Union, the RCMP, Squamish Nation, Musqueam Nation, Kyuquot Nation, the Taku River Tlingit Nation, and the New Relationship Trust. We've also provided product and services for a number of community-based First Nations organizations such as Gitxsan Child and Family Services Society.

Testimonial:

Everything about my CopperMoon experience was beyond expectation. I especially appreciated the "stepping outside the box" approach and attitude in dealing with us as First Nations. Too often, we're subjected to approaches based on past "consultant" methods of [usually] retired bureaucrats that isn't much different from government policies and programs. From the start CopperMoon went way beyond that. I like how you use local knowledge and talent, how you enhance the visions of the client, and how short the timelines are for the work being done. I especially like how local visions are created and/or made acceptable for international eyes/understanding.

- Ardythe Wilson,&#8232;Gitanmaax Band Counsellor and former Delgamukxw Communications Officer

The success of the internal and external communications data collection and reporting is mainly due to having CM consultants spending one-on-one time or drawing from large group discussions with key employees, committee members, leadership, and elders throughout the project duration. It was delightful to review the scope of the creative brief which accurately reflected the opinions and values of the community and served as a basis for developing data collection instruments for enhancing communications and development of the First Nations and corporate Governance structures.

- Sandra Jack&#8232;Spokesperson, TRTFN
