Company Profile


Integrated Safety Management Services (ISMS) is committed to protecting the health and safety of our employees, contractors, clients and the public. We are also firmly committed to conducting our operations in a diligent manner designed to minimize any adverse impacts on our environment. ISMS offers the following safety services: providing and maintaining a safe work environment with proper policies, procedures, standards, training, equipment and emergency response procedures in accordance to all government regulations and industry practices; providing appropriate health, safety and environmental training; developing programs and practices to minimize health, safety and environmental problems; ensuring a timely and effective response and follow-up to incidents resulting from our operations; remaining sensitive to public concerns; communication with the public affected by our operations; establishing health, safety, and environmental goals and regularly reviewing and improving on these goals; utilizing our in house developed “SMS” (Safety Management Systems) software.

All management, employees, contractors, subcontractors and suppliers engaged will be responsible for following all the health, safety and environment procedures as required, and participate in pertinent safety and environmental training. By fulfilling our safety and environmental responsibilities, everyone who works for and with ISMS shares in the benefits of a safe work environment.

Service Name:
COR Auditing
Health and safety audits are a key aspect of ensuring your Health, Safety and Environmental programs are current and performing up to current legislative and industry standards. An audit is a comprehensive review of a Health, Safety and Environmental Management system’s documentation and implementation. An accredited ISMS auditor will review the basic elements within an employer’s safety program by reviewing relevant documentation, conducting interviews and observing aspects of the company’s operations.

Integrated Safety Management Services has an experienced group that is certified to perform Certificate of Recognition (CoR or SECoR) audits. Our team is skilled and has an experienced group of auditors who are certified in a wide variety of industries. Certifying partners include: AASP, ENFORM, ACSA, AMTA, and CBCA.

Contact us today to discuss your needs. We will assign one of our industry specialists to cater to your specific audit requirements. We have auditors based in Southern, Central and Northern Alberta who can come to you, however remote your location.

Service Name:
Safety Consulting
ISMS offers the following Safety Consulting services:

Database Management:

Safety and contractor database registries are currently becoming a significant part of day to day business operations. To keep up with these requirements, Integrated Safety Management Services has deployed a team of database specialists who have extensive experience with a variety of systems.

We have considerable knowledge with databases such as: ISNetworld, ComplyWorks, PICS, Contractor Check, and CanQual. Our database management team can help maintain your electronic data to ensure your safety program’s effectiveness and compliance with Employer requirements.

Safety Program Development:

Employers are responsible for the health and safety of their employees while in the workplace. This responsibility is not to be taken lightly. Workplace accidents, injuries and illnesses can significantly reduce a company’s working morale, the ability to retain valuable clientele, the company’s WCB rates and overall productivity. With a well-developed, implemented and managed Health and Safety Program, workplace incidents can be significantly reduced.

Integrated Safety Management Services offers comprehensive health and safety program development, implementation and maintenance specific to your needs. Our team consists of leading industry specialists in a variety of fields. We can help you build your safety program, prepare for health and safety audits, maintain your day to day health and safety requirements, and/or provide general guidance about any of your health and safety concerns. Contact us today to set up a meeting so we can discuss your needs.


Service Name:
Field Services
ISMS offers the following Field Services:

Safety Supervision:

To maintain the integrity of your projects, the on-site safety of your employees and contractors must remain a high priority. Ensuring the health and safety of all personnel involves a diligent process of site and equipment inspections, process audits, safety meetings, incident investigations, emergency response planning, and worker training, certification and competency verification. By utilizing ISMS Safety Supervision services, this process can be streamlined to create an efficient and safe workplace. Quality Control measures like this can significantly reduce the occurrence of workplace accidents and incidents.

The Field Safety Supervision division at ISMS is experienced and well qualified. All of our Senior Field Supervisors are NCSO certified and will be assigned to their area of expertise. Our team has extensive experience in such fields as construction, manufacturing, mining, as well as oil and gas and oil sands drilling, completions and refining.

Rental Equipment:

Integrated Safety Management Services offers rental equipment to meet the safety needs of your projects. Our dedicated rentals team ensures that all equipment is well maintained and ready for deployment. Rental equipment offered by ISMS include:

- Air trailers
- Air Packs (SCBA)
- Self-Contained Emergency Shower & Eye Wash Stations
- Combo Heated Bathroom Emergency Shower & Eye Wash Stations
- Warm up Stations
- Office Trailers
- Command Centers

For more information or quotes, please do not hesitate to contact an ISMS representative.

UAV Drone Services:

Always striving to stay at the forefront, we are proud to provide Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) or Drone Services. While fixed wing and helicopter time continues to rise in cost, many of these tasks can be accomplished with greater savings utilizing one of our UAV platforms. With the use of an assortment of sensor, HD photographic, and video payloads, these tasks include but are not limited to:

- Survey and Mapping
- Reclamation Assessments
- Aerial Site Assessments
- Scouting Operations
- Emergency or High Risk Observation
- Safety Investigations
- Remote, Real-time Monitoring of Sites
- Aerial Photography and Cinematography
- Sale and Service of UAVs – Custom and Commercial Packages
- Pilot Training

To learn more about this innovative technology and discover how unmanned drones can work for you, please call 1 (844) 877-3277 to schedule a consultation.
