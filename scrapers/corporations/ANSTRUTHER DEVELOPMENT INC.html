Company Profile


Anstruther Development Inc. was founded in 1989 to provide
Manufacturers and Distributors with a completely integrated data
management system for improving the efficiency of their
business. This entrepreneurial enterprise understands the
business of manufacturers and distributors, and has provided
solutions for various customers with individual and unique
challenges. The solutions that have been developed to-date are
varied and flexible.

Anstruther Development Inc. continuously provides enhancements
and features to address individual customer needs. They have the
solutions, resources, creativity, and flexibility to "Make the
Difference". The Management Team at Anstruther Development Inc.
has a wealth of experience in: Software Design and Development,
Project Management, Training and Customer Support. This rapidly
growing enterprise is eager to meet the requirements of the
progressive manufacturer or distributor, utilizing an existing
data management system that can benefit organizations
immediately with improved efficiency and cost savings.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
AD2002© Software - Integrated ERP Manufacturing Systems
The AD2002© was created by Anstruther Development Inc. as a
comprehensive data base system specifically designed for
Manufacturers and Distributors who are leading the trend to
upgrade their business systems for optimum monitoring and
control of their operations, financial management and marketing
projections. The AD2002© is compatible with ISO 9000 programs
and NAFTA requirements.

AD2002© Features Include:
- easy to use order entry/quotation module for rapid customer
response for stock or engineered products
- comprehensive, accurate inventory management
- varied reporting module to track business efficiency
- open interface for e-mail, fax, customer or supplier
correspondence
- multi-level security capability
- automatic re-order module based on pre-determined thresholds
and up-to-date, accurate financial management
- ability to handle multiple currencies simultaneously
landed import costs
- multi-lingual interface
- dynamic error control safeguard

The cost savings and payback of this system are considerable and
certainly worth investigating.
