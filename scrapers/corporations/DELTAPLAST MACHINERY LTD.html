Company Profile


Manufacturers of single screw extrusion & co-extrusion, equipment
for the thermoplastics industry.

In today's increasingly competitive marketplace, it has been
said that a compromise between price and quality must be made.
At Deltaplast, more than two decades of designing and
manufacturing extrusion equipment has given us a wealth of
experience, resulting in developments that deliver both quality
and cost benefits.

We've incorporated an array of features that reduce component
wear, replacement/maintenance costs, and most importantly,
downtime and aggravation. Rather than compromise on quality
components, we believe that emphasis on sound engineering and
top flight manufacturing techniques is what wins respect on the
shop floor, and adds profit to your bottom line. All Deltaplast
extrusion equipment is built without compromise for reliability,
durability, and performance.

cooling tanks stations, dump tables, cut-off saws, pullers,
sheet lines, and air strippers

Sector information

Unique applications:

Deltaplast extruder have separate thrust bearing
and gear reducer housing assemblies. This
separation of oil baths for both gear reducer and
thrust bearing eliminates the possibility of
related damage or failure through oil cross
contamination. The result is increased durability
and lower repair or replacement costs.A removable
and replaceable bimetallic feedbox liner protects
against accidental damage or long term wear,
while eliminating possible replacement of the
entire extruder barrel or feed throat housing.
This innovative design minimizes your downtime
and expense. Our "unitization" concept saves you
space, installation costs, and time. All
controls, including the drive converter panel,
electrical panels and transformers are prewired
and mounted directly onto the unit's base.

At Deltaplast, we don't limit ourselves to
standard extruder designs. Our engineering
capability gives us the flexibility to custom
build specialty designs based on your needs. For
example, should you have an existing competitor's
screw inventory, Deltaplast is able to supply
extruders which will accept competitive screw
shanks. Variation in extruder centerheights,
limited plant space and right-to-the left -to
right gearbox configurations are also available.

Testimonial:

"Deltaplast's electrical utilization concept
where all electrical panels, transformers, and
disconnects are mounted and prewired on the
extruder base, saved us time and money when we
recently moved to a new facility. This concept
allowed us to be up and running within a few
hours of delivery of the first machine. Our
customers never noticed any impact or delivery
problems due to the simplicity of our move."
