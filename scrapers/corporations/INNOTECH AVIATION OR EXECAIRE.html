Company Profile


INNOTECH AVIATION

Our large, modern facility at Pierre Elliot Trudeau Airport, Montreal, is a preferred completion center for Bombardier Global Express and Challenger aircraft.

Innotech Aviation is internationally known as the center for excellence. Innotech performs a wide variety of factory-authorized service for the worlds most popular business jets: airframe inspections, structures, powerplants, avionics, landing gear overhaul and hydraulics.

Innotech has superior refurb capability, excelling in cabinetry, upholstery and avionics. Recognized internationally for craftsmanship and style, we can engineer and design fresh interiors for older aircraft.

We can offer the latest technology in Avionics. Innotech is factory-authorized to service all major names in flight instrumentation, communication and navigation systems.

Innotech Aviation is a world leading facility catering to business aviation. Innotech Aviation is an Authorized Service Facility for the Bombadier Global Express, Global XRS, Global 5000, Bombardier Challenger 600, 601 and 604 and the Raytheon Hawker 800. Innotech Aviation is also an Authorized Completion Center for the Bombardier Global Express, Global XRS and Global 5000.

EXECAIRE

Execaire is Canadas longest serving and most prestigious corporate Aircraft Management Company, with over 50 years of service. Execaire is the only company in Canada that can manage the entire lifecycle of your aircraft through tailored solutions to meet your aircraft needs from its acquisition, to operation and charter, to maintenance and refurbishment, and ultimately to resale. Let Execaire be your corporate aircraft solution provider of choice.
