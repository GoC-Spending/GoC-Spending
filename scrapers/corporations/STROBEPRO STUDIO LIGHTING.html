Company Profile


STUDIO LIGHTING DONE DIFFERENT


Strobepro is Canada's best choice for professional quality studio lighting, photography equipment, strobes, light stands, backdrops, softbox, speedlite flash, umbrellas, background stands, continuous led lighting, reflectors and more at unbelievable prices.

SOLUTIONS FOR EVERYONE

The one thing we don't have are high prices. Compare our product against the competition, Alien Bees, Aurora, Elinchrom and more. We're not afraid, our lights compete with the best at often 50% less.

WHY ARE YOUR PRICES SO LOW?

Well, we feel they're fair. Why does photography have to be so expensive? It doesn't. We sell direct to you cutting off the middle man.

DO YOU HAVE A STORE?

Yes, for sure. All of our products are on display in our Calgary, Alberta showroom. Although we don't run a big retail store, you're more than welcome to see and test everything before you purchase.

WARRANTY

We stand behind our product. If something goes wrong, ship or bring it back. We'll fix it instantly or give you a loaner until it is repaired. Simple.

Product Name:
BACKGROUND STAND KIT WITH CASE
ALUE

At only $135, the STROBEPRO photography backdrop stand kit is an amazing value. Similar stands cost $289.

QUALITY

These 10 foot air cushioned stands are up to the challenge. Rolls of paper, no problem. The crossbars have push pin connectors making them easy to take apart. The crossbar metal inserts are also long and sturdy for your heaviest loads.

VERSATILITY

Works great with muslin backdrops or paper rolls. Sets up in under 3 minutes. Pack it up in the included carry bag when you're ready to go. Width can be 5', 7.5', or 10' wide.

LOOK PROFESSIONAL

Stop hanging bed sheets and take the first step toward pro. If you do any studio work or even family portraits, this is a must buy piece of photography equipment. Customers can't believe the quality for the price.

SIZE

10'x10' Max. Width can be 5', 7.5', or 10' Height can be adjusted from 50" to 10'.

INCLUDES

2 x10' Air Cushioned Light Stands
4 x quick release adjustable crossbars
2 x locking cap nuts
1 x carrying case with handle

Product Name:
ECL 400W LCD STUDIO FLASH LIGHTING KIT
ALL NEW

Building off the success of our best selling ECD strobe line, we've gone and made them even better.

DIGITAL LCD PANEL

Now you can see all your major settings in one location. Power, modelling light, triggering method and more.

BUILT IN RADIO TRIGGER

Have you ever had a light up in the air only to realize you need to adjust it again? No problem, we've solved that. The ECL series has a built in 2.4GHz raidio control that takes charge of all your settings. You can adjust power levels and modelling lights from one remote on multiple strobes.Remote sold seperate.

FASTER

We've made the ECL even faster with recycling times from 0.3 seconds to only 1 second at a full 600 watts.The flash duration has also increased.

FAN COOLED

The ECL is fan cooled with built in overheat protection

MODELLING LAMP

The ECL uses a 250W modelling lamp which helps distinguish shadows and make adjustments on set easily.

HIGH QUALITY GLASS

We use German made flash tubes by Perker Elmir. They provide clean consistent, perfectly balanced flash.

NO MORE BEEP

Many clients use strobes in situations where the recycling beep can be annoying. No problem, now you can turn it off.

FULL METAL CONSTRUCTION

The ECL has a full aluminum casing and metal mounts to hold the heaviest loads.

VALUE

Compare against Elinchrom or Alien Bees and you wont believe the performance and value. For less than the price of a Canon Nikon Sony Metz Pentax or Panasonic flash you can own a complete studio lighting kit and produce incredible results.

2 YEAR WARRANTY

Like all Strobepro strobes, you're covered with a full 2 year warranty.

FIT

All Strobepro strobes use the Bowens S-fit speedring.
