Company Profile


Info-Tech Research Group is a professional services firm
dedicated to providing premium research and objective advice to
IT managers of mid-sized organizations. Currently, we serve more
than 30,000 clients across North America and the U.K.

Our purpose is to provide practical and thorough solutions
that enable IT professionals to bridge the gap between technology and business. Our Web publications, reports, methodologies, benchmarking, vendor selection and consulting services all help IT professionals to keep current, to effectively manage people and technology, and to achieve professional success.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Info-Tech Consulting
Info-Tech Research Group provides information technology advice and information to over 30000 professionals throughout North America and Europe.

Through a constant stream of contact with their clients, Info-Tech understands the importance of high quality custom consulting. It is their belief that providing only the required technical support of any project is only the half way point to solid consulting practices. With the current bridging of technology and business strategy, Info-Tech places a strong focus on ensuring a smooth project that works to the benefit of an entire organization and not merely the IT department.

Info-Tech's strength is primarily based on the level of experience and professional development of their research staff and senior executives.

Market profile

Alliances:

Strategic alliances:

Info-Tech Research Group is interested in forging
partnerships to better serve our current clients, to leverage
our strengths in sales and marketing, and to develop new
business units.

Info-Tech Research Group has a loyal client base of over
30,000 technology professionals, a trained and motivated inside
sales force familiar with the needs of IT executives, and
experience in the development and implementation of direct
marketing campaigns through telephone, direct mail, and on-line
methods. As such, we can be a key partner in assisting
organizations seeking to market and sell IT-related products and
services to organizations across North America.

As a provider of advisory services, custom education, content
and research, Info-Tech is interested in partnering with
organizations that will expand our marketing reach. If you are
seeking to expand the range of services your organization
provides, and you believe our services may fit your needs,
please contact us. For authorized re-sellers, brokers and
suppliers of our products and services, we provide attractive
commission plans, and product training and support.

Info-Tech Research Group is committed to working with
professional associations to provide benefits including member
discounts, revenue sharing, and preferred access to our products
and services.

For corporations seeking to provide high-end technology advice
and information to their clients, partners and employees,
Info-Tech Research Group provides custom content services.
Content Partners can deliver our content through a variety of
models including syndication, custom writing, and private
branding of our advisory services. As a Content Partner, we'll
work with you to meet your technology content needs.
