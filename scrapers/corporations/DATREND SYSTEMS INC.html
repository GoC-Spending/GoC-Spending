Product Name:
Sensitest SpO2 Sensor Tester
Sensitest provides tests for all types of SpO2 probes in the biomedical and clinical environment. This compact devices draws power for two modes of testing from a single 9V Alkaline Battery

Product Name:
Infutest 2000 Infusion Pump Analyzer
Simultaneously inspect up to four single channel IV pumps or four independent channels of a multi-channel device in just seconds with our Infutest 2000 Series E and optional RSM. Better than 1% accuracy on all pumps, including Baxter Colleague and Alaris® Signature.

Conduct the full range of flow, pressure, volume and interval tests on practically any fluid delivery system including single rate, dual rate and patient controlled analgesia (PCA) devices. These tests are performed following easy-to-use manual or autosequence formats using the front panel keypad and graphical display.

Product Name:
Oxitest Plus 7 Pulse Oximeter Tester
The versatile, handheld Oxitest Plus7 provides the most advanced technology available in the market today for verifying the proper operation and performance of pulse oximeter monitors and modules throughout the clinical environment.

The Oxitest Plus 7 has an exclusive Nellcor-Puritan Bennett sensor port, which was developed in cooperation with Nellcor®. This Industry-exclusive feature allows correct selection of the instrument's simulation output to the specific R-Cal value of the attached Nellcor® probe

Product Name:
Infutest Solo Infusion Pump Analyzer
Infutest Solo meets the needs of customers who desire a highly accurate infusion device analyzer capable of a measuring infusion device output flow patterns, including pulsatile flows, but can manage their work load with a cost-effective single-channel analyzer.

Product Name:
20A ECG/Arrhythmia Simulator
Compact and lightweight, our ECG and Arrhythmia simulator is designed to fit your needs, tool case and budget! Use this simulators to test your patient monitors, telemetry systems and anywhere a quick ECG or Arrhythmia simulation is needed.

The versatile 6-lead configuration provides an additional pre-cordial lead and allows you to check reference leads without the need for a full 12-lead layout.

Product Name:
ES601 Plus Automated Electrical Safety Analyzer
ES601 Plus Automated Electrical Safety Analyzer. Automated & Manual Electrical Safety Testing per AAMI/ANSI ES-1, IEC 60601 and IEC 62353, and VDE 751 standards

Product Name:
Phase 3 Defibrillator and Trancutaneous Pacer Analyzer
Phase 3 is the first Defibrillator/Pacer Analyzer specifically designed to test Pulsed Multiphasic defibrillators. Test Monophasic, Biphasic and Pulsed Multiphasic Defibrillators, AEDs and Transcutaneous Pacers with better than 1% accuracy on either line or battery power.

Battery provides up to 24 hours or continuous operation and detachable paddle plate provides easy hands-free testing.

Product Name:
VLM - Variable Load Module
Accessory product to Phase 3. Variable Load Module allows testing defibrillators at maximum energy with selectable loads from 25 to 175 ohms in accordance with AAMI DF-80 and IEC 60601-2-4.

Product Name:
AMPS-1 Advanced Modular Patient Simulator
This compact Simulator packs a lot of punch in a small package. At only 7 X 4 X 1.25 inches, AMPS-1 fits comfortably in your hands and all leads and cables are conveniently fed from the top and bottom ends. Operate from the single 9 volt alkaline battery or with the optional battery eliminator.

The unique modular design of AMPS-1 allows you to get just what you need as you need it. Upgrading and adding modules is easily accomplished by you at your facility. No need to send it to a repair facility just to add features

Product Name:
IncuTest
Datrend Systems Inc. introduces Incutest, the first infant incubator and infant radiant warmer testing system meeting the requirements of the IEC Standards for incubators (IEC 60601-2-19) and radiant warmers (IEC 60601-2-21).

With Incutests unique wireless display and control capabilities, sensing can be done in the infant incubator or infant radiant warmer device under test. Results are viewed externally in real-time on the color graphics display.

For added convenience, changes to the test protocol can be made without disturbing the heating environment.

Product Name:
MPS-2
The MPS-2, a multiparameter simulator, may be the best value you can find in a dual-channel invasive blood pressure simulator on todays market. This little brother to our unique AMPS-1, a modular multiparameter simulator, comes in an even smaller package than the AMPS-1 and offers many similar features and capabilities in a fixed function, non-modular platform.

At just 5.5" x 3.6" x 1.3" and weighing in at only 11.5 ounces, the MPS-2 is a feature-packed multiparameter simulator in one of the smallest and lightest weight physical packages available. Its smaller, lighter, and more cost effective than any other similarly-featured multiparameter simulator on todays market

Product Name:
vPad-ES
The vPad series of electrical safety testing devices combines an Android tablet (wired or wireless) with a measurement platform to create a revolutionary test system. The measurement platform provides the normal assortment of electrical measurements (voltage, current and resistance) of the commonly used medical Electrical Safety Standards.

Automated and Manual Electrical Safety Testing per AAMI/ANSI ES-1 and IEC 60601 standards

Product Name:
vPad-353
The vPad series of electrical safety testing devices combines an Android tablet (wired or wireless) with a measurement platform to create a revolutionary test system. The measurement platform provides the normal assortment of electrical measurements (voltage, current and resistance) of the commonly used medical Electrical Safety Standards (IEC 62353, AS 3551, AAMI/ANSI ES-1 and IEC 60601 standards)

Product Name:
FMS-3 Fetal / Maternal Simulator
At just 5.5 x 3.6 x 1.3 and weighing in at only 11.5 ounces, the FMS-3 is a feature-packed Fetal/Maternal simulator in one of the smallest and lightest weight physical packages available. Its smaller, lighter and more cost effective than any other similarly-featured Fetal/Maternal simulator on todays market.

The FMS-3 Fetal/Maternal Simulator sets a new standard of performance and value in simulators for fetal monitors. Simply stated, the FMS-3 provides enhanced simulation capabilities, more simulation channels, additional functionality, and full 5-lead Maternal ECG simulation, all at a price that is considerably less than competitive models.

Its unique features include three (3) channels of ultrasound simulation for detection and monitoring of Triplets.

Technology profile

Datrend Systems Inc. is a Canadian based biomedical engineering company focused on developing and manufacturing leading edge biomedical instruments and devices for hospitals, clinics and medical equipment manufacturers.

Datrend subscribes to the regulatory standards of CSA, UL, and the EU. Products are designed and manufactured following the quality system principles of ISO9001:1994 and FDA QSR/cGMP. Additionally, Datrends Quality System is registered to ISO13485:2003.

Since our launch in 1991, we have expanded to serve global markets with innovative products.
