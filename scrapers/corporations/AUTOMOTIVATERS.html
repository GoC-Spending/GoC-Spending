Company Profile


Automotivaters is a leader in sales and management training. Since 1987 we have been committed to improving the image, CSI and sales performance of the retail automotive industry.

Our success is the result of providing our clients with exceptional training products and services that have produced real and measurable results. More volume, more gross, more fun.

Our clients range from single dealerships of all sizes to dealer associations and entire dealer networks in Canada, USA, New Zealand, Australia, the Philippines, Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, and French Polynesia.

Service Name:
Sales and Sales Management Training
Sales and sales management training, coaching, and consulting to automobile dealerships, dealer groups, and manufacturer retail networks. Increase sales volume, profit retention, and customer satisfaction.

Service Name:
Auto Manufacturer - Retail Network Assessment
Dealer group assessments are a powerful way to protect brand intergrity and maximize market share. This comprehensive program systematically evaluates all aspects of sales operations. Individual sales consultant evalutions, management personnel, and dealership process assessments. Our assessment tool quantifies performance against industry best practices. The final report includes gap analysis and detailed recommendations.

Implementation of corrective training programs and post training validation will increase volume, market share, profitability, and client satisfaction. Brand integrity is protected.

The assessment is highly customized to the dealer group or retailer network's priorities. However, a typical structure of the assessment looks like this:

Success stories:

The roots of Automotivaters go back to 1987 when Larry Smith started a sales training company called ISI International.

At the same time Gary Weidner and Gary Fisher had a competing training company called P.A.L. Performance Group.

The friendly competitors combined training materials, accountability tools, and human resources in 1995 and merged to form ISI / P.A.L. Automotivaters Inc.
