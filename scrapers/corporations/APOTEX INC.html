Company Profile


Manufacturer of over 300 generic pharmaceuticals. Founded in
1974. Research at Apotex includes the development of both
generic pharmaceuticals and innovative products. R&D
expenditures for Apotex represented close to 20% of sales placing
Apotex in the top 12 research and development companies
in Canada .It is the #1 R & D in the pharma industry.
Through the Apotex Foundation, Apotex also provides substantial
financial support for research to hospitals, universities and
other organizations throughout Canada and the world. The
company's pharmaceuticals can be found in virtually every
pharmacy and Healthcare facility in Canada and are exported to
115 countries around the globe. Apotex has established
a presence through subsidiaries, joint ventures or licensing
agreements. Apotex is a member of the Alliance of Manufacturers
&
Exporters Canada.
