Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
YESAIR 8 Channel Air Quality Monitoring
YESAIR offers more features, flexibility and functionality than any other IAQ instrument on the market today with multi-channel operation, more than 30 different plug and play sensors to select from, including a particulate sensor, huge capacity data logging to SD flash card and much more.

Positive sampling with internal pump ensures good representative samples are taken from all target areas or with diffusion for a quick, direct reading.

Seven sensors are viewed live on the multi-line, backlit, LCD display as well as battery and data logging status.

With a few simple mouse clicks, users can analyze and graph data using the optional YES Viewer PC software and an SD card reader. Alternatively, data could be downloaded through the instrument using a standard USB connection.

Product Name:
YES Plus LGA 15 Channel Air Quality Monitor
YES Plus LGA is a multi-gas air quality monitoring and information recording instrument that simultaneously measures and records temperature, RH, CO2, and much more.

Featuring a 15-sensor capacity, the YES Plus LGA allows users to accurately monitor more within a single, easy to carry instrument. It contains 12 internal plug & play sensors, a fixed temperature sensor, a removable RH sensor, and a remote sensor, such as particulates. Sensors available include electrochemical, catalytic (combustibles % LEL), infrared (PPM or % volume CO2 or combustibles), and PID.

With a few simple mouse clicks, users can analyze and graph data using the optional YES Viewer PC software and an SD card reader. Alternatively, data could be downloaded through the instrument using a standard USB connection.

Product Name:
AST-IS CO2 Transmitters
AST-IS transmitters output a linear 4-20 mA or 0-10 VDC signal that can be sent to a Building Automation System or to a CETCI controller. The transmitter contains an infrared sensor for accurate, reliable carbon dioxide (CO2) detection. The standard housing is an attractive offfice mount enclosure. Duct mount and digitial display versions are available.

Product Name:
YESDUST Affordable Particulate Sensor
YESDUST is a general purpose, low-cost, hand-held, battery powered particulate sensor designed to be used in conjunction with the YESAIR or YES Plus LGA IAQ monitor.

It operates from its own internal rechargeable nickel hydride batteries and connects to the auxiliary port of the YESAIR or YES Plus LGA with the supplied interface cable.

The measured readings are displayed as another sensor on the LCD display of the YESAIR or YES Plus LGA. The readings are also data logged to the YESAIR or YES Plus LGA internal flash card, if utilized.

Product Name:
PET BACnet Transmitters
PET BACnet® digital transmitter is a Parkade Emissions Transmitter that communicates via BACnet® protocol MS/TP. It is available with one or two electrochemical sensors or solid-state sensors and temperature sensor, enclosed in a general purpose ABS enclosure.

Electrochemical sensors available include ammonia (NH3), chlorine (Cl2), carbon monoxide (CO), ethylene (C2H4), formaldehyde (HCHO), hydrogen (H2), hydrogen sulphide (H2S), nitric oxide (NO), nitrogen dioxide (NO2), oxygen (O2), ozone (O3), and sulphur dioxide (SO2). Solid state sensors available include combustibles and refrigerants.

Featuring thermal resetting fuse, tri-color LED indicator for power and alarm, and an optional 4-digit LED display. Includes new sensor CEF (Calibration Extending Firmware) that takes into account the aging of the sensors so that less frequent calibrations are acceptable in non-critical applications.

Calibration and maintenance procedures are easily performed in the field without requiring access to the controller.

For more information on the BTL certification, visit BACnet International.

Product Name:
LPT Low Power Transmitter
The LPT is an economical, single sensor, analog transmitter available with either a carbon monoxide (CO) or nitrogen dioxide (NO2) sensor. For small or large applications, the LPT can be used as a remote sensor or as part of a networked system with a controller. Features include a standard water / dust tight, corrosion resistant ABS/polycarbonate enclosure that is IP54 rated with the optional splash guard installed, a visual LED indicator for power and fault conditions and a linear 4-20 mA output signal. Ideal applications: vehicle exhaust type applications such as parking garages, repair shops, maintenance facilities, etc.

Product Name:
YES IMS 9 Channel Air Quality Monitoring
YES IMS is a self-contained, indoor air quality (IAQ) Intelligent Monitoring System with nine channel sensor capacity including relative humidity (RH) and temperature.

Options include a fixed wall mount or a portable desktop version with a wide range of sensors to choose from, including a particulate, electrochemical, catalytic, infrared, and PID. The fixed wall mount version can be permanently wired with 24 VAC or DC and has one relay output for alarm signaling. The portable desktop version comes with a 6 V wall adapter for 24 / 7 continuous monitoring and logging.

YES IMS has an integrated 2 GB logging memory that will store over a years worth of readings when logging once per minute. Data is saved in Microsoft® Excel friendly CSV format and can be extracted from the unit with a standard USB connection. Users can then analyze and graph data using the optional data logging software.

A common IAQ package consists of temperature, RH, CO, CO2, particulates, and TVOCs.

Product Name:
LPT-A Analog Transmitter
The LPT-A is a full featured, standalone single sensor, analog transmitter available with a range of gas sensor types including, electrochemical, solid state and catalytic. The LPT-A may also be networked with a fixed system controller and act as a remote sensor. Features include an LCD digital display with user selectable function, audible alarm, one standard dry contact relay, linear 4-20 mA or 0-10 volt output and an optional heater for cold environment applications. Idea applications: commercial ice arenas, indoor swimming pools, parking garages, repair shops, fish hatcheries, battery charging rooms, etc.

Product Name:
SCC Self Contained Controller
The SCC is a one or two channel, self-contained controller for monitoring carbon monoxide (CO) and/or nitrogen dioxide (NO2) or combustible gases (hydrogen, propane and methane) in vehicle exhaust type applications. Features include LED indicators for power, channel status and fault conditions, two 5-amp dry contact relays, field configurable time delays and trigger levels and a field configurable integral audible alarm. The SCC may be combined with an LPT or catalytic ESH-A Remote Sensor for more versatile monitoring coverage. Ideal applications: small to mid-size parking garages, auto repair shops, battery charging rooms, buildings on landfill sites, etc.

Product Name:
DCC Dual Channel Controller
The DCC is a one or two channel, self-contained controller for monitoring toxic, combustible and refrigerant gases with either internal and/or remote sensor configurations. Features include LCD display with LED indicators, internal audible alarm with an optional loud, door mounted buzzer, two 4-20 mA outputs and one 4-20 mA input, two standard dry contact relays and field configurable time delays and trigger levels. The DCC may be combined with an LPT, LPT-A, CXT or AST-IS transmitter or an ESH-A Remote Sensor for more versatile monitoring coverage. Ideal applications: parking garages, fire halls, commercial ice arenas, indoor swimming pools, fish hatcheries, etc.

Product Name:
QCC Quad Channel Controller
The QCC is a field programmable controller that offers four gas channel inputs from digital and/or analog transmitters for monitoring toxic, combustible and refrigerant gases in commercial, institutional and light industrial gas detection applications. It offers customizable configurations with logic control to meet individual application requirements. Features include standard Modbus RS-485 communication, LCD display with LED indicators, three dry contact relays, an audible alarm and an optional analog output module with data logging capabilities. Ideal applications: mid to large size parking garages, waste water treatment plants, commercial ice arenas, indoor swimming pools, etc.

Product Name:
ART Infrared Refrigerant Transmitter
The ART is a refrigerant gas detector with infrared sensor technology that can be configured to detect a specific refrigerant gas or groups of several refrigerants. Featuring accurate, low-level leak detection, maintenance-free operation and an easy-to-use interface, the ART can be deployed as a standalone system for small applications or integrated into a building automation system with a Modbus RTU interface. Non-dispersive, infrared sensing technology makes the ART immune to cross sensitivities with combustible or toxic gases, as well as changes in temperature and humidity. Ideal applications: supermarkets, commercial chiller plants, food storage/processing plants, wineries, schools, hospitals, maintenance facilities, etc.

Product Name:
CXT Explosion Proof Transmitter
The CXT is a heavy duty, explosion proof gas detector for reliable and accurate monitoring of toxic and combustible gases in potentially explosive and harsh environments including hazardous-rated areas. Non-intrusive calibration procedures allows for uninterrupted in-the-field maintenance. Further features include large graphic LDC display, heated sensors and electronics for low temperature operation, three relays and a menu driven magnetic keypad for user configuration. Sensor technologies include electrochemical, catalytic or infrared. Ideal applications: oil refineries, drilling rigs on oil fields, loading facilities for flammable gases, liquids and solids, municipal treatment plants, chemical storage rooms, etc.

Product Name:
DCC-MRI Oxygen Sampling System
The DCC-MRI is a self-contained Oxygen (O2) monitoring system developed for Magnetic Resonance imaging (MRI) room applications for hospitals and clinics. Due to the strong magnetic field inside the MRI room, electronic equipment does not operate properly when mounted inside the room. The DCC-MRI system is designed to be installed outside of the MRI room with a sample tubing running from the monitoring system to the sampled environment. Features include two 4 - 20 mA outputs, one alarm level line voltage relay, a blocked flow alarm indicating a dirty filter / clogged tubing, a side mounted audible alarm and an LCD digital display with LED indicators for channel alarm status and fault conditions. This system is accurate enough to measure to government established exposure levels for O2 deficiency for health and safety.

Product Name:
ESH-A Remote Sensor
The ESH-A Remote Sensor offers flexible location monitoring of combustible, refrigerant or TVOC gases in non-hazardous, non-explosion rated, commercial and light industrial applications. It communicates with a controller or transmitter using a current loop into a 100 ohm load or a voltage signal of 0.40 to 2 volts. It can be located at a maximum distance of 200 meters from the controller or transmitter. The ESH-A Remote Sensor comes in a standard water / dust tight, corrosion resistant ABS / polycarbonate enclosure with a hinged, secure door and is designed to protect the sensor and provide easy mounting components. An optional splash guard may be installed to further protect the remote sensor in water spray or washdown applications.

Product Name:
LPT-A-VLT Ammonia Vent Line Transmitter
The LPT-A-VLT-NH3-S Ammonia Vent Line Transmitter is designed for continuous monitoring of ammonia levels in the vent line of refrigeration systems; more specifically to detect equipment failure in ammonia plant vent stack applications. Using the 3/4” cast steel coupler, it can be mounted indoors on the ammonia vent relief stack above the pressure relief valve or mounted outside on the relief header above the roofline. It can be configured to alarm at user specified concentration levels and send an analog signal to the controller or PLC to activate the emergency ventilation system or shut down the equipment depending on the level of alarm. It offers 3-wire VDC or 4-wire VAC power, jumper selectable 4 - 20 mA or 0 - 10 VDC analog output, one dry contact relay, a back lit LCD display and audible alarm.

Product Name:
LPT-M Digital Transmitter
The LPT-M is a full featured, three channel, digital transmitter available with a range of gas sensor types including, electrochemical, solid state and catalytic. It may be used as a standalone system or networked with a fixed system controller. It offers a Modbus® RS-485 output signal for communicating with a controller or a building automation system, an audible internal alarm, one dry contact relay, LCD display with user selectable functions, linear 4-20 mA or 0-10 volt output and an optional heater for cold environment applications. Idea applications: commercial ice arenas, indoor swimming pools, parking garages, repair shops, manufacturing plants, battery charging rooms, etc.

Features include two 4 - 20 mA outputs, one alarm level line voltage relay, a blocked flow alarm indicating a dirty filter / clogged tubing, a side mounted audible alarm and an LCD digital display with LED indicators for channel alarm status and fault conditions. This system is accurate enough to measure to government established exposure levels for O2 deficiency for health and safety.

Product Name:
ESH-A Remote Sensor
The ESH-A Remote Sensor offers flexible location monitoring of combustible, refrigerant or TVOC gases in non-hazardous, non-explosion rated, commercial and light industrial applications. It communicates with a controller or transmitter using a current loop into a 100 ohm load or a voltage signal of 0.40 to 2 volts. It can be located at a maximum distance of 200 meters from the controller or transmitter. The ESH-A Remote Sensor comes in a standard water / dust tight, corrosion resistant ABS / polycarbonate enclosure with a hinged, secure door and is designed to protect the sensor and provide easy mounting components. An optional splash guard may be installed to further protect the remote sensor in water spray or washdown applications.

Product Name:
LPT-A-VLT Ammonia Vent Line Transmitter
The LPT-A-VLT-NH3-S Ammonia Vent Line Transmitter is designed for continuous monitoring of ammonia levels in the vent line of refrigeration systems; more specifically to detect equipment failure in ammonia plant vent stack applications. Using the 3/4” cast steel coupler, it can be mounted indoors on the ammonia vent relief stack above the pressure relief valve or mounted outside on the relief header above the roofline. It can be configured to alarm at user specified concentration levels and send an analog signal to the controller or PLC to activate the emergency ventilation system or shut down the equipment depending on the level of alarm. It offers 3-wire VDC or 4-wire VAC power, jumper selectable 4 - 20 mA or 0 - 10 VDC analog output, one dry contact relay, a back lit LCD display and audible alarm.

Product Name:
LPT-M Digital Transmitter
The LPT-M is a full featured, three channel, digital transmitter available with a range of gas sensor types including, electrochemical, solid state and catalytic. It may be used as a standalone system or networked with a fixed system controller. It offers a Modbus® RS-485 output signal for communicating with a controller or a building automation system, an audible internal alarm, one dry contact relay, LCD display with user selectable functions, linear 4-20 mA or 0-10 volt output and an optional heater for cold environment applications. Idea applications: commercial ice arenas, indoor swimming pools, parking garages, repair shops, manufacturing plants, battery charging rooms, etc.
