This write-up is intended to provide a brief overview of
Accutech Engineering Inc.

Accutech Engineering Inc. is a multi-disciplinary engineering
and project / construction management company, headquartered in
Canada, with activities in Canada, the United States, and Latin
America.

We specialize in providing custom technical solutions to our
clients; these solutions are developed in accordance with the
specific challenges of the project being considered. We
recognize that the correct solution must be a combination of
technological, operational and common sense approaches.

Our areas of interest are varied, and can be summarized as
including most industries where the utilization of specialized
engineering design & management and construction / fabrication
expertise is required.

We are a complete solution company; we can help you through
the entire process from concept development through to actual
implementation. Implementation may include the development of a
new product or the construction / rehabilitation of facilities
to accommodate a process or need.

We are familiar with and can utilize almost any type of project
delivery methodology currently in use.

Accutech Engineering Inc. provides services to a wide variety of
clients including both Public and Private Organizations,
Corporations & Individuals; and we are proud to be of service to
Canadas First Nations Communities.

Forensic Engineering / Technical Claims

Accutech Engineering has significant expertise in providing engineering investigations and expert testimony.

Over the past decade there has been an unprecedented escalation in the frequency and complexity of technical claims and litigation related to the failure of construction, equipment and systems.

These failures range from the catastrophic collapse of a building to the inability of a system to perform at the expected or warranted level.

Many claims are advanced on the basis of detailed expert technical reports and investigations.
In most instances independent specialist consulting engineers must carry out these reports.

Accutech Engineering has the special expertise and courtroom experience that you need in defending or advancing a claim, or just trying to evaluate the causes of a problem.
These types of claims may include:

 Catastrophic Building Failures
 Parkade Deterioration
 Building Component Failure
 Personal Injury
 Construction Disputes
 Building Envelope Problems
 Accidents; Vehicular or Equipment
 Computer System Power Surge
 System Failure to Meet Performance
 Safety Issues
 Water Damage Claims
 Fire Claims
 Vibration Problems
 Industrial Equipment / Explosions
