Company Profile


We help customers design and document their process based management systems including that systems:
- Framework,
- System archecture, including:
- Policy Documents
- Procedure Documents
- Work Instruction Documents,
including the provision of all needed advice and support to the customers staff for them to competently document processes to achieve objectives.

Where contracted we will collect information and data then produce - Policies, Procedures and Work instructions.

We help customers achieve their registration needs for Quality, Environmental and Safety Management Systems to meet
- ISO 9001 Quality Management System,
- ISO 14001 Environmental Management System and
- ISO 18001 Safety Management System and any CSA standard through:
- Design;
- Documentation;
- Consulting;
- Audits;
- GAP Analysis;
- Critical Review of Documents (Internal and supplier)

We provide Process, Quality, Environmental, and Safety Management Training including Auditor Training.

We evaluate Supplier's for their ability to satisfy product and service quality and provide ongoing support through Audits, Monitoring, Control, Inspection, expediting.

Broker: Machining, Metal Fabrication - Your best option to get the best value from your purchasing dollar.
