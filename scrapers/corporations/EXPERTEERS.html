Company Profile


We are passionate engineers who are working to make a difference in telecom contracting services in order to enable our customers to focus on their strategic objectives such as developing new revenue streams, gain and maintain market share and competitive advantages:

• Experteers is a well-established provider of contracting and consulting services to Canadian Telecom and IT industries.
• Experteers has highly calibre teams to cater for the increasing demand of telecom and IT projects implementation.
• Through our services we enable our customers to focus on their strategic objectives such as developing new revenue streams, gain and maintain market share and competitive advantages.
• As a responsible member of the global Telecom eco-system, Experteers is committed to a set of high-standard values and to fulfilling its social and environmental responsibilities in the community, thus contributing to long term sustainability.
• Experteers is a financially sound company.
MISSION STATEMENT
We provide engineering and contracting services to Canadian Telecom and IT industries, by attracting and retaining highly caliber teams to enable our customers to focus on their strategic objectives.
VISION STATEMENT
To be the most valuable service provider for telecom and IT companies operating in Canada by enabling our clients to lead, stay current and achieve total excellence.

Company description

541330 - Engineering Services

Services  

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Telecommunications Engineering and Implementation Services
I. Pre-Engineering:
1. Line of Sight Verification: Experteers highly-qualified teams develop the method of procedure (MOP) to verify microwave line of sight on defined positions and altitudes in a high technical methodology. The teams provided with GPS, Compass, Binocular, and high-res cameras, can verify any line of sight in different terrains.
In the case of LOS prior to building the site, our team developed a unique MOP to walk the path to verify the LOS with more than 99% accuracy. Determining required tower heights, path and propagation notes. Providing an LOS report according to customer’s need including: site data, coordinates, height, address, proposed tower/mast height, LOS confirmation, azimuth and elevation, path description, photographs,…etc.

2. Site Initial Survey: Performing site surveys for candidate sites and provide a site survey report including site data, coordinates, height, address, proposed equipment and mast location, antenna azimuth, tilt & elevation and other data as required by customers.

3. Site Requirement Definition: Our deep understanding of telecom equipment and requirement, we provide full study with designs for new telecom, switching and electrical equipment installation and commissioning for new and existing sites as well as upgrade and downgrade requirements.
II. Engineering:
1. Civil Engineering
a. Civil Design: Design structural civil work (Steel, Towers, Platforms, Mounts,..) for rooftop and green field sites, according to client’s needs and applicable codes. Prepare structural and steel fabrication, AutoCAD drawings and BOMs.
b. Civil Inspection: Inspect civil implementation and provide compliance / deficiency report and suggest corrective actions.
c. Load Verification: Verify existing structures and loads and report deficiencies and provide appropriate solutions.
d. Reinforcement: Tower, Rooftop: Provide a reinforcement studies for tower and rooftop sites.
e. Civil Work Orders: Preparing formal consent with detailed instructions, work specifications, time frame and cost estimates to be used a blanket authorization for contractors and internal suppliers to perform civil work for telecom sites.

2. Electrical Design
a. Main AC Control Panels: Design according to ESA requirements
b. DC power bays designs: Batteries, Rectifiers, Racks/Cable Ladders, and Wiring
c. Power Saving studies and PUE Calculation: Providing full studies for site power consumption and power usage effectiveness and preparing comprehensive power saving plans.
d. DC Power Upgrade Work Orders: Preparing Work Orders for rectifier and battery upgrades.

3. Electro-Mechanical Design
a. HVAC Design: We provide Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning designs for telecom sites as well as enhancing the efficiency for the existing site HVAC systems by using smart controllers that takes over the control of the economizer from the traditional thermostat and used it to save operational cost.
b. Free Cooling Design: We provide smart free cooling solutions to manage site temperature by using the naturally cool air. This can save up to 90 % of the energy used to control the temperature of the telecom sites, thus reducing operating expenses drastically compared to the use traditional air conditioners.

4. Telecom Engineering:
a. Equipment Engineering Design:
• Preparing power, space and cooling designs and technical specifications for new and existing telecom sites.
• Making technical evaluations of installed equipment to ensure compliance with the technical specifications.
• Providing material test reports required for qualification of material used in site installation.
• Developing procedures for testing and inspecting of received equipment.
b. Transmission Design: MW link budget calculations, and frequency planning, Fiber and Copper transmission design.
c. Develop MOP: Providing detailed procedures and work instructions to perform end-to-end telecom functions starting from pre-engineering through implementation to network operations and maintenance.
d. Telecom & MW Work Orders: Preparing formal consent with detailed instructions, work specifications, time frame and cost estimates to be used a blanket authorization for contractors and internal suppliers to perform TI and microwave installations.
e. Provisioning & Testing Work Orders: Preparing written procedures with detailed instructions and work specifications for provisioning and testing of telecom components.
III. Implementation:
1. Civil Installation:
a. New site build: Providing civil installation for new sites including slabs, shelters, masts, steel, mounts, pipes and conduits for telecom sites with high quality and good time frames to meet our customers targets.
b. Steel work: We provide and install required steel construction for new and existing tower and rooftop telecom sites including fabrication, galvanization and installation of masts, chairs, mounts, as well as concrete slabs.

2. Telecom Installation and Commissioning
a. Radio: Providing full TI services for BTS, Node B. and LTE sites including power cables, data cables, fiber patch cords, racks, batteries, power up, commissioning and testing.
b. Transmission: Microwave installation and testing, fiber cables and multiplexers installation and other transmission media.
c. Switching (CS / PS)
d. Data Centers

3. Electrical Power Installation:
a. AC Control Panels: Supplying and installation of AC control Panels according to ESA requirements.
b. DC panels and components: Supplying reliable DC panels and components
c. DC power bays: Batteries, Rectifiers, Racks/Cable Ladders, and Wiring
d. DC Power Upgrades: Rectifier and Battery upgrades

4. Electro-Mechanical:
a. HVAC Installation: We provide and install high efficiency HVAC systems for telecom sites, as well as enhancing the efficiency for the existing site HVAC systems by using smart controllers that takes over the control of the economizer from the traditional thermostat and used it to save operational cost.
b. Free Cooling Installation: We provide smart free cooling solutions to manage site temperature by using the naturally cool air. This can save up to 90 % of the energy used to control the temperature of the telecom sites, thus reducing operating expenses drastically compared to the use traditional air conditioners.
c. Control site ambient environment: Temperature, humidity, air flow, and air filtering of telecom sites.

5. Testing Services:
a. Power up and Function: Inspecting connections and installation specification of telecom installations, powering up and test functionality of installed equipment.
b. SWEEP: Performing sweep testing for fiber cables, LMR400 cables, feeders, jumpers and antennas.
c. BERT: Performing RFC 2544, BERT testing.
d. Signal & RF: Performing drive testing for wireless signal coverage.
e. Fiber, OTDR: Testing of fiber cables and installations and OTDR testing.
f. PIM: Testing of passive intermodulation (PIM)

6. Implementation Management:
a. PMO: Providing a centralized project management office for large scale projects
b. Planning: Preparing project plans, scope and documents to guide the execution and control of telecom network implementation and expansion projects.
c. Quality Control: Formulating written procedures and measure to control the execution of telecom network implementation and expansion projects.
d. Warehouse Management: Providing methodology and facilities to manage and control storing, staging and delivery of equipment, cables and consumables for telecom network implementation and expansion projects.
IV. Post Implementation
1. Site Optimization
a. Adding and integrating new telecom equipment to existing networks
b. Telecom upgrade/downgrade
c. Adjusting installed equipment for live network (radios, MW, antennas, cables and fibers)
d. Dismantling and decommissioning telecom equipment
2. Telecom site maintenance: Performing routine visits and check-ups and corrective visits to including environment, equipment and cabling of telecom sites.
3. Quality Audit: Inspecting site installations and providing detailed conformance reports.
4. As Built CADing: Preparing as built drawings for site installations.
5. Parameter Verification Survey: Checking the compliance of antenna azimuth, tilt and coordinates to design.
6. RF Drive Testing (indoor / outdoor): Providing teams and fully equipped cars for outdoor drive testing and collecting wireless coverage data and providing findings report.
7. Site Inventory: Performing inventory check to existing sites.
8. Commissioning Inspection: checking the parameters of commissioned telecom equipment and sites and comparing them to design documents and provide corrective actions.

Service Name:
Free Cooling on Wireless Telecommunications Sites
Monitor environmental parameters and use free cooling technology with an M2M controller to control the Airconditioning units on sites and use direct fans to cool the indoor temperature to make saving on hydro consumption
