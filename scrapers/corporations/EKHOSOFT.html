Company Profile


Ekhosoft specializes in the design and integration of innovative, highly scalable production data management solutions. Benefiting from an experienced team, they create industrial and manufacturing process management solutions based on an open architecture that offers compatibility with almost any type of system, and can handle an unlimited number of users.

Ekhosoft is a leading supplier of Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) solutions to the Process Industries. The company offers data management and control systems that can be completely configured and customized according to industry specifications, raw materials, finished goods, and the complexity of plants and labs.

The Ekho family of strategic software applications has supplied successful Quality, Overall Equipment Efficiency, Product Genealogy, Material Management solutions to a wide variety of industry and manufacturing requirements. Emphasizing a Customer-Supplier Partnership approach to business, Ekhosoft sets the standard for continuous product and customer support.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
EKHO
Are you concerned about controlling the quality of your finished goods, standardizing your formulas, or justifying material losses?

Would you like to monitor, document, or stabilize the up and down times for all your automated processes and equipment, and pinpoint the source of recurring breakdowns?

Would you like to easily track down the origin of defective batches or production delays, keep a record of the raw materials used, or simply observe the tasks performed at each stage of production?

Are you looking at improving the global earning rate or your plants?

Welcome to the Ekho platform line of solutions! Each product provides a single solution that features all of the functionality required to access, input, configure, correlate, share, analyze, and distribute industrial data. No matter what your information management requirements, Ekho tool windows can be configured to your operations, and to each user's requirements.

Ekho solutions also have the power to display any type of document (work schedule, laboratory analysis, photo, video, quality standards manual, etc.), regardless of what system it was created on.

A Choice That Pays Off
Efficient industrial data management gives your business a substantial competitive edge all the way down the production line, as well as increases customer satisfaction. Our solutions offer you:

 Standardization and maintenance of quality norms

 Standardization and maintenance of delivery lead times

 Fewer losses of raw materials or finished goods, resulting in a potentially higher profit margin

 Fewer recurring breakdowns and breakings

 Fast troubleshooting

 More efficient teamwork

 Shorter processes

 Lower production costs possible as a result of overall productivity gain

 Customer retention

Sector information

Unique applications:

Tembec uses the Ekho platform to manage performance parameters at 50 mills Tembec has deployed the Ekho data management platform in all of its operating units. The purpose of this initiative is to facilitate analysis by centralizing the collection of performance indicators in a single system and standardizing the data input models at all of its its 50 mills. Tembec installed the Spek application, which simplifies the control and monitoring of production settings while enabling that management of data entry and display modes from a central point as the data is input from end to end at its mills. In addition to Spek, Tembec opted for two other Ekho applications: Look, the customized report manager software tool, which organizes information according to each authorized users settings; and Link, which it plans to use for in-depth data analysis. The most substantial benefits that Tembec will derive from using Ekho relate to man-hour savings, particularly in terms of preparing performance reports, which used to take days, but now involves only a few hours of work.

Corporate Agreement with Tembec Industries

We chose Ekho because its cost to functionality ratio exceeds any other application available in the market today. Due to Tembec's quick growth over the last number of years, integrating our site's manufacturing information at a corporate level is a challenge. Ekho's shop-floor data management capabilities and its flexibility to integrate to many different technologies at the same time makes it the perfect fit for our entire organization; small plants to large integrated facilities. Director - Information Technology
Pulp & Paper + Chemical Groups
Tembec Industries

Ekho supports the preventive efforts of Abitibi-Consolidated

"The Synkro system is of great value to Abitibi-Consolidated in ensuring the quality of the Companys preventive communication efforts in its mills. Management and departments are now able to pinpoint problems involving health and safety factors. Abitibi-Consolidated is a world leader in health and safety, and wholeheartedly recommends Synkro for any business wishing to improve individual and organizational attitudes and behaviours. Our Company has been saving $30 million per year in CSST contributions over the past 10 years through the use of mechanisms such as Response Reflex. Response Reflex is only one of the factors that has contributed to these savings, but is nonetheless important to our overall strategy for managing health and safety for all our employees. In order to ensure the success of this type of preventive mechanism, an organization needs a technologically advanced high-performance management system, and preferably one that comes at a reasonable cost."

François Brassard
Corporate Coordinator, Health & Safety and Financial Systems
Abitibi-Consolidated Inc.
