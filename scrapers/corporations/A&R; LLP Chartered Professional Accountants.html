Unique applications:

A&R LLP partners have their training as Management Accountants and have long experience in industry as cost accountants that make them unique compared to other accounting firms. Partner of A&R LLP help their clients in their annual budget preparation, able to develop Bill of Materials, calculate product cost and do variance analysis for their manufacturing clients.

Key / Major clients:

Food Companies, Consulting companies, Automotive Companies, IT consultants, Custom Home Builders, General Contractors, Health Care Centres.

Success stories:

A&R LLP has history of retention of clients year over year because of personalized service. A health care client who was paying huge fee but was having issues with their inventory system A&R LLP took over the client and resolved all their inventory system issues.
There are many other examples to quote...
