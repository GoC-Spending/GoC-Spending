Company Profile


Conceived from its inception as a Software-as-a-Service provider for independent schools, corporations and institutions seeking next-generation Web services look to REAL IO to create a rich online presence and dynamic, targeted communication tool. Our core Content Management Platform allows for modular growth into all aspects of organization administration, and our open API enables us to connect with companies whose cutting-edge products complement our own.

REAL IO helps companies make their web presence felt with our experts in web development & marketing team. With its web focused team has become a single stop for all IT needs of our clients. This team focused on providing highly creative solutions to our clients can help them with,

- Creative and aesthetic web designs
- Complete document and content managed websites
- Complete ecommerce & Secured payment systems
- Advanced custom programming
- High rankings on search engines for high visibility

Our strength lies in being able to work with available CMS (Content Management Systems) and advanced eCommerce open source frameworks like Magento, osCommerce, Wordpress, WebGUI, Joomla and preparing the sites as per our clients' needs keeping the costs lower by re-using maximum of the available components.

REAL IO provides complete Web marketing services and solutions which may range from designing stand alone websites to managing the rankings and promoting the websites faster on the web. Our team carries experience of managing various clients based globally and many domains ranking high in various search engines with support of highly lucrative designs by our designers.

At REAL IO our skilled analysts and engineers are fully aware of the rapidly changing scenarios and requirements of various search engines like Google, MSN, Yahoo etc. and are well equipped to promote your websites and businesses online within time lines, supported by efficient designers and content writers continuously for producing faster changes as per the changing market and search engine requirements.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
ResourceFusion
ResourceFusion is the Event and Conference Room Management System that has been specifically designed to support a multi-site / multi-venue environment on a real time basis. ResourceFusion is a highly scalable application that is capable to change with the growth and demands of the user requirements, and can be set up to operate through the Intranet and over the Internet. ResourceFusion event and conference room management system is comprised of, and compatible with technologies currently available in the marketplace, offering quality and reliability with respect to e-communication, database management, and overall functionality.

ResourceFusion enables responsible staff to make changes in real time, so that the system reflects the most up to the minute information concerning space and resource availability in order to ensure the integrity of any reservations and requests for resources made.

ResourceFusion provides capability to easily change variables such as names, group classifications, corporate identification such as logos, symbols, headers, font type, etc. This event and conference room management solution allows for growth by providing options to build upon current databases and program configuration.

Security access to system modules and database is controlled by the application administrator. The user authentication system includes multiple levels of security and allows staff access to the system and various parts such as screens, modules, etc. and based on individualized clearance levels (role based security). There is a degree of flexibility to add or delete employees from the administration module with no downtime experienced in the operation of the system.

ResourceFusion Features:

- Dynamically create unlimited number of rooms/calendars with customized categories, headers and footers.
- Inline rich text editor generates dynamic Web Page event content
on-the-fly.
- Search or filter bookings and events by keywords, categories and time ranges.
- View each room/calendar monthly, weekly or daily.
- Multiple authorization levels (read-only, user, admin).
- Online event registration for seminars, presentations, etc.
- Online resource booking (meeting rooms and equipment).
- List event attendees(roster).
- Update attendee information.
- Broadcast e-mail to all attendees for an event.
- Dynamic announcements via admin-controlled running ticker.
- Synchronize event/reservation information with Outlook and/or Palm
and Pocket PC

Product Name:
EventFusion

Since EventFusion is web-based and users can access the software from a browser, you don't need a dedicated person to manage or install software on every single PC in the office. You can utilize EventFusion solutions with existing hardware and personnel.

Technology profile

Programming.

Our team of developers is focused primarily on sophisticated IT software development, including Web-enabled database projects and complex solutions, which require data integration from various sources.

We have been using advanced technologies to meet the growing requirements posed by the customers.

Programming languages and development technologies:
Java, Enterprise JavaBeans, C, C++, Delphi, Perl, Tcl/Tk, php3/4, JavaScript; XML, XSL and XSLT

Databases:
Oracle (versions 7/8i) is our favorite DBMS for serious projects but we are also experienced in using MS SQL, Interbase, MySQL, PostgreSQL.

Web application servers:
BEA Weblogic; IBM WebSphere; Allaire ColdFusion (Versions 4/4.5); SUN Netscape iPlanet; Oracle WebDB; Orion; Resin; Tomcat...

Content Management Systems:
WebGUI CMS

Operating systems:
All Unix-like operation systems (Solaris, Linux, etc.); Windows NT/2000

Graphic design.

Designers of our studio are not limited to development of graphic interfaces and web sites design. Creation of systems of corporate style of visual identifications (development of the trade mark, the corporate block, business cards, letters, envelopes and other forms of representation of coprporate style), development of promotional materials, both for Internet, and for offline print materials, - not complete list of possibilities of the design branch of our studio. Add to this operation with multimedia, Macromedia Flash and digital video and you get the approximate vision of what our designers can do.

In summary - few words about cost of our services. We consciously decided not to include a "Services and Fees" section, so often seen on competitor's sites. Our reasoning? We don't sell pre-fabricated one-size-fits-all Web solutions. The scope and complexity of a project and the kinds of staff and technical resources involved will all depend on what you would like to achieve through your website.
