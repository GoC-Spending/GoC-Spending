Company Profile


GPR has over fifty years experience in the Peace Region supplying and servicing electric motors and generators. We are a leader in Northwestern Canada by being one of the largest and best equipped service center in the north. We host many trades including Electric Motor System Technicians, Electricians, Heavy Duty Mechanics, Millwrights, Machinists and Parts.

GPR strives to be the One Stop Shop by utilizing some of the most advanced equipment in the industry. We are focused on getting the job done whether in-house or with field service and this attitude has made us the solution provider for our valued customers. GPR has founded and built our reputation on Western Canadian industries such as Oil and Gas, Forestry, Mining, Agriculture, Construction and Utilities. We have branded ourselves with quality manufacturers best suited to service our customers, both for small and large scale projects. A culture of dedication, commitment and advancement has been the key to GPRs success over the years and our companys sustainability depends on this culture.
"We Keep Industry Running"

Service Name:
Vibration Analysis
Unscheduled downtime may cost you thousands of dollars per hour. Our Vibration Analysis Program can evaluate the condition of your equipment which can save you money. The Benefit of our Vibration Analysis Program is that it can determine if your equipment is running within specification and avoid failures by recognizing and advising you of conditions that will reduce your equipment life. Our Vibration Analysis Program can also predict developing problems before they affect your process. GPR can inform you when a bearing fault, looseness or electric motor rotor bar fault develops and then assist your planning department in the critical timing of repair before it shuts you down.

Service Name:
Laser Alignment
Sometimes even the slightest misalignment can mean a big loss in efficiency and/or production. GPR industries can provide professional laser alignment for direct drive or belt drive applications. Not only will this save you from production downtime, it will minimize your energy loss and increase bearing and seal life  saving you time and money.

Service Name:
Oil Analysis
Oil Analysis is another way that we can detect early indications of problems before they become serious. An effective oil lubrication analysis can help us find unseen corrosion and wear and is an economical step in keeping your equipment running in top condition.

Service Name:
Asset Management
GPR can provide experienced support to document, review and recommend maintenance intervals, identify critical spares, store and maintain your spare motors and even disperse your unwanted motors and or generators.


Service Name:
Maintenance Shutdown Service
We have field crews available to assist or complete the entire project for your Maintenance Shutdowns. We have Millwrights and Electric Motor Technicians for inspection, servicing, electrical testing, bearing changes, laser alignment or removal and replacements. We can also provide lubrication technicians.

Service Name:
Electric Motor Field Service
When time or the expense of removing your electric motor doesnt allow it to come in for service, we have Journeyman Electric Motor System Technicians, Millwrights and Electricians to accomplish testing and/or repairs in the field including bearing changes and motor efficiency testing. We can also remove and replace the motor for you, providing picker service as required. Preventive Maintenance of Babbitt bearing motors is another of our specialized services.

Service Name:
Generator Field Service
5,000 hour and 10,000 hour Preventive Maintenance Packages have proven successful and cost effective to our many Prime Power and Stand-By Power customers. Additionally we have professionally equipped Field Service Trucks ready to tackle everything from planned maintenance to emergency shutdown repairs of your generators.
