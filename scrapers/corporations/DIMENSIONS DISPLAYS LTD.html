Company Profile


At D4 Displays we take pride in designing and manufacturing contemporary portable exhibits that combined with stunning graphics help you, our clients effectively display your brands and messages at trade shows all around the world.

Our passion is helping your company succeed in achieving it's face to face marketing goals by providing you with an extensive range of award winning portable displays. Our displays won these awards because they are:
Displays That Maximize Visual Impact
Displays That Are Dependable And Easy To Set Up
Displays That Represent Good Value For Money

D4 Displays works with distributors who are located across north america to ensure that our products are supported by reliable , professional service.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
D3 Display
The D3 Display is an Award Winning freestanding system that uses innovative LED technology to create a state of the art Lightscreen that is only 0.25" thick, with very low energy consumption. The D3 Lightscreen will add brightness and intensity to your graphics. Graphic images can be changed in seconds with the snap fit magnetic cover.

The D3 Lightscreen can be combined with a variety of different literature dispensing options as well as product shelves to create the perfect solution for your next face to face marketing event.



Images are easy to insert and change and will have intense vibrant colour.


Product Name:
D3 Media Display
The D3 Media - Portable Technology

This unique freestanding multi media display incorporates a low voltage 17" Flat screen TFT display with it's own internal drive. This avoids the necessity for additional DVD player or computer equipment. The D3 Media is completely remote controlled and supports Divx and AVI Movie formats as well as MP3 Music files and JPG images to create slide shows.

The display has an overall height of 65" and weighs only 17lbs making it a truly portable solution for Trade Shows, Exhibitions, Events, Retail Environments, Conferences & Seminars.

The D3 Media Display - Illuminated Thinking

Product Name:
D5 Display
The D5 Display is the latest step forwards in light weight modular displays. Combining recyclable aluminum frames with seamless large format photo realistic fabric graphics.

The D5 Display is available in a variety of Flat, Curved, Serpentine and
Arced formats up to 20 feet long, that can be easily assembled by one person in a matter of minutes without any tools.

One complete 10 foot wide D5 Display packed into it's Travel bag weighs only 29 lbs, making it the perfect solution for your display requirements at any Trade Show or Event.

The D5 Display also has a range of accessories available and can be combined with both the D3 Lightscreen and D3 Media LCD display.

Service Name:
D4 Banner display stands
The D4 banner display system is designed to create a stylish display that allows visual messages to be installed quickly. The system breaks down into a small portable travelbag and is unique in being a cable tension display, developed specifically for printed fabrics. This is benificial in several ways;
*Banners can be changed with ease
*Banner size can be varied to suit graphic requirements
*Printed fabrics are washable for longer life

The D4 is completly self supporting and can be assembled by one
person in less than 5 minutes. The D4 display can be configured in a range of sizes to create different displays to fit different spaces.

Start with one D4 stand and by adding link units, brochures dispensers, lighting, you can build it into a complete retail display or trade show exhibit system as your company and marketing budget grows.
