Company Profile


Meggitt Training Systems (MTS) is the leading supplier of integrated live fire and simulation weapons training systems supporting military, defense and security forces, federal and international law enforcement agencies, large metropolitan training academies, thousands of police departments, ammunition and firearms manufacturers and commercial shooting range owners.

Meggitt’s Crewed Vehicle Training Systems are available in a variety of solutions comprising networked or stand-alone solutions that replicate the vehicle’s crew compartment, turret and fire control system in both physical and functional aspects. The design can be in either permanent, semi-permanent, appended or an embedded solution hosted on the vehicle’s processors and sub-systems.

From small arms, close air support, armoured fighting vehicles and full motion platforms, the MTS technological base spans the world of virtual training. Our commitment to research and development ensures that customers receive the latest technology tailored to meet their training needs. MTS was the first company to introduce wireless BlueFire® firearms that utilize Bluetooth® technology to communicate with system hardware while maintaining accurate weight, balance, recoil and real-time diagnostics.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Weapon Training Solutions
As the leader in the simulation training field, Meggitt Training Systems develops, manufactures, installs and supports small and combined arms training systems and corresponding weapon simulators for military, paramilitary and security agencies world-wide. We specialize in integrating Commercial-Off-The-Shelf (COTS) hardware and software products into realistic training systems for:

- Marksmanship
- Crew and collective missions
- Engagement skills
- Mission rehearsal
- Gunnery
- Convoy and armored vehicles
- Artillery and mortars
- Forward Observer (FO) and Joint Terminal Attack Controller (JTAC)

To date we have fielded over 5,100 Small Arms Trainers (SATs), fielded over 40,000 laser-based weapon simulators (including over 250 types of weapons) and are deployed in more than 130 countries.




Product Name:
Shooting Range Solutions
Meggitt Training Systems is the oldest provider of live fire range equipment in North America, formerly known under the Caswell International heritage.

Meggitt Training Systems brings nearly 90 years of indoor gun range design, development, installation and support experience to every job site. Whether you are a retail shop owner looking to build your first fire arms training system, or a current range owner in need of an upgrade or maintenance to your existing simulation system, we have all the resources you need under one roof to guide you through the processes of commercial range design, development and management.

Meggitt Training Systems also offers integrated Virtual and Live Fire solutions.


Product Name:
Armoured Fighting Vehicle Simulators and Trainers
Meggitt Training Systems Armored Fighting Vehicle Trainers supports Driver as well as Crew Gunnery and Tactical training. Using realistic scenarios, the LAVT provides soldiers the opportunity to practice situations they normally face in combat situations.

Meggitts simulation capabilities enable crews to train target acquisition, identification and weapons engagement procedures using both primary and alternate fire control and sighting systems. The targets and scenarios provide for both mobile and stationary interactive threats, in single and multiple target arrays, in a realistic battlefield environment during day, night and reduced visibility conditions. All systems permit for capture of key data and events for playback and debriefing purposes as an element of the After Action Review Process.

Training systems are available in a variety of applications - Embedded, Stand-alone, Appended and PC/Laptop based systems.

Product Name:
Full Motion Platforms
Meggitt Training Systems Motion Integrated Training System (MITS) provides both individual and crew training for boat/vehicle handling and weapons training.

The motion platforms can simulate real world movement of both waterborne vessels and land-based vehicles, providing heightened training opportunities in changing environments. Meggitts MITS is available in a variety of options, from small single platform configurations to multi-platform configurations that are synchronized and offer command, control and communication functions for leadership and command training.

Product Name:
Integrated Simulation and Training Facilities
Meggitt Training Systems offers a 3D Interactive Virtual Environment for Collective Training comprising the integration such capabilities as Firing Simulation Trainers; Boat/Vehicle Tactical Trainers, 6 DOF Electric Hydraulics, and panoramic screens. All in a solution that is fully reconfigurable and capable o operating independently or collectively. We also provide integrated Command and Control Centre solutions with Instructor Operating Stations, Multiscreen AAR Debriefing Facilities and Viewing Galleries.
