Company Profile


Having a domain and a website equates to "being on the net", but does this suffice? If you are planning to launch a commercial website to display, promote and eventually sell your products or services, your website should have a unique, eye-catching design, straightforward navigation and, more importantly, it needs to set you apart from your online competition!

Jeff Boulton the owner of Reactive Designs has years of experience in website design and development and graphic design. Services include: responsive website layout design, eCommerce integration, search engine optimization (SEO) and social media integration. We specialize in using the Open Source Joomla and WordPress content management systems. We have clients located throughout Canada including small and medium sized businesses. We also develop logos and print business cards on a variety of mediums.

Reactive Designs also specializes in online business backups. Data is backed up to an offsite server daily from servers, workstations, laptops, phones and tablets. Our software encrypts the data through each step of the backup process ensuring the highest level of security.

Our hosting environment is rock solid providing your business fast, reliable, and redundant Canadian hosting. We provide CDP – continual data protection with account information backed-up to an offsite server in Canada. We monitor who visits your site, how often they visit and have security measures in place to help ensure your website is not compromised. Websites today are complex utilizing various technologies – it is important that the server has the capacity (software and hardware) to run the website. Reactive Designs dedicated servers have been created for only Reactive Designs clients for a stable, secure hosting environment.
