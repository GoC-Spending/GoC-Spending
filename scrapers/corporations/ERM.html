Company Profile


Rescan(TM) Environmental Services Ltd. (Rescan) is a multidisciplinary Canadian consulting firm with over 20 years of project experience spanning 45 countries worldwide. Rescan has established itself as a leader in the provision of innovative, cost effective environmental and engineering services for the resource industries around the world. Our services assist industries, institutions and governments in achieving sound environmental practices. While traditionally most of our work has focused on the mining sector, we offer an extensive project portfolio covering a diverse list of resource industries. We have successfully completed numerous environmental projects involving diverse environmental baseline studies, management planning, oceanographic studies and audits and assessments.


The services we offer are aimed at assisting companies in achieving two primary goals: ensuring development is carried out in an environmentally sound manner and ensuring that companies receive the necessary environmental approvals to proceed with projects.


Rescan is a global provider of: environmental audits and impact assessments; environmental management; aquatic, oceanographic, fisheries, terrestrial and atmospheric biophysical studies; integrated coastal zone management; remedial investigations; due diligence evaluations; plant decommissioning, reclamation and closure planning; water and wastewater treatment; coastal and fluvial hydraulics, and physical and numerical modeling.


OUR EXPERIENCE


Visit us at www.rescan.com for a full list of the services that we provide.
The following categories summarize the services we provide:


ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT ASSESSMENT


Comprised of: archaeological and heritage resource evaluations, aquatic resources, climatology, meteorology, air quality, hydrogeology, land use analysis, water quality studies, socioeconomic assessment, soil and terrain studies, surface and groundwater hydrology, fisheries monitoring, water quality and hydrology monitoring, risk analysis and access route/site selection vegetation and wildlife assessment.


BIOPHYSICAL BASELINE STUDIES


Encompassing: aquatic habitat assessment, bathymetric/seismic survey, biological assessment, coastal infrastructure and hydraulic design, conductivity, temperature and depth (CTD) profiling, dye tracer studies, ecological monitoring, effluent transport modeling, fisheries, vegetation and wildlife assessment, ocean current measurements, ocean circulation modeling, seawater corrosion and material wear characteristics, sea, lake and brash ice assessment, trace heavy metal sampling and analysis, tidal height and current measurement and prediction.


MINE WASTE MANAGEMENT


Comprised of: tailings dam and waste dump design, oceanography, tailings management, slurry pipeline engineering, acid rock drainage assessment and control, cyanide destruction, contaminant dispersion simulation, erosion control, mercury problem assessment, metallurgical effluent treatment, reclamation and re-vegetation of mine tailings, mine decommissioning, hazardous complex waste treatment, site investigations and remediation - Phase I,II,III, spill contingency planning, and surface runoff treatment.


ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT


Including: management of information systems, project and program reviews, policies and procedures, issue-specific programs, management organization, oversight and control, day-to-day compliance management, communications, planning procedures, risk assessment/risk management, emergency preparedness and regulatory tracking and influence.


INTEGRATED COASTAL ZONE MANAGEMENT


Involving: communication with participants of the integrated management process; coastal biology and ecological processes; coastal physical oceanography, geochemistry; and water column chemistry; numerical modeling of effluent dispersion and sediment transport; protection and cataloguing of sensitive coastal areas including coral reefs, sea grass beds, mangrove and other wetland areas.


WATER AND WASTE WATER TREATMENT


Entailing: systems for water usage minimization, removal and recovery of heavy metals, sludge volume reduction systems, cyanide destruction systems, chromium removal/recycling, removal of sulphide and recycling of NaHS from de-hairing liquor, production of NaSH from spent tanning bath, removal of nitrogen by biological nitrification/denitrification, acid rock drainage treatment, High Density Sludge (HDS) systems and biosuphide treatment and heavy metal recovery.


OUR GLOBAL ASSIGNMENTS


Rescan has considerable Canadian and international business experience. We are currently active in British Columbia, Northwest Territories, Nunavut, Labrador, U.S.A., Chile, Dominican Republic, New Caledonia, Indonesia and Argentina. We have worked in 45 countries around the world including Papua New Guinea, Australia, China, the Philippines, Norway, Fiji, Africa, Venezuela, Brazil, and Mexico to name a few. Our larger clients include BHP Minerals Inc., Falconbridge, INCO, American Barrick, Placer Dome Inc., Homestake Mineral Development, Teck Corp., RTZ, Minera Escondida Ltda., Cambior Inc., Cominco Resources International, INMET Canada, Noranda Minerals Inc., Manalta Coal, Gulf Canada and ASARCO.

Direct sales, joint ventures and strategic alliances.
Public and private sector institutions and corporations requiring environmental services.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Transport of sediments to deep ocean floor via submarine pipeline systems.
We have extensive international experience in designing, installing and monitoring submarine slurry or sediment pipeline systems for deep ocean placement of solid particulates. Through our specialization in submarine tailings placement (STP) for mines in northern, temperate and tropical areas of the world, we have well-developed expertise in disciplines directly applicable to the siting, permitting, design and installation of overland and underwater pipelines, outfalls and diffusers for domestic and industrial wastes and cooling water intakes and outfalls.

In most instances, STP projects have involved studies of coastal oceanography, including current and wave measurements, dye dispersion studies and plume modelling, biological inventories, fisheries assessments and chemical characterization of receiving-waters, natural sediments and tailings. These investigations have supported the analysis and prediction of environmental impacts. Various onshore facilities have required site selection involving weather and climate, soil and terrain analyses, biological characterization and socio-economic and archaeological studies.

This experience is directly applicable to the development of port facilities, marine pipeline terminals and onshore infrastructure. Rescan offers both the environmental scientific and engineering experience required to ensure compliance with relevant regulations and the functional design requirements of diverse coastal projects.

Product Name:
Design and installation of water treatment removal systems including bio-treatment.
We offer a comprehensive consulting and design service in the fields of physical, chemical and bio-chemical treatment process to remove inorganic and organic contaminants from waste to meet discharge criteria. Our principal focus in water treatment includes solid /liquid separation to remove particulates and chemical and biochemical processes to remove contaminants such as ammonia, chlorine, dissolved metals, sulphates, etc.

Rescan can also provide assistance with the design, permitting, construction monitoring and eventual closure of facilities.

Product Name:
Design programs; manage and conduct data gathering and analysis; and prepare and defend reports and applications for permits and licenses.
Rescan Environmental Services has completed numerous assignments covering the fields of environmental engineering, metallurgy, chemistry, biology, oceanography, geology, hydrology, socioeconomics, and resource planning. Rescan offers the necessary expertise in government liaison and project management to complete Environmental Impact Assessments.

We coordinate reports involving biophysical baseline studies comprising of assessments of climate, water, groundwater and surface hydrology, air quality, fisheries, wildlife and vegetation, soils and terrain, archaeology, and socioeconomics. Through our specialization in submarine tailings placements (STP) for mining projects in the northern, temperate and tropical areas of the world, we have acquired expertise in disciplines directly applicable to the siting, permitting, design and installation of overland and underwater pipelines, outfalls and diffusers for domestic and industrial wastes and cooling water intakes and outfalls.

Additionally, Rescan develops impact mitigation and resources management plans as well as comprehensive mine waste management measures to meet prevailing regulatory requirements in coastal and inland projects. Environmental management also being one of our specializations, we have considerable experience in liaison with regulatory authorities both in Canada and abroad.
