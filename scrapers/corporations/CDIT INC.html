Company Profile


AUTOMATING BUSINESS PROCESSES FOR INCREASED PRODUCTIVITY,
REDUCED COSTS AND ENHANCED COMPETITIVE POSITIONING.

In todays information-driven economy, the key to remaining
competitive is finding innovative ways to improve the efficiency
of your business processes  to compete more effectively, while
maintaining operational costs for greater long-term
profitability.

For years, CDIT has been helping leading organizations define
and implement innovative Enterprise Content Management
Solutions that reduce operational costs and enhance competitive
positioning, by maximizing the availability and value of the
critical information that drives key business processes.

By streamlining the flow and management of information and
knowledge within the organization, leveraging Imaging,
Workflow, and Document Management technologies, CDITs Solutions position organizations to realize optimized
organizational performance, increased productivity, increased
profit margins, improved service delivery, greater sales volumes
and increased market share.

Speed, dexterity, and responsiveness are critical to the success
of organizations operating in an increasingly competitive and
dynamic marketplace. Browse the CDIT website (www.cdit.com) or
give us a call (416-640-0706) to find out how an Enterprise Content Management Solution can position your organization
to realize these critical success factors.

CDIT has been a proud Fujitsu Canada Value Added Reseller (VAR)
since 1995. During this time, CDIT has consistently found
Fujitsus line of products to provide optimum quality and
performance for its clients.

As a result of this relationship and commitment to Fujitsu
products, CDIT has remained one of the Top Ten Fujitsu Resellers in Canada.

CDIT has been a proud Platinum Certified Reseller (ACR) since

match their client's needs with one of the best document and
data capture packages in the industry.

As a result of this dedication to Kofax's Capture
products, CDIT continues to be awarded recognition by Kofax, year after year.

CDIT is a proud partner of global leader, Basware Inc.'s Enterprise Purchase to Pay and Financial Management solutions. We focus on implementing Accounts Payable automation solutions that provide our clients with immediate ROI.

Strategic alliances:

AIIM International
The Association for Information and Image Management (AIIM)
International leads the world in bringing together the users and
providers of document and information management solutions.
AIIM's focus is helping users understand how these technologies
can be applied to improve critical business processes. It does
this by serving as the voice of the industry; providing a
worldwide forum for information exchange; and providing an
industry showcase so that users can anticipate, understand, and
apply next-generation technologies. An ANSI-accredited
organization, AIIM is the universal home for standards
development, and is the umbrella organization for industry
coalitions working together to develop worldwide
interoperability standards.
CDIT is proud to be a long-standing member of AIIM International.
