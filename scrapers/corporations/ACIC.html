Company Profile


Founded in 1974, ACIC has evolved into a full service pharmaceutical and fine chemical company providing specialized products and support services to our clients. The main business activity is the supply of APIs to Global pharmaceutical companies focusing on North American markets. ACIC offers an extensive range of products on an exclusive basis from cGMP and FDA approved manufacturers. Through our global network we offer custom synthesis/fermentation, research batches, clinical batches, commercial batches, scale up and process optimization. We provide complete regulatory service and support to all of our manufacturing partners including cGMP plant inspections and analytical development services. ACIC is also actively involved in complete dosage form marketing and manufacturing solutions. We are interested in discussing joint development opportunities or acquisition of products and/or technologies for introduction to world markets. ACIC puts decades of experience and knowledge on your side. We help you form the best submission strategy and fast-track your products to market. We invite you to speak with us to learn more about what we have to offer.
