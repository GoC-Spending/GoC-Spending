Company Profile


Embvue has been supplying products, tools and services to help its customers design, produce, field and support high-integrity embedded systems and software for aerospace, defense and transportation markets worldwide since 2002.

Embvue provides a diverse, yet integrated, suite of products, tools and systems/software engineering services that enable the specification, development, production, verification and validation, and certification of systems and software under RTCA/DO-178, RTCA/DO-254, Def Stan 055/056, IEC61508, ISO/DIS26262, EN50128 guidelines for use in both mission-critical and safety-critical environments.

More information on Embvue's products and services can be obtained by contacting Embvue or by visiting our website.

Systems Engineering Services
Embvue produces high integrity systems and software solutions for aerospace, defense and transportation markets worldwide. We have developed an extensive and credible array of skills that enable us to deliver trusted solutions for use in both mission-critical and safety-critical environments.

Embvue is a truly multi-disciplined engineering company with a very broad skill set that can be applied at
any stage of a products lifecycle, from initial design evaluation and consultancy, through the delivery of
complete turnkey systems solutions.

Our customers can leverage our team in a wide range of different ways, including:
 Full Lifecycle Software and Systems Development
 Firmware Development
 Software and Systems Architecture Design
 Object-Oriented Development
 Structured Analysis and Design
 Testing & Verification
 Hardware/Software/Systems Integration
 Requirements Traceability
 Configuration Management
 Quality Assurance
 Standards-Oriented Development (DO-178, DO-254, Def Stan 055/056, IEC 61508, EN50128, ISO/DIS26262..)

Our project teams provide a variety of capabilities that our customers can leverage in a wide range of different ways, including:
 Project Planning and Project Management
 Architecture Definition
 Trade Studies
 Peer Reviews & Mentoring
 Requirements Definition
 High and Low Level Software, Hardware and Systems Design
 Real-Time, Embedded Software, Hardware and Systems Development
 Systems Integration & Acceptance
 Quality Assurance
 Designated Engineering Representative (DER) Support
 Post-Delivery Support (On-Site and Remote)

Our test and verification capabilities include:
 Requirements-To-Test Traceability
 Requirements Testability Analysis
 Requirements Validation
 Source-To-Object Verification
 Software and Hardware Unit Test
 Integrated Software Test
 Structural Coverage Testing
 System Test
 Software, Hardware and Systems Failure Analysis
 Test Plans, Procedures and Reports Generation

Additionally we can provide support for:
 Custom Build Processes
 Development and Delivery of Custom Test Tools and Automated Test Environments (Qualifiable as Verification Tools to Level A) that maximize; (a) the speed of test execution, (b) the effectiveness of the tests, and (c) test coverage.

Embvue provides its project teams with the tools they need to efficiently specify, design, manage, deliver, validate and support turnkey certifiable embedded systems. Customers are free to decide to engage with Embvue for all, or just a portion of their project lifecycle. Whether the need is to temporarily augment a customer project team to accelerate a project and/or reduce risk, to add a specific set of hard-to-find skills that complements a customers own, or to find a reliable and cost-effective partner to assist with or deliver a total product solution, Embvue has the expertise and the resources to help.

Embvue has extensive certifiable project experience covering software, hardware and systems/sub-systems and has a track record of delivering certifiable projects up through Design Assurance Level A on-time and within budget. Embvues customers return to them for follow-on certifiable work time and again, and Embvue prides itself on this fact.

In addition to undertaking the specification, design and development of certifiable systems and subsystems, producing the deliverable products and the Level A qualifiable automated test environments to ease the system or unit test burden on its customers, Embvue can assist its customers in the production of the following certification artifacts, providing a one-stop-shop for the outsourcing of any or all aspects of certifiable products development:

Plans
 Plan for Software Aspects of Certification (PSAC)
 Plan for Hardware Aspects of Certification (PHAC)
 Software Development Plan (SDP)
 Hardware Development Plan (HDP)
 Software Verification Plan (SVP)
 Hardware Verification Plan (HVP)
 Software Configuration Management Plan (SCMP)
 Hardware Configuration Management Plan (HCMP)
 Software Quality Assurance Plan (SQAP)
 Hardware Quality Assurance Plan (HQAP)
 Tool Qualification Plan (TQP)

First and foremost, Embvue listens to its customers in order to develop and customize product and services options that our customers need and find compelling.

Embvues activities are strictly process-driven, and we have well-proven internal processes that we use. We are, however, happy to work with our customers to find ways to customize our own processes and the tools we use to better suit their own, making integration and supplier management considerably easier for them.

Our people are our greatest asset, dedicated professionals that are committed to delivering world-class
solutions to our customers. Based upon each individual customers needs, we assemble flexible, multi-disciplined project teams that combine exactly the required mix of project management, quality and
engineering expertise to deliver on-time, within budget and with the minimum of fuss.

By making it easy for our customers to leverage our skills to suit their specific business and technical
needs, and by tailoring our processes and tools to integrate well with our customers own, we have developed an outstanding reputation for delivering timely and cost-effective solutions for environments where regulatory compliance is often a necessity and risk is definitely to be avoided.
