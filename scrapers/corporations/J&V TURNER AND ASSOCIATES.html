The RECRUITMENT ALTERNATIVE SERVICES

Tired of paying 20% to 30 % commission fees
to Headhunters who dont deliver the talent they promise?
Now you have a viable option that works:

Introducing the 10% Recruitment Alternative
that delivers the key management talent youre looking for
at a fraction of the cost charged by traditional Headhunter!

Finding and retaining key management talent is crucial to the long-term growth and success of your business.but at what cost?

Paying large commissions to recruiters for key personnel can be a gamble for an independent business. What if the individual, although professionally qualified, doesnt fit the culture or personality of your organization? What if they get a better offer and move on after a few months? Ouch! Now there is a better way.

Our 10% Recruitment Alternative
is specifically designed to meet the recruitment needs of independent business by providing the key management talent that fits your
organizations unique needs.and your recruitment budget!

We understand that each organization has its own unique key talent skill needs as well as its own culture, personality, values and priorities. Putting the square peg in the square hole is about finding the individual that will meet all your requirements while answering to your recruitment budget demands.

At J & V Turner and Associates, we have more than 25 years of experience in providing recruitment and Human Resource solutions for organizations of all size and across most industry sectors. That is why we developed this unique Recruitment Alternative that delivers the key management talent your business demands while saving you time, money and aggravation. Our unique, Recruitment Alternative process provides your business with and effective and affordable solution that delivers results!





Heres how our process will help save you time, recruiting dollars
and hire the people that meet your companys specific needs:
 First, we will help you define your needs and develop a job / candidate profile.
 Then you will have the benefit of our expertise in developing your recruitment plan and selecting advertising media for posting.
 J & V Turner will then collect the resumes, revue, screen and evaluate potential candidates, and conduct interviews with a minimum of five (5) qualified candidates, either at your offices or outside if you prefer anonymity.
 We will then do a final evaluation and screening of candidates including thorough career background checks, education verification, interview and check references and a security examination.
 Once we are satisfied your criteria have been met, we will select and present two qualified candidates to your hiring committee for final examination. Of course well work with you every step of the way and provide written evaluations of candidates and our professional recommendations.
 Our fees are set well below recruitment agency rates, at a cost-effective 10% of the yearly salary for all positions recruited.

OUR GUARANTEE:
If the employee newly recruited employee leaves your company for any reason whatsoever, within the first three (3) months from the date of hire, we will replace the employee at absolutely NO ADDITIONAL COST to you.

Take Advantage of the J & V Turner and Associates
10% Recruitment Alternative Today!

Please take a few minutes to read the enclosed brochure and discover how we can help you meet your recruitment needs and the full line of Human Resources solutions weve designed specifically to answer the requirements of growing independent businesses like yours.

I would be pleased to meet with you at your convenience to discuss your companys unique recruitment needs and answer any questions you may have. Of course, any information provided is held in the strictest confidence. I look forward to meeting with you in the near future.

Yours truly,



Jim Turner
President
