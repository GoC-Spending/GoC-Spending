Company Profile


HyperNet has built a network of strategic alliances with a number of leading edge Internet-based companies. Through many of the current relationships that HyperNet has developed and continues to maintain, we are able to add value to the breadth of our online capabilities for our clients.

HyperNet has been a member of the Microsoft Developers Network since its inception. From the latest Site Server Technologies, Active Server Pages to MicroSoft's Visual Studio for content management, HyperNet offers a variety of compatible development platforms that keep your projects next generation and state-of-the-art. HyperNet is a recognized Beta site for testing numerous Microsoft software products and is well equipped with a team of Microsoft Certified Systems Engineers (MCSE) on staff.

HyperNet is a Value-Added Reseller (VAR) for INEX, the industry leader in Internet electronic commerce applications. INEX offers cost-effective, secure E-commerce storefronts tailored to meet your online needs. Whether you are a large international organization or small business owner  HyperNet has access to all the tools necessary to help you quickly and easily set up and manage your own business on the Web. As one of the leaders in developing custom templates for the INEX e-commerce packages, HyperNet can customize your solution to with a wide variety of features  all designed to maximize your return from your investment.

MPACT Immedia allow for payment and order fulfilment software that simply plug in to HyperNet's online business E-commerce and E-trade frameworks  contact HyperNet about how we can streamline all of your commercial payment and order processes.

For dynamic web page development, HyperNet uses Allaire's product, Cold Fusion. HyperNet is a recognized Beta test site for other Allaire products. As one of the first users in Canada to use Cold Fusion, we have amassed a gifted team of solution providers abreast in using the Allaire family of products.

Product Name:
Orwell - Web-based Project & Work Management Software
ORWELL is the web-based workflow software that supports the
executive interest.

As an executive management tool, Orwell tracks and analyses
employee productivity, project lifecycle, workflow, security,
operations management, cashflows and expenses - all in real
time!

Orwell has built a strong business case, a software product that
has been five years in development and testing, with over ten
years of implementation for many of the component modules.
Orwell has been implemented in whole or in part in the following
industry sectors: Government; Telecommunications;
Manufacturing; Financial Services; Information Technology;
Corporate Services; Personnel Services; and Entertainment, to
name only a few.

Originally Orwell evolved from a track record of performance
built upon servicing HyperNet's internal business reporting
needs. In its entirety, Orwell has set a new standard for staff
and project accountability in operations management that are
unparalleled in the software industry.
Orwell has made significant contributions by reducing costs on
projects and associated overheads resulting in increased overall
operational profitability, management efficiency and production
throughput times. Global organizations across a wide range of
vertical markets have employed our software to maintain control
over their bottom-line efficiencies.

Orwell's remote access features allow for a distributed
application through a flexible web-based platform - ideal for
home-based workers, the automated workforce, travelling
executives and on-site consultants.

To find out how Orwell can support your fight to control costs
while increasing profits, please e-mail us at
inquiries@hypernetmail.com.

Our goal  to offer a closed knowledge network that facilitates
the free and secure exchange of intelligence, trade or
capital. Through our global partnering expertise, HyperNet
defines international crossroads that facilitate R&D, buying,
selling and marketing initiatives by establishing online
business alliances for our clients.

HyperNet extends the scope of opportunities that can be realized
through pursuing electronic trading environments online. Through
Dynamic Online Frameworks, HyperNet encourages business-to-
business opportunities that allow companies within North
America, Asia or Europe the freedom to merge, control and
exchange their electronic business processes with any company
within Canada that provides a strategic business or economic
advantage for their firm. The core objective is to emphasis a
secure environment for shared business objectives, planning, and
management tools among trading partners characterized by
electronic process interchange (EPI). For additional context,
please visit www.mncan.com or www.saeinterchange.com. as
reference material on the next generation of online procurement,
purchasing and partnering frameworks. These products were
produced in coordination with the Canadian Consulate General and
Aboriginal Business Canada respectively.
