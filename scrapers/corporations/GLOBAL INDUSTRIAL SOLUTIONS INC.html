Company Profile


Global Industrial Solutions, Inc. an exporter of industrial products having office in Canada. Being one of the leading internationally recognized firm specializing in the supply of materials and equipment for the industrial terrain including Oil, Gas, Petrochemical, Power, Agriculture, Construction and Communication.

Here is a brief list of our industrial product range:

* Control Devices
* Data Control Systems & Process Logic Control / Analyzers
* Industrial Battery Charger & Cells
* Industrial Filters
* Industrial Instrumentation & Parts
* Insulation & Refractory Supplies
* Laboratory Instruments and Supplies
* Machine, Hand & Power Tool
* Measuring & Inspection Tools
* Motors & Generators
* Regulating Control Devices / Valves
* Relays & Control
* Stainless & Alloys Products
* Switchgear & Circuit Breakers
* Test & Measuring Instrumentation
* Transformers / UPS System (Industrial)

Some of the major manufacturers we deal with and supply their products are as follows: ABB, Alfa Aesar, ASCO, Bentley Nevada, Cutler-Hammer, Ingersoll Rand, Fisher Control, EnerSys, Foxboro, GE, Hach, Honeywell, ITT Gould, Parker, Rosemount, S & C, Siemens, SKF, Stranley-Proto, Tyco, VWR, Weildmuller and many more. An extensive product and service detail is available on our company web site www.giscorp.ca
