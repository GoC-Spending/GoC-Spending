Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Over-Moulded - Custom Cables - Industrial

3-Ci's distinct moulds set us apart from the competition. With a unique sleek look, a potted aluminum ring, and clean aesthetic lines, our engineered over moulded cables offer our customers the perfect combination of durability and good looks.

3-Ci's products and services are designed to address industrial connectivity needs. Our rugged overmoulded cable assemblies serve some of the largest industries in the world, and can be found in some of the world's most challenging applications. From deep sea exploration to aerospace testing, 3-Ci is your one stop shop for cable and connections.
