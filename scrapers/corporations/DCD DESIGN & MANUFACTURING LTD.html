Company Profile


DCD Design & Manufacturing Ltd. designs, engineers and manufactures Underground and Aerial Cable installation tools as well as Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) down-hole equipment used for pullback of utilities. Our head office and manufacturing facility is located in Vancouver, BC, Canada. We ship to the USA and worldwide.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
HDD Pullback Swivels
Our DUB products are used primarily in the horizontal directional drilling market. DCD carries a large range of product and sizes to fit every contractors demand.

Product Name:
Line Swivel
DCD Line Swivels are typically constructed with a thrust bearing for tensile load and a radial bearing for bending load. They combine a high safe working load with a compact design. All sizes incorporate an oil seal and are manufactured from high strength Stainless Steel.

All sizes are pull tested to verify the 3:1 safety factor and all (swiveling) sizes are fully capable of rotation at full working load. Test certificates are available upon request.

Product Name:
Breakaway Connector
The connectors are a three part product, held together by a group of pins. The pins are designed to break in tension at a specific load. The assembly of the connector is completed by using one or more of the breakaway pins. The sum of the values of the pins is the value at which the connector will come apart.

These products are designed to protect polyethylene ducting from overload during installation by Directional Drilling and are used between the Pullback Swivel and the Duct Puller.

Product Name:
Sealed Duct Puller
These products are designed to install non-metallic ducting using expanding jaws to grip the inside diameter of the duct while the outside diameter is prevented from stretching by a permanent outer sleeve on the Puller. The outer sleeve also provides a complete watertight seal around the ducting preventing any fluids or contaminants from entering either the duct or the pulling eye.

Product Name:
Duct Puller
The Poly Series duct puller together with the 00620 & 00621(Metric) Series of duct pullers are designed to grip polyethylene ducting using expanding jaws to grip the inside wall of the duct. This causes the pulling load to be spread around the circumference of the duct and allows a maximum load to be applied without damage to the ducting.

Product Name:
Cable Pulling Grips / Socks
DCD cable grips are hand-woven by experienced trades people and this guarantees a consistent level of high quality. DCD grips are reverse woven, creating a loop at the end of the grip instead of being soldered or crimped together . The wire is joined under the crimped head and testing proves this is never the weakest link of the product.

Product Name:
Lineman Cable Lasher
The Lineman Lasher is the pull type cable lasher for tough jobs, especially the ones that always seem to come up at night and in dusty, wet or icy conditions. Use it to lash single or multiple cables up to 3" outside diameter and for single and double overlashing. It is perfect for lashing CATV and fiber optic cable.

Product Name:
Eagle Cable Lasher
The all new Eagle Lasher is the latest word in aerial cable lashing for installing small fiber cables or coaxial cable. The new Eagle weighs just 24 lbs, and does not need traction from the strand to drive the drum - it uses the tension from the lashing wire to do that. With a bundle capacity of 2-1/4 or single cables up to 2, this lasher will single or double lash most Telecom or CATV installations as well as many Power applications.

Product Name:
Tube-Lube Cable Pulling Lubricant
The Tube-Lube 326 is an all-purpose lubricant that can be used in most applications from the lightest premise cable to heavy duty power cable. It is also an all-season lubricant - slow to evaporate in the hottest temperatures, yet also easy to use in below freezing climates. One of the biggest benefits of this lubricant is the ease of application - DCD supplies Applicator Kits and also designed many of it's duct entry products around this lubricant.

Product Name:
Fiberglass Duct Rodder
DCD offers the most comprehensive range of Duct Rodders available anywhere in the world. From the smallest 1/8 diameter Fish Tapes to the rugged 1/2 Duct Rodders.

The core of the rod is manufactured by the Pultrusion process. This involves impregnating high strength continuous glass fiber filaments with a liquefied resin. The fibers are drawn into a heated die where the resin matrix is cured, bonding the fibers together and allowing mechanical load to be transmitted through the matrix and distributed amongst the fibers.

Product Name:
Duct Proofing Checker
The 08500 Duct Checker discs are manufactured from high density polyurethane giving them the toughness to clear the duct without scraping or scratching the bore walls. The discs are spaced with gaps to allow passage through tight bends. The wire rope running through the mandrel is high quality, galvanized wire. *Safety factor is 3:1.

The 08400 Steel Duct Checkers are similar with tumbled, steel discs to ensure no burrs and spacers to ensure easy bending around corners.

Product Name:
Tuf-Lugger Cable Puller
The TUF Lugger is a Cable Puller that is designed to accommodate most pulling requirements up to 6500 lbs. loading.

The unit is fully assembled and is mounted on four wheels making it portable and easy to move into any pulling location.

The TUF-Lugger is driven by a removable Power Pack, which can also be used for other applications. The Power Pack is fitted with a load indicator as well as a feed-out sheave, which allows the operator to stand clear of the line of pull.

Product Name:
Maxi DUB-Swivels
The increased range of Maxi-Rig swivels is designed to better fit the capacities of the most common horizontal directional drilling (HDD) rig manufacturers.

Type 1 swivels offer an API box connection at the leading end and a Clevis connection at the trailing end. The rigid connection allows more straight forward pushback, but it also places a more severe side loading on the swivel.

These swivels also incorporate all the well established and patented DUB-Swivel design and features. This series of swivels is designed with service in mind. The Seal Cover is removable and all seals can be replaced in the field without need for major disassembly.
