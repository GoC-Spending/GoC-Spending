Company Profile


eSAX (the Entrepreneur Social Advantage Experience) is an entrepreneur networking group for startups to create connections, gain knowledge from featured speakers and promote collaboration among regional Chambers of Commerce. Events are held every 3 months to coincide with the provincially funded Y-Enterprise Centre Ontario Self-Employment Benefit Program (OSEB).

Sector information

Unique applications:

Jarrod Goldsmith has personally helped thousands of entrepreneurial startups with resources, knowledge, networking opportunities and encouragement.

Both eSAX and Sax Appeal have served to brand Jarrod Goldsmith as a master networker, leader and connector, known to all as a champion of entrepreneurial attitudes, behaviors and activities.

eSAX is known throughout the Ottawa region as THE networking event to attend for startups, is significant for stimulating regional economic development, and for encouraging people to pursue their dreams of entrepreneurship.

The long-term plan for eSAX involves the refining of processes so as to allow a national entrepreneurial networking program to provide startups with educational and supportive networks. Having events coincide with each Province’s Self-Employment Benefits Program, as well as involving all regional chambers of commerce, startup incubator communities / programs, all levels of government and post-secondary entrepreneurship facilities will leave a legacy that will continue to benefit both new and established businesses for generations.

Key / Major clients:

Ottawa Chamber of Commerce, Invest Ottawa, Community Employment Resource Centre, Y Enterprise Ontario Self Employment Benefits Program (OSEB), West Ottawa Board of Trade, Kanata Chamber of Commerce, Nepean Chamber of Commerce, Orleans Chamber of Commerce, le regroupement des gens d'affaires de la capitale nationale (RGA), Sax Appeal (www.saxappeal.ca)

Success stories:

eSAX events have developed a strong reputation for stimulating the startup community in Ottawa by serving as a focal point for the sharing of entrepreneurial resources that regularly host the Mayor of Ottawa, with hundreds of business and community leaders in attendance as well as multiple Chambers of Commerce.
