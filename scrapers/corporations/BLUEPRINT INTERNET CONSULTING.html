Company Profile


Blueprint Internet Consulting can help you get your internet efforts on track.

Is your web team as good as it can be? Are your web projects delivered late, over-budget, or are they just not meeting expectations? Have you scoured the web looking for help with your development methodology, trying to figure out which web process will get the team humming like a well-oiled machine? Blueprint Internet Consulting provides a range of consulting services to help you create a plana blueprintfor your online business.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Internet/Web Consulting Services
Blueprint Internet Consulting provides a full range of Internet and website consulting services. Our services are designed to be goal-oriented so that you see results faster. With 14 years experience building and managing websites for Fortune 500 companies, we understand the forces that drive an online business and we understand what it takes to create a high-performing web team that delivers solutions that exceed expectations.

You may need something as short-term as a 2-hour training session or you may have more complicated needs that require our staff to be on site for a 6 or 12 month project to get your internet department or website up and running.

Our solutions are designed to be flexible and configurable so that your staff can adopt the solutions seamlessly without needing ongoing training. Whether you need internet consulting, training, or project management, we'll design a program that's right for you

Service Name:
Internet and Web Team Management Services
Is directing a web team different from directing any other type of team? Yes. Web and Internet teams have unique challenges. Corporate web teams have time challenges. Agency interactive teams have dollar challenges. Some teams have both challenges. Managing Web and Internet teams require different approaches depending on your companys goals. Blueprint Internet Consulting can help with:

* Creating capability maturity models for your Internet teams current and future structure
* Business case and business plan development;
* Web team staffing, workflow, process audits / recommendations;
* Website management / content management best practices;
* Business alignment and strategy;
* Third-party and service contract negotiation;
* Training services.

Service Name:
Internet and Web Team Operations and Process Services
It is common for Internet and Interactive teams to operate in an ad hoc fashion. Each team member does their job and usually the work gets done. But often, this is a disorganized and difficult process leaving the team and the client less than satisfied. There is a better way. Blueprint Internet Consulting can help you build a team from scratch, improve an existing team, or just improve portions of your existing web process. We can also:

* Implementbest practices across your team, in all areas of expertise
* Create new processes, workflows, and process documentation;
* Improve or clarify existing processes and documentation;
* Make staff training recommendations or train interactive staff directly;
* Provide one-on-one coaching;
* Implement existing process methodologies such as Agile, Scrum, or Waterfall;
* Assist with the hiring process;
* Develop a short- and long-term plan for your Internet team development.

Service Name:
Internet and Web Project Management Services
Some web and internet projects are larger or more complex than your current head count can support. Our internet consulting services include web project management. Blueprint Internet Consulting is available to help manage your website projects from inception through to implementation or just for certain phases, such as:

* Project Portfolio development;
* High-level project management, project planning, ongoing communications, team formation and management, and associated documentation;
* Business case construction and feasibility study;
* Project initiation;
* Project charter documentation;
* Analysis and design;
* Requirements documentation;
* Specification documentation;
* Development or implementation of third-party system;
* Testing;
* Implementation;
* Measure (web analytics) and optimize.

Service Name:
Web Content Management System (CMS) Services
Web content management systems can be daunting, whether you are embarking on installing one or whether you are trying to manage one. Blueprint Internet Consultants have special expertise in the Open Text Web Content Management System (formerly RedDot) but have worked with a variety of web content management systems and can help you with:

* Implementation;
* Configuration;
* Roll-out;
* Adoption;
* Applying best practices.
