Company Profile


SUPPLYING THE LEADING HEAD - CE FRANKLIN LTD.

CE Franklin Ltd. Is Canada's leading distributor of supplies to
the oil and gas drilling and production industry. Through its 42
locations across Canada, the Company sells pipe, valves, pumps,
fittings and maintenance supplies from a comprehensive stock of
over 50,000 stock keeping units.

The Company also provides short-line manufacturing for
specialized products, as well, is a packager of natural gas and
air compression equipment. The Company's head office is in
Calgary and its central distribution hub is located in Edmonton.

CE Franklin also provides materials management support services
for oil and gas producers, including managing more than 50
customer-owned warehouse and operating 30 oil pump rebuilding
shops. More than a dozen oil and gas producers, both large and
small, out source their materials management to CE Franklin.

CE Franklin provides value through its services - value such as
quick & easy access to thousands of quality products, rapid field
service response, comprehensive logistics and inventory
management. We also provide the assurance of reliable quality by
operating under ISO 9002-94.
