Company Profile


Collins Safety Services Ltd.

Collins Safety Services is well known in the industry for our commitment in supporting employers, businesses owners and organizations in all types of industries requiring professional and economical health and safety solutions.

Health and Safety Solutions that fit your business

Collins Safety Services recognizes that organizations small and large ultimately want to keep their people safe but may be feeling the pressure and experiencing the challenges of meeting health and safety legislation requirements while trying to build and manage their health and safety systems.

This combined with the daily challenges of meeting your own demanding business needs can be overwhelmingIt doesn't have to be that way!


We help you Close the gap  so you can focus on your business.


Some of the services we offer include...


Safety Consulting: Experienced Health & Safety professionals ready to help you achieve your safety goals.

Online Safety Training:Industry leading training that saves time and money while training at your own pace.

Health & Safety Programs: A requirement for all companies and a must have tool for keeping your workers safe.

Custom Training Programs: Designed around your specific training requirements and corporate branding.

Project Safety Coordination: Helping you manage and coordinate all aspects of safety for your company or projects.

Medical Stand By & First Aid Services: Ready to respond in any situation to provide the highest standard of service and support.

Safety Management Solutions: S.M.A.R.T. Safety Management, Administration, Reporting and Tracking.

Patient Transfer Services(Ambulance): Approved and authorized by the Ministry of Health Services and the Emergency Health Services Commission of British Columbia.

Service Name:
Safety Consulting
Over 20 years in the industry and a combined 35 years of professional experience within our team, we help you achieve your Occupational Health and Safety goals by providing expertise, experience and assistance with Health and Safety legislation, requirements, and responsibilities regardless of how big or small your company is, your industry, or scope of work.

We take all the pressure off you so you can focus on your business!

Service Name:
Safety Audits / Risk Assessments
Perhaps you want to evaluate your current safety program or safety system already in place to see how effective and compliant it is, or perhaps your organization wants to be recognized in the industry as a C.O.R. (Certificate of Recognition) certified company... we can help.

Our safety professionals and certified auditors can help you by providing an assessment of your current safety performance status as well as supply you with a detailed report that will help you evaluate and determine a plan of action for any areas that may need improvement.



Service Name:
Health & Safety Programs
A requirement for all companies and a must have tool for keeping your workers safe.

Collins Safety Services specializes in assisting employers with the WCB requirements for maintaining their company Occupational Health and Safety Programs.

WorkSafeBC regulation requires all employers to have some form of "Safety Program" within their company. The size of your company, scope of work, and other criteria within the regulation will ultimately dictate the requirements of each individual employer.

Where legislation and/or regulation requires all employers to have some form of "Safety Program" within their company, the size of your company, scope of work, and other criteria within the regulation will ultimately dictate the requirements of each individual employer.

While this can be overwhelming at the best of times for anyone trying to put it all together, we can help you understand the regulation and your company's requirements and get you on the right track...fast.



Service Name:
Custom Training Programs / E-Learning Solutions
Interactive E-Learning Solutions

Cutting edge custom made solutions to suit your business and tailored to your needs. Designed around your specific training requirements and corporate branding.

Making your company safety management system even more incredible, interactive and accessible by turning to e-learning is the future.

Custom made company health and safety solutions such as:
Company Presentations, Safety Orientations, Training Modules like: Forklift, due-diligence, fall protection, confined space, etc, etc...the possibilities are literally endless !

These all help highlight and show off your Goods and Services to your clients by saying... "Safety is important to us".

Service Name:
Project Safety Coordination
Helping you manage and coordinate all aspects of safety for your company or projects.

Acting on your behalf as the company Health & Safety Coordinator, we will address and assist to ensure all legislative responsibilities are met.

We work closely with company management team to coordinate new and ongoing projects to ensure nothing is missed and all regulatory requirements are in place to ensure safety compliance. We act as the company liaison for health and safety issues between WorkSafeBC and other legislative bodies.

We help you ensure that workers under your control are properly trained in safe work practices, equipment operation, and installation.

We coordinate all aspects of your project including training, update and maintain safety records and provide the employer with peace of mind to know their employees are knowledgeable in safe working practices.

Benefits: A strong safety message is promoted by the company.

Overall safety is improved due to focused safety management. Safety awareness is raised leading to less insurance claims by injured workers ultimately lowering company claims costs, resulting in increased production, safe working environment and enormous cost savings.

Service Name:
Safety Management Solutions
S.M.A.R.T. Safety Management, Administration, Reporting and Tracking.

Safety responsibilities have been a constant challenge with companies and industries alike, such as tougher industry legislation, safety requirements and extensive and up to date record keeping and file management capabilities.We have taken that into account when working with our clients to overcome these challenges.

Collins Safety Services utilizes an Integrated Safety Management Database System that allows us to establish and bring together or incorporate all the elements of a company's safety systems quickly and easily. Our system enables us to efficiently and effectively receive, send and update information from anywhere in the world. Even while working in the field we can remotely manage any company regardless of size, location or scope of work. Typically the safety management process of any business would normally be done through a manual process costing thousands of dollars in wages and hours in manpower.

Key Benefits...

Our management system helps the client save time and money by eliminating unnecessary man hours and wage cost and allows for easy integration and overall effectiveness to manage any firms health & safety system including: human resources, contractors, training, incidents, accidents and statistics as well as claims, meetings, inspections, equipment, MSDS, Safe work Procedures, JHA, Statistics and Analysis... and so much more.

From claims costs to statistical graphs and charts our system will produce up to the minute safety information on basically anything we need it to.

Because you need to know whats going on in your organization at every moment, a database that will gather and control all the information and have it ready for when it is needed is well...S.M.A.R.T !

Even better...this service is free as part of our service for all our clients.

Service Name:
Online Safety Training
Industry leading training that saves time and money while training at your own pace. Online Training is your answer to effective alternative learning solutions in safety !

Everyone is turning to the web these day's. Let's face it...we live in a world that demands speed and efficiency. This puts an ever increasing demand on organizations to find alternative ways to keep up. Interest in online or e-learning training is growing more than ever now to meet the needs of most organizations to not only save time and money but to help keep the focus on their business while learning.

What is e-learning?

Most simply put, e-learning is the application of computer technologies to education whether used face-to-face in school or workplace settings, blended with traditional learning, to deliver a course fully online, or for self-directed informal learning.

How does e-learning help Canadians learn?

E-learning provides Canadians the flexibility to learn at their own pace at any stage in the life span thereby fostering positive attitudes about the value of lifelong learning.


» Self-directed: Learners can choose content and tools appropriate to their differing interests, needs, and skill levels.
» Reduces physical and geographical barriers: More educational options to learners with
disabilities, and those living in remote areas.
» Timely: Learning can be delivered and learned when desired or necessary.

To have available all the necessary safety learning information at your finger tips is not only convenient but cost efficient and effective as well. Online training is worth investigating for companies of all sizes

Our online training courses are designed to be effective and comprehensive, maximizing engagement, comprehension and retention in a dynamic, interactive style.
