Company Profile


Manufacturer of Italian specialty meats. Established in 1966 - producing a wide variety of fresh, cooked, and dry cured sausages, including: mortadella, capocolli, cacciatore, genoa salami, pepperoni, and fresh sausages. Bona Foods is federally inspected and HACCP certified.

Product Name:
Bona Mortadella Extrafina
Italian-style bologna, crafted using techniques that are generations old. Federally inspected pork is minced extremely fine and mixed with cubes of pork fat. The mixture is stuffed into a casing and oven-roasted to perfection.
Dimensions:
Italiana: 8" diameter x 15" length, 9kg
Regular: 6" diameter x 18" length, 6kg
Media: 4" diameter x 8" length, 1.5kg
Baby: 2.5" diameter x 6" length, 0.5kg

Product Name:
Paisanella
Stemming from the Italian word "paesano", which roughly translates to "fellow countryman", Bona Paisanella is a testament to how salami was crafted in the 'old country'. Extra lean pork is coarsely ground, spiced and stuffed into thick chitterling casings. These heavy casings impart a unique flavour and aroma to the meat during its extended curing process as well as yield the product its irregular shape. Very few sausage-makers still use such casings because they are so challenging to handle. Bona Paisanella is indeed a rare gem of salami and a true labour of love.
Paisanella Small: 1"-3" diameter x 7"-9" length, approx. 200-650g [Mild/Hot]
Paisanella Big: 3"-5" diameter x 8"-10" length, approx. 750-1500g [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Bona Mortadella Hot
A variation of the extra-fina recipe substituting red chili peppers for black peppercorns.

Product Name:
Bona Mortadella Lean
A variation on the extra-fina recipe substituting lean meat for cubes of fat.

Product Name:
Capocollo
Bona Foods is renowned across Canada for its traditionally-made, and great tasting capocollo. A generous amount of time and labour goes into each piece of these genuine Italian-style cooked hams. First, a federally inspected pork shoulder must be trimmed to yield only the leanest portion. This lean cut of pork is then carefully spiced, hand-stuffed into a casing, and oven-baked.
Bona distinguishes its premium capocollo by utilizing only one whole cut of pork rather than combining many small pieces. Making it in this fashion is considerably more expensive and labour-intensive, but it produces an unquestionably superior product.
Dimensions: 3.5" x 7-9" length, 1.5kg [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Capocollo Extra-Lean
Made with the leanest cuts of pork, this ham contains hardly any fat and sacrifices little of Bona's delicious capocollo flavour. It is a great choice for those who want the great taste of our traditional capocollo, but are concerned with their fat intake. Like most capocollo, the extra-lean is excellent sliced thinly in sandwiches. Bona Extra-Lean Capocollo is sold in half pieces.
Dimensions: 3.5" x 8" length, 1.3kg [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Pepperoni
Bona Pepperoni is a spicy and robust cooked product, offered in a variety of shapes and sizes. Most popular is our Sliced Pepperoni for pizzerias and restaurants. Standard packaging is in 2.5kg bags (2 bags/case = 5kg/case).
With a smaller diameter, and sold in pairs, Bona Pepperoni Packages are intended for home use. Easily hand-sliced, they are ideal for home-made pizzas, or as a spicy snack.

Product Name:
Genova Salame
Bona Genova Salame, named after the ancient port of Genoa, consists of coarsely ground pork seasoned with peppercorns and various other fine spices. The meat mixture is stuffed into casings and slowly air dried. The large diameter of the Genova makes it ideal for deli counters and high volume applications. Bona Genova Salame is available in whole and half pieces as well as thirds. Whole pieces are available with or without vacuum packaging.
Dimensions: 3" diameter x 16" length, 2kg [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Soppressa
Modeled after the 'sopressa vicentina' from the Vicenza province of Northern Italy, Bona Soppressa is made from coarse minced pork, spices, and peppercorns. Similar to our Genova, it is made with a larger diameter, then pressed to form a typical Calabrese, flattened oval shape. The pressing process yields a firmer texter in the salame while eliminating a small amount of oil within the product. Bona Soppressa is superb in sandwiches, or on antipasto plates.
Dimensions: 5" width x 2" height x 16" length, 2.3kg [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Salametti & Soppressate Calabrese
Bona Salametti is a smaller version of our Genova Salame. 'Salametti' means 'small salami'. It is intended for home use, and is a favourite with families. The small diameter of the product allows for easy hand-slicing for antipasto or lunch sandwiches. Our hot version of Salametti is also referred to as Bona Soppressate. Bona Soppressate Calabrese is a smaller version of our Soppressa. It is made by pressing the Salametti flat. Some people like the firmer texture of the flattened Calabrese while other simply prefer the shape, which resembles home-made salami of their past.
Salametti: 2.5" diameter x 7" length, 420g [Mild/Hot]
Sopp. Calabrese: 3" width x 1" height x 7" length, 420g [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Cacciatore & Salciccia Calabrese
'Cacciatore' means 'hunter' in Italian, and this portable mild Bona salame is so named for the hunters who carried it with them on long trips. Bona's Hot Cacciatore is so popular it was given its own name, Salciccia Calabrese, honouring our founders' roots in the region of Calabria, where spicy salami is customary.
Made of coarsely minced pork with premium spices and stuffed into natural casings, Cacciatore and Salciccia are Bona's thinnest dry cured products. They are convenient for travel because of their small size, and can be enjoyed thickly sliced by hand. Slices of Cacciatore can be served as pizza toppings, on appetizer dishes, and whole links are often added to pasta sauce for flavour.
Dimensions: 1" diameter x 7" length, 250g/pair [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Bona Casalingo Salame
'Casalingo' translates to 'home-made'. As a comparison, the size of a Bona Casalingo puts it right between our Genova and Salametti. The meat mixture used to make it is quite lean, equivalent to that used in the Paisanella, making it quite the premium salami. The spiced meat mixture is stuffed into standard artificial casings yielding a uniform cylindrical shape.
Dimensions: 2" diameter x 11" length, 600g [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Dry Capocolli & Prosciuttini
(Also known in some Italian dialects as 'Lonza')
Bona Dry Capocolli and Prosciuttini are domestic alternatives to a traditional leg of prosciutto. Both are similar, except that the Prosciuttini is pressed flat. Visible is their deep red colour, contrasted by natural veins of fat. A whole cut of pork, like in Bona's cooked Capocollo, is salted in brine, then cased and air-dried for an extended period. Their convenient size of about 700g is better suited for home use than a standard prosciutto which weighs about 3.5kg. As well, their dimensions permit easier slicing either by hand or on a domestic meat slicer (normal prosciutto is often too big for a standard slicer)
Dry Capocolli: 3" diamter x 7" length, 700g [Mild/Hot]
Prosciuttini: 4: width x 1.5" height, 700g [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Prosciutto Delizia
Bona Prosciutto Delizia, like our Prosciuttini, is an alternative to a conventional prosciutto. It is made in much the same fashion as a prosciuttini, just on a larger scale. The finest pork hams are timmed to their leanest whole cut by our own skilled butchers, then hand-massaged with salt and cured in brine. The salted meat is later hand stuffed into casings, hung and air-dried for several months. While a traditional prosciutto leg has a considerable amount of waste in its hard shank, Delizia contains only edible, delicious tasting meat. Prosciutto Delizia is best enjoyed in thin slices and makes an elegant antipasto when served with melons or figs.
Dimension: 7" width x 3" height x 5" length, 1.25kg (per half piece)

Product Name:
Pancetta Flat
Bona Pancetta Flat starts with a seasoned pork belly which is dry cured, losing up to a third of its weight. The belly is then carefully trimmed and cut up into small rectangles for packaging.
Pancetta is often referred to as 'Italian bacon'. Unlike English bacon, which can include the sides or back of the pig, and may be smoked, Bona Pancetta Flat comes solely from the pork belly, and is salt-cured only, never smoked. This allows the subtle flavours of the pork to develop slowly and flourish in the final product. Pancetta is widely used in cooking, where it lends its remarkable savory-sweet flavour to many dishes including risottos, pastas, stews, soups, sauces, and cooked vegetables.
Dimensions: 6" width x 1" height x 3" length, 250g [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Pancetta Round
Bona Pancetta Round is carefully hand crafted by tightly rolling salted pork belly meat and skillfully casing it for its long dry curing period. The rolled meat must be free of air pockets, which can cause spoilage during the curing process. The procedure requires strong and practiced hands to craft a flawless product.
Rolled Pancetta was traditionally used as bacon in Italy. It is often moister than American bacon because it has not been smoked. Bona Pancetta Rount may be sliced and eaten as one would eat prosciutto - although it is usually more tender and considerably less salty. Round Pancetta, like our flat, is fantastic for cooking, and is incredibly versatile in the kitchen.
Dimensions: 4" diameter x 9" length, 1.5kg [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Coppa (Head Cheese)
In the old country, it is said that 'no part of the pig should be discarded'. Coppa, also known as Head Cheese, is a fine example of this. Not cheese at all, Coppa is rather a combination of meats from the hog's head. These are boiled together with spices and gelatin, then stuffed into a casing and oven-baked. The result is a mosaic of meats with varied textures. Coppa is an acquired taste, but regarded as a true Italian delicacy by many. It is delicious warm or cold, all by itself, or sliced on sandwiches. Bona Coppa is sold in half pieces.
Dimensions: 5" diameter x 9" length, 2.4kg [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Fresh Sausages
Bona's classic Italian sausages are nothing short of legendary. They contain just pork and spices - no fillers or preservatives are added to this first-rate product. Only the finest cuts of pork are coarsely ground and stuffed into natural casings to make them.
Mild Sausages are seasoned with black pepper while hot sausages are spiced with potent red chillies. Enjoy Bona Sausages barbequed, fried, in sauce, or sliced on pizza - their uses are limited only by your imagination. They are offered 'loose' or 'frozen & vacuum packaged'. Packages of 5 or 10 are available.
Dimensions: 1.5" diameter x 7" link, 160g per link [Mild/Hot]

Product Name:
Fresh Sausages (Thin)
Bona's famous fresh sausage recipe using thinner, Barese-style, natural casings. Enjoy them as a Italian version of breakfast sausages, sliced up in stew, or roasted with potatoes. Their uses are limited only by your creativity. Bona Thin Sausages are offered frozen in packages of 10.
Dimensions: 1/2" diameter x 7" link, 450g per 10pc pkg

Product Name:
Bona Deli Bags
Show your customers than Bona is your preferred brand! Bona has custom printed transparent, flip-top bags for use at your deli counter. Our longstanding company name and nostalgic logo are emblazoned on each one. With a 2" flip top, these durable .55mm bags will keep your customers' deli purchases fresh and flavourful.
