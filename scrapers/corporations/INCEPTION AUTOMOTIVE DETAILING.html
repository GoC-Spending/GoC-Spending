OUR HISTORY:

Inception Automotive Detailing was founded in 2011 by an eighteen years old university student, Lorenzo Escobal. Inception Automotive Detailing started as a mobile service servicing its clients in their homes and workplaces. It became one of the top ranked automotive detailing companies in the Greater Toronto Area.

Inception Automotive Detailing was awarded the 2014 Readers Choice Award by the Mississauga Business Times, a prominent business paper in the City of Mississauga published by the Metroland Media. Inception Automotive Detailing is also a board member of the Mississauga Board of Trade, the chamber of commerce organization for the City of Mississauga.

From its humble beginning, Inception Automotive Detailing has been featured and spoke in many auto enthusiast forums, newspapers, online articles and national radio. Some media outlets include CBC Radio and Top Gear Philippines. The company Inception Automotive Detailing has served many clients ranging from multiple companies and notable individuals such as the owner of a very well-known multi-billion dollar real-estate development in the Greater Toronto Area, a few boards of directors from organizations such as CAMH (Centre of Addiction of Mental Health), Futurpreneur Canada and CAEP (Canadian Association of Emergency Physicians) and many more.

Inception Automotive Detailing has one primary role and that is to provide clients the new car driving experience. Regardless of the vehicle, Inception Automotive Detailing treats every vehicle the same unrivaled workmanship many clients expect for.

Are you ready to receive the new car driving experience?

ABOUT THE FOUNDER, OWNER AND OPERATOR

Lorenzo Escobal is a Class of 2014 university graduate with a tremendous milestone in a short amount of time. He is the founder of a professional mobile auto detailing company, Inception Automotive Detailing, to which it came to one of the leading mobile auto detailing companies in the Greater Toronto Area. It has been awarded the Readers Choice Award for the Mississauga Business Times for 2014. It also has negotiated with many automotive dealerships and a multi-billion dollar conglomerate. Because of this, the company boasts many notable clients such as an owner of a well-known real estate development company in the Greater Toronto Area, a board of director for the Canadian Association of Emergency Physicians, Futurpreneur Canada and CAMH, and many more.

Lorenzo also co-founded Medpro 3D Inc, a medical rapid prototyping company, who works with health care professionals to transform medial scans such as MRI and CT into life-scale sized 3D tangible models.

Apart from operating two businesses, he has a large international network, he is a board & committee member of the Mississauga Board of Trade, the chamber of commerce organization for the City of Mississauga, and a seasoned guest speaker at the University of Ontario Institute of Technology, University of Toronto and many more.

Due to the achievement in a short amount of time, Lorenzo has been featured in numerous news articles, interviews and national radio - CBC Radio One Metro Morning. Also, he has met and rubbed shoulders with many politicians, celebrity business people and super stars. Some people include Al Pacino, Sylvester Stallone, JT Foxx, Dr. Nido Qubein, Jay Abraham, Dragon's Den Venture Capitalists Jim Treliving, Arlene Dickinson, David Chilton, Michael Wekerle, Vikram Vij and Bruce Croxon, George H. Ross - Donald Trump's Right Hand Man and Celebrity Judge on "The Apprentice", Robert Deluce, Christine Magee, Mayor John Henry, City of Oshawa, and Mayor Bonnie Crombie, City of Mississauga.
