Company Profile


Based in Toronto, Canada, Norm Li takes pride in its reputation for providing clients with exceptional service and beautiful visuals. Equipped with strong architectural backgrounds and design sensibilities, our team of visualization experts can help to provide visual clarity at all stages of a project – from schematic design all the way through to marketing campaigns. We are capable of handling jobs of all sizes. From single objects to multi-billion dollar rapid transit systems, we have rendered it all.

Our diverse portfolio spans academic, arts & culture, commercial, healthcare, hospitality, infrastructure, residential (high-rise and low-rise), retail, and urban planning projects.

Everyday we strive to be #alwaysbetter for our clients, offering a process that best meets their needs, providing a service that makes their lives easier, and delivering visualizations that make them look their best.
