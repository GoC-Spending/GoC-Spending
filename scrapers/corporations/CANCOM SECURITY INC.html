Company Profile


Cancom Security is comprised of personnel with over 50 years experience within security and law enforcement fields. All Guards and/or Operatives are selected, screened, and trained according to Provincial/Municipal standards.

Our services include:

-Security Guard Services
-Parking Enforcement
-Security Systems
-Private Investigations
-Loss Prevention
-VIP Close Protection
-Criminal Background Checks
-GPS Tracking
-Security Training
-Integrated Security Solutions


We are fully committed to fulfilling your needs and surpassing your expectations. We recruit and train our personnel to surpass the industry standard by providing them with the appropriate knowledge, training and tools needed to achieve a service level that is second to none. We are leaders in your community. We lead by example, and we do this by initiating community-based security programs in partnership with community-based policing, community initiatives, communication, education and ongoing training.

Cancom Security provides each client with the highest level of security service best-suited to our clients’ environment. Because each environment is unique, a thorough evaluation is conducted to clearly identify and outline specific procedures and needs. Areas of evaluation may include tenant type, level of public relations, areas of high sensitivity, and traffic flow within or on the property. Upon completion, we determine the level of security/service required as well as the number of personnel required to provide the most effective and cost efficient service possible.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Security Systems - Intrusion, CCTV and Access
Intrusion Systems – Cancom Security Intrusion systems will provide your Home, Cottage or Business with the protection you’ll need to have peace of mind. Intrusion systems control access into your home, cottage or business and monitor fire, carbon monoxide and other various devices. It is highly recommended that all intrusion systems be connected to a monitoring station via a phone line, internet or cellular phone line to ensure the safety of its occupants.

Service Name:
Executive Protection
VIP CLOSE PROTECTION
Our Special Security Operatives protect persons from assault, kidnapping, assassination, loss of confidential information, or other threats to the person or their property. Our VIP services are offered to celebrities and other high profile individuals handling the daily challenges of living in the public eye. We provide the most secure level of protection possible, coupled with the discretion needed to maintain the anonymity of the person being protected. We plan, designate, determine, consult, plan again and execute.

Service Name:
First Nations Security Guard Servcies
Cancom Security Inc. is a First Nations Security company based in Wikwemikong, Ontario that provides community based patrol services who’s members are from the community of Wikwemikong. All staff are local band members and are trained locally and are selected to assist in enhancing the community watch and liaise with the local Police service. Cancom Security is a proud progressive partner with the Wiikwemikoong First Nation Territory and Wikwemikong Tribal Police Service.

Cancom Security can replicate the same service in any First Nations community.

Service Name:
K9 Detection Services
Cancom Security provides professional detection dog services. Our scope of business required the selection and acquisition of suitable detection dog candidates. Each candidate then undergoes a series of testing which can take up to several months depending on the detection discipline being trained such as narcotics and explosive detection.
Identified and trained dog handlers are paired dog handlers after detailed interviews and field observations.
Demonstrated consistent success in the selection and acquisition of high-caliber canines from both import and domestic markets.
Our handlers demonstrate proficiency in troubleshooting security issues, identifying vulnerabilities and setting up ongoing dog training for general protection and emergency situations

Service Name:
Loss Prevention
Cancom Security Inc. recruits and develops its own loss prevention program under the experience and supervision of Security professionals who specialize in Loss Prevention. This group of specialists recruit and train in house staff on a on going basis.

The result is a team of specialist who get the job done and the results speak for themselves. Our speciality in Loss prevention is unmatched from start to finish. We guarantee our work and back it up with a 100% percent Satisfaction Guarantee.

The Loss due to shrinkage versus the investment into our services out weigh the cost. The benefit of our services will surpass your expectations. From the point of contact, detention, information gathering, criminal prosecution, civil prosecution and education will be outstanding in demonstrating a bona fide practice that will be professional and represent your organization well.

Shrinkage affects your organization, employees and customers, it is our job to minimize your loses, deter and educate everyone and have an impact on those involved in shrinkage.

Service Name:
Parking Enforcement
Cancom Security Inc. is a private property parking enforcement agency licensed through the Toronto Police Service Parking Enforcement Unit Contract Services. We are dedicated to providing safe and accessible parking on private property. The goal of our agency is to educate the public concerning private parking policies and regulations, ensuring our clients can manage their parking space with the appropriate enforcement. We are dedicated to providing a 24-hour and seven-day-a-week service to our clients to ensure the safe and efficient movement of traffic on private property, maximizing the availability of short term and dedicated parking on our clients’ property.

Service Name:
Private Investigation
Cancom Security Inc. provides high quality investigative services, using the latest technology. We employe investigators with a unparalleled work ethic, ensuring our clients professional and detailed results. We are a multi-faceted team of private investigors, providing a wide range of services to insurance companies, legal firms, municipalities and corporations; acting pro-actively to provide evidence to avoid costly litigation.
Cancom Security Inc. and our diverse network of professionals with extensive experience in all sectors of the investigation industry, obtain information and evidence in an accurate, timely and cost effective manner. This allows our clients to actively manage their files.

Service Name:
Security Guard Services
Security Guard Services - Our Security Guards are fully committed to fulfilling your needs and surpassing your expectations. We recruit and train our personnel to surpass the industry standard by providing them with the appropriate knowledge, training and tools needed to achieve a service level that is second to none. We are leaders in your community. We lead by example, and we do this by initiating community-based security programs in partnership with community-based policing, community initiatives, communication, education and on going training.

Concierge Services - Our concierge Security Guards, in their low profile career apparel, are very well versed in client care, customer service skills, and emergency procedures. All are well supported by our mobile resources. You will feel confident with peace of mind knowing your front desk is represented with professionalism and customer service that goes beyond the expectation of your traditional “lobby guard”. Our on going training will ensure your needs are met for your facility and organization. We train receptionists / concierge / desk attendants to adequately protect their organizations.


Retail Guards - Our Guards are thought of as an integral part of our clients stores, and aside from a visual security presence and an extended customer service representative, our officers provide valuable documentation and reports that can be used for sales associate training, meetings and management tools. Our operations team are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, taking care of routine patrols and checks, alarm call outs and out of hours emergencies. Our presence in your mall will demonstrate true Security presence at its best.

Low Profile & High Profile Guards - Our Guards attire will be customized to best suit your organizations image and needs. We deploy our officers in corporate attire, concierge attire for receptionist duties and high profile attire for an increased visibility. The presence of security acts as a deterrent to threats and uniform attire can determine the level of visibility. In any case we work with you to decide which uniform attire is best suited for your application and image. Your Security staff will look professional and will meet government guidelines.

Service Name:
Security Guard Training
We prepare our students to write and PASS the Ontario Security Guard Test as required by the Ministry of Community Safety and Correctional Services, in order to acquire the mandatory Security Guard License.
