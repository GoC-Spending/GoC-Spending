Company Profile


Averna is a premier manufacturing solution provider for communications and electronics device-makers worldwide, helping them deliver a better end-user customer experience. Key clients in the communications, aerospace, defense, automotive, consumer electronics, and medical device industries use Proligent, RF Test Instruments and other Averna test solutions to accelerate product development, reduce manufacturing costs, achieve superior quality throughout the lifecycle, and solve critical supply chain issues. Incorporated in 1999, Averna has won many awards and has offices and partners around the world.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
DP-360 - DOCSIS Protocol Analyzer
The DP-360 is the industry standard for functional DOCSIS and EuroDOCSIS network analysis, providing exceptional visibility into all layers of the network. Multiple system operators (MSOs), chipset manufacturers, product developers and certification bodies use it to quickly find and correct trouble spots.

Optimized for real-time signal processing with FPGA technology, the DP-360 analyzes up to four single or bonded upstream channels and up to eight single or bonded downstream channels (4x4 or 8x4), with numerous channel-filtering and display features.

By capturing and filtering DOCSIS MAC layer data in real-time, the DP-360 enables full automation and remote operation  so you can quickly troubleshoot network issues and get your products to market faster than the competition.

Product Name:
DCE - DOCSIS Channel Emulator
The DCE allows OEM and ODM labs to accelerate their internal validation and comply with multiple system operators (MSOs) SCTE 40 requirements, as well as helping MSOs to replicate network issues in their labs. It is an innovative, modular and low-cost platform that leverages National Instruments Vector Signal Transceiver (VST), features real-time FPGA-based processing, and deploys user-friendly development tools.

With the desktop DCE, you can acquire, impair and easily generate up to 24 DOCSIS or EuroDOCSIS downstream (DS) channels in real-time, anywhere in your lab. And the DCEs software-based approach and modular architecture protect your investment, allowing you to easily cover your current and future test needs.

Product Name:
Jupiter Design Verification Test System
Jupiter is the industry standard for automated DOCSIS physical (PHY) layer testing. It provides the most comprehensive, off-the-shelf test coverage and accurate results on the market for EuroDOCSIS and DOCSIS 1.1, 2.0, and 3.0 devices.

Certification labs, standards bodies, and leading manufacturers use Jupiter to test cable modems, set-top boxes, residential gateways and other customer-premises equipment (CPE) devices. It enables comprehensive, automated PHY layer testing for data-over-cable CPE developers, helping them dramatically reduce DOCSIS test times early in the lifecycle and to speed up time-to-market.

Product Name:
RF Record-and-Playback
Recording live, impaired RF signals in the field, without de-modulation or alteration, reduces the need for traditional field-testing of RF receivers. By reproducing complex field conditions accurately and consistently in the lab, you can cut back on costly field trips to validate design changes.

RP-5300 Series:
Our RP-5300 2-Channel, 50 MHz Compact RF Recorder captures real-world RF signals, with impairments, for professional GNSS and similar applications  for receiver validation, testing and support. Its perfect for both field and performance testing and, with 50 MHz of bandwidth, it can record multiple GNSS signals in multiple bands (L1, L2, L5), such as GPS or GLONASS.

RP-5100 Series:
Our RP-5100 Multi-Channel, 20 MHz Compact RF Recorder captures real-world RF signals, with impairments, for Broadcast Radio, as well as Navigation and Video receiver validation, testing and support. It is an advanced tool for both field testing and performance testing, featuring support for a broad range of frequencies  from 250 kHz up to 2.5 GHz and 20 MHz.

RP-3200:
The RP-3200 Multi-Channel Wideband RF Record and Playback is a state-of-the-art, wideband L1, L2, L5 recorder for capturing real-world and real-time multi-GNSS signals in the field and replaying them in a controlled laboratory environment. The RP-3200 more than meets the needs of the Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) market, where precision and robustness demand a wideband, high-dynamic-range multi-channel record-and-playback system.


Product Name:
RF Player and Signal Generator - URT
The need to design, validate, test and support increasingly complex Navigation and Broadcast Radio receivers in an economical and timely manner is an ever-growing concern. Averna answers that need with Averna signal generation complemented by
RF playback of real-world signals captured with Averna Record & Playback technology.

URT-5000:
The state-of-the-art URT-5000 software-defined RF Player and Signal Generator utilizes Averna protocol-specific signal-generation toolkits and signal libraries to generate and impair common Navigation and Broadcast Radio signals for receiver design validation, testing, production and support. The URT-5000 supports RF playback of real-world conditions, often too complex to simulate, captured with an Averna Record & Playback field instrument.
The URT-5000 is the RF test instrument of choice in the automotive, semiconductor, consumer electronics, communications and other industries. It enables you to accurately and consistently play back unique, complex field conditions during the receiver development phase, which reduces costly trips back to the field to validate each engineering change.

URT-4000:
The Averna URT-4000 is a state-of-the-art RF signal source that utilizes Averna protocol-specific signal-generation toolkits and signal libraries to generate and impair common Navigation and Broadcast Radio signals for receiver testing, production and support. The URT-4000 has been optimized for radio manufacturing needs and is an ideal RF signal generator for Functional Test, ATE, End-of-Line and other test stations. The URT-4000 is packed with features important for manufacturing test, such as remote-system health and fan monitoring, an easy-to-use remote API for sequencing signal generation to improve throughput, and fast switching time between protocols and level settings.

URT-2200 - RF Player for GNSS:
The URT-2200 RF Player features 50 MHz of real-time playback bandwidth and is based on a PXI platform. This model supports up to four channels of high-coherent synchronization per chassis (and multi-chassis sync), perfectly suited for professional GNSS L1/L2/L5 applications that require reliable repetition of real-world multi-frequency signals.With an optional 24 TB RAID to stream back many hours of RF recordings, and a high-precision 10 MHz reference clock, the URT-2200 helps accelerate your regression testing of new receiver configurations, develop algorithms, and play back scenarios, including those recorded from a GNSS simulator.

Product Name:
Proligent Analytics - Real-time Reports
With Proligent you manage all your test data and test assets from one location  giving you ultimate visibility and control over your product quality, test equipment, processes and operations. Manufacturers worldwide use Proligent to score huge productivity, yield, quality and bottom-line gains.

Proligent Analytics is packaged in a test-ready, rack-mountable appliance with full redundancy, right-sized for your data needs. It maximizes your ROI while requiring minimal integration and maintenance. Whether you need higher yields and quality, better traceability, instant asset information or lower labor costs, Proligent Analytics gives you the edge on all your test activities.
