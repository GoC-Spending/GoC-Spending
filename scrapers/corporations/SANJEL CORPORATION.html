Company Profile


Sanjel is a privately owned, Canadian-based, international oilfield service company with over three decades of industry experience. As a major competitor in the global oil and gas market, Sanjel delivers two product offerings: pressure pumping and completions  each with our own broad range of specialized products and custom-designed and manufactured equipment. Driven by pride and recognized for performance, Sanjel transforms our industry-leading technology into customized solutions and superior job execution.

With approximately 3,500 employees, Sanjel's large-scale, international operations stretch across North America and overseas, incorporating over 30 global locations and Sanjels unique Professional Park. Sanjel is a world-class company and is committed to providing the very highest level of service  a commitment that runs throughout the entire organization, from the boardroom to the well site.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Acidizing Services and Products
Sanjel Corporation offers an extensive array of acidizing services designed to help customers realize the full potential of their wells' productive capabilities. Our experience and success achieved throughout the sedimentary basin of Western Canada and the Rocky Mountain Region of the United States has formed our foundation and solidified Sanjel's reputation as a company that provides solid, engineered stimulation solutions. Supported by our advanced pumping equipment designed and fabricated in-house, Sanjel provides customers with innovative, robust acid packages developed to satisfy virtually every application and deliver the proper treatment every time.

Service Name:
Cementing Services and Products
Sanjel Corporation is an industry-leading global provider of quality cementing products and services, and the leading supplier in Canada. With breakthrough technology in innovative product systems and equipment design/application, Sanjel provides energy producers with customized cementing solutions that maximize well productivity and economic gain. We respond to our customers' individual needs with the highest quality of service, delivering solid field performance in all cementing applications.

Service Name:
Coiled Tubing Services
Coiled tubing is one of the most effective methods of quickly and efficiently placing treatment fluids with pinpoint accuracy to the zone of interest. It is proven to be an effective and economical tool, particularly when utilized as a fracturing conveyance string. Using our own state-of-the-art and custom-built coiled tubing units, Sanjel Corporation provides global energy producers with an unequalled level of coiled tubing services.

Service Name:
Fracturing Services and Products
Sanjel Corporation's fracturing spreads operate at the leading edge of oilfield pumping service innovation and technical advancement. Our custom-built equipment and broad base of proprietary engineered fracturing fluids are designed and proven to perform optimally in producing energy fields. Our experienced well specialists integrate our products and services to provide effective fracturing treatments and high quality job execution.

Service Name:
Nitrogen Services
Nitrogen has been used in the oil and gas well servicing industry for many years, and the effectiveness and versatility of this inert product continues to prove itself more everyday. Sanjel Corporation has assembled a world-class nitrogen fleet comprising some of the most technically advanced equipment applications in the industry. Holding one of the largest fleets in Canada and an exponentially growing fleet in the United States, our in-house custom designed and built equipment perform industry-leading nitrogen services that facilitate effective treatment of global oil and gas wells. Through our customized and cost-effective solutions, our customers realize the full potential and benefit of our wide range of nitrogen applications.

Technology profile

Technical innovations are embedded throughout all aspects of Sanjel's operations from strategic process advancements and specialized well treatment applications, to our custom-built equipment and lab-engineered products. Our approach to innovative solution development combines research, industry knowledge, and operational experience. Focused on our customers' needs and current industry demands, Sanjel's technical professionals make informed decisions toward the advancement of our services and products by combining critical thinking with technical ingenuity. Our customers and industry receive the latest in technical advancement as we provide ongoing innovative solutions that further enhance Sanjel's high quality service offerings.
