Company Profile


ITS Electronics is a leading global supplier of advanced microwave and millimetre wave solid state power amplifiers (SSPA), frequency converters, transceivers, radios and intelligent wireless subsystems for commercial, government and defence customers. The company is housed in a modern 36,000 Sq foot Canadian facility that has state of the art design, development and manufacturing capabilities including clean room automated processes. Canadian owned and founded in 1987, ITS Electronics
Inc. supplies the following products and services:

. Standard components from a diversified product line
. Custom design and manufacturing of microwave components and
subsystems
. Build-to-spec and build-to-print of RF and microwave
components and subsystems
. Design and consulting services on a system and subsystem
level in a variety of fields concerning applications of RF,
microwave and millimetre wave based systems.

RF and Microwave Technologies are utilized in a growing number of
fields and applications such as:
. Terrestrial and Satellite communications
. Radar
. Broadcasting and Cable TV
. Navigation
. Electronic Warfare
. Law Enforcement and Intrusion Detection
. Electrooptics and Fiber Optic Information Transmission
. Environment Monitoring
. Air and Ground Traffic Control
. Remote Sensing
. Medical Science

Microwave Components and Subsystems
Microwave components for integration to sub-systems level

Product Name:
Linear Power Amplifier
Solid State/0.8-2 GHz

These class A GaAs FET amplifiers set a new price/performance
standard for wide band power and reliability. They offer very
high 3rd order intercept point, best harmonic performance in the
industry (-25 dBc in-band and -40 dBc outside the band @ P1 dB
GCP) and high efficiency and dynamic range.

Applications include EMC, EW, ECM, communication, instrumentation
and intermodulation and device testing. Fully protected outputs
enable safe operation into any load mismatch including open and
short circuits. Thermal shutdown feature, continuously monitors
the base-plate temperature and automatically shuts the amplifier
off and provides visual front panel alarm if the base-plate
temperature exceeds factory preset limit.

3 300LFM quiet fans provide sufficient cooling to ensure low
junction temperature to guarantee high MTBF.

The unit comes with 24 months warranty.

Product Name:
Wideband Synthesized Test Translator
So wideband that it can be used in different applications as a
test source!

This new wideband synthesized test translator, P/N:
UC-140/1485-003, is part of the ITS growing family of high
performance products. It is used as a transmission path
simulator for DBS systems and provides a convenient way to
accurately characterize these systems. Fully digital compatible,
it includes a variable power noise source, adjustable power
output from -10 dBm down to -70 dBm and computer controlled
frequency selection via RS-232.

The input frequency range, variable in 10 MHz steps, is 920 to
2060 MHz.

The spurious signals level is lower than -40 dBc and image
rejection is better than -50 dBc.

The unit is self-contained in a type 2U (3.5" high) housing
suitable for 19" rack mount.

Technology profile

ITS Electronics was established in 1987. It is a mobile satellite communication technology company that holds ISO 9001:2008 and SAE AS9100 C certification.

ITS designs and builds High Power Solid State Transmitters and Transceivers for SOTMTM, SOTPTM , Fly-Away Airborne and Marine platforms in L, X , Ku, and K/Ka bands. ITS delivered the 125W Ka-Band SSPA.
