Company Profile


Crowe Soberman LLP (formerly Soberman LLP, Chartered Accountants) is one of the premier, full-service, public accounting firms in Ontario. We provide clients with exceptional professional services desgined to meet their special and ongoing needs.

We are proactive and forward-thinking. We work closely with our
clients in the areas of Audit, Advisory, Tax, Business
Valuation, Claims Valuation, Corporate Reorganisation, HR Consulting, Personal
Insolvency, Succession Retirement & Estate Planning.

Crowe Soberman LLP is an independent member of Crowe Horwath International.

Crowe Soberman LLP is currently ranked as one of the top fifteen
accounting firms in Canada. We take pride in the quality and
timeliness of the services we provide to our clients.

Members of:
CPA Ontario

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Audit & Advisory Services
Our Audit & Advisory Services include:

- audit, review and compilation engagements based on client
needs
- assistance with the development of systems tailored to
provide management with timely, accurate and cost-effective
financial information
- bookkeeping
- payroll preparation
- asset management
- cash flow projections

General Tax Services include:
- Personal Tax - income tax return preparation and e-filing,
tax planning and representation to taxation authorities.
- Corporate Tax - corporate tax planning, income tax return
preparation and e-filing, capital tax compliance, payroll tax
compliance, commodity tax, R&D tax credits and representation
to taxation authorities.
- Estates & Trusts - income tax return preparation and e-
filing, tax planning and compliance and planning for the
orderly distribution of assests.

Service Name:
Business Valuation Services
Our Business Valuation Services include:
- Matrimonial Matters (Divorce Settlement, Spousal Support) -
determination of net family property for purposes of
equalization, calculation of income for purposes of support and
tax efficient approaches to settlement.
- Tax and Corporate Reorganization - Valuations for purposes of
family business/estate planning, valuations for utilization of
available tax exemptions and dealing with Canada Customs and
Revenue Agency
- Shareholder and Partnership Agreements - appropriate
valuation clauses, dispute resolution mechanisms and valuation
for purposes of buy-out
- Mediation and Arbitration - dispute resolution alternatives
and dispute resolution of financial issues
- Expert Testimony

Service Name:
Litigation Support Services
Our Litigation Support Services include:
- expert testimony
- wrongful dismissal proceedings
- insurance quantum analysis
- identification and procurement of admissible evidence
- professional negligence claims
- criminal and civil proceeding

Specialized areas of Litigation Support Services include:
- Alternative Dispute Resolution - acting as neutral
independent Arbitrator on financial matters, and advising one
party on financial matters
- Professional Malpractice - assisting counsel with application
of Generally Accepted Accounting Principles and Generally
Accepted Auditing Standards related to Accounting Malpractice
suits, calculation of financial impact of misapplied accounting
standards, and calculation and rebuttal of economic damages
- Business Interruption - analysis of insurance coverage to
determine potential economic damages claim, analyzing and
rebutting economic damages claims prepared by other parties,
and investigation of potentially fraudulent economic damages
claims
- Intellectual Property - patent infringement, trademark
infringement, theft of customer list, determination of
Defendant's sales and profits of allegedly infringed products,
analysis of financial and market data to determine economic
damages, and reasonable royalties and other damages
- Breach of Contract - calculation and potential rebuttal of
economic damages
- Construction - calculation of economic damages related to
construction delay claims, and construction project cost audit

Service Name:
Corporate Workout & Turnaround Strategies
Service Name:
Personal Insolvency
Our reorganization and insolvency specialists provide a wide
range of services designed to maximize recovery by creditors,
or minimize the loss and disruption resulting from financial
reorganization or insolvency. Our services include:
- Recovery Assistance to Creditors
- Financial Reorganization
- Receivership Assignments
- Bankruptcy Services
- Acquisitions and Divestiture Support
- Proposals

Service Name:
Business Advisory Services
Our Business Advisory Services include:
- personal financial planning
- design and implementation of accounting systems
- development and/or refinement of business plans
- shareholder aggreements
- budgeting
- financial projections
- acquisitions and sales of businesses
- business reorganizations
- development of loan arrangements

Service Name:
Forensics
Forensics services include:
- Loss and Damage Quantification - calculation of loss of
income and future care costs, calculation of income replacement
benefits, and calculation of financial damages
- Professional Liability & Negligence - accountants'
malpractice claims, and accoutants' liability and disciplinary
actions
- Fraud & Forensic Accounting - tracing cash and other key
assets, reconstruction of financial information, recovery of
funds, and integrity Risk Management

Service Name:
Estates & Trusts
Our Estates & Trusts group covers administration, estate/tax
planning, valuation and eldercare.

Service Name:
Tax
Our specialty tax services include:
- Personal Wealth Enhancement - planning of personal finances
and compensation, wealth preservation, and succession planning
- Corporate Tax Services - structuring corporate financing,
reorganizations, and other day to day transactions to minimize
tax costs
- International Tax Services - wide optimization of world tax
for businesses and individuals in the global economy
- Acquisitions and Divestiture Support - planning business
purchases and sales, and structuring of mergers and spin-offs
- Corporate Finance - after-tax financing and private and
public offerings
- Business Valuations & Litigation Support - integrating tax
considerations in valuation and litigation assignments
- Commodity Taxes - compliance and planning for GST and retail
sales tax
