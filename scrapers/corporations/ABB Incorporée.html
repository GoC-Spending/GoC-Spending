Primary Business Activity:
Manufacturer / Processor / Producer

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
AARU : Automatic Assured Receptivity Unit

The AARU is a system for wayside dissipation of energy from electrically braking transit cars (regenerative braking). Its real-time controller will select the most appropriate load resistor to assure the efficiency of the system and avoid burning energy from adjacent traction sub-stations. this system allows to remove braking resistors from the trains.



Product Name:
OCS-AGS Catenary Auto Grounding System

The OCS-AGS is designed to safely apply traction power to designated sections of the contact wire in shops in order to run auxiliaries and move trains. The sections are securely grounded whenever power is removed and personnel can safely access car roofs. The OCS-AGS can control both single and double contactor systems.





Product Name:
CONTROL PANELS, FREQUENCY DRIVES

Product Name:
DC Traction Substation Rectifiers and Switchgear

Envitech designs and manufactures switchgears and rectifiers for DC traction power substations (subway, tramway, LRT, monorail, crane and overhead crane). Custom designed, the power can rate up to several M (Mega Watts).



Product Name:
AGS - LT2500 Floating Neg. Auto Grounding Switch

The LT2500 is an Automatic Grounding System for the running rails. If the voltage between the ground and the running rail rises above a certain value (programmable), the running rails will be temporarily grounded to eliminate any electrical shock hazard.



Product Name:
SCADA SYST. OF CONTROL & DATA ACQUISITION

Product Name:
SPRING-OPERATED GROUNDING & TRANSFER SWITCHES

Product Name:
Anti-Arcing Device

The AAD (Anti-Arcing Device) prevents the occuring of electrical arcs when switch trains to another track. This sideway system will eliminate the risks associated with arcs, i.e. flash, explosion noise and fire ignition and contributes in reducing the wear of the third rail and the collector shoe.



Product Name:
Breaker Tester Cart

The BTC is designed to test breakers (i.e. DC traction breakers). it can be used during routine maintenance operations or after a repair / overhaul of a breaker. Providing up to 15 000 Amps, it can be used to calibrate and test both the breaker and its associated protection relay.



Product Name:
Variable Speed Turbine Controller

Envitech designs and manufactures variable speed turbine controllers for wind mills or hydro turbines. The controller will optimize the power output according to the wind speed or the water flow. Envitech will also integrate the power converter needed to connect the system to the grid.



Product Name:
Optivolt

The Optivolt System will control, optimize and secure the production of electricity produced by several different sources (diesel generator, wind mill, etc.) while reducing drastically the fuel consumption. The Optivolt System is well suited for off-grid electrical power plant optimization.



Product Name:
SPSC : Shop Power Supply Cart

Envitech designs and manufactures a 30 kW Shop Power Supply Cart, designed for transit maintenance shops (subway, tramway, LRT, monorail). This power supply will re-energize sections of the train to allow the completion of tests or repairs while the train is disconnected from the catenary or the third rail.
