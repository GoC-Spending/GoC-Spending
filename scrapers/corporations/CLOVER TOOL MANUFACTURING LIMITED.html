Company Profile


Clover Tool has achieved exceptional growth and success during the years of operation. We attribute this success to our versatility and flexibility in accessing the automotive, housewares, and commercial industries. We offer our customers a wide range of services, from design, to die manufacturing, through to metal stamping welding and assembly, emphasizing the highest level of quality at all times.

In design we employ a combination of computerized and traditional
methods. Computerized programming is utilized for both Wire EDM
and CNC machines.

We successfully meet customer deadlines, relying on our in-house
capabilities, beginning with the conceptual design, through to
the final precision product. Our networking of up to date
technology and designer expertise results in an accurate state
of the industry product, both SPC approved and reliability
tested.

Clover Tool has continuously met the challenges required in
the complexity and diversification of producing a final product.
Meeting these challenges has given us the experience and
knowledge necessary to be successful in a competitive market.

At Clover Tool we design and build dies for a wide spectrum of
needs, including progressive dies with multiple stations,
fineblank, draw, hand transfer and compound dies. We also
build jigs, and fixtures. Our professional staff offers
assistance to our customers to better understand and service
their needs.

Clover Tool is proud of it's expert staff, who continuously
undergo training and educational programs, which have been
established by senior management. These programs assures the
continuation of our commitment to quality ans dependability
that has allowed Clover Tool to retain its reputation of skilled
engineering, quality and precision tooling.

Our unique quality control/assurance department in our metal
stamping division results in maximizing productivity, meeting
our customers stringent specifications. Quality assurance
testing utilizes high technology, and computerized and digital
checking instruments necessary for reliable, repetitive testing.
Meeting exacting customer specifications results in a quality
product from any of our 100 ton to 800 ton presses. The
necessity for quality assurance is maintained in all aspect
of the tooling and stamping process, insuring that critical
tolerances are met.

Clover Tool's future competitiveness depends on maintaining cost
efficiency and expert tooling, with a strict adherence to our
customers' standards.
