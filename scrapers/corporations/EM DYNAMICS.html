Company Profile


EM Dynamics partners with you to design, manufacture and integrate mechanical products and equipment. With over 40 years of experience, our clients around the world have relied on our expertise in engineering services and specialized capabilities that include high precision metal fabrication, CNC machining, integrated assembly, testing and supply chain management. We’re here to help.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
1. Metal Fabrication
We have over 40 years of metal fabrication expertise in providing solutions for comprehensive designs. We provide high precision custom metal parts, enclosures and cabinets. Our dynamic engineering team supports our clients’ requirements using computer-aided manufacturing software to prototype and manufacture single component and more complex assembles at a fast rate; helping our clients complete their projects on time. For custom sheet metal fabricating, look no further – we are certain to meet your requirements with precision and punctuality.

Metal Fabrication Processes Include:
• CNC Laser Cutting
• CNC Punching
• CNC Press Brake Forming
• Secondary Milling Operations
• Welding – Mig, Tig and Arc
• Spot Welding
• Graining, Grinding and Tumbling
• Sand Blasting
• Chem Film
• Hardware Installation
• Fully Integrated and Mechanical Assembly

Service Name:
2. Machining
We are dedicated to manufacturing close tolerance CNC Machining and critical function parts with state-of-the-art multi-axis CNC Milling and CNC Turning centers. We work with a broad range of polymers and metal alloys supporting quick turnaround prototypes, custom designed parts and production runs. When you partner with us, you get much more than an experienced team; you get drastically reduced lead times, faster delivery for production orders and lower costs for your project requirements. From prototyping to short, long or complex production runs, we are here to provide you with all of your machining needs. We machine parts with tolerances up to +/- 0.0005.

Service Name:
3. Integrated Assembly
Our value added integrated assembly department is another reason to partner with our vertically integrated team and facility. Our electrical assembly team provides specialty in circuit board integration, testing, kitting, soldering, crimping, wiring, labeling and many other technical services. Our mechanical assembly team provides your product with a sense of cohesion through a variety of processes such as riveting, PEM-insertion, hardware, hinges, adhesive and several other mechanical applications.

Service Name:
5. Testing & Systems Integration
Our innovative approach to product integration and testing provides our clients with a competitive edge. We are continuously focusing on the needs of our customers. We believe in creating quality products through innovative and efficient practices. In order to optimize time-to-market, our vast integration and testing capabilities improve our First Pass Yield (FPY) results at Final Test; ensuring a quality end product for our customers.

Working closely with our customers, we incorporate component, board, and assembly level testing. We then ensure that the components of your application work cohesively. Through the use of specific Customer Finished Equipment (CFE), customized software and test plans, Functional Testing is performed to capture your precise specification requirements are achieved. In the end, these test strategies come together to ensure quality and customer satisfaction by eliminating supply-line inefficiencies and improving time-to-market.

Service Name:
4. Engineering
Customers utilize our thorough engineering, technology and manufacturing expertise to achieve their vision while reducing design iterations and costs. Key success metrics for our customers are time to market and cost to market, which can be negatively impacted if the design proves difficult to manufacture when volume demands hit. DFX Engineering creates efficient process flows to eliminate excessive operations and ease manufacturability. We minimize the risk of over-expenditure through our well integrated manufacturing team and advanced technologies. Our value-added services have proven to reduce cost and accelerate time to market.

Business success is dependent on effective New Product Introductions and reduced time to market. New products demand that product design and development processes are supported with a prototype and projected volume manufacturing. Our engineering and contract manufacturing support teams routinely interact with our clients from the developmental phase through to the completion of the manufacturing processes.

Our extensive range of turnkey and value-added design and engineering services complement our full system, integrated manufacturing solutions strategy. From prototype to finished product, we bring our customers’ projects to market using these enhanced tools and processes:

• Design for Manufacturability (DFM)/ Design for Assembly (DFA)
• Feasibility Studies
• Concept Development
• Modeling and Analysis
• Prototyping
• Product Commercialization and Militarization
• DFX Engineering (Design for Cost, Assembly, Manufacturability, Testability, Reliability, Serviceability)
• Validation and Verification
• Regulatory Testing and Certification
• Contract Manufacturing
• Guidance on Component Selection
• Chassis Design
• Cable Design and Test Planning
• Innovative Technical Solutions for Micro-Packaging
• Complex Functional Testing
• NPI and Rapid Proto Build Process
• Post-NPI Build Services
• Feedback on Improving Product Quality and Manufacturability
