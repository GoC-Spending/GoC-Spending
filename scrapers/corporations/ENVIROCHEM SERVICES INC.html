Company Profile


COMPANY OVERVIEW: On-Line Corporate Environmental Policies and Procedures:


Emergency Response Planning: Detailed Contingency Plans; call Out Lists. Event Tracking (Fires, Spills, Disasters, Regulatory Inspections): Reporting; generation and recording of required action and follow-up. Hazardous Waste Tracking: Inventory management; TDG waste name, class; storage sites: layouts and details; date in, date out; shipper and disposal site; manifest tracking. Tank Management: maintenance; inspections. Chemical Management: Inventory. Agency Inspection Planning: Comprehensive response plans; legal sampling; chain of custody details. Human Resources Management and Training: Complete personnel training records. Custom Features Can Be Designed to Meet Specific Needs.


Envirochem Services was formed as a specialized consulting firm in 1984. The firm provides integrated engineering and scientific services for the safe management of toxic chemicals and also provides specialized services in the following areas: Assessment: State-of-the-art determination of environmental risks associated with the presence of toxic chemicals. Control: Definition of procedural and engineering controls for industrial activity involving environmental risks. Communication: Presentation of technical information regarding the handling of chemicals.


Envirochem has a deep-rooted commitment to the protection of society and the environment from exposure to chemicals. The Company is dedicated to providing services of the highest standards. Since its inception, Envirochem has established and maintained a reputation consistent with these goals.


Corporate Structure: Envirochem Services is a corporate partnership with two principals; Mr. T.W. Finnbogason and Dr. D.E. Konasewich. In 1988, the principals formed a separate corporation, Envirochem Special Projects Inc., and all Envirochem projects are now conducted under this corporate entity.

Envirochem Personnel: Envirochem personnel are scientists and engineers at the Ph.D. level, and include expertise in: Chemical engineering; environmental chemistry; environmental engineering; hazardous waste treatment; process engineering; soil science.


Envirochem maintains an analytical laboratory to provide support services for its field investigation.


Experience and Qualifications: Envirochem has extensive experience in the safe management of chemicals and special wastes. Since its inception, Envirochem has undertaken projects for both the public and private sectors in the areas of: Contaminated site assessment; dangerous goods transportation; development of codes of practice for industries using chemicals; environmental audits; environmental impact assessment; expert testimony on hazardous waste disposal practices; hazardous waste disposal technology assessment; industrial facility assessment; inventories of hazardous materials; public education regarding the toxic effects of chemicals; quantitative risk assessment; remediation programs; worker training for chemical handling.


Envirochem has considerable international experience, including projects in New Zealand, Australia, Sri Lanka, Malaysia and the United States.

Envirochem's - Environmental Data and Information Tracking System (EDITS(TM)).
Responding to the needs of our corporate clients,we have developed a computer-based Environmental Data and Information Tracking System (EDITS(TM)).


EDITS(TM) is a user-friendly, proactive, software solution to managing the complex environmental challenges in industrial operations. On-line and network access allows operational staff and corporate management to share the environmental data, policies, procedures, and reports needed to make important decisions related to environmental risks, regulatory compliance, pollution prevention, crisis management, safety, and cost control. On-line policy and procedure manuals eliminates shelves of out-of-date manuals.


DITS(TM) includes built-in security to limit access and to control data changes. The soft-ware can manage information from numerous sites, to create both individual site and corporate reports.


EDITS(TM) provides a framework for an ISO 14000 compliant and auditable workplace system. The software has been developed to respond to the trend towards the internationalisation of environmental control standards and incorporates the key elements, functions, and programs required for continued environmental improvement.


ENVIROCHEM'S other software applications include: Sample Management Environmental Permit; administration systems; electronic policy and procedure manuals. Other custom environmental software applications can be developed to meet your needs. This proven system, developed by environmental management, engineering, and software professionals, is now installed and operational at over twenty industrial and government sites. It is a series of standard modules that can be customised to meet your corporation's specific needs. On-site or on-line support, training, and communications services are provided. Regular upgrades are available to meet changing regulatory and client requirements.


EDITS(TM) Key Features: The following features are included: Calendar: Weekly notification of scheduled action items; yearly planning and tracking. Environmental Audits: Trend tracking; corrective actions, ranking, tracking; costs. Permits: Summary Description and Titles; monitoring/reporting requirements; scanned copies of actual permits; discharge Fee Calculations. Environmental Monitoring Data: Detailed Data Summaries; statistical analyses; graphical presentation; data filters. Environmental Management Reports: Customised Templates; outstanding/completed corrective action items and cost; monitoring data with graphs; event summaries. Regulations: Hypertext Links to Pertinent Federal and Provincial Legislation; regular updates. Risk Assessment and Risk Management.


ENVIROCHEM ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT: Envirochem is a leading provider of scientific and engineering services related to environmental management. We specialise in: Environmental Auditing; pollution prevention planning contaminated site investigation & remediation toxic chemical management; risk assessment; risk management; regulatory planning & permitting; environmental strategic planning; environmental management systems and, environmental management software development.


We provide environmental management services to both public and private sectors; from wood products manufacturing facilities to air force bases.


The strength and consistency of this diverse client base demonstrates our total commitment to excellence in all aspects of our work.
