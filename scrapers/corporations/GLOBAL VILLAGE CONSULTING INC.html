Company Profile


Today's Public Health sector presents complex health information management challenges. When public and private sector organizations need solutions they can trust, they turn to ACG. Since 2002, this internationally recognized, highly adept team of experts has been the recognized leader in critical client health information management services.

Using extensive knowledge and skills gained from invaluable field-level experience, we develop solutions that lead to improved health delivery services in Public Health, Epidemiology, Population Health, Chronic/Communicable Disease Management and Surveillance.

As a leader in health information management, the ACG team focuses on helping our clients achieve quality and cost effective improvements that assist in the vital role of improving the health outcomes of people.

Services  

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Project Management
ACG has a successful history of projects completed on time and on budget. We utilize customized project management methodologies for managing and delivering large information systems projects. We adhere to a comprehensive project management methodology developed by the Project Management Institute (PMI), the world's leading association for the project management profession. Our Project Management Certified professionals incorporate best practices tailored to fit Public Health. These best practices, applied effectively, have been proven to significantly increase the likelihood of projects delivering high-quality results on time and on budget, which increases user buy-in.

Service Name:
Epidemiology & Disease Surveillance
The ACG team offers extensive expertise in the area of health surveillance, with in-depth knowledge of previous efforts undertaken by the Public Health Agency of Canada, many of the provinces and the Canadian Integrated Public Health Surveillance Collaborative in the area of health surveillance.

We have participated in the national and international development of disease surveillance systems by advising IT professionals, health professionals and policy makers on the development, maintenance and use of health information, tools and skills to enable timely and informed decision-making.

ACG has designed data access schemas that support production of surveillance products and the associated metadata. Our successful results are evident in improved public health policies, programs and interventions that protect and promote the health of Canadians.

Service Name:
Facilitation & Public Health Business Analysis
Our team has facilitated Public Health workshops at the international, federal, provincial and local levels. Our focus is enabling health surveillance professionals to monitor health trends, identify emerging diseases or threats to health, improve understanding of health determinants and provide input for planning and evaluating services to the public.

Service Name:
Information Management & Business Analysis
ACG has extensive technical expertise to ensure the success of any project, including:
* requirements and joint application development sessions
* data mapping
* data conversion projects
* creating application interfaces
* messaging.

We specialize in RDMS and Business Intelligence Tools. Our senior business analysts can expertly provide the interface between business experts and technical staff to ensure effective communication.


Service Name:
Surveillance Products
ACG has worked with local Health Authorities, provinces, territories and Health Canada to develop key surveillance reports. These range from standardized reports for disease line listing to customized products that outline a Health Authority's immunization uptake, or support analysis of birth events in a particular region.
We have produced health surveillance products that are
used to:
* systematically monitor trends in health and
health determinants
* provide input for evidence-based decisions
* identify outbreaks, emerging issues or threats to health
* develop policies and programs to manage health risks, and for health services planning.
Using these reports improves understanding of health determinants, facilitates research, and gives individuals, health providers, governments and communities the information necessary to take action to protect and improve health.

Service Name:
Software Deployment & Support
The ACG team is also expert at managing implementation, managing change within organizations, and delivering professional training. ACG has developed and implemented training strategies in British Columbia, Alberta, Saskatchewan, Northwest Territories, Newfoundland, Ontario, Yukon, Manitoba and the Bahamas.

ACG was pivotal in the successful completion of the $3M Integrated Public Health Information System (iPHIS) software development project. The iPHIS application was implemented across British Columbia and Canada.

Service Name:
Training Strategies & Support
ACG staff are experts at managing change within organizations and delivering professional training. We have developed and implemented province-wide deployment and training strategies throughout Canada.
