6P Marketing is a full-service marketing firm that collaborates with entrepreneurs and brand leaders to help move their business forward.

Primary sectors served include Technology & Software, Agribusiness & Food, Professional Services, Construction & Real Estate, Manufacturing and Community Development & Not For Profits / Associations.

Whether it's solving a complex problem, building a brand or creating and implementing tactics – 6P Marketing is here to help.

Success stories:

Commissionaires
Canada's largest security guard company.

Working with this evolving company has been a pleasure and a successful challenge. 6P was hired to promote Commissionaires' new services that were to be offered to the public at large.
For over 80 years, Commissionaires has been Canada's largest security guard company. Commissionaires continue to evolve to meet the needs of today's public and private market. They have relied on 6P to help them meet those needs and continue to grow their market share.

---

Manitoba Pork Council
Organization representing and working for the pork industry.

The pork industry is one of Manitoba's largest industries, providing huge economic benefit to the province and Manitobans. Challenged by negative public opinion and misleading media representation, Manitoba Pork Council hired 6P to develop a marketing strategy that included a communications and public relations strategy that would reposition the Manitoba pork industry ensuring a strong future.

---

Academy Road Business Improvement Zone (BIZ)
Non-profit business agency working to improve business conditions in a specific geographic area.

6P was hired to research and write a marketing strategy containing a dynamic mix of conventional and non-conventional forms of advertising promotional opportunities and strategic planning within the capabilities of the Academy Road BIZ and their promotional budget. This strategy also promoted the business members and would improve the business conditions within its zone.

---

Share Our Strength
A leading North American anti-hunger organization.

For eight years, 6P had the honour of providing graphic design, advertising, corporate branding and strategic planning to the local chapter of Share Our Strength, a leading North American anti-hunger organization based in Washington.
Share Our Strength is very strong in the United States with a number of international sponsors such as American Express, Tyson Foods and the Food Network. In Canada, however, its public awareness had not been as strong, or supported in years past. On a local level, the devoted volunteer committee with donated 6P services, had transformed the events into successful, sold-out fund raisers while building a corporate brand. In part, because of its new stature, honourary chairs have included many icons, both business and political, including Hartley T. Richardson, former Manitoba Premier Gary Filmon, H. Sanford Riley and former Winnipeg Mayor Bill Norrie.
With any charity, the focus and ultimate goal is to raise funds. Because of the dedicated volunteers and the support provided by 6P, the past number of years had been overwhelming successes, boasting sold out events and record sponsorship donations. 6P applied strategic marketing, graphic design and media placement/sponsorship to build public awareness and private interest in an effort to grow sponsorship contributions.

Testimonial:

"They have provided us with innovative strategies that have increased the awareness of the Commissionaires profile to the public. They are professionals in their field and we are excited to continue this relationship into the future."
- Tom Reimer, CEO, Commissionaires Manitoba

"It has been a pleasure working with you on the ['Top 12 Reasons Why Now is the Best Time to Build' campaign.] I appreciate your patience with this novice in the field of marketing! I look forward to future projects where you can once again display your creative and innovative talents."
- Mike Moore, President, Manitoba Home Builders' Association

"Robert Mensies' commitment to Share Our Strength has extended beyond his company's sponsorship of our annual events. His passion for the recipients, coupled with his participation and leadership of the local events, have helped Share Our Strength events grow and give back to our community. He has dedicated seven years... for two years (2005 & 2006), he served as Chair of the Taste of the Nation event, donating many hours of leadership to build and strengthen the event. Robert's creativity and ideas to increase fundraising opportunities helped further the success of the Taste event and worked tirelessly to enhance our profile within the community."
- Eric Schon, Canadian President, Share Our Strength Canada
