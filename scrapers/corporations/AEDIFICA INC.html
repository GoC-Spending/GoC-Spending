Company Profile


Ædifica is an award-winning North American architecture and design firm that offers integrated services tailored to the needs of its clients. In addition to its core expertise in architecture, workspace environment, commercial design and engineering, Ædifica has extensive experience in large scale institutional and commercial projects, corporate architecture and real estate development. The firm is also a leader in sustainable design.

Ædifica can count on more than 140 professionals: architects, interior designers, engineers, graphic artists and technicians. Together, they have been developing design solutions that are unique and meaningful for more than 30 years. Initially operating in Canada and the United-States, Ædifica is now also present in Haiti, Europe and Asia.
