Company Profile


Carmel Industries manufactures two product lines: Waxes and
Industrial Markers. We produce wax for the jewellery, investment
castings (foundry) and dental industries. We have a myriad of
waxes including: Injection Casting Wax, Inlay Wax, Sticky Wax,
Wire Wax, Bite Wafers, Occlusal Rims, Utility Wax, Bite Sticks,
Boxing Wax, Orthopaedic Wax Bites, Relief Wax, Base-Plate Wax,
sprue wax, dipping wax, paraffin wax, microcrystalline wax, Bees
Wax, Candelila Wax, Carnauba Wax, Montan Wax, etc. We also carry
Gypsums, Dental Stone and Plasters for the former industries.

Carmel's other manufacturing division produces markers of special
interest to the following industries: automotive, tire
retreading, welding, steel, aluminum, lumber, livestock and
textile. Carmel produces the following types of markers:
Permanent Felt Tip Markers, Wet & Dry Erase Markers, Valve
Activated Markers, Ball-Tip Markers, Paint Crayons, Lumber
Crayons, Tube Markers, Tire Paint Markers, All-Purpose Wax
Crayons, Smouldering Steel Crayons, Hot Mark Crayons, Regular &
Glo Tailor's Crayons, Railroad Chalk, Half Moon Chalk, School
Chalk, Chalk-Line Powder, Soapstone, China Marker, All Pencil,
Carpenter Pencil, Tailor Chalk, Tailor CHalk Wheel, Tailor's
Pencils, Stamping Powder, Laundry Pens, Glo Tips, Beeswax,
Disappearing Felt Pens, Livestock Crayons, Tattoo Ink, Branding
Stamp Pad Ink, etc.
