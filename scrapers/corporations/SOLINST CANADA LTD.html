Company Profile


Solinst has manufactured high quality hydrogeological instrumentation in Ontario, Canada since 1975. Solinst instruments are used in wells and open water, for site characterization studies, long-term groundwater monitoring programs, spill investigations, hazardous materials storage monitoring, landfill and waste site monitoring, mine water monitoring, salt water intrusion studies, potable water recharge area studies, and groundwater remediation. Instruments are designed to obtain groundwater samples, data on water levels, and some water quality parameters.

Standard instruments are made routinely for stock, and customized items can be made to suit particular needs. A strong commitment to research and development and a close association with the University of Waterloo and other research institutions keeps the Solinst range of groundwater monitoring instrumentation at the forefront of technological advances.

Primary Product Offering: The Solinst range of high quality groundwater monitoring instruments includes: water level data loggers with GSM and radio telemetry systems; water quality dataloggers; water level indicators; oil/water interface probes; multilevel groundwater monitoring systems, including the Waterloo System and CMT® Multilevel System which are ideal for assessing contaminant plumes, to enable remediation efforts to be more accurately focussed.

Other groundwater samplers include the Bladder Pump which gives the most accurate samples for analysis of volatile organic content (VOC); a Peristaltic Pump for sampling up to the suction lift limit; a discrete interval sampler for obtaining representative groundwater samples from below oil layers or for obtaining samples of the hydrocarbon product layers themselves; point source bailers, drive-point piezometers, and low-pressure packers and filters. All Solinst instrumentation is designed to be practical, easy-to-use and highly accurate, with both portable and dedicated equipment available.

Geographic Marketing Activities:

Solinst is well established in Canada, the USA, in many European countries, much of the Middle East, Asia, South Africa, Australia, New Zealand and most South American countries. In other countries where Solinst has not yet established an agent, we are sell directly to end users but would also actively assess any company wishing to represent us, preferably companies with hydrogeological or hydrological knowledge.

Product Name:
Solinst groundwater samplers.
Solinst offers a wide range of groundwater samplers for any application. From pumps that meet the highest U.S. EPA standards for groundwater monitoring through to a low cost dedicated sampling pump, virtually any sampling need can be met. Capabilities include sampling depths up to and exceeding 2,000 ft. (600 m) and in tubes as narrow as 3/8" (1.9 cm).

The Discrete Interval Sampler collects samples from specific depths within monitoring wells with minimal disturbance, offering samples from below oil/product layers, DNAPL sampling and from discrete inflows in boreholes.

Packers are also available. Waterloo Drive-Point Piezometers enable economic groundwater sampling in suitable soils without a drill rig. The stainless steel tip connects to LDPE or Teflon (TM) tubing for optimal sample integrity.

Product Name:
Solinst water level meter
The Solinst Model 101 water level meter is ruggedly designed to withstand field use in wells. Constructed of a sturdy, easy-to-manage reel and a stranded stainless steel cable with PVDF marked tape, Solinst water level meters are available for depths up to 10,000 ft. (3,000 m).

Product Name:
Waterloo Multilevel System.
This is ideal for acquiring data on groundwater located in bedrock or in overburden. Permanent and removable systems are available. The System minimizes the number of boreholes required to obtain hydraulic parameters and groundwater samples. It also enables groundwater professionals to obtain three dimensional groundwater information, without the cost of drilling many single monitoring wells.

The flexible, modular system can be adapted to the individual needs of each application. Engineered seals prevent cross-flow between monitoring zones. This cross-contamination may be further avoided by the availability of dedicated monitoring instruments. Both these features can dramatically reduce the ongoing cost of field time. Removable systems with pneumatic packers are also available.

Technology profile

Solinst places great importance on our ability to ensure a steady improvement in the product line. The research and development department has an 8" (200mm) well to a depth of 328 ft. (100m) for test purposes. Strong links are maintained with Groundwater Researchers at the University of Waterloo, and industry associations. Four CNC machines aid high quality, high tolerance manufacturing and two test wells on site, one to 100m/300ft, help in ensuring high quality products. Solinst prides itself on the quality of its service, using technical sales staff who are highly qualified.
