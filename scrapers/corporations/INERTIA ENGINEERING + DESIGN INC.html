Company Profile


Inertia Engineering + Design provides mechanical and electrical engineering, consulting and product development services that deliver exceptional customer experiences and results.

IE+D is a trusted product development engineering partner who understands the importance of getting our clients' products from initial concept to final production quickly and cost effectively. Our creative, collaborative and practical approach coupled with our concurrent engineering and quality-driven design process results in value-focused, common-sense solutions that help shorten product development time and increase client bottom line - what we call Real World Thinking.

Inertia provides manufacturers and start-ups with turn-key engineering teams (and solutions) helping them to significantly and quickly grow their businesses. Inertia is not a staffing firm nor does it "rent out" its people for resource. We believe we can bring more value to our clients by taking care of their challenges using our professional, efficient and collaborative product development engineering methods.

After we worked with the Inertia team, we knew that we found an engineering partner to help us reach our goals."

Dean Davenport, President, Engineered Products and Services Group, IMT Partnership


LightSpeed Environmental - Experience with Inertia Engineering
"Our company was formed to commercialize the QuickSider Battery-Electric delivery van, the first prototype of which was developed by our primary stockholder, Unicell Body Company. During the design and development of this initial prototype, Inertia Engineering lead the mechanical design and engineering, DFMEA evaluations, and overall testing for the completed proof-of-concept vehicle (QS-1).....We have complete confidence in Inertias team for all aspects of our highly-technical project. Included among the services provided are project leadership, design management, engineering and testing protocols, quality engineering such as the aforementioned DFMEA and DVP&R work, and the ability  unique for a firm this size  to perform all vehicle ride, handling, steering, and braking development work. Our experience to date has been outstanding and we do not hesitate to recommend Inertia Engineering to any prospective customer.
