Company Profile


Established in 1992, EXACT G.T.Q. Inc. dedicates itself to the
continuous improvement of products and processes in the
manufacturing industry.

EXACT is well known for its judicious interventions in the field
of design and engineering, especially in Geometric Tolerancing
and Dimensioning (GD&T). In addition, EXACT offers its services
in process improvement, quality management (QS-9000 and PPAP ),
and implementation of information technologies.

Due to its expertise in data analysis and professionalism
demonstrated by its consulting team, EXACT has known how to
forge
for itself a position of preference among its customers. In
addition to simplifying daily operations and reducing the
decision making time, its interventions significantly reduce the
cost of production and inspection.

On its second year of existence, EXACT has developed
user-friendly software, which effectively target the concrete
needs of the manufacturing industry. Today, their two principal
software are: Summum DOE and Positron Y14.5. They allow
efficient data analysis and avoid costly errors.

EXACT's mission is to assist companies in increasing their
efficiency with the communication of know-how and the use of
high-end technology, resulting in a rapid increase of its
client's profits.

Our services:

Design & Engineering:
Our service in Design & Engineering now includes coaching and
training in geometric tolerance (GD&T), implementation and
management of design protocol and implementation of Quality
Function Deployment.

Production and Quality:
With the growing importance of QS-9000 and ISO 9000 quality
assurance system in the automotive and many other industries,
Exact has committed to help companies to meet today's stringent
regulatory. Exact's service in assistance during and after
ISO/QS-9000 accreditation helps you in the quality system
implementation process as well as acting as a third party
quality
auditor. We also support the implementation of PPAP technical
tools where we will help in key characteristics selection, FMEA
approach supervision, Statistical Process Control
implementation,
etc. Our intervention in industrial engineering aims at the
human-machines-materials-capital component optimisation of the
production system, in order to improve your productivity.

Optimisation and system integration:
In this highly competitive environment, companies must
increasingly manage design, production and inspection quality.
We guide your company in identifying the main causes
that affect your productivity. Exact also will introduce and
guide you with its many years of experience in this structured
experiment methodology-Design of Experiments. A Design of
Experiments eliminates the traditional costly trial-and-error
(one factor at a time) method, instead it uses a certain
statistical techniques to help in reaching optimum product
quality with minimum cost. Our experience in industrial
information technologies can help you in many fields such as
system integration, setting up your network and control loop
system, etc. This stage facilitates the management and the
control functional production system. Our industrial computing
service also includes the development and design of software
applications in response to specific quality and productivity
needs (data analysis, statistical control, etc.).

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Summum DOE v.2
Everyday, your company faces local and international
competition.
To stay competitive, you must increase the added value of your
products and reduce your design and manufacturing costs. The
design of experiments is a fast and efficient tool to help you
reach these goals.

Summum DOE 2.0 adds two (2) design methods (Mixture Design and
Fractional Factorial) in addition to Taguchi, Plackett-Burman
and
Full Factorial, allowing a much greater flexibility to solve
your
design problems or improve the efficiency of your processes.

With this software, you will be able to:
* Reduce launch time of your products to the market.
* Eliminate the expensive trial and error method.
* Rapidly identify main causes affecting quality of your
products
and productivity of your processes.
* Gather significant information for a better control of your
products and processes .

Summum DOE is outstandingly simple to use. Engineers and
technicians who design and test products, and production staff
operators who work on processes, need quick and efficient
solutions to their problems. They usually don't have much time
to learn complex statistical concepts. Easily solving complex
problems is what Summum DOE does best. For example, the
creation
of your experimental design is greatly facilitated through the
construction of a Causes Tree, because it helps you define the
basic elements of a model such as factors, interactions, and
performance indices to be optimized. In Summum DOE, the Causes
Tree module has been completely revised! With only a few clicks
of the mouse, you can enter or edit all your project parameters!
Once the Causes Tree is completed, Summum DOE Design Wizard
presents all available experimental designs which reduce the
number of tests and the experimental costs.

New and improved graphic and statistical tools of Summum DOE
decrease the analysis effort of your experimental results.
Analysis Of Variance (ANOVA), regression and effects graphs
allow
you to identify factors and interactions which have a high
influence on the performance indices.

People, who are familiar with design of experiments, will be
very
satisfied with this software. Here are a few examples of the new
commands and options included in Summum DOE:
* Drag and Drop of every element of the Causes Tree.
* Interaction and Standard Run columns displayed in the
Experimental Table.
* New Run sheets printout.
* Cut/Paste functions throughout the software.
* Residual, Response Surface and Contour Graphs.
* Factorial and regression analysis.
* Only five steps!

To optimize your products and processes, you just have to go
through five steps by using the following icons.

1 Causes Tree
Use a Causes Tree to define the performance indices of your
product or process. Complete it with factors, (with up to 12
levels!) and interactions.

2 Experimental Table
Whether you are new to experimental design or a professional,
Summum DOE 2.0 works for you! Is building an experimental
design
a heavy burden for you? Using the Design Wizard, you will get
structured experimental designs (Taguchi, Plackett-Burman,
Mixture, Full or Fractional Factorial) minimizing the number of
experiments.

3 Analysis Table
The software computes the Analysis Of Variance (ANOVA) and/or
Regression Analysis. The results help you identify major
influential factors and interactions relative to various
performance indices.

4 Graphs
Use effects graphs (factors and interactions) to interpret your
data and get the optimal factor settings. Create Response
Surface
and Contour Graphs to estimate a quadratic model's surface.

5 Confirmation Test
Use the Confirmation Test to validate your model. Summum DOE
uses
a statistical test to check if the results obtained are
reproducible.

Summum DOE is user-friendly!
* Push-button and Vertical Menu interface insure an easy way to
go from one module to another.
* Print Causes Tree's report, Run Sheets for data input, graphs
and statistics.
* Powerful graphic tool provides clear and comprehensive
understanding of the experimental results to the user.
* Also available in French.

With Summum DOE, you get answers quickly!
Summum DOE offers the user an extensive on-line help as well as
comprehensive manual and samples!

Minimum requirements
Personal computer (80386 or higher) using Microsoft(r) Windows
(r)
95 / Windows NT(r) 4.0 or later versions.

Please visit our WEB site http://www.exactgtq.com for
more information concerning our product Summum DOE 2.0.

Product Name:
Positron Y14.5
Everyday, your company faces local and international
competition.
To stay competitive, you must increase the reliability and the
efficiency of your measurement methods. Usually, industrial
standards establish the rules that quality controllers must use
and follow.

Unfortunately, even if the rules are clear, they are difficult
to
implement. Among all measurements and checking operations,
inspecting geometric position tolerances remains one of the most
complicated and difficult tasks.

In spite of the fact that automated tools make measuring easier,
analysing, interpreting and writing reports are still tricky and
time consuming. Imagine the following situations:
* Material conditions and their effects on tolerances (bonus).
* The relative position of a set of holes.
* The presence or absence of datums.
* Calculating corrections and adjustments.

Based on the international standard ASME Y14.5-1994, Positron
Y14.5 software allows you to analyse, interpret and write an
inspection report in an efficient, fast and reliable way. From
your manual or automated measurements, Positron Y14.5 gives you
automatically all the inspection steps to follow for geometric
position tolerances for any tolerance specifications.

The use of Positron Y14.5 gives you many beneficial effects,
such
as:
* Greatly reducing the time taken for analysis and
interpretation.
* Eliminating uncertain and restrictive manual methods.
* Eliminating misinterpretation between suppliers and clients.
* Promptly identifying mistakes and automatically calculating
adjustments and corrections.
* Precisely identifying real and effective tolerances of the
measured parts.
* Keeping track of all your measurements and analysis.
* Increasing productivity and efficiency of your inspection
processes.

Positron Y14.5 is an outstanding software, easy to learn and
use.
Using a simple, logical and user-friendly sequence, it performs
an analysis using the position tolerances as defined on a
technical plan. Then, the software automatically creates a data
sheet, with a graphic window, showing all the elements to be
analysed. Positron Y14.5 also offers various graphic tools, in
order to analyse and interpret measurement results rapidly and
efficiently.

Finally, Positron Y14.5 allows you to register, with your
measurements results, all the information needed to keep track
of
your projects, as required by QS-9000, D1-9000, ISO 9000 and
ISO-10012 Quality Assurance Standards (project title, part name
and number, supplier, description, date, instrument number,
employees etc.).

Characteristics
To make an analysis, just go through the following steps:

1 Data Input
You can define your problem specifications (number of elements
to
analyse, material conditions, datums, tolerance values etc.)
through various questions found in three sequential windows
(wizard).

2 Data sheet and graphic window
Is calculating each element's deviation and allowable BONUS, as
requested by the rules of the standard Y14.5, a heavy burden for
you? Let Positron Y14.5 takes charge of it! Since an
illustration
talks by itself, Positron Y14.5 shows all the elements, which
have relevant information, in a graphic window.

3 Validation
Positron Y14.5 gives you an optimised and completely automated
solution to validate your analysis. It also includes a manual
validation solution, which allows the user to set the position's
pattern of the analysed elements. In all cases, the results are
systematically calculated.

4 Printed Report
To get a report, just press one button! The report contains all
the general data (company, date, part number etc.),
measurements,
deviations and the validation results. You may even have your
final report as a data sheet or graph.
Positron Y14.5 is user friendly!

* Simple menus and push button interfaces.
* Automated calculations.
* Access to all the information from the graphic window.
* Copy and Paste functions that allow you to easily transfer
data
from Positron Y14.5 to any other software.
* Contextual Help that lets you find your answers quickly.
* Samples which are included to help the user.
* Also available in French.

Our dynamic graphic tool!
Positron Y14.5 software uses a graphic window to help you
interpret the results. All the essential information and
functions of the graphic window are gathered in a graphic
control, which makes Positron Y14.5 an easy-to-use software.

Minimum requirements
* Personal computer (80486 or higher) using Microsoft(r)
Windows(r) 95/Windows NT(r) 4.0 (or later versions).
* 8 Mb RAM (16 Mb recommended).
* 2 Mb hard disk space.

Please visit our web site at http://www.exactgtq.com for more
information concerning our product.
