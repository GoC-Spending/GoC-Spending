Company Profile


MotionFab Ltd. in New Brunswick, Canada produces contract precision machined parts, assemblies and turn-key automation solutions. MotionFab Ltd. is committed to developing service offerings to support a wide range of commercial and industrial markets, industrial process and equipment automation, and custom machining services either Build To Print or to Design. Our current customers range from, but not limited to, manufacturing, aerospace, healthcare, oil and gass, window, ophthalmic and military industries.

Precision Machining

Our key precision machining feature capabilities include:
->CNC Vertical Machining
->CNC Turning
->CNC Part Programming
->Manual Milling & Turning
->Assembly & Fabrication

Design Engineering Services

Our key automation capabilities include:
->Mechanical Design including 3-d solid Modeling & Analysis
->Process & Application Engineering
->Electrical control & Panel Design including schematic generation
->Factory Automation and Supervisory Control & Data Acquisition Systems (SCADA)
->Soft & Hard PLC Programming / IEC Controls Programming (IEC61131 & 61499)
->Microcontroller Programming
->Assembly, fabrication, Integration & Test
->Post Delivery Service & Parts Support
->Full Turn-Key automation solutions

Our design engineering service offering is tailored to provide a single vendor solution whether it be the design of a simple mechanical part or the design and implementation of an automation solution integrating mechanisms and control. Our engineering affiliates bring a broad range of experience including but not limited to:

->Process, Tooling, Control and Machine Design for cutting of plastics and organic materials
->Process, Control and Machine design for polishing of plastics and organic materials
->Automated Material Handling Solutions for both pneumatic and linear servo applications
->Vision Systems for measuring, sensing, inspection & robotic positioning
->End-of-line case packaging systems
->Multi-Axis custom servo CNC milling & drilling systems
->Winding Systems
->Conveying and pick & place systems
->Conceptual prototyping
->Factory Automation & SCADA Systems
->Barcode & RFID Identification Systems
->Liquid Dispensing & Spray Delivery Systems
->Machine tool qualification to UL and CE Standards.

Field Technical Services and Parts Distribution

Full range of Installation and Field Service capabilities to provide Field Support, Project Management, and Training for the automated production equipment market.

Parts sales and rebuild capabilities for the support of Ultra-Lab line of production equipment. Parts stocking depots in both Columbus and Dallas to provide same day or next day delivery of specialized parts and components.

Field Engineers are located in Columbus , Ohio and Dallas , Texas , with an average of 16 years of experience in automated production equipment maintenance and installation.

Specialties include, Automated control Systems, mechanical and electrical diagnosis and repair of highly automated production equipment.

Project Management and onsite support for equipment re-locations and new equipment installations, production start-up, and technical training.

Motionfab is pleased to develop key customer relationships and produce high quality services & products.

www.motionfab.com Feel Free to email info@motionfab.com with factory automation project details,part RFQ's, or other inquires .
