AIOLOS ENGINEERING CORPORATION
An Executive Summary

The Aiolos business offering can be broadly classified as test
facility design and supply, with emphasis on those used for air
flow or noise effects investigation.

Aiolos has earned a leadership role in wind tunnel and
other test facility design and supply worldwide. In total, our
staff have the experience of over 100 projects in more than 14
countries.

Aiolos staff, numbering 40 in total, have all the necessary
disciplines in house to execute major engineering and turnkey
projects:
· aerodynamics and aeroacoustics
· mechanical/structural
· electrical and controls
· heating, cooling and refrigeration
· project management

Project and marketing support is assisted by branch offices in
Loughborough, UK and Seoul, Korea.

AIOLOS ENGINEERING CORPORATION
An Executive Summary

The staff of Aiolos Engineering Corporation were all key technical members of the former DSMA, which earned a leadership role in wind tunnel and other test facility design and supply worldwide. In total, our staff have the experience of over 100 projects in more than 14 countries.

Wind tunnel facilities are used in the aerospace, automotive and
industrial sectors:
Aerospace
· low speed wind tunnels for landing and takeoff aircraft
configuration optimization
· high subsonic wind tunnels for cruise configuration drag
minimization
· very high speed wind tunnels for fighter and weapon system
development. An example of this is the 1.2m trisonic wind
tunnel designed by DSMA for the National Aeronautical Laboratory
in Bangalore
· icing wind tunnels for aircraft component performance degradation
tests under severe icing conditions
Automotive
· climatic wind tunnels for a complete range of environmental
simulation for automobile systems and component testing,
including engine start-up and endurance, fuels and lubricants
and emissions testing
· aerodynamic wind tunnels for vehicle handling and fuel economy
testing
· aeroacoustic wind tunnels for external noise and passenger
comfort testing
Industrial
· boundary layer wind tunnels for wind effects on buildings,
wind engineering, pollution dispersion and wind-wave
interaction testing

Other test facilities include
· specialized rigs for zero-g simulation of spacecraft
· reverberant acoustic chambers for spacecraft payload noise
survivability testing
· anechoic acoustic chambers for low noise testing aerospace and
automotive systems

Aiolos staff, numbering 40 in total, have all the necessary
disciplines to execute major engineering and turnkey projects:
· aerodynamics
· mechanical/structural
· electrical and controls
· heating, cooling and refrigeration
· project management
There is no substitute for knowledge and expertise. The founding
of Aiolos in 1994 earmarked a new beginning for both the staff
and customers alike. Our clients continue to recognize that we
Examples of recent projects contracted to Aiolos include:
· concept development and subsequent supply of an enhancement to
the noise generation system for the reverberant acoustic
chamber previously supplied by DSMA to the European Space
Agency
· design and supply of a low speed aeronautical wind tunnel for
the Agency for Defense Development in Korea
· design and supply of an aeroacoustic wind tunnel for Hyundai
Motor Company in Korea
· design of an advanced low speed aeronautical wind tunnel for
Centro Italiano Ricerche Aerospaziali in Italy
· design of the Thermal/HVAC Center, consisting of three climatic
wind tunnels, for General Motors in Warren, Michigan
