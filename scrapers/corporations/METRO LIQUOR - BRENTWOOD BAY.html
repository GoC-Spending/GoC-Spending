Company Profile


We are a private, full service, liquor store with 3 locations in Victoria and one location in Kelowna, BC. We offer a broad range of all products that savvy customers expect, including chilled wines and ales. In addition, we specialize in hard-to-find wines, spirits, and ales from B.C. and around the world. Buy online or in store. http://metroliquor.com
