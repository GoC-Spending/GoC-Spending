Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Little Angels Dayhome
A fun, educational day home located in Auburn Bay South East Calgary across from the new South Health Campus. I have an eight month old son and a fourteen year old daughter. Originally I am from England where I achieved my BTEC National Diploma in Early Years making me a level 3 supervisor here in Canada. I have been in Calgary for six years, before and during my pregnancy I was the director of a busy Montessori Daycare for five years. In England I ran a successful day home for six years. Since moving to Canada I have studied Montessori and have acquired a level two in the education. I have my police clearance and I am certified in first aid. If you are looking for childcare for 2014 please contact me to set up an interview.

Look forward to hearing from you.
Kimberley

top of page

Sector information

Key / Major clients:

Along the road to achieving my BTEC National Diploma in Early Years (Level 3) I studied:
Positive environments for children's care, learning and development
Positive relationships for children's care, learning and development
Reflecting on and developing practice for children aged 0 - 8 yrs
Meeting additional requirements for childcare, learning and development
Modern beliefs and religions for the early years practitioner
Promoting children's development
Children's learning activities and play
Independent learning in children's care, learning and development
Safe-guarding children
Supporting children's numeracy skills and literacy skills
Psychological perspectives on children's behavior.

During my studies I also did placement which was my favorite part of the course. I got to work at schools with age ranges of five-seven years, nursery which was working with the three-four years range, daycare's which ranged from six weeks to five years. I thoroughly loved being involved with the children's education and got the opportunity to make lessons for them. I did all this studying in England and went on to run my own day home, until I moved to Canada in 2007.

Once arriving here in Canada I actually worked at superstore in the bakery for six months. I missed the children so much, I guess bread just was not my thing, that I started to look for a job I knew I would enjoy. I started to work in a busy Montessori daycare, during my five years at the daycare, I became the director. I also achieved a foundation level two in the Montessori education.

I am now on another chapter in my life, starting up my own day home again but here in Canada. I have an eight, almost nine month old son Keaton, my how time flies, he is going to be one before I know it. I also have a teenage daughter Leah, that crept up on me too, wow a teenager!!! I have been busy getting all my paperwork in place and the fun part shopping for all my toys and equipment. I am really excited and so looking forward to this new chapter.

I have fifteen plus years of experience working with children. So lucky to find a career that I love so much!!
