Company Profile


We manufacture and install patented weight counterbalanced overhead door Lift
System using a system of chain linked weights connected to the door
panels through a unique pulley system. The Lift System itself requires
very little expertise to install and is nearly service free,
making it very popular in rural, remote, or high usage
applications. The Lift system is supplied with or without the door panels. The cost of the product is very competitive in price with quality torsion spring overhead door systems with
only a fraction of the maintenance required. These systems are
especially well suited to very high openings (where torsion
spring mechanisms are ineffective and multi leaf doors may be
cost prohibitive), as well this lift system is very effective
where the openings are extremely wide (up to and more than 40') when using
slide away mullions. The greatest benefit is the nearly
unlimited number of cycles that lift system is capable of with a
minimal amount of service required during the life of the
building. Systems are also available with aluminum panels, or polycarbonate panels making
it ideal for car washes, potatoe sheds and other high humidity
applications. Remote and rural commercial, city commercial, and
industrial are primary sales regions. These panels are supplied and or installed (or installation supervised ) all over North America. Many Door Companies across North America assist buisinesses and industry in their trading areas with the sale and installation of these systems.
We also process from bulk Richards Wilcox panel stock, metal
insulated overhead commercial and industrial doors. These doors
use either our weight counterbalanced Lift System or Richards-
Wilcox torsion spring hardware.
We also carry a complete line of Richards-Wilcox finger protect residential products.

Our company has developed a unique method of manufacturing small buildings of polyurethane insulated panels. These utility sheds require very few tools to set up. They can be shipped knocked down as 6 (standard) pieces, (more pces for larger buildings) doors
and windows pre-installed. They feature prefinished metal 2 sides, open ceiling, rodent proof and much more. Can be used as a shed or a cabin or a variety of enclosures around existing equipment or machinery. Largest size is 14x28 with optional overhead door. Standard wall heights are 6'8" and 7'. Other heights available.

Hanover also supplies and installs numerous lines of Steel Roll-up doors, Dock Levelers, loading dock seals, dock bumpers, commercial man doors, high speed fabric doors, polycarbonate doors, and most door related products.

Service to overhead doors, ours or any other manufacturer is
another main aspect of our buisiness. We carry a large supply of
parts, panels, motors, etc. to look after almost any overhead
door service or repair. We do a lot service, sales and installations for local buisiness, local gov't.,provincial gov't, federal gov't across the far north, coast to coast.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
sectional overhead doors
1. Weight counterbalanced sectional overhead doors using Hanover
Door weight system/hardware and Richards-Wilcox track.
2.Torsion spring operated sectional overhead doors using
Richards-Wilcox and Amarr sections and hardware.
3. Service and parts for all and other makes. Territory includes
Manitoba, Sk., NWT, N.W. Ont.
4. Sales to almost anywhere.

Product Name:
weight counterbalance systems for sectional overhead doors
Hardware and weight counterbalance lift mechanism for sectional
overhead door panels. Include standard overhead door sizes,
doors up to 40' wide. Can be wider with sliding mullions. For very high doors, doors higher than 24' where springs are ineffective and for high cycle doors where frequent service is unacceptable.

Product Name:
multi-use steel insulated sheds
Economical sheds made of insulated steel polyurethane panels.
(recycled new door panels) Available in 6 piece knock down
packages.(more pieces for larger sizes) Sizes from 6'x6' up to
15'x28' or longer. Included as standard are double swinging doors opening up to 4'.
Options include windows, overhead doors, 5' wide double swinging
doors, floors built on site and building set-up. (depending on
location) Knocked down shipping available. See under Buildings
at www.hanoverdoors.com

Product Name:
Polycarbonate Doors
We Supply, Install and Service as Authorised dealers, Richards-Wilcox , Air Lift and Sunshine Polycarbonate doors and products.

Service Name:
service and repair - all products
Service and repair for most types of doors in all of Canada and it's Territories. Carry large inventory of parts, panels and lift systems. Service and repair for all makes of electric overhead door operators, dock levelers, dock seals, bumpers, man-doors, rolling steel doors, highspeed doors, polycarbonate doors, weight balanced sectional doors and torsion spring sectional doors.

Sector information

Unique applications:

1.grain elevator overhead doors - very high doors
where springs are ineffective - especially
economical when compared to leaf style doors.
2.very wide doors using sliding mullions - any
width of clear openening possible in "as
required" width increments - i.e. - side by side
door widths could include many doors up to 26'
wide or as little as 6' or 8' wide. Doors can be
raised to full open position and all the track
mullions moved to the sides. The width of the
opening is dependant only on the clear span
engineered into the building.
3.unlimited number of cycles of use. There are no
weak links built into the entire lift system
including track and face hardware. This is
especially useful for remote locations where
service may be difficult to find on short notice.
It is also cost effective where a door may have
to open up to a 1000 times per day such as a
public garage. The startup cost is only slightly
higher than standard torsion spring doors but the
payback in service free reliability over the life
of the building is enormous.
