Company Profile


Pilot Performance Resources Management Inc. a Canadian company, since 1995, specializes and leads in Management System integration and implementation to international standards - ISO working with global companies, providing training, consulting and auditing services to drive business sustainability. For further details visit our website at www.pilotims.com

President - Jayne Pilot, EP (CEA), EMS(LA), CPEA,
Speaker/Author/Auditor/Consultant/Trainer with over 30 years in business

Pilot specializes in integration and implementation of management systems for quality, environment, health and safety to meet international standards - ISO, following Pilot's proven 3 Step Process  Identify, Insure, Improve.TM

Services Include:

Board Member for Corporations

Business Sustainability & Leadership Consulting & Mentorship Programs
Management standardization to International Standards - Quality (ISO 9001), Environment (ISO 14001), Occupational Health and Safety (ISO 45001 - OHSAS 18001),

Speaker: Key Note, Business, Association Conferences

Auditing Services - internal and third party on Management Systems

Training - ISO Management system implementation & integration & auditing


Author of 2 books.
1) "Driving Sustainability to Business Success", published by Wiley 2014, available on line - Amazon. Provides a roadmap for leaders of business to "Assess", "Reflect" and then take "Action" to drive their business to success.

2) ISO 9001 - sold to over 2,000 major corporations in North America on how to implement and integrate a management systems for quality. On-line training now available.

Awards:
Canada/Pakistan Business Council - Professional of the Year 2011
Top 100 Women in Canada
Outstanding Business from the City of Brampton, Economic Development and Board of Trade, 2006, 2002
Pinnacle Award for Business Women of Distinction
Presidents Award - Environmental International Firsts for Browning Ferris Industries (Medical Waste Disposal, International Waste Disposal, Pharmaceutical disposal)

Partnerships:

Pilot's solution to improve global adversity includes the effective and efficient management of business operations to work in the global marketplace.

We have partnered with companies in providing leadership sessions on "Innovation" and "Automation" and Driving Business Success. Sponsors include: ABB, Zochem, Sheridan College, Boards of Trade, etc.


We are looking for partners in countries around the world, to sponsor or partner with us to provide our leadership principles and training around the world to help small and medium sized enterprises (SMEs) be competitive in a global marketplace and manage risks.

Clients: **
Registrars, government, industry (manufacturing, mining, chemical, automotive, waste management, health care, etc).

Jayne Pilot is Vice President with the Canada Pakistan Business Council and is active in International Trade Groups.

Ambassador for the City of Brampton
Chair of Emerald Energy from Waste (previously Algonquin Power) Public Liaison Committee (waste to energy)
Chair - City of Brampton - Environmental Committee
Board of Trade Mississauga (Brampton) Committees: International Committee, Policy
