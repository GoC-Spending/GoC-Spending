Company Profile


Harris: A Global Provider of Critical Communications Solutions

Communications are the most critical component of any air traffic management system, and over the years Harris has successfully designed, engineered, integrated, and operated some of the most sophisticated and reliable mission critical communications systems in the world.

An international communications and information technology companyHarris has been a trusted partner to the global air traffic control (ATC) community for over two decades. With approximately 23,000 employeesincluding 9,000 engineers and scientists; Harris vast knowledge of global ATC communications and technologies is unparalleled and ranks it as a leader and pioneer in the industry.

Serving both civil and military markets alike, Harris helps air traffic controllers manage air traffic safely and securely with state-of-the-art communications innovations. From system design to installation and training, Harris is your source for comprehensive, turnkey communications solutions including:
- Voice Communications Control Systems (VCCS) - IP & TDM-based systems
- Radios
- Radio Control Equipment (RCE) (for networking, lines management, remote radio control, asset sharing, alternate routing)
- Weather systems
- Air Operations Management Solutions
- Air Traffic Queue Optimization & Display Solutions
- System Wide Information Management (SWIM)

Harris ensures every system we deliver provides the highest quality performance, reliability, and value offering warranty and long-term maintenance support with every system we build. Harristransforming todays ATC systems into the NextGen systems of tomorrow

Expert Service
Harris works with our customers to provide life-cycle system management tailored to meet operational, service, and maintenance requirements with stable and predictable operating budgets. We are ready to support you with services that include:
- Site surveys
- Extensive project management experience
- Installation and commissioning
- Factory and/or onsite training
- 24/7 technical support
- Timely repairs

Our team of expert engineers, project managers, and support technologists is ready to solve your communications needs on time and on budget. Our complete range of communications systems and services cant be beat.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Liberty-STAR Voice Communication & Control System (VCCS)
Harris provides an integrated, end-to-end solution for ATC voice communication, from the switch directly to the operator position. Used by more than 200 customers in over 40 countries, the ICAO-compliant Liberty-STAR ensures dependable communications in some of the most demanding environments in the world. From Antarctica to the Amazonian rainforests and Middle Eastern deserts, the Liberty-STAR ensures that information is reliably exchanged in the air and on the groundregardless of conditions.

The Liberty-STAR uses a modular architecture, to provide a reliable, scalable solution tailored to meet air traffic control operational requirements.

The complete Liberty-STAR VCCS solution comes fully equipped with microphones, headsets, footswitches, and other ancillaries as required, including: weather controller, recorder, master clock, VHF and UHF transceivers.

Product Name:
Liberty-RADIO CONTROL EQUIPMENT (L-RCE)
Liberty-RCE is a flexible gateway product that provides aviation authorities with the next generation of integrated radio control, network routing and circuit adaptation solutions. A COTS device, the Liberty-RCE was specifically engineered to multiplex voice and data signals transmitted between ATC facilities and deployed radios over satellite, traditional telecommunications links or newer topologies like Internet Protocol (IP). The Liberty-RCE will facilitate the transmission of data services including; digital voice, legacy analog voice, SatCom, datalink, ADS-B, TIS-B, FIS-B and is interoperable with any brand of VCCS or ATC radio.

Capable of emulating any radio link and broadcasting it over a variety of transmission mediums; the compact, fully redundant Liberty-RCE will enable communications in harsh conditions and in isolated environments where digital service is typically scarce and reliance on legacy links and older analog technology is still required.

The Liberty-RCE offers unparalleled flexibility allowing fluctuations in network traffic and demands on operations to be easily managed. By compressing audio traffic and consolidating services, the system will reshape a network and allow remote narrow bandwidth locations to be reached, minimize delays across the network and maximize bandwidth availability.


Product Name:
Crash Alarm & Red Telephone (CART)
The Crash Alarm and Red Telephone (CART) system is a PC/touch screen-based alert and voice communication system that links ATC operators with critical first responders providing improved command and control capabilities by enhancing vital communications and response time. CART is a VoIP-based system modeled off the Liberty-STAR platform and can be integrated into every operator position.

Product Name:
Low Visibility Procedure (LVP) Intercom
The Low Visibility Procedure (LVP) Intercom system is a PC/touch screen-based voice communication system that allows ATC operators to alert essential airport personnel located in various airport facilities to commence LVP procedures. The LVP is a VoIP-based system modeled off the Liberty-STAR platform and can be integrated into every system.

Product Name:
Weather Solutions - Liberty-MET
Harris provides customized weather solutions which range from compact metrological systems integrated into the VCCS to comprehensive, stand-alone solutions.

Liberty-MET is a real-time weather system that provides accurate weather data to aviation officials in the control tower. The system comes optionally equipped with a weather sensor and will interface with all brands of meteorological sensors. Weather readings including wind speed and direction, temperature, dew point, relative humidity, and barometric pressure are integrated into the Harris ATC weather software and displayed on the weather information panel.

Product Name:
Liberty-RADIO GATEWAY (LRG)
The LRG is a multi-purpose networking interface engineered to provide operators with the ability to interconnect audio and data signals between facilities while enhancing capabilities and minimizing operational costs. The LRG simplifies network management connecting any brand of legacy VCCS with local analog radios via a common IP infrastructure. As a result, the useful life of all existing equipment is prolonged and the burden associated with having to replace costly radio equipment as standards evolve is alleviated. The LRG's interfaces take advantage of today's emerging digital technology and will connect any brand of HF/VHF/UHF analog radio to a remote and/or local control site such as an en route centre or ATC tower.

A fully redundant LRG supports a variety of system configurations from a basic analogue radio connection to more complex configurations involving multiple radios of varying vendors and remotely dispersed VCCS'  creating an IP Radio wide area network (WAN). The LRG can also simultaneously control and back-up radio resources from up to four VCCS sites allowing air navigation service providers (ANSP) to consolidate service during periods of reduced activity.

Product Name:
Voice Communications System for the 21st Century (VCS21)
Harris VCS21 is a Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) system designed for the global Air Traffic Control (ATC) voice communications market. VCS21 was engineered to address the voice communications requirements of the future ensuring a safe, orderly, and efficient flow of air traffic. VCS21 enables ATC modernization programs by delivering net-centric voice communications and reducing dependency on traditional point-to-point communications. Our net-centric approach replaces proprietary Time-Division Multiplexing (TDM) communications hardware with standards-based VoIP enabled voice nodes. The VCS21 product line combines the proven ATC voice functions and algorithms from Harris own Voice Switching Communications System (VSCS), Liberty-STAR Voice Communication Control System (VCCS) and Liberty-Radio Control Equipment (RCE) product lines into a modernized, next-generation platform. VCS21 allows air navigation service providers to deploy, operate and manage a truly dynamic airspace while providing interoperability with existing communication equipment enabling authorities to seamlessly transition from legacy to Internet Protocol (IP) technologies. Scalable from 1-1000 controller working positions, the VCS21 also employs a Parallel Redundant Protocol (PRP) which provides authorities with unmatched network reliability.

Service Name:
Global Satellite Communications Services
Harris provides the latest field-proven satellite technologies to deliver secure, highly-reliable managed communication services including broadband Internet access, voice over IP, networking and real-time video delivered to harsh and remote locations globally. Harris designs, implements and operates sophisticated, end-to-end managed network solutions including multiband, portable and fixed satellite communications services supported by four international teleports (Houston, U.S.; Aberdeen, Scotland; Macaé, Brazil; and Jakarta, Indonesia), four 24/7 network operations centers, eleven field dispatch and customer service centers, and service on more than 50 satellites around the world. Harris supports over 2,000 sites in over 120 countries.

Success stories:

Proven Solutions Deployed Worldwide
Our customers trust Harris to provide high-value solutions, tailored to their specific operating requirements. Variable topography, extreme climates, and densely populated urban centers are only a few of the challenges we face when designing efficient, reliable, cost-effective solutions for air traffic control.

U.S. FAA National Airspace System Voice System
Harris was awarded a 15-year, $291 million contract to provide the communications network that will serve as the cornerstone of the U.S. FAA's NextGen transformation of their air traffic control system. Harris will integrate its VCS21 at more than 500 ATC facilities nationwide while also providing remote radio equipment and network integration and management services.

United Kingdom NATS Modernization
Harris was selected to provide the U.K. NATS with it's next-generation, VoIP communications system to support the UK's ATM services. The project will see Harris' VCS21 systems modernize three facilities in the UK that will include more than 600 controller working positions that will access up to 2000 radios.

U.S. FAA Flight Services
A hybrid TDM/VoIP communications system was provided for the FAAs Flight Services 21st Century (FS21) project. The project included the system design and supply of communications infrastructure for a dynamic communications network of FAA automated flight services stations.

NAV CANADA - Backup Communication System (BUCS)
A unique Backup Communication System was engineered for NAV CANADA that was designed to take over communication on a progressive, position-by-position basis should the main VCCS fail. For this program, 12 large-scale Liberty-STAR networked switches are used in Area Control Centers, Terminal Control Units and Control Towers across Canada.

NAV CANADA - Radio Telecom Interface Multiplexer (RTIM)
As part of a nation-wide radio replacement program, NAV CANADA is installing over 2,000 units at 320 sites across Canada. The RTIM was designed to provide remote radio control capabilities, improve radio coverage in the north and interface with any legacy equipment while at the same time being ready for future communications protocols.

Abu Dhabi International Airport
One of the most advanced air traffic management systems in the world Abu Dhabi International Airport serving as a center for air operations in the Middle Eastchose the Liberty-STAR VCCS along with an Emergency Communications System with VHF Radios, a voice and data recorder, Crash Alarm and Low Visibility Procedures Intercom System package from Harris.

U.S. SCOPE Command
SCOPE Command is a highly automated, High-Frequency (HF) communication, command, and control (C3) system for globally deployed strategic and tactical airborne platforms.

United Kingdom Defence HF
Voice communications for the modernization of HF communications were provided to the United Kingdoms Defence High Frequency Service. Seven fully digital Liberty-STAR voice communication switches were delivered.

NASA SLF
The Liberty-STAR VCCS was chosen by NASAs Shuttle Landing Facility for its ease of use, reliability, and maintainability.

Antarctica
Liberty-STAR is the primary VCCS at McMurdo Station in the Antarcticthe logistics hub of the U.S. Antarctic scientific research program. It provides reliable communications in a truly harsh and demanding environment.

NATO Afghanistan
Liberty-STAR was also selected by NATO for the Kandahar air base as well as deployed by the U.S. Air Force in five mobile systems supporting Operation Freedom.

NORAD
A customized voice communication system was engineered whereby air-to-ground and ground-to-ground circuits would connect to the VCCS exclusively over T1. The circuits in this system connect to remote radios using an efficient, proprietary, tone-based scheme for control.

Taiwan
The Liberty-STAR is deployed in mobile and fixed towers by both the Taiwan Air Force and Civil Aviation Authority for ATC operations.
