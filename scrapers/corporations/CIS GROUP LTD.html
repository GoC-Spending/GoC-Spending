CIS Group is one of the largest providers of DIRECT STORE DELIVERY (DSD) and SALES FORCE AUTOMATION (SFA) solutions in North and Latin America and Europe. They have been creating innovative software solutions since 1977, always helping businesses optimize and streamline their unique business process, reducing operating costs and having a positive impact on revenue. CIS strongly believes in investing greatly in R&D, always looking to offer leading software solutions to respond to real business needs. The CIS management team brings extensive project and corporate management experience to the company. A board of directors, ensuring a strong business discipline, oversees the companys operations. CIS main business focus is on mobile automation:

SALES FORCE AUTOMATION (SFA)
FIELD MERCHANDISING
DIRECT STORE DELIVERY (DSD)  ROUTE ACCOUNTING
FIELD SERVICE MANAGEMENT

The extensive mobile experience of its team with over 9000 routes and reps automated, a strong customer base in various industries, and outstanding professionalism made CIS a leading provider of software solutions for the mobile workforce. CIS other divisions include bus transportation, truck transportation and fleet maintenance:

CHARTER BUS TRANSPORTATION
SCHOOL, CITY AND INTERCITY BUS ROUTES
TRUCK TRANSPORTATION
FLEET MAINTENANCE

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
COMPANION® REP
Companion® REP is a sales force automation (SFA) software solution for field sales representatives and/or merchandisers. This solution allows reps to carry out their daily activities directly on portable computers or PDAs. Sales objectives, visit sequence, order entry, inventory, merchandising activities and much more are available, anytime, anywhere. At point of sale, all activities are carried out with customer-specific information (pricing, products, promotions, suggested quantities, facings, planograms and more). This SFA application is industry-specific, so it can be adjusted to your particular workflow needs. The Companion®Suite 2 solution operates in a bidirectional environment, synchronizing all information with the back-end system.

For management, Companion® Rep offers a higher level of visibility and control over field activities, to evaluate opportunities, measure performance, and execute actions that maximize efficiency and revenue. More effective reps, shorter visit time, reduction of administrative duties and better-served clients will definitely have a positive impact on your bottom line.


Product Name:
COMPANION® ROUTE
Companion® Route is a route accounting application that optimizes DIRECT STORE DELIVERY operations. Using handheld devices, Companion®Route provides DSD drivers with all the necessary information to efficiently carry out their daily activities, such as loading, visit sequence, products, pricing and more. Some of the key functionalities in Companion® Route include order entry, payment processing, inventory management, invoicing, returns processing and route settlement. At point of sale, all activities are carried out with customer-specific information (pricing, products, promotions, suggested quantities and more!). This direct store delivery solution is industry-specific so it can be adjusted to your specific workflow needs. The Companion®Suite 2 solution operates in a bidirectional environment, synchronizing all information with the back-end system.

For management, Companion® Route offers a higher level of visibility and control over direct store delivery operations, to evaluate opportunities, measure performance, and execute actions that will maximize efficiency and revenue. More effective drivers, shorter visit time, reduction of administrative duties and better-served clients will have a positive impact on your bottom line.


Product Name:
Coach ® Suite
The Coach® Suite is a complete, multi-user software solution built to efficiently manage bus transportation operations. This solution has three modules tailored to the specific needs of charter bus transportation and school, city & intercity bus routes
Charter Bus Transportation
Coach® Chartered allows you to manage your chartered trip operations from a dynamic planning board. This board gives a graphical view of all your trips, quotes, resources and their status. Coach® Chartered has a task board where you can assign drivers and vehicle resources as well as generate and print contracts. After completion of a trip, adjustments can be made along with the selection of payment mode for the drivers. The Coach® Chartered solution can interface with the CIS Accounting and Payroll modules as well as with other back-end applications, transferring all revenue and pay information directly to these applications.

School, City and Intercity Bus Routes
Coach® Route allows you to build and manage your school, city and intercity routes. This module gives you the possibility of building and assigning tasks, which are composed of one or several work references. The Coach® Route solution allows you to efficiently manage driver assignments and vehicle resource allocations as well as to make payroll adjustments through drivers daily logs.
Incident Tracking
Coach® Event is a software application that complements other Coach® solutions. It allows you to record and to categorize events (complaints, breakages, accidents and others), letting you analyze previous entries. This solution also permits you to set reminders for future follow-ups. Users can identify trends and make smart decisions based on actual data, while never missing important dates. Bus route and chartered transportation operators can both benefit from this application.



Product Name:
RAPID®
Rapid® is a software solution specifically built to respond to the needs of the truck transportation business. This application lets you process your orders rapidly, allocate appropriate resources and view the status of trips in real time. Rapid® has enhanced communication capabilities that allow dispatchers to stay connected with the drivers, forwarding messages, pickups, drop-offs and more. The application goes further, allowing you to manage your drivers pay, fuel, incidents and claims, cross-border documentation, cross-docking and freight rate management. The Rapid® solution brings trucking companies to a higher level of efficiency, streamlining processes and maximizing profitability.
