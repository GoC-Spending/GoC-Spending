Company Profile


General Dynamics-OTS Canada is a world-class developer and manufacturer of ammunition adapted to the needs of the armed forces and law enforcement agencies. The company has a work force of 1,100 employees. As prime contractor and ammunition system integrator, General Dynamics-OTS Canada is designated by the Canadian government as the centre of excellence for the maintenance and further development of an industrial defence capability in production, technical know-how and research relating to ammunition products. General Dynamics-OTS Canada is a member of the North American Technology Industrial Base (NTIB). The company is headquartered in Repentigny, Québec and is wholly owned by General Dynamics Corporation.

Technology profile

As one of the worlds leading manufacturers of high-quality
munitions for field and training, General Dynamics-OTS Canada develops and manufactures an extensive line of ammunition from small to large calibres (5.56 mm to 155 mm); as well as pyrotechnics; demolition devices; grenades and various components. Through its SIMUNITION® product line, General Dynamics-OTS Canada has developed a full range of
small-calibre training ammunition, systems and accessories for
law enforcement agencies. The company enjoys an outstanding
reputation for delivering quality products to Defence Forces
worldwide. Advocating a partnership approach to its relations
with both customers and suppliers, General Dynamics-OTS Canada constantly seeks to identify and fulfill current requirements as well as anticipate upcoming needs. Today, General Dynamics-OTS Canada is strategically positioned at the forefront of technology, enjoying a well-deserved reputation as a supplier of outstanding products and services that are always on target!
