Company Profile


Nova Communications is a wireless voice and data communications company, and the largest communications system integrator in Atlantic Canada. Our team of over 25 staff service a wide variety of corporate and government customers across the region and increasingly internationally. Our customers span the public safety, transportation, construction, resource, offshore/onshore oil & gas as well as utilities.

Along with a solid reputation for the design, integration, and implementation of wireless solutions, we also are proud of our reputation for after sale service and support.

Our track record reflects our capability to deliver solutions from simple two-way radio systems to complex integrated telecommunications solutions for the offshore. Our customers benefit from a wide range of services starting with support in defining requirements, design, procurement, installation and ongoing system maintenance. With over 30 years of experience and relationships, we have the ability to work across many technologies including: Wi-Fi, satellite, handheld computing, RFID, barcode scanning, two-way radio, wireless broadband, SCADA and cellular.

We are proud of long-term relationships with customers who select Nova as their solution provider of choice and our partners such as Motorola, GEMDS, Alpha, Iridium, Bell, Cambium and Cisco - all leaders in the their industry. We are proud of the awards we have received including Motorola's Partner of the Year for Canada in 2012.

Our customers rely on wireless communications for business productivity and safety. We are ISO 9001:2008 certified reflecting superior engineering and our focus on quality.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Two-way radio
Nova has a solid track record of providing analog and digital two-way radio systems for public safety and corporate customers. Nova is a fully authorized partner for companies such as Motorola, Icom, Peltor and David Clarke. NovaCommunications.com is also the leading online source of two-way radio equipment in Canada.

Product Name:
Onshore/offshore oil & gas telecoms
As a telecoms systems intergator for the energy sector, Nova's credentails range from design concept to engineered solutions using innovative technologies, system integration, engineering documentation (complete ILS packages), testing, installation commissioning and training anywhere in the world. Project experience includes digital microwave, SCADA/telemetry, fixed and portable satellite, GPS and tracking, PA&A systems, HF radio, telecom UPS and two-way radio systems.

Nova Communications has many teaming arrangements or partnerships with well-known equipment manufacturers from around the world. As a proudly Nova Scotia company, we bring global relationships such as ABB, Federal Signal, Harris, Alcatel, and Automatic Power to meet the demanding needs of our customers.


Product Name:
Satellite communications and tracking
Nova can provide a range of fixed and mobile satellite services for voice, data and tracking applications. Nova is an authorized partner for Iridium, and also can provide MSAT, Inmarsat and other services as required.

Product Name:
Broadband Wireless
Wireless broadband connections using point-to-point, point-to-multipoint, Wi-Fi, mesh and WLAN technologies - both fixed and mobile. We have a track record of large successful outdoor and indoor deployments. Also able to provide the in-building, video, VOIP, security and wireless SCADA applications to take advantage of these IP-based networks.

Product Name:
Rural Broadband
Design, supply, stage and implement wireless infrastructure to support the rollout of wireless broadband. Specialists with deep experince particularly with the deployment of wireless broadband internet access to rural areas. A multiple winner of Motorola's broadband partner of the year for Canada, we understand the technology models as well as the interdependency of sustainable business models.

Product Name:
Mobile Computing
Handheld and mobile computing devices such as scanners, rugged computers, smartphones, etc. using technologies including barcode scanning and RFID.
