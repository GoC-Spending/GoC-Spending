Company Profile


iLogic Inc. is an information technology company established in 1979. We are a pioneer in developing reusable assets management software and have been delivering cost-effective solutions for over three decades. Our systems are currently deployed by hundreds of companies and not-for-profit organizations worldwide, with core competencies that span from providing enterprise level software solutions to consulting and assisting with project implementation and training.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
masLogic
The objective of masLogic is to give businesses and organizations control of their reusable assets. The management of reusable assets presents a unique set of challenges  without the proper tool and management process the task is daunting, time consuming, frustrating, and costly. masLogic offers organizations better control over their reusable assets by providing the tools to address these important questions:

Where are my assets?
Who is responsible?
Where do leakages occur?
How to optimize the utilization rate?
How much are we spending?

iLogics reusable assets management software has reliably managed millions of transactions and hundreds of types of containers. Our technology has been used by hundreds of companies in a multitude of industries including Fortune 500 corporations, industry container pools, postal services, container brokerage/manufacturing firms, 3PLs, transport companies, and industry cooperatives. These organizations have been able to realize millions of dollars in annual savings as well as benefit from the vast improvement in the logistics efficiency of supply chain.

Product Name:
Electronic Asset Settlement
iLogics proprietary Electronic Asset Settlement (EAS) technology enables users of reusable assets in a geographically dispersed network to resolve balances amongst multiple parties by reducing or eliminating the need to physically move those assets. EAS users have documented substantial savings in fuel and transportation costs, handling fees, as well as reduction in greenhouse gas emission. We call this process liability optimization.

Our EAS technology can be deployed as an optional module of our masLogic software or as a complimentary add-on feature to your corporation ERP or transportation system.


EAS can scan a network of trading partners to identify opportunities to reduce or eliminate movements of empty assets. The EAS optimization process takes into consideration factors such as handling costs, shipping charges, greenhouse gas emissions from vehicle travel, damage and labour costs as well as company specific business rules. Then, based on these input variables, our technology constructs and evaluates hundreds of thousands of possible scenarios to come up with the most optimized recommendations for the users approval.

The EAS technology was developed between 2010-2012 as the result of a C$2.48 million project involving three parties: iLogic Inc., the Canadian grocery industry consortium, and Sustainable Development Technology Canada.

Unique applications:

For three decades, iLogic has provided reusable asset tracking software, allowing users to manage their reusable assets, in particular containers, across and outside their organization. In a complex network of reusable container trading partners, there exists opportunities to reduce or eliminate the need to transport empty containers in order to satisfy outstanding liabilities (i.e. a company that receives products in reusable containers needs to return those containers once the product has been removed from them).

EAS was developed as the result of a $2.48 million, 2.5 year project involving the Canadian grocery industry consortium, the Sustainable Development Technology Corporation (a Canadian Crown Corporation) and iLogic Inc. The project was completed in 2013. Our goal was to provide a software tool to optimize reusable asset liabilities amongst trading partners, thereby reducing or eliminating the need to transport empty containers. It permits companies to save money on transporting, repairing and replacing mobile assets such as pallets, crates, totes, refrigerated boxes, marine shipping containers, etc.. It also allows them to realize significant CO2e reductions.

EAS encourages collaboration of reusable assets users amongst corporations, industries and countries to maximize the efficiency and mutual savings amongst trading partners.

Key / Major clients:

iLogic reusable assets management software has successfully and reliably managed millions of transactions and hundreds of types of containers. Our technology has been in use by hundreds of companies in a multitude of industries, including a long list of Fortune 500 corporations, industry container pools, postal services, container brokerage and manufacturing firms, third-party agencies, transportation, and industry cooperatives. Together, these organizations have accumulated millions of dollars in savings annually and witnessed vast improvements in the logistics efficiency of the supply chain.

Success stories:

Our success is built on our commitment to our clients and our attention to detail. Throughout every project, we maintain a close partnership with our customers to stay in tune with their exact requirements. This efficient and communication-driven approach is the key to producing effective and refined solutions. Its also the reason why we have been in business for over 30 years.
One of our successful implementations is involving Canadian grocery industry consortium. It is a Web-based container tracking system that has revolutionized the way that businesses are tracking their returnable assets. Members of the consotium have saved millions of dollars through the use of this state-of-the-art system.
