Company Profile


VisionMAX is an enterprise software and systems integration company leveraging hybrid/cloud for omni-channel solutions. Over its 17-year history, it has built a powerful, customizable software platform for enterprise and SMB cloud applications. Its enterprise deployments in leading companies in North America span retail/POS, e-commerce, m-commerce, digital signage, indoor/outdoor transaction-based kiosks, media, hospitality, logistics, customer service, and other markets. These deployments process 100's of millions of transactions/year.
VisionMAX cloud applications can be customized easily for enterprise and mid-market end users. They are easily integrated with legacy systems. In addition, the company's VisionEngine business rules flow-engine and services-oriented orchestration platform (our VESB - Visionengine Enterprise Service Bus) allows development of new applications more quickly and cost-effectively than traditional IT development methods. VisionMAX will continue to offer IT integration services leveraging the VisionEngine platform, and will grow its software distribution through a close partnership with SYNNEX (a $12B Distribution company with 30,000 active resellers) over the coming years. In addition, Visionmax provides resellers of SYNNEX with a platform for configuring, quoting, financing and generating a proposal for all components related to retail (hardware, software, network, wireless, security, services, support, installation nationwide in US and Canada).
VisionMAX's leading-edge applications are readily enhanced to support emerging needs. Their installed base at Global 5000 customers demonstrates the battle-hardened readiness of their work.

Product Name:
Curb2Curb
Curb2Curb® is an intelligent curbside dispatch system that manages private pickup arrangements, such as at an airport terminal curbside or hotel valet parking, where a passenger has arranged to be picked up by a private taxi, limousine or special bus service.

An attendant dispatches the driver to make the pickup once the passenger appears at the managed pickup area. The attendant is then able to confirm that the authorized driver is available and is on his way.

Curb2Curb® manages the payment of fees in those cases where the driver pays a pickup registration fee as part of a value-add managed service.
