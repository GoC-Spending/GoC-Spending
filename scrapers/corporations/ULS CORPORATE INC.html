Company Profile


The ULS Corporate Inc. is a group of holding enterprises specialized in businesses related to the oil and natural gas industry. Our head office is located in Toronto, Canada with offices in Houston, TX, USA; Mexico City, Mexico; Caracas, Venezuela and Beijing and Tianjin both in China. Our business has also extended to overseas markets.
The operational scope of the company covers multiple sectors including oilfield service, oil drilling tools, oil pipes, rolled pipes, petroleum equipment, chemical new materials, and international trade, which includes: oil prospecting operations, onshore drilling (workover) engineering construction, oil pipe production and processing, manufacturing of petroleum equipment and precision instruments, coating and internal/external anti-corrosion for pipes and equipment, coating material, welding wire and production of other oil chemical new materials, oil pipe inspection and R&D for related products. Our major products include: drill pipes, drill collars, heavy weight drill pipes, sour service, high torque, low temperature and other oil drilling tools, seamless steel pipes, OCTG pipes, line pipes, valves (mechanical and control), welding materials, precision instruments and various oil drilling and extracting equipment.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Mechanical Valves
ULS Corporate Inc. is a manufacturing company that offers a wide range of industrial valves in different lines of products for the control of industrial processes.


Our experience and efforts in innovation have made ULS a company capable of providing satisfactory solutions to various industries, such as petrochemical, gas, electric power, mining, metallurgical, geothermal, cryogenic and renewable energy industries.

ULS designs, manufactures and performs testing in compliance with:


• API
• ASME
• ANSI
• DIN
• MSS
• BS
• ISO
• ASTM
• NACE

We provide different types of valves: ball, gate, check, globe and others.


Product Name:
Pipeline
In ULS Corporate Inc, we offer to our customers in the oil and gas industry a wide range of products, relying on a comprehensive system of quality, a extensive experience in the selection of materials, the correct manipulation of piping systems and the technical advice of our ULS specialists, which allow us to meet the most demanding needs from our Clients.

For oil and gas wells we offer casing and tubing, drill pipes, tubular fittings, pumping rods and coiled tubing for use in activities derived from the drilling and completion of wells.

ULS meets the oil and gas industry needs for offshore applications in deep and ultra deep waters, supplying tubular products of high quality for diverse maritime projects.

Some of our products are:

• Pipelines for distribution and conduction
• Pipelines with different grades of alloy
• Coated pipes
• Ascending pipeline
• Marine Drilling riser
• Steel pipeline, according to specific needs


Our offshore pipeline presents a variety of marine applications:

• Production piping systems
• Submarine pipeline systems
• Marine Drilling risers systems
• Pipelines for injection and distribution
• Structural pipe systems
• Systems for well completion & rehabilitation


All our products are manufactures in compliance with international standards such as API Spec 5L & API Spec 5CT, ASTM, GB/T9711.1-1997, GB/T9711.2-1999. We even meet specific requirements from our customers.


Product Name:
ANTI-CORROSIVE COATINGS
ULS offers products and services to the general industry, providing effective coating solutions and professional advice that result in the corrosion protection of the customer's assets, which means an increase of productivity and lifespan of the materials, equipment, and industrial facilities.

We use 100% Epoxy Coating Advanced Technology and Polyurethane Coating (non VOC).

Used for:

• Protection of pipelines
• Protection of equipment and structures in marine environments
• Industrial equipment

Our products provide cost-effective anti-corrosion solutions. We provide technical documentation with recommendations on applications and we offer long-term protection in tough conditions.
