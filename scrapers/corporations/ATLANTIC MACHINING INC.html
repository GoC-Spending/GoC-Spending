Company Profile


Atlantic Machining Inc. is a Canadian based company established in 1989. Our company prides itself on maintaining high quality manufacturing standards and having low margins for error with skilled and experienced personnel. As a client-oriented machine shop we have developed relationships with clients in production, maintenance, and design. Producing engineered parts for OEM equipment manufacturers in the Mining & Forestry, Oil & Gas, and Automation Sectors. Supplying replacement parts for the ongoing needs of Steel Production operations, Food and Beverage processing and packaging plants, Commercial/News Printing Facilities as well as working with clients in product development stages i.e. Prototypes, Retrofits, Tooling. We Work with a wide variety of materials from Carbon & Alloy Steels, Bronze, Stainless Steel, Aluminum, Tool Steels, and Engineered Plastics. We continually welcome new challenges and business, and feel that our professionalism, competitive rates, and quick turnover would benefit you and your business by choosing us as your supplier. Feel free to contact us with your current project requirements as we are looking forward to the opportunity to work with you.

Service Name:
CNC-Turning
We offer CNC-Turning services for products ranging in sizes from 11" to 120" in length with diameters between 1.5" to 12.5" for long workpieces, and 20" max diameter up to 16" in length for large diameter workpieces. Products can be machined from various materials e.g. 1045, 4140, 316SS, 17-4PHSS, 6061, 7075. The typical tolerance held in most critical dimensions ranges between +/- 0.001" and +/- 0.0002". Surface finishes as high as 8RMS can be achieved when required.

Service Name:
CNC-Milling (3-Axis)
CNC-Milling services are offered for parts fitting a 22" x 42" x 28" work envelope. Milling, Drilling, Tapping, Contouring, Profiling, Engraving, Facing, can be done on a variety of materials from Alloy Steels, Stainless Steels, PEEK, Vespel, Aluminum. Typical GD&T tolerances for these types of applications ranges from +/-.001" to +/-.0005" depending on the material and application.

Service Name:
Prototype and Design
We offer Reverse Engineering, Prototyping, as well as In-Process manufacturing support on a project to project basis whether it be for private businesses, research laboratories, or commercial design/build firms. Feel free to contact us with your project requirements.

Service Name:
CNC-Milling (5-Axis)
5-Axis CNC-Milling services are offered for parts with complex geometries and tight tolerances requiring multiple operations. For longer parts requiring radial indexing and machining we offer a work envelope of up to 14" Diameter and length of 28". For full 5-Axis machining requirements we can accommodate parts that fit a 12" x 12" x 14" work envelope.
