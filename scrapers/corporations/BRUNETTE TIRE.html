Company Profile


Centrally located in the Vancouver Lower Mainland, Brunette Tire offers a wide range of products and services for materials handing, construction (OTR), airport support, tracked equipment and equipment requiring polyurethane load wheels.

We also offer retail and wholesale tire sales to businesses through North America through our extensive industrial tire inventory. In most cases we can ship products the same day the order is received. For our customer convenience, we offer secure on-line orders and tracking, inventory checks and pricing for accounts with access.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Camoplast Solideal Industrial Tires
Camoplast Solideal is the world leading specialist in the design, manufacturing, and distribution of off-road tires, wheels, rubber tracks, undercarriage systems, to serve the material handling, construction, agriculture, powersports, and defense industries.

We use the full power of our experience, engineering expertise, innovation skills, support and best-in-class manufacturing capabilities to deliver high performance products of unrivaled quality and value.

On the strength of a global team of more than 8000 dedicated employees, we operate advanced R&D centers, as well as manufacturing plants in the U.S.A., Canada, Europe and Asia. We distribute our products in the replacement market through our worldwide distribution network. In each location, our team is dedicated to be the best at meeting customer needs. As a strategic supplier to leading Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM), we also deliver our products directly to their assembly lines.

Product Name:
Continental Industrial Tires
Continental Industrial Pneumatic Tires provide comfort on all surfaces and therefore offer the protection of people, goods and material in many transporting and lifting tasks.

Continental Solid Tires are best for tough applications on slow vehicles or trailers with a high risk of impact and cut damage. They are extremely stable, pincture resistant and maintenance free.

Product Name:
Westlake Industrial Tires
Westlake Tires are available in pneumatic and solid pneumatic and are designed to meet most budget requirements. Offering low cost of ownership, Westlake continues to provide a value tire to most customers. Westlake offers a wide range of products for materials handing, construction, and OTR (off-the-road) equipment needs.

Product Name:
Galaxy Tires
Dynamic Tire Corp (DTC) was founded in 1997 by Robert Sherkin and Peter Koszo. Since its inception, DTC has expanded from being a distributor of passenger and light truck tires to sourcing on and off-the-road tires globally as well as being involved in the tire production process from inception. Dynamic Tire's global headquarter is located just outside of Toronto, Canada, and has distribution centers in Edmonton, Montreal, and Moncton.

Product Name:

Service Name:
Industrial Tire Sales & Service
Brunette Tire understands the importance of minimizing your equipment downtime. Normally a call in the morning results in a service call by our tire technicians later that afternoon for our local British Columbia customers from Vancouver to Aldergrove, including our on-site solid tire service, during normal business hours.
