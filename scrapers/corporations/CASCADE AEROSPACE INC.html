Company Profile


Cascade Aerospace specializes in commercial and military fleet management and support at its state-of-the-art, 250,000 square foot facility at the Abbotsford International Airport where more than 600 skilled aerospace professionals have developed a reputation for quality services and products.

Cascade's commercial and military fleet management and support services include:

Fleet and Program Management
 Maintenance planning, bridging & scheduling
 Fleet life cycle planning
 Annual and long term planning
 Aircraft operational capability planning

Aircraft Maintenance and Modifications
 Line & heavy maintenance
 Avionics installations and upgrades
 AD/SB and post-delivery modifications
 Interior reconfigurations
 Cargo conversions

Full Spectrum Engineering
 Engineering services (Cascade is Canadas longest-serving Design Approval Organization (DAO)
 Modification design and certification including systems modifications (e.g. avionics and IFE)
 STC product development, certification and kits
 specialty aircraft including aerial fire control aircraft
 freighter conversions including Q400, CRJ200 and B757 aircraft
 technical data & publications management
 product life cycle support management

QUALIFICATIONS
Cascade Aerospace is:
- Canada's longest-serving Design Approval Organization;
- an Approved Manufacturing Organization;
- an Approved Maintenance Organization;
- an Approved Training Organization;
- an Approved Boeing Engineering Supplier Level 5 (highest level);
- an Approved ISO 9001 Organization;
- EASA Approved
- FAA Appproved (through bilateral agreement)
- a Member of the Canadian Association of Defence and Security Industries (CADSI), the Aerospace Industry Association of Canada
(AIAC), the Aerospace Industry Association of BC (AIABC), the
Canadian Aircraft Maintenance Council (CAMC) and the
International Society of Transport Aircraft Trading (ISTAT).

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Q400-MR multi role aircraft
Cascade Aerospace is the STC holder for the Q400-MR aircraft, a multi role aircraft designed and certified for aerial fire control, cargo carrying and passenger carrying roles.

Product Name:
CRJ200 Freighter
Cascade Aerospace is contracted by Bombardier Aerospace to convert CRJ200 aircraft to cargo versions known as the CRJ200PF. The CRJ200PF is a low cost option for operators looking for a simple solution.

Service Name:
Supplemental Type Certificate (STC) Kits
STC Kit Development and Supply - Design, certification,
prototyping, kit supply and installation of aircraft systems,
structures, and other.


Service Name:
Aircraft Modification
Modification of aircraft structures, systems, avionics, etc.
Engineering design, certification (Transport Canada, FAA, EASA),
prototyping, kit development and incorporation/modification, as
required.


Service Name:
Aircraft Maintenance
Complete airframe maintenance services for Boeing
737, Boeing 757 and Lockheed Martin C-130 aircraft, including all letter checks; depot level checks; Service Bulletins; Airworthiness Directives; SSIP; avionics upgrades;
interior refurbishment and reconfiguration, bridging inspections and modifications.

Service Name:
B737 and B757 Maintenance
Complete airframe maintenance and modification services for B737
and B757 aircraft, including maintenance planning, heavy checks, interiors, avionics (GPS, EGPWS, TCAS, Mode S, ACARs
installations), in-flight entertainment (IFE) system
installations; lease returns: bridging inspections and livery
changes. Turnkey management of engineering, international certification, parts procurement, and any/all necessary modifications.

Service Name:
Aircraft Fleet Management and Support
Cascade's fleet management and support services enable operators to focus on what they do best - operate the aircraft. Cascade customers select which elements they would like Cascade to manage for them - from vendor selection to fleet life cycle management - and Cascade provides whatever solutions the customer needs.

Service Name:
Aircraft Line Maintenance
Cascade offers customized line maintenance services at Vancouver International Airport (YVR) where a dedicated team provides services for security checks, transit checks, meet & greets, on call maintenance, and line maintenance for many aircraft types.

Service Name:
C130 Component Repair & Overhaul
Cascade's in-house component repair and overhaul team provides quality service in minimum time and specializes in flight controls, doors, and other structural components.

Service Name:
C130 Maintenance, Engineering, Modification & Logistical Support
Cascade assembled Herc Solutions in 2004, a won a performance-based, $420+ million contract for the support and fleet management of Canada's 32 aircraft CC-130 fleet. With Cascade as prime contractor, Herc Solutions also consists of Marshall Aerospace, the world's most experienced provider of C-130 maintenance and engineering support, supporting over 40 forces and 12 military Hercules fleets worldwide; Derco Aerospace, world's leading supplier of C-130/L100 spares and component repair and overhaul services, supporting more than 60 Hercules fleets worldwide; and Standard Aero, DND's C-130 propulsion maintenance provider and specialists in technical publications and electronic manuals.

Technology profile

In 2001, Cascade built a state-of-the-art, purpose-built
maintenance, repair & overhaul (MRO) and modification facility at the Abbotsford
International Aiport. Cascade's
facility was designed to maximize the efficiency of our
maintenance processes and save our customers
time and money. Some of its features include:

- 250,000 square feet with a capacity for eight narrow-body
aircraft;
- work cell-design to maximize productivity and efficiency;
- automated system for delivery of tools and parts to/from
inventory to the hangar floor at 35 feet per second; and
- vending machines that allocate consumables to each job.
