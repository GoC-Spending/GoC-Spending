Company Profile


Brian Titus Strategic Consulting provides Professional Services for Governance and Economic Development organization. Brian has 23 years of experience as a Senior executive for First Nation Organizations such the Osoyoos Indian Band Development Corporation and the Chehalis Indian Band in British Columbia.

Services to Provide

o Strategic and Operational Planning for Governance and Economic Development

Every Development Corporation should have a plan in place on what the direction you would like to take your organization. A Plan should be used as a tool to measure effectiveness of your management. This work entails time with the Management and Staff and assessing the current situation, then a two day planning session with Management and the Board of Directors or Council.
After the planning session is done, Brian Titus Strategic Consulting will develop a Draft Plan for review with Management for a final document.

o Remedial Management Plans

More known as a Management Action Plan, this would entail reviewing the Financial stability of your company or First Nation by reviewing your Current Financial Statements, meeting with Management, reviewing current Structure, review assets and Human assets to Revenue stream, reviewing credit obligations and meeting with the pertinent creditors for possible debt restructure, and then developing a draft plan for Review with the Management or Directors, then implementation of final document.

o Finance Policy development

Finance Policies is a key tool to any successful Corporation or Government, it gives clear direction to Management on the Policies and Process to properly management the Finances of your organization. This would entail reviewing the current finance laws, policies and processes, then talking with the Accounting Department and Management. After reviewing the current situation, then a draft policy will be developed. This will be reviewed with the Management and Board of Director for Final document approval.



o Business Plan Development

Business plans are very crucial in giving access to financing projects; Brian Titus Strategic Consulting can develop effective Business Plans to meet your Financing needs.

o Business Development guidelines

It is important to have your Business practices documented, for clarity and transparency, and control. Developing your guideline will allow your organization to follow proper business practice so when decisions are needed by Management or the Board of Directors, those individuals will have confidence that approved practices have taken place in order to get to certain decisions.


o Proposal Writing

We can provide you with our expertise in developing successful proposal for funding whatever your needs may be in Human Resource development, Capital Funding for infrastructure, Land Code Development etc.

o Tax Bylaw development

We can provide assistant in the development of the Tax Bylaw for your First Nation. This will involve working with the Management and Chief and Council on developing this Tax Bylaw.

o Land Code Development

We can provide assistant in the development of a Land code for your Community, this will entail meeting with Community members, Management, and Council. Once the Land Code has been fully developed and consulted with your Community members to ensure the Land code is developed to meet your needs in the Management of your Lands on Reserve.

o Negotiations on Business Deals and Impact Benefit Agreements

Brian Titus Strategic Consulting has extensive experience in Negotiations all kinds of deals such as Joint Ventures, creation of new Business Partnerships, Land Leases, Building leases, and Resource agreements that lead to Impact Benefit Agreements.
