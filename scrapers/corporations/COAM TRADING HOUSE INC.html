Company Profile


Coam Trading House Inc. (Coam) is an international trading company headquartered in Coquitlam, BC, Canada. We have specialized in between N. America & Korea and have been focusing on dealing with dietary supplements & their ingredients and biomass such as wood pellets, wood chips & saw dust these days.

The company was founded on the first day of October 2001 under the laws of the province of British Columbia. The first main business had been to export a variety of North American and European pet products to S. Korea, the other Far East countries, and CIS countries. Since early 2004, in response to the requests of our clients, we have kept working on adding new product lines and services to our operation and on improving the quality of them to the top level of the industry. Through such efforts, we have been able to accumulate invaluable experiences and know-hows in internationally trading biomass, dietary supplements, ingredients of dietary supplements, foods, pipeline supplies, scrap metals, mining & base metals, ferro-alloys, and pet products primarily between North America and Asia.

On foundation of such experiences, know-hows, business system and business networks, we currently have focused on serving the clients who need the following services between North America and Korea in the following product categories:

Products:
1. Dietary supplements made in Canada/USA for distributors, brand manufacturers and retailers in Korea and more: Contract manufacturing / private labels / brands
2. Ingredients for dietary supplements, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, foods and beverages supplied by manufacturers in USA/Canada/Korea for distributors and contract / brand manufacturers in Korea and more
3. Biomass such as wood pellets, wood chips & saw dust

Services:
1. International marketing services for manufacturers/suppliers who want to export the following products: Consulting / Export Business Development or Export Sales Agent / Buy & Sell Service
2. International sourcing services for customers who want to import the above products: Consulting / Sourcing or Import Purchase Agent Service

Thank you!!!

Product Name:
Fruit and Vegetable Extracts
As an export management company of the leading competative manufacturers/suppliers of ingredients for dietary supplements, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, foods and beverages in Canada, USA and Korea, we have exported the following fruit and vegetable extracts to customers(distributors and contract / brand manufacturers in dietary supplements, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, foods and beverages industries) in Korea, Canada, USA and more.

1. Fruits: Concord grape, apricot, aronia, blackberry, blueberry, cherry, cranberry, kiwi, peach, plum, raspberry, strawberry
2. Vegetables: Carrot, corn, peas
3. EXtract powder

We offer 100% all-natural powders from a wide variety of fruits and vegetable as additives, supplements and ingredients to bring color, flavor and nutrition to consumer goods, health and wellness products and beverages.

Whole Fruit & Vegetable Powder Product Characteristics
- Preserved nutritional values and high ORAC and phenolic values
- Concentrated phytonutrients from producing with skin and seeds
- 100% all-natural color and flavor with no artificial color or preservatives
- Low moisture, low fat and low in sodium
- Shelf life: minimum of one year under correct storage temperature and conditions
- Kosher certified by OU. Click here for additional certification information.

Whole Fruit & Vegetable Powder Applications
Our fruit and vegetable powders are ideal for adding natural color, intense flavor and real fruit nutrition to new food products such as:
- Breakfast cereals
- Fruit pieces
- Bakery goods
- Snack chips
- Smoothies and yogurt
- Spreads
- Candies and chocolate
- Juices

Packaging
Our fruit and vegetable powders are packed in a 48 gauge metallized PET bag inside a double build corrugated box and should be stored in a cool, dry environment preferably below 70°F (21°C) and 50% R.H. Shelf life of two years from date of pack under recommended storage conditions (protected from moisture and excessive heat) in unopened containers. Each pail or drum is labeled with lot number, product ID and Julian date of production.

Product Name:
Dietary Supplements - Contract manufacturing
As an international sourcing service provider specialized in sourcing contract manufactured dietary supplements made in Canada or USA, we have been constantly developing the globally competitive contract manufacturers as our partner suppliers and have helped contract customers(distributors and brand manufacturers in dietary supplements industry) in Korea and the other countries to find the best contract manufacturer and to get the custom solution for their unique needs.

Our partnering full service contract manufacturers offer a total solution utilizing their GMP-certified plant and processes. From product ideation, formulation, packaging, and fulfilment, they have the resources and expertise in house to handle any project in the following product categories.
- Tablets
- Capsules
- Powders
- Softgels

In partnership with our contract manufacturers, from product development to delivery, we are committed to satisfy buyersneeds in the most effective and efficient way as below.
- Recommendation on the best manufacturer for your needs
- Formula development and evaluation
- Ingredient sourcing
- Quality assurance & control
- Manufacturing
- Packaging
- Import documentation support
- Logistics support
- Our manufacturers have several key certifications such as cGMP, Kosher, NSF, Halal, and USDA Organic.

How to Begin
Need assistance selecting active ingredients? We can do that! Want to know what flavors work best with your formula? We can do that! With our expertise in the industry and knowledge of cutting-edge ingredients, we can help you fine- tune your formula to maximize its potential.

1. Determine Product Ingredients
The first step is to select the ingredients for your product. Our knowledgeable staff can help you optimize your formula for efficacy and cost.
2. Order Size
The next step is to determine the approximate size of your order. We recommend a standard minimum batch size of at least 300,000 capsules or tablets to optimize manufacturing and testing costs. Actual batch sizes are determined on a per product basis.

Labels
All labeling materialslabels, stickers, hang tags, sensor tags, etc.are supplied by the customer. We can answer your labeling questions and refer you to graphic designers experienced in designing labels for dietary supplements as well as experienced printers in label production. Just ask, were here to help!

Supplying Labels
Customers are responsible for providing labels that comply with all regulations. We suggest that you have your labels reviewed by legal counsel. Please include enough labels for the number of bottles ordered, plus an additional 10% to cover losses during labelling.
