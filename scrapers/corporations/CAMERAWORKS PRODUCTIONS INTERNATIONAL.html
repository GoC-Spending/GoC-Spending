Company Profile


Cameraworks Productions International was established in 2002 by a passionate entrepreneur, media production specialist, Broadcast Engineer and Video producer. Our creative team of media production specialists with a combined industry experience of 105+ years is thoroughly trained to provide expedient, customized media production services. Our team works with passion in providing creative and effective video solutions to a diverse range of clients in sectors including but not limited to Corporate, Energy/Oil, Finance, Government, Non-Profit and Small Business.

Our facilities have been designed to achieve impeccable video and audio qualities. Having our own full-service production facility means that we can include value-added production services without passing on any hard costs to our clients – And most important, we have full control on the quality of production.

Over the years, we have kept pace with changing client needs and media production trends by adding the perfect blend of pre- to post-production services and media production technology to compliment needs.

From playback on the big screen to broadcast to mobile and web, Cameraworks can deliver compelling stories that help initiate calls-to-action. As part of our Quality Assurance Policy, Cameraworks Productions is committed to understanding and superseding the quality needs and expectations of all our clients. Our creative team is prepared to help you with all of your media production needs, at flexible hours seven days a week!

OUR PRE- TO POST-PRODUCTION SERVICES & EQUIPMENT  Under one roof, Cameraworks has full ownership of its specialty broadcast equipment and editing facilities and provides in-house pre- to post production services. As a result, we consistently provide our clients with cost-effective productions. Our commitment to quality extends to employing additional specialty equipment in any given project, regardless of our clients budget. All of our editing suites, video production technology and equipment are upgraded to current industry standards on an annual basis to ensure continuity of quality and service.

Our in-house specialists provide the following design services:

PRE-PRODUCTION - Production planning, Concept Development, Script Development, Story Boards, Location Scouting, Talent Coordination, Narration and Translation Services.

PRODUCTION- Aerial Video, Studio Shoots, On-Location Shooting, Multiple Camera Shoots, Live Streaming, Live Camera Switching, Green Screen Production.

POST-PRODUCTION - 2- & 3-D Animation, Animated graphics, video editing, special effects creation, audio production, multi-file format deliverables (USB, DVD, Transmission, electronic file transfers, MOV, H264, MP4, etc.).

Total plant size:

Our video production facility is located in Vaughan, ON at 131 Whitmore Road, Unit 19 just west of HWY 400 and north of HWY 407. The facility houses all portable and fixed production broadcast equipment as well as a green screen studio.

Distribution of employees:

Our Toronto facility currently employs individuals in senior management, production planning, cinematographers, editing, motion and graphic animation, audio recording and mixing, accounting and sales and marketing. All work is done in house so that a steady workflow is maintained throughout the production process and also to ensure that quality and ingenuity is never compromised. Cameraworks has gained a reputation for providing production services in a professional and efficient manner at highly competitive rates to our very satisfied clients. Our success is a result of our very skilled and conscientious creative team focused on consistently providing high-quality video assets (over 105 years of combined industry experience). We work closely with clients to fully understand their expectations and to create an accurate Scope of Work (SOW), which sets the expectations, direction, and timing to ensure we meet and exceed our clients expectations. An accurate SOW combined with the fact that we are seasoned broadcast media individuals, allows us to work independently with minimal supervision from the Project Lead.

Unique applications:

Benefits of Cameraworks Solutions

- We are a full-service Production Facility...From PRE- to POST-PRODUCTION we make the process efficient and deliver a broadcast quality product on a timely basis because we have the know-how, technology and equipment. As we are in the media business, our services are extremely flexible and we make ourselves available 24-7!

- Our Team is made up of industry experts and specialists! Our creative team is highly seasoned and experienced in the production business with a combined experience of well over 100 years.

- We believe in long-standing relationships and enjoy a successful rate of repeat business!

- We have the right tools to do the right job! Your production will follow broadcast-quality production standards. Also, we have the flexibility to employ any piece of specialty broadcast equipment regardless of budget because we have them available under our roof.

- We are ALWAYS on-time and on-budget! Due to the magnitude of our clientele, nature of projects completed, number of services offered, and having complete ownership of our entire facilities, we demonstrate our flexibility to deliver to YOU a cost-effective and timely production every time!

Key / Major clients:

Cameraworks Productions International (Cameraworks) serves clients globally and across a broad spectrum of business sectors including, but not limited to: Tourism & Hospitality/Retail & Services, Energy/Industrial, Non-Profit Organizations, Finance & Technology, Government Ministries and the general corporate market.

Major clients include: Atlantic LNG, Canadian Alliance of British Pensioners, City of Vaughan, Entera, First Citizens, Guardian Group, Jroberts Manufacturing, Petrotrin, Phoenix Park Gas Processors, PowerStream, PowerGen, Royal Bank, The Judiciary, Trans Power, Utility Contractors Association of Ontario

Success stories:

Our video production specialties include, but are not limited to Occupational Health and Safety Training Videos, industrial/corporate videos, Marketing/Promotional Videos, Television Commercials and Public Service Announcements. We always educate our clients on how to maximize the use of their video assets through the various communication platforms and as a result, clients have successfully implemented their videos the following ways: training/sales presentations, international & local trade shows, transmission, corporate websites, FaceBook, YouTube, video link via email signature, etc.
