Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
China project management for North American SMEs
Provision of all required services and China-based monitoring and control for Small-Medium Enterprises seeking cost-effective ways of conducting business in China without necessarily having to establish a China branch office. Includes legal services, accounting services, HR management and recruitment services, logistics, Quality Control, government relations, market research, product positioning, partner search and validation, etc.. through personnel in most major cities in China. We act as your eyes and ears in China.

Product Name:
Canada-China Bio/Pharma Bridge
Full service facilitation for North American SMEs in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology sector seeking to reduce costs or expand their markets through judicious use of opportunities available in China. FDA audited CROs and CMOs, potential co-licencing and research partners, potential Chinese investors, etc...

Service Name:
Training, Business Facilitation, China Business,
Professional training services provided in Canada, China, Singapore, Belgium, Switzerland, U.K., and U.S.A. and guidance for companies operating between Canada and China as described above.

Service Name:
Expat preparation (culture, protocol, communication and negotiation...)
Preparing expatriated employees to be effective in their destination culture. Features workplace communication, management differences, adapting to local culture, minimizing turnover through good integration of expatriated employee as well as accompanying family members.

Service Name:
Cross Cultural Communication Training
Cross cultural training for business travel, marketing and branding and domestic workplace cultural diversity issues.

Testimonial:

I recently took a course offered by Peter Loorits at Dragonbridge called "Succeeding with Chinese Business Delegations and Relationships Training." The course was given over one day because I had some time constraints. The insight it gave us was remarkable in terms of communication and protocol with the Chinese, building personal capital most effectively with the Chinese and leveraging relationships on an ongoing basis.There was a very good mix of theory and practical teaching. Peter has given other courses for BMO and all participants from these courses are very happy with their experiences and have been able to apply the concepts. I would highly recommend working with Dragonbridge Corp in the future. I would avoid doing only one day courses. To be fair, I imposed that constraint on Peter and he handled this very well, but in order to properly cover all segments thoroughly, two to three days are required. September 8, 2012 Filip Papich BMO Montreal

My company has worked with Peter Loorits and Dragonbridge Corporation over the last two decades. Peter has always been personable, efficient and optimistic. I am impressed with how he has built up his business in dealing with international clients, particularly those in China. I would recommend Peter and Dragonbridge for their professionalism and dedicated customer focus. March 12, 2012 Brenda Fay, PMP Brendaniel Communications

As a teacher of International Business at the Faculty of Management at UQAM (Université du Québec à Montréal), I had the opportunity to present Mr Peter Loorits as guest lecturer for my course. I had previously collaborated with him in an international project (business mission to China) organized by Dragonbridge Corporation in 2007.
I have known many consultants and trainers during my career and I consider Peter as one of the most skilled and brilliant experts in the field of international trade in general, and especially in the Chinese business environment.
After four years, I have come to know him well and would like to recommend him as an excellent international management consultant and trainer.
Peter is a dedicated person and several Quebec and Canadian universities (UQAM, McGill and University of Sherbrooke) have benefited from his expertise in business with China. In my course he has given my students a very remarkable presentation about Chinese Negotiation in particular and doing business in China in general.
As a manager and a consultant/trainer, Peter Loorits has a strong sense of leadership and organization. Furthermore, Peters personal skill and patience for both verbal and non verbal communication and cross-cultural management, makes him an exceptional talent in the domain.
In my opinion, Peter's unwavering devotion to his clients and his community shows not only exemplary technical skills, but also a strong moral fiber and character. He is very punctual and trustworthy. He would be an excellent consultant/trainer for your international business, particularly for any business with China. November 23, 2010 Issam Telahigue UQAM

The first time I traveled to China I was rather ill informed on how business was conducted and the nuances of the culture there. I decided the next time I traveled to China I would seek some professional advice. That is when I met Peter at Dragonbidge Corporation. Dragonbridge combines training in China business essentials with hands-on facilitation for implementation of China strategies. Peter is a true specialist in doing business with China and has fascinating insights into the culture. My second trip to China was much more rewarding both in the business sense and in being able to enjoy and embrace Chinese culture. I highly recommend Peter and Dragonbridge for anyone working with China or exploring business possibilities in China and looking for sound practical advice from a company that is expert in this domain. May 28, 2009
Sean Creaghan Entrepreneur

Peter and his team did a great jobvery worthwhileI would recommend his company. Peter and his team
