Company Profile


The Centre for Excellence in Communications (CEC) provides practical training solutions to the challenges faced by people and organizations working in communications, marketing, media and related areas. We enable improved performance through:

Professional Development/Training
Facilitation
Coaching
Consulting

The Centre offers some sixty professional development workshops in strategic and personal communications, marketing, media relations, web/social media and writing as well as a complete training analysis service to identify and respond to individual and organizational professional development and training needs.

The Centre's team of experienced professionals delivers consulting services in all the areas in which the CEC offers professional development and training.

The CEC provides:

Individual coaching in all the skill areas in which the Centre works tailored to meet specific needs as well as specialized coaching support in leadership development and interpersonal communications;

Training in facilitation and direct facilitation services such as for strategic planning, participatory planning, group problem solving and decision-making, group process consultation and team building;


Normally any course given in English can, upon request from a department, agency or other organization, be made available in French and delivered to your group.
