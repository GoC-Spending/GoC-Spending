Company Profile


Windsor Feedscrews has been providing quality products for the plastic industry including blow molding, extrusion, and injection, for the past 20 years and we are confident that we can work with you to provide you with all your plastic component needs.

We specialize in the manufacturing and rebuilding of screws, barrels, removable mixers, tips, end caps, nozzles, and a large variety of other specialized machining components, including augers, tie bars, and rolls, just to mention a few. We can handle the most complex designs in sizes up to 12 inches diameter by 295 inches in length.

To find out more information about our company please visit our website or email us any questions you may have. We look forward to talking to you.

Thank you for your time,

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Feed Screw
Windsor Feedscrews Inc. offers a complete line of screws, including:

Blow Molding Screws
Extrusion Screws
Injections Screws
Rubber Processing Screws
Twin Screws
Foam Screws
Side Feeder Screws
Timing Screws


Product Name:
Barrels
Windsor Feedscrews offers a full range of single barrels for extrusion, injection molding, blow molding, and rubber processing. We also offer barrel repair services which includes honing, short-sleeving, and relining.

Product Name:
Front-end Components
To fulfill your injection molding requirements, Windsor Feedscrews is a full service provider and can provide you with:

Valves
Endcaps
Adapters
Nozzles


Product Name:
Removable Mixers
Depending on many variables including mixer length, tube inner diameter, the number of elements and their design, we can manufacturer removable mixers to your precise specifications.

Product Name:
Rotors
Depending on the rotor design, whether you require the high intensity shearing action in the nip as found with tangential rotors, or the cutting and folding of the sheet as found in intermeshing rotors, Windsor Feedscrews will manufacture to your precise specifications.

Product Name:
Augers
Choosing the right materials and design of an auger, can help to create the right mixing action to homogenize the blend of the plasticating material, this is especially useful when adding colors or when regrind is being mixed with virgin materials.


Product Name:
Side Feeders
The use of side feeders provides higher downstream addition rates than compared to gravity feeding into a downstream barrel. Proper screw design and manufacturing is thus required to handle the higher rates and to achieve the required extrusion objectives.


Product Name:
Tie Bars
We guarantee the structural integrity of our tie bars, which will provide you with a sturdy and accurate alignment mechanism to ensure that the mold is always properly located and positioned in reference to the machine during the injection molding process.

Service Name:
Service Capabilities
We also have the capacity and ability to do a variety of machining work. Our machining capabilities just to mention a few include, CNC lathe work up to 20 inches diameter by 234 inches in length, manual operated lathe work of 12 inches in diameter by 315 inches in length, large cylindrical grinding up to 12 inches in diameter by 295 inches between centers, and CNC 4 axis milling of 20 inches in diameter by 315 inches in length. We also have strong capabilities on polishing and buffing jobs and a plasma arc welding machine for hard surfacing.
