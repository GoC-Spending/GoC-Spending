Product Name:
Natural Crystal Salt Lamp & Candlholder
Hundred million years ago, under the intense pressure of a primordial sea, crystal salt was formed deep within the Himalayan Mountains (Underground 700 ft. to 800 ft.). Possessing 84 of the nutritional elements our bodies need, it actually contains all the elements of which the human body is comprised.

Himalayan Crystal Salt is in its natural, unadulterated state just like it was when it first crystallized so many millions of years ago. It is far more than just sodium and chloride. Conversely, table salt is processed and altered, and is completely devoid of anything besides sodium and chloride. Therefore the body can obtain very little nourishment from its crystals.

The lamps & candle holders are naturally varied in colourations, and often include shades of peach and pink, as well as radiant shades of orange and deep red. And, like the great sculptures of the Ancient World, each lamp is completely unique. No two lamps are exactly alike in shape, colour, or appearance.

More specifically, research has shown that heated salt crystals emit negative ions, which are beneficial to our health. Negative ions freshen and improve the air quality by removing harmful particles from the air. Source of harmful particles (positive ions) are emitted by exhaust fumes from automobiles and trucks, tire dust, factory pollution, electronic devices such as televisions and computer monitors, and anything else that has an electromagnetic field. In spite of their name, negative ions actually help to alleviate fatigue, anxiety, stress, depression, and asthma, amongst other conditions. Over all, they contribute to a general feeling of well being and help to melt the stress away.

Exclusive microclimate of salt mines is being used effectively for more than 30 years in the treatment and psychotherapy of patients having symptoms of bronchial asthma and other Chronic Non-specific Lung Diseases (CNLD). Dr. Albert P. Krueger, University of Californias microbiologist and experimental therapist and founder of the air Ion research laboratory revealed through his study that ionized air significantly lowers the level of airborne germs is covered area. Surprisingly a small level of negative ions can finish these bacteria and throw them out of the air to keep the people safe from their harm, he added.

Salt's healing powers have been known for centuries, going back to the time of Alexander the Great. Unfortunately, in those times, these healing salts were only accessible to royalty. The centuries-old salt mines in Europe have been used by many for their healing powers for several generations. This form of treatment is known as speleotherapy. But now you don't need to venture across the world to take part in this effective therapy. You can now enjoy the benefits of these salt mines right in the comfort of your own home by becoming an owner of one of these Himalayan Salt Lamps.
You can put in the house, office, meditation place or massage room.
