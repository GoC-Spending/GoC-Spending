Product Name:
Adline Classifieds
Adline is an e-commerce enabled online classifieds platform for newspapers.

Adline is an eBay-like solution for newspapers. Adline provides your website with feature-rich classifieds that generate new revenue opportunities and save time spent on ad-entry. Adline offers a user experience comparable to online marketplaces that integrates with your back-office publishing systems.

What is Adline?

Adline is an e-commerce enabled online classifieds platform for newspapers. Adline provides a front-end user experience comparable to dedicated online marketplaces that integrates with your back-office publishing systems.

Adline helps to position newspapers as a preferred medium for local, online advertising. Through a rich and intuitive user interface, it can help attract online advertisers and drive online classifieds revenue.

Built using the latest in web technologies and following open standards, Adline can integrate with your back-office publishing systems. Give your classifieds a face-lift without changing your entire publishing system.

Product Features

- Online ad-entry with upsells such as photos
- Integrated payment and credit-card processing
- Statistics tracking of web site customers
- Auto-generated print ads
- Advanced browsing and searching
- Easily integrated into back-office publishing systems
- Intuitive and rich user interface
- Full page display and description for classified ads
- Unique URL to each classified ad for including in print ad
- Feature-set can be customized to newspaper's requirements
- And many more...

Benefits to Newspapers:

Increase Your Classifieds Revenue

Adline's online ad-entry allows publishers to upsell additional features for creating more powerful online ads. This includes the addition of photos, bolding, longer descriptions, featured ads, and more. Adline complements print ads by using print as a platform to drive online revenues.

By offering powerful online upsells, publishers can generate new revenue streams and improve margins per ad.

Online ad-entry also gives advertisers increased visibility on all available options and allows advertisers to place ads 24 hours a day. Being able to reach customers outside of regular business hours can help increase the volume and frequency of classified advertisements.

Streamline Ad-entry and Save

Taking ads over the phone is expensive. On average, it costs $2 in resources for every ad taken. By comparison, ads taken online cost but a few pennies and can reduce ad taking costs up to 90%.

With Adline, advertisers can create, place, and pay for ads entirely online. Adline's online ad-entry system simultaneously generates both print and online ads, freeing up resources and saving time on manual ad-entry.

In addition, most online classifieds solutions put into place require constant management by technical personnel. Adline's solution is managed and supported by Apption technical staff, virtually eliminating all IT costs.

Improve User Experience

The emergence and success of dedicated online marketplaces like eBay has helped show the potential for online classifieds. Adline is an eBay-like solution for newspapers.

Adline provides advertisers with many of the front-end features that customers have come to expect from online classifieds. These include improved browsing, navigation, and more powerful search capabilities.

Through Adline, newspapers can offer the rich user experience of a dedicated online marketplace while maintaining the advantage of a strong local brand.

Track Customers More Effectively

Measuring the success of classified ads has been traditionally difficult to achieve. Adline's classifieds platform enables publishers to measure the effectiveness of their ads and to analyze the behaviour of their website customers. This can lead to a wealth of opportunities to serve website customers better, including more targeted and efficient advertising and marketing campaigns.

Apption is a software engineering firm specialized in web and wireless custom development and application integration. We bring disciplined engineering practices and creative approach to the design of software systems.

While we actively keep pace with new technologies, our first commitment is to our clients. We are committed to helping our customers drive value by putting software to work for them.
