Company Profile


Westkey Graphics is a Canadian owned and operated company with its head office and manufacturing facilities based in Burnaby, Vancouver, British Columbia.
Westkey is West Canada's largest independent one stop shop printer offering a world class customer service and outstanding print quality. Westkey has one of the most knowledgeable teams of printing professionals in North America that provides customized printing solutions and distribution services including warehousing, on-line procurement and inventory management.
Westkey employs more than 150 full-time staff running 3 shifts 24 hours per day from its 150,000 sq. ft manufacturing facility and offers a wide range of printing including business forms, cheques, labels, digital printing, envelopes, commercial printing and various forms of integrated promotional products such as labels and magnets.
With our plant operating 24 hours a day, you can be sure your job will be completed in the fastest time frame possible without compromising quality.
In addition to our Vancouver manufacturing plant, Westkey has
offices in Edmonton, Calgary, Abbotsford and Kelowna.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Commercial Printer
Continuous forms: From invoices and shipping documents to
statements and cheques, we can produce forms with as many as 14
parts using up to eight colours of ink. We can also help you
streamline your manufacturing and shipping departments by
integrating labels and/or bar codes onto your forms. Almost any
manufacturing option is available including a variety of paper
colours and weights, back printing, perforations, gluing,
crimping and so much more!

Snap sets: At Westkey every form is a custom form because we
have the ability to give you exactly what you want at an
affordable price, including one to eight colours of ink,
different weights and colours of paper, NCR paper, carbon and
patterned carbon. Back printing, perforations, punching and
numbering can all be done online. We can also shrinkwrap any
quantity as well as book your forms in several different
functional options.

Laser sheets, laser jumbo rolls and laser cheques: Whether you
want a simple laser sheet with your logo on it or an invoice
laser sheet with perforations and debit M.I.C.R., we can produce
all custom combinations. We can also add integrated laser-
friendly labels and cards for shipping or marketing purposes
with the options of printing one colour on one side to full
process colour on both sides. In addition, we offer a variety of
pre-set laser cheque formats to match your software requirements.

Envelopes: With our specialized envelope press we can print both
stock and custom sized envelopes to match your other printed
business correspondence material. We can print envelopes in one
to four colours, face and back.

Full process colour marketing pieces: Bright vivid colours will
make your marketing materials stand out from the crowd. In
addition to high quality printing we can also fold, die cut,
glue, perforate, add integrated cards, labels, magnets and much
more to your marketing pieces. We will help you produce
marketing materials that will get results for your company
and/or clients. If you have an idea for a marketing piece, we
can find a way to make it happen. Westkey has one of the most
experienced teams of printing professionals in North America at
your service.

Digital printing: Get full process colour on your short run
printing - comparable quality at a fraction of the price. Many
of our customers tell us they are trying to reach niche markets
and need small quantities of high quality printed materials to
catch their customers attention. Printing from digital files
can give you high quality printed brochures, sell sheets,
presentations, flyers, folders, posters and other marketing
materials. Whether you create the files yourself or our in-house
design department does it for you, Westkey can help you produce
material that will wow your market.

Promotional marketing pieces: We have several products that are
extremely effective for special promotions, tradeshows or
contests. Scratch and win tickets can be printed on a variety of
paper stocks, in almost any size and with full process colour.
For your next promotion why not use pull-tab marketing pieces?
They are fun for customers and something they just cant resist
opening. We can also produce sweepstakes pieces with stickers or
labels with pressure sensitive or re-moistenable glue. We can
print everything from the envelope to the inserts and even help
you co-ordinate the delivery with our mail house service.

Integrated cards, magnets and labels: Turn your forms, mail-outs
and promotional printing into interactive marketing pieces.
Integrated cards are perfect for discount or frequent shopper
cards, membership cards, event passes, customer service
information cards or any other application where you need a
highly durable card at a very affordable price. Integrated
magnets can be added to forms and mailers so your customers
can stick your contact information on their fridge or filing
cabinet. A wide selection of materials is available for almost
every application.

Returnvelope® & self-mailing products: Perfect for fundraising
campaigns, surveys and order forms, this product provides you
with an interactive marketing piece which you can send out in a
mail or flyer drop. The product is designed so the customer can
fill out required information, tear off the envelope at one end
and then mail back their order or response.

Direct mail: If you have a marketing piece to send to your own
database of customers we can co-ordinate your entire campaign
through our mail house program. We can print, address, stuff,
seal and mail your marketing material so it gets out in a timely
fashion and at an affordable cost.

Booklets, newsletters and presentation folders: From a simple
instruction manual or test booklet to an elaborate full process
colour newsletter or presentation folder, Westkey can deliver.
Our in-house bindery offers quick turn around times and
professional service.

Labels: If labels are a large part of your day-to-day operations
and/or marketing, our knowledgeable sales team can help assess
your needs and provide products that will save you time and
money. Some of our label products include: mailing and shipping,
bar code, blown-on, integrated, pressure sensitive or re-
moistenable glue and laser or pin feed labels on rolls or sheets.

ATM rolls & thermal transfer rolls: As the leading supplier of
these products in Western Canada, you can be assured of both a
great price and great quality. These rolls are available in a
variety of standard sizes or we can customize them to meet your
specific needs.
