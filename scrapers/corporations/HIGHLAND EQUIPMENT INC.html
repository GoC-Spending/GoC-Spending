Company Profile


Since our inception in 1973 we have become a leader in the design, fabrication, and installation of sanitary stainless steel equipment for those industries whose products require this special type of handling.

We maintain an extensive on-hand inventory of valves, fittings, special hoses, gaskets and other ancillary equipment.
Our plant is approximately 54,000 sq.ft., and is a truly Stainless Steel manufacturing facility, using only non-ferrous metals. We do not accept orders for carbon steel fabrications.

Highland Equipment is Certified by The National Board of Boiler and Pressure Vessel Inspectors Section VIII, Division 1 and CSA Standard B51, Boiler, Pressure Vessel and Pressure Piping Code, and authorized to Register NB Stamp, as well as ASME "U" Stamp. We are registered with the Technical Standards and Safety Authority (T.S.S.A) for Pressure Vessel Design and Fabrication in accordance with CSA Standard B51 Boiler, Pressure Vessel and Pressure Piping Code and A.S.M.E. B31.1 Power Piping.

We are also members of Contractor Health and Safety Management Systems ISNetworld, PICS and Cognibox.

We offer tank fabrications in a variety of metals from the Standard T304 and T316 Stainless Steel to the more exotic metals and finishes from 2B to 5-10RA and electropolished.

Highland Equipment services the pharmaceutical, dairy, food, cosmetic, breweries and beverage industries throughout Canada and the United States with a quality product... on time.

Our Professional Engineer, along with his team of CAD and Solidwork operators and Design Estimators, work together to ensure any tank, piping system or fabrication you require are built and installed to the proper Codes and Standards.

Our primary objective is to maintain the Company and its products as the recognized standard of quality, service and reliability
against which all other competitors are judged. We will also be active in pursuing new opportunities which are complementary to
our business and consistent with the expertise which we have developed. We will above all, succeed in our commitment to
quality, by providing the conditions necessary to achieve our objectives.

Technology profile

Highland Equipment, in cooperation with the Government of Canada, has succeeded in designing a Continuous Protein Process
System. This system will produce soft cheese requiring heat and acidification, as well as tofu. The system can be built in
three sizes. Model HEL2500, flow rate 2500 liters/hr to 3500 liters/hr, HEL5000, flow rate 5000 liters/hr to 7000 liters/hr
and Model HEL7500, flow rate 7500 liters/hr up to 10000 liters/hr.
A working system is available on a rental agreement basis.

Our Quality Control specialists work hard to ensure our shop fabrications, field assemblies and piing systems are in accordance with and comply to our following certifications:

* National Board Certificate of Authorization to apply the "NB" Mark and Register Boiler and Pressure Vessels and other pressure
retaining Items with the National Board. Scope of Authorization limited to A.S.M.E. Designator "U".
* A.S.M.E. "U" Certified Manufacture of Pressure Vessels.
* T.S.S.A. Boiler Pressure Vessels and Pressure Piping Code fabrication and assembly in accordance with CSA Standard B51, Boiler,
Pressure Vessel and Pressure Piping code and ASME B31.1 Power Piping.
* T.S.S.A. Manufacturer of Pressure Vessels in accordance with ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code, Section VIII, Division 1 and
CSA Standard B51, Boiler, Pressure Vessel and Pressure Piping.
* T.S.S.A. Fabrication and assembly of process piping and fabrication, assembly and erection of process piping in accordance with CSA
Standard B51, Boiler, Pressure Vessel and Pressure Piping Code and ASME B31.3 Process Piping.
* T.S.S.A. Repair and Alteration Organization of boilers, pressure vessels, piping and Category H type fittings at Corporate location and
for repairs and alterations of field sites in accordance with CSA Standard B51, Boiler, Pressure Vessel and Pressure Piping Code.
* ASME B31.3 Process Piping.
* Highland Equipment complies to U.S. Dairy 3A requirements.
