Business Leadership, Change and Technology



Service Offerings

We have defined a set of offerings that position where we can
bring value to an organization and they are the following:

Business Leadership

This service offering is related to creating an environment in
which change can succeed and
intended business results can be achieved. It is a means of
preparing the Executive and
their business leaders to work together towards a shared vision
and strategy. Through facilitation, we guide the organization
to a shared vision and align their planning and thinking to
that vision.

Transition Planning and Implementation

This service offering supports the planning and implementation
required by an organization faced with making a significant
change. This change may be a technology implementation,
consolidation such as merging similar functions, cost cutting,
outsourcing, huge growth, organization renewal and/or
organization restructuring.

Planning will help an organization develop a transition plan to
achieve their intended business results and close the gap
between their current and preferred future state. Transition
planning is a high level effort that puts a framework around
the change and provides leadership with the magnitude of the
gap giving some sense of timing, cost and scope. It can
identify any required technology change, whether business
processes will be affected, what impact will result from a
cultural perspective and whether any organizational redesign is
required.

Implementation relates to the effort required by an
organization to detail and act on simplifying operations and
improving efficiencies and effectiveness while striving to
maintain productivity. This is a disciplined approach for
achieving the benefits and successfully transitioning to the
preferred future state.

Managing Business Change

This service offering will prepare an organization facing a
wide range of business change situations with varying degrees
of magnitude and complexity. It is a formal approach for
mobilizing both individuals and groups at all organizational
levels.

It will assess and validate the organization's change readiness
and its capacity for change. Barriers to succeeding with change
will be identified and steps to overcome the resistance will be
prepared in detailed action plans.

Communications will be a key component to managing business
change and communication events and milestones will be
integrated throughout the project plan. It will also involve
identifying and assessing stakeholders and developing plans
that enroll and involve them at all critical junctures and
milestones.



Program Management Office

This service offering is related to the setup of a Program
Management Office (PMO) for an organization wishing to manage
several related initiatives in a consistent and integrated
manner or for managing a complex, enterprise wide project. A
PMO will support common functions across various initiatives,
monitor progress against plan and budget and direct day-to-day
activities of the Program Office.

Delivery Assurance
Our delivery assurance offering provides an independent review
of an on-going project. It can be established at the start of
a project where certain key milestones will be reviewed
independently to provide a Quality Assurance report on where
the project stands against objectives, cost, benefits
acquisition and schedule. Another aspect of this offering is
that it can be instituted at any stage of a project to provide
an independent assessment of the condition of the project.
Again the key evaluation is how the project is doing against
the stated objectives, cost, benefits acquisition and schedule.

Project Management
This service offering is related to providing project
management services to an organization either at the Program
Management level or for a significant project. We can
leverage our background and experience to ensure project
objectives are well established, communication within and
outside the project is in place, budgets are defined and
maintained, statuses are timely and project timelines are
monitored.

Information Technology
The information technology service offering will bring support
to Executive type issues within the information technology
field. Some of the items that we can provide support to
include:
 IT Strategic Planning
 Technology Sourcing
 Operational Planning
 Project Definition and Prioritization
 Coaching for the IT Leadership Team
 Requirements Definition

Human Resources

The human resources service offering will support
organizational and divisional challenges associated with:

 HR Strategic and Operational Planning and Goal Setting
 HR Alignment to the Business
 HR Assessment of Organization Design, Roles and
Responsibilities
 HR Process, Structure and Technology Solutions
 Coaching for the HR Leadership Team
 HR Programming for Restructuring
