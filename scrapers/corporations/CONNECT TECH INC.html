Company Profile


For more than 30 years, Connect Tech has built a solid reputation for expertise in providing professional design services, delivering unsurpassed technical support and developing innovative products for embedded applications and empowering IoT. Our products include commercial off-the-shelf and modified solutions for COM Express®, Qseven, and SMARC Carriers, Single Board Computers, AMD and NVIDIA GPU solutions, Rugged Tablets, FPGA, Digital & Analog I/O, Power Supplies, Enclosures, CAN Controllers, Solid State Drives, Ethernet-to-Serial, Multi-port Serial Cards, Adapters & Development Tools.

For specialized requirements, Connect Tech offers custom solutions to create an exact technology fit, shorten the design cycle, and reduce development costs. Our products are installed in a wide range of operating system.

Headquartered in Guelph, Ontario, Canada, our customers include energy, government, manufacturing, transportation companies, and a variety of automation, point-of-sale and other users of embedded technology.

Product Name:
Titan/cPCI
The CompactPCI adapter combines the best of ruggedized packaging
with the Peripheral Component Interconnect bus, while enabling
hot-swapping, an important feature for operators of real-time
systems. Connect Tech recently applied its design expertise to
this bus technology to better provide our customers with the
quality products they require. Available in rear or front I/O.


Product Name:
Blue Heat PCI and Low Profile PCI
Fast, powerful, and easy to install, the Blue Heat family offers
high-performance solutions for your multi-channel serial I/O
requirements. A wide variety of asynchronous port combinations,
electrical interfaces, and cabling options ensure you get what
you need to fill your communications, data acquisition,
industrial and office automation, POS and
scientific/instrumentation application needs. Blue Heat products
are backed by a one-year warranty, a full-featured software data
analyzer, and a first-class support team.

Product Name:
Xtreme 104
Connect Tech's Xtreme/104 family offers adapters that are small
in size and big on functionality. Perfect for any application
requiring embedded serial communications, our products offer
flexibility in port density and configuration, electrical
interface options, cabling selection and operating system
support. Backed by a one-year warranty and a top-notch support
team, all provide rugged, trouble-free communications for your
application needs.



Product Name:
NT960 PCI
Ease of expandability in the RISC-based NT960/PCI ensures
efficient performance no matter how large your needs. Available
in 16 to 128 port densities, it is equipped to handle the
requirements of your complex communications demands.



Product Name:
BlueStorm/LP
The low profile BlueStorm/LP is the perfect fit for your high
density chassis or low profile PC. Fully Universal PCI 2.3
compliant, the asynchronous two and four port RS-422/485 adapter
can be installed in low profile and standard height PCI
computers with the change of a bracket. This enables you to
connect multiple devices to your system, whether 3.3 or 5V,
taking full advantage of the space saving qualities of its MD1
low profile design without sacrificing performance. Data speeds
up to 1.8432 Mbps ensure your data acquisition, office
automation, POS or scientific/instrumentation applications
communicate in a reliable, cost-effective manner.

Product Name:
Universal Blue Heat/PCI
Fast and reliable, the new, adaptable Universal Blue Heat/PCI
485 offers a number of options for your application needs.
It provides an ideal solution for all applications requiring
single node or multi-drop communications over short or long
distances using any PCI bus compatible computer. This high
performance, multi-port serial adapter is ideal for data
collection devices, POS and Kiosk equipment, and industrial
automation equipment. The universal card includes support for
all the popular operating systems and a number of cabling
options. And since it will fit into any PCI system, it will
seamlessly make the transition to the future of the PCI bus
along with your application.

Currently the Universal Blue Heat is available in eight ports of
RS-422/485, and four RS-232 plus four RS-422/485 port densities.
Watch for more Universal PCI options coming soon.

Product Name:
ComSync/104
This two-channel, multi-protocol adapter for the PC/104 bus
offers reliable, synchronous or asynchronous serial
communications for your most demanding applications. The ComSync
offers the flexibility of choosing from multiple electrical
interfaces, protocols and encoding schemes to ensure your
hardware/software solution is ideally suited to your situation.
Register Bit Cloning and programmable security options add
convenience to the full list of ComSync features.


Product Name:
WhiteHEAT USB
The high performance WhiteHEAT/USB brings a revolution in multi-
port serial communications to USB bus compatible computers. This
low profile adapter easily and conveniently connects your
peripherals to your computer, be they industrial infrared
sensors or home office fax machines. Affordable, reliable, and
fast, the WhiteHEAT lets you Plug and Play to get the most out
of your POS device, modem or security device.

Product Name:
Single Board Computers
Connect Techs Xtreme/SBC PCIe/104 and PCI-104 Single Board Computers offer a variety of embedded processor options including Intel Atom, Freescale i.MX51, TI OMAP and NVIDIA Tegra. Xtreme/SBC solutions are modular and completely scalable, with access to current embedded processors that are easily upgradeable to accommodate future generations of Intel Atom processors, such as Intel Atom E6xx, or ARM based processors, such as Cortex A8. The on-board connectors of our Xtreme/SBC products provide access to SATA, USB, Ethernet, LVDS and VGA Video, and RS-232 & RS-422/485.

Product Name:
Qseven Carrier Boards
Connect Techs PCIe/104 and PCI-104 Qseven Carrier Board solutions are small embedded carrier boards that allow complete integration with industry standard Qseven modules. Using any off-the-shelf Qseven module provides a variety of embedded processor options including Intel Atom, Freescale i.MX51, TI OMAP and NVIDIA Tegra. This modular approach easily accommodates future generations of Intel Atom processors, such as Intel Atom E6xx, or ARM based processors, such as Cortex A8. Easy access to SATA, USB, Ethernet, LVDS and VGA Video, and RS-232 & RS-422/485 via on-board connectors.

Product Name:
FreeForm/Express FPGA
FreeForm/Express
Connect Techs FreeForm/Express is a reconfigurable computing platform that is based on the Xilinx Spartan-6 FPGA; featuring integrated PCI Express® blocks, advanced memory support, and 3.125 Gbps low-power transceivers. FreeForm/Express includes an industry-standard FPGA Mezzanine Card (FMC) connector, which provides a flexible I/O interface for future scaling and customization. Additional features include a 128MB DDR3, two 10/100/1000 Ethernet ports, and an RS-232 serial port. FreeForm/Express is ideally suited for applications requiring both prototyping and field deployment including real-time data acquisition, high performance video processing, and software defined radio.

Product Name:
Xtreme I/O Opto FPGA
Connect Techs Xtreme I/O Opto features digital I/O with isolation and high voltage capabilities, along with the reconfigurable Actel ProASIC3 FPGA for customized applications.

Xtreme I/O Opto is designed with 48 isolated digital I/O channels  24 optically isolated inputs and 24 optically isolated outputs, 2kV RMS isolation on all channels, a voltage range of +0 to +40V DC, and the programmable Actel ProASIC3 FPGA. The ProASIC3 low-cost, low-power consumption FPGA supports 15,000 to 3,000,000 gates and up to 620 high-performance I/Os. Xtreme I/O Opto is ideal for demanding embedded technology applications in the military, aerospace, medical and industrial sectors.

Choose from our off-the-shelf solution for rapid proof of concept, or take advantage of our development kit or custom FPGA design services for an exact fit solution.
