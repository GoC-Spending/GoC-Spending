Company Profile


GreenStep has helped over 1600 businesses and organizations to become more environmentally friendly and responsible. Our purpose is to change the world by making sustainable business more profitable than business as usual, and our big, hairy, audacious goal is to become the business most known for improving the social, environmental and economic performance of small and medium sized enterprises.

We aim do this by helping businesses go green through a variety of consulting services, certifications and software, all designed to be accessible and affordable for the average small and medium-sized business. Our consulting services include energy assessments, carbon footprint measurement, waste audits, employee training, green business action plans, sustainability strategy development, and more.

Through our certification offerings, Green Tourism Canada or ecobase Certified, GreenStep helps businesses measure their carbon footprint, create a green action plan, and tell a credible green story in their marketing, based on international standards, with the opportunity to generate thousands of dollars in an eco-fund to invest in energy efficiency and carbon reduction projects right at their own business.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
ecobase Carbon Software
ecobase Carbon Software is the most affordable carbon footprint calculator for businesses to measure, reduce and report on their carbon emissions. Based on the Greenhouse Gas Protocol standard, the software uses Environment Canada, US EPA and other internationally recognized emissions factors to help businesses of all sizes to measure, report and reduce their carbon footprint. Visit www.ecobasecarbonsoftware.com for more information.

Service Name:
Green Tourism Certification
Green Tourism is the world’s largest and most credible sustainable tourism certification program, and GreenStep is the exclusive provider of this program in Canada. The Green Tourism assessment and certification program helps hotels, motels, resorts, wineries, breweries, distilleries, restaurants, tour operators and tourist attractions improve their sustainability performance and become recognized for their efforts in this area. There are more than 2,300 tourism businesses certified internationally, and over 100 here in Canada. Visit www.greentourismcanada.ca for more information.


Service Name:
ecobase Certified
ecobase Certified generates an eco-fund for businesses to invest in projects that reduce their carbon footprint, while simultaneously reducing energy, water and waste costs. We provide third party credibility and green certification to all types of businesses including restaurants, hotels, retailers and other businesses. Our expert team provides green business advice to help businesses make the best choices about where to invest their eco-funds. This way, businesses can meet the growing consumer demand for responsible business. Visit www.ecobasecertified.com for more information.

Service Name:
Sustainability Consulting: Strategy, Action Plans, Waste Audits, Energy Audits, Carbon Footprint Measurement
GreenStep can provide a wide range of sustainability-related consulting services, customized to meet your needs. We do a lot of our work remotely, which helps to reduce our carbon footprint and make our services more accessible for small and mid-sized business throughout North America. Visit www.greenstep.ca for more information.
