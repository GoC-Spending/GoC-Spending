Company Profile


For over 45 years, L-3 Electronic Systems has been at the forefront in the design, integration, manufacturing and support of a wide range of systems and electronic assemblies. Specializing in complex, advanced technology programs, we supply customers in all areas of the aerospace industry, as well as military agencies and governments around the world.

Our aim is to provide our customers with a complete service offering, backing up our products and services with a customer-focused approach to support. We put high emphasis on quality while striving to develop flexible and value-added solutions. As a result, the Company has earned a solid reputation and an ever-growing list of world-class customers.

Our principal lines of business are: Display Solutions
* Ground Vehicles Electronic Solutions
* Weapon Systems Management
* Repair and Overhaul
* Systems Engineering
* Value-added Manufacturing

The Company applies innovative technologies and know-how to supply customers with reliable defence products and services. Some of our current activities include:
* Development of advanced flat panel colour displays for the Canadian Air Force, Royal Australian Air Force and other F/A-18 aircraft users
* Optimized weapon system support and avionics mission systems life cycle support for the CP-140 and other aircraft
* Manufacturing of light armoured vehicle electronic assemblies, chassis and turret control systems for the Canadian Air Force, U.S. Army, U.S. Marine Corps., Australian Army and New Zealand Army
* Development and support of advanced LED-based displays for various aircraft applications, including Apache, F-16, C-17 and Japanese F-2
* Development of vehicle mobility enhancement solutions for ground vehicles, such as Electronically Controlled Active Suspension System (ECASS)
* Development of C4ISR solutions for the Canadian Air Force and other agencies
* After-sales and continuing product support for military and commercial INS users worldwide

The Company employs a dedicated workforce of over 500 experienced personnel, including engineers, researchers, and skilled technicians. L-3 ES is ISO 9001:2000 and AS 9100:2004 certified, and a preferred supplier for both Lockheed Martin Corp. and The Boeing Company.

Technology profile

L-3 Communications Electronic Systems Inc. (L-3 ES), a division of L-3 Communications, is an internationally recognized supplier of integrated systems and highly sophisticated electronic equipment for both defence and commercial applications.

With facilities in Toronto and Halifax and support staff in Ottawa, L-3 ES employs approximately 500 highly skilled people.

L-3 Communications is a leader and prime defence contractor in Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (ISR), secure communications, training, sensors and simulation, specialized products, aircraft maintenance and modernization, naval vessel and army vehicle systems. L-3 provides these products and services to governments, military forces and commercial customers in Canada, the US and internationally.
