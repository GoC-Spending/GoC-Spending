Company Profile


Porcupine Trailers Ltd. manufacturer of Timmins semi-logging
trailers & wagons from 20' to 52' long & 8' to 12 ' wide.
We manufacture the Timmins spring suspension
& Timmins Air Lift for rugged road conditions. We also manufacture floats and pup wagons. We service
all types of trailers & sell all related parts.
