Company Profile


Fitter International Inc. has been Leading the World to Better Balance since 1985 with our unique line of health and wellness products. We are a world leader in rehab and prehab products including Balance Boards, Exercise Ball Chairs, Foam Rollers, Resistance Tubing and much more. We carry over 300 products many of them made in our home plant, and sell via the web (retail, wholesale and professional) as well as our 24-page catalog. We also supply a worldwide distribution network of proven health and medical resellers. We now offer a full line of Active Office tools and accessories including sit stand desks and desk toppers, along with Active Sitting tools. Fitter is the exclusive Canadian reseller for the New Varidesk which is a cost effective desk topper solution that makes any work surface in to an adjustable Sit Stand desk in Seconds. No assembly, no installation and easy up easy down. Ships USP anywhere in Canada with 7 models to choose from stating under $400.

Product Name:
Swopper active sitting chair
Fitter offer a wide range of Active seating chairs and surfaces to help improve your movement and health in the work place.
The Swopper® seating platform is the result of German innovation in technology and design. Incorporating motion into the act of sitting, the Swopper chair allows for a range of natural and intuitive movements, including tilting, swaying and bouncing. Its 3-D motion makes shifting from one position to another effortless and fun. The constant movement provided by the Swopper makes it the best office chair for posture and overall well being. Active sitting has never been so stylish!

• Adjusts to user’s height, weight, or work surface
• Improves posture and strengthens core muscles
• Helps stabilize the spine and reduce back pain
• Increases circulation and enhances alertness
