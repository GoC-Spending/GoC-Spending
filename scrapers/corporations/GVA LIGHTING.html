Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
STR9® - LED Wall Grazer, MONO, RGB or Dynamic White, for very long runs
STR9® is a high performance, high power, linear surface mount LED luminaire designed for exterior architectural lighting applications such as: wall washing, wall grazing and cove lighting for facades, bridges, airports, shopping malls.
STR9® is totally weatherproof and IP66 rated for use in dry, damp or wet locations. GVA's Unibody design, proprietary sealing process and sealed connectors (even when unmated) contribute to the STR9's unmatched reliability.
New for 2014, the STR9 ®now features through-wiring for end-to-end fixture connection; greatly simplifying installation. New, more compact mounting brackets are also featured on the new design. Its clean, modern look and compact size make it virtually invisible on the structure to which it is installed.
INFINITY technology is available for the STR9® . Up to 264m or 866ft of STR9 luminaires can be connected end-to-end and powered from a single source.
STR9® is available with mono color LEDs, as well as RGB color changing and Dynamic White.


Product Name:
HIGHLIGHTER® HL-COVE - modular LED cove luminaires for large scale installations
HIGHLIGHTERrHL-COVE is a long run modular LED lighting system designed for architectural cove lighting where long runs are necessary and it is undesirable, impractical or impossible to have numerous power feeds or power supplies. HL-COVEs small profile also makes it ideal for very small installation spaces.
HIGHLIGHTEHL-COVE uses GVA Lightings proprietary power and control technology, INFINITY, which allows it be installed in exceptionally long runs. Up to 672 modules can be connected to one feed for a single run of up to 200m.


Product Name:
HIGHLIGHTER® HL-DL - modular LED architectural delineation lighting system for bridges, skyscrapers
Highlighter® HL-DL is a modular LED lighting system for large scale installations. Typical applications include architectural delineation where long runs are necessary and limited power feeds are available.
Highlighter® HL-DL which features GVA Lightings proprietary power and control system, INFINITY®, which allows it be installed in exceptionally long runs, up to 672ft or 200m, with a single power and data feed.


Product Name:
HIGHLIGHTER® COVE-LITE - lower cost LED cove lighting luminaire
HL-COVE-LITE is part of the HIGHLIGHTER® product group; a long run modular LED lighting system. HL-COVE-LITE is specifically designed to simplify long run interior cove lighting applications and reduce overall system cost.
The HL-COVE-LITE is a 1800mm (6ft) long linear luminaire producing up to 2700lm and consuming a maximum of 30W. Each unit is connected end-to-end to form long continuous straight lighting runs.
Using GVAs proprietary power and control technology, INFINITY®, up to 432ft (130m of HL-COVE-LITE can be fed from a single point. This means no power supplies, junction boxes or branch connections are required for the entire length of the run.


Product Name:
STR7 - a low profile strip LED luminaire
The STR7 is a low profile strip luminaire designed for installations where performance is essential and space is limited.

Product Name:
STR8® - unique LED strip luminaire for under-cabinet, display case, retail and task lighting
STR8® - a patented LED strip luminaire. The unique form of the STR8® body performs the dual function of heat sink and glare control valance. Typical applications include cove, under-cabinet, display case, retail and task lighting.

Product Name:
DUCAT-IV - an adjustable aim recessed LED downlight
DUCAT-IV is an adjustable aim recessed downlight using 4 high power LEDs. Typical applications include general and display lighting for retail locations, office and residential down lighting, spot lighting for displays and exhibitions and effect lighting.

Product Name:
KRONA-IV - a fixed recessed LED downlight
KRONA-IV is a fixed recessed down light using 4 high power LEDs. It is designed for interior and display lighting. Typical applications include general and display lighting for retail locations, office and residential down lighting, spot lighting for displays and exhibitions and effect lighting.

Product Name:
FL - a mono color or RGB color changing LED projector and flood light
FL is a monochromatic or RGB color changing high power flood light designed to project high intensity, highly saturated light onto almost any surface. Typical applications include flood and facade lighting, building illumination, up lighting, back lighting and accent lighting.

Product Name:
PixelDOME - a compact monochromatic of RGB color changing LED pixel luminaire for direct view.
PixelDOME is a compact monochromatic of RGB color changing LED pixel luminaire for direct view. It is designed to be used in an array for low resolution image creation, color changing or effect lighting. Typical applications include Architainment, media façades, low resolution video displays, marker and special effect lighting.

Product Name:
PDB-INF-2000 - Power-Data Box 2000W 380VDC
The Power-Data Box PDB-INF-2000 is a complete solution for supplying both power and control data to GVA LED luminaires.
PDB-INF-2000 is a 2000 Watt, 380V DC model with INFINITY® technology for long-run lighting installations.

Product Name:
LIRA - a miniature bracket mount LED spot light for architectural and landscape lighting
LIRA - a miniature bracket mount LED spot light for architectural and landscape lighting. Color changing RGBW.
