Company Profile


Our customers are businesses from a wide range of industries including manufacturers, processors, distributors, government, 3PL's, and oil and gas companies. Most are long term clients where we add value through a combination of industrial equipment, supplies, and services.

Our first "cam|mandment" is to create ecstatic customers. This standard of excellence is achieved through a process that starts with gaining an understanding of the unique business needs and objectives of each customer. The foundation of customer knowledge results in solutions that resolve the immediate challenge and also align with longer term business objectives.

From a service perspective, our mandate is to "deliver solutions in a manner that exceeds customer expectations", combining superior solutions with extraordinary service.

Our primary product lines include Material Handling, Storage, and Packaging equipment as well as Safety, Maintenance, Corrugate, and Packaging supplies.

Specialties

Material Handling, Storage, Packaging, Safety, Maintenace, Corrugate

Success stories:

cam|industrial supply provides a full range of system related products and services for customers who are interested in Turnkey Solutions. We can provide everything from start to finish including; Design, Engineering, Supply, Project Management, Installation, Service and Preventative Maintenance. While similar products and services are available elsewhere, where we stand apart is in our business approach, the additional services we offer, and the customer experience we deliver.

In short, while we have products and services available, we are not simply in the business of selling products and services. Instead, we are in the business of creating value for our business partners, using our experience and expertise to properly align various products and services to achieve that goal. We believe this to be a substantially different approach than what is most common in the industrial market where vendors focus on their products and themselves.

Our process is thorough and requires dialog, resulting in solutions that provide higher value in the long term.
