Company Profile


Based in Pasadena, CA, Tetra Tech is a full-service engineering and science firm with a substantial global presence. We help our clients conceptualize and execute innovative solutions to their most difficult problems.

From front-end science and planning to design, construction management and operations, Tetra Tech’s global service network, facilitated by our Initiatives program that coordinates resources for specific markets and provides best-in-class experts with worldwide project experience. They deliver a high level of integrated services for the full project life-cycle in five service areas: water, environment, infrastructure, resource management, and energy.

Tetra Tech has offices and operational infrastructure throughout the United States, Canada, and abroad. With 13,000 employees at 300 offices in more than 120 countries on six continents, Tetra Tech’s technical knowledge and hands-on site work is broad and deep. Our staff is supported by a uniform administrative and management system that project teams can access immediately to ensure work is completed effectively.

Tetra Tech has expanded its geographic presence significantly in recent years through strategic acquisitions and internal growth, especially in Canada, Latin America, and Australia. We also have considerable operations in Asia, Europe, and the Middle East.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Engineering Services
Oil and Gas
Tetra Tech oil and gas teams are comprised of industry experts who understand our client’s needs and manage every project with exceptional safety.

Tetra Tech provides EPCM services (engineering, procurement, and construction management), EPC services (engineering, procurement, construction), and health and safety, at work services as well as environmental services such as siting studies, permitting, risk management, regulatory evaluations, state-of-the-art soil and groundwater hydrology, and remediation and restoration analysis services.

We deliver sustainable solutions with flexible and customized approaches to meet our client’s goals. Our engineers have the expertise, talent, and broad experience to address our clients’ challenges.

Tetra Tech offers a proven track record of solutions in the following oil and gas sectors:
• Upstream: Tight oil, gas processing, oil sands, and conventional
• Midstream: Pipelines and stations, terminals, LNG/CNG
• Downstream: Refining, petrochemicals

North American-based expertise with a local approach
Our extensive experience within the industry allows us to provide our clients with unparalleled customized service. We combine this with our belief in working directly alongside our clients to develop long-term relationships that ensure they achieve continued success. Although we have a worldwide presence, we make sure to take a local approach for every project.

In Eastern Canada, Tetra Tech has built long-term relationships with its clients and offers comprehensive services that encompass all major processes of refining as well as a wide range of services for liquid and gas pipelines, terminals, pumping and compressor stations.

Mining and Metals
Our "Mining and Metals" team offers a fully integrated range of environmental solutions, engineering and construction for mining and industrial processes.

We provide complete mine life cycle from exploration, pre-development, project permitting and approvals to construction, operations and reclamation, remediation and closure. We also have extensive experience in industrial risk management and emergency response planning.

We perform exploitation, extraction and processing of a vast array of metals. In most cases we are called upon to modify, improve, modernize or expand existing facilities. These projects are often carried out by our personnel at the client's site—a formula we call “Plant Engineering”.

The success of our structured approach to plant project delivery services is evidenced by the execution of thousands of engineering projects of all types and all sizes.
