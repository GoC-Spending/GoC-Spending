Company Profile


Avizo Experts-Conseils is the only team of dedicated professionals with more than 25 years of expertise in wastewater characterization and flow measurement. Our team specializes in industrial and municipal wastewater and provides comprehensive, defensible data used to assess, monitor, repair, and improve wastewater flow management systems.

Avizos clients benefit from a fully integrated state-of-the-art fleet of vehicules. It allows for fast, accurate and safe on-site actions.
