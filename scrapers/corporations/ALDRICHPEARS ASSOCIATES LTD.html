Company Profile


AldrichPears Associates (APA) is an internationally recognized and award winning interpretive planning and exhibit design firm that specializes in creating visitor experiences for museums, interpretive centres, science centres, zoos, aquaria and other themed attractions. We are a talented and experienced team of interpretive planners, exhibit developers, researchers, exhibit designers, graphic designers, interpretive writers and project managers. We serve an international clientele and have extensive experience on projects across North America and around the globe.
