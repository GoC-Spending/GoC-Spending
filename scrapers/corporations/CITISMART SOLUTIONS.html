Company Profile


Citismart Solutions is a market leader in Wireless Intelligent Lighting Controls, Outdoor Wireless Sensors and remote management of outdoor (street and parking lot) lights and sensor network.

Based on a patented presence detection solution, our adaptive control products offer on-demand lighting, meaning the right amount of light when and where it is necessary. Our solution is suitable for both traditional (PLL, SON, HID) as well as new LED street lamps. During off-peak hours lights dim to a pre-defined level. Upon detection of a pedestrian, bicycle, or car, all lights in the vicinity increase to full brightness. In this way the solution combines safety with savings. The solution reduces energy consumption by up to 80% and maintenance costs by up to 50% while providing safety and security.

Citismart delivers a powerful, modular, open source sensor platform for the Internet of Things to implement reliable Smart Cities and M2M solutions.
Our versatile platform allows implementation of any Wireless Sensor Network such as:
• Monitoring of parking spaces availability in the city. Traffic Congestion
• Monitoring of vehicles and pedestrian levels to optimize driving and walking routes. Smart Lighting
• Intelligent and weather adaptive lighting in street lights. Forest Fire Detection
• Monitoring of combustion gases and preemptive fire conditions to define alert zones.
• Smart Grid Energy consumption monitoring and management.
• And many other types of sensors

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Citysense
The CitySense is a compact, sensor based “smart control” device for presence based monitoring and control for street lighting. The product is compatible with both existing and new street lighting fixtures (such as LED).

The CitySense offers ‘on demand lighting’ in which lighting levels adjust based on the presence of pedestrians, bicycles or cars. The lights ‘dim’ during off-peak hours when there is no one in the vicinity and upon detection of a human presence, all the streetlights in the surrounding area ‘glow’ to a user defined brightness. This results in substantially reduced energy consumption in a safe and comfortable manner.

Inbuilt monitoring notifies users (via CityManager) of lamp failures, decay and other maintenance triggers. This greatly reduces the need for expensive visual inspections enabling a reduction in operation and maintenance costs of up to 50%.

Product Name:
Skylight
SkyLite is a plug-and-play wireless lighting controller, which allows remote monitoring, management and control of outdoor luminaires.

A range of SkyLite models can be installed internally or externally to lamps depending on the project needs and available infrastructure.
User-configurable, multi-level dimming schedules enable a substantial reduction in energy consumption by up to 60% in a safe and comfortable manner.
SkyLite supports automatic wireless communication with CitySense, and can be remotely accessed via CityManager creating a smart, economical, energy efficient and safe environment
Inbuilt monitors notify users via CityManager about lamp failures/ decay and important maintenance aspects, thereby significantly reducing the need for expensive visual inspections as well as operational and maintenance costs.

Product Name:
City Manager
CityManager is a user-friendly web-based software for remote monitoring, management and control of street lighting infrastructures. The software is directly coupled with the CitySense through the internet and features:

Remote programming and modification of settings of individual and groups of streetlights.
Context specific lighting levels to reflect local conditions such as weather changes, special events, emergency situations, etc.
Analytic tools such as energy savings/consumption, detection patterns and lamp failure detection.
Failure reporting and planning/maintenance support resulting in reduced maintenance costs.
An open platform that can interface with hardware from other providers (e.g. traffic and local weather monitoring) enabling additional services on the same platform.

Product Name:
Waspmote
There are more than 60 sensors available to connect to Waspmote: CO, CO2, soil moisture, presence, humidity, temperature, vehicle detection, radiation, current, liquid, luminosity, etc. The Sensor boards allow developers to easily use them in Waspmote.

Service Name:
Design and Remote Monitoring Services
Citismart uses digital technologies to enhance performance, safety and well being, to reduce costs and resource consumption, and to engage more effectively and actively with its citizens. Key 'smart' sectors include transport, energy, health care, water and waste
