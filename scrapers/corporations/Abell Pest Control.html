Company Profile


Abell Pest Control is a family-owned business now in its third generation. Since 1924, Abell Pest Control has grown to be the largest Canadian-owned pest management company with branch offices in most principal centres across Canada, Michigan and Ohio.

Earl F. Abell founded Abell Pest Control in 1924. In the beginning, our company was primarily a manufacturer of pesticides, distributing our product in the city of Toronto Ontario Canada. It did not take long, however, for us to evolve into a service company. We were among the first organizations to recognize the application of pesticides as a valuable public service: the industry of pest management was born. It was during the depression years of the 1930's that the company also recognized the importance of effective and affordable pest management as an instrument of food protection and public health safety.


Earl's son, Ralph E. Abell, joined the firm in 1952. During his tenure, the company began a long period of expansion. The 1960's and 1970's saw the company make significant growth gains and expansion into several other cities within Canada. We were becoming a strong force in the Canadian pest management industry.


The third generation began to have impact in 1976 when John R. Abell, the current president, joined the firm. This started the years of diversification and international expansion. During the 1970's and 1980's, we established ourselves as the largest Canadian-owned pest management company. Our plan for the 1990's is to continue building on our foundation of introducing innovative products and services and providing top quality technical skill and knowledge.


Much of our company's success has been attributed to the attention we give to the specific needs of our customers in conjunction with our position of being a leader in our industry. To be considered the best at what we do is the only goal for which we strive. The success we have had at reaching our goal means that you cannot go wrong choosing Abell Pest Control to serve you.


Geographic Markets: Abell Pest Control provides pest management services to food processors, property managers and commercial and residential accounts. We provide service coast to coast throughout Canada and similarly throughout the continental United States.

Product Name:
Abell Pest Control Inc. Integrated Pest Management program (IPM), Abell Plus Program.

Integrated Pest Management is a combination of various techniques designed to maintain control of pests. This method can be successfully implemented and result in the overall reduction or elimination of the use of specific pesticides. Abell Pest Control Inc. recognizes the need to reduce pesticide usage in food processing and handling operations while still addressing the ingress of insect, rodent, and avian pests that are inherent to this process. Abell has successfully demonstrated in processing facilities coast to coast that this process is an effective alternative to conventional control methods.


In cases where pesticide usage is required, Abell Pest Control Inc. will evaluate usage patterns and recommend the least toxic, most effective alternative to existing pesticides.
