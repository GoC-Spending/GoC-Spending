Company Profile


Since 1953, Smith Packaging has been committed to innovation in
packaging excellence. Our team of designers utilize advanced
computer-aided technology to create customized integrated
solutions for our clients. We manufacture and distribute a
comprehensive array of packaging products that make high-
quality, high-performance packaging cost-effective. Whether
your packaging requirements are for cushioning, surface
protection,control of ESD or high impact graphic and
Point-of-Purchase displays, from the automotive industry to
the high-tech industry, Smith Packaging can satisfy all your
packaging needs.

Smith Packaging has the knowledge and the materials to meet
the requirements and challenges of the automotive industry.
We can produce custom designed, returnable, plastic corrugated
totes complete with interior dunnage for any application. In
fact, Smith Packaging can work with almost any material,
whether you need a returnable or expendable product, whether
you need cross-linked or polyurethane foam, we can provide a
cost-effective solution.

Smith Packaging's team of experienced design professionals
use advanced C.A.D. technology to create the packaging
solutions you need. Our vast selection of raw materials and
processing methods are tailored to find the packaging solutions
which meet your value and performance requirements. Excellence
in package design can enhance your competitive edge.

Smith Packaging has successfully implemented ISO-9001 - the
model for Quality Assurance and the standard for companies
engaged in all aspects of manufacturing and service. Smith
Packaging's ISO-9001 registration is a testament to our
continuing commitment to the highest standards.

At Smith Packaging, design innovation is an interactive
experience. From the moment the Packaging Design Request is
passed on to the designer, to the time when the completed
pack is ready for just-in-time delivery. Smith Packaging is
dedicated to customer satisfaction, every step of the way.

Innovative design capability is part of everything that we
do at Smith Packaging. The seemingly miraculous transformation
of a basic 2-D sheet of material into an advanced, fully-
functional, 3-D packaging system is but one element of a vast
myriad of technologically superior capabilities that can be
expertly created by our dedicated design team.

Smith Packaging has the in-house ability to produce a wide
variety of customized packaging solutions. Our fleet of
trucks assures punctual just-in-time ( JIT ) delivery to meet
the toughest requirements of any industry. Our JIT program
is so efficient that it can actually lower your company's
inventory requirements. Smith Packaging's extensive capabilities
ensure the highest level of integrated packaging excellence
from design solution to delivered product.

Achievements Awards
Smith Packaging has been recognized as one of the 50 Best
Managed Private Companies in Canada by Arthur Andersen & Co.
and The Financial Post. As part of our continuing commitment
to excellence we have obtained ISO-9001 registration which
is your guarantee of the highest level of professional
standards. Our performance, productivity, competitiveness and
overall innovation puts Smith Packaging ahead of the pack. We
have received several design awards for our innovative and
effective ability to meet our customers challenging packaging
requirments.
