Company Profile


Bevco custom engineer and manufacture a wide variety of
conveying systems and equipment for the food and beverage
industries. Bevco is committed to quality, flexibility and after
sales service. Some of Bevco's benefits are: custom engineered
using latest in CAD technology, proven design and performance,
efficient and flexible modular systems built to North American
standards, safety features standard, factory assembled/tested
prior to shipment, on-time delivery and quick on-site service,
quality assured warranty.

CONVEYORS, CONVEYING EQUIPMENT SYSTEMS & SUPPLIES
Bevco's standard conveyor package includes many items only
available as options by competitors. Catenary guards and hinged
sprocket covers are just two examples of the many items Bevco
includes in the basic conveyor. Bevco's modular design offers
flexibility and is simple to assemble. Each section of the
conveyor line has been pre-assembled in the shop, then numbered
and separated for shipping. Legs, drives, chains and other
attachments are crated separately to avoid damage. Bevco's shop
drawings will make your installation quick and easy.

Hardware

- Line controls using Allen-Bradley family of controllers as
standard
- All major controllers can be used as an option, i.e.Modicon,
Omron,etc.
- Allen-Bradley AC inverters are standard. Others available as
an option.
- Custom designed and built control panels. All control panels
are assembled, programmed and pre-tested prior to shipment to
reduce start up time.
SYSTEM
- Integration of major components [Filler, Labeller, Packer,
etc] to provide coordinated line control.
- Various levels of control can be provided from basic
stop/start control to full line control featuring AC frequency
inverters with analog control and operator interfaces.
- Analog control of the conveyor frequency inverters allow speed
of the major equipment. Provides smooth ramping of conveyors,
eliminates the need to reset conveyor speeds when filler speed
changes on different container sizes.
- Operator interfaces can be provided to allow graphic display
of line condititons and line control as well as production down
time reporting.
- Complete commissioning and strart up assistance provided.
Training of operators and plant maintenance on system operation
and maintenance.




Product Name:
GRIPPER RINSER
Choice of water or air rinsers with options to preheat glass or
condition rinse media. Fully adjustable for different container
sizes without changing parts. Models available to speeds of 1200
bpm.

Product Name:
WARMER/COOLER PASTEURIZER
Warmer/cooler Pasteurizer - Low water usage and high volume of
air flow and recirculated water increase evaporative cooling.
Standard features include stainless steel construction, blowers,
pumps and controls.


Product Name:
HIGH LEVEL BULK DEPALLETIZER
High Level Bulk Depalletizer - Designed to automatically
depalletize layers of containers from a pallet and sweep them on
to the depalletizer's transport/accumulation table

Product Name:
COMBINER
Combiner - To meet your packaging needs. zero pressure, slimline
and dual discharge single filers to combine round and non-round
containers at various speeds.


Product Name:
BI-DIRECTIONAL ACCUMULATION TABLE
Bi-Directional Accumulation Table Designed to operate
independent of your existing equipment or easily integrate into
your present system to add flexibility to your line.

Product Name:
HIGH SPEED ELEVATOR / LOWERATOR
High Speed Elevator/Lowerator Fast line speeds to 1200
containers per minute. Fully adjustable to accommodate round and
non-round containers to 9" wide. Variable elevation options
available.


Product Name:
HOT PACK RINSER
The Bevco "HOT PACK" rinser has been developed to prevent
thermal shock and related breakage of containers during the hot
filling process.
