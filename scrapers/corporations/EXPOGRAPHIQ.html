Company Profile


Let's begin this conversation as we begin every client
conversation ... by talking about you for a moment.
After all, you know your business best. You know your
customers. And by taking the time to listen to what you
have to say, we develop a clear understanding of how to
shape an effective communications strategy.

In a very real sense, we ask you to play a defining role in
helping us produce effective results, from a relatively
small-scale signage project to large-scale custom exhibit
designs. So when discussing your projects with our account
executives and designers, expect some provocative questions.
The answers you provide will fuel their understanding and,
ultimately, fuel their imaginations.

CUTTING EDGE
Years ago we bought into the concept of using the latest
in technology. We've been buying into the concept ever since.
- Mac, PC network with UNIX backbone
- 2D & 3D computer aided design environment
- CAD/CAM automated routing system
- 3M certified 55 inches encapsultion laminator
- 10' x 12' flatbed 1200dpi inkjet direct printer

Because we have been in this business for almost two decades,
we intuitively know which is the best way to proceed and we
make sure that you also understand the process.

DESIGN AND THE BIG IDEA

Ultimately, we design and build things that help you communicate.
Whatever your message, we'll give it shape, colour and substance.
Our team of designers has a depth of knowledge in industrial,
exhibit, product and graphic design. They're ideas generators.
Problem solvers. They're also looking out for your interests,
making sure the everything they propose integrates with your
corporate image. Because in design, image is everything.

When you hire a firm to do a job, you want the assurance that
it's led by a solid management team. Here's something to think
about.

We've been in business since 1979, growing from a small
organization to more than 50 employees today. Our client
list has grown as well, and includes such corporations as
Air Canada, Thales, Ross Video, Museum of History, Science and Technology Museum.
The list also includes a number of government departments such
as Canadian Heritage and Public Works. Spanning two generations,
our management team blends management experience with youthful
insight.

In 1993 and 1995, Expographiq was recognized as one of Canada's
50 best managed private companies as surveyed by Arthur Andersen
and the Financial Post. It's and award we were honoured to
receive, yet it would mean little if our management style didn't
suit our customers. That style is one of frankness and openness
to new ideas. It's a style that recognizes that our future
success rests on the quality of the relationship we develop with
you right now.

CONTROLLING INTEREST

Whether it's a brochure for your sales force, wayfinding
signage, or a custom booth to increase your trade show presence,
or a museum that requires design and or fabrication,
Expographiq has put controls in place to ensure you receive a
consistently high-quality product. Having a quality control system permeates our thinking,
from the administrative office to the shop floor.

Technology profile

-The drafting department uses an optimal combination of 2D (AutoCAD) and 3D (SolidWorks) software both allowing to extract precise CNC cutting profiles from shop drawings for acurate millwork and assemblies.
-Specialized custom CNC cutting for materials are made on our 8’ x 12’ CAD/CAM automated routing system run by AXYZ Software
-High Quality graphic production. These 1200 dpi inkjet and direct print machines (DTS) provide a faster,more productive and even wider media solution to large format graphics with output available on high quality papers, films, vinyl’s and canvas.
-Our signmaking department is equipped with two 48” automated, high-speed vinyl/cutters with Signshop links to our CAD/CAM design workstation.
-Our artists and specialists can reproduce any scientifically correct artifacts, props, environments and interactive that may be required in any exhibition or diorama. If we do not have the expertise in-house, we have partners in the industry with whom we work with on regular basis.
