Company Profile


Diplomatic Security Service (DSS) provides a variety of licensed security services. While others advertise being the leading providers of what can only be described as cheap security solutions, we opted to take a different approach to security. Our service begins by supporting fellow soldiers through employment and decent wages. With the management and support of our parent company, Defense Intelligence Service, we truly offer a unique combination of services including risk assessments, security personnel, security technologies, close protection, prisoner and detainee escort, secure transportation, and screening service solutions.

In a world now touched by terrorism, being able to work, play and live in a safe and secure environments can be a complex challenge. With the experience obtained from Private Military and Security contractors, we are able to provide our clients with customized security solutions unique to their security challenges. At DSS, we believe that challenges are meant to be overcome, and that security only comes from real experience.

DSS delivers services locally while meeting regional, national and international standards. DSS promotes close client relationships for quick and efficient decision making. Competent managers are assigned and are responsible for ensuring that our services are flexible, cost-efficient and of high quality. The clear organizational structure of our service promotes effective client care and security. Let us help you secure your world.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Security Officers
- Extensive selection and vetting process involving enhanced background and police checks.
- Comprehensive training program, officers are well prepared to service the client.
- Reliability and punctuality.
- Better pay to ensure stability of personnel, staff turnaround is minimal.
- Responsive and resourceful in identifying and resolving incidents.
- Officers protect your property against unauthorized entry, vandalism and theft.
- Providing a security escort for valuable cargo.

Service Name:
Mobile Patrol & Alarm Response
- Mobile Patrol Units: are equipped and operated by fully trained and competent operators.
- Key Holder Service: open and close facilities at any time of the day.
- Facility Checks: Officers will check client's property outside of business hours or at the request of client.
- Alarm Response: Officers are properly trained in alarm response to ensure the client's property is adequately protected.

Service Name:
Threat and Risk Assessments
- Conducting assessments relating to the vulnerability of physical and intellectual property.
- Conducting interviews of known and potential threats, to determine ability to follow through.
- Conducting site assessments in order to "harden the target."

Service Name:
Close Protection
- Based on threat assessment, provide appropriate level of escort for client.
- Teams are trained to take control of security detail to ensure complete protection of client.
- Personnel are experienced in both individual and team protection.
- Our personnel are trained in all levels of protection, ranging from low risk to diplomatic.

Service Name:
Private Policing
- Have experience with community policing programs based on the San Francisco Patrol Special Police model.
- Our Patrol Special Program is specifically designed to work with Business Improvement Associations.
- Our program utilizes all levels of patrol, ranging from bike and foot patrol, to patrol cars.

Service Name:
Consulting services
- Security Evaluation
- Emergency Measures
- Fire Safety Plan
- Crisis Management Plan
- Business Continuity

Service Name:
Loss prevention
- Like other Loss Prevention agencies, we can place a uniformed and/or plain-clothed officer on site to detect and apprehend shoplifters.
- Our Loss Prevention program is overseen by a former police officer.
- Gathering intelligence, analyzing for crime trends and identifying active suspects, issuing BOLOs to affected businesses.

Service Name:
Screening services
- Screening Officers are trained using stationary and portable screening devices.
- Training includes physical searches of baggage, packages, passengers, people, and vehicles.

Service Name:
Secure Transportation
- Our drivers are trained in escape and evasion driving, in addition to be licensed in security.
- Transportation can be conducted using our vehicles (sedans and SUVs) or the clients'.
- In special circumstances, armored vehicles may be employed.
- Can provide secure transport for valuable cargo.

Service Name:
Prisoner / Detainee escort
- Transportation of prisoners and detainees using our vehicles (vans and SUVs), or the clients'.
- Escort individuals in custody for hearings, or other requirements.
- Our officers have experience working with CBSA, Corrections Canada, B.C. Sheriffs, and B.C. and Military Police.

Service Name:
24 Hour Monitoring Services
- 24 Hour Operation: Control Centre staffed at all times by trained and experienced officers.
- CCTV Remote Monitoring: Remotely monitor client's facility via camera through a LAN or via radio frequency.
- Lone Worker program: Client and Officers check in to ensure personal safety and emergency response.

In-License (Seeking):
K9 Narcotic Detection
- Narcotic detection dogs are trained solely on real narcotics acquired from, and licensed by, Health Canada.
- Training includes a variety of common scenarios to expose the dogs to as many narcotics commonly discovered in society today.

In-License (Seeking):
K9 Explosive Detection
- We use an experienced Dog Master to help train our explosive detection dogs for wide-ranging applications.
- Training includes the use of real explosive substances, which are certified by Natural Resource Canada. These samples are closely regulated.

In-License (Seeking):
Marine Section
- Using federally certified boat operators and trained divers, we can offer water-borne security services.
- Boat teams have experience in evidence and body recovery.

FSC Code:

R100-Intelligence Services
U000-Educational and Training Services
