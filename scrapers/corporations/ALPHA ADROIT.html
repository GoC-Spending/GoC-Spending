Company Profile


Alpha Adroit Engineering Ltd is a Canadian consulting corporation with global reach, providing geotechnical engineering, material testing (soil, concrete, rock, asphalt, soil cement, RCC, etc), construction quality control, and geo-environmental consulting to various industry sectors.

Alpha Adroit Engineering Ltd provides services in Western and Northern Canada— Alberta, British Columbia, Saskatchewan, and Northwest Territories— and The Middle East. Alpha Adroit also provides services in other international locations through their main office in Edmonton, Alberta.

Alpha Adroit is all about providing excellent quality services, always on time and on budget.

Alpha Adroit is licensed by APEGA, APEGBC, APEGS, and CNSC.

Alpha Adroit's head office is located in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Geotechnical Engineering Consulting
Alpha Adroit Engineering Ltd provides a wide range of Geotechnical Engineering services for buildings, land developments, infrastructures, dams and levees, mining, oil and gas, and pipeline industries, including:

— Geotechnical site investigation, instrumentation, and advanced and routine field and laboratory testing
— Numerical modeling, risk analysis, and construction inspection
— Design and inspection for shallow and deep foundations, retaining walls, embankments, excavations, towers, and engineered fills
— Geotechnical investigation and design for ground improvements, retention ponds, sewage systems, and flood diversion systems
— Geotechnical design and monitoring for pavements, roads, bridges, dams, tunnels, tank farms, open-pit mines,
— Geotechnical analysis and design for mining site support facilities, pipelines, oil and gas and manufacturing infrastructures
— Analysis and control of landslides, groundwater seepage, frost heave, thaw instability, swelling, settlement, liquefaction
— Analysis and remediation for ground collapse and large scale subsidence
— Senior and peer review of reports, construction QA/QC, and project management
— Forensic, preventive, and remedial geotechnical engineering
— Research and development

More information:
http://www.alphaadroit.ca/services/geotechnical-engineering.html

Alpha Adroit Engineering Ltd provides geotechnical engineering services throughout Alberta, British Columbia, Saskatchewan, and Northwest Territories— and The Middle East. Alpha Adroit also provides services in other international locations through their main office in Edmonton, Alberta.



Service Name:
Material Testing and Construction Quality Control

Alpha Adroit Engineering Ltd provides services for accurate determination of representative in-situ material properties, proper selection and design of construction materials, and quality control of construction materials and their compliance with design specifications, including:

— Standard, advanced, and custom-designed field and laboratory testing on soil, rock, aggregate, concrete, and asphalt (ASTM, CSA, AASHTO, etc)

— Testing, review, interpretation of test results, technical specifications, and mix design for Portland cement concrete, grout, mortar, shotcrete, plastic concrete, soil-cement, lime-treated soil, fly-ash treated soil, RCC (Roller Compacted Concrete), zero slump concrete, and light-weight fills


We provide construction Quality Assurance (QA) and Quality Control (QC) for concrete construction, earthworks, and other geo-constructions to ensure the construction procedures and materials conform to the design specifications and assumptions, as well as, to the proper construction practices. Some examples include:

— Monitoring utility line placement, site grading, and construction of embankments, engineered fills, excavations, and retaining walls

— Monitoring driving of steel pile and construction of concrete shafts, caissons, belled piles, Compacto piles, footings, and mat foundations

— Rebar and form inspection and testing of fresh and cured concrete for reinforced concrete constructions

— Pile load testing, plate load testing, embankment load testing, and proof rolling

— Monitoring groundwater fluctuations, pore-water pressures, and seepage flow

More Information:
http://www.alphaadroit.ca/services/material-testing-and-construction-quality-control.html

Research and Development

Some of the innovative achievements and R&D interests of Alpha Adroit Engineering Ltd are listed below:

Inventions

— Inventing FRP piezometer for measuring pore-water pressure in partially frozen soils- Awarded Patent: US 9,140,615 B2
— Inventing instrument for characterizing frost properties of soils for road and other construction purposes

Research

— Developing project-specific load and resistance factors for Limit States Design (LRFD- LSD)
— Developing practice procedures based on the state-of-the-art, best practices, and special practices for special projects
— Enhancing and exploring applications of tire crumbs and other industrial wastes in civil engineering projects
— Developing and testing new construction materials and equipment
— Researching containment and remediation of Petroleum Hydrocarbon Contamination (PHC) in permafrost and cold regions
— Researching geo-material properties at extreme environmental conditions
— Deep excavations
— Permafrost and frozen ground engineering; engineering of frozen, freezing, and thawing ground as well as artificial ground freezing

Algorithms

— Data mining and development of database for large data
— Coding artificial intelligence
— Modeling with genetic algorithm



Service Name:
Environmental Services

Alpha Adroit Engineering Ltd provides a variety of environmental consulting and related services for residential and industrial land developments and buildings, including:

— Phase I and Phase II Environmental Site Assessments (ESA) and Environmental Audits

— Specialized numerical modeling for environmental hazard assessment, contamination transport, contamination retention, and contamination clean up

— Environmental cleanup consulting and monitoring for site remediation projects involving hydrocarbons, heavy metals, and other hazardous contaminations

— Providing recommendation for concrete and steel constructions in harsh soils

— Water quality tests for rural and recreational developments

— Design and monitoring of frozen ground barriers for controlling contamination transportation



Service Name:
Frozen Ground Engineering Consulting - Permafrost
Alpha Adroit Engineering Ltd provides geotechnical engineering services for applications involving frozen, freezing, and thawing ground conditions. Some applications include:

— Stability of soil masses in warming permafrost
— Design of shallow and deep foundations in discontinuous permafrost
— Analysis and control of frost and thaw penetration
— Geotechnical engineering for pipelines in continuous and discontinuous permafrost
— Design of thermal insulation
— Evaluation of frost susceptibility and thaw instability
— Investigation and prevention of damages due to artificial ground freezing induced by ice rinks and freezers
— Design of advanced field and laboratory testing


Alpha Adroit Engineering Ltd provides permafrost engineering and frozen ground consulting services throughout Alberta, British Columbia, Saskatchewan, and Northwest Territories. Alpha Adroit also provides services in other international locations through their main office in Edmonton, Alberta.
