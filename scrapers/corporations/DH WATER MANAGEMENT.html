Company Profile


Consult, design, project management, educate, RFP's, specifications, for reclaimed water (rainwater, stormwater, greywater, condensate water) reuse and city water for high efficency irrigation sub-surface, dripline, point source, low volume and conventional irrigation systems. Perform system assessments and audits. Past Board Member of the IA - Irrigation Association in the US. Certified Designer, Certtified Water Conservation Manager, Certified Irrigation Contractor, Certified Landscape Irrigation Auditor, and Golf Irrigation Auditor. Accredited provider for irrigation certification throughout North America and Europe.

Testimonial:

Mr. Haveruk, I would like to express my thanks for your very professional approach to our water reduction initiatives. This was the perfect first step for us toward reduction of water waste and associated costs. This process was a great education for my team and me. Initially we requested your services to help in our environmental initiatives, but through the process you also helped

 Work toward legal compliance with your back flow prevention analysis
 Improve our irrigation in sports turf including root depth analysis and hydro efficiency.
 Improve our specialty irrigation for clay tennis courts that always plagued us.
 Achieve a game plan for our garden irrigation, and lawn bowling sports surfaces.
 Flag a serious potential irrigation main break by pointing out a rusted coupling.
 Create a budget and priority list for immediate and long term planning.

I rarely take the time to write these letters, but when I receive this kind of service, I am happy to recommend the firm as this makes me look good too!


Steven Pataki
Facilities Manager,
Toronto Cricket Skating and Curling Club
