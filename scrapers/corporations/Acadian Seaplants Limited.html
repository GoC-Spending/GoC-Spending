Company Profile


Acadian Seaplants Limited (ASL) is a bio-tech company and the largest independent manufacturer of marine plant products of its type in the world. It is engaged primarily in the sustainable harvesting of wild seaweeds and in the cultivation of unique marine plants for the development of technically-superior seaweed products. Through four processing plants located in the Maritime Provinces, the company processes thousands of tons of marine plants annually providing quality products to global food and food chain (agricultural) industries. Its value-added products are sold into health, beauty, brewery, nutriceutical and pharmaceutical markets, agricultural markets involving animal feeds and specialty fertilizer products such as nutritional products, plant growth regulators and crop biostimulants. The company also cultivates and processes unique seaweeds for the Asian food market. Over 98% of these products are exported to over 70 countries worldwide with the majority of these export sales occurring in the United States, Asia and Europe.

Acadian Seaplants has been producing innovative and functional marine plant products for over 30 years, achieving worldwide distribution and recognition as a diversified manufacturer of novel and high quality products from a variety of seaweeds. It is 100% Canadian owned with all of its products having 100% Canadian content.

Product Name:
FEED SUPPLEMENTS NES

ACADIAN (TM) Kelp Meal is produced from pure Ascophyllum nodosum
seaweed hand-harvested from the cold, clean and nutrient-rich
waters of the north Atlantic coast of Canada. It is a naturally
balanced nutritional supplement for all feed rations and
provides a complete and balanced range of naturally chelated
micronutrients, trace minerals, vitamins, carbohydrates and
amino acids.

ACADIAN (TM) Asco Flour is made from pure Ascophyllum nodosum
seaweed, the most nutrient-rich marine plant available for
aquatic species' use and is a natural source of balanced and
chelated trace minerals, vitamins, amino acids, carbohydrates
and essential nutrients.

SPECTRA-MIN (R) Plus for Dairy is a 100% natural feed supplement
derived from a blend of marine plants containing a broad range
of minerals plus other essential nutrients necessary for optimal
animal nutrition. Producers using SPECTRA-MIN Plus report a
reduction in the symptoms of sub-clinical nutrient deficiencies
common to today's commercial dairy operations.

nutritional feed supplements for Swine. Acadian Seaplants

commercial beef and swine operations. TASCO (R) for Beef and
TASCO (R) for Swine products are safe, all-natural feed
supplements and ingredients.

Technology profile

Several noteworthy aspects of Acadian Sepalnts' operations include: the advanced methods used to harvest seaweeds as a sustainable, renewable resource and the technologies created to process the natural seaweed resources into value-added finished products for sale in exacting global markets. To demonstrate, Acadian Seaplants has developed the worlds largest land-based seaweed cultivation system for edible seaweeds and has created biological seaweed extracts for global agricultural and horticultural markets as well as technical products for animal feed.

Acadian Seaplants sustainable and scientific management of the marine plant resources is recognized locally, nationally and internationally. It has received two environmental awards and it continues to share its sustainable resource management model with other countries.

Acadian Seaplants operates a research center and employs 22 scientists, 9 of whom are PhDs.
