Company Profile


We are a Saskatchewan family owned and operated sanitation supply full line distributor and we have serviced the entire province
since 1967. Quality lines and superb after sale service and
follow up are very important to us in establishing a partnering
effort with our clients. The ability to provide superior
customer training is a top priority and we are upgrading our
skills with some of our major vendors. SPARTAN CHEMICAL COMPANY is one of our main business partners and they help us to provide quality training on floor care, carpet care, health care training,wood floor care, laundry, bio-augmentation, blood-borne pathogens to mention but a few.
