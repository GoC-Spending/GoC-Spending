Company Profile


Small arms and associated services. Colt Canada is the manufacturer of the C7 family of small arms systems. The C7 rifle and C8 carbine are produced under a licence issued by Colt Defense of the USA. The company's market comprises military and police clients in Canada and primarily NATO countries.
