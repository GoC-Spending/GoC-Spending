Company Profile


Services Provided: Consulting Engineering and Project Management.


Year Established: 1952


Crandall Engineering Ltd. is a New Brunswick based company offering a broad range of consulting engineering and project management services. The company has been in operation since 1952 and has successfully completed projects for all levels of government, industrial, commercial and private clients. Crandall works primarily in Atlantic Canada, but has also worked in other areas of Canada and several overseas countries.


The majority of the company's work has been in municipal and industrial water (supply, treatment, storage, pumping and distribution) and wastewater (collection, pumping, treatment) but also has expertise in civil/structural, mechanical, electrical, and industrial disciplines of engineering. Crandall prides itself on being able to offer its clients complete services including: advise, project evaluation, preliminary engineering, detailed design, coordination of government financial and technical approvals, construction administration, commissioning assistance, and financial control.


Crandall Engineering Ltd. provide complete services for projects from small insurance investigations to multi-million dollar municipal and industrial projects. This has included water and wastewater systems for entire municipalities; wastewater treatment plants up to 23,000 m 3 /day (6.0 million U.S. gal./day); water treatment including filtration, reverse osmosis, ion exchange and disinfection; electrical and mechanical systems for municipal works, schools, hospitals, industrial and office buildings and energy conservation retrofits; planning and design for several thousand hectares of industrial parks; design and construction of marine structures including wharves, transit sheds, dredging and marinas; design of transportation works including roads, railway bridges, airport terminals and runway rehabilitation; underground storage tank replacements to meet environmental standards; others.


Crandall recently completed a major design role in a $250 million (Cdn.) 400 MW electric power generation plant for New Brunswick Power Corporation.


Because of the depth of our in-house expertise and project management capability, we frequently pursue large projects and associate with specialists to supplement our group and fully satisfy our client's requirements.


The firm applies total management concepts that have produced efficiently and economically completed projects, including all phases between initial concept and system operation.


The company is registered with the Canadian International Development Agency, Inter-American Development Bank, the World Bank, and the Caribbean Development Bank.


Geographic Marketing Activities: America: Canada, Mexico, Latin; Caribbean.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Environmental Products and Services
Product Name:
Intelligent design of upgraded facilities and developing a specific construction sequence.
Crandall Engineering Ltd. has been involved in the upgrading of several wastewater treatment facilities. Where major expansions are involved, it is often difficult to maintain an acceptable degree of treatment while this work is being done, risking negative environmental impacts.

Working with our clients and government environmental agencies, Crandall has developed innovative design approaches to maintain acceptable levels of treatment during construction. This typically involves unique construction procedures and sequences which must be environmentally acceptable while resulting in a cost-efficient project. This engineering, technical and planning expertise is available to similar projects.

Product Name:
Design of reverse osmosis based water treatment systems.
Combustion gas turbines used for electric power generation require high purity injection water for NOx control. Cation-anion exchange is the common method of water treatment. However, for peaking power generating plants not operated continuously, cation-anion exchange units are not able to start up on demand and produce the high quality water needed.

Crandall developed a water treatment system for a 400 MW peaking power plant based on reverse osmosis to satisfy the standby requirement. It utilizes multi-media filtration for suspended solids removal, pH adjustment, reverse osmosis for dissolved solids removal, and mixed bed ion exchange for high quality polishing. Continuous analysis of the treated water insures quality. An added benefit is a significant reduction in the requirement of acid and caustic, reducing the environmental risk associated with these chemicals.
