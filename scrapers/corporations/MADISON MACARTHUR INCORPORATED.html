Company Profile


Madison MacArthur is one of Canada's most highly respected executive search firms. Our seasoned recruiters provide superior service, results and value to our client's and candidates. With specialty recruiters who focus on job search, we provide superior service, results and value to our clients and candidates. Our business philosophy is in sync with today's economic realities. Founded and incorporated in 1994 in Toronto, Ontario we are a unique, market driven organization with a corporate mission we all embrace.

Key / Major clients:

We have successfully completed over 1200 client searches including international searches in the U.S., U.K., Europe, Russia, the Orient and Mexico. Since 1994, Madison MacArthur has consistently raised the bar in executive search by identifying the highest caliber of talent in the marketplace - talent that meets the unique cultural needs and business goals of our clients. As a member of IRC Global Executive Search Partners, we combine the critical benefits of global reach, local savvy and breadth and depth of industry expertise.

Success stories:

Our philosophy of working with a smaller number of clients than the industry average underpins our success. Not only does this unique exclusivity mean that our accessible talent pool is much wider than other search organizations, it also ensures that our commitment to deliver results is at the very top of our agenda. We perform each search with a commitment to excellence and to the development of long-term client relationships. As a result, more than 70% of our firm's business is realized with existing clients who have regularly renewed their confidence in Madison MacArthur.
