Company Profile


FABRIQ architecture is an architectural firm based in Montreal. Since the year 2000 they have completed a wide variety of projects including showrooms and retail stores, community centers, a golf clubhouse, multi-unit housing, senior housing, offices, factories and warehouses, as well as a series of projects for a variety of government agencies. Current projects include many majors projects for the national defense: kitchens and dining hall projects in Farnham and St-Jean, QC and Borden, ON, major renovation of a training center in Farnham and a new sports complex in St-Jean,etc. All of this projects aim for LEED silver or Gold certification.
