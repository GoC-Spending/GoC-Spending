Company Profile


Since the eighties, Electronique Micro Tech Inc. has been working to serve organizations using QUEUING MANAGEMENT SOLUTIONS. Our clients include financial institutions, government agencies, universities and health care establishments.

Our mission is to design solutions tailored to each of our customers needs regardless of the volume.

Our queuing management systems rapidly, efficiently and unambiguously guide clients towards available wickets or offices.

Our modular, reliable and user-friendly products range from basic, autonomous systems to sophisticated, computerised solutions.

We provide complete services  from consulting to after sales support.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
TRAFFIC DIRECTIONAL SYSTEMS
QUEUING MANAGEMENT SOLUTIONS  WITH NUMBERED TICKETS

Computerized System

Our computerized product offering is the most complete to date. It is a powerful tool composed of a customizable base system with a software interface which includes a variety of modules.

It is a multi-service system specially designed for customer service counters, wickets or desks providing one or multiple services. Due to the systems flexibility, it is capable of fulfilling the simplest to the most sophisticated requirements.

With a ticket obtained at the reception from a numbered ticket issuing printer after selecting the appropriate service, waiting customers are directed towards the various service areas according to the number called.

The constant progress information on the various services contributes to creating a calm environment by avoiding unaccounted delays and contention amongst customers. Consequently your image will emerge as organized, professional and concerned with the well-being of your customers.

Of all the additional modules, the statistics module proves to be an excellent tool for offline historical analysis of past activity, in the form of reports and graphs. As for our real time supervisor module, it will inform and assist you through immediate decision-making.

System advantages:

- Better management and optimization of waiting time in real time
- Precise flow analysis
- Information on service time and waiting time
- Improved allocation of human resources: where each agent is able to maximize their skills
- Efficient tools, for example to measure performance relating to the impact of advertising and marketing campaigns
- Production of customer visit reports which can be used, for example, to analyze sales patterns
- Increased customer satisfaction
- And many more advantages.

This system is ideal to build customer loyalty in addition to delivering competitive and efficient customer service.


Non-Computerized Systems

Our range of non-computerized systems has been specially designed to direct your customers towards available service areas. These systems manage customer arrival order by allocating customers a numbered ticket, taking into account their choice of service pre-selected at arrival time.

Thus your customers will be reassured and informed of their status while waiting for their turn. You ensure your customers a quiet and stress-free wait by ensuring consideration of their order of arrival.

This type of system is suitable if you wish to improve your customers waiting time, allowing you to inform them in a precise and reassuring manner. Our systems will quickly become an essential tool in managing your customer flow.


QUEUING MANAGEMENT SOLUTIONS - WITHOUT NUMBERED TICKETS

Our systems without numbered tickets were specifically designed to direct customers in queue (up to 3 queues) as needed towards available service areas in the shortest delay, with our without the intervention of agents.

Additionally, our unique and reliable presence detectors use innovative technology to automatically call the next customer to be served. These systems allow simple and effective management of your customers waiting queues.

System advantages

- Quicker service
- Improved allocation of customer flow
- Eliminates uncertainty in directing customers to an available wicket
- Automatic customer calling system via presence detector
- More efficient calling procedure
- Improved agent work task allocation
- Increased customer satisfaction.


Product Name:
ELECTRONIC DISPLAY BOARD
Electronic display boards are utilized to diffuse information, general or specific, and as foreign currency exchange rate boards. They may be interconnected and are available in a wide variety of sizes.

Product Name:
AUTOMATIC TELLER MACHINE ASSISTANCE SYSTEM
At the request of financial institutions, we have developed this concept in order to reassure customers using automatic teller machines, with the intention of improving customer service.

This also addresses apprehensions such as:
- Bank card becomes jammed
- Requested transaction not executed correctly or interrupted by an error message
- Or even just a forgotten PIN

In considering all these potential risks we have developed this assistance system, installed at each automatic teller. It allows intervention when a customer requires any kind of assistance.

This assistance system has already proved itself in the opinion of our customers who even attest to recording increased usage rates following the installation of such a system at their automatic tellers.

Innovative and reassuring, it encourages the loyalty of customers using automatic tellers who prefer locations where the system has been deployed over locations where it is not available. This is due to the fact that customers know that there is human assistance available when necessary.
