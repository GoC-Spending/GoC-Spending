Company Profile


Our vision for a universal platform for resilient, reliable technologies is what drives us. We design, build and service robust technology solutions our clients leverage to reduce maintenance, duplication and cost.

Our strength is in our people, and we know it. Our advantage is in the integration of our skills to deliver a complete solution to our clients. We are a high-quality technology firm with an aim to be one of the prominent names in the Canadian technology services industry.

Services  

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Audio-Visual
Design/build of presentation environments including video presentation, audio reinforcement, automation through Crestron, and the necessary cabling and corresponding infrastructure.

Product Name:
Unified Communications
DVAI is a proud dealer of ShoreTel's VoIP appliances and telephones. We design, install and service ShoreTel equipment and Plantronics accessories. ShoreTel offers the lowest Total Cost of Ownership and hightest client satisfaction in the industry. Their equipment is fault-tolerant and offers the five 9's availability.

Product Name:
Wireless LAN
DVAI designs and builds wireless networks as a Ruckus Wireless Big Dog. Our staff and designers are manufacturer-trained guaranteeing optimal service and uptime. Ruckus is a high-end wireless offering with patented Beamform technology, reducing the number of access points required while providing higher bandwidth than offered by other manufacturers.

Product Name:
Conferencing
DVAI designs and builds comprehensive conference solutions with Polycom, Aver and ShoreTel products. Our collaboration solutions provide the means to travel to multiple destinations each day without leaving your office, all made possible by a number of different technologies: voice conferencing, web conferencing, webcasting, web data conferencing, video conferencing, media teleconferencing, media video conferencing, data conferencing.

Product Name:
Broadband Video Distribution
DVAI designs, installs and services head-end and distribution systems in educational, corporate and government facilities using optical, coaxial or CAT5 (or better) UTP cabling.

DVAI's CATV distribution systems provide video to user groups of 1 to 14,000. Our systems account for community-antenna television (CATV), satellite, internal, and live video feeds. Systems can also be set up to deliver video-conferencing capabilities within a campus, allowing large groups to collaborate from different locations. Other sources include live video from remote cameras, airing to the entire campus.

Product Name:
Environmental Monitoring
Protect your critical assets from physical and environmental threats. DVAI offers a fully integrated, open-platform, vendor-neutral suite of hardware and software applications to manage your data centre and networks IT physical infrastructure throughout its entire lifecycle.
While traditional approaches to physical infrastructure management have focused on individual functions or relied on proprietary tools that manage vendor-specific products, DVAIs Enterprise Management Solution adopts a full-lifecycle approach that integrates the different functions into one open, intelligent tool  no matter whose equipment you use.


Product Name:
Power Conditioning and Back-up
DVAI provides intelligent and efficient network power protection from entry level to scaleable run-time power systems. Ideal for servers, point-of-sale, routers, switches, hubs and other network devices, these systems protect your critical equipment from surges and spikes through double-conversion technology.

Our power solutions have extremely high efficiency at low, medium and high load levels making them ideal for todays multi-core or virtualized servers that have varying load consumption.

Product Name:
Structured Cable Systems
Often cited as the most critical component of your network, the physical layer provides the pathways to connect the various electronics. Our design team includes BICSI Registered Communications Distribution Designers (RCDD's) to ensure the solutions we provide incorporate appropriate technologies and adhere to industry standards, applicable codes and safety practices.

We install and maintain optical, coaxial and UTP cabling systems.

Product Name:
Network Systems
Voice, data, and communications services are the cornerstones of an effective organization. They provide links to clients and suppliers, and any interruption can have dramatic effects. With this in mind, DVA designs, installs and maintains network systems, incorporating redundancy at many levels into the infrastructure.

We consider power conditioning and back-up, grounding, pathways and spaces, and other services that can negatively affect your network. With industry-standard certifications in telecommunications and networking, we can help manage your technology implementation for campuses, new facilities, expansions, or renovations.

Service Name:
Managed Services
Our team of Network Support experts are your IT department, responding to your IT support issues quickly, often before you even know about them. Covering everything from your servers and network infrastructure down to your computers, workstations, and mobile devices, we provide end-to-end solutions for all of your IT needs.

We offer a variety of maintenance contracts and can custom design a maintenance plan to fit your IT infrastructure. We service small, medium and large businesses, making it possible for any company to have a full IT department at their disposal. Our software, monitoring services and remote access allow our network engineers to provide IT services that traditional full time staff would offer, at a fraction of the price.

We offer full off-site and on-site network server administration to all of our business customers. Our certified network administrators and network engineers can support and administer virtually all servers on the market to:

reduce or eliminate downtime
reduce day-to-day issues
proactively monitor your environment
secure your assets and information
know all your IT expenses ahead of time
quickly recover from outages or failures through disaster recovery methods and best practices

Service Name:
Professional Services
Those designing facilities and technology-related systems look to companies like Davidson Violette to plan not only the equipment and connectivity of equipment, but also the services it requires, the finishes of the room(s) and the selection of furniture to maximize the value and longevity of their investment.

Our designers and project managers work with the end-users, architects and engineers to provide superior solutions that address the short and long-term technology goals of the project.

Our services are engaged by those planning retrofits, office relocations or system upgrades.
