Company Profile


Tekron Communication is a Canadian manufacturer with a main focus on providing solutions to TV/FM channel redundancy switching, monitoring & alarm, and remote control of TV & FM channel and Broadband feeds in remote CATV Head-end & Hub sites, as well as Broadcast TV & FM transmission sites.

Tekron product & system soultions are designed to provide:
- Scheduled & Controlled Signal switching.
- Monitoring & automatic signal (redundancy) switching.
- Remote site Monitoring & Control.

(2) Monitoring for Automatic Redundancy Switching
=============================================

RSS-1R
Monitors a primary Broadband RF Trunk Feed and internally
switches to a secondary feed input when the primary feed drops
below its threshold level setting. Connections are provided for
remote switch override control & status monitoring.

RSS-2D
Monitors independently Low & High Bands of a primary RF Trunk
Feed (received over split-band fiber link) and internally
switches to a secondary feed input if either the Low-Band or the
High-Band of the primary feed drops below its threshold level
setting. Connections are provided for remote switch override
control & status monitoring.

RSS-2R
Monitors independently the Primary and the Secondary Broadband RF
Trunk Feeds and internally switches to the secondary feed input
when the primary feed drops below its threshold level setting
while the secondary feed level is above its threshold point.
Connections are provided for remote switch override control &
status monitoring of both feeds.

RSS-2B
Accepts three video/Audio inputs to monitor the (Sync) level of
the Primary and the Secondary Video input feeds independently. It
automatically switches to the secondary input feed when the sync.
level of the primary feed drops below its threshold level
setting, but when both primary & secondary video feeds are not
present, it will automatically switch to the third input.
Optional audio detectors can be added to monitor primary &
secondary audio feeds. Connections are provided for remote switch
override control & status monitoring.

RSS-2AC
Monitors independently a primary and secondary (Left & Right)
Stereo Audio feeds to switch to the secondary feed if either Left
or Right primary feed drops below its threshold level setting. On
loss of both feeds, it switches to a built-in Tone Generator.
Connections are provided for remote switch override control &
status monitoring.

RSS-1CB
Monitors selected RF channel at input for Color-Bar pattern
recognition and internally switches to a back-up (Substitution)
channel when detected. Unit can be supplied

(3) Remote Site Monitoring and Alarm
================================

(A) TV SIGNAL MONITORING
RMS-100
Monitors video & audio carrier levels as well as video modulation
presence of every channel in the Head-end with automatic dial-out
to a central control station when alarm condition is encountered.
Optional Contact inputs/outputs for remote Site monitoring &
control. Complete spectrum channel carrier level measurements,
with logging capability of selected channels to monitor level
variations over time.
...
RMS-2V
Monitors independently video & audio carrier levels as well as
video & audio modulation presence of selected (by Dip-switch)
channel, for alarm or automatic switching to a back-up signal.

(B) SITE EQUIPMENT MONITORING & CONTROL
RMS-816
Monitors (15) contact (or TTL) inputs for alarm and Controls (15)
external devices via relay contact closure. Dial-out to central
station or a pager when alarm condition is encountered. Remote
site control by DTMF tone from regular touch-tone (or Cell)
telephone with pass-code protection, and from PC/Windows control
station. Additional contacts can also be monitored and/or
controlled at each remote site with additional units installed
and connected together to a single modem.

RMS-1A

- Aural carrier level
- Demodulated output: audio presence
- External feed audio presence

RFS-1
Integrated RF sampler & power level detector for Low Power TV and
FM transmitters.

PLC-100

into the RMS monitoring units
