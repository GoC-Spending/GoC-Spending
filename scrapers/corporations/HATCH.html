Company Profile


Hatch is an employee-owned, multidisciplinary professional services firm that delivers a comprehensive array of technical and strategic services, including Consulting, Information Technology, Engineering, Process Development, and Project and Construction Management to the Mining, Metallurgical, Energy, and Infrastructure sectors. Hatch companies have served clients for over 80 years and have project experience in more than 150 countries around the world.
With 11,000 people in over 65 offices, the firm has more than US$35 billion in projects currently under management.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Environmental Services Group
Our Environmental Services Group offers the most comprehensive environmental and community interface solution. Knowledge of our customers' processes and business helps us turn these challenges into strategic opportunities and mitigate project risks.



Service Name:
Coal
The global coal industry is pivotal in meeting the demand of the worlds needs for energy and metals in the coming decades. The combination of replacing existing exhausted mines and increases in demand exceeding 10 percent per annum is generating excellent opportunities for Hatch.
Hatch has over 300 of our colleagues providing study and project services to blue chip companies such as Anglo American Metallurgical Coal, BMA, BHP Billiton, Xstrata Coal, Kangra and Sasol Mining, among others. In Australia, we have particular expertise and passion in coal handling, process design and commissioning and with the recent acquisition of Process Consult this team has been further strengthened. Our coal group in South Africa has been very successful in growing a world class group. Together we are expanding our capabilities in Southern Africa, Australasia, and the Americas.


Service Name:
Industrial Minerals
Our mining and metallurgy clients are among the most successful businesses in the world.
We undertake major expansions and metallurgical plant upgrades, from concept to design to construction anywhere in the world. We rebuild furnaces, introduce new process controls, and develop unique technologies that will give our clients substantial edge in cost control and productivity.


Service Name:
Infrastructure
Hatch's Infrastructure businesses serve a broad range of industrial, public, entrepreneurial and financial clients. Hatch is known globally as a leader at bringing together advisory, technical, engineering and project delivery services in the resources sectors. Our infrastructure businesses reflect our unique knowledge of these sectors. This knowledge and our global connections are what differentiate us.
Areas of expertise include:
Rail and Transportation Systems
Ports and Marine Terminals
Water (desalination, treatment, planning, process separation and membranes)
Infrastructure services (such as program management, tailings and recovery, capital program infrastructure services and camps).


Service Name:
Iron & Steel
The Iron & Steel group comprises professionals world-wide, with corporate responsibility for steel industry related projects.
We are process engineers, automation & process control engineers, metallurgists, environmental engineers, plant simulation & modelling experts.



Service Name:
Energy
Hatchs Energy business unit provides a broad range of consulting, design, technologies and EPCM services for renewable power and other energy projects.
Hatch provides engineering for hydro, wind, tidal, solar and other renewable power generation facilities; thermal and nuclear plants; electricity transmission and distribution facilities; oil and gas developments.



Service Name:
Hatch Management
Hatch Management Consulting, the management and technology consulting business unit of the Hatch Group, supports senior management in strategy development, market reviews, acquisitions, investment and performance improvement projects, and also provides a full range of due diligence services to the financial services sector around the world.
We combine consulting process with technical depth. Our clients comment on our straightforward approach and success in working together with people at all levels of their organizations.



Service Name:
Light Metals
Through offices in major cities worldwide Hatch Light Metals delivers projects and expertise in aluminium, magnesium, and titanium to clients on six continents.
Aluminium expertise covers the full range of bauxite, alumina refining, aluminium smelting and downstream finishing.
The Light Metals sector is a core business for Hatch, with more than 4,000 full-time employees available to deliver any-sized projects. Hatch has been synonymous with aluminium for more than 40 years. Today, we work with the world's leading aluminium producers on some of their most significant projects.



Service Name:
Mining & Metals
For 50 years Hatch has been serving the world's mining industry with feasibility studies; design for greenfield and brownfield facilities; enabling technologies; global procurements; project and construction management; commissioning; maintenance management ; and on-site operational services.
We are organized to understand in expert-depth the processing of those metals and minerals that are the principal assets of our continuing clients.



Service Name:
Iron Ore
In the iron ore sector, Hatch works extensively developing major projects with international minerals and metals producers such as Vale, ArcelorMittal, Kumba Iron Ore, BHP Billiton, Rio Tinto, Xstrata etc. Hatch assists iron ore producers with almost all aspects of iron ore exploration, mine development, beneficiation, materials handling, storage, logistics (heavy haul rail, road, and transportation systems), and port facilities including all supporting infrastructure

Service Name:
Mining & Mineral Processing
Hatchs Mining and Mineral Processing Group specializes in the areas of mine engineering, data management, mine technical computing and mineral processing. The Group interacts closely with other Hatch groups to provide a complete range of services to the mining industry, allowing integrated application of all aspects of resource project development through geological modeling to mine optimization and design, process optimization, mill design and commissioning assistance, financial evaluation and operational support.


Service Name:
Non Ferrous
The non-ferrous business unit serves clients worldwide in base metals (copper, nickel, cobalt, lead, zinc, tin), and ferroalloys.
We develop unique metallurgical plantdesign and technologies to help our clients maintain their leadership and productivity.


Service Name:
Nuclear
The Nuclear Business Unit operates in North America and around the world, focusing on developing the companys business in the areas of nuclear power. Experience includes nuclear plant prudence audits for facilities in the USA. The business unit has provided services for the conceptual design of radioactive waste repositories.




Service Name:
Oil & Gas
The Oil & Gas business unit serves clients worldwide for onshore and offshore hydrocarbon production and refining. We develop innovative solutions and designs to help our clients maintain their competitive advantage in the industry.
Our expertise covers:
Conventional oil
Oilsands and heavy oil
Gas and LNG
Unconventional oil
Pipelines, storage and transportation
Business Systems


Service Name:
Operational Services
Hatchs Operational Services brings together a number of expert services that concentrate on effective project ramp up, upgrading ongoing plant operations and asset management support in all stages of asset life cycle.
The objective is to create value by offering smarter and more efficient solutions to operating issues.
Hatchs insights and knowledge help improve operational effectiveness and reduce the need for capital investments. Project experience has shown that the combination of our cutting-edge technologies and operating know-how can significantly improve a clients competitive position.
