Company Profile


Wholesale and retail refinishers and repairers of aluminum alloy wheel rims....The Best in the Country. We have 36 years experience with original equipment wheels, wheel centres & wheel covers for all foreign and domestic cars & trucks. Our prices are well below new car Dealer prices. We can also supply chrome rims and have the best connection for re-chroming. We have a wheel exchange program and we pay the freight.
Our reputation for satisfaction guaranteed is well-known from coast to coast. Locations in Windsor and local telephone connections in Toronto and Winnipeg, MB. Please see our website www.wheelcoversunlimited.com

Unique applications:

When a wheel needs replacement because of damage, we can repair and refinish that wheel to look like new, even if that wheel has been discontinued. If you have aftermarket rims we have contacts with dozens of manufacturers to replace your wheel(s) if one or more of them are totally beyond repair.

Key / Major clients:

Chrysler Canada dealerships ,General Motors dealerships and
Ford Canada Dealerships across Canada.
Auto Body Shops from coast to coast and New Car Dealers for Toyota, Mazda, Nissan, Honda, Acura, Infiniti, BMW, Jaguar, Mercedes Benz, Kia..and the list goes on....

Success stories:

New Car Dealerships order reconditioned wheel rims to satisfy warranty claims and replacement or repair and refinishing of collision-damaged wheels and/or wheel covers. Bodyshops buy our products to repair collision-damaged wheels. We also sell aftermarket custom wheels to the general public at very affordable prices. We also satisfy insurance claims for lost or damaged wheels, wheel covers. and wheel centres. T-Top replacement with original equipment is also our speciality.

Testimonial:

Your local New Car Dealership Service department and Body Shop or accredited Independent Body Shop.
