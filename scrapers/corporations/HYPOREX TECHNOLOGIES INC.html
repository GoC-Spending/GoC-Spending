Company Profile


Hyporex is a duly registered Company under the Federal jurisdiction of Canada's Business Corporations Act (Corporation #852968-0). Hyporex is a small self-funded group of intellectual and professional individuals, who hold a combined industrial experience of over 100 years in the fields of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, machining, sand casting, die casting, injection molding, sales & marketing, mass production and/or manufacturing. Hyporex and its associates have successfully researched, developed and designed various, unique, industrial grade, commercial products, applications are included but not limited to: Fuel saving, emission reduction, alternative & renewable green energy, power generation, heating and burning devices.

Our Vision:
Hyporex believes in the alternative green energy to resolve several global issues confronted by mankind on a daily basis such as poverty, health, global warming, emission, fossil fuel reduction, higher oil cost and environmental concerns of todays world.

Our Mission:
To offer 100% environment-friendly, affordable energy devices to worldwide end-consumers in need of heating, cooling, cooking, drinking water and power generation systems.

Achievements:
Hyporex has become a successful manufacturer in Pakistan with a track record of export of its products to over 50 countries around the world including USA, Canada, Mexico, Brazil, UK, France, Switzerland, Netherlands, Germany, Thailand, Singapore, Saudi Arabia, India, Australia, New Zealand, UAE, Spain and Italy.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Stirling Engine Power Generator
A Stirling engine is a heat engine operating by cyclic compression and expansion of air or other gas, the working fluid, at different temperature levels such that there is a net conversion of heat energy to mechanical work. Like the steam engine, the Stirling engine is traditionally classified as an external combustion engine, as all heat transfers to and from the working fluid take place through the engine wall.

Hyporex stirling power generator has an output of up to 100 Watts, it can recharge any 12 volt automobile battery, ideally 100 amp hour through a charge controller of 10 ampere. Once battery has been recharged, consumers may connect it to a 500 watts inverter for household voltage.

Alternatively, hyporex 500 watts power bank can be used which contains a built-in battery, built-in charge controller and a built-in inverter. The power bank can be fully recharged with the stirling engine power generator within 3 hours. A stirling engine generates 4X more power than a typical solar panel since it generates power 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and needs only 25% energy storage when compared to Solar which saves up to 75% annual cost of battery bank replacement. The generator utilizes a flame equivalent to only 5 candle lights from any heat source and operates noiselessly. The generator uses less than 2 square feet and generates up to 2.4 Kilowatt hour every 24 hours. In developed countries, the generator can offer back-up power, in case of emergency, black-outs or natural disaster, while it can serve as a prime source of electricity in the under-developed or poor countries.

Research & Development:
Our R&D team is working to increase the overall generation capacity of the system by adding on more engines or micro-generators. Presently, each module supplies up to 100 watts output with our built-in 12 volt DC micro-generators and utilizes a flame equivalent to 5 candle lights from any heat source. The generator is intended to operate noiselessly or less than 40 dba sound level and use a space less than 4’ X 4’. We are committed and target to generate up to One Kilowatt per module, the genset would gradually be capable of supplying power for the whole household eventually. Multiple modules are to be combined on a single structure to minimize the overall physical size of the system. Our kilowatt system is intended to recharge bigger battery banks through a charge controller and a pure sine wave inverter which would convert DC voltage into AC voltage to supply grid quality and standard power to the main control panel or circuit breakers panel or distribution box as a grid substitute. The following features may be added to the system:

Single Push Electric Starter
Piezoelectric igniter
Gas pipe fittings and Jets
Aluminum Enclosure
DC Output Terminals
Digital Watt meter Display
Internal Charge Controller
