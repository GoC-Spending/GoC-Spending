Company Profile


Ownership Structure: Limited Company (closely held corporation)

Established: 1992 Burnaby, BC

Employees: 15-25

G3 Consulting Ltd. is a privately owned British Columbia company
with corporate offices located in Surrey, BC. G3 offers unique and
innovative environmental research and spatial data system
services to a wide variety of clients, combining quality science
and leading edge technology with proven expertise and
experience. G3 directs a team of scientific and technical
professionals on numerous projects from statistical design and
project management, through applied field and laboratory work,
data quality control and analysis,and comprehensive data
interpretation, mapping and reporting.

Primary Service Offering: G3 Consulting Ltd. is a service-first
based company providing innovative solutions in the fields of
environmental risk and aquatic effects evaluations, comprehensive
ecosystem assessments, forestry, mining, fish and fisheries
resource evaluations, watershed impacts and restoration
strategies, GIS, inventories mapping and environmental impacts
assessments, foreshore marine surveys, diver and ROV studies, oil and gas impact assessments, biophysical surveys, sediment and water studies, benthic/periphyton taxonomy and fisheries analysis.
Utilizing state-of-the-art assessment, data collection, mapping
and reporting technologies and highly experienced personnel, G3
ensures solutions are of high quality and usability, tailored to
the specific needs of its clients. G3 offers cost-effective
services world-wide, while ensuring a commitment to domestic
markets. Other services range from personnel training and
aquatic evaluations to environmental policy, data management and
systems design.

Key Technologies/Products/Services: Using proprietary
information systems and technologies, G3 Consulting Ltd. offers
a unique series of environmental evaluation and sampling
procedures, inventories, GIS, GPS and other mapping applications
and customized data management systems. These applications,
systems and technologies are designed to be flexible, handling a
wide variety of media and application needs, tailored to the
clients requirements and extensively tested for practicality and
reliability under rigorous test conditions.

Marketing Strategy: G3 Consulting Ltd. is committed to
scientifically defensible studies, designed and executed in a
cost-effective, efficient manner, ensuring that clients are well
represented and receive a product commensurate with intended
use. To this end, G3 maintains and develops relationships with
firms committed to these ideals and which offer complementary
services (engineering, laboratories, hydrology, etc.). G3 is
currently developing environmental, management system and
information exchange opportunities and expertise in the US and
South American regions. Inquires from like-minded firms wishing
to discuss strategic alliances or collaborations on joint-
venture opportunities are welcomed.

Key Clients: G3 Consulting Ltd. has developed a client base
which includes: federal, provincial and municipal governments
(Department of Fisheries and Oceans, industrial sector clients
such as Abitibi Consolidated, Fletcher Challenge, MacMillan
Bloedel Ltd., Harmac Pacific, Weldwood Canada, BC Rail, CN Rail,
CP Rail, Finlay Forest Industries, Riverside Forest Products,
Interfor, Vancouver International Airport, First Nations,
Stewardship groups, as well as additional national and
international companies and consulting firms.

Service Name:
Environmental Effect Monitoring Program
G3 Consulting - environmental effects, risk assessment, or trend
monitoring programs.

Environmental monitoring programs are designed and implemented
to define effects of industrial/municipal operations on the
receiving environment (e.g., pulpmill effluent discharge). These
programs encompass a broad range of disciplines from engineering
and statistics to fisheries and limnology. Effects monitoring
programs generally span a number of years and require the
management of numerous logistical details and personnel. G3 has
developed data management and handling criteria with rigorous
Quality Assurance/Quality Control (QA/QC) measures ensuring a
high level of data confidence.

Trend monitoring programs encompass the comprehensive monitoring
of industrial receiving environment constituents, both
temporally and spatially. Many of these programs are conducted
on behalf of the pulp and paper, forestry and mining industries,
and are designed to assess changes in environmental matrices as
a result of industrial process changes. G3 is focused on
appropriate study design integrity and program sensitivity which
are paramount in that program results are often disseminated to
public interest groups and the media. These programs often
require the analysis of a wide variety of environmental
components including sediments, receiving water, effluents, and
biological samples (vegetation, invertebrates, fish, benthos,
periphyton, and crab tissue, etc.). Ultratrace dissection,
handling and analyses of many of the samples collected
necessitated the development of exacting protocols for field,
laboratory, and data handling. G3 conducts ongoing marine and
terrestrial evaluations on behalf of government and industry.

G3 personnel are specialists in studies of this scope and work
with government and industry to develop scientifically-sound
programs which comply with or supersede environmental
regulations and policies. Areas of specialty include pulp and
paper, forestry, mining, petroleum and fisheries-based programs.

Service Name:
Systems Analyses & Database Designs
G3 Consulting Ltd. directs a team of analysts and programmers
that develop custom software solutions for a variety of
industries. Environmental monitoring programs in numerous marine
and freshwater environments required the development of new and
sophisticated data management methodologies.

These programs required the accuracy and sensitivity to data
integrity of results from programs that required the analysis of
samples to ultratrace levels (parts per quadrillion).

As a supplement to G3 reporting systems, field and laboratory
data sheets are tailored to ensure that the appropriate
information is obtained and in the necessary format for entry
into a specific database. G3 provides custom database analysis
and programming services to the federal government and a variety
of industries.
