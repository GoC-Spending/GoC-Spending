Company Profile


Christie® is a global visual and audio technologies company. Consistently setting the standards by being the first to market some of the worlds most advanced projectors, display systems and immersive audio solutions, Christie is recognized as one of the most innovative technologies company in the world.

Utilizing DLP®, LCD and LED technologies, Christies broad range of visual solutions include: media walls, digital cinema projectors, networking solutions, cutting edge 3D virtual reality and simulation projection systems and Christie MicroTiles®, the worlds first fully modular digital display tiles. Christies technology expertise also includes immersive audio solutions, laser projection, High Frame Rate (HFR) visual solutions and medical imaging devices.

Christies Managed Services group helps to design, deploy and support enterprise-wide networks around the world.

At Christie, we help our customers create the worlds best shared experiences.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Simulation and Virtual Training Display Solutions
Christie display technology is used in high-performance simulation environments all over the world. Our turnkey solutions are used in various applications including high-resolution power walls, curved screen displays, domes and faceted displays. Comprising leading-edge display technology and world class system integration expertise, Christie simulation solutions can produce images in any size or shape, front or rear projected, spherical, cylindrical, on conic or flat screens, from small-team scale screens to large scale dome configurations.

Our display solutions include:

 Christie Matrix StIM WQ DLP LED-based scalable environment projection technology. This system has the unique capabilities of achieving eye-limiting resolution while stimulating Night Vision Goggles and true 120Hz operation for maximum dynamic image quality.

 Christie Matrix WU7K-J. A 3-chip, WUXGA, DLP, 6,300 lumen projection system that produces real-world simulation results with full scalability, built-in geometric warping and blending and high-performance 120Hz capability.

Christies ISO certified project management practices see you through the design, installation, integration and complete training and support of your project requirements. Christies experienced team will integrate and install the system and fully train your employees and provide you with ongoing support.

Product Name:
Digital Signage Display Solutions
Christie offers a full range of digital signage display solutions for corporate, retail, hospitality, education, and control room settings. Products include: tiled display walls that leverage LCD, LED, and/or rear-projection technologies, large-format LCD flat panels, interactive touchscreen displays, and a variety of image processing solutions. Highlights include two products that can be configured into virtually any size and shape and are designed for 24/7/365 use:

 Christie MicroTiles® rear-projection displays are ideal for near seamless displays with high pixel density
 Christie Velvet LED displays that create a seamless display wall

Christies content management and image processing are combined with the products to complete the display solution.


Product Name:
Control Room Solutions: Video Walls and Controllers
Christie® is a trusted and reliable partner delivering complete end-to-end integrated solutions for highly collaborative monitoring environments. Christie offers a variety of video wall solutions to suit the needs of any control room environment. Christies controllers and video processors provide a range of solutions to best match any video wall application. Our systems allow the flexibility of displaying content from various source types at extremely high resolution across multiple displays.



Product Name:
3D Stereoscopic Visualization and Virtual Reality Display Solutions
Christie 3D visualization and virtual reality solutions make it easy for customers to create a simulation out of any surface and environment imaginable.

Choose your virtual reality:
 Immersion (scale)

Produce life-like visualizations on a variety of surfaces:

Our integrated immersive displays are engineered for collaborative visualization that requires high-resolution, stable, reliable and powerful 2D and 3D capabilities. Christie has a broad platform of high-resolution, professional performance display technologies: DLP®, 4K, 3-chip DLP, 1-chip LED, and Quad HD panels.

Some of our integrated solutions include:

 Christie CAVE
 Christie HoloStage Mini
 Christie HoloStation
 Christie Powerwall

Product Name:
Processing and Collaboration
Video processor: Christie offers the most powerful, most flexible, and most creative video and widescreen display multi-windowing processors available today. Every model is easy to deploy and install, and can reduce the amount of wires, boxes and rack space traditionally required. Christie offers two versatile and easy-to-use options: Christie Vista Spyder and Christie Spyder X20. Both models are hardware-based video processors that enable users to blend, window and scale a very wide array of video signals with unparalleled quality and ease. Easy to deploy and install because the advanced architecture, they use less racking space than most of the competition.

Universal routing switcher: The Christie Vista URS is an all-in-one matrix switcher that accepts multiple sources in any format (analog composite to digital 4K) and seamlessly routes any of these sources to any of 8 user-configurable outputs, each of which can be independently configured for any analog or digital output format. The URS performs all the processing to match the source to the output, including scaling, aspect ratio, color-space, and frame rate conversion repeats. Normally, these functions require additional equipment and complex, restrictive control systems; however with the URS the entire process is simplified, saving you time, money and resources.

Christie Brio: Christie® Brio presentation and collaboration solutions remove technical barriers and enable team members to drive for results together. Designed to address common meeting technology challenges, Christie Brio solutions enable teams to wirelessly present, share and interact with each other  within the same room or across multiple locations, in real-time. When everyone is focused on the content rather than the technology, more energy can be put into clearly and confidently exchanging information and ideas. Decision-making becomes less time-consuming so you can get more value out of the time you spend meeting together.


Product Name:
Projectors: 3-chip DLP, 1-chip DLP, LCD
Christie 3-chip DLP® projectors feature high brightness and high frame rates for ultra-realistic images. Several models are upgradeable to 3D. They are ideal for amusement parks, auditoriums, education, classrooms, museums, planetariums, and more.

Christie 1-chip DLP® projectors are ideal for small auditoriums, boardrooms, classrooms and houses of worship. Delivering image blending and warping, stacking capabilities and precision color matching they are available in multiple resolutions and brightness levels.

Christie LCD projectors offers a broad range of brightness and resolutions, making them ideal for boardrooms, houses of worship, government, higher education and more, with a low cost of ownership.
