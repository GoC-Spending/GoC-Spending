Company Profile


Dave Chaudry founded his company in 1976. He is a leader who stimulates his employees by his exemplary personal life and the quality of his professional activities. He creates and maintains relationships with his partners that are based on honesty, discipline and transparency.
He is very proud of his contribution to the metallurgical industry, the jobs he creates, the employees who are loyal to him and the plant he makes available to engineers who need an OEM for their clients.
This Canadian industrialist is recognized around world, based on his companys international scope and its array of prestigious and faithful customers.

Background
DEC Industries Ltd. www.decindustries.com fabricates custom-built machinery and production lines for a variety of industries including mining, steel works, aluminum smelters, pulp and paper mills and hydroelectric dams.

The company was founded by Dave Chaudry in 1975 in Ville St-Laurent with just 3,000 square feet. It went on to hire about thirty employees, and in 1986 purchased its current facility and occupied 16,000 square feet. In 1996, the plant expanded to fill the entire 60,000 square feet of space.

Expertise
The company specializes in designing, fabricating and repairing machinery, equipment, precision tools and different kinds of industrial jigs. With its state-of-the-art technology and precision machinery it can weld a variety of steel and aluminum alloys of different sizes. It also offers a high level of customized service ranging from installation to the replacement of machinery and parts.
It is the exclusive manufacturer of the Gearflex coupling system. This system transmits the full power from one shaft to another and protects the ball bearings and rotary components in connected units. Its flexibility reduces the inevitable damage caused by ball bearing wear.

Total plant size:

Plant and machinery
The 60,000-square foot plant is divided into two production and assembly units with four doors.
The production unit is certified CSA W59 and ASME Sec IX, and all the welders and machinists are CWB- and AWS-certified. The assembly unit has all the equipment needed to handle complex hydroelectric projects and conduct final tests. An inventory of raw materials is stored close by to support operations.
The company has all the equipment required to handle highly complex projects:

Distribution of employees:

Employees
The company currently has 16 employees. The president is supported by a plant manager, quality controller and administrative assistant. The production team comprises 12 employees, including machinists, welders, fitters, shippers and delivery persons.
All of the managers and most of the technicians graduated from recognized engineering schools and have more than 100 years of experience between them. All the employees are dedicated and have been with the company for more than 15 years on average. The plant is not unionized and has never had a work stoppage.

Key / Major clients:

The companys main clients are large engineering firms such as Dominion Engineering, United Engineering and many more. Over the years, the company has served leading multi¬nationals such as GE Canada, Sidbec-Dosco, Italimpianti of America, SMS group, Dofasco, NKK Steel, Alcan, Stelco, US Steel, Steel Dynamic, Essar, Tata Steel, etc.

Dec Industries Ltd. has worked on a large number of export projects and has expertise in international activities. It has overseas clients in Italy, Germany, Brazil, India, Mexico, Indonesia, and many other countries.
