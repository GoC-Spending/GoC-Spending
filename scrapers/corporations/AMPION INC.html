Company Profile


Ampion Inc. possesses the required technical knowledge and expertise, facilities and the state of the art diagnostic gadgets/equipment to test and repair complex electronic circuit board assemblies found in aerospace, military, communications, medical, mainframe and industrial industries. Ampion is a contract manufacturer and our technical staff is fully trained to diagnose and repair electronic products such as printed circuit boards (PCB),drives, power supply modules, controllers and displays.
Ampion is well positioned to be a formidable global supply chain partner being a defense contractor with a Canadian CAGE code L0CZ4. Our company handles all orders regardless of availability of National Stock Numbers (NSN). Our well trained and proficient technical staff is ideally suited for military, aerospace, naval and industrial equipment manufacturing, design and repair.

Technology profile

Since its inception about 5 years ago, Ampion has been involved in research, development ( R&D), repair, rework, test, inspection and manufacturing of electronic circuit boards and assemblies used in various lines of products in the fields of Defense, Aerospace, Medical, Industrial, Telecommunication and Robotics.
Ampion is a prime defense contractor operating under the Canadian CAGE code L0CZ4 serving the Defense and Aerospace Industries and has specialization in reverse engineering, design and manufacturing of complex RF military equipment, digital, analog and various obsolescent electronics circuit boards and assemblies.

We invite you to visit us at our Electronic Circuits Repair and Technology Center in Mississauga Ontario or for additional information about Ampion and its services, please visit the Canadian Government website.
