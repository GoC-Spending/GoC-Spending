Company Profile


Will Davidson LLP has been operating as a boutique litigation firm specializing in personal injury and insurance litigation since 1924. In our more than 90 years in business, we have tried thousands of cases including hundreds argued before the Ontario Court of Appeal and the Supreme Court of Canada.At Will Davidson LLP, our plaintiff lawyers specialize
in car accidents, slip and falls, CPP dismissal and denial, property loss, birth injuries, spinal cord injuries and brain injuries resulting from the negligence of others.
The Will Davidson LLP’s insurance defence team specializes in subrogation, coverage opinions, appeal and co-counsel work, and fire and property loss defence to name a few.
We are conveniently located in Toronto, Oakville, Huntsville,Midland, Lindsay, Bowmanville, Orillia, Whitby, Markham and Burlington, to assist clients with their legal needs. We offer a free consultation and work on a contingency basis, so we do not get paid unless we win your case. Our plantiff lawyers hold people responsible for their negligence in injuring others. We encourage our clients to focus on their recovery while we focus on obtaining the best result for their case.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Car Accidents
If you or a loved one is injured in a car accident, life as you know may often be turned upside down. Our experienced personal injury lawyers understand that car accident victims may be overwhelmed suffering from physical, financial and emotional burdens. The injuries suffered may prevent the injured victim or family member from working and participating in regular activities. We also know during this time it might be quite difficult dealing with third party institutions that require bills to be paid.
Our car accident lawyers serve residents in Toronto, Oakville, Burlington, Orillia, Huntsville, Midland, Lindsay, Bowmanville, Whitby and Markham. Our legal team is available to assist you through this process and will help you with receiving the settlement you deserve for your claim.

Service Name:
Slip and Fall
If you or a loved one experienced an unexpected fall it can be quite troubling especially when it occurs in a location that is supposedly deemed to be safe and well-maintained The personal injury lawyers of Will Davidson LLP have years of experience handling accidental falls. Our lawyers have access to some of the best experts in the industry who have a vast knowledge of the engineering and maintenance of public areas, including the way local sidewalks are designed and maintained. These resources, combined with our expertise assigning financial value for loss, are excellent tools for securing fair compensation for your injuries. If you are seeking an experienced lawyer to assist you with the compensation you deserve after a slip and fall accident, contact the personal injury lawyers at Will Davidson LLP.

Service Name:
CPP Denials
If you or a loved one have recently received a denial on your application for your Canada Pension Plan (CPP), you may be at a lost on what to do. If you are considering appealing the decision but do not know the steps to take, the lawyers at Will Davidson LLP can help you and provide you with the guidance needed for an appeal. The window for filing specific paperwork is quite slim and by acting immediately you can ensure your appeal is reconsidered. Contact our lawyers today to discuss your options and have a trusted lawyer advise you on the steps needed to obtain a decision best for you.

Service Name:
Medical Malpractice
If you or a loved one experienced a serious injury due to the negligence of a health care professional you may be entitled to compensation. The legal system has specific laws in place to protect individuals from medical negligence. Healthcare professionals are trained to act in such a way as not to cause unreasonable risk of harm to others. The standard of care required of a doctor is that of a reasonable doctor considering the circumstances they are dealing with. Examples of medical malpractice can be surgical error, birth injuries, emergency room errors which resulted in serious illness or death.

A personal injury lawyer at Will Davidson LLP has the experience investigating your possible losses as a result of a medical mal practice claim. Contact us today to assist you in holding the parties responsible for your lack of proper healthcare.

Service Name:
Product Liability
Manufacturers of pharmaceuticals and consumer medical products have a duty to ensure their products’ safety for public use. When the marketplace receives unsafe goods for distribution, or defective products become part of a treatment plan, there is no positive result for consumers. Our personal injury lawyers have litigated on behalf of clients harmed by defective products for decades. If you or a loved one has been injured by a defective product contact our personal injury lawyers who have the experience to assist you receive the compensation you deserve.

If you or a loved one has suffered a brain injury you may not be aware that these injuries are often referred to as “silent injuries” because there are unseen and are often undiagnosed. In your community thousands of people sustain head and brain injuries yearly, the worst of these injuries often lead to permanent brain damage or death. If you or a member of your family suffered a brain injury as a result of a car accident, slip and fall or sporting accident, you should consider speaking to a personal injury lawyer at Will Davidson LLP.
