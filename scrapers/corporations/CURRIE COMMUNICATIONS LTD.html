Company Profile


The principal of Currie Communications Ltd. has over
15 years' experience in event management, communications,
marketing consulting, and public relations. The company offers
effective, cost-efficient, and pragmatic services to help
businesses, not-for-profit organizations, government departments
and other agencies maintain a professional public profile.

Currie Communications specializes in all aspects of
corporate, business, and organizational communications; and fund
raising with attention to Corporate Social Responsibilty. These
services include:

Business communications - We help you ensure you get a clear
and appropriate message to your business audiences consistently
and efficiently by providing:
- internal communication strategies
- external communications strategies
- communication planning

Public Relations - You need to manage how you are perceived by
the public to ensure the message is accurate and understandable.
We assist with:
- development and implementation of organizational visual
identity programs
- corporate or organizational positioning
- planned giving strategies
- public communication strategies
- advertising strategies and implementation
- media relations

Event Management - Mounting a successful event demands attention
to detail. Making it "look easy" requires a meticulous
sense of the minute, and a keen ability to organize and
co-ordinate. As experienced event managers, we can help with
all aspects of event planning and implementation:
- event timelining
- budget planning and control
- committee structuring and management
- development of collateral materials
- event marketing and public relations
- media relations
- facilities and site preparation
- co-ordination of subcontractors (masters of ceremonies, sound
lighting, catering, director/producers, entertainment)
- post-event debriefing

Publications - Most communication and marketing activities,
whether they are events or communicating with clients, require
the development of collateral materials and publications. We
have a strong writing and publishing background and experience
producing materials such as:
- newsletters
- brochures
- directories
- catalogues
- programs
- proposals
- speaking notes

Here are some of the projects we've undertaken and clients we've
served:

- Alberta Heritage Foundation for Medical Research
- Alberta Economic Development and Tourism
- Alberta Education (Excellence in Teaching Awards)
- Alberta Foundation for Environmental Excellence (Emerald
Awards)
- Alberta Women's Enterprise Initiative Association
- Alberta Restaurant and Food Services Association
- CIPS - Canadian Information Processing Society
- The Conference Board of Canada
- Edmonton Art Gallery
- Industry Canada
- Fraser Milner (Pinnacle Awards)
- TELUS Corporation
- Western Economic Diversification
- Xerox: The Document Company
- Thinking for a change society
- Canadian Financial Planners Association
- Kids' Kottage
- TransAlta
