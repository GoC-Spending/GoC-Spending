Company Profile


RETAIL DESIGN + MANUFACTURING

Peregrine, operating for over 38 years, is the largest manufacturer of custom high-end retail environments in Western Canada. Serving clients primarily in the retail and banking industries, Peregrine has worked with leading brands, including Lululemon, Kit and Ace, Aritzia, BMO, Vancity, and MEC.

We are a team of creative and skilled fabricators collaborating to design, develop and manufacture highly effective retail environments. From concept to prototype to production, we work closely with our clients to bring their most ambitious ideas to life in their retail spaces.

We are adept with hardwoods, laminates, solid surfaces, plastics, glass, lighting, metals and print. Our expert teams specialize in creating unique fixtures, merchandising systems and signage in our 30,000 sq/ft facility in the heart of Metro Vancouver.

Product Name:
003: HEARTLAND Signature Furniture Line
The Heartland Collection celebrates the complexities of nature by juxstaposing the non-linear blue stain ribbon that permeates each unique piece of wood with the clean lines and transparency of synthetic-based Lucite.

The Heartland Collection is a tribute to the 500 million cubic metres of Lodgepole Pine that have been killed by the Mountain Pine Beetle (MPB) in the Interior of British Columbia.

Each piece of this line carries clean, rectangular lines and edges. Each remains faithful to the urban design appeal yet showcases the natural warmth and softness of the wood hovering in the face of gravity.

MODERN SUSTAINABLE DESIGN

From tables, chairs and benches, to corporate furniture, to shelves and coffee tables this suite of furniture is uniquely "BC".

In keeping with Peregrines enduring philosophy, each piece of our signature furniture line is crafted from the highest quality materials - selected, fabricated and hand-crafted right here in British Columbia, Canada.

The Mountain Pine Beetle naturally "blue-stained" pine beams we incoporate into our Heartland furniture is considered a natural artisan product - each one completely and truly unique. Checking, knots, and minor cupping are considered part of each piece's natural, individualistic and inconsistent aesthetic appeal.

Service Name:
001: Ideas | Design | Build
IDEAS

Our in-house creative team combines creativity with technical insight to offer design solutions that are buildable, without compromising visual appeal.

We start the conceptual process by getting a thorough understanding of your needs. The ideation phase is all about bridging the gaps between those needs and a powerful idea, worthy of your brand.

Using concept sketches and computer renderings, our team of designers and artists apply their insight and expertise in product and industrial design to your project and work with you - honing concepts, function, and aesthetics - until your vision is realized.


DESIGN

We are often asked to design-assist some of the most challenging projects. By pairing comprehensive design services with state-of-the-art fabrication capabilities, we coordinate every step of the process for our clients with creativity and efficacy.

Our designers and project managers will take your boldest ideas and transforms them into buildable designs, adding value and reducing potential costs along the way. Experts in working with a wide variety of materials and maximizing efficiency without compromising quality, our team will find the shortest path to an end product that you will be proud to associate with your brand.


BUILD

You’ve finalized your design and shop drawings: now the magic happens. In the hands of our skilled fabricators, your best ideas and designs will become the kind of tangible reality that turns heads and functions with perfect efficiency.

Since 1977, Peregrine has manufactured gallery quality products for some of the most distinguished brands, retail, and corporate spaces in North America. In keeping with our tradition for quality craftsmanship, our team of in-house fabricators will handcraft your product with the highest degree of efficiency and care, using the finest materials and cutting edge technology.

We aren’t done until your custom fixtures, millwork, displays, and architectural features are properly installed, and your store is fully rolled out. From idea to installation, Peregrine exists to ensure that your brand achieves its full power and potential.

Service Name:
002: Services
We are a team of creative designers and skilled fabricators collaborating to design and manufacture highly effective retail environments. From concept to prototype to build, we work closely with our clients to bring their most ambitious ideas to life in their retail spaces.

Adept with acrylics, plastic resins, glass, hardwoods, laminates, solid surfaces and metals, our teams specialize in creating unique fixtures, displays, environmental signage, millwork, furniture and other architectural features in our 30,000 sq/ft facility in the heart of Metro Vancouver.

Operating from a modern 10,000 square foot facility, our shop floor is equipped with the latest equipment to provide a full range of services in single run items or for large quantity runs. Our experienced fabricators and control processes ensure all projects are produced to the highest quality and on-time.

Service Name:
003: Product Development
Peregrines full-spectrum experience in design and manufacturing has allowed us to form strategic partnerships that have led to innovative product developments and new companies. We offer an inspiring, entrepreneurial environment and the opportunity to collaborate with a diverse team of professionals. Our designers and fabricators specialize in discovering practical solutions for our partners and clients. We invest with people first, and ideas, second.

Service Name:
004: Materials
We work closely with North American designers, architects, retailers, OEM producers, millworkers and specialty contractors to design and fabricate displays, furniture and architectural features and ensure the best materials suited for the project application are selected.
Peregrine offers a complete range of materials with extensive experience working with plastics, to help our clients maintain their competitive edge, budget, logistics, brand, design and application requirements:

PLASTICS
ABS, Acrylic (ie. Plexiglas®, Lucite®,) , Expanded PVC, Foam Board, PETG, Polycarbonate (ie. Lexan), Polystyrene, and specialty resins from 3form®, Lumicor®, Parapan® and Corian®. Specialized plastics with properties such as UV resistance, weather resistance, scratch/abrasion resistance, impact, blast & bullet resistance, anti-reflection and high optical clarity are also available.
For a more detailed listing of our plastics, brands and their suited applications please refer to our Plastics Materials Guide which has been compiled as an information source for the different types of plastics and resins we can fabricate with.

METALS, WOOD, GLASS & LOW-WATTAGE LIGHTING
For further help with selecting the material most suitable to your need please contact us or call (604) 251-3174 to talk with a Peregrine sales associate.
