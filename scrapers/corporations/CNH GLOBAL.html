Company Profile


Flexi-Coil , a divison of CNH global,located in
Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada, has developed a wide range of
farming equipment to meet the changing needs of farmers world
wide. The company manufactures farm implements specializing in
tillage, seeding, planting and chemical application equipment
providing a comprehensive system for various types of grain
growing. The Flexi-Coil system offers the maximum in moisture
conservation, reliability, seeding accuracy, high productivity
and ease of use with minimum manpower requirement.

After many years of development, Flexi-Coil has achieved an
international reputation for product quality and superior
design.
In particular, the Flexi-Coil air seeding systems stand alone in
terms of innovation, efficiency and effectiveness for dry land
grain farming. The world wide trend towards soil conservation
tillage practices has created several new markets for air
seeding
technology. With a focused marketing effort, the company stands
to solidify its position as the leader in seeding technology and
as the leading supplier of seeders for Canadian and other
markets. Flexi-Coil has also made significant gains in
capturing
a large portion of the dry land market of the United States and
Australia, and is actively developing a line of equipment suited
for the row crop farming industry.

Flexi-Coil is Canada's largest farm implement manufacturer and
has become and important employer in the Saskatoon area. The
580,200 square foot (54,068 square meter) plant is operating at
capacity. The company has a competent and highly motivated work
force. As well, Flexi-Coil has recently purchased a nearby
100,000 square foot (9,290 square meter) facility, which will be
used as a permanent showroom and training centre.

Flexi-Coil is proud of its accomplishments and its tradition of
supplying the world's most advanced seeding systems for dry land
farming. Its corporate goal continues to be to provide
technology, marketing, service and support to the existing and
developing markets in the agricultural world.

Mission Statement

To be the global leader in seeding, tillage and spraying
equipment by incorporating superior technology, innovation and
providing customer specific solutions.

We will provide our customers and our dealers the best in
products and services in terms of quality, durability,
simplicity, safety, and efficiency.

Our business will be conducted in a manner that will result in
long term success, growth and a reputation for treating all
staff, customers and suppliers with integrity and fairness.

Farming demands precision in planting, fertilizing, application
of seed treatments, spraying and tillage. That's what we
deliver.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Air Carts
Air Carts

Flexi-Coil air carts are designed to deliver seed and fertilizer
quickly, efficiently and accurately. Split tanks, versatile
configurations, simple rate adjustment, in-cab monitoring and air
velocity control bring precision to your operation.

50 Series Air Carts
Ideal for growers with big farms, diverse seeding and fertilizing
requirements.
- 340 bushel tank, split to 154, 89 and 97 bushels (9.25 tonne,
split to 4.2/2.4/2.6 tonne)
- Single-, double and triple-shoot capability
- Mechanical or variable drive
- Optional 10 in. (25.4 cm) auger for quick filling and unloading
- Tow-behind and tow-between available

1720 and 2320 Air Carts
Mid-sized, full features air carts that offer a range of seeding
and fertilizing options.
- 170 and 230 bushel (4.71 and 6.26 tonne) capacities feature
40/60 split tanks and standard 7 in. (17.8 cm) auger
- Double-Shoot (or triple-shoot with optional third tank)
- Tow-behind, tow-between with 3-point category II or IIIN hitch
(1720 only)

1330 Air Cart
Advanced air delivery and economy in an adaptable package.
- 130 bushel (3.54 tonne) capacity with 40/60 split tank
- Adjustable tracking is ideal for row crops or matching tractor
tire widths
- Tow-behind; Tow between with 3-point category II or IIIN hitch

Seed Treatment Unit
Safe, effective seed treatment in the field, on-the-go.
- Safe, accurate and convenient
- Treats seed with water-based treatment products
- Designed for Flexi-Coil air carts


Product Name:
Precision Seeding
Precision Seeding

No matter what your field conditions or management style,
Flexi-Coil offers a variety of precision air seeding tools to
meet your needs.

6000 No-Till Air Drill
Barton openers provide ultra-low disturbance seeding.
- Size: 30 and 40 ft. (9.2 and 12.2 m).
- Spacing: 7.5 or 10 in (19.1 or 25.4 cm) row spacing.
- Barton opener:
- 18 in (45.7 cm) bevelled disc, angled for improved
penetration
- Fully adjustable down pressure from 150 to 525 lb.
(from 68 to 238.1 kg) per opener.
- 4 in (10.2 cm) V-shaped semi-pneumatic press wheel
- 4 po (10.2 cm) semi-pneumatic cleaner wheel
- Double-shoot option

5000 Air Drill
From summer fallow to heavy residue, the 5000 provides pinpoint
drill accuracy and consistent on-row packing.
- Sizes: 27 to 57 ft. (8.2 to 17.4 m).
- Spacing: 7.2, 9, 12 and 14.4 in (18.3, 22.9, 30.5 and 36.6
cm).
- Trips: 350 and 550 lb. (160 and 250 kg) cultivator style or
adjustable pressure floating hoe style.
- Your choice of press wheels:
- 2¼ or 3½ X 22 in (5.7 or 8.9 X 55.9 cm) steel V-shaped
wheels
- 4¼ in (10.8 cm) split press wheels
- 3, 4, and 5,5 in (7.6, 10.2, or 14 cm) rubber wheels

820 Precision Seeding System
The versality of solid seeding with coil packing, or row seeding
with mounted press wheels.
- Sizes: 3 section, 25 to 44 ft. (7.6 to 13.4 m); 5 sections,
43 to 62 ft. (13.1 to 18.9 m);
- Spacing: 7.2, 9 and 12 in (18.3, 22.9 and 30.5 cm).
- Trips: 350, 550 and 650 lb. (160, 250 and 295 kg).


Product Name:
Row Crop
Row Crop

Precision air delivery products are designed to save time and
money.

8000 No-Till Air Drill
Specifically designed for no-till planting.
- Size 20 ft. (6.1 m)
- Spacing: 7.5 or 10 in (19.1 or 25.4 cm) row spacing
- Can be configurated with 1720 or 1330 air cart

Barton Single-Shoot
- The 8000 is equipped with Flexi-Coil's Barton angle disc opener

beans.

Barton Double- Shoot (New Product)
- For seed and fertilizer separation, equip the 8000 with
double-shoot Barton openers.
