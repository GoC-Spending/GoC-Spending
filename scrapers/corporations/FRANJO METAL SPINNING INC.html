Company Profile


Franjo Metal Spinning specializes in the art and craft of high quality hand metal spinning and CNC machine metal spinning.

We can produce all shapes in all metals, our master hand spinning craftsmen and state of the art CNC metal spinning machines
can meet all your metal forming and metal spinning needs.

We can form metal up to 42" diameter and thicknesses from .010" to .250" with tolerances up to .005"

We specialize in Aluminum Metal Spinning, Steel Metal Spinning, Stainless Steel Metal Spinning, Copper Metal Spinning and Brass Metal Spinning. Our custom metal forming abilities can produce complex technical metal shapes.

Aluminum is a commonly used metal and many alloys are available for a multitude of uses, almost any shape is possible and the aluminum metal spinning product can be highly polished.

Steel is one of the most commonly spun metals, a very workable metal with a wide range of uses within the metal spinning industry.
Many size and alloy combinations are stocked and available for immediate production. We carry a large stock of size and alloy combinations to assure quick deliveries and prototype development.

Stainless Steel has many special metal spinning applications. Popular for permanence, strength and non corrosive attributes. This steel is noted for its hardness and is used for many industrial uses and exposed exterior applications.

Copper metal spinning products are very popular in the lighting and decorating industries, prized for its heat resistant qualities.We carry a wide range of copper alloy stocks to suit your immediate needs for production or copper prototype development and testing.

Brass metal spinnings are most commonly used for decorative purposes with a wide range of quality finishes.
