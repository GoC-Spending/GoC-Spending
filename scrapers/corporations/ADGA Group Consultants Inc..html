Company Profile


ADGA is the Canadian leading provider of technology solutions in defence, security and enterprise computing. We are proud to be one of Canada’s world-class innovators in the delivery of engineering, technical and professional services with extensive national and international reach.

ADGA is headquartered in Ottawa with offices in Gatineau, Montreal, Toronto, Calgary and Halifax. ADGA is a privately owned and operated Canadian company with a committed team of highly qualified people whose recognized expertise is built on a strong culture of commitment, trust, security, discretion, privacy and ethics. These proven attributes are backed by the highest security clearances and leading industry certifications.

Our progressive approach to delivering technical solutions are backed by robust project management, complex systems-of-systems expertise, sound business practices and strong partnerships. ADGA’s five decades of success and growth is a result of our strategic foresight, sound leadership but most importantly our people, who have the proven ability to plan, build, deploy and operate multi-year programs of strategic significance.

Management Consulting
