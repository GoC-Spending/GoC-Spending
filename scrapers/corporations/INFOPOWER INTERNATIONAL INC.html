Company Profile


InfoPower International Inc. develops, markets and supports
InfoPower fxDBO® ERP Applications Suite software. InfoPower
fxDBO® ERP Applications Suite represents one of the most
complete, flexible, innovative, integrated yet modular, and
technologically advanced set of Enterprise Business Management,
extended ERP, E-Commerce, CRM, SCM, WMS, TQM, MRO, EAM, VMI software
products available today.

fxDBO® is a technically and functionally advanced suite of IT
solutions, developed and continuously enhanced over last 29
years, focused to provide comprehensive enterprise solutions for Supply Chain,
Finance, Distribution, Manufacturing, Project Management,
Customer Relationship Management, Business Intelligence, and
Maintenance Management. fxDBO® is leading edge and
international in scope, technologically based significantly on latest
internet-centric RDBMS and tools (Oracle and IBM), with multilingual and
multi-currency capability.

fxPDBO® consists of over 75 application modules covering all
critical business processes, and has been installed at over 360
sites worldwide in manufacturing, distribution, supply chain, construction and
utilities sectors. fxDBO® has enhanced Enterprise Business
Intelligence and performance metrics features through dynamic
real time KPI monitoring cockpits and scorecards.

fxDBO® uses Dynamic Business Objects for defining customer
process specific business rules, empowering organizations to
rapidly adapt the software to any of their current
company-specific or newer market-driven and evolving business
processes, ensuring 100% fit now and into the future.

Utilizing Oracle's and IBM's latest Design and Development tools, InfoPower
International Inc. remains committed to proven powerful and
modern technologies, setting enterprise standards with true
state-of-the-art products. The end result is an effective,
efficient and profit-oriented enterprise-wide solution offering
excellent functionality for client-server as well as
internet environment.

As an authorized IBM Business Partner, we are also in a position
to offer IBM and other brand-name hardware at very competitive
prices. We can deliver a complete turnkey package of hardware
and software fully pre-installed and quality tested for a
customer if so required.

InfoPower ERPDBO® Success Stories

Talley Defense Systems (TDS), USA
TDS is an Aerospace & Defense manufacturer based
in Mesa, AZ, USA. It is an employee owned company
and on an aggressive growth path.

TDS tried another so-called 'brand name' ERP
product for 1½ years but finally found it totally
inadequate to handle their complex Aerospace &
Defense business requirements. They reviewed over
50 of the 'leading' ERP Packages available in the
market before selecting InfoPower ERPDBO®.

Jim Speer, Director MIS, Talley Defense Systems
comments:

"I have been in the business of evaluating and
implementing MRP/ERP systems for the past 20
years. I constantly evaluate ERP Software
Applications to determine systems functionality,
trends and changes in the industry. I personally
have evaluated over 50 different packages, many of
which are listed in trade magazines to be in the
top 100 ERP Software packages. Most packages
being pushed by the large consulting firms are for
one reason, MONEY. It is in their best interest
to recommend and provide consulting services for
products that provide the best result for
themselves, not necessarily best for the company
they are providing the consulting for. Infopower
ERPDBO® is not a package that you will hear about
from most of these consulting firms. This is a
complete package filled with features and
functionality that exceeds most packages and an
implementation cycle that can be in as little as 6
weeks. The name of the game is flexibility and
ease of use."

For the first stage, TDS went live in 6 weeks, and
the software has been functioning virtually
trouble free for over the last 2½ years,
continually adjusting the software to fit ongoing
business process improvements with extreme ease.

Talley Defense Systems (TDS), USA
TDS is an Aerospace & Defense manufacturer based

inadequate to handle their complex Aerospace &
