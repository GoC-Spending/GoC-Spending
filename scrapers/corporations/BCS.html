Company Profile


BCS Brooklyn Computer Systems has developed a new technology Security System that is suitable for Govt. Buildings; Jails; Correction Centres; Border Patrol;
High Security Buildings; Hospitals; Hotels; Embassies; Condos; Apartment Building; Commercial Buildings; Businesses; Mansions and anyplace where there is an employed security Guard.

It prevents security guards from sleeping on the job.


Founded in 1985 as an Information Technology Company. BCS has provided Solutions to the Security Industry since 1989. Their Technology Solutions gives you Peace of Mind. It monitors the Security Guards activities and prevents Guards from Sleeping and ensure that the Security Guards are Patrolling on their scheduled time.
The Silver Guard Monitoring Systems consists of specialized electronic hardware and software components that are used to monitor the activities of the Security Guards during their inspection and patrol tours. Silverguard is a high-tech replacement to the traditional mechanical time clocks used by security personnel.

The SilverGuard RFID Proximity Wand is the only RFID Proximity Stainless Steel Wand in existence in the world today. It is constructed from stainless steel to provide for unsurpassed durability and for operation in harsh environments. It is incredibly easy to operate - by just pressing a button: it can rapidly read an RFID tag within its scan area without errors. As long as the RFID tag is within the Wand scan area, it can be read without physical contact. It will even read an RFID tag embedded in concrete walls. The SilverGuard RFID Proximity Wand is very versatile - it can download its data directly to a Printer, a PC, a Modem or a Remote Mobile Collector.

 Reads 125KHZ RFID proximity checkpoints of varying sizes
 Downloads via the Computer Transfer Station to

 Visual and audible confirmation
 Operating temperature: -30°to 80°Celsius
 Memory: 128K SRAM, Processor: DS5001@11MHZ
 Checkpoints can be embedded behind any non-metallic
surface
