Company Profile


Allow us to introduce Drechsel Business Interiors, one of Canada's largest providers of commercial office furnishings and furniture management services.

Our Objective
Drechsel's goal is to deliver smart office solutions to meet our client's changing workplace needs on time, on budget and without disrupting operations.

Our Approach
We measure the quality of our service by how well we help our customers. We help define facility needs, assist in the selection of appropriate furnishings to meet those needs, source, supply and install these products, and provide ongoing service to assure that they continue to function at their best. The smart office solutions we deliver stand the test of time. As a single source for a comprehensive range of products and services, we free our clients, enabling them to concentrate on their business objectives.

Drechsel Delivers Smart Office Solutions
Providing seamless services in support of the solutions we deliver so that our clients are assured that the products they select will best meet their business objectives.

The services we offer fall into four broad categories:

Sector information

Total plant size:

We can offer our clients a 50,000 square foot warehouse where furnishings may be stored to simplify installation, minimizing its impact on their day-to-day operations. One of the advantages of our large storage area is that we can use it for extensive quality checking before furnishings are delivered to the client location. This further reduces the time taken and inconvenience caused during
implementation.

Our warehouse space is also used to store furniture which temporarily exceeds a client’s needs, but for which there is a future requirement.

Distribution of employees:

As an organization, Drechsel Business Interiors brings together a tremendous breadth of knowledge and experience to address your facility needs. Drechsel's team of specialists in sales and marketing, customer service, project management, installation and finance. All of the team members have extensive experience in the office furnishings industry. From our office, warehouse and showroom in Toronto, we serve businesses throughout Ontario, across Canada and abroad.
