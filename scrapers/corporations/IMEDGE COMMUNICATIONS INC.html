Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Voice Over Narration Services / Translation & Proofing
For over 15 years imedge communications inc. has provided media outlets & corporate clients voice over narration for medical, pharmaceutical, scientific, technical, financial, legal and government voice over projects.

With talent and studio we aim to make the creative process efficient, quick and convenient.

We provide ethnic translation, proofing & narration featuring over 20 languages specializing in localization. We offer an elite roster of voice talent, including ethnic talent, and specifically showcase North American English voice talent Rory OShea.
