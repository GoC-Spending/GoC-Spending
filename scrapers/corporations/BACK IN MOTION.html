Company Profile


For over 20 years, Back in Motion has been dedicated to helping people overcome barriers and reach their full employment potential. Back in Motions purpose is to help people to improve their health, enhance their opportunities and to live better lives. We achieve our purpose by delivering employment and rehabilitation services.

Employment Programs
Back in Motions Employment Services addresses both sides of employment-related challenges: the client searching for meaningful and sustainable employment; and the labour shortage experienced by BC employers. We currently offer Employment Services to clients and employers through 4 programs (Avia Employment Services, Customized Employment, Job Search Support Services and Skills Connect for Immigrants) in 10 communities in the Lower Mainland. To learn more how we can help you, visit our website at http://www.backinmotion.com/employment-services.

Rehab Programs
Back in Motion rehab programs include accessible interdisciplinary rehabilitation, disability management and in-depth assessments that recognize the uniqueness of each person. Our interdisciplinary team of professionals develops an individualized treatment plan for each client, which can be customized to include return to work, medical, and/or psychological supports. To learn more how we can help you, visit our website at www.backinmotion.com.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Customized Active Rehabilitation (CAR)
Customized Active Rehabilitation helps injured employees return to work by reducing the impact of medical, physical, functional, and psychosocial barriers to their recovery.

Components of this program include (but are not limited to) the following:
physical therapy, physical and functional testing, flexibility, strengthening and cardiovascular exercises, work simulation, personal training (community setting), education modules, job site visits, home assessments, family conferences, return to work support services, hospital discharge evaluations, vocational services, medical assessments, psychological assessments and/or treatment, etc.

Service Name:
Workplace First Disability Management Program
Disability Management is a collaborative strategy that optimizes the health and productivity of an organization's workforce through prevention, education, rehabilitation, stay at work, and return to work services.

Components of this program include (but are not limited to) the following:
company audit/needs analysis, policy and procedures development, job demand analysis, functional capacity evaluations, workplace assessments, educational seminars, medical examinations, occupational and physical therapists assessments, return to work planning, coordination, and implementation, physical and psychological rehabilitation, etc.

Service Name:
Stride Depression Management Program
Stride Depression Management Program assists clients who have been diagnosed with depression to return to work. Stride helps clients become more active and functional by reducing symptoms through cognitive-behavioural interventions, active rehabilitation, and coordinated return to work program.

Components of this program include (but are not limited to) the following:
assessment, coordinated treatment planning with other health providers, treatment conferences, physical activity in the community or at one of Back in Motion's clinics, cognitive-behavioural treatment and education based on Chageways treatment, return to work planning, coordination, monitoring and support, etc.

Service Name:
Compass Comprehensive Assessment Services
Compass Comprehensive Assessment Services provide clear, specific, and practical recommendations to our customers and clients. Compass services help answer medical, functional ability, psychological, vocational and learning disability questions.

Compass services include learning disability & Vocational Assessment, Psycho-Vocational Assessment, Functional Capacity Evaluation, Aptitude & Vocational Interest Testing, Achievement Testing, and Complex File & Medico-legal Assessment.

Service Name:
Triumph Vocational Services
Triumph Vocational Services provides support and skills development for persons with disabilities who are seeking to enter or re-enter the workforce. Triumph assist client through Pre-Employment Services (PES) and Planning & Employment Servies (P&E).

Triumph services include (but are not limited to) the following:
career exploration, computer workshops, skills training, work experience, job search, job coaching, physical reactivation, disability management, labour market information, job task/analysis, psychovocational assessments, neuropsychological assessments, learning disability assessments, employment counselling, etc.

Service Name:
Health Enhancement Program
Back in Motion provides corporations with assistance to help their key people maximize performance and effectiveness, enabling them to carry out their positions and responsibilities at the highest levels possible.

Services include (but are not limited to) the following:
medical management, rehabilitation, expedited tests and specialists appointments, exercise, diet coaching and support, and arranging surgical consultation if needed.

Service Name:
Pre- and Post-Surgical Rehabilitation
Back in Motion has developed a strategic relationship with False Creek Surgical Centre (www.nationalsurgery.com). The synergy and dedication to quality of our two organizations is unparalleled. Our desire is to provide full-service rehabilitation as soon as feasible after surgery. Patients also receive pre-surgery consultations, and a commitment to quality and outcome that exceeds all industry standards.
