Company Profile


David Lane Office Furniture Ltd. first opened it's doors as a
one man operation in 1978, supplying custom furniture solutions
to a burgeoning market. Over the next couple of decades, the
company grew largely by word of mouth, expanding product lines
and striving for ever greater quality of product and service.
We have been privileged to work with some of the most prominent
companies and institutions in North America over the last 37
years, and take great pride in supplying them with innovative,
attractive and enduring products.
Serving customers North America wide, our expertise is available
to you. Lifetime Warranty. Proven track record. High profile clientele. Unparalleled customer service.

Technology profile

David Lane Office Furniture Ltd. first opened it's doors as a
one man operation in 1979, supplying custom furniture solutions
to a burgeoning market. Over the next couple of decades, the
company grew largely by word of mouth, expanding product lines
and striving for ever greater quality of product and service. We
have been privileged to work with some of the most prominent
companies and institutions in North America over the last twenty
years, and take great pride insupplying them with innovative,
attractive and enduring products.

We specialize in flexible manufacturing, allowing us to work
with individual companies to create unique designs and palettes
to suit individual needs. In these times of information
overload, differentiation is everything and a clearly defined
corporate image can be worth a fortune. We offer the opportunity
to combine the critical elements of design, functionality,
durability and aesthetics into a furniture solution crafted just
for you. Corporate colors, logo inlays,design details, custom
sizes and options, all can be oriented around your specific
wants and needs. The days when buying 'off the shelf' was your
only option are over. The plethora of bland, beige and gray 'one
size fits all' office furniture can be safely relegated to
distant memory. You can have what you want. All you have to do
is ask the right people. From executive office suites to stylish
home offices, from ergonomic computer workstations to
breathtaking reception stations, from a coffee table to a
conference table, we offer you the finest in design and
execution. Choose from our hand selected fine quality hardwoods,
rich veneers and contemporary Designer laminates, all detailed
with our line of quality hardware.

Serving customers North America wide, our expertise is available
to you. We have done what we can to take the guess work out of
selecting your furniture manufacturer. Lifetime Warranty. Proven
track record. High profile clientele. Unparalleled customer
service.
