Company Profile


Gullco International is a world renowned manufacturer of welding
and cutting automation and welding accessories. Gullco's weld
automation equipment improves quality and increases productivity
while reducing costs. Gullco International serves the global
welding market through offices in Canada (Newmarket, Ontario
head office), United States, United Kingdom, India, Australia ,
and China through an extensive distributor network.

Formed in 1954 to serve the demands of an emerging Canadian
welding industry, Gullco International is still committed to
providing state-of-the-art technology for today's welding needs.
Gullco is engaged in a continuing programme to provide equipment
and systems geared to the fast changing needs of the Welding
Industry.

Product Name:
Gullco's Kat®Travel Carriage
Kat® Variable Speed Travel Carriages with various models for rigid track or flexible track, complete with heavy-duty self-aligning wheel assemblies, closed loop tack feed-back for accurate speed control when running in any plane, regardless of load. (Maximum 100 lbs.) (45 kgs). Speed ranges vary depending on model. The carriage is controlled by the Gullco low voltage 24 volt GSP microprocessor pulse width modulation motor control, interfacing with the forward/stop/reverse switch and infinitely variable 4-turn speed potentiometer. The LED display is in IPM or cm/min. The motor control can be upgraded to provide auto forward/reverse, limit switches and auto wire feed start.


Product Name:
Gullco Kat® Carriage / Compact Oscillator Combination
Various models of either Linear or Radial Kat® Oscillator Systems for
Rigid Track or Flexible Track. Carriage complete with heavy-duty self-aligningwheel assemblies. Carriage controlled by Gullco microprocessor based 24 volt D.C. programmable control with closed loop tack feedback for accurate speed control when running in any plane, regardless of load, maximum 100 lbs. (45 kgs.). Speed ranges vary depending on the model.

Product Name:
Gullco Kat® Auto-Weld Travel Carriage
Kat® Travel Carriage/Auto Weld Combination with various models for either Rigid Track or "Flexible Track", with heavy duty self-aligning wheel assemblies, closed loop tack feed back for accurate speed control when running in any plane, regardless of load. Maximum 100 lbs. (45 kgs). Speed ranges vary according to model. Carriage is controlled by the Gullco low voltage 24 volt GSP microprocessor pulsed width modulation motor control, interfacing with the forward/stop/reverse switch and infinitely variable 4-turn speed potentiometer. Complete with three (3) limit switches  forward, reverse and center. LED display in IPM or cm/min and with a ten position rotary switch to adjust various functions to provide the desired automatic cycles.


Product Name:
Gullco Kat® Carriage/Torch Indexing Combination
Kat® Travel Carriage/Torch Indexing Combination with models for either Rigid Track or Flexible Track, complete with heavy duty self-aligning wheel assemblies, closed loop tack feed back for accurate speed control when running in any plane, regardless of load. Maximum 100 lbs. (45 kgs). Speed range 1  32.7 IPM (2.5 to 83.1 cm/min). Two (2) limit switches with adjustable track mounted limit switch activators for auto forward and reverse of carriage. The carriage and torch indexer are controlled by Gullco low voltage 24 volt GSP microprocessor pulse width modulation motor controls interfacing the KAT Carriage with the forward/stop/reverse switch and infinitely variable 4-turn speed potentiometer, limit switches, LED display in IPM or cm/min and interfacing the torch indexing arm with various functions listed below to provide the desired automatic cycle, programmable through a 10-position rotary switch.

Kat® CARRIAGE PROGRAMMING OPTIONS:
1. Travel start delay after arc start signal, adjustable 0-9.9
seconds.
2. Arc on/off during index.
3. Crater fill delay, allowing the wire feed signal to stay
active after carriage stop. Adjustable 0-9.9 seconds.
4. Post weld delay. Sets the delay adjustable 0-9.9 seconds
after each crater fill delay for burnback or postflow to
occur.

INDEXER CONTROL PROGRAMMING OPTIONS
1. Forward or reverse index direction
2. Index distance, 0.01  9.99 inches or cm.
The indexer is complete with a 36 (914 mm) long rack arm and indexer, heavy-duty rack box, 1-1/8 (29 mm) complete with motor assembly. Limit switches to stop the auto cycle and prevent rack arm over travel. Adjustable Rack Arm limit switch stops, 4-motion semi-automatic gun holder to provide 6 (152 mm) of vertical torch adjustment.


Product Name:
Gullco's Moggy® Travel Carriage
Various Moggy® Fillet Welding Trackless Carriages...models include Carriage with control for continuous travel; Dual Torch Carriage and Carriage with control for stitch welding or continuous travel. Other models include a Magnetic Moggy® Carriage with control for continuous travel or a model for stitch welding or continuous welding, with magnetic base for out-of-position operation.

Product Name:
Gullco KBM® Bevelling Machines
KBM Bevelling Machines, with adjustable bevelling head to produce bevel angles 22-1/2° through 55°. Bevelling speed 120 IPM (305 cm/min).
KBM-18-100 ideal for bevelling mild steel, stainless steel and aluminum plates from 1/4" to 1-1/2" (6.0 - 38.1 mm).
KBM-28-100 ideal for bevelling mild steel, stainless steel and aluminum plates from 5/16" to 2" (8 - 50 mm).
Above two models are available in an 'inverted' model complete with undercarriage assembly for bevelling the underside of plate.

Product Name:
Gullco Kat II® Light Duty All Position Travel Carriage
Kat II® Variable Speed Carriage, 2 - 46 IPM (5 - 114 cm/min) electronic drive with dynamic braking, speed potentiometer, fuse, forward/reverse switch, run/stop switch, power ON light with top housing drilled and tapped for 1-1/8" (29 mm) "KAT" rack boxes and a 3-hose manifold block. Load capacity 50 lbs. (22.68 kgs) vertical.


Product Name:
Gullco Kamel Rolls
Gullco Kamel Power Turning Rolls with a turning capacity of either 1,000 lbs. (454 kg) or 1,500 lbs. (680 kg), together with an optional Idler Roll for above two models, having a 750 lb. (340 kg) load capacity.

Product Name:
Gullco Weld Positioner
Various models of Gullco Welding Positioners -- each model coming as either a Standard Model; a 2" Through Hole Positioner and Programmable Welding Positioner models and can be equipped with gas purge.

Product Name:
Gullco Self-Centering Welding Grippers
Model WPG-250, Self Centering Bolt-On Gripper, capacity
.375" (9.52 mm) through 15" (381 mm).
Model WP 400 Self Centering Bolt-On Gripper, capacity
3 1/2" (88.9 mm) through 20" (508 mm).


Product Name:
Electrode Stabilizing Ovens
Various models of Electrode Stabilizing Ovens, ranging from a 20 lb. capacity up to 1,000 lb. capacity, together with various temperature ranges.

Product Name:
Gullco Rotary Weld System
The Gullco Automatic Rotary Weld System is a low cost automated welding machine that produces consistent, precision welds on cylindrical workpieces. It is designed to provide reliable quality welds, either in high or low production. It is easy to operate while providing sophisticated operational flexibility.
