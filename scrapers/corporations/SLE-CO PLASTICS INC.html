Company Profile


Sle-Co Plastics Inc. was started in 1988 as an injection mold
trial facility for the automotive industry. Since this time, Sle-
Co Plastics Inc. has developed into a high volume, high
precision QS 9000 certified molder of full production automotive
components. Sle-Co Plastics Inc. currently employees 35 full-
time employees with sales of over 5 million dollars. Sle-Co is
located in a 55,000 square foot, full climate controlled
facility located in London, Ontario, Canada. Sle-Co Plastics
Inc. is a Tier II automotive parts supplier serving OEM markets
for both interior and exterior molded components and assemblies.
At Sle-Co Plastics Inc., our focus is to assist our customers to
achieve optimal product design with minimal cost, through early
collaborative involvement. We provide an innovative engineering
team based upon a careful selection of Sle-Co Plastics Inc.
employees, tooling suppliers and raw materials suppliers.

Through innovative and proven techniques, Sle-Co Plastics Inc.
is consistently able to produce the best quality products and
tooling while maintaining a very cost effective pricing
structure.

We can directly attribute our growth and success to our
relentless dedication to customer service. Coupled with world
class quality, Sle-Co Plastics Inc. has become the preferred
source for Tier I suppliers primarily due to our teams ability
to react to our customers ever changing needs. Sle-Co Plastics
Inc. specializes in the manufacture and assembly of automotive
components.

We excel at the following tight tolerance, or high visibility
components.

- Interior trim components.
- Exterior trim components.
- Structural support components.
- Buttons.
- Bezels
- Interior lenses
- Encapsulations for door closing and locking mechanisms.
- Insert molding of steel components
- Insert molding of plastic components.Sle-Co Plastics Inc.
supplies completed components and assemblies by offering the
following secondary services or operations either in house of
through our network of local suppliers or related companies.

- Interior painting
- Exterior painting
- Pad Printing
- Hot stamping
- Laser etching
- Assembly
