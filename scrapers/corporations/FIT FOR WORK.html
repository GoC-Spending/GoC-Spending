Company Profile


FIT For Work consists of a team of professionals with
backgrounds in occupational therapy, kinesiology, pedorthics,
recreation, and industry. They offer a broad range of services
that include assessment and treatment of people who have
suffered injuries, functional capacity evaluations, job site
analysis, work station reviews and ergonomic set ups. Customers
come from all over Newfoundland and Labrador. They are able to
facilitate a smoother return to work for injured clients so they
receive a lot of business from Workers Compensation
Commission. As well, auto insurance and disability insurers
find FIT For Work helpful in objectifying the amount of
disability suffered. The assessment they use has validity
determination so they can determine if the person is truly
depicting their pain or suffering.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Occupational Therapy Services
FIT For Work is an innovative leader in the field of injury
prevention and injury management. We assist people who have been
injured , either
at work or otherwise, to return safely to the workforce. Our
approach is to balance the job demands and environment of the
employer with the work practices and fitness of the employee.
FIT For Work acts a liaison between the employer and
employee to arrange on-the-job modification of duties, ease-back
and workplace ergonomic reviews. Our services include:
Occupational Rehabilitation Services (including Functional
Capacity Evaluations, Job Site Analysis, Job Matches,
Workstation Reviews, Return to Work Set Up and Monitoring, Work
Conditioning and Work Hardening, Pre-Employment Functional
Screening, Educational Services), Medical Services (including
Independent Medical Evaluations, Medical Consultations, Pre-
Employment Medicals, Drug Testing, Executive Medicals),
Disability Case Management Services (including Return to Work
Monitoring and File Management), and Labour Market Re-Entry
Services (including Transferable Skills Analysis, Psychometric
Testing, Job Readiness). Our team of professionals has
backgrounds in occupational therapy,kinesiology,pedorthics,
recreation, occupational health, nursing, psychology and
industry.


Service Name:
Medical Services
Medical Consultation  Medical assessment provided to the
examinee by a physician who acts in an advisory role to assist
and compliment the clinical management plan and subsequent
rehabilitation plan for an injured person.

Independent Medical Evaluation  An examination that is
performed by a physician not involved in the care of the
examinee for the purpose of clarifying issues associated with
the claim. This is an impartial, unbiased and objective
assessment in which no physician/patient relationship or
privilege exists.

Pre-Employment Medical  An examination which concludes if an
individual is medically fit for work.

Physician Assessment - A medical history followed by a physical
assessment.

Spirometry - for the detection of obstructive and restrictive
lung disorders.

Audiometry - Monitors the acquity of an employer's hearing and
may include education in the prevention of hearing loss.

EKG - Electrocardiogram (EKG) recordings provide baseline
tracings for comparison of any emergency cardiac events.

Blood Work - Our occupational nurse can draw blood for Hospital
testing, and can conduct routine tests.

Urinalysis - Urinalysis can provide rapid screening of acute or
chronic illnesses; generally for the diagnosis of diabetes,
infection and long term chronic conditions.

Drug Testing  A variety of panel testing which can identify use
of alcohol and various prescription and non-prescription drugs.

Preferred Provider  Accessibility to Physicians specializing in
the areas of Internal Medicine, Psychiatry, Cardiology,
Neurology, Chiropractic, Dermatology and Orthapedic Surgery.

Specialty Medical Consults - FIT for Work's network of specialty
medical advisors ensure priority access. We have preferred
provider arrangements with specialists in Psychiatry, Internal
Medicine, Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopedic Surgery,
Dermatology, and Chiropractic.

Executive Medical - Protect the investment that a company has in
its Executives and reduce the liability that an ill executive
would create. It is designed to be a thorough test that is
conscientious of the time restraints on an Executive's schedule.

Employee and Family Assistance Program - The FIT For Work
Employee and Family Assistance program is designed to provide
short-term assistance to employees and immediate family members
who are experiencing problems. EFAP provides confidential,
professional assistance for a wide range of personal and
interpersonal problems to help employees live healthy and
productive lives. The program encompasses a full spectrum of
services including family issues, financial concerns, addictions
counseling, mental health issues, marital issues, stress and
conflict.

Disability Case Management
Return to Work Monitoring- Can involve a number of tasks
including development of a return to work plan, monitoring the
return to work process, meeting with health care providers and
general support.

File Management  Involving an aspect of case management such as
dealing with red flag cases, meeting with a family physician,
monitoring a specific aspect of the return to work plan, etc.

Service Name:
Training in Health and Wellness
