Company Profile


East Eagle Contracting is a Joint-Venture owned by MEMSKI PROJECTS INC (51%) and EAST COAST METAL FABRICATION INC (49%). Drawing on the strengths of its owners, EAST EAGLE is poised to deliver top quality performance on large construction, metal fabrication, consulting, and installment projects. Utilizing MEMSKI's expertise in strategic skilled First Nation trade-labour supply coupled with EAST COAST METAL's proven track-record in fabrication and installment projects in the Energy/Oil&Gas/Industrial/Mining industries - EAST EAGLE is a obvious choice for Major Project Procurement.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Metal Fabrication
Our top-notch shop and field crews enable us to deliver full turnkey services on virtually any metal fabrication project throughout the commercial and industrial sectors. Our site is situated on a 22-acre dockside lot strategically connected to the Sydney Harbor. The site includes 4,250 ft of wharf space with 20-36 ft of water depth alongside. Offshore oil and gas supply vessels, bulk carriers, product tankers, ferries, coast guard vessels and ocean-going barges have all loaded or been serviced at our facility. We have built, assembled and loaded out skids for the offshore oil and gas industry onsite, as well as to northern mining projects. The wharf allows East Coast Metal Fabrication to assemble and deliver components in much larger sections than what can be transported by land. Our partners allow us to offer everything from design/inspection/engineering consulting to concrete foundation work and the erection or large metal structures. If anyone can do it, so can we.
