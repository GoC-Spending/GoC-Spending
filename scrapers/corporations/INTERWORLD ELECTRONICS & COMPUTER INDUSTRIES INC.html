Company Profile


Interworld Manufactures a broad range of High Voltage, Low
inductance wire wound resistors as well as current shunts
and voltage dividers for High Voltage applications.
Resistor applications include tail and front resistors for
Impulse generators and any other applications where high
power, low inductance and high energy dissipation is required.
Interworld has been manufacturing and designing HV resistors
for 32 years. References are available by viewing our web site
under high voltage products at http://www.interworldna.com
Interworld also supplies HV Capacitors, Shunts and dividers.

GTCO Digitizers
GTCO Manufacture a complete range of electromagnetic and sonic
digitizer systems for 2D and 3D applications. Tablets range in
size from 12"x12" up to 42"x60" in both corded and cordless
models. Applications range from manufacturing to mapping.
Excellent tool to speed up area and distance measurement from
Plans, maps or drawings. Interworld also market a complete range
of software to allow you to use these digitizers.

Product Name:
American Advantech Corporation
American Advantech manufacture a complete range of high quality
heavy duty industrial rack mount chassis which can accomodate
passive back plane CPU's or Mother board systems. These systems
are ideal for factory automation in harsh environments. Units
feature high shock withstand capabilities, filtered air intake
card hold downs etc.
Other products from Advantech include Data Acquisition Systems
for ISA and PCMCIA systems.
