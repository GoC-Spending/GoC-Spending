Company Profile


i³ International Inc. is an industry leader offering integrated video solutions that assist businesses increase profitability. Through usage of algorithms, pattern and trend targeting calculations, i³s provides customers with multi-level data correlations to help identify loss and theft in their businesses. i³ utilizes video technology that offer a complete solution to any market sectors, retails, industries, institutions, or banks.

i³s strength is in retails. Central Management Software (CMS) is designed to accurately predict retail consumer behavior and enable retail professionals to benefit from understanding that behavior and allow them to extend the relationship with their customers. To achieve this functionality, i³s engineering team will use in-house Video Analytics module that includes proprietary features such as VideoLogix® (human tracking), VisionCount® (drive-through and door counting), Queue Time (service timer), and Object Detection database. By utilizing the CMS advantage, i³ customers can form effective strategies to improve their Key Performance Indicators (KPI) and follow retail business best practices.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Digital Video Management System
i³ Internationals proprietary technology uses sophisticated video compression techniques and large capacity, relatively inexpensive hard drives to store and rapidly retrieve compressed video images. Our technology platforms presently include data management software, license plate recognition software, and human/object recognition and tracking software.

The data management software, PAC DM (POS, ATM, and Card Access Data Management), is a unique software solution that allows users to easily keep track of inventory, investigate suspicious transactions, and conduct thorough trend analyses. The software enables raw data from devices such as cash registers, ATMs, card access machines, and others to be overlaid onto a video image.

This data is then populated into a usable database which can be accessed remotely via LAN or WAN. By storing these images in their millions and associating each frame with event information in text format, the technology searches for events of interest and thus quickly and cost efficiently recovers the associated images of the surveillance camera we are viewing at the time of the event.

A second proprietary technology, our human/object recognition and tracking software, helps users monitor and protect high pedestrian areas. This intelligent software solution recognizes what human beings looks like in comparison to inanimate objects. Suspicious human actions such as entering into restricted zones, changing directions, or loitering are instantly recognized and reported.

While most human/object detection and tracking systems perform well in static environments, they fail to compensate for situations where there is a sudden change in light, or where there are objects such as tree branches and leaves moving and shaking in the background. i³s proprietary human/object detection and tracking technology, however, has the unique ability to deal with exactly these kinds of environmental challenges. Through false object detection, the system learns what should be considered a false alarm, making it significantly more stable and robust.

Finally, license plate recognition (LPR) software is an image-processing technology that is used to immediately identify vehicles by their license plates. Most LPR software currently on the market recognizes a license plate only after the vehicle passes through a pre-defined alarm zone. Once the vehicle triggers the alarm, the plate information is extracted or is manually removed from the picture.

Unlike these other types of LPR software, i³DVRs proprietary technology works on conventional cameras, capturing the plate in real time and with minimal latency. While other LPR technology informs the camera when to take the picture utilizing alarm triggers, i³s technology isolates the best possible frame within that second, then grabs the picture in real time format and extracts the information needed from the picture.

i³DVR took top honors at CANASA this year when it walked away with two of six Dealer's Choice Awards. To select winners, experts from different areas of the industry are asked to vote on products in up to six categories. i³DVR received awards in both the Specialty Products and CCTV Products categories.
Vy Hoang, i³DVR's Executive Vice President of Sales, gave the winning presentations on our PAC DM Software solution and i3 SRXE-48032 ARM . Each fifteen minute presentation was followed by a five minute question-and-answer period in which he fielded questions on the features and benefits of our products.

According to Vy, competition in these two categories was exceptional this year. Although the Canadian security market is considerably smaller that that of the United States, its potential for growth is enormous. Our edge, he argues, lies in the speed with which our security technology continues to advance. That is why recognition at these awards is particularly meaningful. These awards come directly from Canadian Dealers and their Association, says Vy. We respect their opinion and hope their endorsement will help our product become widely accepted in the international and Canadian market".

i³DVR is proud to accept this honor and grateful to the dealers who chose to acknowledge our entries among the numerous other quality competitors. As the recipient of two awards, i³DVR will receive significant media exposure including personal recognition in SP&T, Canada 's leading publication in the security industry.
