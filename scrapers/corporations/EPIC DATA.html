Company Profile


For 40 years Epic Data has been a leading developer of manufacturing execution systems and warehouse management solutions for the worlds most progressive manufacturing organizations.

Epic Data solutions enable clients to:
- Improve accuracy of plant and warehouse inventory including real-time location of parts and assets
- Improve availability of materials to the production floor - exactly when required
- Provide real-time visibility of the production processes and the material flow to the plant floor
- Improve synchronization of the material flow with the demands of the production line
- Increase accelerated production velocity through measurement of continuous improvement activities

Our typical solutions include software, hardware for which we provide customization and integration services to deliver solutions that meet the customer's requirements.

We market our solutions to the discrete manufacturers in North America and Europe, while providing implementation services and post-sales support for installations in these regions. Our customers include Lockheed Martin, Bell Helicopter, Komatsu, Learjet, GKN Aerospace, Martin-Baker, Viking Air, Smithfield Foods, Solar Turbines, Rockwell Automation and Volvo.

By consistently providing the most innovative solutions, we can confidently guarantee years of reliable data capture and a fast return on our customers' technology investment.

Epic Data is headquartered in Richmond, British Columbia, Canada and has a worldwide network of representatives in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom and continental Europe.

Technology profile

Technology Profiles: Epic Data's Integra Suite of software solutions (IntegraNet, IntegraTrak, IntegraTLC) driven by real-time data collection about people, parts and processes that a manufacturer work force uses to improve productivity, efficiency, responsiveness and profits.

Epic Data pioneered scalable, real-time shop floor information capture systems to drive manufacturing operations systems for discrete manufacturers. For 40 years, leading defense contractors, aerospace, automotive and machinery manufacturers have relied on Epic Data solutions to optimize their manufacturing operations and maximize the ROI on their existing manufacturing IT investments.

Now, the essence of these systems is encapsulated in the Integra suite of software solutions. These stand-alone Integra solutions draw on three decades of Epic Data's experience, incorporating the best-practices of industry-leading manufacturers, and employing modern technologies to protect and enhance the return on your assets and investments.

Select only the Integra products you need, or stage their implementation sequentially. Epic Datas seasoned business analysts and implementation specialists will work closely with you to configure your Integra solutions so that you achieve immediate and significant productivity and efficiency gains.
