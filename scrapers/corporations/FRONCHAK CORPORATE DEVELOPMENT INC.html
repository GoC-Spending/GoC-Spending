Company Profile


Fronchak Corporate Development is a business advisory firm located in the Kitchener-Waterloo-Cambridge area, serving all of Ontario. We provide confidential professional advice to small and medium sized businesses including Business Plan development, Financial and Business Review, M&A Advisory and transactional support for the sale or acquisition of a business. For business owners seeking a cost-effective solution for every-day financial management, we also offer part-time CFO and Controller services.

Our professional advisors have combined over 40 years experience in Finance, Operations, Marketing, Strategic Planning, Mergers-Acquisitions and Divestitures.

Fronchak Corporate Development specializes in the Construction and Building Materials industry and is an active member of the Grand Valley Construction Association.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
M&A and Business Advisory Services
One of the most important decisions a business owner will make relates to buying or selling a business or company. This decision could arise due to changes in the industry, retirement or succession planning, or new business opportunities presenting themselves. Whatever the reason, Mergers, Acquisitions and Divestitures (or M & A for short) is a specialized area that small and medium sized businesses do not have covered internally. In such a project, it is highly recommended that an M & A Advisor (or in some cases a Business Broker1) be part of your team.

Fronchak Corporate Development provides M & A and financial advisory services, specializing in transactions involving the acquisition or sale of small to medium sized businesses. While a large portion of our past transactions have been in the Construction and Building Materials / Supply industry, our detailed process for transactions is applied to all situations. We have worked with clients in retail and manufacturing operations, and a variety of business sizes from small business to large multi-national corporations.

Whether buying or selling a business, our thorough process will help guide the project to a successful conclusion. We utilize your team of professional advisors as needed, and only in their area of expertise, which we our past clients have found is the most efficient use of resources. We work in an extremely confidential manner so that your project, whether the sale of your company or an important acquisition, remains out of the eye of the industry, competitors and general public.

If you are interested in learning more about this service, please contact us for additional information or to arrange a no-obligation initial consultation


Service Name:
Part-time CFO & Controller Services
For many businesses, an in-house bookkeeper along with the external accountant satisfy only basic financial reporting and governance needs. Paying the bills and keeping the government satisfied is essentially all they get. Realistically, they cannot afford to hire a professional financial executive even though there are substantial benefits to having such an individual within the company.

One of the services offered by Fronchak Corporate Development is the role of Part-Time CFO or Controller. The benefits of having a professional in this role within a company include:

 Business and Cash Flow Forecasting
 Financial and Operational Models to help evaluate the business
 Daily / weekly metrics to evaluate performance and change course quickly if necessary
 Preparation and Analysis of interim Financial Statements
 Coordination and Preparation of Business Plans
 Seeking and evaluating financing alternatives
 Business Process review and optimization
 Risk assessment and reduction
 A sounding board for Key Decisions

Your company can engage a seasoned CFO / Controller on a flexible part-time basis  a day or two per week, once per month or as much as needed  within your own companys means and budget. If you are interested in learning more about this service, please contact us for additional information or to arrange a no-obligation initial consultation.


Service Name:
Business Plan Development
Most business owners have developed a Business Plan, Strategic Plan, Marketing Plan or Budget at a certain point in the life of their company. Many times, this is the result of requiring financing for a new venture, expansion plans or even starting a new business. Ultimately, the Business Plan should provide you with a forward-looking view of the business, providing an action plan to long term success for the company. The Business Plan document should be a road map, and be reviewed, adapted and updated regularly  recommended every year.

Fronchak Corporate Development can help develop a plan which will help the company mitigate threats, capitalize on opportunities, meet long term objectives, and simply be better than the competition. As professional advisors, we guide you through the process of developing a Business Plan including financial forecasts. We have helped past clients obtain business financing, gain approval from a franchisor, restructure their business, and expand into new ventures.

Whether the Business Plan process can be handled internally, utilizing your accounting firm, or engaging a Business Plan specialist such as Fronchak Corporate Development, this function is an important part of running a successful business.

If you are interested in learning more about this service, please contact us for additional information or to arrange a no-obligation initial consultation.


Service Name:
Business / Financial Review
At certain times during the lifecycle of a business, the owner may realize that things are just not going as planned, the industry has changed and you company has not adapted, you seem to be missing out on opportunities, or you just feel We can do better. At that time, it may be worthwhile to consider bringing in a specialized outside advisor to conduct a Business and Financial Review.

Fronchak Corporate Development will execute a professional Business and Financial Review of your company, with the goal of providing confidential and professional advice to the owner and management team, highlighting areas where improvements can be made. This review can be incorporated into the companys Business Planning process and reassessed on an ongoing basis.

If you are interested in learning more about this service, please contact us for additional information or to arrange a no-obligation initial consultation.

Service Name:
Succession Readiness Assessment
One of the most critical issues facing small to medium sized businesses in Canada is Succession Planning. Many owners, in fact over two-thirds, plan to exit their business in some form over the next 10 years. While many have either formally or informally stated their exit plans, whether it is to pass on the business to the next generation, sell it outright, or wind it down, they have not made significant progress toward readying the business itself for this major event

Our Succession Readiness Assessment involves a complete review of the business  financial, sales and marketing, operations, systems and processes. Included in the analysis is our 53-point inspection which will identify actions management can take to enhance the value of your business, regardless of when or how the owner plans to exit the business.

If you are interested in learning more about this service, please contact us for additional information or to arrange a no-obligation initial consultation.
