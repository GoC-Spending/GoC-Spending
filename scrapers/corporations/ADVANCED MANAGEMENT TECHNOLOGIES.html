Other relevant information:
AMT is a small group of trainers, consultants & practioners,
offering a substantial depth and breadth of services based on
decades of experience as industrial managers, professors, and
consultants.

Advanced Manufacturing Technologies is a Consulting company with
over 25 years experience in a variety of industries (from
automotive to food processing). We specialize in work
measurement (M.O.S.T.) and plant layouts. We also direct ERP
software preparation efforts, aid software selection. We provide
managerial mentoring and supervisor training as well.
