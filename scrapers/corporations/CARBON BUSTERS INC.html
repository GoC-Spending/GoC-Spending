Company Profile


Carbon Busters Inc. is an Edmonton based company with the objective of reducing greenhouse gas emissions through sustainability planning, design and strategy. Our services include the design of low impact carbon free developments and communities, zero carbon building designs, energy modelling, energy auditing, utility consumption monitoring and tracking & energy workshops and seminars. Our self funding Carbon Busters program alone has saved over CDN $24 million in utility costs and 78 million kg CO2e emissions in school districts, municipalities, industry and commercial buildings in North America and overseas. Our education programs have reached over 147,000 participants on two continents to date.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Self-funding school programs
CBI offers energy conservation programs at no up-front cost to school districts. These programs include energy auditing, utility consumption monitoring and tracking services, and extensive education for students and staff. CBI takes a percentage of the savings generated over a 5-year period.

Service Name:
LEED Green Building Design
Carbon Busters has four LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Education) Accredited Professionals on staff. Because the best building efficiency can be realized in the design phase, green design is an important component in reducing utility costs over the building lifetime.

Service Name:
Energy and Environmental Education
Carbon Busters provides workshops, seminars, presentations and training sessions on a variety of topics including energy and water conservation, environmental protection, climate change, and HVAC training. Carbon Busters research on how families can save over 17,000 US and reduce their carbon impact by 78% is available in bookstores as the; "Carbon Busters Home Energy Handbook" by Godo Stoyke, published by New Society Publishers. The newly published; "Carbon Charter" by Godo Stoyke on how cities and municipalities can go zero carbon in 10 years is also now available in all local bookstores and at carbonbusters.org

Service Name:
Utility Monitoring and Tracking
Carbon Busters provides detailed utility consumption monitoring and tracking. We establish consumption and greenhouse gas baselines using historical data and track current consumption through frequent meter readings. Carbon Busters also analyzes past billing records for accuracy and uses this information to predict savings potentials.

Service Name:
Energy Auditing
Carbon Busters provides professional energy audits of lighting and mechanical systems for clients. These audits focus on no-cost, low-cost, maintenance and retrofit options for utility savings. Our auditors are among the most experienced in Western Canada.

Service Name:
SeriousWindows for Residential and Commercial buildings
Full frame rated high R- value super insulated windows (R5 to R11.1), that exceed energy efficiency ratings, providing savings, comfort, and extreme durability even in the harshest of climates. These fiberglass SeriousWindows offer a variety of styles, colors and performance levels custom made to match the needs of a home or a business. They deliver exceptional sound insulation through their designs that enhance homes and commercial buildings from both the inside and outside, reducing noise by 25% to 85% over existing single-pane and dual-pane windows.

Unique applications:

Carbon Busters Inc. combines experienced auditing professionals and cutting-edge savings and retrofit recommendations with extensive educational programs and workshops. It is this combination of technology, experience and education that makes our programs so successful. Our company offers a comprehensive package of energy management services to our clients, which results in substantial utility savings.

Key / Major clients:

City of Lloydminster, AB/SK
Horizon School Division No. 67
Battle River Regional Division No. 3
St. Thomas Aquinas Roman Catholic Separate Regional Division No. 38
School districts and municipalities in Germany
School district in Luxembourg

Success stories:

Our self-funding energy conservation programs for schools have saved over CDN $16.2 million in utility costs for schools in the last eleven years. Over this time period, our programs have also reduced greenhouse gas emissions by over 44.4 million kg CO2e. All of these savings have occurred with no up-front costs paid by the participating school districts.

Testimonial:

Our school division has been with the Carbon Busters program for almost a year. When deciding on what energy conservation program to go with, our school division gave consideration to ease of implementation in our schools. Not wanting to add extensive work to our schools and classroom teachers, program structure, support materials and curriculum alignment were important. Of the different programs looked at, Carbon Busters had a structure in place for program implementation that was sequential, clear and distributed the responsibility. The program had the best fit with Alberta Science curriculum at the elementary level and corresponding lessons that did not require extra work by our teachers. As well, CBI was accommodating, thorough and friendly throughout the time they inserviced schools on the use of the program.

The City of Lloydminster has had the pleasure to work with your company [Carbon Busters] on a number of energy audits for various facilities that we own and operate.

From the first meeting prior to award of the project we knew we were dealing with a client-oriented organization. We appreciate the time and effort spent by your entire [Carbon Busters] team ensuring that we were happy with the end project. This included detailed on-site reviews of each facility, in-depth analysis of energy consumption, on-site and follow-up review with staff and operators as well as a complete wrap-up presentation upon completion of the project.

Throughout the course of this project, CBI kept us informed and ensured they knew our complete requirements for this project.

I have absolutely no hesitation in recommending CBI to anyone that may be considering an energy audit of his or her facilities.
