Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Drawstring Bags
Polyethylene drawstring bags are a great way to package products. They are well suited to applications that require a hygienic and have a very convenient opening/closing feature. The bag can have a perforated opening feature and be reclosed by pulling the drawstrings together. GH can provide a full service - purchasing the polyethylene film through to supplying finished bags - or only a converting service allowing you to continue working with your trusted suppliers of polyethylene film. Bags are provided on wickets which allow you to use them in your automated packaging lines.

Product Name:
Smart Sheets
Polythyelne static sheets used for presentations. Sheets are individually perforated on a roll, 40 sheets per roll. Require no tape, tacks, pins, glue, etc. to adhere to most flat surfaces.
Available also as printed sheets for advertising.

Product Name:
Polyethylene Bags
Polyethylene wicket bags are a great way to package your products. Our lines are state-of-the-art and allow for some of the smallest wicket bags to be produced with a very high quality. As packaging trends to smaller sizes  our bags can accommodate this trend at a high speed with a high level of quality. GH can provide a full service - purchasing the polyethylene film through to supplying finished bags - or only a converting service allowing you to continue working with your trusted suppliers of polyethylene film. Bags are provided on wickets which allow you to use them in your automated packaging lines.

Service Name:
Contract Packaging
o Flow Wrapping Flow wrapping - also called fin-seal wrapping as it produces a "fin" on the bottom and at each end of the package. The flow-wrapped package utilizes an Oriented Polypropylene (OPP) film, which can be clear or pre-printed. Ideal for samples and small products for individual sale.
o Shrink Wrapping Shrink wrap is applied around your product by our automated equipment. It is then heated through a heat tunnel for shrinking. Perfect for multi-pack items or as a closure to ensure your products are not tampered.
o Pouching GH can package your product in a bag  printed or unprinted  using our automatic equipment. Perfect for premium samples and small products for individual sale.
o Cartoning Semi-automatic cartoning equipment seals your cartons with hot-melt. Big or small GH can do it all.
o Labelling Applying labels to your product using semi-automatic equipment efficiently.
o Manual Assembly Assembly, kitting, etc. our trained team can provide a cost effective solution.
o Bagging Semi-automatic bag sealing equipment  provides a secure seal. Polyethylene or polypropylene  printed or unprinted  GH can package and seal your products.
o Display Ready Pallets (DRP) Four-way shoppable pallets for club stores. GH can turn your brown box products efficiently into club store formats.
o Floor Stand Displays Used to merchandise product and make your product stand out from the competition. GH can assemble, load and ship your floor stands direct to your customers.
o Point of Purchase (POP) Displays A product merchandiser designed to hold product and influence purchase at retail  GH can assemble, load and ship POP displays direct to your customers.
o Repacks Experienced in repacking your product efficiently and effectively.
o Inspections GH Team is experienced in inspecting your products to pre-defined specifications/instructions.
o Direct Shipping Using Canada Post or any of the couriers  GH can ship your product to your customers. As e-commerce grows  let us be your partner for an effective way to ship your products.
o Direct mail programs GH can provide from full to partial service on your direct mail programs. With many partners we can help make your direct mail programs a success.
o Web-based fulfillment Let GH be your fulfillment partner  we can warehouse, pack and ship your products  allowing you to focus on sales and growing your business.
o Bulk sample packaging No order is too big for GH. Bulk ship us your product to be packed in a variety of ways using our semi-automatic equipment.
