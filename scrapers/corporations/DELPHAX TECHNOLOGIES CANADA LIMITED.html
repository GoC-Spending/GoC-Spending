Company Profile


Delphax Technologies Inc. is a global leader in the design, manufacture and delivery of advanced digital print production systems. For more than 35 years, Delphax Technologies has pioneered high-speed digital imaging innovations that improve throughput and enhance efficiencies for publishers, direct marketers and other commercial print customers where cost and quality are important. The Delphax Technologies portfolio includes the “New elan” full color, cut sheet, inkjet system plus the web fed monochrome inkjet UV Hawk series. Delphax also offers its traditional toner based technology systems (Imaggia and CR) that are ideal for a wide range of applications over a broad spectrum of substrates from ultra-lightweight paper to heavy stock in both roll-fed and cut-sheet environments.

Product Name:
RS Series
The RS Series printing system family is excellent for printing transaction-based and other data center applications that require throughput up to 330 images per minute (ppm) and very high levels of reliability. There are three versions availableRS330 (330 ppm), RS280 (280 ppm), and RS220 (220 ppm). For organizations with expanding requirements, the RS220 and RS280 are field upgradeable to RS 330 systems.
Fast-paced data centers can take advantage of the RS Series ability to process diverse application types. With the simple addition of a MICR verification kit, the RS Series provides superior quality MICR printing for all your time-sensitive financial applications. The systems can accommodate up to three four-pocket stackers for maximum output capacity, or it can be bypassed in favor of a compatible, third-party post-processing device. With the ability to handle Metacode, LCDS, IPDS, PostScript, PCL, and PDF, the RS Series is versatile enough to be a network and/or mainframe printer.

Product Name:
CR Series
The Delphax CR Series continuous roll-fed digital presses provide ultra high-speed production for applications requiring high-volume output, short print windows, profitable short-runs and/or variable content. Our customers use the CR Series to produce a wide range of books and booklets, legal/financial documents, packaging inserts, direct mail, and several other commercial printing applications.

The CR Series of digital web presses consists of 3 major models; the CR2200, the CR1500, and the CR1000. Each model number generally reflects the number of 8.5" by 11" pages per minute that the press can produce.

The CR2200 is the latest addition to the CR Series family of digital web presses. The CR2200 is the world's fastest toner based digital press. It produces printed materials at a continuous production rate of 500 feet per minute (152 meters per minute) at full 600dpi by 600dpi print quality. This equates to 4000 - 6" by 9" book pages per minute or 2182 - 8.5" by 11" standard size pages per minute. The CR2200 and other CR Series presses offer customers industry leading throughput, outstanding print quality, and an extremely low cost per page.

All CR Series presses use Delphax's unique and patented Electron Beam Imaging (EBI) technology with true 600 by 600 dot per inch print quality to produce excellent text, line art, and grey scale images on every page. Each is a single-pass duplex press with a tight web design for substrate flexibility, excellent front to back registration, and speed.

The tight-web design also makes each CR Series press compatible with a wide range of pre and post-press equipment solutions that have been proven in the offset printing world. For example, the CR Series digital web press was the first digital press in the world to be sold with an in-line automated web splicer for uninterrupted paper supply to the printer. This feature alone can add 15% or more production time to a 3-shift operation.

CR Series presses support PostScript, PCL, PDF, IPDS and other commonly specified print data streams making them ideal for most short-run black-and white applications.

Product Name:
Imaggia
The latest generation of the IM Series, the Imaggia II, is the most advanced printing system of its type in the world. With models that feature document throughput between 220 and 300 pages per minute, the Imaggia II has the worlds fastest digital cut-sheet output capability in the world. Coupled with its blazing speed, the Imaggia II supports the largest media sizes on the market.
The print quality of the Imaggia II meets the highest worldwide standards for non-impact MICR encoding for secure documents. Imaggia IIs exclusive Intelli-Pick dynamic collation technology provides the ultimate in job and substrate flexibility. Utilizing EBI technology, the Imaggia II gives you state-of-the-art features and performance with reduced operating costs.
Imaggia II models include the IM300 and the IM220.
