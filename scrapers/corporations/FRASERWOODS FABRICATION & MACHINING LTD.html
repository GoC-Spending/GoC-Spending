Company Profile


Fraserwoods is Western Canada's premier service and repair facility for truck mounted and industrial service vacuum/pressure blowers. We are the market leader in the repair, upgrade, and rebuild of PD blowers, pumps, gearboxes, and rotary air lock feeders. In operation since 2001, our focus is the quality service and repair of rotating equipment. Using uniform methods and processes, the latest industry tooling and technology and over 300 years of combined industry experience between our staff, we are committed to quality.

All repairs are done in-house and backed by our welding and fabrication departments as well as our fully equipped CNC and manual machine shop. We are a warranty, sales, service and repair facility for leading rotating equipment OEMs. All sales, services and repairs are covered by warranty.

Our field service team supports our clients with general millwright and welding capabilities, along with precision alignment and vibration analysis services. Our millwright/welder crews address on-site issues and handle a wide array of customer requirements form equipment failure and replacement to asset management.

PD Blower Repairs
Fraserwoods operates Western Canada's premier rotating equipment service and repair facility for truck mounted and industrial service vacuum/pressure blowers. All repairs are done in-house and backed by our fully equipped machining and fabrication departments.

Our commitment to quality is paramount in everything we do. When your blower arrives at our service facility our industry proven tools, processes and procedures will have your PD blower returned in "as new" condition. We believe that quality is the key to ensuring that our work and the solutions we provide will benefit both our customers and their equipment.

Gearbox Repairs

Service Name:
Machine Shop Services
Fraserwoods operates a fully equipped precision CNC and manual machine shop service facility providing high quality products to the Western Canadian market. We partner with our customers to provide innovative solutions required to support their manufacturing, maintenance, and repair requirements.

Our clients represent numerous industries across Western Canada including mobile equipment, oil and gas, petrochemical, mining, power generation, municipalities, wastewater, and pulp & paper. We take great pride in our ability to provide turnkey products and quality service solutions.

The Fraserwoods Machining Advantage:
Facilitate ongoing investment in our machine tool technologies to ensure exacting standards and tight tolerance control are maintained
Engage top-notch machinists, programmers, and technical support staff dedicated to precision work and dedicated customer service
Offer inventory support initiatives and replacement parts programs
Supply CNC machining: prototype to large volume production runs

Maintain network of approved outsource partners to compliment our service offering

Service Name:
Dynamic Balancing & Vibration Analysis
Fraserwoods has the tools and expertise to dynamically balance your rotating equipment. Our certified trades technicians utilize the industry proven Schenk HM40B Balancing & Diagnostic System. Known in the industry as a highly precise balancing system for a wide spectrum of rotors, we balance to meet or exceed OEM tolerances and specifications.

We balance:
Rotors
Fan Wheels
Adapters
Couplings
Gear Shafts
Rollers
Other types of rotating equipment

Service Name:
Welding & Fabrication Services
Fraserwoods offers a full service welding and fabrication facility with capabilities ranging from minor repair jobs to complex fabrication projects. Our facility is well equipped with overhead cranes, ample floor space and up to date welding equipment technology. We pride ourselves in achieving cost effective customer solutions on time and on budget.

We utilize numerous welding processes including shielded metal arc, gas metal arc, flux cored arc, and oxy-acetylene capabilities. Our knowledgeable trades and technical staff offer many years of experience in all types of quality welding and custom fabrication.

Combined with the support of our other resource departments we ensure the highest level of quality and utilize a number of welding processes on low carbon steel, alloy steel, stainless steel, and cast iron.

Projects completed:
Unitized blower & pump skid packages
Acoustical noise enclosures
Specialized equipment packaging
Gangways and manwalks
100-barrel double wall tanks
Overhead crane installations

Service Name:
Field Service
Our Field Service team consists of highly motivated, well equipped and trained personnel dedicated to customer service. We offer one stop service capabilities for welding, fabrication, installation, rebuild and alignment of all types of rotating and industrial machinery.

Specializing in general millwright and welding, along with precision alignment and vibration analysis services allows us to satisfy a wide scope of customer's needs. Millwright/Welder crews can address on-site issues and handle a wide array of customer requirements. We continue to achieve the highest standards of safety and job performance to bring projects in on-time and within our customer's budget requirements.

Fraserwoods Field Services provides a wide range of services to the oil and gas, oil sands, pulp and paper, power generation, mining and drilling industries.

Our Capabilities Include:
Precision Alignment
Machine Base Fabrication & Installation
Shutdowns
Laser Alignment
Rotating Equipment Installation & Repair

Service Name:
Rotary Air Locks & Feeders
Fraserwoods is the leading rebuilder and remanufacturer of machined rotary airlocks and feeders. Experienced technicians from our machine shop, welding and fabrication, and rotating equipment departments employ uniform methods to bring your equipment back to "as new" condition, and to OEM specifications.

Our expert technicians can:
Reset rotor clearances to factory tolerances
Install new bearings and packing
Rebore airlock barrels
Rebuild or fabricate new rotors
Face or replate end plates
Plate interior surfaces
Replace rotor shafts
