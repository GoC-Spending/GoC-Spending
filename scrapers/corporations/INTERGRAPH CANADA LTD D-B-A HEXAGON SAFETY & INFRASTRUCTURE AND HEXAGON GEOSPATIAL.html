Product Name:
GeoMedia
GeoMedia, a comprehensive and dynamic GIS, extracts compelling intelligence from geospatial data and integrates it to present actionable information. GeoMedia is offered within the PRODUCER SUITE of the Power Portfolio. The Producer Suite empowers you to collect, process, analyze and understand raw geospatial data, and ultimately deliver usable information. This includes Hexagon Geospatials desktop-based GIS, remote sensing, and photogrammetry offerings.

Product Name:
Business Intelligence for Public Safety
Intergraph understands that to make fast, accurate decisions, you need actionable intelligence. We extend the capabilities of traditional business intelligence for analyzing incident activity in a geospatial context. All incidents have a positional characteristic, which is used to spot trends in frequency, based on geography.

The resultant analysis allows decision makers to target areas to effectively deploy resources and create intelligence products that detect spatial patterns to aid tactical analysis. Our interoperable solutions enable multiple agencies to easily collaborate with one another, share critical information when and where it is needed most, and coordinate a multi-agency response if required.

Vast amounts of complex data are stored within CAD, RMS, and other databases. The mining and transformation of this data into actionable information is vital to running an efficient agency. Based on the Business Objects toolset, our applications facilitate enterprise performance management. For more sophisticated needs, we also offer tools for ad hoc query and analysis, dashboards for performance monitoring, and visualization in a geospatial context. These tools and applications are all built on a common Business Intelligence platform and can be updated with data from multiple sources by our world-class tools to enable enterprise information integration.

Intergraph's geospatial Incident Analysis application facilitates discovery and evaluation of incident information in conjunction with Intergraph's business intelligence platform. The use of this geospatial analytics capability coupled with Business Intelligence for Public Safety allows you to identify and establish relationships and trends based on geospatial attributes that otherwise would have been missed through standard text or temporal analysis.


Product Name:
Computer Aided Dispatch
Our unparalleled Computer-Aided Dispatch (CAD) solution provides call-center and communications center operators with easy-to-use tools to field emergency and non-emergency calls, create and update incidents, and manage critical resources through real-time management of crucial resources. CAD resources, combined with historical and local search capabilities, ensure operators have timely and accurate information available when making urgent decisions

Product Name:
Security
Intergraph's suite of security applications extend Intergraph's Computer Aided Dispatch (I/CAD) offering. By integrating multiple state-of-the-market systems and traditional security infrastructure into a single, all-inclusive solution, Intergraph provides the tools and technology needed to safeguard people and important resources both at home and around the world.

Product Name:
Image Scout
Image Scout® enables quick and accurate broad area search operations on digital imagery, providing the tools to quickly build image mosaics and direct searches using geospatial data. Once points of interest are identified in the imagery, related images can be exploited using electronic light table functionalities to chip, enhance, and mensurate image elements.

Product Name:
ImageStation
ImageStation powers high volume photogrammetry and production mapping workflows.

ImageStation is offered within the PRODUCER SUITE of the Power Portfolio. The Provider Suite allows you to easily store, discover, manage and deliver your data across desktop, web and mobile applications. It also alleviates pressures associated with expanding imagery data holdings through the optimization of disk space requirements.


Product Name:
G/Technology
Intergraph's G/Technology is a feature-rich application platform designed to meet the geospatial resource management needs of utilities and communications companies. It is based on Intergraph's knowledge of utilities and communications company requirements collected during our more than 35 years serving these industries.

Product Name:
Video Analyst
Video Analyst® System is a comprehensive, effective, affordable, and user-friendly solution for video enhancement and analysis used for law enforcement, surveillance, or security applications in government agencies or private industry. Video Analyst incorporates a wide-ranging toolset, making it ideal for providing video analysis capabilities in a variety of applications. The robust Microsoft® Windows® platform allows even a novice operator to quickly learn and operate the Video Analyst System, eliminating the need for a highly trained and costly video specialist.

Services
