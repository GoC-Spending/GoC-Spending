Company Profile


Biogenie is specialized in the remediation of contaminated sites
and serves public organizations as well as major petroleum,
petrochemical and utility companies throughout Canada, the
United States and Western Europe. Biogenie has earned
recognition in its field for our ability to provide customized,
cost-efficient, and guaranteed remedial solutions, a product of
its integrated approach to contaminated sites.

ASSESS
By transforming site investigation data into clear images,
Biogenies 3D Visualization tool will dramatically increase the
understanding of contaminated sites while minimizing
investigation costs. In addition, our 3D Visualization will
dramatically increase the understanding of contaminated sites
while minimizing investigation costs. In addition, our 3D
Visualization will support decision-making and help clarify
regulatory, legal and technical issues.

DESIGN
Relying on our technical know-how and experience as a remedial
contractor, Biogenie is able to rapidly assess the feasibility
and cost-effectiveness of various remedial strategies and
technologies. This enables Biogenie to design an optimal
solution for every site.

IMPLEMENT
Biogenie has repeatedly demonstrated outstanding contracting and
project management capabilities by successfully completing many
challenging projects. Our distinctiveness lies in our ability to
implement remedial solutions on a turnkey basis and under the
provision of a total project guarantee.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Environmental Products and Services
Product Name:
Mercury extraction.
Design, development and exploitation of a physico-chemical procedure with a mercury extraction capacity of 5 t/h. The procedure consists of the following steps: sieving; gravity separation; flotation; and filtration.

Product Name:
Bioremediation of contaminants.
Following the selection of bacteria which can degrade the pentachlorophenol, the bioremediation of contaminated soil takes place with the help of a high-performance "Biopile" process. The gases resulting from the process are treated through a physico-chemical process.

Service Name:
Site remediation
Biogenie is a remedial contractor with a unique ability to
design and implement guaranteed remedial solutions. This unique
ability stems from our systematic approach to contaminated sites
that builds on our combined experience as an environmental
consultant, technology vendor and remedial contractor. In
essence, this approach and our experience enables Biogenie to:

Rapidly assess the feasibility and cost-effectiveness of a
wide range of remedial technologies and strategies
Design an optimal solution for every site: customized to
individual site characteristics while remaining the most cost-
efficient in attaining all remedial objectives
Implement remedial solutions on a turnkey basis and with a
guarantee that suits your needs either performance or project-
based.
