Company Profile


Shred-Tech, a world leader in reduction technology, designs and manufactures reduction systems and shredding machinery to cost-effectively solve the world's most difficult waste reduction and recycling problems.


Located in Cambridge, Ontario, Canada, Shred-Tech has international offices in the United States and United Kingdom. The company is recognized globally for a first class product as well as a continual program of engineering innovation and quality.

Principal fields of specialization include shredding systems for scrap tire processing, municipal solid waste reduction, product recovery and/or destruction, plastics reprocessing, metals reduction, and medical and hazardous waste disposal, confidential document destruction and electronic hardware reduction.


Shred-Tech's complete line of high torque shredders range from 7.5-1000 HP (5.5-750 kW) with electric or hydraulic drives for stationary or mobile applications.

Shred-Tech designs and manufactures fully integrated material handling systems including infeed and outfeed conveyors,
airveyors, separation equipment, trommels and rinse systems.

Complete in-house engineering & design services enable Shred-Tech to customize all standard shredders to meet specific client
requirements.

Thoroughly tested systems reduce start-up costs and ensure 100% reliability in the field.

Parts & Service are available around the globe 24/7. Continuous maintenance support is ensured with a high level of after the sale attention. After a world wide search for reduction technology, Hitachi Zosen in Japan and BASF Corporation in the United States chose Shred-Tech as their partner. Many of the world's largest corporations including Boeing, BFI (Browning Ferris Industries), Ford, IBM, Lever
Brothers, Northern Telecom, the United States Treasury Department and the U.S. Navy and Army are successfully using Shred-Tech systems.

When you purchase a Shred-Tech shredder, you'll receive more than a state-of-the-art product. Shred-Tech will also equip you with a wealth if industry knowledge and technical expertise, acquired through years of experience providing long-term solutions to the world's waste reduction and recycling problems. It's this extensive experience and insight that sets Shred-Tech apart from
the competition and has made the company an industry leader in reduction technology for the international marketplace. Put Shred-Tech's equipment, technical consultants and engineers to
work for you today.

Product Name:
Carpet recycling pilot plant
We has engineered the development of a Carpet Recycling Pilot Plant used to reclaim individual carpet components for the production of alternate products.

The pilot project is the result of a partnership between Shred-Tech and BASF (a supplier of pre-coloured and natural nylon yarns to the carpet manufacturing industry) and their efforts to establish an effective recycling program for used carpeting. Shred-Tech has developed an integrated carpet recycling system to reclaim post consumer carpeting. The carpet is reduced and separated into individual components ready for further processing. The reclaimed nylon component of the carpet can be manufactured into parts for the automotive industry.

Product Name:
Hazardous waste processing system
We custom designs and manufactures hazardous waste reduction systems which allow for the safe handling and disposal of a wide variety of hazardous materials. Site remediation is made possible through the reduction of containers and contents including steel drums, capacitors, transformers and other hazardous materials. Products such as automobile oil filters may also be shredded. The oil can be recovered and the filter's metal carcass is recycled.

Shred-Tech has recently installed a hazardous waste system for Booy Clean of Belgium, one of Europe's largest waste management companies specializing in the safe processing of dangerous materials generated by industry and municipalities. Once shredded, waste is further processed for recycling or incineration. With Shred-Tech's proven and reliable shredding and material handling technology, Booy Clean has processed a variety of hazardous materials with substantial economic payback. Shred-Tech's staff of professional engineers has integrated electrical and mechanical explosion proof components for safe equipment operation.

Shred-Tech's hazardous waste processing systems are in use worldwide and include installations employed by the U.S. Army, Laidlaw Environmental (Canada) and Sakab Ab (Sweden).

Product Name:
Shred-Tech 400 HP hydraulic low speed shredder
Three years ago, the Government of Jersey (UK) purchased an 400 HP hydraulic low speed shredder from Shred-Tech Ltd. The shredder is capable of sizing 20 tonnes of material per hour, thereby allowing the island to size 100% of their industrial, commercial and residential waste for incineration. This is a safe (no risk of explosion) and cost efficient (including capital cost and ongoing maintenance) solution to a waste disposal problem.

The shredder has now been in use for over three years with performance above expectations and maintenance lower than expected.
