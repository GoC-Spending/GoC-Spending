Company Profile


Gemite Products Inc. provides Licensing & Manufacturing Agreements for it's Patented Building System for Housing & Commercial, Specialty Construction Chemicals, such as Crystalline, Cementitious & Flexible Waterproofing, systems for Protection & Restoration of Concrete Structures, Anticorrosive Coatings and Admixtures, Self-levelling Industrial Floors & Underlayments, Exterior Insulation & Finish Systems and Wall & Floor Coatings & Sealers.

Gemite direct sales network covers Canada and USA and its expertise and materials are available worldwide through Gemite Group Companies and Distributors.

Gemite Group has, over the last 20 years, acquired comprehensive Worldwide experience in all segments of construction, which is now available to the Investor and Consultant as part of its Technical Service. Gemites technology satisfies any climatic conditions from the extreme heat of the Sahara to the icy cold of the Arctic, complies with International Codes and fulfills Local Approvals.

Gemite is Looking for Partners Worldwide - either as Local Manufacturer, Distributor, or Sales Agent.
