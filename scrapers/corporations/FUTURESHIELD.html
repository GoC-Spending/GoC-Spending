Company Profile


FutureShield Inc. was founded in Toronto, ON, Canada in 2005, on the premise that there is a strong need for domain expertise in integrating software solutions for security, emergency management, and critical infrastructure protection. FutureShield combines years of software expertise within a process of: Prepare, Mitigate, Respond, Recover. During the first meeting, we work with our clients to understand where they are within the process and which tools they already have in place. Our consultation services help solve the problem of where to begin  and it is different for each customer scenario based upon potential threats (accidental and intentional) and assets to protect including people, process and property. Once the solution requirements are set, FutureShield organizes vendor presentations from our portfolio of pre- evaluated and approved suppliers. In some cases, a single solution is all that is required and other cases require fully integrated solutions from leading vendors.

Product Name:
BeWare - Leverage Cameras & Sensors
 Monitor, manage, predict and prevent crime & calamities
 Smart data gathering
 Real Time advanced Video Content Analysis ( VCA )
 3D Virtual representation for planning, training, & situational awareness

BeWare is an innovative decision support system for the control room operator streamlining surveillance by only displaying images and events that have the ability to do harm. It is able, at breathtaking speed, to analyze and combine data from alarm panels and systems, fence detectors, security cameras, public sources and even police files. The system unravels patterns and signals anomalies by making comparisons with historical data. Any anomaly that has a high enough risk index triggers a warning when a threshold is exceeded. Another important feature is that BeWare is self learning and will continuously suggest further improvements to optimize the security measures. The learning process even continues during trainings. It is a dynamic concept that constantly adapts to changes in the environment and takes into account the reactions of security and emergency personnel. Thus, BeWare is always optimally suited to quickly identify new threats and then tries to prevent or at least contain them.

Technology profile

FutureShield customers balance efforts ranging from risk assessments, incident management, facility management, emergency response to business continuity. They are also expected to have a good understanding of IT and physical security convergence. Thats a lot of knowledge to be contained in what is often a very small department.

FutureShield assists our customers by helping them automate processes for security, emergency management, and business continuity. For many, the only tools they have are pen and paper, Word, Excel, or a home grown database. We are proud to work with many organizations that are part of the critical infrastructure sectors to assist them in the transition to technology adoption.We also translate your requirements to your internal IT. As technology AND security/emergency specialists we speak your language as well as that of your IT department.

FutureShield does not supply cameras, access control, guards, gates, or guns. We do supply software and other technical solutions to assist you in better managing the business processes within your department.

FutureShield works on behalf of our customers rather than our vendors. This mandate allows us to maintain an open process in evaluating solutions that have the most current and leading edge technology combined with vendor longevity. You will find on our website many products that we have implemented on numerous occasions and consider the gold standard for the industry; however, we work for you the customer and our commitment is to always act in your best interest.
