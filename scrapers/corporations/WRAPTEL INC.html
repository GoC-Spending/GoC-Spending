Company Profile


Wraptel is a Mobile Virtual Network Operator (MVNO) that specializes in reducing international roaming fees & international long distance fees for personal & business travel.

The Wraptel International SIM Card is supported by a network of 400+ international partnerships across five continents, providing high-quality, carrier-grade talk, text and data services in 200+ world destinations - with savings of up to 80%, compared to traditional wireless carriers.

Whether you have one traveler or thousands, Wraptel is ready to assist Enterprises with a variety of flexible solutions that accommodate their business requirements and adherence to corporate policies.

Fully Managed Services such as Order Management, Provisioning, Implementation, End User Training and ongoing Savings Reports & Analysis are just some of the ways in which we successfully help businesses deploy the Wraptel Platform and SIM Cards.

Wraptel is located in Toronto, Canada and caters globally to the personal traveler through to Enterprise clients.

Technology profile

Wraptel has developed a proprietary platform that includes next-generation GSM technology and advanced switching, billing and administrative software, providing unique features & controls that are simply not available with legacy alternative GSM providers.

Fully Managed Services such as Order Management, Provisioning, Implementation, End User Training and ongoing Savings Reports & Analysis are just some of the ways in which we successfully help businesses deploy the Wraptel Platform and SIM Cards.

Wraptel offers partners and resellers the option to co-brand or white label. We offer a full turn-key back office infrastructure which allows for the ability to sell, provision, fulfill, support and care for multiple telecom services, including SIM cards, airtime and hardware; in both pre-paid & post-paid environments.

Wraptel makes it possible for international corporations & partners to run and maintain their own fully customizable mobile network. Alternatively, organizations may wish to become an MVNO re-seller.

By using Wraptels mobile roaming MVNO solution, you can:

*Increase your product range, distribution and client base
*Achieve additional sources of revenue and profitability
*Significantly cut the costs of International voice and data charges
*Build customer loyalty by providing alternative services, promotions and rewards
*Enhance brand awareness through your direct communications
*M2M technology

Over the last decade there has been a significant increase in Mobile Virtual Network Operators (MVNOs) entering the market. The cost of entry can be high and setting up can be very complex. The lack of technical understanding for many businesses has also been a major barrier when entering the MVNO market. In order to support our partners, Wraptel has developed a full turn-key platform that includes Provisioning, Billing & Maintenance. This allows potential service providers to enter the MVNO mobile market and drive their business forward with minimum financial risk. In addition to the solutions & platform, we offer advice and consulting while walking you through every aspect of the business from start up to customer engagement.
