Company Profile


Established in 1996, Ronco is a world class manufacturer of safety and plastic products. With a focus on consistent quality products and outstanding customer service, Ronco has developed a strong presence across Canada. Roncos safety product line includes solutions for head, hand and body protection. In the plastic category, Ronco offers totes and polywoven bags and a variety of plastic bags. For more information, please contact Ronco at (905) 660-6700 or ronco@ronco.ca or visit www.ronco.ca.

Product Name:
Flexsor 78-800
Ronco's Flexsor line of industrial gloves has been especially developed to address challenges in precision handling hand protection.

The lines breathable seamless nylon shell provides excellent dexterity for great comfort and performance. And the different light but strong coating empower the user with hand protection and incredible precision handling.

For strong clean protection choose Flexsor 78-800. Its clean white polyurethane coating provides excellent protection against abrasive tasks while maintaining maximum tactile sensitivity.

Product Name:
Flexsor 76-600
Ronco's Flexsor line of industrial gloves has been especially developed to address challenges in precision handling hand protection.

The lines breathable seamless nylon shell provides excellent dexterity for great comfort and performance. And the different light but strong coating empower the user with hand protection and incredible precision handling.

For light oil precision handling challenges choose Flexsor 76-600. Its light foam nitrile coating provides excellent dry, wet and light oil grip. The glove's non-slip grip also makes it ideal for operating forklifts and other plant equipment.

Product Name:
Flexsor 76-400
Ronco's Flexsor line of industrial gloves has been especially developed to address challenges in precision handling hand protection.

For extensive everyday wear and tear choose Flexsor 76-400. Its light but solid nitrile coating provides excellent dry grip and an unmatched combination of dexterity and protection 24/7.

Product Name:
DEFENSOR 69-580
Heavy duty cut resistant glove designed for jobs entailing high cut and scrape hazards. PU palm and finger coating for excellent dexterity and breathability. Cut resistant thumb-crotch reinforcement for extra protection and durability.

Product Name:
INTEGRA PVC Plus
Premium chemical protective glove made from a special blend of polymer materials to enhance performance. Excellent resistance to oils, fats, solvents and a variety of other chemicals, including acids.
