Product Name:
Environmental Products and Services
Product Name:
Conduct an environmental audit and/or implement an environmental management system (EMS).
ALTECH is known internationally for environmental auditing and implementing environmental management systems. ALTECH participated in the development of the ISO standards, making significant contributions. We have Certified Environmental Auditors (CEA) on staff, as accredited by the Standards Council of Canada. ALTECH has performed in excess of 400 audits in industry sectors such as food and beverage, printing and newspaper, pulp and paper, petroleum and petrochemicals, metals and metal finishing, automotive, steel production, waste management industry, pharmaceuticals, paints and coatings, electrical utilities, mining and coal operations, plastics, and numerous general manufacturing operations. ALTECH also provides the development and implementation of environmental management systems tailored to the activities and culture of the organization. Where a company requests, the EMS can be compatible with ISO 14000 when certification is important.


With regard to process optimization, a comprehensive audit is a valuable way of evaluating the performance and cost of the management control system. Recommendations focus on cost reductions as well as compliance to environmental regulations.


This can include: water characterization and audit; waste management and minimization; energy audit.

Product Name:
Conduct a subsurface assessment of groundwater and soil contamination and assess cost effective remediation options.
With ALTECH's scientific expertise and protocol sampling methods, ALTECH has conducted a large number of contaminant migration studies and decommissioning projects.


Contaminant migration and tracking may take a number of different routes. The most effective is determined by type of contamination and location in the soil horizon. Delineation of contamination is completed by ALTECH professionals in all of the following four area of a soil horizon: soil testing into the unsaturated zone; and delineation of soil contaminant characteristics and extent; contaminant presence in the capillary zone directly above the water table; migration of LNAPL liquids; contaminant presence and movement on the water table, or saturated zone; presence of LNAPL liquids, and delineation of extent, characteristics, groundwater speed, depth, and direction of flow; contaminant presence and movement in a groundwater strait; DNAPL presence and characterization at base aquacludes and their delineation, speed, and direction of saturated zone water flow.


Decommissioning of a site includes the most effective remediation techniques to meet the most cost effective schedule. Remediation options available for contaminated site clean up include: bioremediation; the process of contaminant destruction by naturally enhanced microorganism activity; soil washing; an ALTECH innovation which releases the contamination from the soil to be treated in a water phase - this physically and chemically separates the chemical contaminant from the soil; groundwater investigations and remediation; the identification and clean up of contaminated groundwater - groundwater remediation strategies which are sight specific, ie. carbon filtering.


Insitu remediation techniques can often be the most cost effective approach for remediation. ALTECH owns and operates insitu remediation equipment such as pump-and-treat as well as vapour extraction systems.

Technology profile

ALTECH is a full service environmental and health and safety
company concentrating on environmental solutions for industry.
With our focus on environmental management, environmental
auditing, technical analysis and troubleshooting, and
environmental engineering, we pride ourselves on practical,
business oriented solutions that improve productivity.

Specifically, our products and skills can be summarized as;
Management Services
- developing environmental management systems in compliance with
ISO 14000;
- environmental auditing with certified audit teams;
- professional training programs and manuals in all elements of
environment and health and safety.

Technical Analysis
- full range of geotechnical services;
- air, noise, water sampling;
- engineering and performance based studies especially as it
relates to pollution prevention, and technology application
for pollution control.

Engineering Services
- turnkey project management for pollution prevention and
control equipment including design, construction and
installation;
- turnkey project management for Phase I, Phase II and Phase III
contaminated site remediation services.

Client Types
Our major client group is;
1) Industry
- providing management and technical advice for improved
environmental performance.
2) Government
- providing advice on industrial/environmental policy and
completing technical programs with regard to the impact of
industry on the environment.

Sales Methods
Our focus is on improving environmental and health and safety
performance using good business principles. Sustainability and
pollution prevention are the terms we make come to life. Good
environmental management is good business.

In many of the countries outside of North America where
environmental regulations have been somewhat behind, the new
environmental emphasis and resulting programs are directed at
industry to control pollution. We can help industry and
government through this transition by demonstrating cost
effective approaches to pollution control. Innovation with
resource management, recycling, pollution prevention,
environmental sustainability, etc. puts the focus on industry
productivity and growth rather than regulatory enforcement.

MARKETING STRATEGY

We would concentrate on the United States, Mexico, Central and
South America, Europe however, we have also looked at
opportunities in Asia and Australia, Sri Lanka and India. We

Technology

Technology profile

ALTECH is a full service environmental and health and safety
company concentrating on environmental solutions for industry.
With our focus on environmental management, environmental
auditing, technical analysis and troubleshooting, and
environmental engineering, we pride ourselves on practical,
business oriented solutions that improve productivity.

Specifically, our products and skills can be summarized as;
Management Services
- developing environmental management systems in compliance with
ISO 14000;
- environmental auditing with certified audit teams;
- professional training programs and manuals in all elements of
environment and health and safety.

Technical Analysis
- full range of geotechnical services;
- air, noise, water sampling;
- engineering and performance based studies especially as it
relates to pollution prevention, and technology application
for pollution control.

Engineering Services
- turnkey project management for pollution prevention and
control equipment including design, construction and
installation;
- turnkey project management for Phase I, Phase II and Phase III
contaminated site remediation services.

Client Types
Our major client group is;
1) Industry
- providing management and technical advice for improved
environmental performance.
2) Government
- providing advice on industrial/environmental policy and
completing technical programs with regard to the impact of
industry on the environment.

Sales Methods
Our focus is on improving environmental and health and safety
performance using good business principles. Sustainability and
pollution prevention are the terms we make come to life. Good
environmental management is good business.

In many of the countries outside of North America where
environmental regulations have been somewhat behind, the new
environmental emphasis and resulting programs are directed at
industry to control pollution. We can help industry and
government through this transition by demonstrating cost
effective approaches to pollution control. Innovation with
resource management, recycling, pollution prevention,
environmental sustainability, etc. puts the focus on industry
productivity and growth rather than regulatory enforcement.

MARKETING STRATEGY
ALTECH has completed assignments in;
 Mexico  Chile  USA  Venezuela
 Central America  Europe  India

We would concentrate on the United States, Mexico, Central and
South America, Europe however, we have also looked at
opportunities in Asia and Australia, Sri Lanka and India. We
have an interest and some experience in capital projects
including those funded by International Financial institutions.
