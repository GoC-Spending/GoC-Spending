Company Profile


CurryCorp can help you and your organization be what you want to become. We offer individuals and organizations assistance and support to recognize and operate at their full potential, reduce fear and instead focus on the future, on growth, learning and innovation. We do this by working with you to discover, test and articulate your preferred futures, design related data collection, examine results and facilitate collective direction determination. We then make suggestions to align governance, management and operations to realize that preferred future. We also offer training and advice on leadership, governance, management, personnel selection and promotion as requested. Contact us through our website at www.CurryCorp.net

PRODUCTS/ SERVICES
All CurryCorp services and products are designed to facilitate competence building and knowledge transfer. For that reason collaborative processes are preferred for the design and conduct of projects. A broad range of techniques and strategies are used in identifying potential and preferred futures, organizational analysis, evaluation of performance, products, programmes or personnel. Change facilitation includes board and staff development, alternative futures definition, choice making and planning supports. Monitoring, coaching, and conflict resolution are supplied to assist change implementation.

SPECIAL EXPERTISE
Dr. Curry has developed the following proprietary packages:

1. Strategic Development Processes: A composite of processes and techniques to collect and integrate context information, identify pivotal issues, develop workable agreements about how to proceed, plan appropriate strategy/ tactics, introduce and sustain change, monitor results and adjust strategy as necessary.

2. Facilitated Deliberative Inquiry: Useful for any practical problem involving fundamental choices, multiple viewpoints, policy formulation and action required in complex situations in which values and belief systems play an important role.

3· Individual in Context Analysis: Individual span of responsibility, authority, influence and control are described and examined for fit with immediate organizational unit. The process can be repeated with each subsuming organizational level unit until the alignments within the entire organization are available for improvement consideration.

4· Organization in Context Analysis: This analysis begins with market position and opportunity descriptions, then derives goals, specific objectives, appropriate structure, human and technical resources, output and impact definitions.

5· Curricular Analysis Systems: An evidence-based system to establish real social need and current best practice and then translate that demand information into competency definitions, educational goals, course objectives, instructional methods, learning supports and competency assessment strategies. The system is self integrating and points out linked discrepancies throughout the production function.

Strategic Development Processes
CurryCorp helps individuals and organizations remain true to themselves and to their missions in times of turbulence and opportunity. In order to survive, contribute and be rewarded, all organizations must continuously re-invent themselves. This requires close analysis of current and future organizational environments, events and opportunities critical to each business line. Goal definition and operational planning must be adjusted to fit new and emerging realities. Organizational structure, function and personnel can then be aligned with new goals. Supportive monitoring and coaching help individuals grow into their new challenges and achieve the results they desire for themselves and for the organization. As a research trained psychologist with long experience in public and private sector organizations, Dr. Curry can assist organizations and individuals to define and achieve the futures they would prefer.
