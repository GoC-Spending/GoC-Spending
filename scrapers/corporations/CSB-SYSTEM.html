Service Name:
CSB SYSTEM
CSB-System International, Inc. is a leading developer of innovative, platform independent, industry-specific software solutions for process manufacturers in the Food & Beverage, Pharmaceutical & Cosmetic, Chemical & Paint, and Retail Logistics sectors. Since 1977, CSB-SYSTEM has enabled our customers to achieve their business objectives by providing them with the tools required to increase their operational efficiencies, strengthen their customer relationships, and maximize their profits. With its modular composition the CSB-System is designed to grow with your organization, providing functionality for the entire enterprise including enterprise resource planning (ERP), supply chain management (SCM), warehouse management systems (WMS), automated data capture (ADC), computer integrated manufacturing (CIM), electronic data interchange (EDI), HACCP, and customer relationship management (CRM). For more information, visit www.csb-system.com.
