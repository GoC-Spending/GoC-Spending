Company Profile


The company is a leading Canadian strategist on behalf of higher education internationalization initiatives.
It focusses particularly on opportunities in emerging world economies in Asia, Africa and Latin America.
Its clients consist of universities and colleges, governmental agencies, NGO education consortia, and private enterprise.
Higher-Edge is differentiated by its ground infrastructure and expertise, based upon offices worldwide (Beijing, 5 in India, Dubai, Tehran, São Paulo, Lagos, and many more).
Higher-Edge assists clients in emerging markets with strategic planning. Its strengths include market research and reporting, trade show representation, overseas liaison, logistical delivery of product or service launches, legal and political issues, etc.
It has demonstrated particular prowess in international student recruitment with Canadian clients coast-to-coast for over 15 years. Within this specialization is a wealth of experience in implementing fraud-busting techniques to protect institutional brand and honour governmental policy objectives. This a theme Higher-Edge has reported on and it has presented at fora around the world in this regard from its inception.
Higher-Edge has received widespread commendations from the Canadian higher education sector. It has served as a regular commentator in Canadian media such as Radio Canada International, CBC, and The Globe and Mail.

Higher-Edge is well-disposed to serve Canadian and foreign
institutions and companies.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
International Student screening & verification
We vet international student representations through a variety of methods: detailed reference checks, independent information gathering, and detailed in-person interviews.

Since 1997 we have used systems developed by us to massively reduce misrepresentation, and protect and advance the brand image of our higher education institutional clients.

Service Name:
International Student Recruitment
Higher-Edge is Canada's leading international student recruitment company.
We work with universities to develop and implement strategic plans for increasing international student enrolment numbers.
Our international student recruitment arm is the Canadian University Application Centre (see www.canada123.org).
It is widely cited in the report of the Association of Universities and Colleges of Canada (AUCC) to Citizenship and Immigration Canada "Recruiting International Students in India: A Good Practices Guidebook Canada", January 2010
www.aucc.ca/_pdf/english/publications/&#8203;recruitment-guidebook_&#8203;e.pdf





Service Name:
Internationalization for Higher Education Institutions
With over 50 staff worldwide, in some 20 offices, we offer expert strategic planning for universities, colleges, or education consortia and NGOs on a host of internationalization issues.

The following is a considerable sampling of arenas in which we have consulted: include "study abroad", "on-campus services to international students", "application processing - enhancing efficiency", "issues of student misrepresentation", "cultural sensitivity", "working with overseas scholarship bodies", and "twinning arrangements for student transfer purposes".
