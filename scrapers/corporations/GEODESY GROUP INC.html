Company Profile


Geodesy Group Inc. has been in business since 1987.
We have been actively involved in aerial photographic surveys
throughout Canada and overseas for the past seventeen years.

Geodesy's experience in Canada covers all of the Prairie
Provinces, the Yukon and the Northwest Territories including
the High Arctic islands. With two aircraft the firm
has averaged, in an April to October season, 600 hours of
photo work that produces approximately 40 to 60 film rolls of
production.

The projects worked on cover virtually every application that
survey photo is put to. Geodesy does many
low level, large scale projects for development and
engineering projects. Our client list includes many
engineering and photogrammetry firms in private industry.
Much of the work performed is with all three levels of
government, including agencies and crown corporations. The
company's work with this group of customers includes large- to
small-scale projects of all sorts that require the whole range
of film types. Geodesy has worked on projects where scales
have ranged from 1:1,200 to 1:70,000.

A majority of our work has been for various government departments throughout Western Canada,
Alberta Government ESRD and a variety of Federal
Government departments. Geodesy also has experience working for
the United States National Science Foundation on the Antarctic
Continent.

Geodesy is a mature and established company with extensive experience in aerial survey operations and photogrammetric mapping with both domestic and international experience. Key individuals in the
organization have extensive experience in excess of 100 years. In seventeen years, we have flown over 11,000 hours of survey and over 4,000 individual projects have
been carried out. This work has produced over 1,000
rolls of film and 3 million line kilometres of photography.

Geodesy has stayed current with changing technologies and supplies an ALL DIGITAL solution for mapping projects. We operate a large format digital camera manufactured by Vexcel.

In the area of remote sensing Geodesy partners to conduct Lidar surveys and offers a wildfire mapping service on conjunction with Itres Research using their TABI 1800 (Thermal Airborne Broadband Imager).

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Aerial Photography
Geodesy provides aircraft, camera systems and film lab services
for for all manner and types of photo survey projects. We operate
both piston and turbine powered aircraft with current technology
cameras to provide for all scales of photo any where in the
world. All our platforms are equipped with modern geodetic
quality GPS(global positioning systems)integrated to flight
management systems for both conventional and
kinematic/differential photo surveys.

Service Name:
Photogrammetry and Mapping
Geodesy offers a complete range of mapping services: project
planning and consultation, digital orthophotos and mosaics, film
and print scanning, anaglyph stereo rendering, raster/vector
superimposition, hi-resolution plotting, GPS(global positioning
system)data reduction, kinematic airborne surveys, aerial
photography and CAD/GIS database integration for geoengineering.

Service Name:
Remote Sensing
Geodesy provides the following aircraft based services in remote
sensing:
1)Forest Fire Mapping System designed to detect and delineate
wildfires. The product is a georeferenced image map designed and a variety of additional deliverables for use
by the fire fighting team.
2)LIDAR: Geodesy provides aircraft support for lidar surveys
3)CASI(Compact Airborne Spectral Imager):Geodesy provides
aircraft support to a multi/hyperspectral imaging system for
studies in a variety of environmental, forestry, agriculture and
hydrography applications
