Company Profile


Hyduke Energy Services Inc. is a Canadian manufacturing solutions provider that has been meeting the demands of customers worldwide for over forty years. Hyduke is a continuous improvement driven manufacturer of drilling rigs and oil-field equipment, industrial and commercial structural steel, and field erected storage tanks.

Hyduke has centralized purchasing, design and engineering to ensure we have the most cost effective solutions for our customers. Implementation of lean manufacturing, 5S and 6 sigma initiatives ensures that out entire Team is driven in continuous improvement concepts.

Highest adherence to standards in Safety, Quality, Technology and an unwavering commitment to our Customers Total Satisfaction sets Hyduke aside from its competitors.

Product Name:
Field Errected Storage Tanks
Hyduke Tank Solutions engineers, designs, fabricates, inspects and repairs storage tanks ranging from 5,000BBL to 400,000BBL. From heavy oil applications and pipeline terminals, to water and chemical storage, Hyduke provides engineered solutions for all your storage requirements. With over 35 years of experience, Hyduke’s dedicated team of sales repre-sentatives, project managers, and engineering professionals ensures a smooth transition to our highly efficient production specialists with all services performed in-house.
Hyduke is designed to support your storage tank needs from cradle to grave. With our in-house engineering team we can design the right tank for your needs. Our construction team are skilled and trained to execute on any site, either for new builds or on repairs of existing tanks. Our in-house inspection and engineering group work collaboratively to pro-actively support your tanks with API-653 inspections to prevent issues before they start.

Product Name:
High Precision Machining
Hyduke Machining Solutions has assembled an elite team of industry professionals set to tackle all of your machining requirements. Our Calgary facility is equipped with the latest in program-ming and inspection technology. A dedicated staff is constantly looking for ways to increase productivity while maintaining the highest standards of quality. A diverse range of equipment enables us to take on almost any project from small manual jobs to 6 axis horizontal CNC work. HMS is also the home of the original Stratex line of overburden drilling tools. Welding and stress relieving are also available onsite.
The right people, the right equipment, the right attitude, makes Hyduke Machining Solutions your Premier choice for Machining Solutions.

Service Name:
T&T Inspections and Engineering
T & T Inspections & Engineering ("T & T") - T & T is a Nisku, AB, based company specializing in the inspection, engineering, repair procedure and certification of equipment assets and components. T&T's inspectors are all certified by the Canadian Government Standards Board and are associated with the American Society of Non-Destructive Testing. Certifications held include level II magnetic particle, ultrasonic and liquid penetrant. Our engineers are registered with The Association of Professional Engineers, Geologists and Geophysicists of Alberta as Professional Engineers. All inspections are in compliance with the Canadian Association of Oilfield Drilling Contractors Recommended Practices for Drilling Rig and Service Rig Equipment. All engineering is in accordance with the applicable American Petroleum Institute specifications.

Technology profile

Since 1972, Hyduke has been a leading global manufacturer of drilling rigs and equipment. We engineer, design, and manufacture, land-based drilling rigs, pump units, cranes, winches, center sections, mud systems, catwalks, pipe tubs, skids and oilfield buildings. Our products have prov-en their reliability in the most harsh arctic, desert, and tropical environments throughout the world.
Recently Hyduke has diversified into custom steel fabrication for civil and industrial projects, offering high-quality structural steel fabrication, modular unit supports, and more.
In addition, Tank manufacturing is a new venture for Hyduke, and the company has managed to gather a deeply experienced staff for all tank engineering, manufacturing, and field installation.
Hyduke’s supply division outfits crews with all operational and day-to-day supplies required to keep productive. With an experience procurement staff, Hyduke also supply’s general contractors with all spec’d material required to complete their projects.
