Company Profile


Protecting workers should be a positive experience and a positive business decision. At Alliance Borealis Canada Corp., we create and provide access to online incident tracking software, and online HSE programs. All of our online systems are customizable, content rich and updated regularly to be compliant with regulations and Canada HSE industry standards.

Our Health, Safety and Environment and Incident Tracking applications are in compliance with COR, SECOR, ComplyWorks, ISNetworld, PICS, and we provide assistance throughout the registry compliance process.

Workers have a right to be protected and so do CEOs. Our online user friendly HSE Management Systems and HSE Software (LibraSystem and ScorpioSystem) replace traditional paper safety manuals to more effectively address HSE management and responsibilities across all industry sectors. Whether youre a small contractor, an independent Canadian HSE professional, or a mid-sized company, our HSE Management Systems help to compile and maintain health and safety documents and policies for your workforce.

At ABCanada, we also believe that every near miss and every incident is an opportunity to learn and improve. ABCanadas Incident Reporting and Tracking System, ABCan-Track, allows clients to identify, track and resolve problems that cause incidents and threaten workplace health and safety. Our Incident Tracking System allows clients to identify and fix problems that cause incidents because they have access to most accurate information necessary for prevention.

Product Name:
LibraSystem Health, Safety and Environment Program

Replace Paper Safety Manuals, Save Time & Money

The LibraSystem is designed to make your life easier by providing you an affordable way to access a modern health, safety and environment system. Unlike traditional paper safety manuals, the LibraSystem requires far less of your time and money, which we understand are limited resources. We work hard to deliver you a fully compliant program that is easy for you to use and customize. It is online making it accessible to anyone, anywhere, in a way that people are used to receiving information. The best part is that we keep the system current so you can focus your energy on day-to-day operations and not writing policies and procedures.


The content and features of the LibraSystem were developed specifically to make your life easier. It contains a broad selection of safety and environmental management procedures designed to protect your workers, the environment and your assets. This content is developed by health, safety and environment experts to be practical and user friendly, and it can be customized to meet your specific needs.


The LibraSystem allows you to assign user access, set privileges, track who logs in and what they look at. It is a great way to ensure your workers are using your health, safety and environment program. Lastly, it allows you to store related documents like safety meeting minutes, inspection reports and completed safe work permits, and make them accessible to anyone who needs them.











Product Name:
ABCan-Track Incident Tracking System

Spend Less Time Managing Incidents


Our Incident Reporting and Management System ABCan-TrackTM is online so you spend less time managing incident and investigation reports and have more time available for identifying and fixing problems that cause incidents. This System is designed to be user-friendly so that anyone regardless of their computing ability can input incident data with ease. This means that management has access to more accurate information necessary for prevention of future incidents.


ABCan-Track helps you accurately record and manage all kinds of incidents, from hazard identification to near misses to major catastrophes, and once vital information is recorded, you can generate statistics and identify trends in a matter of minutes. The best part about utilizing our online incident reporting system is that theres no software to install and we look after training and maintenance. It is very efficient at compiling data from multiple locations and identifying trends. The system is secure and hosted on our servers so your data is protected.


Save time and money and modernize your incident reporting process.












Product Name:
LibraSystem O&G Health, Safety and Environment Management Program

An Online Safety Manual for Oil & Gas Companies

If you have to blow the dust off your health, safety and environment program when you pull it from the shelf, then its out of date and out of touch with your responsibilities. Its time to take a closer look at the LibraSystem O&G. This online safety manual is designed specifically for Oil and Gas companies to reduce the time and money spent on development and maintenance of your health safety and environment program. It is completely unique as we are the only health and safety program provider to continuously update your customizable safety program so that it is never out of date (unlike paper safety manuals).


The LibraSystem O&G is designed specifically to make your life easier. It combines the essential elements of a comprehensive health, safety and environmental program along with forms, checklists and links that you can use on a day-to-day basis. It also has a suite of standard oil and gas operating and drilling and completions procedures. Best of all, the program is regularly reviewed and updated by our experienced specialists and can be customized to fit any operation. Using the LibraSystem O&G within your operations will help to protect your workers, the environment and your assets.


Modern online delivery of the LibraSystem O&G also allows you to assign access, set privileges and track who logs in and what they look at. It is a great way to ensure your workers are using your health, safety and environment program. Lastly, it provides a database for you to store related documents right in the LibraSystem O&G, like safety meeting minutes, inspection reports and completed safe work permits. Once saved in the system, you can make them accessible to anyone who has an internet connection.











Product Name:
ScorpioSystem Health and Safety Program for Small Companies

If you belong to a company of 10 or fewer people, you still have the same health and safety responsibilities as larger industry players, but you likely do not have the same resources available to develop the necessary program. ScorpioSystem is designed specifically for companies with the same requirements as large industry companies and is especially popular with single owner-operators.


It is our goal to provide you with an affordable health and safety program designed to meet all of your compliance needs. ScorpioSystem provides all the essential elements of a health and safety manual ensuring you meet industry requirements. We also provide quarterly updates to the system because we know you are busy and may not have time to keep up with continuously changing government regulations and industry standards. You can rest easy knowing that health and safety experts have designed your health and safety program and keep it current.


We also provide assistance with navigating the health and safety world. Many small businesses are required to show they meet their health and safety responsibilities by employer companies, who use registry organizations such as ComplyWorks, ISNetworld and PICS or are interested in contractors with a SECOR. We will help you understand and meet these requirements.


The ScorpioSystems modern online delivery is completely unique. It allows you to assign access, set privileges, and track who logs in and what they look at. This is especially useful if you work for an employer company, you can simply provide them access to your personalized health and safety program. You can also store related documents right in the ScorpioSystem, like safety meeting minutes, inspection reports and completed safe work permits.












Service Name:
HSE Registry Compliance Support Service

Helping Contractors Receive Registry Compliance


If you are required to be listed with registry organizations such as ComplyWorks, ISNetworld, or PICS, or are interested in achieving your COR or SECOR you may not be sure where to start. Having a health, safety, and environment program is an essential first step, but your responsibilities in these areas are greater than that.


We are here to help. Not only do we create and provide access to first class health,safety and environment programs that are fully compliant with COR, SECOR, ComplyWorks, ISNetworld, PICS, but we also provide assistance to clients using our programs when going through the registry approval process. This often includes provision of advice for finding specific information within the programs, but can also include preparation of new materials required by the registries that can be added to the programs.


We also have an official partnership with ComplyWorks to ensure that contractors meet all of the health, safety, and environment requirements specified by ComplyWorks employer companies.


It is our goal to help you minimize the frustrations of completing the registry process or applying for SECOR and COR certifications so that you can obtain the approvals necessary as quickly and easily as possible.
