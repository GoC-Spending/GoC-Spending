Company Profile


EDP Software has been in business since 1981. We have two products  SchedulePro, employee scheduling software (available in various editions) and JIS Pro, a court case management system.

Schedule Pro is a sophisticated yet easy-to-use automated employee scheduling software package. This powerful system saves time, money and frustration. It saves up to 80% in scheduling time costs and simplifies the scheduling process. Please visit http://www.edpsoftware.com for more information.

JIS Pro is a proven Court Case Management System that is currently being used by all New Brunswick provincial courts. It provides fast, simple access to data, and enhances productivity, accuracy & efficiency. By producing various forms and reports, case processing is accelerated. Warrants, court dockets and many other forms can be printed with the touch of a button. Tracking of fines and restitutions reduces costs and increases revenue by ensuring collection of all outstanding balances.

JIS Pro improves the administration of justice through the creation and operation of a comprehensive integrated management information system. The JIS Pro system manages justice information using the latest in database and network technology. Seamlessly integrating resources from several justice agencies, JIS Pro streamlines their coordination and communication work. Please visit http://www.edpsoftware.com for more information.

EDP Software also specializing in the design and development of custom computer based information systems. EDP Software has used the most sophisticated design techniques, methodologies and tools in developing systems for our clients.

Our specialties lay in the development of custom software solutions, legacy system conversion and information system consulting services. We also specialize in the development of bilingual/multilingual systems.

We have a long list of customers who are very satisfied with our services.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
SchedulePro (Staff Scheduling Software)
SchedulePro is available in Standard, Enterprise and Web editions. Web edition is offered as a fully hosted service (SaaS).

This software is ideal for manufacturing, correctional institutions, EMS, nursing homes, hospitals, utility companies, fire departments, law enforcement agencies, 911, call centres, service companies such as restaurants, hotels stc. Schedule Pro can export data file for your payroll package. It prints large number of management reports to aid in cost analysis and forecasting.

SchedulePro is easy to use and very user friendly. It can take care of your headaches associated with the preparation and maintenance of staff schedules.

Schedule Pro can operate in a multi-lingual environment. Currently, it operates in English and French and user can change the language of operation at demand. Other languages can be added.

Please visit http://www.edpsoftware.com or http://www.scheduelpeoweb.com for more information.

Product Name:
Court Case Management System (JIS Pro)
JIS Pro improves the administration of justice through the creation and operation of a comprehensive integrated management information system. The JIS Pro system mamanges justice information using the latest in database and network technology.

Providing fast, simple access to data enhances productivity, accuracy and efficiency. By producing various forms and reports, case processing is accelerated.

JIS Pro is a proven Case Management System that is currently being used by all New Brunswick provincial courts. Based on the Windows platform and using client-server technology, JIS Pro provides a comprehensive solution for justice officials. With an easy to use interface, multi-lingual support, and a highly scalable architecture, JIS Pro is ideal for all justice institutions.
