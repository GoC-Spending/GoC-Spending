Company Profile


Welcome to Bangkok Garden, the premier Thai restaurant in Toronto for authentic cuisine.

Bangkok Garden is proud to be one of only four Thai restaurants in Toronto to be awarded aThai Select Premium designation from the Thai Consulate for exceptional fare and five-star service. We are pleased to offerdiverse and deliciousselectionssuitable for everyone, such as fresh vegan and vegetarian options, a children’s menu and gluten-free dishes at our Downtown Yonge Thai restaurant.

Toronto’s best weekday lunch offers specialties à la carte and buffet-style dining which includes our famous Noodle Bar and more for just $15.95. We invite you to join us for the ultimate in fresh seafood, spicy curries, soups and stir-fries at the Bangkok GardenThai restaurant, Toronto’s first and finest of its kind. Or, choose take-out or delivery to experience the taste of Thailand at home.

Located in a historic neighbourhood, our dining lounge shares a distinctive heritage space with the acclaimed Elmwood Spa, making our address a true oasis in the city. Reserve your table at our Thai restaurant’s Toronto centre location today online or by phone at 416-977-6748. Lunch Monday to Friday 11:30-2:30, and dinner 7 days a week 5:00-10:00 pm.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Buffet
Join us weekdays for our lunch à la carte or famous Noodle Bar and buffet. Toronto’s premier Thai restaurant, Bangkok Garden is recognized by the Thai Consulate for authentic flavour and uncompromising customer service. Experience the taste of Thailand with our satisfying buffet-Toronto’s best- featuring house-made Thai curries, stir-fries, fresh salads and specialties from the grill, all for just $15.95. Unique amongst buffets in Toronto, ours shares a historic building with the renowned Elmwood Spa.

We take pride in the fresh flavours and seasonal entrées offered at our popular buffet, Toronto’s favourite for great value and selection. We are pleased to present a variety ofof gluten-free, vegetarian and vegan options, as well as a menu just for kids-just ask! You’ll find our authentic Thai buffet in Toronto’s Downtown Yonge district, a neighbourhood we love and have been proud to serve, since 1982.

Perfect for groups, families and business lunches, we are pleased to present what we believe the be the finest buffet in Toronto, open to serve you from 11:30 to 2:30 Mondays to Fridays all year.


Service Name:
Delivery
Take home the taste of Thailand with Bangkok Garden Thai delivery in Toronto.

Offering a wide selection of appetizers, mains, salads and sides, Bangkok Garden is your choice for crowd-pleasing Thai food delivery in Toronto’s downtown core. Find your favourites from our authentic menu of savoury Thai curries, seafood specialties and noodle dishes, seasonal stir-fries and selections from the grill.

Located on a leafy street in the heart of the city, Bangkok Garden shares its heritage building space with the Elmwood Spa. With our Thai delivery in Toronto, we’re bringing an oasis of fresh flavours from our home to yours.

We’re proud to be Toronto’s first Thai restaurant and one of only four to have received the prestigious Thai Select Premium designation from the Thai consulate for our outstanding service and exceptional dishes. Can’t decide on a favourite? Try our 3-course chicken and beef or vegetarian combos for two with steamed jasmine rice and fresh fruit.

For delivery from Toronto’s favourite Thai restaurant, place your order online daily from 6:15pm.

Service Name:
Vegan Menu
At Bangkok Garden, we offer a wide selection of authentic Thai favourites for the discerning vegan. Our restaurant’s Toronto location is pleased to present fresh and flavourful meatless dishes among our creative menu options based on the Thai principle of balancing the five tastes.

Offering delicious, seasonal dishes to rival the best vegetarian restaurants in Toronto, Bangkok Garden is proud to cater to a variety of dietary requirements and preferences, affording the highest quality fare at the best value every day. Look for the green leaf on our menu to find options suitable for the vegetarian or vegan. Our restaurant’s Toronto heritage building is shared with the renowned Elmwood Spa, making our location a relaxing getaway in the centre of it all. Join us at our vegan-friendly restaurant’s Toronto dining room for truly authentic Thai cuisine, open for lunch 11:30-2:30 Monday through Friday and dinner 5:00-10:00 pm 7 days a week.


Service Name:
Gluten Free Menu
At Bangkok Garden, we are pleased to offer an authentic Thai dining experience and a specialty menu featuring items which are exclusively gluten-free. Our restaurant’s Toronto location is pleased to serve a distinctive selection of Thai favourites, including spicy curries, rice noodle bowls, grilled meats and seafood as well as seasonal selections all made deliciously gluten-free. Restaurants in Toronto may not all cater to dietary restrictions, and those that do often provide only a limited selection of options.

At Bangkok Garden, we’re proud to serve an extensive menu to rival any gluten free restaurants in Toronto. Conveniently located just steps to the Eaton Centre and Downtown Yonge, we share a heritage building with the rejuvenating Elmwood Spa. We hope you’ll join us 11:30-2:30 weekdays for lunch à la carte or for dinner from 5:00 to 10:00 pm. Can’t stay? Take the taste of Thailand home with you with our Take Out and Delivery menus.


Service Name:
Private Events
Bangkok Garden welcomes you to experience authentic Thai at your next work or social event. As venues in Toronto go, we’re one-of-a-kind, offering award-winning menu selections in a casual yet elegant atmosphere. We are proud to be one of the only restaurants and event venues in Toronto to receive the Thai Select Premium designation from the Thai Consulate for customer service excellence and outstanding cuisine.

Bangkok Garden delivers your best choice for crowd-pleasing fare, with options including vegetarian and vegan dishes, a variety of gluten-free items and a children’s menu. Experience the flavours of Thailand with your choice of stir fry, grilled meats and seafood, and savoury curries from the mildly spicy to the fiery hot. Party venues are available on-site by reservation for corporate events and gatherings with family and friends. At Bangkok Garden, we offer truly authentic Thai cuisine and event venues in Toronto at our heritage location in the heart of downtown.
