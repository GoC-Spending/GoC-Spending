Key / Major clients:

Successfully completed completed business analysis, process facilitation, documentation management, RFP coordination, technical writing or editing assignments for Communications Research Establishment Canada (CSEC), Algonquin College, Infrastructure Canada (INFC), the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA), the Royal Canadian Mounted Police (RCMP), Public Works and Government Services Canada (PWGSC), the Department of National Defence (DND), Human Resources and Skills Development Canada (HRSDC), the Department of Foreign Affairs and International Trade (DFAIT), Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada (AAFC), the former Canada Communication Group (CCG), Transport Canada, the Software Human Resource Council (SHRC), the Canadian Space Agency, and the Health Care Health & Safety Association (HCHSA) of Ontario.

Worked with SNC-Lavalin ProFac, KWA Partners, AiT Corporation, Spar Aerospace, TRW Canada, DMR Group, Dataware Technologies, EDS Canada, Ameridata Canada, Gandalf Canada, Acart Graphics, Valcom, Lockheed Canada, Bendix Avelex and Martin Marietta Canada on a variety of multi-million dollar business development proposals.

Private-sector experience also includes projects on behalf of what is now Allen-Vanguard, ADGA Group, TrueArc, Nortel Networks, Silicon Access Networks, CGI Group, Bell Canada, LINMOR, the Ottawa Heart Institute, Andersen Consulting, and Storgaard & Associates.

Knowledgeable in the terminology and tendering processes of the federal government, NASA, the Canadian, US and British militaries, several foreign governments, NATO and the commercial sector.
