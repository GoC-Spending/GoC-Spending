Company Profile


I have been a creative professional for more than 20 years, 12 of it as a graphic designer in my own consulting practice. I take great pride in providing my clients with personalized service. I employ active listening to suss out their design needs. I love to design creative a practical business solutions in a variety of areas including: developing instructional materials, websites, brochures, online flipbooks, presentations, white papers, newsletters, marketing collateral, direct marketing, e-marketing, social media landing pages, corporate brand identities, logos, business cards, stationery suites and invitations. I also facilitate project management, manage budgets and suppliers/contractors.

I enjoy making other people look good while enabling them to be able to focus on their unique abilities and business responsibilities. I have a high personal need for perfection which keeps me focused on creating high quality deliverables.

On the client side, I work with large corporate clients, business consultants (both internal & independent consultants), marketing professionals, HR / training professionals, instructional designers and small business owners. On the design side, I'm fortunate to be able to collaborate with graphic designers, web developers, writers and translators in my every day business practice.

My primary language is English (spoken/written), my secondary language is French (oh, I wish I was bilingual)! Although I feel my languages may be limited, it has not stopped me from producing successful projects in French, Spanish, Swedish and Urdu!

My creativity and business practice is supported with skills in Adobe Illustrator / Adobe InDesign / Adobe Photoshop / Adobe Acrobat, Adobe Dreamweaver, MSWord & PowerPoint, FTP, Skype, Go-to-Meeting and Quickbooks.
