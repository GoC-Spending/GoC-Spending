Company Profile


Manufacturer of the highest quality conventional and thermal
wellhead systems available today. We have a vast inventory
of wellhead isolation equipment which includes our patented
Isolation Sleeve for high volume/ high pressure well fracturing.
We maintain an ISO 9001 (latest) Registered quality system and
are licensed by the American Petroleum Institute for Spec. 6A
wellhead equipment (PSL 1-4). We also manufacture other ancillary
oilfield products to Canadian Boiler and Pressure Vessel Standards.
(CSA) We offer domestic manufacture, fabrication, customer
inventory control and re-integration management, onsite
installation/service and complete prime contractor project control
for Pad completion initiatives.
