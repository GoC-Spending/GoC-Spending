Company Profile


JPMA was founded by Joseph Paventi in 1968. Today, he continues to guide his family operated business with leadership, inspiration and the highest standards of production. We are now one of the largest store fixture producers in North America. We have more than 600 employees, as well as over a million square feet of manufacturing and warehouse space. However, the strength behind our enterprise goes beyond just manufacturing capacity.

We act as our client's partner in the process of in-store marketing. Bringing together cutting-edge technology and contemporary design, we help attain our customer's full marketing potential. The essence of our success is simple. We are a company built on ideals and innovation; a company that embraces enduring partnerships with satisfied clients and a commitment to ongoing support. We are also one of the 50 best managed companies in Canada

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Custom Metal and Wood Fixtures for Retail Environment
JPMA is at the cutting edge of retail design trends. Our design team develops solutions for the ever-changing retail market. Using the latest computer aided design software we produce 3D models and color renderings throughout the design stages of your product. These visual representations ensure consistency with your vision until completion.

Our superior innovative design and creative solutions are always unique in fulfilling your needs, while minimizing manufacturing lead-time and cost.

Service Name:
Customer Serivice/Assembling and Warehousing/Shipping and Installation
Customer Service
Since 1968, we believe in establishing solid partnerships with our clients. Our ultimate goal is to support you in selling more products. To ensure your success, our team of experts will accompany you through every level of development. Everything from manufacturing and logistics, to design and installations, our qualified personnel will guide you through the process.

Assembly and Warehousing
Our assembly and warehousing facilities cover over 500,000 square feet. By utilizing our three tier high-capacity storage system, we can simultaneously handle multiple large rollout programs.

Shipping and Installation
We work hard on your projects from the beginning to the very end. That is why we make sure your delivery and installations go as smooth as possible. As packaging and shipping specialists, we oversee more than 100 shipping docks and a full fleet of trucks. Experts in Worldwide International Shipping, we are CTPAT Certified, allowing us to efficiently clear customs, saving valuable transit time. We are also experienced in coordinating both large and small installations. Whether you're around the corner or across the globe, you can count on us to finish the job professionally and efficiently.
