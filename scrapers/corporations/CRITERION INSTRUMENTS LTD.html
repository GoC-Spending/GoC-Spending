Company Profile


Criterion Instruments Ltd. has been involved in the manufacturing
of high quality electronic test equipment for public utilities
and industrial applications since 1938, to satisfy the
requirements of the electrical and electronics industries and the
electric utilities.

Criterion Instruments is recognized by CSA, the Electrical
Manufacturing Industry and the Electric Utility sector as a
qualified manufacturer of custom test equipment to satisfy the
ever varying requirements brought about by more stringent test
specifications imposed upon current electrical products for
reasons of improved quality, energy management and user safety
considerations.

Product Name:
Dielectric Strength Testers
The AVC series of breakdown testers has for many years, been a
standard with Canadian electrical manufacturers to assure that
electrical appliances meet the dielectric strength requirements
specified by CSA and UL. Two of our standard models, the AVC-25V
(2500 V ac) and the AVC-50V (5000 V ac) cover more than 95% of
these requirements. Both units offer a continuously adjustable
output voltage to their maximum rated values. All units are
equipped with a high-voltage pilot lamp, power and fault lamps,
automatic cut-out and a heavy duty retractable high-voltage
probe. These units also automatically verify the grounding and
polarity of the supply receptacle.

AVC-25V and AVC-25VA are CSA approved.

Other units, for special applications, are available on request.

Features AC, DC, and AC/DC outputs:

. Adjustable output potentials to 15,000 volts
. Automatic cut-out - wide range of trip current
. Continuously indicating high-voltage voltmeter
. Voltage measured directly at output
. Optional millimeter
. Indicator lamps
. Safety device for incorrectly wired outlet
. Heavy-duty retractable probe (to 5 kV)

For testing to meet UL and CSA specifications.

Other voltages and currents on special order up to 100kV, up to
20 kVA.

Regulated DC Power Supplies
SPECIFICATIONS:

- Unit will operate with +/- 10% change in input voltage.

Output Voltage: - Continuously variable from zero to the

Output current: - Zero to maximum indicated in above table.

Load Regulation: - For changes in load current from zero to

than 0.15% or 50 mV, whichever is greater (at any line voltage

Ripple: - The rms value of the ripple voltage will be no
greater than 0.05% or 20 mV, whichever is greater (at any
line voltage or load current within rating).

Short circuit: - Output may be shorted continuously without
damage to the unit. Power supply recovers
Proof automatically to
normal when short is removed.

Product Name:
Portable Oil Test Set
AVOT-600-A high-voltage test set to establish dielectric
breakdown
of transformer and other insulating oils. This unit features 3
selectable motorized rates of voltage rise to a maximum of 60 kV.
It incorporates a memory voltmeter, which retains the value of
the breakdown voltage. The unit meets ASTM specifications D-877
and D-1816.

Product Name:
Micro-Ohmmeter
Model DOT-100 Micro-Ohmmeter is intended for the four-terminal
measurement of contact resistance and other low resistances, such
as: bus bars, circuit breakers, large relays and contactors, and
fuses. The resistance range is 0.1 æ to 2 (nominal), at a test
current up to 100A dc (filtered). The resistance is calculated
by an analog computer so that the reading is independent of the
current. Current and resistance meters are digital. Shielded
potential leads minimize errors due to stray pickup. The unit is
housed in a portable steel case with carrying handle and
removable cover.

Product Name:
High-Voltage Cable Test Sets
Designed for testing buried cable, switchgear, transformers,
etc.. Available in outputs up to 100 kV dc. Current outputs 5
and 16 mA standard. Single or two-unit construction.

Product Name:
Transformer-Resistance Test Set
Model DOT-20 portable Transformer-Resistance Test Set is intended
primarily for measuring the dc resistance of large inductors,
such as transformer windings, without the long settling time
associated with the usual low-voltage ohmmeters. A switch is
provided to save energy when measuring non-inductive resistances,
such as fuses, resistors, etc.. The resistance range is 1 æ to
200 (nominal). The unit uses a highly regulated test current, a
four-terminal measurement system, and a digital resistance meter.
Two heavy-duty test clips are used, modified the sensing
contacts, so that only the two leads need to be connected to the
item under test. The unit is protected against inductive voltage
spikes if a lead falls off, and it includes a lamp to show when
it is safe to remove the leads after a test is completed.

Product Name:
Relay Test Sets
The RT series of portable relay test sets delivers all commonly
required ac and dc outputs for checking: relays, circuit
breakers, overload protectors and similar devices. A variety of
test circuit options are available. There are three basic
models: RT-0.5, RT-1 and Rt-2 (kVA) Operation is from 120 V, 60
Hz.

Product Name:
Capacitance Bridge
Model TCB-108 Capacitance Bridge is a portable general-purpose
bridge for moderate-accuracy measurements for public utility and
laboratory use for the measurement of capacitors or dielectric
material. It comprises a transformer-type bridge operating from
an internal 108-Hz generator, with digital readout of capacitance
from 1 pF to 100 æF over four ranges, and digital readout of D
(dissipation factor) in percentage from 0 to 50%.

Product Name:
Line-Cord Tester
Model LCT-3 Line-Cord Tester is an automatic test set to check
continuity, polarity and dielectric breakdown on three-wire line
cords.

Continuity resistance is measured and compared to a pre-set value
in the range 0.1 to 0.4. The test limits may be set individually
for each wire.

Dielectric strength is tested for all wire combinations up to
2500 V ac.

Many variations are possible to suit your individual needs.

Product Name:
Hot Sticks
Sometimes called phasing sticks. Manufactured in ranges up to 80
kV. Models HS-5, 15,25,35,50 and 80 kV. High insulation
strength, 3.5 kV/cm. Dual ranges also available - for Utilities
with two distribution voltages on their system and/or to assure
true zero on phasing applications. (For safety, sticks are
always on high range until low range is selected).
Proving Unit- A 5-6 kV AC portable battery powered unit to
verify the operation of Hot Sticks in remote locations.

Product Name:
Safety Tester
Leakage and Continuity Tester for Power Tools and Extension
Cords.

The ST-04 will reveal a hazardous tool or appliance before it is
operated. Checks the supply for ground presence and correct
polarity. The tool or appliance is tested for: "U" ground
continuity to housing, leaking current (phase to ground and
neutral to ground), and current drawn to 25 amperes. The unit is
also equipped with a GFI test circuit.

Product Name:
Panel Meters and Contact Meters
We offer a broad range of ac and dc models, accuracies from 3.0%
to 1.0%. Square and rectangular designs are available from 2-in
to 7-in wide with scaling up to 250ø for high resolution. All
produced in our manufacturing facility, scaled and calibrated to
suit your requirements.

Instrument transformers and shunts are also available.

Contactless contact meters are available for a broad variety of
control and relaying applications.

Precision analogue portable meters are also manufactured.

Product Name:
Power Supplies
A broad variety of low and high voltage and current power
supplies are manufactured to standard designs and for special
applications.

AC: Our ac designs are rugged and include ammeter and voltmeter
as required. Regulated high current designs are available for
special applications, up to 10000 A.

DC: Manufactured in bench-top or rack-mount models, standard and
custom designs are available with both fixed and adjustable
outputs.

Typical Specifications: Line Regulation -ñ0.03% of output
voltage for line variation of ñ10%. Load Regulation - 0.03% from
zero to full load. Ripple - 2mV rms. Short-circuit protection.
Voltmeter and ammeter as required.

Product Name:
Cable Test Sets
The CTS series of Cable Test Sets includes several models
differing from one another in the number of wires, voltage and
current specifications, and other features. Typically, the
standard units can test up to four wires per cable (plus ground
and armour) and up to 10 cables, or 40 wires in a single cable.
Continuity tests are done, and then a hipot test on each wire (up
to 10 kV). The operation is fully automatic with individual
failure-indicator lamps. Many variations are possible on special
order.

Product Name:
Automatic Heater Test Sets
Designed specifically for manufacturers of electrical resistance
heaters, the Model HTS-5 performs all the necessary test required
by CSA and UL for production testing. Dielectric Breakdown -
0-3000 V ac adjustable; trip current - 2-95 mA. Insulation
Resistance - Range 0-100 M_ AT 500 V dc. Element Resistance - to
999.9 Max. Test and Fault Lamps on each test. Provision is made
for individual manual testing.

Custom models are also available.

Product Name:
Custom-Built High-Voltage Testing Equipment
Criterion Instruments Limited specializes in the design and
manufacture of special high-voltage test equipment, for
dielectric strength testing or for other applications.

. Any ac line input.
. Output voltage to 100 kV.
. Rating to 20 kVA (higher on short duty cycle).
. More than 20 special options are available.

Product Name:
Automatic Test Equipment- Custom Designs
A broad variety of both manual and automatic sequential testers
is offered to satisfy the needs of both high and low volume
applications. High voltage, high current, leakage current,
insulation resistance, element resistance, ground continuity,
operation timing are some of the features that can be combined in
these units.
