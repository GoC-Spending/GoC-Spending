Company Profile


3V Communications is owned and operated by Ric Phillips, and works with other coaches, trainers and companies in it's pursuit of excellent communication training. Our mission is to provide the private and public sector with the highest standard in interpersonal and professional communication skill development. Since 2007 we have aided individuals, organizations, government and companies in reaching their communication development goals, sky-rocketing confidence levels, and realizing new potential.

At 3V Communications we know how vital it is to communicate in a clear and confident way that has people paying attention and under your influence. We excel at quickly getting your personal and professional communication to the next level. We train confident communications for executives, managers and staff so you can improve internal messages, persuade clients and win business in your industry.

What Can We do for You?
We offer one-to-one coaching for business individuals and staff, and create highly engaging and interactive corporate training workshops for companies both here and abroad. 3V also creates industry-specific communication assessments for organizations that help evaluate candidates for recruiting and training purposes. All coaching and training uses our unique 3V system that achieves fast, effective results in your verbal, vocal and visual communication messages. We train both native English speakers and non-native English speakers, and always offer customized communication skills development.

Our specialties are effective interpersonal communications, manager communication training, public speaking & presentations, body language analysis and technique, customer service and conflict management, professional office communications for both native and non-native English (ESL) speakers and immigrants, and industry-specific communication assessments.
