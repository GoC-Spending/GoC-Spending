Company Profile


Welcome to an insight of Hike Metal Products Ltd.

Since 1958, and through the early ears, Hike Metal enjoyed modest
but steady growth while bing given support form the local fishing
industry.

The late 1970's and early 80's however brought about the need to
redefine our corporate goals and the strategies by which we were
to achieve those goals.

Therefore, the firm's facilities and infra structure were
subjected to a finely tuned "re-structuring plan."

This upgrading has resulted in providing customers today with
one of the most modern, well equipped shipyard facilities on the
Great Lakes in which they can turn to.

One of our greatest assets, however, is our team of highly
skilled personnel, some having been with our firm since its
conception.

Stressing individual creativity and imitative, we have cultured
an attitude of both pride and performance in every new building
or repair turned out. This is evident throughout each phase of
construction. Our reputation is now internationally renowned for
versatility, quality, reliability and competitiveness.

We're proud of our company and the vessels we build, but we're
especially proud of our people who build them. The value we've
placed on human resources has been a rewarding one, one in which
we attribute our continuing success.

We eagerly look forward to your inquiries. Whether you're
planning new construction or in need of a repair and refit, you
can be assured of our commitment to integrity and quality in
every job we do.

DESIGN & PRODUCTION
Initial discussions with customers establish and define the class
and functional requirements of the vessel. A close working
relationship with the customer right from the beginning ensures
that this phase of an order proceeds with efficiency. This
further develops a more pleasant working relationship and one in
which helps us to accommodate the owner's needs easier.

Our large, modern plant facilities and metal working machinery
enable us to build both large and small vessels simultaneously.
Vessels up to 100 ft. in length can be built completely inside
under heated, environmentally controlled conditions, while larger
vessels are built in sub-sections and assembled on building
berths outside. These facilities are strategically laid out to
afford smooth throughput flow of materials and components from
delivery... to inspection and stocking... to preparation and
incorporation into the vessel allocated.

Systems and installations are pre-engineered before work is put
in hand. This alleviates discretionary work habits during
construction and provides the customer with an efficient and
professional "purpose" built vessel ensuring layouts are
functional and appearance is eye appealing. There is no
compromise.

Close communication kept between estimating, design,
administration and production staff ensure timely and effective
problem solving without interrupted production. What this means
to the customer is a quality product built and delivered on time;
at the best, most competitive price possible.

SUPPORTING SERVICES
As a policy of our continuing commitment and support to this
industry, we provide a full range of other services connected
either directly with new construction and repair, or
consultation.

These include:
- Naval architecture & Marine Engineering - required particularly
when involving complex vessels which require extensive
calculations systems analysis & submissions for classification
purposes, eg. Passenger carrying vessels & yachts.
- Marine Surveying & Inspection - accomplished by eith our own
staff or by independent consultant.
- Non-Destructive Weld Testing - ultrasonic, x-rays
- Electronics & Installations
- Electrical components & Installations
- Sandblasting & paint work
- Interior Joinery Work & Outfitting
- Machine Shop work
- Drydocing, Repairs & Refits
- Mechanical Repairs
- Civil Engineering & Structural Work
- General Fabrications
- Authorized Distributor of Various Marine Components.

Product Name:
Ships, Boats, Aerospace Fabrications
Hike Metal is primarily a shipbuilding/boatbuilding company and
produces vessels ranging in size from 30ft to 150ft in both
welded steel and aluminum construction. Typical vessels include
Patrol boats, crew boats, supply boats, passenger ferries, car
ferries, fishing boats, research vessels, tugs, fireboats, pilot
boats, search and rescue craft, and hovercraft. We also
fabricate and weld aluminum components for the aerospace
industry.
