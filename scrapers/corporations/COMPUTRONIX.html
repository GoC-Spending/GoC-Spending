Company Profile


With over 35 years of business experience, Computronix continues to provide contemporary IT solutions to government and business organizations across North America. Our core competency is implementing flexible, adaptable business process automation and e-business solutions for State, provincial, and large metro regulatory and compliance departments and agencies. Our perpetual, subscription, and Software as a Service (SaaS) licensing models offer competitive, affordable enterprise and point solutions for almost every budget.

Our primary business solutions include:
 POSSE®  business process management software for government business;
 SuperClass®  .Net development software for Web and client applications;
 cxPathFinder  decision tree software;
 Consulting services and custom software solutions.


Computronix is an Oracle Partner, an ESRI Business Partner, a Microsoft Gold Partner, a Sybase iAnywhere Partner, Citrix Business Partner, and a HP Developer and Solution Partner. We also have alliances and business relationships with several other business partners including CGI, MobileHWY, Selectron Technologies, and Can-Am Technologies in order to provide our clients with total business solutions.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
POSSE®
In todays digital world, citizens, customers and agencies expect more from government services. With constrained resources and rising expectations, governments need a focused and powerful solution that delivers.

POSSE® is a Commercial-Off-the-Shelf (COTS) automation software that provides the depth, breadth, and staying power for governments that need a powerful business process automation and IT solution that can grow and change as your business changes.

POSSE enables you to deliver the right customer and business solutions that meet the business and regulatory challenges of today and tomorrow  in the office, in the field, or in the home.

POSSE allows you to capture best practice and expert knowledge in your organization  you are not limited to generic hard-coded processes and you dont need to change your present business processes.

POSSE allows you to create value-added services and potential incremental revenue streams.

With POSSE, you can reduce overall operational and maintenance costs, and reallocate staff to more important work by integrating with other systems and/or decommissioning legacy systems

Service Name:
Consulting Services
Custom Solutions
Since 1979, Computronix has been providing government and industry with state-of-the-art business solutions and services. Computronix is a proven practitioner in consulting, designing, developing and supporting successful browser-based, mobile and smart client applications. The Computronix team has a solid reputation for delivering high-quality, client-focused service. This comes from our technical excellence, innovation, and a team approach to finding the best solution for our clients.

We provide a complete range of services to build and support your business solution, including:

Requirements/needs analysis
Architecture and design
Development and testing
Training and implementation
Systems support.


Web-based Applications
Computronix has been building Web-based applications since 1995. As with all our projects, we strive to provide outstanding business solutions to our clients. We use best-of-breed technologies such as .Net, IIS with ASP and COM, Microsoft Transaction Server and much more, while delivering an easy to navigate, powerful, browser-based interface.

Additional Services
Computronix staff are highly experienced in system integration and data conversion activities. Whether your project requires integrating new systems into an existing system's environment, or transferring existing data into your new Oracle or SQL Server system, Computronix staff can ensure your data and systems needs are met.

Computronix has qualified Oracle DBAs to provide Oracle-based application and database support. Small business and government agencies may not have access to a full-time certified Oracle Database Administrator, but still require Oracle DBA services on a regular basis for routine system maintenance. Computronix can supply these services through a high-speed remote connection.

Infrastructure Consulting
Computronix and Computronix Technologies* can help you define and meet your IT objectives:

Infrastructure design and auditing
Technology deployment and project planning
Microsoft technology consulting
Network design and troubleshooting
Security policy and practices
Backup and Disaster recovery
Managed hosting services*
