Company Profile


Since 1995, K2 Geospatial has been committed to bring spatial information and analysis tools within everyones reach by developing software solutions that provide effective visual aids to decision-makers. These solutions are designed for land, building, infrastructure, and security management purposes. They are used by municipalities, regional governments, ports, airports, public utilities, and natural resource companies.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
JMap
JMap is a map-oriented platform for systems integration  a Web-GIS that is an open, neutral, fast, robust, and scalable tool. JMap simplifies collaboration and information sharing between heterogeneous user groups by allowing systems to converge around the organizations spatial data.

JMap provides a simple way to connect and work with systems that are often complex and operating in silos within organizations.

JMap is designed to meet land, building, infrastructure, environment, and security management needs and is deployed and used by hundreds of organizations in North and South America and Europe. Every day, thousands of employees working in various contexts and facing all sorts of issues and queries use JMap to communicate with each other and share map-related data in real time.

JMap allows processes to run more smoothly and adapts to an organizations existing technical and operational environments. It essentially seats on top of an organizations existing systems and allows a comprehensive view of all of the data contained in them.

JMap is easy to implement and allows organizations to optimize their human, informational, operational, and financial resources.
