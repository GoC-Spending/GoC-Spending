GARDEN - FOREST NATURE TRAILS - TOURS

We offer several different types of tours:
A garden ranger will guide you to learn about the many plants,
ecosystems and trees, along the scenic short tour of 45 minutes,
or the longer more diversified 2 hour tour - A self guided tour
book is available upon request - Overnight tour to eco-cottage:
- camping - fishing - boating - swimming - canoeing - campfire
-music - food provided. View diverse natural plant &
animal ecology
Day trip to picnic: Swim in crystal clear spring fed lake -
Bathe
in natural clay for healthy skin - enjoy plant life and unique
ecosystems of our pure and tranquil wilderness environment-
changing tent and food provided. Bring your camera,
binoculars and good hiking boots.

GARDENING - PLANTS - NATURE EDUCATION CENTRE WORKSHOP -
COURSES

Learn more about: gardening plants - horticulture and the nature
of our forest gardens
Subjects covered:- soil mixes, ph soil tests, - seed plants and
tree planting -dividing perennials - how to propagate biennials -

pruning trees, caring for roses - vegetables & shrubs -
designing
& making flower beds - herb gardens & uses, and more...
Workshops and courses are held at the garden on saturdays:
10:00 AM to 2 PM. Special schedules to suit your needs,
prepared for your group, school, class, club or organization -
includes tour, class room lecture, lunch and workshops.
We arrange courses and workshops for your group at your location
on request.
