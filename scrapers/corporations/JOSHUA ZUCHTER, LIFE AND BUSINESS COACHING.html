Company Profile


We offer professional and custom tailored presentations and workshops for both national and international organizations and corporations as well as business advisory, executive and individual coaching services. Our main areas of focus for presentations include stress reduction, communication skills development, relationship building, conflict resolution, presentation skills development and more.

For our one-to-one work, it is primarily related to the following main areas: business marketing, business strategizing, business start-up, entrepreneurship, creating more life balance, becoming more productive, time management, life and business planning, leadership development, to name just some of the areas within which we focus.

Service Name:
Presentations and Workshops
Since 1997, our presentations have received many endorsements and testimonials. Our presentations have been called dynamic, engaging, powerful, meaningful, touching, inspirational, full of depth, and many others.

We have a selection of topics and formats that will generally suit most requests. We also customize our presentations to the corporation or organization who requests us and follow up with a confirmation proposal so that we are on the same page.

Service Name:
Executive, Business, and Life Coaching
Many Fortune 100 and 500 Companies have coaches for their CEOs, VPs, and in some cases for the rest of their teams.

Today, to thrive in our more internationally based and highly competitive environment and job markets, companies, employees, and individuals are beginning to recognize the value of executive and personal life coaching. If high level athletes work with coaches to achieve higher levels, why wouldn't anyone in any profession do the same?

We can help to increase sales, income levels, and productivity, while at the same time decrease stress. We also improve performance, time management, and help individuals on a personal level to find more life balance. We strive to help our clients thrive for the long-term rather than burn out in the short term.

We also mentor our clients in new, leading edge models of consciousness so that our clients can become more self reliant, less dependent, and more aware so that they can lead themselves to even higher levels.

Mastery is also an important component of our work and that includes reinforcement, goal setting, planning, strategizing, brain storming, and all with confidentiality.

If you are wanting to improve your situation or enhance it, then coaching is a natural progression and fit.
