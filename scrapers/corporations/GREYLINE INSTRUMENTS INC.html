Company Profile


GREYLINE INSTRUMENTS INC. manufactures flow and level instruments with non-contacting, ultrasonic sensors. These products are used in a variety of industries, water and wastewater treatment where flow of liquids or tank level require
automated monitoring or control. The products are distinct because they monitor level or flow without contacting the materials being measured.

Greyline instruments are distributed or represented by a worldwide network of over 80 independent sales agencies. Main activity is in North and South America and the Pacific Rim. Greyline's primary market is in wastewater treatment systems. Customers include municipalities, heavy industrial plants and environmental service industries.

Main industrial markets are pulp and paper, chemical, mining, textile, petroleum, and food processing industries.
Applications range from wastwater flow monotoring to meet environmental regulations, to tank farm automation including level monitoring and control. Municipal markets include water and wastewater treatment plants for flow applications and wastewater influent, effluent, sludge and chemicals, and level applications including pump station level control, and chemical tank recovery.

Greyline products are CSA and CE certified and intrinsically safe sensors are available with many models.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
LEVEL & OPEN CHANNEL FLOW MONITOR
SLT 5.0 LEVEL & FLOW MONITOR
A versatile monitor/transmitter/controller for chemical storage
tanks, pump stations, sumps, or for open channel flow monitoring.
Includes digital displays, 3 control relays, isolated 4-20mA
output, a watertight electronics/display enclosure, and a
non-contacting sensor.

Product Name:
LEVEL INDICATING TRANSMITTER
LIT25 LEVEL INDICATING TRANSMITTER
A simple, low cost level transmitter for tank farm systems.
Includes digital display and non-contacting sensor. Provides
isolated 4-20mA output and one control relay. RS485 output is
optional.

Product Name:
OPEN CHANNEL FLOW MONITOR
OCF 5.0 OPEN CHANNEL FLOW MONITOR
Monitors and totalizes wastewater flow through any flume or
weir. Built in data logger stores daily flow reports (daily
minimum, maximum, average and total flow). USB and isolated
4-20mA output. Includes non-contacting sensor and watertight
enclosure.

Product Name:
FLOW METERS, NON-CONTACTING
DFM 5.1 DOPPLER FLOW METER
Monitors flow from outside a pipe, with a strap-on sensor. Ideal
for wastewater, slurries, sludge, chemicals, and viscous liquids.
Standard sensor fits any pipe 1/2" ID or larger.
Includes digital display, totalizer, 2 control relays and
isolated 4-20mA output. A 2 million point data logger is optional.

Product Name:
FLOW METERS, PORTABLE
PDFM 5.0 PORTABLE DOPPLER FLOW METER
Strap-on ultrasonic sensor installs in minutes to measure flow from outside a pipe. Ideal for troubleshooting 'difficult' flows. Includes
backlit display, totalizer, data logger, 4-20mA and USB outputs.
Battery powered, carrying case.

Product Name:
FLOW SWITCH, NON-CONTACTING
DFS 5.1 DOPPLER FLOW SWITCH
Controls flow from outside a pipe with strap-on sensor. Ideal for pump protection, high and low flow alarms and includes bargraph flow rate display plus DPDT control relay with adjustable On/Off set-points.

Product Name:
PUMP STATION LEVEL CONTROLLER
PSL 5.0 - PUMP STATION LEVEL CONTROLLER
Monitors, displays, controls and transmits wet well level for sewage and water pump stations. Includes a non-contacting, ultrasonic sensor and accepts a redundant sensor input for reliable operation plus 6 control relays and pump run time reports.


Product Name:
PORTABLE ULTRASONIC FLOWMETER
PTFM 1.0 Portable Transit Time Flow Meter
Measure clean fluids from the outside of metal or plastic pipes. Displays, totalizes and datalogs. Includes clamp-on ultrasonic transducers and a rugged, watertight carry case.

Product Name:
METER - WASTE WATER
Product Name:
MONITOR - LEVEL
Product Name:
AREA-VELOCITY FLOW
AVFM 5.0 Area-Velocity Flow Meter
Measures fluid level and velocity to calculate flow. Includes a
submerged ultrasonic sensor installed at the bottom of a pipe
or channel. Recommended for wastewater, irrigation water and
stormwater flow measurement. Displays, totalizes and transmits.

Product Name:
DIFFERENTIAL LEVEL CONTROLLER
The Greyline DLT 2.0 Differential Level Transmitter includes two non-contacting ultrasonic sensors to measure level. With sensors positioned above a channel, up and downstream from the barscreen, the DLT 2.0 can display and transmit differential level. The downstream sensor can also be installed above a flume or weir to measure and totalize open channel flow.

Product Name:
ULTRASONIC FLOW METER
TTFM 1.0 TRANSIT TIME FLOWMETER
With clamp-on ultrasonic transducers the TTFM 1.0 measures flow of clean fluids from the outside of metal or plastic pipes. Ideal for water, chemicals and oils. Displays, totalizes and controls. Includes a user-friendly, multi-language menu systems for calibration and set-up.

FSC Code:
6680-Liquid and Gas Flow, Liquid Level, and Mechanical Motion Measuring Instruments
