Company Profile


Creative Post: Where creativity meets technical excellence.

Since opening the doors to our downtown Toronto facility in 1985, Creative Post has established
itself as one of Canadas premier post production facilities.
Known for our industry-leading technology, innovative thinking and roster of top tier talents,
Creative Post is committed to bringing your vision to life  on time and within your budget.
We offer full-service post, interactive development, duplication and media distribution services.
Exceeding your expectations and making your job easier is our primary goal.
Our clients include some of the industrys largest film and broadcast companies, and multiple Fortune 500 corporations. They continue to trust us with their projects because of our proven track record of consistently delivering outstanding work.
Creative Post is on the forefront of post production technology and innovations. Our inspired work environment exemplifies where creativity meets technical excellence.

Service Name:
Post Production
Post Production Services

At Creative Post, we pride ourselves on being on the forefront of post production innovations, from SD to HD to 4K to IMAX. Our editors are dedicated to their craft, and committed to bringing your vision to life in our wide variety of comfortable, cutting-edge Edit Suites.

Our spacious High Def and Standard Def suites are designed to accommodate the various stages of post production for film, broadcast series, specials, MOWs, documentaries, commercials, corporate and music videos and more.

Creative Post is one of the industrys most sought-after providers of dailies to deliverables. We offer same-day or next day turnaround, and support all formats. We can take your 16mm or 35mm film and transfer it to any editable format, and supply set copies on DVD or online as required. We can process Red Cam set material and provide edit-ready files and set copies for all projects. We also offer post-synching services for all shot formats, and we can supply on set data wrangling and/or colour management for all digital image capture formats.

Service Name:
Film Transfer & Colour Correction
Film Transfer/ Colour Correction

Creative Post is one of Canadas best-equipped film transfer and colour correction facilities. Our skilled technicians use state-of-the-art Data Mills and Millennium and Rank scanners to transfer your material to virtually any format you may require, from HDCAM SR to edit-ready hard-drives and DVD screeners. We use the Pablo, Pablo Neo, DaVinci and Avid Nitris HD colour grading systems to perform whatever correction tasks you may need  from a basic best light pass to a fully supervised final colour correct.

Creative Post can work with all older film and video formats, including ¾ tape and 8mm  35mm film, to convert them to current HD standards and film formats. We can handle all your material  new or old  to convert to your choice of digital archive format and store it for the future.

We can also take a complete inventory of Masters or any video footage you may want archived and process it at the best quality available using our Digital Asset Management System.

Authoring Services

A continuous upgrade program of our high-end video equipment ensures you always have access to the best technology available.
