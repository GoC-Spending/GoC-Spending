Company Profile


Spaceballs Canada is located in Central Canada.
Our product is used by Cabinet Door Manufacturers, Kitchen and
Vanity Cabinet Makers and Furniture Manufacturers across Canada,
because of our location shipping to either coast line is
effortless. Spaceballs are small in size, therefore shipping
is very inexpensive. Our company has the ability to serve the
small, medium and large manufacturers and we are always willing
to customize our service for each customer.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Spaceballs
Spaceballs are used in the manufacturing of solid panel wood
doors. They fit in the rail and stile quite easily and fast
and they don't fall out. Once they are in the frame it's back to
standard door construction.

What spaceballs do is KEEP THE PANEL CENTERED, no more problems
with those unsightly lines when the panel moves in or during
the finishing process which is a problem that is time consuming
to fix and everyone knows time is MONEY!

Spaceballs also COMPRESS AND EXPAND. They are made with a
material that has a memory to return to its original shape and
size. This is the reason Spaceballs work well with wood,
considering wood changes with the different seasons.

Spaceballs also eliminate the CHEAP RATTLING SOUND the door
makes when you close it. This rattling is due to the drier
period
and is very annoying. With spaceballs in the door frame you
don't
have to make the panel so tight or pin the panel to stop the
rattle or to keep it centered which also solves our next problem
the CRACKING JOINTS. This happens because the door was made too
tight to keep it centered for the finishing. When the wood
expands due to humidity changes, it causes the door to crack
near
glue joints. When you use spaceballs you don't have to make the
panel as tight in the slot and the balls will hold the panel
centered. The panel will not rattle when you close the door
either.

The recommended amount of Spaceballs per door is 8-10.
Spaceballs will CUT OUT MOST MAJOR CALLBACKS on solid flat or
raised panel doors.
