FIRMEX COMPANY PROFILE

Firmex is the leader in helping organizations securely share confidential information and collaborate online.

Since 2006 Firmex has been licensing Online Virtual Data Room solutions for corporate transactions, governance and compliance matters. Firmex focus on delivering highly secure, reliable, fast and intuitive technology with top tier 24/7 client support.

Firmex Customers:

Our key client segments are Investment banking, Legal, Private equity, Life Sciences, Real-Estate and Resources. Clients are primarily located in the US, Canada and Europe, and range from boutique organizations to global investment banks, law firms and fortune 500 companies.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Virtual Data Room for Online Document Sharing & Collaboration
What is Firmex?
***********
Firmex is a Virtual Data Room (VDR or Online Deal Room) that can be used to collect, organize and post documents online quickly and easily so multiple parties anywhere in the world can access documents in a secure and controlled environment. Firmex also extends the traditional file sharing capability of a VDR to include the management of versioning documents and closing transactions.

What you get with Firmex:
********************

* One low price
* Branded website
* Unlimited projects
* Unlimited guest users
* SAS Type II Storage
* 256 bit encryption
* Water marking
* Automatic email alerts
* Password Management
* Audit reports
* Free training
* Free 24/7 support
