Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Custom Database Systems
Our customers most frequently come to us because theyre frustrated with inefficiencies in the way their business runs: redundant paperwork that has to be filed in three different places, data entry that takes forever to do (and always gets left to the last minute), and reports that everyone is sick to death of filling out.

These kinds of tasks suck up valuable time, and because theyre boring and tedious, mistakes often crop up. Even worse, despite having all this data filed six ways from Sunday, its impossible to get any kind of big picture because theres just too much data to sift through.

Custom database systems are one of the biggest money-saving solutions we provide for our clients. Our systems automate tasks, integrate with other systems, reduce redundancies and put all your data in one centralized location. Not only does this cut down on work across the board (freeing your employees up for tasks that are a far better use of their expertise), it allows you to get a better sense of how your company is performing overall and how you can improve as you grow.

If this sounds like something your business could use, wed like the opportunity to take a look at the way your business works now and come up with an estimate of how much money a custom database could save your company. Were confident youre going to like the looks of that ROI.

Service Name:
Android and iPhone App Development (Enterprise Mobile Apps)
We love telling clients about mobile apps, because most of them dont realize that its possible to create apps just for your business. If your employees are frequently out in the field or traveling from city to city, theyre often without critical information thats only stored back at the office, or on the companys private servers. With mobile apps, you can give them access to that information anywhere and anytime  saving time, money, and frustration.

Getting that information in real time gives your employees the freedom to react immediately. They can give the client the numbers they need right away, even if they didnt anticipate the question beforehand. They can find out how to solve a problem in the field within seconds, even if theyve never encountered it before. It works both ways, too: employees in the field can enter data into the app in real time, giving your office an update right away  not two weeks from now when the paperwork is finally filed.

Mobile apps reduce redundancy, improve efficiency, and give your employees the power to be everywhere at once. Your best sales guy might have been in Chicago during the last big meeting, but he got the highlights that same day  and he is now armed with that information before he meets with that big potential client.

We create user-friendly interfaces based on the technology your employees are already familiar with, making it easy for them to use the mobile app  which makes it much more likely they will embrace it and start using it for all its worth. We know theres no point to an app no one uses, so we make sure theyre usable.
