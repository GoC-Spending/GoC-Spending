Product Name:
Zoho CRM
Zoho CRM empowers organizations with a complete customer relationship lifecycle management solution for managing organization-wide Sales, Marketing, Customer Support & Service and Inventory Management in a single business system.

FEATURES
Sales & Marketing: Zoho CRM aligns your Sales and Marketing by integrating sales with campaigns, leads, sales pipeline, forecasts, etc.

CRM with Email
With the Zoho Mail add-on you can track all your customers email correspondence inside Zoho CRM.

Plug-in for Microsoft Outlook & Office
Improve business productivity by synchronizing contacts and creating mail merge docs.

Inventory Management
Zoho CRM empowers you to complete the post-sales activities with an integrated inventory management system.

CRM API for Third-party Integration
Developer API for integrating CRM modules with third-party business applications such as accounting, ERP, etc.

Extensive Product Customization
Drag-and-drop UI tools for customizing Zoho CRM as per your business process.

Role-based Security
Zoho CRM empowers you to control the CRM data access based on users' job role in your organization hierarchy.

Reports & Dashboards
Zoho CRM helps you to analyze sales & marketing trends and key CRM metrics by reports and dashboards.

BENEFITS
1. Award-winning, complete CRM solution. Zoho CRM offers all the modules and tools that you might need to run your sales & marketing.

2. Value for your money. We not only provide a great CRM solution, but also have a great price too. Professional Editon is only $20 /month, per user. No strings attached!

3. No long term contracts, pay as you go. You are not bound by any long, yearly contracts. You have the flexibility of paying monthly, quarterly or yearly.

4. It's all hosted. When you choose Zoho CRM, we do the hard work for you. You do not have to buy expensive servers, software, backup solutions, etc. Just focus on selling and leave the rest to us.

5. Ease of Customization. We understand your need to customize our service to your specific needs. Our drag-and-drop customization helps you get things done fast.

6. It's not just CRM. When you create a Zoho CRM account, you also get access to the Zoho suite of products - from online storage and web meetings to other business products, such as project management, invoices and more.

Product Name:
Zoho Projects
Zoho Projects is a web based project management, collaboration and issue tracking software that allows teams to collaborate and get things done. Planning and Tracking helps you keep your project on schedule. Collaboration helps improve the communication within the team and keep everyone on the same page. And the new Bug Tracking module helps your team iron out the very last details.

1. Task Management: Keep your projects organized with milestones. Set dependencies for your tasks, assign ownership and priorities. With Zoho Projects planning your projects becomes a snap.

2. Bug Tracking Software: Our bug tracker combines a clean interface to organise issues with customisable workflows, set business rules and fix bugs which makes bug tracking painless and simple.

3. Time Tracking Software: With timesheets you can record your working hours, export data and quickly generate reports in no time. But it doesn't stop there you can send invoice and follow your clients for payments.

4. Project Reports (Gantt Charts): Our reports visually represents how your team is progressing against pre-defined goals. You can track and report exactly how much time you're spending on what tasks for which clients.

5. Project Calendar & Meetings: Project Calendar gives you the power to revolutionize the way your project team works. In additon, you can schedule meetings, gather notes, post links all from within Zoho Projects.

6. Online Project Chat: The always-on built-in chat room allows everyone on the project to quickly come together to discuss important topics and make decision faster. Best of all, it runs entirely on your browser.

7. Project Wiki (Intranet): Wikis offer a simple and quick way for anyone to create web pages and publish them for anyone to see. Zoho Projects offers an integrated wiki interface for your projects with seamless navigation.

8. Document Management: Documents area provides a centralized place where you can store and organize files in folders. You can tag your files with intuitive labels and keep track of changes with the version history.

Product Name:
Zoho Invoice
Zoho Invoice is an easy-to-use online invoicing software that helps you send invoices for your projects and customer expenses, receive payments and track the overall health of your business quickly and efficiently. It makes invoicing and following-up really easy - reducing the time it takes you to manage the paperwork and get paid, so you can remain focused on your customers and your business.

1. Manage Invoices Online: Create and send professional invoices. Track them and follow up for payment. Let your invoices look great; decide their look and feel by using our default templates or design one that matches your corporate identity.

2. Track Expenses: Get an accurate picture of your financial position by recording expenses. Invoice the reimbursable expenses, such as travel or materials, to your clients with ease and get them reimbursed.

3. Invoice in Multiple Currencies and Languages: Transact globally with Zoho Invoice's multi-currency and multi-lingual capabilities. Record foreign currency invoices and expenses without compromising on decimal precision. Send a Facture to your French client and a Rechnung to your German customer.

4. Accept Payments Online: What good is sending invoices if you don't get paid? Use Zoho Invoice and get paid faster. Encourage your customers to pay online using popular gateways like Paypal. Track over-payments and customer advances by recording customer credits.

5. Stay on Top of Your Business: Our reports offer critical insights into how your business is doing. See what sells most, who are your top paying customers, the average time it takes your customers to pay and who owes you what and when.

6. Invite, Share and Collaborate: Having a lot of invoicing to do can only be a good thing! But you need not do all of that yourself. Zoho Invoice support multiple users with different permissions, so you can delegate some of the workload.

7. Safe and secure: Our servers are housed in most secure data centers. We use 256 bit SSL encryption, state of the art infrastructure and software security to ensure the safety of your data. Your data is regularly backed up and replicated across secure locations.

8. Remain in control of your data: Use our APIs to mash up Zoho Invoice with any of your business applications. You also have complete freedom to import or export your data from the system whenever you want.

9. Access anywhere, anytime: Traveling for business or pleasure? You can access your data anywhere, anytime. All you need is a web browser and an internet connection. Its that simple!
