Company Profile


Ivation Datasystems Inc.

Founded in 1987 by a group of experienced professionals, Ivation
Datasystems Inc. had been a boutique consulting services firm,
specializing in statistics, micro-simulation and socio-economic
computing. In 2001 Ivation Datasystems changed names and
became Beyond 20/20 Inc. Based in Ottawa, Canada, Beyond 20/20 today maintains a
long-term service relationship with a number of customers,
including Statistics Canada, which has among the highest of
reputations among statistical agencies worldwide.

In 1988, the long experience of Beyond 20/20's founders in
socio-economic computing permitted them to recognize the
opportunity for innovation work in the development of
multi-dimensional software tools. On the basis of early successes
and encouragement from customers, Beyond 20/20 made the business
decision to build upon its successful consulting practice by
launching a software product, also called Beyond 20/20.
Deliberately, the company maintains a complementary consulting
practice, for financial stability and as a source of knowledge,
experience and depth, feeding the product development business.

Beyond 20/20 Product Suite

Used worldwide by statistical agencies and individuals who need
to find the knowledge held in statistics, Beyond 20/20 is the
leading software product for accessing, tabulating and exploring
statistics in multiple dimensions. Beyond 20/20 offers unique
characteristics and superior performance to manage, visualize,
examine, manipulate and disseminate socio-economic statistics.
Easily integrated to support and leverage traditional processes,
Beyond 20/20's speed and simplicity of use provides measurable
productivity improvements.

The Beyond 20/20 Browser is the simplest, fastest user interface
for dealing with socio-economic statistics, including both
aggregated data and microdata. Agencies worldwide use Beyond
20/20 to disseminate statistics on trade, census, health, crime,
justice, education, literacy, the labor market, financial
indicators and other types of socio-economic data via
CD-ROM, and the World Wide Web. Beyond 20/20 also
delivers solutions based on Microsoft OLAP cubes and Reporting
Services.

Professional Consulting Services Group

In recent years, Beyond 20/20's Professional Consulting Services Group
has developed a notable expertise in micro-simulation, as an aid
to public policy formulation. In addition, the company has
successfully completed assignments in demographic, occupational,
and financial modeling; socio-economic programming; statistical
computing; time-series and demographic data management and
retrieval; portfolio management; human resources planning; and
real-time data management.

For more information about Beyond 20/20 please visit our homepage on
the Internet at http://www.beyond2020.com

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Beyond 20/20 Web Data Server
Beyond 20/20 Inc. develops, markets and fully supports Beyond 20/20, the unique and acclaimed data browser and publishing product for easy viewing, manipulation and dissemination of socio-economic data in multiple dimensions. It is a remarkable software product for handling multi-dimensional data. It is currently used by leading national statistical agencies, prestigious international organizations, state agencies and other originators of socio-economic data to manage and deliver statistics to their internal and external customers.

The Beyond 20/20 Browser is the simplest, fastest user interface for dealing with socio-economic statistics, including both aggregated data and microlevel data. Agencies worldwide use Beyond 20/20 to disseminate statistics on trade, census, health, crime, justice, education, literacy, the labor market, financial indicators and other types of socio-economic data via CD-ROM and the World Wide Web.


Product Name:
Beyond 20/20 Perspective
Beyond 20/20 Perspective is an OLAP solution suite that leverages the power of Microsoft technologies for web-based analysis and visualization of data. Perspective demonstrates true seamless integration of browser, OLAP, and relational elements, and through the efforts of our data/operational experts delivers a one-stop OLAP solution.

Predefined and ad-hoc reporting
Tabular and graphic displays
Ad-hoc calculations
Sorting, ranking, filtering, and exception highlighting
Nest and drag-and-drop data manipulation
Intuitive drilldown and drillthrough
Extensive descriptive metadata, including footnotes and summaries
Download to XLS, XML, CSV, and CUB
Multi-level security access
