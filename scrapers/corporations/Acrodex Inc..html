Company Profile


Acrodex Inc. is an end to end IT solutions provider, enabling large-enterprise, mid-tier and small organizations to grow, innovate and gain competitive advantages. Our solution-based services are founded on our core competencies, including hardware procurement, software licensing, managed services and our infrastructure-as-a-service cloud offering.

Our national professional services include:

Enterprise Systems Solutions
Enterprise Network
Enterprise Server Solutions
Enterprise Virtualization and Enterprise Storage
Unified Communications and Collaboration

In business for over 25 years, Acrodex leverages the experience and partnerships with IT industry leaders to bring best-fit, cost-effective IT solutions to businesses. Our offices are located in: Edmonton, Calgary, Ft. McMurray, Regina, Winnipeg and Toronto.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Virtualization

Acrodex specializes in providing end-to-end virtualization solutions for enterprises and SMBs. From server to infrastructure to desktop virtualization, we can assess your existing IT infrastructure, recommend need-based improvements, and then move to the planning and implementation of an appropriate solution. We also offer remote server and IT management.

We offer certifications with industry-leading vendors and manufacturers, including Gold Certified partnership status with Microsoft®, Cisco®, Citrix® and NetApp®, Converged Infrastructure Elite status with Hewlett Packard®, and we are a VMware® Enterprise Solution partner.





Service Name:
Cloud Computing

Our hosted virtual infrastructure combines network, storage and compute into a bundle, all hosted in a data center environment and often managed by the service provider, although businesses owning the infrastructure can choose to manage it themselves.



Service Name:
Managed Services

Acrodexs suite of Managed Services offerings are ideally tailored for businesses of any size. These services allow us to take care of your IT needs so you can focus on your core business. Acrodex has helped clients of all sizes, in both the private and public sector.

Managed Print Solutions
Our Managed Print Solution allows you to gain control over printing and imaging costs, improve your return on IT investments, minimize support costs and maximize network and device uptime  all at a lower cost per page.

Enterprise Software Licensing Services
We bring expertise on most volume licensing programs from major software publishers as well as a number of niche software solutions specific to our client needs.

Software Asset Management
The Acrodex Software Asset Management (SAM) Services offering includes a multi-disciplinary consultative approach to software asset management and license compliance where we consider a program as a joint partnership between ourselves and your organization.

Service Desk
Our Service Desk offering allows your internal IT staff to focus on higher value tasks and company-specific line of business application support while we take care of day-to-day tasks.

Desktop, Network, and Server Management
Acrodexs managed services combine proven practices, tools, and IT expertise to deliver efficient, cost-effective management of your IT infrastructure. We can provide both remote and onsite managed services.

Staff Augmentation
Our Staff Augmentation services leverage Acrodexs 27 years of IT experience to understand our clients needs and provide best-fit subject matter experts in a timely fashion; projects range from virtualization to infrastructure deployments and custom IT projects.





















Service Name:
Unified Communications

Our end-to-end business productivity solutions give your organization that competitive edge by optimizing the way your team members communicate, share knowledge, gain business insight, and organize information and deliver regardless of physical location and time differences.

Unified Communications
Our team can help your team communicate more effectively with the integration of IP Phones, Smart Phones, Voice Mail, Email, Video, Web Conferencing, Instant Messaging (IM) and Calendaring.

Mobility
Acrodex's mobility management solutions can help assess, plan, order, secure and support devices in your environment.
