Company Profile


The Center provides the highest quality psychological care to individuals, families and children in need of guidance through emotional difficulty, who is presenting a learning disability, or who is exhibiting some delays in the developmental milestones. We are a group of very qualified and capable doctoral level psychologists and neuropsychologists with numerous years of experience between us. We also have access to several outside services and individuals in related fields that will further ensure the complete care of our patients. Whether it is in speech-language pathology, occupational therapy, audiology, resource teaching or psychiatry, we will be able to help you find the appropriate specialist to assist you no matter what your need are.

Service Name:
Neuropsychological assessments
Neurophychology is the study of our brains different abilities. It is a crossroad discipline that is born primarily of the interaction between neurology and psychology. In this sense, the neuropsychological assessment is a diagnostic process that seeks to highlight both the strongest and the weakest aspects of an individuals cognitive functioning. To do this, the neuropsychologist measures and describes various aspects of our cognitive functioning using specialised and standardized tools. For instance, our tools allow us to assess a person's intellectual functioning, memory, attention, language, motor development, processing speed, perception, and many other functions that we take for granted in our daily lives.

Subsequently, this information enables us to develop the cognitive profile of the patient, a diagnosis and a full and comprehensive report concerning these functions. Our report thus becomes an indispensable tool through which the patient may receive accommodations and / or specialized services in hospitals, rehabilitations center or even in schools. The neuropsychological assessment is often the starting point for interventions, since it covers the entire cognitive development of an individual, and thus serves as a guide for the individual, the parents and/or all other caregivers in our healthcare system.

Service Name:
Psychological services
Our caregivers aim is to provide the highest quality psychological care to individuals, families and children. Our staff offers a vast variety of evaluation and intervention services that are in line with the best practices in the field of psychology. Our approach is holistic and multidisciplinary and therefore we also collaborate with other professionals involved in the care of our patients such as: doctors, speech and language pathologists, teachers, for example.

We will help you through the consultation process. We recognize that seeking psychological help can be overwhelming for many reasons. Often people do not know exactly what type of evaluation or therapy would be most appropriate for them or their family members, so it can be difficult to reach out for help or to find the right professional that meets your needs. Our professionals understand these issues and are available to answer all your questions and to guide you in this process. Once your needs are understood, our psychologists and neuropsychologists will guide you with regards to a personalized action plan. You can be reassured that you will have the appropriate services you seek for your wellbeing, or that of a loved one.

Service Name:
Remedial Tutoring
The Centre now offers remedial services for all students from age 6 to 17, as well as for adults completing their high school education. These services can come in the form of traditional tutoring, as well as an innovative program called Cellfield Canada.

We have introduced resource teachers to our team who will provide individualized services for all students who have learning difficulties or disabilities. Regular follow ups in remedial teaching will allow students to catch up on their learning, to avoid accumulating more academic delays over time and prevent academic demotivation.
The specialised Cellfied program is for students who continuously find themselves falling behind in reading due dyslexia for example, and/or students that have found little or no success in traditional tutoring. Cellfield addresses the heart of the problem making it an intervention method rather than a band-aid solution to a complex problem.
All the services mentioned above are available six days a week, from Monday through to Saturday. The schedule is adapted to fit your schedule, as well as that of the student. We look forward to meeting with you.

Service Name:
Pharmaceutical consultation
In our profession, we come across many cases that require medication, and as such, many of our clients have questions and express concern pertaining to the medication that their children or they themselves are taking. Many have questions regarding the action mechanism and why a specific medication is recommended rather than another or whether there are side effects. Other clients use natural health products and want to know if they are compatible with their recommended treatment or that of their child.

These are all valid concerns and excellent questions. This is why we have developed a service wherein our clients can consult directly with our in-house pharmacist who can address any concerns regarding medications. With experience in both pediatric and adult care, our pharmacist will definitely enlighten and guide you in your pharmaceutical interventions. Our pharmacist works closely with our psychologists and neuropsychologists, as well as all other varieties of caregivers to meet your needs such as: physicians, psychiatrists, speech-language pathologists, occupational therapists, psychoeducators, social workers, etc.
