Company Profile


Greely Accounting Services provides tangible and intangible value to our clients. Our objective is to help small and medium-size businesses grow. We don't look at the business from a historical perspective as many ordinary accounting firms do, but actively work with you to develop the business and improve it.

We provide quality accounting services to small and medium size companies in the Ottawa area. Our staff can provide advice to owners on how to maximize their small business tax deductions and minimize the amount of tax.

Thinking of growing your business, we can help you decide on which strategies work best for your business.


Our focus is achieved through practical, customer focused, strategic solutions developed in partnership with our clients. We accomplish this by providing clearly defined, highly structured and extremely responsive services. We bring peace of mind for our clients
We work to make the complex understandable
We act in an advisory role to our clients that enable them to make better informed business decisions
We desire to help our clients make more money and sleep better


Greely Accounting Services excel in meeting and exceeding the needs of entrepreneurs and business owners. We work closely with our clients to provide insight and assistance into the controls and financial management of their business.

Bookkeeping and Accounting

Careful record keeping is essential. It is a challenge to maintain accurate supporting records and receipts for tax, managerial, and financial statement purposes. Allow Greely Accounting Services keep the books and accounts, and you can focus your energies where they are needed.
If you are planning to finance the expansion of your business; if you decide to merge or sell; if you want to know how well your business is doing, proper bookkeeping is imperative.
Budgets


Budgeting is a key component of a successful business. In particular; if your revenues are relatively stable from year-to-year, then careful budgeting, combined with ongoing monitoring of actual income and expenses, can help control your expenditures. Contact Greely Accounting Services and allow us help you prepare your budgets.

Cash Flow Projections

Financial Statement Preparation


Regular financial statements are necessary for a companys fiscal health. Generally prepared monthly, these statements provide an up-to-date view of your companys assets and liabilities, and your revenue and expenses.

Well-prepared financial statements indicate much more than just actual figures. If you are working with a budget, these statements can reveal the discrepancies between anticipated and actual results. A recommended monthly financial statement can highlight areas of concern in time for you to take action.
