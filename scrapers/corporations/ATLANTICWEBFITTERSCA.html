Company Profile


Atlantic WebFitters is a Nova Scotia based developer and distributor of Browser-Based Software. This emerging technology allows organizations to establish Internet websites, corporate Intranets, information portals and storefront delivery systems. Companies maintain the content of these systems easily and quickly without specialized computer skills.
