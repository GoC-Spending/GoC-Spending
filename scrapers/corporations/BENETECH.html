Company Profile


Benetech, since 1988, has focused on medical software
development. The companys expertise in software development in
the insurance industry resulted in our being approached to
create a prenatal screening program. Presented with other
opportunities within the medical field we have expanded our
software development to include new and innovative programs as
well as consulting and custom development unique to the
individual client. In addition to expanding our products based
on suggestions and feedback from our clients, Benetech
introduces custom pieces that would appeal to our target market
based on our existing product line. Benetech has developed
software to improve laboratory efficiency and productivity for
North York General Hospital, the Childrens Hospital of Eastern
Ontario and the Toronto Hospital Genetic Repository. Software
developed by Benetech is used worldwide.

Our services reflect the most current concepts and practices in
the industry. Considerable effort is spent regularly upgrading
our products and technical skills to keep up with the latest
trends in clinical practice.

Benetechs values are demonstrated by the dedicated service we
deliver to our clients and the community, and by our commitment
to integrity and to quality personal and professional
development for our employees. The relationship we have with
each of our clients is based on mutual trust and respect.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Benetech PRA
Benetech PRA is an expert database used in prenatal screening
programs. It takes the result of multiple markers from maternal
serum in both first and second trimesters of pregnancy, as well as AFP from amniotic fluid in second trimester and Nuchal Translucency (NT) in first trimester. It then calculates a risk factor for Down syndrome, Open Spina Bifida, Trisomy 18 and Smith-Lemli-Opitz syndrome; and generates an interpretive report for the physician.

Service Name:
Shire's Genetic Software
Benetech is the American distributor of Shire's Genetic Software
which is comprised of modules for cytogenetic, clinical,
molecular and biochemical genetics, and prenatal screening, the
ideal solution to the administrative requirements of a modern
genetics department.
