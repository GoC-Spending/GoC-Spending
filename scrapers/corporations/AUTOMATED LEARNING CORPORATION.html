Company Profile


ALC is the leader in just-in-time training for various industries including healthcare and manufacturing
assembly, providing both custom development and access to an inventory of LearnTech® training courses. Courses can be provided to your workforce via one or more of the following methods: installed on your corporate LMS, accessed from from a hosted LMS, installed onto a local server c/wa local Automated Curriculum Manager, delivered as DVD video.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
LearnTech® Interactive Training
Under the LearnTech® label ALC develops interactive process and skills training courses for management, technical or workforce audiences. These are delivered via LAN or WEB. Current topics in the LearnTech® Manufacturing Suite include ESD (8 titles), safety (Laser Safety, Workplace Hazardous Materials. MSDS, Eye Wash, Lead Hazard Control, Lockout/Tagout, Ergonomics in the Workplace), LEAN: (Lean Concepts, Lean Tools, Value Stream Mapping, 5S Organization, Visual Controls, Total Productive Maintenance, Quick Changeover, Kaizen Events, Mistakeproofing.) Solder and Rework, Lead-Free Soldering (4 titles), Component Identification (12 titles), Circuit Card Assembly Introduction, Mechanical Assembly, Inventory Practices, Ergonomic Seating, RF training (Radio Frequency Basics), Controlled Goods training (ITAR, CGP). Some titles are also available in Spanish, French, German, Chinese, and/or Korean. All 9 Lean courses are available in Traditional and Simplified Chinese.
Interactive courses related to Lean Healthcare focus on: Lean Concepts, Value Stream Mapping, 5S, Mistakeproofing, Kaizens, and Flow. These are designed to support full workplace training. We offer both Yellow Belt on-line Certification and Green Belt Certification in Lean Healthcare.

We are constantly updating and expanding these off-the-shelf libraries. If you don't see what you need, let's build it together. Ask about our special offer where you can really stretch you budget with a partnering that combines licences in existing courseware and development of approved new courses.

Product Name:
Custom e-Learning Development
ALC takes pride in our track record for delivering quality e-learning programs on time, on spec, and on budget. If you need a custom designed training program, we can deliver. We are a leading e-Learning development company focused on workforce skills training. In addition to our off-the-shelf courses, we have extensive design and development capabilities to customize existing products to meet your specific content and graphics requirements. Or we can design, develop, and deliver full-custom e-Learning solutions that meet your specific training needs in any subject. Our developers are experts in creating high quality, interactive, full-media, self-paced training and certification that really works.

ALC courses can interface with industry standard Learning Management Systems (LMS) using AICC or SCORM. Our courses can also come complete with built-in Automated Curriculum Manager (ACM), or Learning Content Management System (LCMS). We have the solution to match your delivery platform.

Service Name:
Consulting Services
Consulting in Training, , Training Design, and E-learning.

ALC also refers trusted consultants in Lean Production, ESD Control, Solder and Rework Technology.

We enjoy collaborating with trusted clients in the real-world workplace to deliver programs that meet their needs. In addition to a wide offering of available training courses, we also offer expert consultation in planning, development and implementation.
