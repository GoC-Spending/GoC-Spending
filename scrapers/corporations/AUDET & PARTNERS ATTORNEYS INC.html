Company Profile


A DIFFERENT APPROACH TO BUSINESS LAW

Over fifteen years as Senior Executive to publicly held corporations have allowed Mr. Audet to develop a legal practice based first and foremost on a business perspective. His nine years as Vice-President of Legal Affairs at Groupe Coopérant Inc., a Québec-based financial institution with consolidated assets worth over one billion dollars, and his five years as Vice-President of Corporate Affairs at Télé-Québec have allowed Mr. Audet to gain exceptional insight into the business world. Prior to applying a legal solution to a problematic, executives prefer to consider business solutions. Such is Mr. Audets approach.

Another meaningful distinction: Mr. Audet acts in the interest of the client rather than the case. The strategic vision of his practice is best reflected in the results of his clients. His focus is on building long-term relationships with his clientele, rather than attempting to gain the most out of each case.

This global approach to problematics generates more favorable and less devastating solutions for the firms clientele which mostly includes small and medium businesses. These solutions are also easily integrated with the daily

Mr. Audet has been a member of the Québec Bar Association since 1981. He is also a civil and commercial mediator accredited by the Superior Court of Québec and is a practitioner of Getting to yes negotiating, an approach popularized by Havard University professors, Ury and Fisher. In order to further his abilities in the courtroom, Mr. Audet has also had intensive and specialized training in the field of trial advocacy.




A PRACTICE FIRMLY DEDICATED TO NEEDS OF THE SMALL & MEDIUM BUSINESS

The business law firm of Audet & Associées Inc. was founded eight years ago by Mr. Audet with the mission to provide business law solutions to SMEs.

Their clientele is mainly comprised of the following sectors:

- Retail trade;
- Wholesale trade: wholesale traders and officers, as well as distributors of personal items;
- Construction: specialized contractors;
- Manufacturing: manufacturing of computer-related products and high-end technology;
- Finance et insurance: intermediaries and private investors;
- Franchisors and franchisees;
- High-end technology: equipment, software and Internet;
- Accommodations and catering services: bars, B&Bs, breweries and restaurants;
- Commercial real estate: owners, tenants, lenders and investors;
- Foundations and personal services;
- Professional services: lawyers, accountants, architects, computer science and health.


A CUSTOMIZED SOLUTION FOR THE SMALL & MEDIUM BUSINESS


The firm offers business solutions adapted to clients who wish to:

- Protect themselves against the theft of client lists, confidential information, trade secrets as well as any unfair competition:
Services include protection of strategic data, prevention, legal audits, rapid interventions before the courts, commercial solutions.

- Create a external corporate legal department:
Be familiar with the company, act in an effective manner, and intervene locally within the scope of a global vision.

- Protect and enforce their rights before the courts:
Act with firmness and rigor while favoring the advantage of mediation and out of court settlements.

- Resolve disagreements between partners or shareholders:
Protect the company first, find a solution acceptable to all parties involved, avoid the courts if at all possible, and analyze the possible procedures while attempting to settle the manner amicably.

- Purchase and sell a company, whether franchised or not:
Bring forth a business perspective, challenge choices, strategies and intended outcomes, and propose the appropriate legal solutions.




- Negotiate, analyze and draft agreements:
Reflect and defend to the best of our ability the interest of clients in the fields of computer science, real estate, distribution, franchises, trademarks, copyrights, business takeovers, advertising and promotional means, human resources, non-competition, financing and product distribution.

- Start a business:
Establish clear rules and regulations to avoid and solve all conflicts, properly design a corporate structure and realistic association or shareholders agreement.

- Protect and defend their intellectual property:
Bring forth business solutions and, if necessary, consider all legal intervention in order to protect patents, trademarks, copyrights, etc.

- Recrute and terminate employement:
Proceed correctly, avoid costly mistakes, negotiate and protect the rights of the employers and reduce costs.


THE MEDIA RELIES ON THE EXPERTISE OF MR. AUDET

Mr. Audet is consulted by all forms of media regularly for both his legal and business expertise. Copies are available upon request.

The law firm Audet & Partners is a leader in legal services for the small and medium businesses in the province of Quebec and of foreign companies doing business in the province of Quebec and in Canada. Our firm offers all required legal services and concentrates on Business law. The founding partner is Mr. Luc Audet, member of the Quebec Bar Association since 1981. Mr. Audet is also a civil and commeciaal mediator accredited by the Superior Court of the province of Quebec and is a practitioner of the «Getting to yes» negotiating, also named Harvard method and Fisher and Ury Method.
Our expertise includes the following areas of Business law : protection and defense of non competing obligations, incorporation under the Quebec, the Canada and the American jurisdictions, shareholders Agreements, termination of partnerships, purchase and sale of an economic activity and intellectual property ( copyright, trademarks, patents). Our fees can be on an hour rate, on a lump sum, on a percentage or a mix of those modes.
Our web site www.CanadianBusinessLaw.com is a division of our law firm, located in Montreal, Quebec.
We are a small firm, but big on service !
