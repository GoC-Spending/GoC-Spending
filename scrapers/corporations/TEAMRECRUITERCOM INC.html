Teamrecruiter.com:  SelfIdentified Database Program (Diversity Initiative)

We encourage candidates who are registering in our database to identify themselves under the following categories for the purpose of participating in one of several diversity campaigns which we co-ordinate on behalf of our clients.



Cutural Self-Identification



The long-term success of any business calls for a diverse body of talent that can bring fresh ideas, perspectives and views to their work. Diverse individuals are described as but, are not limited to, New Immigrants, Minorities groups, those with Disabilities, Aboriginal Canadians etc.



We canvas our database registrants to create a stream of self-identified candidates for entry into our cultural diversity program.



Disability Self Identification



Persons with disabilities" means persons who have a long-term or Recurring physical, mental, sensory, medical, cognitive, psychiatric or learning impairment and who

(a) consider themselves to be disadvantaged in employment by reason of That impairment, or

(b) believe that a employer or potential employer is likely to

Consider them to be disadvantaged in employment by reason of that impairment, and includes persons whose functional limitations owing to their impairment have been accommodated in their current job or workplace



We canvas our database registrants to create a stream of self-identified candidates for entry into our disability program.

Cutural Self-Identification

We canvas our database registrants to create a stream of self-identified candidates for entry into our cultural diversity program.



Disability Self Identification



Persons with disabilities" means persons who have a long-term or Recurring physical, mental, sensory, medical, cognitive, psychiatric or learning impairment and who

(a) consider themselves to be disadvantaged in employment by reason of That impairment, or

(b) believe that a employer or potential employer is likely to

Consider them to be disadvantaged in employment by reason of that impairment, and includes persons whose functional limitations owing to their impairment have been accommodated in their current job or workplace



We canvas our database registrants to create a stream of self-identified candidates for entry into our disability program.
