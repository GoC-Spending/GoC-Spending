Company Profile


GPEC International Ltd. (GPEC) was founded with a concise
mission. Our mission is to provide our clients with a full range
of innovative and cost-effective solutions to a complex number
of environmental and engineering issues. With this objective in
mind, GPEC has become a leading environmental and engineering
firm offering our clients comprehensive services and solutions.

These include Site Assesment, Site Remediation & Restoration,
Low Tempreture Thermal Desorption (LTTD), Storage Tank
Management, Waste Management (Municipal and Bio-Medical Waste),
Waste-Water and Groundwater Treatment, Compliance Promotion and
Pollution Prevention, Environmental Impact Assesment (EIA's),
Landfill Construction, Housing Developments and much, much, more.

Our exceptional and highly technical staff of multi disciplinary
engineers and scientists are committed to finding effective and
technically sound solutions for our clients' long term needs. Our
strength lies in our foremost priority -- client satisfaction.
This committment is why we continue to consistently expand and
provide ever more integrated environmental and engineering
management services.

Our success is built on our dedication to our clients and our
response to their needs. GPEC offers environmental and
engineering solutions for the present, but our sights -- and
our sense of responsibility -- remain firmly focused on the
future.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Environmental Products and Services
Product Name:
Environmental Site Assessments
GPEC provides extensive expertise in performing successful and cost-effective environmental site assessments. We tailor a site assessment to the particular characteristics of a property. Using innovative and cost-effective methods and technologies we will provide a comprehensive and understandable evaluation of any contamination that is present.

GPEC's comprehensive site assessment programs include: Comprehensive Quality; Assurance & Quality Control (QA/QC) Program; Records Review and Historical Research; Preliminary Field Investigations; Exploratory Boreholes & Excavations; Olfactory and Visual Observations; On-site Contaminant Analysis; Detailed Subsurface Investigation; Groundwater Monitoring Wells; Geophysical Survey; Hydrogeological Investigation; Laboratory Analysis; Interpretation of Data; Risk Assessment; Remedial Investigation/Feasibility Study (RI/FS).

Product Name:
Environmental Remediation Services
GPEC provides clients with comprehensive environmental remediation services. We can design and implement cost-effective and site-specific remediation strategies for the most challenging remediation problems. We have a highly qualified, multi-disciplinary staff which can provide turn-key solutions to various contamination issues in an environmentally sound and safe manner. We can interface with governmental agencies and other primary contacts, and provide all necessary documentation from laboratory analysis to closure reports.

Our remediation experience and capabilities include, but are not limited to, the following technologies: Bioremediation; Low Temperature Thermal Desorption; Stabilization/Solidification; Incineration; Barrier Walls; Solvent Extraction; Soil Washing; Groundwater Pump and Treat; Air Sparging; Vapour Extraction; Ultraviolet Oxidation.

GPEC is Canada's most experienced mobile Low Temperature Thermal Desorption (LTTD) operator, having treated more contaminated soil in more locations than any other firm, while having passed all post-treatment laboratory analysis. Low Temperature Thermal Desorption (LTTD) has been proven to be the most efficient and most environmentally friendly method of remediating hydrocarbon contaminated soil. LTTD is commonly used for treating a variety of hydrocarbon contaminated soils resulting from fuel spills (e.g. gasoline, diesel fuels, kerosene, jet fuel), oils, and other products. One of the greatest benefits of the LTTD process is that contaminants are removed and destroyed at a very high efficiency - contaminants are not merely transferred from one location to another.

Product Name:
Services that can address all facets of the tank management process.
Leaking underground storage tanks (USTs) and aboveground storage tanks (ASTs) have contaminated untold millions of tons of soil and groundwater with petroleum products and other hazardous and non-hazardous products. Improper installations, pipe and tank deterioration and poor maintenance of tank systems can result in costly clean-ups and can have serious implications on human health and the environment. GPEC provides comprehensive services that can address all facets of the tank management process.

GPEC's storage tank management services include: Inventory and Registration of UST/AST Systems; Installation & Upgrading of Tank Systems; Permitting and UST/AST Equipment Design Requirements; Tank Closure, Removal & Replacement; Leak Detection and Monitoring; Tank Tightness Testing Regulatory Compliance; Data Management and Record Keeping; Emergency Response Plans; Contamination Investigation and Site and Impact Assessment Studies; Sample Collection and Analysis

Product Name:
Services to solve the most difficult water and waste-water concerns.
GPEC understands the unique importance of water and we provide a variety of services to solve the most difficult water and waste-water concerns. These concerns can range from expanding or building a new industrial facility, municipal water treatment plant, reservoirs, industrial holding ponds, and improving river and lake quality, as well as surface runoff. We can recommend practical and cost-effective solutions through experience and in-depth scientific studies to ensure that we give our clients the most technically sound recommendations.

Our comprehensive water and waste-water management services include: Water Sampling and Analysis; Treatability Studies; Waste Minimization at the Source; Process Modification; Concept Engineering; Geotechnical Testing; Facility Design; Construction Management; Operations Startup, Monitoring, and Consultation; and Personnel Training.

Product Name:
Turn-key services for large and small scale construction projects.
GPEC can provide comprehensive turn-key services for large and small scale construction projects. GPEC's professionals have designed, constructed, and provided project management services for a variety of developments. The types of construction have varied greatly from standard wood beam construction, concrete block, brick, stone, concrete, steel framed construction, pre-engineered buildings, hollow core slab construction, tilt-up concrete panel construction, Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) block forming technology, to various combinations of these.

The experience of GPEC's personnel has spanned a wide array of construction projects, including the following types of developments: Shopping plazas; Residential subdivisions; Industrial complexes; Bridges; Tunnels; Roadways; Dams; Airport infrastructure; Apartment buildings; Hotels; Commercial buildings.
