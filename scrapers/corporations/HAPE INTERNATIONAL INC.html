Company Profile


HaPe International Inc. was founded in Alberta, Canada in 2004 when its holding company, HaPe Group, purchased Educo, a manufacturer of educational toys made of wooden beads and frames and colorful sculptured wires.   Since then the collection has expanded to eight brands with over 250 interactive products favored by parents and children at home, daycare, doctors offices and schools.  The range is distributed to North American specialty retailers, gift stores, zoos and museums gift stores and select ecommerce sites and catalogs.    

HaPe has over two decades of experience in the toy industry and has achieved industry acclaim from peers, consumer groups, independent testing groups, governments and the international media, earning awards for business excellence, exportation of products, service to children with special needs, quality control and contributions to society.  

Displaying the highest standards in product design, quality craftsmanship and educational play value, each toy is created with careful attention to detail.  The company proudly displays more than 60 international awards including Parents' Choice Gold and Silver Medals, National Parenting Honors, the British "Best Toy" award and the prestigious Oppenheim Platinum Seal of Excellence.  In 1994, the award winning Original Supermaze was inducted into the Toy Hall of Fame.  

A strong network of manufacturers' representatives, under the direction of HaPe's U.S. Sales Manager, sell the range annually at major national and regional toy, educational and gift trade shows in North America and abroad.  

The HaPe Mission
HaPe's mission is to develop and produce products for children to help them grow and develop.  Their products are reliable and safe and in line with their goal: "shape our kids - our future" by quality, design and innovation.

As a manufacturer Hape is extremely forward thinking and considers the environment in all their actions.  They have a deep concern about providing a safe, clean environment for children.  They strive to stay "natural" in the design, manufacture and delivery of products as possible.  They have achieved many environmental manufacturing standards by fulfilling and completing the following requirements:

Product Safety according to EN 71
Product Safety according to ASTM
ICTI
ISO 9001
ISO 14001

What Is HaPe Holding AG in Switzerland?
HaPe Holding AG in Switzerland is the head office for our company group, located in Lucerne, Switzerland, in the heart of Europe.  All national and international investments are made by our holding company.  

Managed by the Holding AG, our Distribution Centers are located in 3 different regions around the world
1.  HaPe International Inc.
in Edmonton/Alberta/Canada for North America, South America and Canada
2.  HaPe International AG
In Mücke/Germany for Europe
3.  HaPe International (Ningbo) Ltd
In Ningbo/China for the Asian Market as well as for Oceania.

CORE ACTIVITIES
HaPe International Group has 4 main activities
1. OEM Manufacturing facilities with Third Party OEM and ODM processes
2.  Manufacturing of our own HaPe brands and distribution of them into more than 40 Countries world wide
3.  Educational Developments for school suppliers, Kindergartens, Daycare Centers and support for educational suppliers
4.  Third-Party Logistics and Distribution from our own Distribution Center world wide

HaPe concept
Since the very beginning when HaPe started manufacturing toys, their focus has been on children having fun and developing.  This remains a priority today as they create new products.

The products are separated into different main categories :
HaPe Shaping the future

With total environmental responsibility in mind products in this category are made with highly sustainable materials.  

EDUCO
Naturally Fun  
Educo playthings are educational toys made of wooden beads, wooden frames and colorful sculptured play wires. Today, Educo bead and wires mazes are a favored toy among parents and preschoolers who enjoy them in medical offices, airports,
schools and home. The Educo collection features over 70 interactive play products now used throughout the world to stimulate imaginative minds, encourage three dimensional
thinking, enhance motor skill development, and provide rewarding rehabilitation exercises. Carefully written activity guides accompany each model to help parents, teachers, health, professionals, and care-givers extend educational opportunities,
therapeutic options and playtime fun.
Educo products are toys where learning and fun meet during play.  With design focused on on simplicity the priority is to ensure that the product can be produced and distributed at an affordable price.  Educo products are toys with a high play value.  

Woody Click
Bring out the best in your child
Developed in order to let the child grow into his own imagination world, Woody Click supports role play, an essential activity for young children.  Through a childs imagination they create their own world.  Woody Click is a thoughtful combination between rubber wood and Baltic birch and is treated with only water-based stains for color.  

All items are developed for 3 years + because of small parts.  

Odysey
Bend and Pose ... playing and learning about history
Odyssey Toys was founded in 2002 offering bendable and poseable figures in a variety of themes.  Within a few years they had a strong network of gift, museum and toys stores.  HaPe purchased Odyssey Toys in February of 2008 with a focus of expanding distribution of the HaPe brands into the existing Odyssey Toys market and extending the reach of Odyssey Toys worldwide.  

Beleduc
Educational wood puzzles and games and puppets and marionettes designed in Germany.  
The award-winning line includes puppets for storytelling for children three years and up and puzzles and games for children four years and up.  

QUADRILLA®
Intelligent Playing in Wood
Founded and developed by Mr. Wilfried Braun of Quadrilla®, this line is manufactured and distributed by HaPe International Group.
Quadrilla marble runs are a system of blocks, tracks and accessory pieces that allow the energy of a marble to travel in a make-your-own path.  Beautiful wood tracks and a rainbow of colored blocks, each with a different function, are the starting point for endless contraptions for marbles to drop to a new level, go right or left, swirl in a funnel, or pour out of a teeter totter.    Their path is determined by the builder and the options are endless.  Simple 2 or 3 level switches or complex multi-level, multi-direction layouts are possible depending on the builders understanding of the function of each piece.  Introduced in 2005, Quadrilla immediately won numerous awards including the Oppenheim Platinum Award, reserved for only 10 toys in a given year.  

Anamalz
Designed in Australia, the quirky, poseable Anamalz, with life-like characteristics in an artistic and moveable form has been roaming North American toy stores and embraced by thousands of children.  Made from organic maple, water-based paints and formaldehyde-free glue, each anamalz is individually branded by the natural grain of the wood and the touch of a craftsman.  The time spent in creating this range will be outnumbered by the hours children will spend in role play and story-telling with their favorite new kangaroo, camel or black sheep.  

Moonworker
Moonworker is distributed - but not manufactured - by HaPe and helps a child to strengthen his imagination while playing outdoor.  Being a "worker on the moon" who is digging into the earth of the moon and searching for adventures.

Packaging

South Europe        - Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese
North Europe        - Danish, Norwegian, Swedish, Finnish

HaPe's Vision
HaPe's goal is to become one of the world's leading toy manufacturers who support childrens learning and fun with creative, innovative and educational play ideas and products with high play value.

The strategy is to set up and treat a long term business relationship with everybody in this industry who shares their philosophy and core beliefs.

HaPe looks to the future with confidence and welcomes the prospect of co-operative ventures that will enhance a solid reputation, continue to manage steady growth and build upon a proud record of profit and achievement.
