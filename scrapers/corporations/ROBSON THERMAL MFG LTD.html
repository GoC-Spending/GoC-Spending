Company Profile


We manufacture specialty products for the mechanical/process
insulation trade, HVAC industry, plus specialty paints,
coatings, and adhesives. We sell through distributors
and are looking for full-service insulation and HVAC distributors
in various market areas. Our products include:
1. Flamex FR Canvas - a 6oz fire rated canvas ASTM & ULC 25/50
rated. For covering exposed mechanical insulation indoors.
2. NO SWEAT - anti condensation paint for cold pipes, fittings
air ducts, walls, window frames, and other areas that sweat.
Prevents condensation from forming on cold and dual temperature
surfaces. Non-flammable, non-toxic.
3. Therma-Lite - coating that insulates hot pipe fittings and
other heated surfaces to prevent heat loss and for personnel
protection. Fire resistant, non-toxic, indoor/outdoor.
4. Ticki-Tuff - adhesive for securing acoustic duct insulation inside of sheet metal ducts and concrete shafts. Also for sealing cuts and joints in the duct insulation to prevent fiber erosion. Non-flammable, non-toxic, water based. Black or white colours. Brush grade and spray grade.
5. White Lag - lagging adhesive for applying canvas and glass
cloth over exposed mechanical system insulation. Ultra white
colour is a close match to white PVC jacket colour. Water based,
non-flammable, non-toxic.
6. Robson's Duct Seal-WB - Sealant for HVAC ducts and plenums,
all pressure classes, indoor/outdoor, water based, non-toxic,
non-saggging, flexible, grey and custom colours, lifetime
warranty
7. TRV Insulation - Ultra thin insulating panels for tents,
vehicles, buildings, windows, and special uses. Flexible,
non-flammable, non-toxic, variety of facing options including
fabric, foils, metals, plastics, and veneers. Permanent or
removable. Double the insulation value of fiberglass.
8. Robson's Fire Retardant - Water based, non-toxic, clear fire
retardant for most fabrics, and all natural porous materials
including wood, paper, and cardboard.
9. Robson's Primer - White, latex primer and texture undercoat
for drywall. Spray-on fast drying formula. Low odour. Resists
flashing. Non-toxic.
10. Liner Coat - Anti-erosion neoprene coating for acoustic duct insulation. Prevents insulation fiber erosion and repairs damaged acoustic facing on the insulation while maintaining acoustic performance of the insulation. Water based, non-toxic, non-flammable.
11. Robson's RT-80 - Solvent based duct sealant that is resistant to damage from UV sun rays. It forms a shiny metallic finish on top that reflects UV rays so the duct sealant stays flexible and crack free. Low VOC's and a very mild solvent base makes this duct sealant suitable for LEED projects and for use indoors when water based duct sealants can't be applied and strong solvent odors are not allowed. No cancer causing chemicals. Fire resistant.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Flamex FR Canvas
Flamex FR Canvas is a fire resistant canvas jacket for covering
mechanical system insulation. It is factory treated and meets
all fire codes, safety codes, and building codes. It is 6oz/sq.yd
and 25/50 fire rated to ASTM & ULC standards.

Product Name:
NO SWEAT
NO SWEAT is a paint that prevents condensation on cold surfaces
including water pipes and fittings, air conditioning ducts,
concrete walls, window frames, and metal beams. Non-flammable,
non-toxic, water based, white or black. Can be colour tinted.
Fast drying. Apply like latex paint.


Product Name:
White Lag
White Lag is a lagging adhesive for applying canvas and glass
cloth jackets over mechanical system insulation. White Lag is
ultra white in colour so that it matches the finished jacket
colour with PVC pipe fitting covers. White Lag can be tinted to
any colour so that there is no need to apply paint on it. Non-toxic,
non-flammable, water based. Also available in black (Black Lag).

Product Name:
Ticki-Tuff
Ticki-Tuff is an adhesive for securing fiberous duct insulation
to sheet metal and concrete. Also used to seal insulation joints
and cuts to prevent fiber erosion. Water based, non-flammable,
non-toxic, white or black colours. Fast drying. Can be applied by
all standard methods, including spraying.

Product Name:
Robson's Duct Seal-WB
HVAC duct and plenum sealant

Product Name:
Therma-Lite
liquid and formable insulation

Product Name:
TRV Insulation
ultra-thin insulating panels for windows, tents, walls, etc.

Product Name:
Robson's Fire Retardant
fire retardant for fabric and natural materials

Product Name:
Chip Brushes
wood handle, disposable paint & glue brushes

Product Name:
Robson's Texture Undercoat & Primer
fast dry spray-on latex undercoat and primer for drywall

Product Name:
Robson's Foam
Unique formable liquid foam for making stage props, artificial scenery,
faux decorating, and special effects for stage and movie sets.
Water based liquid foam, non-toxic, non-flammable, ultra-light weight, can be
formed into large complex objects by hand or with tools without
sagging or slumping. Holds it's shape while drying. Can be cut,
sanded, carved, drilled, joined together with wet foam, tinted,
and painted. Allows creative projects to be done faster and
easier than any other method.

Product Name:
Liner Coat
Black, water based, neoprene acoustic coating for repairing the acoustical facing of acoustic duct insulation. Liner Coat repairs damaged acoustic duct insulation to like new condition for less than half the cost of replacing the damaged insulation. Liner Coat also binds with any loose insulation fibers and locks the fibers into the insulation.

Product Name:
Robson's RT-83
RT-83 is a unique anti-rodent paint that keeps rodents from chewing on wires, pipes, and other materials. RT-83 is non-toxic and does not harm rodents, other animals, or the environment. Rodent entry holes can be plugged by dipping a sponge or steel wool into RT-83 and stuffing it into the entry hole. RT-83 is effective for all rodents and other animals.
