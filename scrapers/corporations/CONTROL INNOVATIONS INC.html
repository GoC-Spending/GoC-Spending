Technology profile

Control Innovations Inc. is an electronics and instrumentation manufacturing company which was founded in 2000 in Calgary, AB. Through our 13,500 sq/ft production facility and 5,600 sq/ft materials warehouse, we specialize in Turn-Key project delivery across a broad range of industries including OEMs, Oil & Gas, Mining, Construction, Transportation, Medical, Power and Utilities. From design, manufacturing and installation, to the servicing, repair and re-calibration of field equipment, our microelectronics & electromechanical manufacturing capabilities provide our customers with a fully integrated product solution.

Our services include but are not limited to the following:
Project Design/Management - from product/project specification and mechanical, electrical & packaging design through to manufacturing, installation and commissioning.
Prototype Design & Assembly - allowing our customers to move products into the marketplace faster than otherwise possible.
Production Assembly - a broad range of controls and instrumentation as well as panel assembly and the wiring of equipment. This can be done on consignment or as turnkey where we assume responsibility for procurement of components, final assembly, packaging and shipping of product to customers.
System Integration - with challenging adaptations from off-the-shelf measurement and control systems to customer specific applications.
Equipment Supply - for use in measurement, instrumentation and control systems including transducers, and display devices, cabling, control panels, test equipment and spare parts.
Repair & Calibration - of used customer field equipment and instrumentation.
Test Services  of our clients field equipment to their exacting standards.
Follow-Up - we offer field installation and wiring paired with repair/servicing of industrial and oilfield electronics systems, including 24 hour emergency on-call support.

We also sell a wide range of products in these key product categories and have local inventory.
Switches and Senso
Connectors - MS, CA, PT/KPT, MDM, Deutsch
Connector Accessories
Panel Displays & Controllers
Industrial Encoders
Panel Indicators/Meters
Mag Pickups and Accessories
Industrial Rotary Switches
Disconnect Switches
Waterproof Automotive Connectors
Control Handles & Modules
Pressure Transducers
Power Supplies - AC/DC, DC/DC, DC/AC
Molded Cable Assemblies
Custom Wiring Harnesses
Custom Calibration & Test Equipment
Circuit Breakers
Gauges
Potentiometers
Semiconductors

In todays environment, successful businesses must be able to rapidly adapt and adjust their costing and production strategies. Those that can respond quickly have a stronger, more competitive ability to meet their customers needs. Our customers actively seek out ways to shed those parts of their production that may be too costly to maintain. Their labour, HR allocations or the demands of sustaining a competitive production infrastructure is usually much higher than ours as we are able to amortize such costs over many projects. Working in close concert with our customers, we assume the responsibility for certain manufacturing and private label production needs with a faster turnaround time and a lower cost than they can on their own. Control Innovations Inc. is in the business of helping enterprises like yours achieve your objectives by allowing you to focus your companys time and resources on its core competencies.
