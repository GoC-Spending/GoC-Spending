Company Profile


Custom Case Company has been manufacturing ATA style cases since
1978 and has been supplying businesses and individuals around
the World with solutions for their packaging requirements.

Our prices are very competitive.
Our cases can provide you with reliable protection for your
equipment and trade show displays. We build quality long lasting
durable transit cases for computer systems and accessories,
architectural models, monitors, camera equipment, audio-visual
and many other applications. This ensures that your sales teams,
technicians or other personnel arrive at your clients location
with your equipment working, organized and easier to setup. Our
cases can also be used to display or house a particular piece of
equipment. Pelican Protector® cases , Pelican Flash Lights , Pelican-Storm, Pelican-Hardigg Cases, Nanuk cases, Plasticase Products, SKB Products, HPRC Cases and Underwater Kinetics cases are also available from Custom Case Co.

All of our cases can be custom designed to meet your specific
requirements. We look forward to the opportunity to provide you
with a free estimate.

Our number one goal is to service our customers in a friendly,
courteous and efficient manner because your repeat business is
important to us.


Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide you with high quality products that
exceed all your shipping requirements and to continue to design
and develop new and improved methods of packaging and protecting
your valuable equipment.
