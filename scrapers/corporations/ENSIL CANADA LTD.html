Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Repair of Electronic Systems
Ensil Canada Ltd. has the required technical expertise, facilities and the advance diagnostic equipment to test and repair complex electronic circuit board assemblies found in communication, industrial, medical, mainframe, aerospace and military industries. Ensil is a contract manufacturer and our technical staff are fully trained to diagnose and repair electronic products such as printed circuit boards (PCB), power supplies, displays, drives and controllers.

Ensil's customers come from manufacturing and utilities industries as well as government, health care and military sectors. We have the ability to repair any type of electronic equipment.

Ensils commitment to quality engineering, repair, logistics and best-cost pricing paved the way for diversification into many other related engineering fields over the last quarter century. However, no other growth area of Ensil was a more natural extension to our business than the repair, rework and failure analysis of pcb and electronic assemblies for: AEW&C (Airborne Early Warning and Control) Systems, Identify Friend or Foe (IFF) Transponder, LRU (line-replaceable unit), Military ECM devices, different platforms of Navy Radar Systems, SATCOM, Sentinel Radar, SINCGARS, Submarine Sonars, Tactical and Communication Radios. For more information please check the List of Defence Platforms supported by Ensil.

As a defence contractor with a Canadian CAGE code L1679, registered with Canadian Controlled Goods Program (CGP) and having Military Critical Technical Data Agreement DD 2345, Ensil is positioned to be your global supply chain partner. Our outstanding technical staff is suited for military, aerospace, naval and industrial equipment manufacturing, design and repair. Our company handles all orders regardless of availability of National Stock Numbers (NSN).

We at Ensil are confident that our world-class technical personnel will provide a diverse range of services and deliver optimal satisfaction to our customers. Ensil Canada Ltd. is ready to be your business partner and your electronics repair depot.

Ensil Canada Ltd. focuses on pcb research and development enhancing mission critical electronic technologies in the areas of aerospace, defence, telecommunication and medical diagnostic equipment. Ensil's R&D divisions' goal is to improve and develop existing circuit technology through in-house research activities. We have the required resources to modify, re-design or repair printed circuit boards (pcb) used in medical, industrial and military equipment.

Technology profile

Ensil Canada Ltd. is a member of the Ensil Group of Companies. For nearly 30 years Ensil Group of Companies have been involved in research, development, repair, rework, test, inspection and manufacturing of electronic circuit boards and assemblies used in various lines of products in the fields of Defense, Aerospace, Medical, Industrial, Telecommunication and Robotics.

Ensil is registered with the Canadian Government Controlled Goods Program (CGP) this registration entitles Ensil to examine, possess or transfer controlled goods in accordance with the Defense Production Act and the Controlled Goods Regulations.

Ensil is a prime defense contractor operating under the Canadian CAGE code L1679 serving the Defense and Aerospace Industries and has specialization in reverse engineering, design and manufacturing of complex RF military equipment, digital, analog and various obsolescent electronics circuit boards and assemblies.

We invite you to visit us at our Electronic Circuits Repair and Technology Center in Markham Ontario or for additional information about Ensil and its services please visit the Canadian Government website
