Company Profile


Company specialized in construction of industrial, commercial and institutional buildings. If we talk about detailing and fabrication, she performs also in bridge structure. Proco is also leader in metallic cladding facility or other coatings currently existings in the market. "Constructions PROCO Inc." specialises in the design, detailing, fabrication and the realization of steel structures and turn-key
projects since 1961.

During these years, PROCO has acquired expertise in many fields of activity "touching"
industrial and commercial projects at different levels of
complexity. PROCO covers industries such as aluminium, pulp and
paper mills, sawmills, hydroelectric powerhouses, bridges, as
well as institutional and commercial.

The Constructions PROCO Inc. plant has a steel fabricating shop
measuring 80,000 sq. ft. including offices and storage. The
installations are equipped with travelling cranes of different
capacity and a large number of other specialised equipment used
for welding, machining and tinplate working. We have realized
projects of several million dollars.

Constructions PROCO Inc. has the ressources, machinery and the
necessary equipment. All designs and drawings are done on
computers in order to guarantee precision and quality during the
realization of your specific project.

Constructions PROCO Inc. has a staff of over 250 full-time
employees and many others on a part-time basis. They all work
under the supervision of Michel Toupin, P.Eng. General Manager.
