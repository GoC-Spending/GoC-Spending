Company Profile


Ingenico Canada, Ltd. is a regional country office of Ingenico S.A., a global payment services and products provider. Ingenico (Euronext: FR0000125346  ING) is the worlds #1 provider of payment solutions, with 4,000 employees strong and over 20 million terminals deployed in more than 125 countries. Our products and services are used by one-third of the global population. Delivering the very latest innovative electronic and mobile payment technologies, transaction management and security solutions, Ingenico is shaping the future direction of the payment market. Our secure EMV, Contactless, NFC-enabled, and Magstripe payment solutions take financial institutions, banks, merchants and businesses beyond payment by unlocking powerful customer intelligence from each and every transaction to enable the delivery of unique and competitive services.

Ingenicos expertise goes far beyond a simple terminal interface at the point of sale. Utilizing the most advanced, secure and reliable payment hardware and software solutions, Ingenico provides a trusted and secure touch point to accept payments, activate loyalty programs, issue gift cards, and build the bank's brand through interactive communications at the point of purchase. Whether our clients wish to deliver promotional messages or short surveys, Ingenico hardware and software solutions will deliver these in monochrome or color, at the point of sale, kiosk, unattended or on mobile devices.

By choosing Ingenico, users are aligning themselves with the worlds premier supplier of payment solutions, ones that they can trust to support all types of innovative, reliable, and secure transactions for many years to come. Visit us at www.ingenico.ca

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
iCT220 Countertop
Sophisticated merchant services in a compact standalone countertop device

Even the smallest independent merchant needs to accommodate its customers preferred payment methods, and most look to their banks or merchant services providers for practical, economical solutions. Lightweight, stylish and self-contained, the iCT220 will give your merchant customers the freedom to accept all types of electronic card payments without requiring a cash register or POS system interface. All iCT220 functions  from payment processing and receipt printing to settlement reporting and brand enhancement programs  operate flawlessly from a single piece of equipment. This rugged, easy-to-use device also carries our advanced security measures, which means even the smallest shop can benefit from Ingenicos global leadership in secure payment processing. Designed to securely accept all forms of electronic payment, including EMV smartcard, contactless, magnetic stripe, and NFC-enabled mobile transactions.


Product Name:
iCT250 Countertop
A complete set of sophisticated merchant services in a single, compact color device

Banks and merchant services providers have long struggled to keep their independent merchants supplied with the sophisticated payment systems they need. Ingenicos innovative iCT250 has the intelligence and versatility to stand alone, in even the most demanding retail environments. This countertop device gives your merchant customers the freedom to accept all existing forms of electronic payment  including contactless, EMV and mobile (NFC-based)  because it requires absolutely no interface with a cash register or POS system. All operations  from payment processing and receipt printing to settlement reporting and brand enhancement programs  generate from a single, secure and easy-to-use color device.


Product Name:
iWL2xx Series Wireless & Mobile POS Device
Todays consumers are moving targets, so being ready to accept payment when/where theyre ready to pay is essential. Our secure iWL series lets you accept all electronic payments quickly and easily  in the store or on the road, long or short range. The iWL series represents the smallest, lightest and most convenient payment devices on the market. Garden centers, warehouses, showrooms, home deliveries, pop-up stores, curbside checkouts and morewherever your sale takes place, count on the iWL range of products for unparalleled speed and versatility. The iWL Series comes with a variety of connectivity options:

iWL221 (GPRS/2G)
iWL222 (Bluetooth)
iWL251 (GPRS)
iWL252 (Bluetooth)
iWL255 (GPRS/UMTS/3G)


Product Name:
iSMP Smart Mobile Payment Device
Transform your iPod touch®, iPad® or iPhone® (Canada Only) into an EMV Chip & PIN secure mobility payment solution!

Our iSMP leads the revolution in mobile payment solutions for traditional retailers and mobile merchants alike. By combining your iPod touch, iPad or iPhone (Canada Only) with the iSMP, Ingenico has invented a unique and smart POS payment option that is officially MFI certified, meaning it is 100% compliant with Apple performance and development standards. With embedded Bluetooth Class III, an integrated 1D/2D barcode reader, and a long-lasting battery in a rugged docking sleeve, iSMP gives you the freedom to do business anytime, anywhere, with no worries.


Product Name:
iSMP COMPANION Smart Mobile Payment Device
Turn ANY Bluetooth® tablet or smartphone into a secure wireless mobile payment solution you can trust!

Does your business call for a need to accept electronic payments away from a traditional/ stationary checkout lane? Are you interested to explore innovative mobile technologies to help you better engage with customers? If you answered yes to either of these questions, then you should consider Ingenicos iSMP Companion. Our latest mobile payment device is designed to accommodate the evolving mobile marketplace and tech savvy consumers. iSMP Companion connects via Bluetooth to almost any iOS, Android or Windows-based smartphone or tablet to create the fastest and most secure mobile payment and checkout solution. It gives you freedom to accept securely payment on virtually any mobile device you prefer. iSMP Companion is lightweight, compact and fully commercialized for the most demanding and high volume use in retail, hospitality, transportation, ticketing, and other mobile business environments.


Product Name:
iPP320 PIN Pad
Plug and Play, PIN and Pay  its the fastest, most secure, easiest-to-install PIN Pad on the market.

When time and space are at a premium (in retail, when are they not?), the compact iPP300 series of retail PIN Pads is the unmatched option for adaptability and convenience. This PCI PTS V3.0 certified payment solution is lightweight, stylish and self-contained. Both the iPP320 and iPP350 plug directly into your cash register for instant installation and easy integration into existing POS systems. When you need a transaction solution that maximizes performance in minimum countertop space, there is no better choice than our iPP320 or iPP350. Customer transactions are fast, private, efficient and secure.


Product Name:
iSC Touch 250 Signature Capture Device
The payment powerhouse with a small footprint  and an exceptionally large presence.

Interact with your customers in ways that were never before possible with our sleek new iSC Touch 250  the fast, secure PCI PTS V3.0 certified payment solution that energizes your brand and loyalty-building programs. Combining signature capture and touchscreen technologies in a robust, compact design, the iSC Touch 250 showcases a full range of revenue producing promotions  ads, videos, couponing, targeted up-selling and cross-selling opportunities, gift card sales, extended warranties, digital signage and more  on a colorful, interactive, high-resolution touchscreen display that engages and involves your customer throughout the payment process. For multilane retail checkout, sales kiosks and self-service environments, the iSC Touch 250 is the most reliable, flexible and interactive payment device on the market.
