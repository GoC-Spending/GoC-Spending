Company Profile


As a new immigrant to Canada from Germany, Helmut Eppich, Chairman, founded Ebco Industries in 1956 as a small Tool and Die shop. Over 52 years ago that Tool and Die shop became the birthplace of Ebco, which has now become an association of companies that specialize in the custom manufacturing of metallic based products and components.

Ebco is a large custom manufacturer specializing primarily in the machining, fabricating, and assembly of large and small components and equipment. Headquartered near Vancouver, Canada - Ebco's facilities include 300,000 sq. ft. of manufacturing
space - including advanced CNC machines, large steel and alloy fabrication and welding equipment, and lifting capabilities to
180 tons. The company serves a wide range of corporations in North America and around the world including pulp and paper and
mining companies and the power generation, offshore oil production, aerospace, defence, and medical industries.

CAPABILITIES

Ebco is best known for its great diversity in skills and capabilities. We manufacture a wide range of products, from large extra heavy components used in dragline mining shovels to off shore oil and gas equipment to hospital related pathology equipment. And Ebco's highly skilled workforce has demonstrated its versatility by assembling and testing Demag mining shovels as well as fabrication and machining of complex products for many OEM's.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

With uncompromising attention to detail and strict tolerances, Ebco is certified to ISO 9001.2008 standards. Ebco's quality assurance staff are certified and equipped to ensure that your quality requirements are maintained at every stage of the
manufacturing process.

Ebco's inspectors are certified to ASNT level II for non-destructive testing using liquid dye penetrant and magnetic particle inspection. Inspectors are also certified by the Canadian Government Inspection Board in Non-destructive testing techniques, as well as AWS and CWB welding inspector techniques. Our inspectors are responsible for the supervision of sub-contracted radiographic and ultrasonic inspections of welds. For pressure vessels, we adhere to the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel code, and as an ASME certificate holder, we can apply the U and U2 stamps.

EBCO INDUSTRIES DIVISIONS

HEAVY FABRICATION

This division has an established reputation as
one of the top quality providers of large-scale,

the mining, pulp and paper, power generation,
offshore oil production, and aerospace and
defence industries.

Profiled Product: The Tunnel Borer

Ebco has produced a variety of tunnel borers
ranging upto 28' in diameter for the Robbins
Corporations. Robbins selected Ebco for its
unique ability to provide machining, fabricating
and assembly of large electromechanical
equipment. For more information:
www.ebco.com/tunnel_borer.html

HEAVY MACHINING & ASSEMBLY

The Heavy Machining Division offers a wide range
of machines and machining skills to handle the
most demanding requirements. From nuclear shield
valves to steel housings, Ebco's Heavy Machining
Division has the capabilities to produce
precision made, high quality products.

LIGHT METAL FABRICATION

LMF specializes in the fabrication, machining and
assembly of products which are built of lighter
to medium weight metals. Ebco LMF is one of only
two Boeing approved tooling vendors in Canada.
LMF produces assembly jigs and fixtures, drill
and locating jigs and other tooling items for
Boeing and other aircraft industry companies.

Key / Major clients:

BUCYRUS INTERNATIONAL, SOFEC,INC., ANDRITZ HYDRO, VOITH SEIMENS

Success stories:

Ebco Receives Awards for Excellence

Ebco's ability to meet the most demanding
requirements has been demonstrated in the awards
it has received from its customers, including:


The coveted Boeing Eagle award for outstanding
performance in cost reduction and quality
improvement.
The Project Management Institute's Project of the
Year award for an extremely complex cyclotron
project in a tight time frame and
McDonnell Douglas performance rating of a perfect
1000 points. So it comes as no surprise that
Helmut Eppich, founder and Chairman, was recognized as
an Entrepreneur of the Year by B.C. Business
Magazine.
Light Metal Fabricating Division Wins Two Awards
from Boeing
One from Boeing Commercial 777 for performance in
delivery of a critical tooling job ahead of
schedule. The other was from Boeing Defence and
Space Group, Sea Launch for delivery of a complex
tool, once again ahead of schedule.
