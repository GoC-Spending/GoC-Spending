J070 TUBE - COMMERCIAL DESCRIPTION

The J070 is a tubular cabinet built for exterior and interior
use. This cabinet is mainly constructed of intersliding
extrusions which make it virtually impossible to distinguish the
number of extrusions planks and give a surface with no apparent
joints. The J070 comes in 4 different colours of anodization and
can also be painted.

Four different heights are available 84", 79", 74" and 62" for
drive-up. A sliding payphone plate allows you to change in a very simple and fast way the height of your phone when installing your phone. This gives the same tube a lot more flexibility in your arrangement.

The aluminum inclined floor iscovered with non-skid rubber titles.
This helps to minimize maintenance and also prevent wears.

Access to electricity, from the lens and/or cover plate are
protected by security screwsé

Compact fluorescent 15W lighting.
According to statistics from suppliers, the life is around
10,000 hours which is very interesting.
You may also use an ordinary incandescent bulb, maximum 100 watts.
LEd lighting modules can last around 100,000 hours.

Directory holder may be installed inside the tube.

A tubular directory holder is also available. With its 36"
height it can accommodate a directory up to a maximum of 4"
thick.

Note: Following an agreement between "Burger King Canada" and
"Bell Canada", the J070-B-79 C54 B.K. tube was chosen to be the
official telephone cabinet for all Burger Kings across Canada.

Product Name:
Jaro J-400 payphone enclosure
Originally built for exterior needs. It may be mounted on walls
or on pedestal at car driver's and wheelchair height as well as
walk-up or truck-up height.

The J-400 is a model built with 3/16" anodized aluminum. Several lighting systems and identifications are available.

COMMERCIAL DESCRIPTION:
The J400 boothette is made for exterior use. It may be installed
to a wall with a mounting bracket #40B005 or mounted on a
pedestal. Different heights for pedestals are available: car
height (#40A454), universal or handicap (#40A545) and pedestrian
(#40A635). A special bracket #40C305 for back to back
installation is also available (option).

The boothette is mainly constructed of 3/16" (15mm) anodized
aluminum. Stickers are of scotchlite quality, 3M luminescence
and are silk screen printed. 5/8" (16mm) aluminum bars are held
by means of "one way" security bolts. The bars may be replaced
by a stainless steel shelf. Also available is a rod to hang the
directory at the same level as the telephone or a shelf with a pocket.

The J400 allows the installation of different types of telephone,
such as the "Centurion", "Millennium" and "Protel".
Visible fasteners are of stainless as well as security screws for the
lens.

Fluorescent lighting (1-HO 18" (46cm) tube) operates up to a
temperature of -40øF (-40øC). This electrical system is CSA and UL approved.
If you need long lasting lighiting you can use LED modules, around 100,000 hours.
The lighting is in the upper part of the J400 and this section is separated by a white polycarbonate diffuser.

The J400 is very popular for drive-up and truck-up use, and is
also in demand for places where resistance to vandalism is
required.

Technology profile

As we were among the first to use aluminium in the architectural
sector, working with sheet and extrusion is one of our main
strenght. Our knowledge about wood working since 1909 can be
added to a lot of achivements in stell, stainless steel, copper
and plastics.

All of these give us the edge on versatility for our client's
needs.

We have the most recent technology in numerical copy router (3
axes CNC router with a table of 5' x 10' or 1,5 m x 3 m). We
also have cutting and punching equipment for extrusion, sheet
metal working, sanding and welding.

We also have a vast exprience in assembly with extrusion and
lighting system in small and larger batches.

Our products are CSA and UL approved.
