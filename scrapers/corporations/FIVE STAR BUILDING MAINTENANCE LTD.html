Company Profile


Five Star Building Maintenance is strategically located to provide services in the Lower Mainland and throughout British Columbia. You demand exceptional standard of service that is measured by high quality outcomes and Five Star is able to achieve this through a well organized and structured team. This commitment to quality is evidenced through our high client retention rate and the quality profiles which we deliver.

As the market continues to change due to labour and other market conditions, Five Star has the expertise, experience and structure to deliver on the quality profiles that you and your tenants demand in todays competitive market. Our goal is to alleviate the day to day activity that is associated with most building maintenance programs. Five Star, though our structure and programs allows for a robust building maintenance program to unfold by providing unmatched customer service, immediate attention and follow-up with our clients.

You require accurate reports and audits compiled in a timely and meaningful way and at Five Star we keep you informed on the conditions of your properties and offer pro-active support and team work along with site tours, observations and suggestions. As a Property Manager you will experience the valued-added benefit of team work and calibration that Five Star brings to the various markets that we service.

Five Star Building Maintenance is fully insured, bonded and covered by WorkSafeCB and is member in good standing of the BCCCA (British Columbia Contract Cleaners Association) and PAMA (Professional Association of Managing Agents)

Commercial Building Services

Five Star offers full janitorial services for office buildings, high tech buildings, hotels and fitness facilities tailored to fit your unique needs.

 Daily, weekly, monthly cleaning services

 Initial cleaning / one-time jobs

 Project work / all types of floor maintenance

 Monthly building reports and audits

Supplementary Service Packages include:

 Window cleaning

 Pressure washing

 Carpet cleaning

 Garbage removal

 Special events cleaning

 Relamping Programs


At Five Star we understand the importance of being available for you and your tenants 24/7. Five Star managers can be reached directly or through our 24-hour call centre. Five Star also has a full-time equipment service technician, training facility, warehouse facility and a delivery system. This ensures that all services and products are delivered efficiently and on time.

Five Star is dedicated to your success by building relationships with you through:

 Regular client and tenant contact.

 Building inspections.

 Annually scheduled Quality Assurance Audits

 Immediate response to tenant needs and challenges.
