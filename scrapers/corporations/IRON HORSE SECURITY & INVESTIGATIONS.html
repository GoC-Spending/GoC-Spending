Company Profile


Founded in 1994, employing over 1500 security guards, Iron Horse Security is a premium full service, all Canadian, private security provider.

We successfully service clients across Canada in the sectors of government (Federal, Provincial, and Municipal), property management, retail, manufacturing, special events, financial, construction, residential, hi-tech and logistics.

Our approach to security is 100% customer driven. We excel at providing customer service-oriented guards willing to provide service above and beyond their job description. Customer Service with a Smile, has been a trait that has separated Iron Horse from our competitors since our very inception. We pride ourselves on being a company that is more approachable and customer centric. Our staff is trained this way, our managers practice it, and our overall policies revolve around the rejuvenating and revitalization of customer service in security contracts where the previous provider has fallen short.

We bring back quality to our customers where the previous provider has lapsed in customer service, management follow up or accountability.

Feel confident and safe knowing that behind that friendly smile every single Iron Horse Security guard is fully trained in all aspects of safety and security. Conflict resolution, proper and safe use of force, First Aid, CPR, Defibrillator combined with the securing of your property and patrons are the core of our services. Safety and security is our business, but that doesnt mean we cant do it in a positive and friendly manner. Each one of your guards is first and foremost a security professional; we just train them to ensure they are also impeccable brand ambassadors for you, your staff and your patrons!

Do not just take our word for it, our award winning business practices have gained national recognition through countless best practices awards such as;

-18 year Honour Roll certification from the Better Business Bureau
- 7-time winner of Profit Magazines Profit 100 Fastest Growing Companies in Canada
- 2 time Chamber of Commerce Business Excellence Award
- Profit 500 Fastest Growing Companies in Canada
- 9 consecutive time and current Consumers Choice Award winner

Professionally representing your corporate identity in appearance, demeanor, values and ethics while strengthening our service relationship is what you will find from every single person at Iron Horse, from our front line premium guards, to our extraordinary account managers, to our committed executive team.

That is why our customers have chosen us and why you should too. At Iron Horse Security the most powerful statements require no words.

Sector information

Unique applications:

Iron Horse operates Ottawas largest security officer training facility. Based out of our head office, The Iron Horse Security Academy professionally trains over 3500 students per year. This 11 section curriculum is approved by Canadian Standards Board - CAN/CGSB - 133.1-99, and has been awarded the Canadian Governments highest level of certification for Security Guard Training. Iron Horse Security is the ONLY company in the region that offers a training facility like this.

Key / Major clients:

Founded in 1994, by our President, Robin St. Martin; The Iron Horse Group has grown from just two employees to employing approximately 1500 security personnel annually. Iron Horse Security is a full service, all Canadian, non-union security company with our headquarters located here in Ottawa. We have additional offices in Gatineau, Montreal, and Toronto. We have successfully serviced clients in the sectors of property management, retail, manufacturing, special events, government, financial, construction, residential, hi tech and logistics.

Success stories:

Iron Horse has become an industry leader. Our award winning business practices have gained national recognition by Profit Magazine when for SEVEN years in a row we have won a position in their coveted, nationally ranked Profit 100 Fastest Growing Companies in Canada . Additionally we have been selected by the Greater Ottawa Chamber of Commerce for Business of the Year in their business Achievement Awards category. Iron Horse is also the winner of the Consumers Choice Award for the past 9 years in a row. Along with these awards our company President has also won the Ottawa Business Journals 40 Under Forty Award for business excellence amongst the top 40 business owners under 40 years of age. No other company in our field has won these industry leading awards. These awards are a direct reflection of our continued dedication towards maintaining our founding principles that puts the success of our clients and our people first.

Testimonial:

This almost always resulted in at least embarrassment if not a disaster of one kind or another. Keep up the good work fellas! - John Singleton, Thomas Fuller Construction
