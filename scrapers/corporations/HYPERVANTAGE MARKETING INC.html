Company Profile


Hypervantage Marketing Inc. is an internet marketing firm specializing in generating more web site traffic, greater online exposure and increased brand awareness for small businesses and professionals using cutting-edge digital marketing strategies including:

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
Pay-Per-Click Advertising Management (PPC)
Social Media Marketing & Monitoring
Reputation Management & Marketing

Founder and CEO, Stacy Quinn began his internet marketing career in 2008 where he polished his skills running several internet-based direct sales and affiliate businesses.

In his book, Stacy Quinns Guide to Marketing Your Business Online, Stacy takes internet marketing from A to Z and condenses it into an easy-to-read format that teaches business owners how to market their businesses online.

Stacy is an active member of the Local Internet Marketing Association (LIMA)

Service Name:
Internet Marketing
Internet marketing services generate more web site traffic, greater online exposure and increased brand awareness for small businesses and professionals through cutting-edge digital marketing strategies including:

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
Pay-Per-Click Advertising Management (PPC)
Social Media Marketing & Monitoring
Reputation Management & Marketing

Service Name:
Search Engine Optimization
Search Engine Optimization is the process of maximizing the number of visitors to a particular website by ensuring that the site appears high on the list of results returned by a search engine.

1) SEO begins with keyword research where we identify the best words your buyers are using to search for your products and services. These top phrases become the target of all other SEO activities.

2) On-page-Optimization is the one-time process of updating your website with content, structure and behind-the-scenes codes that feature your target keywords. This shows the search engines that your website is a good match for your keywords and that it should be listed in the top search results.

3) Off-page-Optimization is the on-going process of creating interesting social media posts, articles and videos etc. that are posted on other high ranking websites. This gives the search engines third-party validation that your website is good providing value for the people who are using their search services and that is should be listed in the top search results

Service Name:
Search Engine Marketing
Search engine marketing (SEM) is a form of internet marketing that involves the promotion of websites by increasing their visibility in search engine results pages through optimization and advertising.

SEM involves activities designed to create greater exposure of your web site within search engines. Some of these include search engine optimization (SEO), article creation and publishing, video creation and publishing and paid listings (PPC) .

Service Name:
Pay-Per-Click Advertising Management
Pay-Per-Click advertising is a strategy to create website visitors by paying advertisers to display your ads and offers. You pay the advertiser each time the ad is clicked and you get a web site visitor.

With the proper research, training, structure and on-going management, that Hypervantage provides, PPC campaigns are the fastest way to get website visitors and create ROI and profit.

Service Name:
Social Media Marketing and Monitoring
Social media marketing promotes you, your company, and your products and services to people using social media sites. It works well to enhance brand awareness. HMV's Social interaction and word-of-mouth strategies will also create new connections, leads and customers.

Social media monitoring is actively monitoring all your social media accounts for new friend requests, comments, inquiries, and especially complaints. Promptly responding to customers and prospects will create tremendous positive buzz for your company.


Service Name:
Reputation Management & Marketing
Reputation Monitoring is the on-going process of monitoring all your social media accounts as well as the 100's of business directory sites for negative online reviews. Early detection gives you the opportunity to fix customer complaints before new prospects or customers see the negative reviews and choose not to do business with you.

Reputation Marketing is the on-going process of creating a 5-Star online reputation for your company. Unlike reputation management, which is a defensive strategy, reputation marketing is an offensive strategy. HVMs systems, strategies, software and employee training encourages customers to provide you with positive on-line reviews.

Both reputation management and reputation marketing work together to create a comprehensive reputation strategy.
