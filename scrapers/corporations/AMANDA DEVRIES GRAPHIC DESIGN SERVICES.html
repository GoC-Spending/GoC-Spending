In the hopes of extending my client base, I approached Amanda about creating a website. Right from the outset, Amanda demonstrated a high degree of professionalism. Ultimately, I am extremely happy with the site and it did extend my client base. Thank you Amanda.

Shannon MacFarlane
Owner, New Arrivals Doula Services

I approached Amanda to help me create a web presence for my research program at McMaster University and the Public Health Agency of Canada. In my field, knowledge dissemination and information is critical and I wanted to create a site that was information-rich, easy to navigate, expandable and easy to update on a regular basis. Amanda listened to my vision for the site, made excellent suggestions and went on to build two prototype sites for us that were notable for their professional look, contemporary design and ease of navigation. Amanda has a keen sense for design and colour and incorporated these elements to create a site that flows. I have received numerous compliments on the site from professional colleagues and students, who use the site for front-line information about our research and lab developments. Thanks Amanda for bringing our science to the world.

Dr. Brian Coombes
Professor, McMaster University
Research Scientist, Public Health Agency of Canada
