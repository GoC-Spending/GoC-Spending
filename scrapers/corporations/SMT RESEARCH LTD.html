Company Profile


SMT is a supplier of analysis and monitoring solutions for clients engaged in the restoration, design, construction and management of buildings. SMT designs software and electronic systems used to monitor and evaluate specific parameters required by contractors, architects, engineers, builders and property managers.

Product Name:
Analytics Monitoring Software
SMT's Building Analytics monitoring center is an enterprise class transactional database system with an advanced web based front end. Analytics provides data collection, processing, analysis, and long term storage of sensor data.

Product Name:
Mobile Analytics Software
Lightweight web-based version of SMT Analytics targeted for smart phones such as the iPhone and Blackberry. Permits remote and mobile access to live and historic structural monitoring data.

Product Name:
Mobile Wireless Data Acquistition Unit

Product Name:
Industrial Data Acquisition Unit

Product Name:
Flood Detection Unit
Enhanced risk mitigation from flood damage. Rapid sampling provides near instantaneous response to flood alarms. Alarms can be forwarded to pagers, cell phones, email, and to building management systems.

Product Name:
Moisture Detection Sensor
Linear detection moisture sensor placed in areas prone to water intrusion. Self-adhesive flat, tape like sensor, permits easy installation and provides wide coverage

Product Name:
Point Moisture Detection Sensor

Product Name:
Embedded Moisture Sensor
Sensors are embedded in gypsum or concrete to detect relative moisture content through capillary absorption.

Product Name:
Indoor Air Quality Sensors
CO2, RH and Temperature Sensor for use in monitoring and evaluating indoor air quality.

Product Name:
Building Science Sensors
Sensitive pressure sensors, displacement sensors, solar radiation sensors as well as Relative Humidity and Temperature sensors are available.

Product Name:
DigiScan 360
The DigiScan 360 unit is a revolutionary leak detecting instrument for roof systems. Using a multiprobe sensor plate, the direction and severity of a membrane breach is displayed on an LCD interface.

Service Name:
Structural Health Monitoring
Complete Structural Health Monitoring solutions including specifying, sensor deployment as well as electronic and software implementation are available to clients. In addition to implementing the system, data collection, monitoring services and report generation are included.

Technology profile

The product suite designed by SMT is comprised of unique moisture sensors, high precision data acquisition electronic system and enterprise level analysis software. Moisture sensors consist of linear moisture detection tape capable of monitoring an entire roof surface or building envelope for leaks. Calibrated research grade moisture sensors are used by researchers and practitioners and most recently a new capacitance based moisture sensor capable of monitoring moisture content in masonry materials. Other third party sensors such as RH, temperature, heat flux, differential pressure and CO2 can easily be integrated with the system for the purpose of providing the necessary sensor data required for building performance validation.
