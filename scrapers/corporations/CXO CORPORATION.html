Company Profile


CXO Corporation (CXO) is a Consulting, Technology Solutions and Outsourcing company.

CXO Consulting specializes in helping companies implement Business Intelligence, Analytics, Data Integration, Machine Learning, CRM and Web and Mobile solutions to facilitate effective decision-making at all levels of the organization. CXO Technology Solutions designs, develops and implements innovative solutions to help your business to achieve better business results. CXO Outsourcing provides application outsourcing services. Using CXOs Global Delivery Network we are able to provide high-quality solutions, lower costs, reduced risk and quicker results.

CXOs consulting approach is to establish a collaborative relationship with our clients, understand their business needs and challenges, and leverage the extensive experience of our highly skilled professionals to devise the best solution. Our main focus is to generate measurable business value for all our clients.

At CXO, our mission is to help executives and managers make unique, lasting, and substantial improvements to the performance of their companies or organizations.

Service Name:
Business Analytics Consulting
Business Analytics Consulting Services

Companies these days are constantly dealing with complex issues and growing global competition. Business executives need to make proactive, decisions to improve corporate results every day. With our Analytics Consulting services, you can transform your information assets into true competitive advantage. Insights from data about customers, suppliers, operations, performance and more are buried in your ERPs and business intelligence systems, our Analytics services empowers you to:

Understand complex business problems.
Manage performance to achieve measurable business objectives.
Drive business growth through innovation.
Use sophisticated time  trending analysis to identify potential risks and opportunities
View data graphically for faster decision-making.
Business analysis is a vital component of corporate performance management and can help a company compete more aggressively. The right analytics consulting services can make the difference between producing analysis and putting it to use.

Our Analytics consulting services and robust analysis tools will enable your executives to perform advanced segmentation to isolate and analyze subsets of your business information. Our analytics projects will allow business users to perform advanced data analysis, including multiple dimensions, filtering and information visualization features. Fast-on-the-fly tools will let you dig deeper and manipulate data right in the report tables without requesting any help from the IT Department.
