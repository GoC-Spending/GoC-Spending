Company Profile


SST is a world leader in providing multi-network connectivity
solutions. SST designs and markets communication products for
the industrial world. Whether you're an end-user, systems
integrator or OEM, SST has the solutions you need for your plant
floor communication needs. Our innovative technologies keep you
connected with the ever-growing number of industrial protocols.

SST is the world's leading independent supplier of industrial
communication technology with a valued reputation for providing
reliable communication solutions.

1st Company in the world to pass PROFIBUS DP Master Conformance
Test
1st Company in the world to pass DeviceNet ODVA Conformance Test
1st Company to offer a 10 year warranty on industrial networking
interface cards
1st Company to develop PROFIBUS Multi-slave technology
1st Independent Company to develop ControlNet Chip technology
Serving the industrial controls marketplace for over 14 years,
SST has design experience with 40 plus industrial networks.

And with Offices in North America, Europe and Asia, SST is ready
to respond when and where we're needed.

SST has been the recipient of prestigious awards:

Innovation Award in 1991 from the Canadian Government
Highly Commended Award in 1996 by the UK Manufacturing Industry
Achievement Awards
50 Best Managed Private Company Award in Canada for 1997

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Embedded Network Solution
SST Universal Communication System (UCS) allows OEMs to adapt
their devices to a wide spectrum of network protocols. UCS
consists of interchangeable personality modules that ``bolt-on``
to the UCS host interface designed into the OEM product. These
plug and play modules feature full field upgradability and
provide connections to the major industrial networks without
additional programming or hardware redesigns.

Product Name:
I/O Simulation Software
SST's PICS Simulation software performs control system testing
and operator training. Using this to quickly create a dynamic
model on a PC that duplicates the behavior of the actual process
and provides the control software with simulated device
feedback. By simulating your process, you will save time and
money, manage the technical and scheduling risks associated with
a project, minimize process downtime and greatly enhance
operator training.

Product Name:
Network Gateways
SST's X-Link allows you to create a bridge between dissimilar
network protocols. With a large and expanding library of network
protocol drivers, X-Link is capable of creating over one thousand
gateway combinations without programming. Simply download the
appropriate network driver combination and set up data mappings
using a fill-in-the-blank interface.

Product Name:
Network Interface Cards
Feildbus and network communication cards for industrial
automation market.

Product Name:
Network Diagnostic and Configuration Software
Software programs that allow you to monitor, diagnose and
configure a network.

Product Name:
PLC Network Modules
Add versitility to your programable controllers by allowing it
to communicate with otherwise uncompatible networks or I/O
devices.

Sector information

Unique applications:

Amoco Canada has improved its control over core
processes to speed operations at its Fort
Saskatchewan, Alberta, processing and
distribution facility. All this happened because
Amoco found just the right way to solve a system
incompatibility problem.


Amoco was looking for a way to improve operations
at the Fort Saskatchewan facility. Controls there
were based on Allen-Bradley's family of
programmable logic controllers (PLCs), but the
protective relay systems, flow computers, and
stand alone controllers that Amoco needed for
faster, more detailed process control were based
on different operating systems.


The X-Link Gateway Engine transfers data between
industrial networks in real time, without
programming. Unlike conventional gateways, X-Link
generate a range of possible gateways from a
library of more than 1300 network protocol
drivers. Because X-Link can potentially provide
as many gateways as there are combinations or
protocols, it will continue to deliver value to
Amoco. Also X-Link can be applied as a general
purpose network communications tool.


After two years of use Amoco continues to be
impressed with how easy it is to set up and
operate X-Link. Once X-Link is installed and
configured, the gateway basically runs itself.
