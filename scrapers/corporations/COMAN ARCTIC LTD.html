Company Profile


We offer a wide variety of friendly and reliable service, unique and ever changing as the North itself. These services include: commercial property leasing, property management and maintenance, logistics, small or large moving services and general deliveries (including small local orders up to large cargo shipments to local food chains).

We also offer long and short term storage facilities. Let our long time Let our long time northern experience work for you! We have a strong advantage over our competitors! Coman Arctic Ltd has air side clearance and is fully insured with 10 employees on staff.
Coman Arctic also offers commercial car, van and truck rentals on site.
We also have certified polyurethane foam installers on staff. Our fleet also includes a mobile spray rig for convenient and efficient transportation to job sites. Also we now offer a fireproofing spray-on application.

Coman Arctic is a distributor of heat-line freeze protection products.

Newest addition to our growth is that we have raw bandwidth to available for purchase in the Arctic. www.comanarctic.com
