Company Profile


Established in 1958, Canadian Puregas Equipment Limited is an
independent Canadian owned and controlled corporation. Since
that time Canadian Puregas Equipment Limited has provided air
network services with a dedicated and increasing level of
service. These services include complete air systems,
installation, training and maintenance. Originally, dryers and
pressurization equipment manufactured by the Puregas Equipment
Company were sold and serviced for the Canadian domestic
telecommunications market.

In 1975 Canadian Puregas began manufacturing pressurization
equipment, progressing to complete dryers and panel systems by
1979. This greatly improved our ability to provide custom
requirements to our customers. Canadian Puregas has established
itself as a leader in its field and provides air network
services worldwide. These services include complete air systems,
installation, training and maintenance.

Since 1990, Canadian Puregas has been supplying and servicing
cable pressure monitoring systems built by Nicotra System of
Italy. Canadian Puregas has installed systems and worked with
Telcos to adapt the systems to existing facilities and
requirements. These systems use dedicated solid state pressure
and flow transducers for computer generated alarming and leak
location. These systems are also capable of monitoring fibre
optic cable and cabinets for security.

Canadian Puregas has full production facilities located in
Canada. We also supply and service industrial puregas dryers
used for instrumentation, spectrometers, CO2 and hydrocarbon
adsorbers. Other related products are sold and serviced by
Canadian Puregas. These include ventilation and heating
equipment and aerial lift trucks. Full training on products is
carried out at Canadian Puregas facilities or at the customer's
site. Canadian Puregas sells and services dryers and associated
equipment to the telecommunications, microwave and
instrumentation industries as well as for radar applications.

Our head office is located in Dunnville, Ontario, Canada with
representation in Montreal for Quebec..
International representation in Mexico, Costa Rica, Uruguay, Italy, South Africa, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, Malaysia and China.
