Company Profile


ADFLOW Networks has over a decade of in-store experience and offers patented technology that is innovative, scalable and simple to use. ADFLOW provides a web-hosted solution for the deployment and operation of digital signage and interactive touch screen enabled networks, and the online management of digital media supply chains. The ADFLOW DYNAMIC MESSAGING SYSTEM is simple to deploy and requires zero I.T. infrastructure.

Top brand leaders like Mark's, TELUS Mobility, Meridian CU, WIND Mobile, WAWA, Select Comfort and many other have selected ADFLOW to manage their networks, drive sales and productivity, reduce costs and enhance the customer experience where it matters most: at the point-of-purchase.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
ADFLOW Digital Messaging System

As a leader in customer engagement technology from the very beginning, ADFLOW has continually “pushed the envelope” in providing innovative and important software platform capabilities.
The challenge in creating scalable software is providing the ease of use, and installation required for a small installation of a few sites and a few screens, and yet have the robust functionality to automatically schedule content to hundreds of locations and thousands of screens. At ADFLOW satisfying both ends of the market, has been a design goal since the beginning. Our software was designed by marketing for use by marketing, so ease of use has been a hallmark since day one. However, managing hundreds or thousands of screens with the same software requires functionality that automates complex scheduling, and other repetitive tasks.
ADFLOW’s DMS 10.0 represents the new standard of excellence for digital signage software. With far too many features to list here, a few highlights are:
One of the biggest complaints about digital signage solutions are comments such as “the system keeps going down” or “we have blue screens in our stores” or “we lost the internet connection and now have no content playing. Reliability is about having a system you can count on. At ADFLOW our average uptime is 99.8%. Our track record in this regard has been maintained with our current client base over the last five and a half years.






Product Name:
ADFLOW Interactive Digital Signage Kiosk

While traditional “static” digital signage is very effective, today’s digital technologies allow for the creation of unique, one on one, interactive experiences. Allowing customers to interact with a touch screen to learn about a product, compare features, self-educate and entertain themselves takes digital signage technologies to a whole new level.

Unlike many of our competitors that require either a separate technology or extensive modifications to provide interactive, ADFLOW’s DMS provides support for both static and interactive digital signage experiences.

ADFLOW’s new patent for “Interactive Digital Signage Kiosk Technology” relates to the use of our “lift and touch” technology.

The patent simplifies the complex problem of maintaining and updating content related to devices tethered in an interactive product display. The technology has the ability to identify and align the appropriate content with each tethered product. ADFLOW’s customers can manage a promoted campaign easily and with minimal effort.













Service Name:
ADFLOW Content Strategy and Project Managment

CONTENT

At ADFLOW we live by the motto that “Content is king. Without the right content, a digital signage solution is not going to deliver the desired results. Our content team will work with you to ensure that your content is timely, relevant and targeted; whether advertising through influencing POS decisions, or educating clients about services and current news and weather. Our content team has experience and thus a set of best practices when determining factors relevant to creating content for different digital displays; such as font and image sizes and colours, video or image lengths, and appropriate numbers of words or images.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

A successful digital signage project requires the coordination of a lot of moving parts. Synchronizing the arrival of hardware, installation crews, and the timely delivery of content requires a project management methodology that accounts for all activities and their ownership. Over our 13 years we have managed single site, single screen installation and installation of hundreds of sites and thousands of screens. We have developed best practices for managing both. ADFLOW’s project management service is your insurance policy that the project will be done, on time and on budget in the least invasive way to your business

Unique applications:

Mark’s Work Wearhouse, a subsidiary of Canadian Tire Corp

Mark’s new strategy is based upon customer communications with emphasis on the added value of innovative clothing, which is to be showcased by entertaining and visually appealing displays. Ultimately this will enhance the customer’s in-store experience and educate them on the hidden value of Mark’s clothing in a compelling manner. The screen content is focused on customer buying opportunities and encouraging purchases - this is an essential part of the Digital Signage equation.

he new Mark’s look consists of various in-store screens including 52” World Screens, which are LCD panels that are mounted around the walls of the store to create a targeted brand ambiance. The stores will also have a Product Innovation Fixture, which are 40” LCD panels build into the product ﬁxtures to drive sales. Mark’s will also have 40” portrait style screens called Tech Towers that will be touch enabled to deliver an “edu-tainment” experience. Behind the check out counter there will also be a video wall consisting of four 40” screens to provide an excellent overall brand experience.
