Company Profile


Entec Corporation is recognized for being on the cutting edge of measuring employee satisfaction, employee engagement, employee emotional well-being and leadership. We now offer all of these surveys for free at www.EmployeeSurveyToolkit.com in a fully automated format. The system is very easy to use but provides detailed reports for location, organizational units down to departments, job levels, job categories, union/non union, demographics etc. The user chooses the data cuts. These are available instantly both during the survey process as well as at the end. Results are shown in tables as well as pie charts-all instantly. This is an amazing technology and its all for free.

Employee performance is affected by several components most
notably: how employees feel towards their company, managers, immediate work environment and employee support of company values. These are the dimensions typically measured by most research firms. A component that is not measured and that has a significant impact on an employee's performance is the employee's emotional/psuchological make up. Recognizing this additional component, and the desire to view employee engagement from a holistic perspective, Entec assembled a team comprising experts in strategic management, leadership, organizational development, psychiatry, medicine and behavioural psychology to develop a comprehensive diagnostic to measure employee engagement. A new diagnostic was developed to measure both organizational factors and employee emotional wellness. The diagnostic is called the Employee Engagement & Individual Health Survey©.

Our surveys are available in French and English.
