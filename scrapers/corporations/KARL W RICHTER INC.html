Company Profile


At Karl W. Richter we pride ourselves at being experts in fulfilling the complete spectrum of Supply Chain Management services as well as offering you the finest quality, best represented product lines. KWRtools.com promises to become one of your best resources for navigating through our in-depth, well stocked inventory, and provide you with the necessary technical details required to streamline and simplify your ordering process.

Over the years, Karl W. Richter has become well recognized for specific categories of products. The following pages quickly demonstrate how extensive and complete the entire Karl W. Richter product line has become and how extensively our range of services has developed.

We would also like to take this opportunity to thank you for almost 50 years of support. Many of our innovative service and product concepts have been born out of your needs and requirements, which has given Karl W. Richter the opportunity to expand and improve our product offering. We do not take for granted the fact that our achievements to date, and overall longevity, are a result of your patronage and support.

From individual product innovations to complete turnkey solutions, Karl W. Richter is your partner in production.

Technology profile

Technology is one of the key strengths that Karl W. Richter Inc. offers. We believe data interfacing and the use of technology to share information with our client base is critical to the success of both our businesses.

Listed below are a few of the ways we have invested in technology to make us a great supply option. Of course, we always look forward to your requests on how any feature of technology would make us a better supply partner, so we invite you to challenge us in this area.

Blackberry Enterprise Server for real time e-mail for salespersons and key personnel.

SolidWorks for complete in-house CAD capabilities.

SupplierCONNECT web interface creates an unmatched communication link to our manufacturing supply base.

CustomerCONNECT web interface, which offers information about your part numbers via a dynamic search engine, including:

CAD file linking
Catalog page downloads
Manufacturers crossover
Duplicate identifications
Contract pricing
All contract parameters (if applicable)
Video demos
MSDS sheets
Promotions & special offers
ROHMS is our exclusive software, which manages customer site inventory programs from hand-held scanners. It has been developed to manage all levels of expediting via an EDI portal directly from your production floor. Although developed as our key utility for our VMI Field Agents, your staff can also access this user-friendly technology where preferred.

WHSEManager is being employed to manage our own warehouses completely wireless, including receiving, order management, overstock, quick-pick locations, quarantine and returns.
