Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Municipal Database
Municipal associations and organizations can utilize this Microsoft Access database to maintain perpetual control over their policy and resolution documents. The database is used by staff throughout the year and on-site at conventions it can be displayed on projector screens for live resolution viewing, amendments and voting. It can be easily integrated into any website for an ongoing easy way for members and/or the public to keep up-to-date with resolutions and policies. Created for the Association of Manitoba Municipalities and now in use elsewhere as the Resolution Database.
Please see http://www.municipaldatabase.com or call 204-999-8844 for more information.

Product Name:
Resolutions Database
Unions, member-based associations and all sorts of other organizations can utilize this database to maintain perpetual control over their policies, resolutions or any other type of official documents.
The database is used by your office staff throughout the year and even on-site at conventions it can be displayed on projector screens for live resolution viewing, amendments and voting. It can be easily integrated into any website for an ongoing easy way for members and/or the public to keep up-to-date with document changes (resolutions, policies). Created for municipal and political parties and in use elsewhere as the Municipal Database.

Saves time, eliminates double-entry, keeps you accountable to your members. Creates a searchable database usable on a local network or remotely (with Citrix or Terminal Services) to replace a paper filing process that may currently exist.

Please see http://www.resolutionsdatabase.com or call 204-480-4484 for more information.

Product Name:
Food Broker Database
The Food Broker Database has been custom designed in Microsoft Access to be used by all types of Food Brokers. In use across Canada and now in the USA, this software allows the order taker(s) to enter and send orders (printed, faxed or emailed as PDF) to vendors/principals. Maintains customers, vendors, products and prices as well as commission tracking reports (password protected) and features customer-specific pricing (optional). Full featured program supports all standard food broker scenarios.

Please call 204-999-8844 for more information or to setup a demo.

Service Name:
IT Consulting, Development and Support
We provide small and medium businesses with smart advice and can further design, develop, implement and support various software, hardware and network solutions.
