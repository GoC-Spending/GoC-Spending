Company Profile


Located in Thetford Mines (Quebec, Canada), DSD International
Inc. was founded in 1987 in order to produce its first invention
the STOP FLOW safety spout. Over the years, we have diversified
our activities from injection molding to include mold and die
design, development and fabrication as well as plastic and metal
processing equipment and specialized tooling.

In 1993, we moved into a new ultramodern factory which houses
separate injection molding and mold & die departments. State of
the art equipment and highly qualified personnel contribute to
the enviable position which we have established in the market
place. 40% of our activities consists in subcontracting
components for different clients. The principal activity of our
company remains the production of our own line of hardware and
horticultural products.

Our horticultural line of products offers two models of
permanent and reusable stakes for plants, shrubs, trees and
gardening STAKE+PLUS and ECONO-STAKE as well as our tree trunk
protector PROTEC-TRUNK an our new INFO-STAND. Our stakes are
made of UV resistant material and come with a solid anchor base
and adjustable attachments to which can be added our
STRAPS+PLUS.

STAKE+PLUS made of fiberglass and of resistant plastic is
available in different heights and sizes of rod. ECONO-STAKE
can be ``extended`` to meet different height requirements.
Economical and easy to use, our ECONO-STAKE is made of sturdy UV
resistant fiberglass reinforced of plastic. Adjustable and
reusable, PROTEC-TRUNK provides maximum protection from string
trimmer, mowers and small rodents. Our new product INFO-STAND,
is an information stand especially designed for garden centers.
INFO-STAND is available in two base types, a flat base or a high
grip anchor base.

Let us now introduce you to some of our hardware products:
Safety STOP FLOW the unique safety spout with spring loaded
valve which safely controls the flow of liquid. Between each
use the spring loaded valve remains closed preventing evaporation
and spillage. No evaporation, no pollution, no spill, no mess
and no danger. Available in two models: for gasoline containers
and for chemical containers (windshield washer, insecticide,
etc.)

You may already have heard of our line of driveway markers or
boundary posts VISION+PLUS. VISION+PLUS is provided with a
strong anchoring base, a resistant linear fiberglass pultruded
rod and a smart looking top marker. VISION+PLUS is offered in
different models, heights and sizes of rod. New: VISION+PLUS
WITH DOUBLE REFLECTORS! The reflectors are made of acrylic,
like the ones we find on cars and bicycles.
