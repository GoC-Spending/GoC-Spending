Company Profile


The Professional Printing Solutions Group (PPSG) is a division of Canon Canada. Formed in January 2014 after Océ-Canada Inc. was amalgamated with Canon Canada Inc., PPSG is the leading provider of printing solutions for professionals. PPSGs product offerings include digital presses, high speed digital production printers, wide format printing systems and workflow software for transaction, direct mail, graphic arts, technical documentation and colour display graphics.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Wide Format - Technical Document Systems
Copy, print, plot, scan, manage and electronically distribute both black & white or colour wide format technical documents with our full product portfolio of large format printers, plotters, copiers, scanners, finishing equipment and software. Additionally, our print job submission software solutions will help increase your productivity for your large format plotter applications in conjunction with our portfolio of high quality colour and black & white scanners and integrated folders that deliver the professional output you are looking for in technical document drawings. Our products are designed for the AEC, GIS, Government, reprographic market, and in-house wide format printing.

Black-and-white systems => Océ PlotWave 345, Océ PlotWave 365, Océ PlotWave 450, Océ PlotWave 550, Océ PlotWave 750 and Océ PlotWave 900.
Colour systems => Océ ColorWave 500, Océ ColorWave 700, Océ ColorWave 810, Océ ColorWave 910.
Scanners => Océ TC4 Scanner, Océ 610 Scanner
Finishers => Our folders for large format colour and monochrome technical documents, can be integrated with Océ large format printers as stand-alone folders.

Product Name:
Wide Format - Display Graphic Systems
Providing printing solutions for sign and display applications to the graphics arts industry, the advertising sector and the print-for-pay industry. These wide format colour printers are to create indoor and outdoor advertising products such as full-colour posters, banners, billboards and other forms of wide format graphics communication as well as industrial applications (tile, plastics, textiles, wood, metal, etc.) that are less than 2" thick.

Products include:
Océ Arizona 6100 Series (6160XTS, 6170XTS)
Océ Arizona 1200 Series (1240GT, 1240XT, 1260GT, 1260XT, 1280GT, 1280XT)
Océ Arizona 2200 Series (2240GT, 2240XT, 2260GT, 2260XT, 2280GT, 2280XT)
Océ Arizona 318GL


Finishing Options include:
Océ ProCut flatbed cutter line includes nine highly accurate, automated cutting systems that deliver exceptional cut quality, versatility and productivity for all display graphic applications. Part of a complete digital workflow system including Océ ProCut workflow software, Océ ProCut digital cutting tables can streamline your large format digital printer workflow from pre-press to finishing.

Océ display graphics software to streamline workflow from prepress to finishing and ensure consistent output and colour management.
Products include:
ONYX Workflow Océ Edition
Océ ProCut software
ONYX Thrive Océ Edition

Product Name:
Production Printing Systems
Production Printing Systems (PPS) offers an extensive and wide range of colour and black & white cutsheet and continuous feed production printers and digital presses as well as an open architecture of Software platforms to drive these products for transaction, graphic arts, corporate environments and for printing and publishing. Sheet fed and web fed production printers for black and white and full colour print production. Software for creation, job submission, production management, pre-press and make-ready, order processing and archiving of print jobs.

Performance ranges from 155 to 2,700 A4 pages per minute with various technologies to suit the financial sector, the telecom businesses, energy suppliers, government and educational institutions as well as all book publishers and businesses in trade and industries.

Our Océ duplex cutsheet and sheet fed production printers provide high speed and high quality printing for commercial printers and corporate environments. With finishing options such as booklet makers, book makers, binders, stackers, hole punching and roll feeders.
Products include:
B&W => Océ VarioPrint 6160, Océ VarioPrint 6200, Océ VarioPrint 6250, Océ VarioPrint 6320 Ultra+ Line.
Colour => Océ VarioPrint i300
Finishing options include => Océ BLM200, Océ BLM550, Océ BLM6300, Océ BGB3202, Océ iHCS, GBC Power Punch.

Our Océ continuous feed black and white printers offer flexible and versatile production printers provide high quality prints for commerce and financial services including monochrome invoices, bank statements, mailings and graphic arts-type applications.
Products include:
B&W => Océ VarioPrint 4000 Series, Océ VarioPrint 7100 Series, Océ VarioPrint 8000 Series.
Finishing options include => Hunkeler Roll Feeder, Tecnau Roll Systems Roll Feeder, Videk Camera Systems

Our Océ continuous feed colour printers offer near-offset quality production printing. They provide high quality colour prints for service bureaus, graphic arts operations, offset printers and other corporate printers.
Products include:
Colour => Océ ImageStream Series, Océ ColorStream 3000Z Series, Océ JetStream Dual Series, Océ JetStream Wide Series.
Finishing options include => Hunkeler Roll Feeder, Tecnau Roll Systems Roll Feeder, EMT, Videk Camera Systems

Software for creation, job submission, production management, pre-press and make-ready, order processing and archiving of print jobs.
Products include:
Job submission => Océ PRISMAdirect, Océ DPconvert.
Production Management => Océ PRISMAproduction, Océ PRISMAspool.
Pre-Press and Make Ready => Océ PRISMAprepare, Océ DocSetter, Océ TrueProof.
Archiving => Océ PRISMAarchive

Product Name:
Imaging Supplies
Supplies a broad range of wide-format media for all Océ printing systems. Comprising plain paper, recycled bond paper, coated paper and specialties.

Banner Stands: A full line of PrimeX and ExpoLinc Banner Stands for short or long term applications.
Bond Paper: 20, 24, 35, 46 lb from 12" to 36" roll on 3" or 2" cores.
Inkjet Display Media: Papers and films for inkjet printing, both aqueous and solvent.
PVC and Scrim Vinyls: A complete line of PVC and Scrim Vinyls for most solvent printers.
Recycled bond: 20lb
Textiles for inkjet printing: A broad range of textiles for solvent printing.
Toner / Ink: Toner and ink for Océ printers and plotters.
