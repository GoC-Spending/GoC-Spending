Success stories:

We were the official customs broker for the
International Society for Heart Research
convention held in Winnipeg. When one of the
participants from Australia encountered
difficulties with a shipment held up in Toronto
due to Lack of proper documentation and import
information. It was a shipment of Koala bears
and other promotional materials promoting the
next I.S.H.R conference to be held in Australia
in 2004. We received a call from the conference
and the Australian coordinator asking for
assistance. Although the company did not
originally utilize our services our President Bob
Hobson, intervened and did what was necessary to
ensure the shipment got to Winnipeg, cleared
through customs and delivered on time. Needless
to say, our customer was very happy.

Testimonial:
