Company Profile


We welcome this opportunity to introduce you to Ottawa's newest communications and advertising agency. Forest Communications
Inc. was founded in December of 2000 by an experienced and innovative team with a fresh energy and an exciting vision.

Our agency is comprised of talented individuals with extensive and diverse knowledge. They bring to Forest Communications Inc. a
wealth of experience with organizations such as Skate Canada, Industry Canada, BMO Financial, Correctional Service Canada, Human Resources Management Agency of Canada, Law Commission of Canada, Octasic Inc., Canadian Platics Council, National Seafood Council, and Agriculture Canada just to name a few. Already, we have a proven track record with our clients for delivering high quality work on time and on budget.

We are truly dedicated to our clients and we are diligent in analyzing their communications needs by drawing from their experienced input and conducting our own market studies. We take a great deal of pride in our work and creativity and we look forward to the possibility of working with you and your team in the near future.

If you require any additional information or clarification, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Our Vision
Forest Communications is a young, vibrant, creative agency. We strive to create simple but effective communications solutions. No matter which medium or vehicle you choose to communicate your message, we make sure you get the right results.

Creative
From the root of the problem to the peak of the solution, the sky's the limit when it comes to our creative solutions. We invite you to take a deep breath and explore the possibilities at Forest Communications.

Services
You want services, we've got services! Print management, web solutions, radio and television production... we can deliver your message using a wide variety of methods.

Corporate
From corporate branding to visual communications, we can create the right solution that will GROW your company and its image. Whether it's consumer based, internal needs, or business to business, we can deliver your projects on time and on budget.

Advertising
Need to recruit more talent, sell business to business, get a product in the public eye, or GROW your image? From television to radio, or newspaper to direct mail, we can build a package that will suit your needs and get the results you're looking for!

Interactive
Web site anyone? Flash or PowerPoint presentation? Interactive
CD? Dynamic Web pages? Or how about a smart, slick interface design to cure your usability headaches? Here at Forest Communications we strive to create interactive solutions for your interactive needs.

Sports
He shoots he scores! From creating great new identities to promoting the latest sports franchise, our extensive experience in promoting sporting events and teams is your best weapon against the competition. We can help you get fans in the stands and build excitement in the air! Step up to the plate and get in the game!

Print
Printing can be a chore... but no worries, with our knowledge of the printing industry, we can make sure your projects look great. Whether it's a sign or an annual report, we can manage your printing needs and make sure your products are printed on time and on budget.
