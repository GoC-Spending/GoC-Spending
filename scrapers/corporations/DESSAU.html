Company Profile


Dessau is a Canadian consulting firm providing
multidisciplinary services in the engineering field. Dessau represents
50 years of experience and counts 4700 employees.
Active in both the national and international markets, Dessau offers a wide range of expertise:

· Transportation · Energy
· Telecommunications · Environment and Sustainable Development
· Geotechnics,Quality/Materials Engineering· Building Engineering
· Urban Development
· Project & Construction Management
. Mangement and operation of infrastructures

While remaining true to traditional engineering services,
Dessau seeks continually to respond to emerging issues
and technologies by developing and demonstrating its expertise
in fields such as operations management, soil decontamination,
energy efficiency services and building management.

Dessau's corporate philosophy is one of commitment to
quality of its work and the satisfaction of its clients. An
approach based on a quality management constitutes an integral
part of the execution of its mandates. Dessau and Soprin, as
well as their subsidiaries, have all achieved ISO 9001 (2000
Version)Certification in all fields of expertise offered across
Quebec and internationally.

Environmental Profile

DESSAU offers a full range of environmental services:
integrated contaminant management, waste management,
environmental assessment, monitoring and follow-up, risk
analysis and management, ecological studies and natural resource
management, site characterization, decontamination,
rehabilitation and solid waste management, biophysical
inventories, environmental impact studies of various
infrastructures (roads, ports, airports, lines and stations,
dams and so forth), urban and environmental acoustics, municipal
and environmental engineering, wastewater management and
treatment (purification plants and collection), drinking water
management and treatment (filtration plants, pumping stations),
oceanographic studies, hydrogeology, strategic planning,
methodologies and training, geotechnical studies, metallurgy and
material testing and quality assurance.


The globalization of markets and the emergence of huge
engineering projects in many countries have led the firm's
specialists to offer services on several continents. DESSAU is currently operating offices in Algeria, Chile Comlumbia and Peru. DESSAU wishes to
establish contacts with senior managers in the public and
private sector, mainly in the areas of wastewater and drinking
water management, transportation, energy (production,
transportation and distribution), telecommunications, natural
resource management and quality assurance.

Product Name:
Environmental Products and Services
Product Name:
Multidiscipline testing facility that provides a full-range of sampling, testing and analyses.
As well as providing the services of a chemical testing, research and consulting laboratory, the company can also provide consulting services to international clients on the construction and operation of laboratories. These services can include the conceptual design and final construction, equipment acquisition, the selection and training of laboratory staff in analytical techniques, quality assurance programs, operations and laboratory management, development of computerized data management and reporting systems.

Product Name:
Multidisciplinary consulting services that provide scientific analysis, environmental impact assessment and innovative solutions.
Multidisciplinary environmental impact assessments allow a site to be assessed for past or future environmental impacts. The firm works closely with the client in researching the past uses of a site, as well as determining the possible effects of any proposed uses or projects. The client's needs, and the parameters of the environmental problem or project are taken into consideration throughout the assessment. The assessment may include the use of tools such as GIS, computer simulation and modelling, and statistical analysis. The firm will then develop an approach or master plan tailored to the client's needs and problems and issues outlined. If necessary, communication plans, training and public education programs may also be implemented.

Technology profile

In response to the challenges of globalization, Dessau
has positioned itself at the crossroads of potential users and
suppliers of technology and knowledge. The firm specializes in
those fields that best reflect the excellence and the
recognition of Canada on the international scene.

Founded in a country with vast open spaces as well as a severe
and varied climate, Dessau has been successful in
developing solutions adapted to local conditions. The firm
carries out projects in the fields of rural electrification,
road rehabilitation, access to telecommunications, water supply,
and economic development. Dessau involvement in such
projects is evidence of the firm's commitment to improve quality
of life. Mindful of the economic, social and cultural concerns,
the firm intends to contribute to the well-being of local
communities. Through its experience in key areas of development,
Dessau has become a recognized partner in the
implementation of international projects that combine ingenuity,
flexibility and imagination.

Dessau considers technology transfer as a crucial
component in international projects and, as a result, has always
encouraged the participation of local specialists and
contractors. The company also urges managers, engineers and
professionals in the host countries to join its project teams.
Special attention is placed on planning and preparing for the
effective transfer of knowledge and proper management processes
as well as on the training of its clients' personnel. Aware of
the existing dynamism of local communities, Dessau
capitalizes on their initiatives and seeks private sector
involvement.

Knowledgeable about many international regions and their
particularities, Dessau proposes viable and sustainable
solutions. Its technical savvy combined with its ability to
develop quality relationships over time and around the world
have earned the firm the privilege to count itself among
Canada's
most important contributors to community development in many
countries.

Technology

Technology profile

In response to the challenges of globalization, Dessau
has positioned itself at the crossroads of potential users and
suppliers of technology and knowledge. The firm specializes in
those fields that best reflect the excellence and the
recognition of Canada on the international scene.

Founded in a country with vast open spaces as well as a severe
and varied climate, Dessau has been successful in
developing solutions adapted to local conditions. The firm
carries out projects in the fields of rural electrification,
road rehabilitation, access to telecommunications, water supply,
and economic development. Dessau involvement in such
projects is evidence of the firm's commitment to improve quality
of life. Mindful of the economic, social and cultural concerns,
the firm intends to contribute to the well-being of local
communities. Through its experience in key areas of development,
Dessau has become a recognized partner in the
implementation of international projects that combine ingenuity,
flexibility and imagination.

Dessau considers technology transfer as a crucial
component in international projects and, as a result, has always
encouraged the participation of local specialists and
contractors. The company also urges managers, engineers and
professionals in the host countries to join its project teams.
Special attention is placed on planning and preparing for the
effective transfer of knowledge and proper management processes
as well as on the training of its clients' personnel. Aware of
the existing dynamism of local communities, Dessau
capitalizes on their initiatives and seeks private sector
involvement.

Knowledgeable about many international regions and their
particularities, Dessau proposes viable and sustainable
solutions. Its technical savvy combined with its ability to
develop quality relationships over time and around the world
have earned the firm the privilege to count itself among
Canada's
most important contributors to community development in many
countries.
