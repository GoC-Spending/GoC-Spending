Company Profile


At CAP, we don’t just embrace change. We drive it. In the engineering and construction industry, new technologies and skillsets are more important than ever. We’re not only keeping pace with this ever-changing landscape. We’re shaping it, here + now.

Since 2005, we’ve provided engineering, construction, and management services to clients across Canada. Our interdisciplinary team traverses many fields, both professionally and geographically. From project development and design, through to delivery, we’re continuously expanding our capabilities and advancing our regional expertise.

CAP’s integrated services allow the right people to work together, ensuring greater foresight and project efficiency. We take the greatest minds – the thinkers, dreamers, and doers – and let them do what they do best. This is how we continuously deliver dynamic results.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Construction Management Services
Our management services are integrated, from development and design, through to construction. We’ve streamlined our processes, allowing us to deliver simplified solutions.

First, we plan your contract, schedule, and budget. Next, we work with our hand-selected team of architects, engineers, suppliers, and subcontractors to realize your project’s vision.

Clear and consistent communication is key. Our construction specialists are on the ground, providing real-time updates as your project progresses. We consult and collaborate with our partners to get the job done right, efficiently and effectively.

Service Name:
Engineering Solutions
We’re problem solvers. We’re thinkers, dreamers, and doers. We know dynamic engineering doesn’t just tackle a particular design or analysis challenge. It’s about turning the problem on its head and confronting our fundamental understanding of it.

With expertise in civil, geotechnical, structural, mechanical, and electrical engineering, our team can streamline every aspect of your project.

Service Name:
Construction
Our drive is undeterred. Nothing excites us more than tackling construction projects that pose complex logistical challenges. With expertise in heavy civil and high voltage utility projects, we have a fleet of equipment ready to mobilize anywhere across Western Canada.

At CAP, we ask questions. We rethink solutions, before they become problems. From our resourceful office staff to our knowledgeable field crews, we’re dedicated to completing your project on time and on budget. And, when undertaking the build component of a project, CAP performs a detailed design review to uncover cost savings. No matter the project parameters, together we’ll achieve remarkable results.
