Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Electrical Systems Contractors
We possess the infrastructure and competencies to provide a
single point of contact for turn-key needs including high
voltage systems above 750 volts, overhead and underground power
lines, PLC control and industrial automation, low voltage
distribution and wiring, fiber optic, program logic control,
data and communication systems, information metering systems and
pyrotenax installations and repairs. During our operating career
we have successfully offered these services to a great variety
of clients and industries.

Industries Served

Tri-R Systems offers a wide range of specific services to many
industries.

Industrial Consumers & Large Manufacturers:
Replacement equipment for failures of normal supply  Remote
site power requirements  Facility maintenance  Testing
supplies  Backup for OEM equipment failures - New equipment
installations  Renovations - Site services

Entertainment Industry:
Substation installation and rental - Permanent and temporary
power equipment - Set electrical systems

Transportation  Railway, Marine and Harbor Operations:
Movable bridges power & control - Signals and Communications
trackside services - Switching yard floodlighting & building
maintenance - Pollution monitoring and control systems - Gang
consist boarding car systems - Locomotive sanding and fuelling
facilities - CCTV Surveillance and operations camera systems -
Shorepower services for vessels - Barge slip and loading ramps

Digital PCS , Ident, Cellular, Wireless Data sites:

Site engineering - Shelter construction and installation -
Heliax cabling,

Terminations and testing - Antennas installations and testing -
Site Services


Commercial Construction  (restaurants, schools, retail outlets,
hotels, light manufacturing and office buildings):
Complete new construction, renovation and repairs from
substation to lighting.

Farming Operations:
Pumping Systems - PLC / Control Systems - Powerlines

Cities, Municipalities, & Districts:
Sewage & water pumping systems - Street lighting - Parks &
recreation lighting

Operating Equipment

We are constantly reviewing and modifying our equipment and tool
inventories to enable us to respond quickly and safely to our
customers regular and emergency needs. We maintain all of our
equipment, both vehicles and tools, to ensure availability and
employee and customer safety in their use.

Digger / Derrick Poleline Truck
Flat Deck Crane Truck

Tandem Axle Tilt Trailer (2)

Excavator
843 Bobcat Loader

Off Road Vehicle (powerline access - hi-rail equipped)
Aluminum Jet Boat (20 ft) (rescue/work boat for bridges)

All licensed vehicles meet operations requirements including
Motor Vehicle Inspection, Dielectric and Non Destruct Testing
Certifications.
