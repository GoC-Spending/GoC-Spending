Company Profile


Environmental Remediation Equipment (ERE) Inc. Is a Canadian
distribution and manufacturing company established to serve and
equip the industrial, petrochemical, wastewater, environmental
and governmental sectors. So as to provide solutions to meet the
demands of stringent regulations required for a healthier and
safer environment.

ERE has supplied various industries with a broad range of
environmental remediation equipment ad remediation services
throughout Canada and overseas.

The eastern office in Montreal was established in 1994 and has
supplied and continues to supply environmental equipment and
services throughout Quebec and bordering regions. Since it s
conception the eastern office has grown in staff and expertise.

The wester office was founded in the fall of 1996 to better serve
and existing clientele and western companies that express
interest in the extensive ERE product line.

ERE is considered a reliable ally in aiding its clients to manage
their environmental concerns, offering proven sound and cost
effective solutions to the challenges posed by them. Technical
know how, research and development, flexibility and customer
service sets ERE apart from the competition and assures
courteous, prompt and confidential response. ERE personnel are
experienced professionals and practitioners from various facets
of the environmental industry, our knowledge and expertise
guarantees clients satisfaction.
