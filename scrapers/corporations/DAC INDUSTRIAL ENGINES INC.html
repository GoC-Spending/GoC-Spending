Company Profile


DAC Industrial Engines Inc. is representing the following product lines; Deutz, Kubota, Hatz, Honda Engines, Ford Power Products, Kohler Engines, Robin Subaru: engines, generators and pumps, Lister Petter, Wisconsin Continental, Cummins Onan, RK Compressors, Mann & Hummel Filters, Hayes Couplings, HiPower Generators, Murphy and Lofa Control Panels, and Interstate Batteries. We are also a certified Wacker Tamper Plate, factory trained on Weber tamper plate repair and Norton Clipper repair along with trained pressure washer repair technicians backed with quality stocked OEM parts.
Our repair facility professionally overhauls and services many makes of diesel engines (marine and Industrial) as well as offering on-site repairs. We also custom build generator sets for our clients.
We thank you for your time and certainly thank you for your business.
