Company Profile


Jimbob Rentals (2000) Ltd. has been fabricating Bridges from our main yard in Rocky Mountain House for approximately 30-years. We would confirm that our Bridges are fabricated, certified and designed to CAN/CSA-S6-88 Standards and are welded to the W59 code. Further, we have approved Welding Procedures in place.

Designed in conformance with all relevant CSA specifications, Jimbob portable bridges are quickly and easily installed to provide safe, convenient crossing over streams & creeks. Our fleet of Bridges, in stock, includes 30, 40, 60 and 80 structures, which are 14 wide and have either a 50-ton or 75-ton rating. We are also able to provide non-typical size and multi-span structures.

ACCESS BRIDGE

Access Bridges are designed to provide access to the opposite river/creek bank while installing temporary or permanent bridges. These 40 50-ton structures have two tracks with an adjustable width to accommodate various equipment and vehicles.
This particular bridge is very useful during bridges placement when water disturbance or entry is prohibited.

ENVIRO BRIDGES

Designed to assist with bridge installations to provide access to the opposite river/creek bank our Enviro Bridge fleet includes 30 & 40 structures with either a steel top or wood running deck. Enviro bridges low profile height of 12 inches makes them very accessible and their light weight design allows them to be easily moved around on a location. This 50-ton rated bridge has a 6 inch guard running along the base of the hand rail to prevent debree from falling into the stream. These bridges have been quite popular with pipeline construction companies.

WELLSITE TRAILERS

Jimbob Rentals 2000 Ltd. Alta-Fab Wellsite Trailers are available in various sizes. These air conditioned units come completely loaded with central vacuum, washer & dryer, TVs, satellite receivers, stereos, intercom, barbecue & microwaves. Most models feature a Geologist end and an Engineer end with private sleep areas, kitchenettes, office areas and bathrooms. The engineers end comes complete with a Jacuzzi. Single end units are also available.

TANKS


SURFACE (mud) TANK
These self weighed open top tanks are 40 in length with a 350 barrel capacity. These tanks have a sloped design for easier removal and cleaning complete with submerged valves. Walkways with handrails and ladders allow for access to the various compartments.
Mostly used for fluid flocing and containment.

HOLDING TANKS
These open top tanks are 50 long 10 wide and 5 high with a 400 barrel capacity.
Round bottom design sits on skids and is very accessible for a back hoe etc. for cleaning.
Mostly used for containment. Some tanks do have dividers/baffles.

SHALE TANKS
The external brace design of these 3 sided open end tanks leave the interior walls smooth. The base traction make entering and exiting the tank simple for the front end loaders under slippery conditions. Available with 4 or 8 walls.
