Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Cosmetic Dentistry
Aesthetic dentistry through dental reconstruction, ceramic crowns, veneers, composite fillings, dental implants, gum regeneration, re-alignment of jaw and other cosmetic dental services are all available in-house at Ali Farahani DDS. Many of our cosmetic dental services are also non-surgical. Small yet precise changes to the front teeth and replacing back teeth with dental implants can restore the lost youthful dimensions of our face.

Service Name:
Dental Implants
Dental implants are the best way currently available to replace a missing tooth or missing teeth. They are essentially roots or anchors used to replace your own missing teeth. Implants are used to SUPPORT individual crowns, bridges and dentures. Imagine it like this: your dental implants are the foundations of a house, while crowns, bridges and dentures are the house sitting on this foundation. You know how important the foundation is to your home, imagine how important it is to the health of your jaw, gums and teeth.

Dr. Farahani has extensive experience with dental implants. Our advanced clinic completes all the work in-house from start to finish with titanium or ceramic post materials. We will happily discuss which dental implant is the best material for you.

Service Name:
Silver / Mercury Filling Replacement
For safe removal of mercury amalgam fillings, you want a dentist who is trained to remove amalgam fillings safely, and trained to replace the old silver fillings with safe composite, gold or porcelain fillings. Many people choose to replace their silver fillings with composite or porcelain fillings for aesthetic reasons (composites and porcelain can be made in many shades of white to create a filling that is nearly identical to the original tooth colour). But there are other benefits to replacing amalgam fillings too – your health. Silver amalgam fillings are approximately 50% mercury mixed with silver, tin, zinc, and copper. Mercury is a highly toxic substance. The vapours from the mercury in your teeth are constantly being released and absorbed by your body. The amount increases whenever you eat, chew gum, grind your teeth, drink hot beverages, etc.
Dr. Farahani is one of a handful of dentists in Ontario who is accredited with the IAOMT to safely remove and replace mercury fillings.
