Company Profile


Esna is a privately held Canadian software company that provides embedded collaboration solutions. Esnas solutions let people collaborate anytime, anywhere by putting real-time communications inside the productivity, CRM, ERP, social and cloud applications they use every day. For 25 years Esna has pioneered innovative solutions that connect people, process and information to get more work done faster.

Esna's customers and partners include organizations such as Canada Health, City of Edmonton, RIM, Bell Canada, Cisco Canada and others.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Esna Officelinx
Esna Officelinx is a comprehensive unified communications and collaboration platform. Officelinx offers next generation cloud-enabled communication features that improve collaboration and boost productivity, including:

Unified messaging with voicemail that integrates voice and fax messages with email and social collaboration applications from Google, Microsoft, IBM, Jive and others.

Real-time communication applications like voice and instant messaging (IM) communications, presence, click-to-call, voice conferencing, and geo-location mapping integrated inside business applications like email and CRM.

Voice conferencing that enables you to easily create or join collaborative voice conference meetings.

Officelinx interoperates with any SIP or TDM based PBX or phone system and is purpose built to work across multiple PBXs simultaneously. It is available for Windows and for Mac as well as Android, iOS, Windows Phone and Blackberry smartphones and tablets. With Officelinx you choose how to connect, communicate and collaborate with others using the applications and devices of your choice.

Key Features of Officelinx include:
:: Unified Messaging
:: Real Time Presence:
:: Instant Messaging
:: Click-to-Call
:: Calendar Integration
:: Integrated Fax
:: Mobility
:: Geo-location Services
:: Fixed-to-Mobile Convergence (FMC)
:: Speech to Text, Text to Speech
:: Auto Attendant and IVR
:: High Availability

Product Name:
Esna Cloudlink for Cisco
Collaborate faster and more effectively with Esna Cloudlink for Cisco. With Esna Cloudlink you can easily access unified communications and collaboration tools like presence, instant messaging (IM), voice and video communication, click-to-call, call management, geo-location services and unified messaging inside cloud applications like Google Apps, Salesforce, Jive and IBM SmartCloud.

Esna Cloudlink leverages standards-based SIP, Cisco APIs and Cisco Jabber to connect these cloud applications with Cisco Unity Connection, Cisco Unified Communications Manager, Cisco WebEx, Cisco HCS and Cisco Business Edition 6000.

Communicate from the Applications You Use Everyday
Stay productive and get work done faster by accessing Cisco collaboration tools like IM, voice and video communications and presence inside the applications you use everyday like email, CRM, groupware and social collaboration.

Collaborate from Anywhere
Connect, communicate and collaborate with others from any web page or application with Esna Cloudlink and Esna iLink for Cisco, an HTML5 based Cisco Jabber client. With Esna, you can access Cisco Jabber voice and video communications, presence, IM, click-to-call and call management from any Chrome or Internet Explorer browser.

Unified Messaging for On-Premise Apps
Esna Cloudlink for Cisco also integrates Cisco Unity Connection voicemail and unified messaging platform with on-premise versions of IBM Notes (formerly IBM Lotus Notes), Zimbra and Novell GroupWise. With Esna Cloudlink you can access and manage voice messages from your Notes, Zimbra and GroupWise email inbox and from your smartphone.

Esna Cloudlink for Cisco runs in an existing virtualized environment and supports VMware Vsphere 4.x and higher and Microsoft V-Sphere. Esna Cloudlink is Cisco Compatible Certified for Cisco Unity Connection 8.5.1ES78 or 8.6.2ES20 and higher, Cisco Call Manager 8.x and higher and WebEx 27 and higher.

Product Name:
Esna iLink for WebEx
Esna iLink for WebEx makes it easy to book WebEx meetings right from Google Apps, and Salesforce. With Esna iLink for WebEx you can schedule and start WebEx meetings without ever leaving the cloud application you are working in.

:: Schedule WebEx meetings just like any other calendar event
:: Eliminate the need to book meetings on the main WebEx site
:: Update WebEx automatically any time you change your meeting in Google, Salesforce or from your smartphones calendar
:: Start an instant WebEx meeting right from any web page or application

Esna iLink for WebEx is a browser extension that makes it easy for you to schedule and start a WebEx meeting instantly with just a few clicks. Download and try Esna iLink for WebEx free for 30 days from webex.esna.com.


Product Name:
Esna iLink for Cisco
Easily find, connect and collaborate with others anywhere, anytime with Esna iLink for Cisco browser extension. Access Cisco Jabber voice and video communications, presence, instant messaging (IM), voice messaging and geo-location sharing from any cloud application or web page. With Esna iLink for Cisco you are never more than a click away to find people, to see their availability and to collaborate with them.

With Esna iLink for Cisco you can:

:: Share and see others status and availability
:: Instant message 1:1 or with a group of people
:: Make Jabber voice and video calls from any Chrome browser (Windows and Mac only)
:: Click-to-call any phone number
:: Share your location of others
:: Access Cisco collaboration tools inside Salesforce

Esna iLink for Cisco can be installed as a Chrome browser extension or Internet Explorer plug-in.

Product Name:
Esna iLink for Avaya Scopia Desktop
Esna iLink for Avaya Scopia Desktop is a browser extension that makes it easy to schedule and start Scopia meetings right from Google Apps and Salesforce. With Esna iLink for Avaya Scopia Desktop you can:

:: Add a Scopia meeting to any calendar event
:: Start an instant Scopia meeting right from any web page or application


Product Name:
Esna WeLink Conferencing
Integrated audio conferencing services from Esna enables your employees to easily host or join collaborative audio conference meetings. Esna WeLink audio conferencing supports up to 100 participants on a single system and gives you the ability to configure unlimited virtual private conference rooms.

Esna WeLink offer different audio conferencing services to meet your needs including:

:: On-demand audio conferencing
:: Scheduled audio conferencing
:: Auditorium (large group) conferencing
