Company Profile


At Junk Hero, our mission is to remove your junk using up to date eco-environmental disposal practices while keeping you on a budget. We are an experienced junk removal company servicing Toronto, and surrounding cities and towns throughout the GTA.

We specialize in removing junk from a wide variety of residential and commercial buildings. This includes homes, apartments, condos, construction sites, demolition sites, estate clean outs, garage demolition, fence removal, deck removal, and much more.

If you’ve got junk that needs to be removed immediately, give us a call today!

We have a staff of friendly and reliable junk removers that bring professionalism to every job site. Our team will provide you with peace of mind, so sit back and watch your junk disappear! At Junk Hero we are an eco-friendly company that donates all reusable goods, and we recycle any materials that can be salvaged.

With regards to service and cost, you will not find a better option for junk removal in the GTA. Our trucks are two times larger than the competition so you will get the best possible value. Just as importantly, we guarantee the best prices in the GTA. We promise to beat any price you receive from another junk removal company in the GTA.

Our team is fast, and we offer same day service. If you need your junk removed immediately, we can help. Give us a call today!
