Comprehensive Staffing Services
Campbell, Edgar Inc. has been enhancing careers and company teams through careful placement of professional executives, mid management and key staff employees into permanent, contract and temporary positions within reputable companies in Canada and beyond since 1993.
Recruitment Expertise in all areas of retail, including store operations, corporate positions, warehouse and logistics, buying and marketing and both B2B and B2C sales. Our president/founder is a Governor of the BBB Mainland BC - you can count our our honesty and integrity in all business practises.
