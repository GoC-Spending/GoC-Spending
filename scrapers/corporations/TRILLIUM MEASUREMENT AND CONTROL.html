Company Profile


Trillium Measurement and Control offers a wide range of technologies for gas and liquid flow measurement, level measurement, and pressure measurement, including products, accessories, system integration, training and service. We are a registered ISO 9001:2008 company. Our products are marketed to major clients in the following industries:

Chemical
Power
Biotechnology
Metal Processing
Environmental
Petroleum
Pharmaceutical
Food and Beverage
Automotive
OEM


Trillium Measurement and Control also offers flow calibration service. Our flow lab is capable of providing flow meter calibrations traceable to NIST and NRC, both for liquid and gas calibrations.

Our technical team possesses many years of experience in providing clients with measurement and control solutions to improve productivity, reduce waste, and enhance product quality. We can help you achieve the most cost effective solution for your project, using the most appropriate devices available from our product line, which may be incorporated into a system or on a component basis.

Service Name:
Liquid & Gas Calibration
Trillium Measurement & Control provides Liquid Flow and Gas Flow meter calibrations, covering a wide range of measurement principles and manufacturers. Our mobile Compact Prover is capable of field calibrations (load rack, truck terminals, LPG bulk delivery terminal and/or trucks). Our in-house calibration facility is equipped for both Liquid and Gas flow measuring devices, with an average turn-around time of 5 working days. All of our equipment is traceable to NIST and/or Weights & Measures of Canada, and our technical staff are factory trained and certified, many with 20 or more years of experience.

Meter Types Calibrated:

PD meters
Turbine
Coriolis (Mass Flow)
Ultrasonic
Magnetic Meter
Rotameter (VA)
Thermal Mass


Service Name:
Service & Training
Trillium Measurement and Control provides On Site product Training and Service support including start-up assistance, and product set-up and programming. We also will train your operators and maintenance personnel on proper use and maintenance requirements.

We offer a wide range of custom technical services for your particular needs, including new installations, modernization of older facilities, and a full range of services for Bulk Terminal Facilities. Compact and Master Meter proving operators/technicians are trained to solve or recommend changes to any metering problems you may have, and are familiar with most metering and electronic devices/systems.

Scheduled maintenance on Daniel Compact Provers (typically 4-5 years), has our certified technicians rebuild your Prover, replacing all seals, providing a water-draw and re-certifying it with equipment traceable to Industry Canada (Weights & Measures).
