Company Profile


1- Specialty distributor and retailer of medium to high end patio furniture for residential and commercial applications. We are offering also other outdoor leisure products such as retractable awningns, sun shelters, parasols and umbrellas, hammocks, outdoor rugs, infra red heaters and others.
2- Distributor of construction tarps and winter golf covers Agent for reflective insulation and curing blankets made from PE air bubbles manufactured by Covertech Mfg. JML has been in business for over 20 years.

Unique applications:

All of our products are the best available on the market and are designed to last many years outdoors. For example, the frames of our patio furniture are made with either strong aluminum extrusions or casting, marine grade polymer resin, synthetic wicker resistant to U.V. degradation, stainless steel or exotic woods like Ipé and teak. The fabrics used are also U.V. resistant such as 100% solution died acrylics from ''Sunbrella'' and others.

Key / Major clients:

Commerical customers: Manoir Rouville Campbell, Auberge le Baluchon, Quebec Museum of Arts, Domaine des Forges, Résidence Magog, Stade Uniprix, Restaurant Madison, Hôtel Loews La Concorde, Restaurant et bar Newtown, Restaurant l'Ambroisie, Hôtel Delta, Hôtel Sheraton, Holiday Inn, Condo Le Sax, etc..
Many residential individuals accross the province.

Success stories:

Because we offer the largest selection of outdoor patio furniture and accessories in Quebec and all the products manufactured by our suppliers, our customers were able to create their own settings meeting exactly their needs and this from one source, JML Inc.
