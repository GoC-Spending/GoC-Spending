Company Profile


Atlantic Speakers Bureau offers quality local, national and
international presenters to all sectors. We are a full-service
provider of keynotes, seminars and workshops listing over 100
professional speakers in over 600 topic areas on our primary
listings. Our secondary listings has access to over 30,000
Professional Presenters for all events.
General areas of delivery include:

Personal & Professional development.
Sales & Customer Service.
Management development.
Tourism and Hospitality.
Sexual & Workplace Harassment.
Retirement Planning.
Computer Software Applications.
Entrepreneurship and Small Business Development.
Health Sciences.
Customized Programming.
Consulting and Needs Analysis.

Nominated Small Business of the year 1991.
Award of Excellence from HRD-Canada 1989.
A solid reputation built on quality.
We can provide you with a variety of presenters for
practically any topic.

We can accommodate most budgetary considerations with our mix of
local, national and international presenters.

Give Marlene or Allen Phillips a call, fax or e-mail, we would
be pleased to hear from you. Check out our web site
www.asb.nb.ca

We will co-broker with other bureaus and meeting planners.
