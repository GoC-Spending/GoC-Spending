Company Profile


Integrity Testing Laboratory Inc. (ITL), the recipient of the prestigious 1996 Business Excellence Award (Company of the Year Creativity) and the Canadian Sustaining Member Corporate Innovation Award by ASM International Canada Council in 2004, is a high tech, diversified technology oriented company. The mission of Integrity Testing Laboratory is to identify new and emerging materials and process technologies, develop appropriate capabilities and expertise, and utilize them to provide services of superior quality to the aerospace, space and other high technology industrial and scientific communities in Canada and abroad through transfer of technology or by supplying the new products. Major fields of interest include interaction of materials with space environment, space materials testing and characterization, protective coating's technology development and evaluation, failure analysis of materials and structures in aerospace, micro electronic and automotive industries.
Integrity Testing Laboratory (ITL) Inc.,since it was formed in 1989,
provided analytical and consulting services to the industrial and
research communities in Canada and abroad to over 350 industrial companies, government agencies, and research
institutions.

ITL provides a full range of consulting, testing and characterization, and research and development services in the following areas:
- Failure Analysis and Investigations (Metals, Plastics, Polymers
Ceramics)
- Thermal Analysis of Plastics and Polymers (DSC,TGA, DMA)
- Scanning Electron Microscopy, X-ray microanalysis (light
element detector)
- Thermal, Optical, Infrared Spectroscopy
- Inductively coupled Plasma (ICP) analysis or trace elements and heavy metals
- Mechanical Testing (Tensile, Flexture, Creep, etc..)
- Metallography, phase and structural analysis
- Hardness, Microhardness
- Surface analysis (XPS,Auger, SIMS, Raman, Contact angle)
- Full evaluation of protective coatings (aerospace, automotive,
space)
- Chemical, Physical analysis,
- Reverse-Engineering
- ASTM specified testing

ITL Inc. serves the following industries with confidence:
- Automotive - Aerospace - Building - Chemical - Construction - Electrical - Electronic - Electroplating
- Environmental - Metallurgical - Plastics - Polymers - Space - Semiconductor - etc..

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Residual Stress Management System
Residual Stress Management system consists of: 1. A portable
instrument for measuring non-destructively the resudual
stresses in materials and welds, 2. An Expert software system
for evaluating the influence of the resudual stresses on
fatigue life of components and 3. a portable Ultrasonic Peening
Instrument for redistribution or removal of residual stresses
from welded elements and structures.

Product Name:
Space Environment Simulator
Fully computer controlled system for simulation of Low Earth Orbit (LEO) space factors like atomic oxygen (AO), vacuum ultraviolet (VUV) radiation, near ultraviolet (NUV) radiation, thermal cycling (TC).

Product Name:
System for determination of thermal physical parameters using an original monotonous heating method.
A high temperature furnace with a specially designed monotonous heating hardware and software. Allows to measure very accuratley and fast thermal physical properties of refractories, insulations,e tc.

Product Name:
Surface Modification Technology "PhotosilTM"
Surface modifcation technology for polymers and plastics taht impairs oxidative resistant properties to most plastics and polymers.

Product Name:
Planetary (Lunar) Space Environment Simulator
The Planetary (Lunar) Space Environment Simulator is used for simulation and evaluation of the effects of various factors of the Lunar and, if required, Martian planetary environments on materials, components and planetary activities equipment used repetitively or required for prolonged periods of time in long duration missions.

Product Name:
Outgassing System to comply with the ASTM Standard Kinetic Outgassing Test E1559
The outgassing system allows generating data to characterize the kinetics of the release of outgassing products from spacecraft materials. This technique will determine both the total mass flux evolved by a material when exposed to a vacuum environment and the deposition of this flux on surfaces held at various specified temperatures.

Service Name:
Product/Service/Technology
Products:
- Surface modified FEP Teflon to reduce the diffuse reflectance
- Software for evaluation of erosion properties of carbon-based
polymers in Space environment
- Equipment for non-destructive evaluation of residual stresses
in welded components and materials by ultrasonic methods.
- Equipment for redistribution of stresses in welded structures
by ultrasonic peening technology.

Service:
- Full range of testing of materials (plastics, polymers,
rubbers, metals, ceramics)
- Conductance of R&D projects for customers
- Conductance of educational seminars
