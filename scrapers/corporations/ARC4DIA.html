Company Profile


Comprised of former specialized members of the Canadian national security community, Arc4dia possesses unmatched experience combating a sophisticated form of cyber attack known as Advanced Persistent Threats or APTs.

Arc4dias team includes handpicked experts with the technical know-how and operational experience of having exploited the worlds most heavily fortified IT systems, undetected by the in-place defensive systems and IT security operators.

Arc4dias unique experience and in-depth knowledge of offensive tactics enables Arc4dia to think like the adversary. Arc4dia experts can anticipate threat vectors and take action before attack can do real damage.

Cyber attacks occur at incredible speeds and Arc4dias approach accelerates defensive actions to match or outpace the adversarys  speed is a key factor that cannot be overstated when combating APTs.

Arc4dia's technologies and services are listed on the Enhanced Priority Technology List (EPTL) and we also meet the Small and Medium Enterprise (SME) Requirement.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Snow Cover (Active Defence)
Arc4dias core service is Snow Cover, an active defence solution providing constant over-watch while actively hunting advanced threats operating on a clients networks. Using proprietary heuristics and technologies, with the active oversight of cyber experts, Snow Cover provides unrivalled protection against APTs.

Snow Cover accelerates the detection of APTs that may otherwise remain undetected for years
simultaneously providing surgical mitigation and remediation of new and emerging threats.

Snow Cover supports and complements existing cyber security efforts to make the clients network extremely difficult for hackers to navigate, adding multiple additional layers to existing defence-in-depth solutions.

Arc4dia clients become much harder targets, inciting the enemy to move to more vulnerable targets.

Arc4dias clients frequently stress the value of Snow Covers transparency. Most traditional security products create additional work for clients cyber security staff where employees are expected to manage and monitor these products. These systems act more like fire alarms than active defence mechanisms.

Snow Cover does not generate additional work until an actual intrusion is confirmed; even when this occurs, Arc4dias team can take immediate action based upon pre-arranged standard operation procedures that include the clients cyber security staff.

Service Name:
Dynamic Deception (Cyber Attack Mitigation)
Successfully countering APTs requires an in-depth understanding of the attackers methodology
or attack chain. Arc4dia describes this methodology as a chain because disrupting any stage interrupts or stops the entire process. No one technology or solution is comprehensive enough to address all security concerns. This contemporary environment demands a layered strategy to defend against adversaries throughout all stages of their attack chain.

Dynamic Deception is an innovative approach that complements Arc4dia's active defence solution by providing timely and measurable mitigation options in the wake of a network compromise.

Dynamic Deception provides the clients with insight into the attackers identity and motivations while confirming exactly what information was compromised. Arc4dias team works with the client to craft misinformation to flow back to the attacker, changing his perceptions to mitigate the impact to the client. Attackers can also be lured into leaving a digital trail, exposing them to positive attribution and potential future prosecution.

Service Name:
Training
Arc4dias experience and unique expertize is enriched by the strategic partnerships it maintains with influential think tanks, leading-edge corporations, and independent associates specializing in the fields of cyber analytics, cyber security risk management, and security operations.

Arc4dia offers five tailored short courses (1-2 days) for mid to senior level IT security professionals. These courses focus on sophisticated and high impact threats, innovative defensive techniques, malware re-engineering and other forms of identification and exploitation methods. Arc4dia also offers courses for non-technical, security minded managers that enable participants to understand different threats, attack indicators, and security best practices.
