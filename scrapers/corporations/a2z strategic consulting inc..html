Company Profile


Our goal at a2z strategic consulting is to ensure that the next version of any organization’s strategic
plan becomes a living and sustainable tool that reaches the people it intends to support through the
work it does in its field of expertise, rather than simply languishing as a shelf document.
We believe in guiding organizations through a series of simple phases that bring together familiar
elements of strategic planning while making use of current research and trends that elaborate plans
toward innovative solutions:
“Our job is to provide organizations not with what they want, but with what they may never
have envisioned wanting; and which when they get, they recognize as something they
wanted all the time.” Stephanie Vucko, CEO, a2z strategic consulting

a2z strategic consulting is a consultancy firm that operates out of Laval, QC, and offers services to organizations across Canada with a vision to export internationally in upcoming months.

Its principal business is change management through three targeted offers of service:
• Strategic Planning
• Strategic Plan Implementation Support
• Strategic Plan Innovation

Service Name:
Strategic Planning

Phase 1 | Commence
Phase 2 | Capture
Phase 3 | Create
Phase 4 | Crystalize
Phase 5 | Circle back














Service Name:
Strategic Implementation Support

STRATEGIC PLAN IMPLEMENTATION SUPPORT

a2z strategic consulting believes that a client’s team is the key to its success. The easy part is the
development of a strategic plan. The REAL work begins when the plan is handed over to your people
to be put into action.

Implementation support is our unique expertise at a2z strategic consulting. We want to make sure
the client and their teams are supported to connect all the dots, from a-2-z, to ensure the plan has a
positive impact on its organization and stakeholders.

o On-site and virtual support plan
(Identify the practical needs to ensure strategic plan success: staff development, modeling,
coaching, resources, etc.)
o Monitoring and reflection tools
(From paper plan to action plan—how is it going? Asking critical questions and adjusting with
solutions that maintain the positive change momentum)
o Implementation plan
(Task-level mobilization of the strategic plan, quarterly review of tasks)
o Hands-on support
(Site visits, observations, and virtual support, customized to your need)
o Meetings with leadership team and/or staff,
(quarterly review of progress, group and individual coaching, etc.)
o Professional development to fill gaps in competencies
o Ongoing gap analysis




























Service Name:
Strategic Innovation

STRATEGIC PLAN INNOVATION
Taking your plan from a-2-z-ippididoodah!
Strategic plan innovation is unique to a2z strategic consulting. This is a very customized process and
specific to the culture of each organization. Our teams will work with your teams to devise a highly
customized strategy for improving upon the strategic elements that have revealed themselves to be
ripe for renewal or revision to give you that unique advantage in your industry.
This service is offered to organizations who may already have a strategic plan in place, but two or
more years into the plan, they realize they are not addressing each of their goals. Often, lack of internal resources or innovation leads to strategic plans becoming stagnant plans and in the end,
targets are not met.

Other organizations incorporate the innovation support into the implementation of their plan,
requiring guidance from the expert teams at a2z strategic consulting to help their teams look through
new lenses at your services and delivery.

Innovation support includes:
o Establishing lagging targets to prioritize
o Partnership development toward common goal
o Strategy or project case examples form other organizations
o Project development
o Project Management
o Grant seeking and writing
o Custom resource development and training
