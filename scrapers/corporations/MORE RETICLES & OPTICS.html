Company Profile


More Reticles & Optics is a high-tech optics company which is registered in Canada, specializing in precise optical photoetching technology. We mainly undertakes all kinds of reticles, gratings, encoders, modulators, raster plates, resolution plates and other high-class photoetching products' processing business.

In China, More Reticles & Optics has its own main production and processing base. It was established in Nanjing China in 1991, which is one of the most famous photoetching technology manufacturers in China. In 2006, 7000 square meters scientific research and manufacture centre was built in TangShan Industrial Park, Nanjing, which makes it possible to constantly develop new products and expand production capacity.

Relied on advanced technology, based on strict quality management system, aimed at customers need, it makes sure our products are kept in continued high quality and reputation. Companied with the idea of high-class and precision and the features of products, we provide matched photoetching processing services and products with exquisite techniques and reasonable prices to optical & photonics instruments, theyre covering many aspects, such as: Telescope, Gun sight, Scope Sight, Rangefinder Scope, Riflescope, Refractometer, Theodolite, Total Station, Sight Level, Surgical Microscope, Hemocytometer, Diopter Tester, Planetarium, etc. Our cutting-edge techniques and solid reliability make the rapid increase in the number of international orders from Asia, Africa, Europe, and America all year round.

Our products have received commendation from many famous optical instruments manufacturers at home and abroad. Through strict inspection and evaluation, we have been listed as their qualified vendor and our brand has become well know. With the rapid development of international processing business, we are gradually becoming an international famous and high-tech optics company in precise photo-etching technology. More Reticles & Optics is your most reliable partner!

Product Name:
Gun Sight Reticle Series
The sight for short-middle distance is the important core component of artillery of various tanks and warships; even under the conditions of modern warfare, its also essential. Various reticles are designed according to the use, the range and the different ballistic trajectory of diversified artillery. Our products have large coverage, wide variety, complicated figures and exquisite technique.

The reticles we process, for the high accuracy sniping riflescopes, are superb in numerous of gun sights. For example, MJ2-10 reticle thickness is 8mm, cylinder polished, the figure has a width of 0.26mm aiming circle and four width of 0.014mm ranging lines, its exterior surface needs plate broad-band anti-reflection coating, and its used not only in daytime but also at night. This product cannot be completed by a single photo-etching technology, combination of different techniques, we successfully processed this product. After a number of rigorous testing including high and low temperature (+80Degree Centigrade,-50Degree Centigrade), raining, vibration, and salt atmosphere, the product conforms to all the design requirements. We also provide various precise reticles for such as laser measuring & ranging system and single soldier guidance system.


Product Name:
Grating, Encoder and Modulator
Grating, encoder and modulator are our companys main product lines which provide powerful technical support for digitizing in various related fields. Incremental circular grating, absolute coder and modulator have been widely used in the field of photoelectric translating systems such as angular displacement sensor and linear displacement sensor. Our products angle accuracy can reach 0.2seconds;and the length accuracy can reach ±0.5um. Many kinds of single loop absolute coders we processed have been widely used in total station and electronic theodolite systems, which not only replace the existing products but also upgrade the technology. Different kinds of long gratings and phase gratings play important roles in digital display, digital control, photoelectric integration and other coordinate measuring systems.


Product Name:
Raster Plate Series
The high-precision Raster Plate is mainly applied to various display & control systems, detection equipments in the electronics industry, display screen of image signal terminals, parallax testing, etc. We can process various kinds of raster plates according to different customers technical requirements in different fields.

Product Name:
Resolution Plate Series
All different kinds of Resolution Plates are important testing methods for geometric optical image evaluation. We can process the pivotal inspecting and testing resolution plates for optical bench and collimator, such as the ZB N35 003-89 and WT 1005-62 standard resolution plate, radial resolution plate, asterism resolution plate for image evaluation, Polo board for focal length testing, etc. The minimum line width of the resolution plate is 1um, and the minimum resolution is 500lines/mm. Based on the current popular demands of digital camera objective lenses and CCD lenses as for image evaluation, we successively developed micro-length resolution plate series, which have been adopted by many related companies.

Product Name:
Geodetic Surveying Reticle Series
Grating, optical dial and various reticles are widely used in the geodetic surveying field. The geodetic surveying apparatus include total station, electronic theodolite, optical theodolite, laser rangefinder, automatic sight level, various field equipments, and large-scale and high-precision measuring equipment (such as satellite remote sensing and aerial surveying apparatus). We process various specifications of high-precise reticles and provide related services for each domestic and foreign measuring company. The large quantities, varieties and high quality make us become the one of the best suppliers.



Product Name:
Astronomical Phenomena Reticle Series
All kinds of particular reticles are the important components of astronomical instruments, aerospace measuring and control system, image processing system, etc. Our products include azimuth reticle of astronomical telescope, alignment reticle of target guidance device, stimulant star field reticle for planetariums (it is composed of 32 star zones, including nearly ten thousand stars which have twenty different magnitudes, the density (D) >6, and the smallest star diameter <2um), meridian reticle, horizon reticle, equator and ecliptic reticle, etc. There are up to 54 kinds of related reticles, and our products include many images of moons, various star cluster, nebulae, comet, and artificial celestial bodies. We provide pivotal components for modern astronomical instruments, which use for astronomy exploration and popularization of science.


Product Name:
Medical Instruments Reticle Series
We have an extensive market in the field of medical photoelectric instruments. We can process the key components for the surgical microscope, hemocytometer, medical microscope, nuclear magnetic resonance imaging device, diopter tester, visual degree gauge, etc. With the powerful working master technology, our products not only have high accuracy, but also have delicate designed figure, which make examination more reasonable, accurate and user-friendly.


Product Name:
Portable Refractometer
Portable refractometer is an instrument that measures the concentration of an aqueous solution by measuring its refractive index. All water based solution can make light bend. Light is refracted at a proportional rate to the increased concentration solution.

The portable refractometer is a precise optical instrument. Its characterized by small volume, light weight, graceful shape and easy operation. With the powerful working master technology, our products not only have high accuracy, but also have delicate designed figure, which make examination more reasonable, accurate and user-friendly.

We can provide ATC style and ORD style, our products are widely used in measuring Brix, Salinity, Protein, Battery/Coolants/Cleaner, Oil, Wine, Milk, Fruit Juice, Emulsion, Gem.


Product Name:
Other high-class Photoetching Products
We have accumulated long-term professional working experience and developed our particular techniques, we are able to undertake and process special photoetching components. For example the variable-transmission MTF sinusoidal grating, the variable-frequency OTF optical transfer function grating, etc. In particularly, the novel wavelength gratings which have the wavelengths of Helium and Neon as their raster unit, we overcome the unstable error in laser length measuring, and the accuracy of our products reaches 0.2nm. Also, our products such as germanium crystal and white gem coders, modulators are widely used in infrared guidance and measure & control systems(M&C). Moreover, we can make the pattern on the sphere shape surface; it makes the special structure of the products and special light path design as possible.

Technology profile

Photoetching technology, which has existed for nearly two centuries, has run to the modern sub-nanometer figure and nanometer precision photoetching from archaic spider line reticle. We persist in traditional techniques, develop modern technology and are devoted to the combination of these two. We have created our particular super-minute figure processing technology after many years of research and exploration.

We not only have conventional photographic copy technology by using SHELLAC and P.V.A, but also have modern copy technology by using E spectral line photoetching super micro-particle dry plate, G spectral line positive and negative photo-resistant chrome plate and H spectral line positive and negative photo-resistant chrome plate. We not only have etching figure technology by using mechanical chemistry method, but also have precise photo-etching technology by using high precise two-frequency laser orientation electron beam photoetching system. We not only have regular films plating technology by using D450 conventional vacuum coating machine, but also have high precise films plating technology by using magnetron sputtering film formation equipment. We use our own characteristic technology successfully to solve development and production problems of various high difficult photoetching components.

Substrate materials diversify. From the materials application of conventional optical glass (K9, BaK7), optical quartz glass, single color optical glass (red, green, yellow) and other common spectral carriers to the application of far-UV and far-infrared spectral region materials such as Germanium crystal, white gem, MgF2, and ZnS, it has extended photo-etching components' application field and practical range. Because of the complete technology, after several years effort, the varieties of our products have reached thousands of kinds. Furthermore, we not only can process all kinds of high difficult, specialized photo-etching components but also have the ability to aggressively develop new products and mass production.
Super-micro components (Diameter: 2.8mm),
Super-thin components (Thickness: 0.4mm),
Super-thick components (Thickness: 20mm),
Super-large components (Diameter: 420mm, Area: 15inchX15inch),
High-precision components (Length accuracy: 0.2um, Angle accuracy: 0.2seconds).
