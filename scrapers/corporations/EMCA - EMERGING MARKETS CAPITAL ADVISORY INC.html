Company Profile


We specialize in establishing Canadian companies in Turkey.

Although trade between Canada and Turkey has been increasing steadily over the past five years, many Canadian corporations are still not positioned to compete against their more aggressive European counterparts as they capture market share throughout the region. EMCA understands and fully respects the way Canadian companies have traditionally been risk averse and we know that expanding into these new markets need not be an exercise in unreasonable risk taking. A methodical and well thought out plan is the first step and when coupled with expert guidance, opportunities become more realistic and risk becomes more manageable.

You need a partner who speaks the language, has access to the right information, and knows the right people to make sense of complex business issues, manage risk, and fully represent and protect your interests.

EMCA guides your investment in Turkish opportunities through unsurpassed experience in local capital markets, investment strategy, and management consulting. We are a partner that provides Canadian investors and corporations with opportunities in Turkey through our connections and understanding of both Canadian and Turkish markets.

Our aim is to build, compliment, verify and ultimately execute your expansion strategies. Contact us and let us show you how we can represent and protect your interests in Turkey.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Business Development & Representation in Turkey
EMCA can represent you by making sure that your products and services are appropriately positioned to successfully compete in Turkish markets. We can establish your company presence, promote your brand and build your sales & distribution network throughout Turkey and ultimately the greater region.

Why should you have physical representation in Turkey?

- Canada and Turkey share a unique competitive advantage - both countries represent a springboard for corporations and investors to access a broader region: Canada for North America through the NAFTA agreement and Turkey for the Balkans, Eurasia and the Middle East.

- With a rapidly growing consumer base of 74 million strong, Turkey has become a critical channel of revenue growth for many international corporations in an environment of stagnation across much of the developed world.

- Turkish economic, business, social and political influence is strengthening throughout the region. Companies need proper representation in an increasingly competitive landscape.


Service Name:
M&A and Strategic Acquisitions in Turkey
EMCA can help you penetrate the Turkish market through co-investing with reputable local private equity firms, whose job is to grow the business. We can help you identify the right strategic acquisition target and represent your interest both as an investor or as an operating partner.

Why and how should you make a strategic acquisition in Turkey?

- Strategic acquisitions have become the dominant expansion strategy into Turkey (and ultimately the wider region) for many international companies.

- Expanding through a co-investment with local private equity firms can ensure that the company you are buying is positioned for rapid growth.

- Identifying the right acquisition target is only part of the solution. EMCA can help you secure your acquisition at the right valuation.

- With over 60 private equity firms operating in Turkey, EMCA has direct access to many of the leading general partners and their current/target acquisition portfolio.


Service Name:
Joint Ventures and Operating Partnership in Turkey
EMCA can help you build ongoing business relationships while giving you the opportunity to identify new financing options for your projects. We have an established business network of private equity firms, hedge funds, banks, family offices and corporations from a diverse array of sectors. We have the capacity to identify the right joint venture partner.

Why should you consider a JV structure in Turkey?

- Joint ventures and strategic partnerships between Canadian and Turkish businesses can offer tremendous benefits to both parties, including knowledge/technology transfer, new market exposure and extensive project financing opportunities.

- A successful JV structure may lead to financing opportunities from Canadian as well as Turkish institutional investors.


Service Name:
Investing and Trading in Turkish Capital Markets
EMCA has positioned itself to represent the investment strategies and execute institutional trades on the Turkish capital markets on behalf of Canadian clients. We bring years of established relationships with both institutional as well as professional trading communities in Turkey.

Why should you consider investing into the Turkish capital markets?

- MSCI index performance over four years for EM Asia, EM Latin America and Turkey suggest a level of correlation between those markets.

- Market correlation represents relative predictability in the bull/bear cycles and therefore the movement of funds in/out of emerging markets by foreign institutions.

- Value can be created two ways: investing into the cycle or acquiring access to intimate local information in order to identify valuation and pricing discrepancies in specific stocks regardless of market cycles.

- EMCA has partnerships with leading securities research houses as well as institutional traders who have access to the right information to make the most informed investment decisions.
