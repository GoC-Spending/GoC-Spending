Company Profile


At Beaver Books Publishing, we've been designing and producing original educational products for children that combine quality and fun since 2004.
Our mission is creating books and resources that:

Keep children engaged as they learneducation should be an adventure!
Help parents and educators feel supported and excited as they teach kidsvia top-notch materials that never neglect the fun factor.

We're based in Montreal, Canada, with a sales and distribution center in Nashville, TN. Our customersparents, teachers, school administrators and boards, as well as corporate clientsare in dozens of countries across the world.

Beaver Books is a division of PAPP International Inc., a privately held Canadian corporation that offers a wide range of printed products for all ages. In addition to its many retail products, PAPP (Publishing and Printed Products) offers content development services to companies in many industries, and specializes in developing customized promotional products.

Education is a necessity and I believe that your company is a great tool for educators, parents and children.

Thank you for your time!

Joseph Caskey
