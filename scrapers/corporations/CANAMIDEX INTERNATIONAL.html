Company Profile


Canamidex International Corp. was founded in 2007, as a result of our thirty plus years of Engineering and Management experience and expertise in the water and wastewater treatment, energy generation, fluid transfer industries, spare parts and utilities within North America, Middle East and North Africa (MENA) regions. We started in response to demand within our networks, for advice and guidance on carrying out various projects within certain timespans, budgets, and rigorous quality requirements. As our knowledge of project requirements, equipment strength and weaknesses and market behaviors grew, we expanded to an end-to-end turnkey provider of solutions to our clients, which includes the, Municipal, Utilities, Industrial, Real Estate Developering, Manufacturering and Tourism Sectors. Today we specialize in, Water Treatment, Waste Water treatment and reuse, Industries utilities, Sludge dewatering, Fluid Transfer, Fluid Mixing and Energy generation.



Over the years we have forged strong business relationships with some of the most efficient manufacturers worldwide. At Canamidex we believe in, and maintain, strong, long-term relationships. We have proven to be an important link between manufacturers and end users within the industries we serve. Our success stems from the benefits that we deliver to both ends of our industry’s value chain. We achieve this by creating more value through the efficient and innovative design, cost advantages and competitive advantages that we deliver to our customers.



Our future looks bright. We strive to expand our network of customers and suppliers to different international regions. Canamidex will do its best to be an important part of an essential global movement that is the preservation of the world environment

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Steel product and fabrication
Canamidex has a fully located fabrication shop with capabilities in CNC Machining, rotational axis laser cutting, automatic welding.

Canamidex is a leading Ontario-based custom steel fabrication workshop, who supplies engineering firms and contractors with quality cost effective products on time and on budget.

Canamidex has comprehensive, integrated design and process capabilities that support our many demanding clients. Our focus is on tolerances and commitment to quality at competitive rates


Product Name:
Sludge dewatering - Blet filter press
Canamidex can provide world-class, high quality, latest technology sludge dewatering equipment which are used for water and wastewater treatment in municipal, refinery, paper mill, mining and food industry applications. These systems are manufactured in joint venture projects between Canamidex and its Italian partner, based on customer specifications and sludge/water analysis. The base components are supplied by us from a variety of known manufactures with the highest quality; the finished products are manufactured and tested at our factory in Ravenna/Italy

Product Name:
Mixer
In cooperation with our partners we manufacture world - class,
latest technology, Mixers and Agitators, which are used in water and
wastewater treatment, refinery, paper production, mining, chemicals
and food processing applications.
These systems are manufactured at our plant in Italy, based on
customer specifications and individual requirements. The base
components are supplied by a variety of credible internationally
recognized manufacturers with the highest degree of quality, and
the finished products are rigorously tested at our production
plant before any shipment occurs

Product Name:
Generator (Diesel, Gas, Hybrid)
Canamidex manufacture the highest quality, mobile and stationery Diesel Powered Generators between
10kva to 3000kva and Gas Powered Generators between 300kwe to 1mw . We use Perkins (UK), Cummins
(USA), MTU (UK), Duetz (Germany) and Volvo (UK) Engines which are coupled with Stamford (UK), Meccalte
(UK) or Leroy Somer (France) Alternators. The generating sets are made in the UK incorporating some of the
best components possible; this combination contributes, to our products' superior quality

Service Name:
Water and wastewater treatment
We in corporation with our partners as a strong group focusing on water- and waste water treatment. Our trained and experienced engineers and personnel in our affiliate companies worldwide- are experts when it comes to the design and execution of water- and waste water treatment plants.

We accumulate several years of practical and international experience in full design, supply, realization and commissioning of water- and waste water treatment projects.

Canamidex and its partners has gained extensive technological know-how and experience for treating all types of water and wastewater.


Service Name:
Industrial Procurement Management
Canamidex International Corp. is specializing in the supply of instruments, equipments, and materials for Canada Government services needs, United Nations, infrastructure projects including buildings, water and wastewater treatment plant, pipe line, power plant utilities, oil and gas industries.

Based on the growing worldwide demand for efficient and capable expertise our business partners are the world's best manufacturers, having strong supplier relationships allows us to obtain the preferred pricing, guarantee fulfillment and strong manufacturer after sale services.

Procurements frequently involves technical expertise for client specific needs which require us to leverage our internal knowledge base for producing the best possible result, this has allowed us to excel in our business development endeaours
