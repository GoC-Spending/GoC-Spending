Company Profile


Image Inspection Services manufactures remote television cameras for inspecting underground sewers and pipelines. The camera is a self contained unit which is drawn through the pipe, and using it's own light source, transmits a picture of the inside conditions of the pipe to the operator. The inspection results are video recorded for a permanent record. Defects along with their corresponding distance measurement are noted on the recording as well as in a computer generated log report. Radio location from above ground exactly locates the camera position to minimize digging. Various robotocs are employed to transport the camera through a variety of pipe diameters ranging from less than 1" in diameter to over 48" in diameter.

The camera is typically drawn from manhole to manhole either by a winch or a pressure flushing machine. A remote crawler is
available for conveying the camera when access is restricted to one end. The camera can be used for inspecting pipe diameters from 3" through 48" in diameter with a maximum cable length of 2500". Similarly, the video camera can be lowered into wells and boreholes.


Environmental Profile

Image Inspection Services Ltd. is a specialty manufacturer of underwater television inspection systems designed for the non-destructive inspection of sewers, wells, boreholes and pipelines. Our products have found numerous applications in both the municipal and industrial sectors. The majority of our equipment system sales are to municipal governments; the minority of sales are to contractors to the industrial and municipal market.


We also provide a consulting service utilizing video inspection equipment for the detection and evaluation of pipeline corrosion, pipe and vessel condition, and sewer system ratings and evaluations. Training in the safe and efficient operation of the sewer equipment is provided to new system owners. The company has developed various cameras for a diverse range of applications from sewer pipe to drinking water well to oilfield pipeline inspection.


Image Inspection Services Ltd. also acts as a design consultant for CCTV video applications, including night surveillance, computer imaging and machine vision, and security and surveillance applications. We have custom designed many specialty composite video cable, underwater housings and underwater lighting systems. 65% of all sales are within Canada with 50% in Western Canada and 15% in Eastern Canada; the remaining 25% are in the US market, and 10% overseas. Regional repair centers throughout Canada and the United States provide convenient service centers for equipment repair or upgrading.


Our in-house capabilities include a machine shop, CADD system, electronic repair center, and an electronic manufacturing facility including a wave solder machine. Image markets its products and services to governments, hospitals, utilities, petroleum companies, and private water and sewer contractors.

Product Name:
MINI CAMERA INSPECTION SYSTEMS
General Description
The Image Mini Camera Inspection System is a versatile camera system available in many configurations. Many camera heads
are available including color mini cameras, and Pan and Tilt cameras. All camera heads have
CCD construction, water tight, excellent resolution, low light sensitivity, and are all interchangeable.

The camera systems are useful for inspecting a variety of piping and vessels including sewer laterals, house plumbing, chimneys,
catalyst tubes, boreholes, electrical and telephone ducts and industrial piping. The systems are compact, light weight, and cost effective. The cameras and pushrods will negotiate multiple bends and deliver a quality picture over hundreds of meters of cable. All controls units are housed in heavy duty aluminum flight cases with compartment lids to protect components during transport.


Model 2

The most popular, rugged and versatile model of portable camera control units. It features a built in industrial color monitor, industrial DVD, DVR, or VCR, and rack mounted camera control unit. It has an adjustable light power supply and can power both mini color and pan and tilt cameras. A built in a heavy duty aluminum flight case with compartment storage to hold remotes, disks, and cords. The DVD unit is region free and records in MPEG4 format. The DVR records onto standard camera memory cards.

The control unit has many optional upgrades including a built in auxiliary lighting kit for mainline inspections, built in crawler controller, optional basic on screen distance counter, optional deluxe distance, date, time and full on screen text counter overlays, optional grade indicator, and compulink connector enabling a portable computer to interface with the unit for data collection and report generation. Screen text overlays are controlled by a standard computer keyboard that plugs into the front of the control unit. The rugged flight case features optional configurations including built in retractable dolly handle and built in heavy duty roller wheels, side by side desktop configuration or compact flip top design with 10.4 LCD monitor.


Model 8

Our smallest model of portable camera control unit. It features a built in color LCD monitor, video out connector for connecting to a recording device, and camera control unit. It has an adjustable light power supply and can power both color and black and white camera heads. The control unit and monitor are housed in a rugged waterproof case about the size of a small briefcase. The case also hold the optional roll up keyboard for on screen text.

The control unit has many optional upgrades including built in crawler adapter, optional basic on screen distance counter, optional deluxe distance, date, time and full on screen text counter overlays and compu-link connector enabling a portable computer to interface with the unit for data collection and report generation. Optional on screen text overlays are controlled by a roll up computer keyboard that plugs into the front of the control unit and is stored inside the lid.


Model 9-11-12

The model 9 and its interchangeable pushrods are our most economical models of portable camera control units. It features a built in color LCD monitor with sun shade, digital recorder with USB, and video out connector for connecting to a separate recording device. It has an adjustable light power supply and houses a complete mini reel with slip ring and mini colour camera.

The control unit, monitor, and camera reel are housed in a rugged waterproof case about the size of a briefcase. The case also holds the battery, battery charger, and interconnect cables. The Model 9 system is idea for pipe, wall, and air duct inspections as well as many industrial type applications.


Model 10

The model 10 control unit is the smallest portable color pan and tilt camera units. It features a built in color LCD monitor with sun shade, digital recorder with USB, and video out connector for connecting to a separate recording device. It has an adjustable light power supply and houses a complete mini reel with built in inclinometer for its pan and tilt camera head.

The control unit, monitor, and camera reel are housed in a rugged waterproof case about the size of a briefcase. The case also holds the battery, battery charger, and interconnect cables. The Model 10 camera is a fully remote controlled pan and tilt camera head measuring 1.6 in diameter and is ideal for industrial inspections.

Product Name:
HAND POWERED REELS
The hand reel is a compact slip ring reel capable of holding up to 700 of mini camera cable for Mainline Sewer Inspection. The reel is compact and light weight enabling it to be moved easily by hand. It is equipped with a high quality mercury slip ring and optional electronic distance encoder.

Product Name:
POWERED REWIND REELS
Our powered reels are available for any cable length and diameter and feature variable speed rewind, level wind, and mercury slip ring assemblies. All reels are constructed with full steel tubing in frame, with epoxy powder coated for durability. Motors are available for power in AC, DC or hydraulic use. The winch reel is normally reserved for fixed vehicle mounting due to its size and capacity, but comes with detachable wheels for easy relocation between jobs.


Product Name:
SKID SET & AUXILIARY LIGHTING
The Skid Set is used for manhole to manhole inspections. It consists of a pair of sled runners with front and back stainless tow leads, a protective tube, and adapter plates for centering the camera in pipes from 6 to 24 in diameter. An auxiliary light is used for larger pipes needing more illumination.


Product Name:
PUSHRODS
The integrated pushrod reels is designed to propel the mini camera series in small diameter pipes such as building sewer laterals. Several cable diameters are available for the fill range of pipe diameters from 1-1/2 to 8 diameter. Cable lengths up to 400 are available. The cable reel can be operated in both a standing up and laying down position.

Cables are contracted around a rugged fiberglass core with copper conductors wrapped along with Kevlar and steel reinforcing members. The cable jacket is a tough and slippery high density polyethylene plastic in bright safety orange color. The cable end connection is contained within a heavy duty spring assembly with metal shelled military specification connector.

A high quality mercury slip ring assembly ensures completely noise free, maintenance free service. There is also an optional accurate electronic distance counter and built in 512 Hz radio transmitter for above ground locating. The wheeled frame assembly is lightweight and easy to maneuver on built in wheels.

Product Name:
PIPE LOCATORS
Pipe transmitters are available as separate or built in units ranging from 16Hz to 223kHz . A radio receiver can then locate accurately the camera position from above ground.


Product Name:
CAMERA CRAWLER
The Camera Crawler is a wheeled, motorized housing for a camera. Fitting into pipe sizes ranging from 4" to 36" standard, and lengths up to 2000'. Each crawler body is cut from a solid piece of either aluminum, stainless steel or navel brass. Each comes with their own wheel kits, instructions and lubricants for ease of maintenance. All parts are manufactured in house and are available with their respective accessories. All models are interchangeable with any Image Inspection camera control boxes.


Mole Crawler

The smallest member of a family of 3 robust wheeled camera crawlers. Pipe diameters from 4 to 12 are possible with the use of various sizes of solid or pneumatic wheels and spacers. The camera carries a built in high resolution color camera and lights, and best suited for lateral lines. It comes with its own high torque 48 volt motor, or a duel motor for added distance and strength. comes with forward, reverse, and variable speed abilities. Able to pull up to 500' of mini camera cable.


Gopher Crawler

The intermediate member of a family of 3 robust wheeled camera crawlers, and the most popular. Pipe diameters from 6 to 24 pipe are possible with the use of various sizes of solid or pneumatic wheels and spacers. The camera can carry standard or pan and tilt cameras, but requires a front mounted mini camera when negotiating 6 diameter pipe. This crawler is best suited for lines up to 500 in length.


Wolverine Crawler

The largest member of a family of 3 robust wheeled camera crawlers, and one that features a dual motor drive for steerable operations. Pipe diameters from 8 to 36 pipe are possible with the use of various sizes of solid or pneumatic wheels and spacers. The camera can carry standard or pan and tilt cameras and is ideal for demanding 8 and larger operation.


Grizzly Crawler

Pipe diameters from 24 and larger are possible with the use of various sizes of pneumatic wheels and spacers. The camera can carry standard or pan and tilt cameras and is ideal for demanding large pipe operation. The Grizzly features 4 high torque 48 volt motors, independently drawn for steerable operation. Variable speed options and transmission neutral available. 3 sizes of pneumatic storm tire available for this model, and interchangeable in tires from all crawlers.

Product Name:
CAMERA HEADS
A variety of interchangeable sealed camera heads are available including micro color, high resolution, and pan and tilt cameras. Able to be mounted on hard pushrod cable, soft cable, or on crawler control units it allows versatility to many operation conditions. Cut from a solid bar of aluminum or stainless steel, each has recessed lighting, shatter proof lenses and rugged metal military connectors.


C17B

The most popular camera has 570 lines of resolution color and 0.3 Lux sensitivity. It has a white LED solid state lighting consisting of 30 LEDs high output in bullet nose.


C20 Screw Pump Camera

This camera has 350 lines of Resolution color and 0.3 Lux sensitivity. It has LED solid state lighting consisting of 15 high output LEDs in white.


C26SL

This camera is a self leveling unit with an internal gold slipring. 570 lines of Resolution color and 0.3 Lux sensitivity. It has a 30 LED white solid state light ring consisting of consisting of 15 forward and 15 angled high output LEDs.


Pan and Tilt camera

This camera head can be pushrod or crawler mounted to provide advanced viewing of pipe wall defects. Its ultra small size enables it to be used for 4 pipe inspections where a high detail of wall conditions are not possible with straight viewing mini camera heads. Designed specifically to provide a close-up view of sewer pipe walls and lateral entrances through the use of a low light sensitive movable camera head with directional lighting. This color unit is designed for operation through up to 2000 of multi-conductor coax cable in sanitary and storm sewers. Chassis construction is 100% solid state circuitry designed to withstand shocks and vibration normally sustained while being pulled through 6 or larger pipe. The camera provides 360 degrees of radial rotation viewing with chip rotation to view all sewer wall surfaces and lateral connections.

Electronic Service and Repair

We custom design circuit boards and provide assembly services for small to medium sized production runs both by hand and 12 wave solder machine.
