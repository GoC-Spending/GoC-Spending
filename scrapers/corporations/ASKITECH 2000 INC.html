Success stories:

PowerTerm has proved to be the ultimate software connectivity solution for access to information residing on host systems.

Developed in 1994, PowerTerm has been a major success in the global access market and it currently holds over 15% of the host access market across 45 countries worldwide.

Unlike other terminal emulators that consume 30-100MB of hard disk space, PowerTerm provides full functionality while only occupying 5MB of hard disk space.

PowerTerm offers simple and fast installation as well as an effective means of working with legacy applications running under Windows platforms on a variety of hosts such as IBM AS/400, IBM Mainframe, Digital and Unix, Hp and Tandem.
