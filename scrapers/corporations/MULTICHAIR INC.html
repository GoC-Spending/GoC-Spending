Company Profile


Based in Mississauga, Ontario, and operating under the names Multichair and Multitube, the company has grown steadily since its inception, and has been registered under both the international ISO 9002 and the automotive industrys QS-9000 quality designation since 1997.We are currently an ISO 9001:2008 registered company. Multitubes steel tubing is sold for consumer and commercial applications, such as furniture, automotive parts, fitness equipment, store fixtures, shower curtain rods, and fencing. We also support Steel and Tubing Warehouse operations throughout North America.


Multichair frames are used in the manufacture of quality office and contract furniture, and our reputation for quality products and service are second to none.

Product Name:
AUTOMOTIVE
AUTOMOTIVE PARTS

Multitube is a ISO 9001:2000 registered world class automotive tube supplier. We produce tubing which can currently be found in seat components, instrument panel crossbeams, engine cradles, trunk lid hinges, as well as radiator supports.

Multitube can accommodate virtually any of the high strength low alloyed (HSLA) steel grades now integrated into the engineering of many automotive parts.

As is well documented, the needs and demands of the automotive industry are high and unforgiving . That is why Multitube has invested in Eddy Current testers for all of our tube mills, a lab that is equipped to perform microstructure analysis of our welds to ensure fusion, and a hardness tester that will give us the hardness of both our parent raw material and our tubes.

Multitube's facility is also equipped with an extensive array of up-to-date Re-cutting, De-dimpling and Wirebrushing machines to meet all of our customers' length and end-finishing requirements.

Finally, Multitube is able to provide customer support and services including full lot traceability by original heat number, EDI-generated Advance Ship Notices, Order Release Schedules and others.

All AIAG and other Automotive industry standard documents are accommodated by Multitube.



Product Name:
FURNITURE
FURNITURE - CHAIRS, SWIVEL BASES,TABLE LEGS & SYSTEMS
Furniture is the backbone of Multitube. These product lines are where it all started, and serving the Office and Contract Furniture industries has been a specialty of both Multichair and Multitube.
Our size and guage range is extensive. We can offer our tubes in Hot Rolled Pickled and Oiled (HRPO) material, Cold Rolled Commerial Quality (CRCQ), or in Cold Rolled Bright and Semi-Bright finished surface.



Product Name:
FENCING-CONSUMER & INDUSTRIAL
FENCING

Multitube offers a full array of sizes required by the fencing industry. We can offer either galvalume or G90 material. The tubes can be produced either with sprayed seams or without. Swaging is also available on a limited number of sizes.
