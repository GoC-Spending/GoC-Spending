Company Profile


ControlGMC inc. was created by the merger of two complementary Montreal-area engineering firms in the early 1980s, which brought together a team of experienced engineers, programmers, and technicians.
ControlGMC inc. soon earned an excellent reputation for the quality of its services and the ingeniousness of its automatic production systems.
At ControlGMC inc., design, programming, manufacturing and testing are all done under one roof. In addition to reducing production costs, this structure enables our team to monitor the quality of our machines at every stage of the project.
After about 25 years of growth, the firm continues to put as much effort into designing and manufacturing simple and efficient machines. ControlGMC's systems now improve plant productivity throughout North America. Its customers include multinational corporations, as well as small and medium-sized companies.
Now we propose our equipment in Europe, with CE marking of our machines, and in Central America.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
FDS2500 - Fully automated trays, tubs, buckets filling / packaging / packing line
The FDS2500 is a filling line made with one motorized indexing conveyor that dispenses automatically empty containers, bring them to the filling position(s), activates the optional plastic film sealer, bring the containers under the lid(or over-cap)-placing unit, and then under the closing unit.

Product Name:
DV2000 - Volumetric piston filler for liquid, semi-viscous & viscous products
Filler designed specifically for fresh food manufacturer.
In particular, chunks are neither cut nor smashed.
It fills liquid and semi-viscous products like: hot soups, jams and spreads, yogurts with fruits, salsa, hummus & cheese dips, cottage, white cheese.
It fills viscous products like: protein salads, potato or pasta salads, cream cheese based dips, creamy coleslaw, sour cream based dips, peanut butter, cake & cookie doughs.
It can also fills sticky products like: glues, epoxies, paints, caramels.
It can fills hot products as well as cold products.

Product Name:
PL500 - Automatic plastic film sealer / lidder / sealing / lidding machine for trays, cups and tubs
The PL500 is a plastic film sealer / lidder which automatically centers the arriving container, put the plastic film on the top of it, heat seal it and then cut it right around its edges.
It can be proposed for a single lane filling line, a dual lane filling line as in the picture, or for more lanes.

Product Name:
FS1500 - Semi automatic tubs & buckets roller capper / closing machine
This closing machine is simple and efficient.
It closes tighly and effortlessly round, square or rectangular plastic containers, including tamper evident tubs with snap-on lids or overcaps.
Changeovers for different-size containers are done in seconds without tools.
The FS 1500 has a motorized conveyor with rough top belt for traction to pull the containers under the pressure rollers closing head equipped with an air-removing wheel.

Product Name:
FDS1800 - Fully automatic filling / packaging / packing line for buckets and industrial pails already denested
The FDS1800 is a packaging line composed of two motorized conveyors mounted on the same frame, equipped with a double pneumatic air burping wheels, and specially designed for the closing of the industrial type pails and buckets.
The first conveyor has a slippery tabletop plastic belt that bring empty containers to the filling position (in option), then under the lid-placing unit.
The second conveyor has a rough top belt for traction to pull the containers under the pressure rollers closing head equipped with an air-removing wheel.

Product Name:
FDS1400 - Fully automatic filling / packaging / packing line for tubs and buckets already filled
The FDS1400 is a packaging line made with one motorized tabletop belt conveyor that bring already filled containers under the lid-placing unit, and then under the closing unit.
The FDS1400 exists in two configurations.
The first configuration, for liquid and semi-liquid products, put the focus on keeping the product inside the container, while in the second configuration, for dry or viscous products the focus is on speed.

LC50 - Lanes concentrator

A set of guides pushes the containers of the various lanes onto one single lane, thus concentrating the containers fluxes.

Technology profile

Control GMC was created by the merger of two complementary
Montreal-area businesses in the early 1980s. One was Contrôle
GMC, which at that time provided consulting services for
automation of industrial production lines. The other was
Posistep, a manufacturer of high-tech automatic production
equipment.

This shrewd merger brought together a comprehensive team of
specialists: experienced engineers, programmers, technicians
and professionals. Control GMC soon gained a excellent
reputation for the quality of its service and the ingeniousness
of its automatic systems.

After more than 25 years of growth, the firm continues to expend
as much effort designing and building simple, efficient
machines. Contrôle GMCs systems now improve plant productivity
throughout North America and Europe. Its customers include
multinational corporations, as well as small and medium-sized
companies.

Control GMCs greatest reward is to see yesterdays customers
coming back to buy new equipment. Because this means that
Control GMC has helped a partner grow.

It closes tighly and effortlessly round, square or rectangular plastic containers, including tamper evident tubs with snap-on lids or overcaps.
Changeovers for different-size containers are done in seconds without tools.
The FS 1800 has a motorized conveyor with rough top belt for traction to pull the containers under the pressure rollers closing head equipped with a pneumatic air-removing wheel.

Product Name:
LC50 - Lanes concentrator
The LC50 is a lane concentrator made of one (or more) table top conveyor(s) with, at the entry side, as many lanes as there are incoming lanes.
The aim is to concentrate those incoming lanes into one lane.
A set of guides pushes the containers of the various lanes onto one single lane, thus concentrating the containers fluxes.

T50 - Accumulation turning table
The T50 is a heavy duty stainless steel accumulation table designed and built to provide long lasting service in the demanding fresh food manufacturing environment where sanitation requirements impose constant washing.

PV1000 - Medium capacity piston transfer pump for liquid, semi-viscous & viscous products
Medium capacity transfer pump designed specifically for fresh food manufacturer.
In particular, chunks are neither cut nor smashed.
It pumps liquid and semi-viscous products like: hot soups, jams and spreads, yogurts with fruits, salsa, hummus & cheese dips, cottage, white cheese.
It pumps viscous products like: protein salads, potato or pasta salads, cream cheese based dips, creamy coleslaw, sour cream based dips, peanut butter, cake & cookie doughs.
It can also pumps sticky products like: glues, epoxies, paints, caramels.
It can pumps hot products as well as cold products.

Technology profile

Control GMC was created by the merger of two complementary
Montreal-area businesses in the early 1980s. One was Contrôle
GMC, which at that time provided consulting services for
automation of industrial production lines. The other was
Posistep, a manufacturer of high-tech automatic production
equipment.

This shrewd merger brought together a comprehensive team of
specialists: experienced engineers, programmers, technicians
and professionals. Control GMC soon gained a excellent
reputation for the quality of its service and the ingeniousness
of its automatic systems.

After more than 25 years of growth, the firm continues to expend
as much effort designing and building simple, efficient
machines. Contrôle GMCs systems now improve plant productivity
throughout North America and Europe. Its customers include
multinational corporations, as well as small and medium-sized
companies.

Control GMCs greatest reward is to see yesterdays customers
coming back to buy new equipment. Because this means that
Control GMC has helped a partner grow.
