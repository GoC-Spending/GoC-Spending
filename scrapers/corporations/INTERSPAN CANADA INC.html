Company Profile


Interspan Canada was founded in 1989 as a professional, global trade organization importing and exporting a wide variety of products. Expertise and experience in a wide variety of fields, enable Interspan to function successfully in all phases of international commerce, including marketing, sales, import/export management, sourcing, procurement, warehousing, shipping and documentation. With capability in over 16 languages and staff from many different cultures of the world, Interspan is well positioned to serve their clients in over 40 countries in the Far East, Middle East, Central and South America, Europe and the Caribbean. We also have a solid supplier base in Canada and other countries.
