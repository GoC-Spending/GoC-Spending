Company Profile


The highest quality materials and manufacturing standards are
combined by skilled, experienced craftsmen utilizing proven
technology to ensure customers receive the
best product possible.

Operating its own electrostatic powder coat finishing facility provides Accro Furniture Industries the ability to offer a multitude of colours and textures on the company's extensive line of contract and residential furniture.

Innovation in design, commitment to quality, and many years of
experience have enabled Acme Chrome Furniture to establish a
presence across Canada, the United States and the UK.
