Company Profile


If you are in need of some residential moving or commercial moving professionals to help you with your next move, get in touch with us. We are Premiere Van Lines, a experienced and dedicated moving company and we are offering our expert services to people living in Halifax and its surrounding areas.

Whether you want to move in the next neighborhood, or you just bought a home in a different country, Premiere Van Lines will help you get there. We can also help you relocate your business in a quick and professional manner, to ensure you will not lose too much of your precious time.

To us, moving is not a simple business: it's an art. Our team will handle, pack and move your possessions with care, and you can trust us to deliver the highest level of service in the moving industry. With 50 years of experience, we have successfully taken care of many different types of moves, and we know that no job is too big or too complicated for us.

Our residential moving and commercial moving services include planning, packing, moving, unpacking, and storage. If you would like to experience the Art of Moving according to Premiere Van Lines, let us handle your moving needs.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Residential moving
Whether you are moving in a home on the next street, in another Canadian province, or on the other side of the world, Premiere Van Lines is there for you. For us, handling your possessions with care is as important as ensuring your peace of mind through the whole process of moving.

From carefully planning your move to packing, moving and unpacking your belongings, our professional team can take care of everything for you. All you will have to do is tell us where you are moving, and when.

If you own a piano, antiques or fine art pieces, you can count on us to wrap and handle them with the highest level of special care. When you choose the residential moving services of Premiere Van Lines for your next move, you are in fact choosing to experience the peace of mind that comes with the Art of Moving.

Service Name:
Commercial moving
If you are a business owner, you know that your business cannot afford to lose time. When you have no choice but to relocate your office, you need a team of professional movers who will help you minimize downtime while respecting your furniture and your budget.

Premiere Van Lines specializes in commercial moving. Our uniformed, bonded and trained movers have access to the latest technologies and to custom equipment, and they will use them to offer you fast commercial moving services of the highest quality.

When you choose us for your commercial move, your Premiere Van Lines Project Manager will listen to your needs, and carefully plan and supervise the relocation of your business from start to finish. Your budget and your expectations will be respected, and you and your staff will keep your peace of mind through the whole process.
