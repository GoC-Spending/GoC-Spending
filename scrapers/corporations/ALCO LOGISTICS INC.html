Company Profile


Alco Logistics Inc. is an international freight forwarder based in Richmond, BC for over 12 years.

We provide logistics and transportation solutions for imports and exporters. Alco provides complete range of import and export freight services for companies in international trade.

With our staff holding over 25 years of international freight forwarding experience and strong relationships with reputable suppliers, Alco Logistics is glad to be the service provider of hundreds of companies world wide.

We arrange air & ocean, import, export freight services, break bulk cargo, RORO, customs brokerage, export documentation and most other related shipping procedures and logistics coordination.
