Company Profile


Septimatech is the leading provider of innovative quick changeover packaging line products and services. Our Customer Centric Changeover Solutions reduce changeover costs creating value to the bottom line. Each line change is reliable, accurate and repeatable every time. Immediate productivity and efficiency gains move products to market quicker with less cost. Our right the first time philosophy to provide Customerswhat they want, how they want, when they wantforms part of our core values.

Our line of products include Change Parts, Feed Screws, Guide Rails, Storage Carts and Label Handling Systems. All are engineered custom to your specific application for quick, easy change and efficient adjustment.

Septimatech brands - RXNT®(Rapid eXchange No Tool) Change Parts and the Unison® Modular Flex Guide Rail Adjustment System (Rapid Adjustment No Tool) - work on the principles of World Class Lean Manufacturing.

Associations: Member of PMMI, Kitchener-Waterloo Chamber of Commerce, CME, and CTTEN.

Product Name:
Change Parts
Changeparts available in: Quick Change (RXNT®) and OEM Standard

The RXNT® (Rapid eXchange No Tools) Change Parts system is based upon the one-time installation of Nucleus components mounted on any rotary or in-line machine. Satellite changeover parts, customized to the specific container geometry, are changed quickly and efficiently to maximize line performance... without tools!

Septimatech's experienced team of changeover experts build upon the OEM standard to enhance the fit, function, and performance of the Change Parts and application. Septimatech provides Change Parts for over 225 different packaging machines.


Product Name:
Feed Screws
Feedscrews available in: Quick Change (RXNT®) and OEM Standard

Septimatech Feed Screws feature a pocket to accurately fit the container profile - whether it is round, square, oval, or something in-between. Our Feed Screws provide flawless container transfer without scuffing or marking the container or label surface. Available in RXNT® (Rapid eXchange No Tool) and OEM Standard.

Custom designed single or dual Feed Screw Drive Systems integrate into your equipment or can be stand-alone units to tilt angle neck containers, separate, time, dwell, group, turn, divide, or combine. Available in RXNT® (Rapid eXchange No Tool) and OEM Standard Feed Screw mount configurations.

Maintain prime in Feed Screws at all times with the Programmable Infeed Control System (PICS). This Human Machine Interface system uses dual split servo drives to operate as a bottle stop, to separate even the most difficult containers and skip machine stations while maintaining consistent container flow for optimal line performance.

Product Name:
Guide Rails
Guide Rails available in: Unison® Modular Flex Guide Rail Adjustment System and RXNT® Guide Rails

Unison® Modular Flex Guide Rail Adjustment System provides rapid line changeover with consistent, accurate and repeatable set-up. Change up to 80 feet of guide rail including S-bends, crossovers, and corners from a single position without pneumatics.

Achieve enhanced control specifically for smaller containers or cap handling applications. The Mini Unison® System provides solutions to the most difficult conveying challenges.

Quick Change RXNT® (Rapid eXchange No Tool) Guide Rail Systems reduce changeover time, increase container handling efficiency and improve line productivity. Offered in straight rail, side transfer, crossovers and corner guide sections.

Product Name:
Storage Concepts
Storage Concepts:
Simplify and optimize the entire changeover process with Custom Storage Concepts. A place for everything, everything in its place; this is the simple concept of World Class Manufacturing and 5S Lean Principles. Storage Concepts are customized to store any changeover parts.

Ergonomic Carts:
With our ergonomic carts you can reduce health and safety risks caused by repetitive actions and decrease the loss of time due to worker injury. The ergonomic carts are custom designed to meet the requirements of your work areas.

Our experienced team of changeover experts will build upon the OEM standard to enhance the fit and function of the parts. Parts are customized for optimal performance and label placement without any modifications to your existing labeler.

Meyer Labeler:

Technology profile

Technology and R&D Technology
Our philosophy at Septimatech Group Inc. is based on the premise that technology is only as good as the results it achieves. Technology is the systematic application of industrial arts, sciences, and common sense principles to your problem using the most up-to-date tools available. Our commitment to continuous Research & Development in the area of product and container motion control ensures that every computer-aided Design and Manufacturing process is being brought to bear on your needs, and that every laboratory advance in exotic and cost-effective materials is utilized. Even the most sophisticated machinery, by itself, cannot be innovative or encourage improvement. These performance enhancements come from people and, at Septimatech, we believe our people are our biggest advantage.

Research and Development
Todays packaging lines operate in a highly competitive global environment where flexible, lean, and sustainable throughput practices are essential in obtaining corporate objectives. We have the expertise in providing solutions to make line changeovers virtually tool-less while conforming to World Class Manufacturing and 5S Lean Principles. Products such as the Unison® Modular Flex Guide Rail Adjustment System combined with RXNT® (Rapid eXchange No Tool) Change Parts and Custom Storage Concepts provide our Customers with solutions to maximize throughput on their packaging lines.

Septimatech is a niche-market, innovative, Customer centric organization. This provides the opportunity to focus on Customers' specific needs by customizing results for specific applications, turning around projects in a timely manner, and offering innovative solutions to the most complex challenges, all while improving production efficiencies, reducing employee training, reducing changeover time, and improving container handling. We are confident that you will be more than satisfied with the relationship Septimatech will provide and the results you will achieve.

Septimatech Group Inc. is ISO-9001 Registered and has vast experience working with over 225 packaging machines.
