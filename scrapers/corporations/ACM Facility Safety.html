Company Profile


Located in Calgary, AB, ACM Facility Safety is Canada's largest independent provider of Hazard Analysis, Safeguard and Risk Assessment services. By providing expert risk consulting, process safety education and safety lifecycle software solutions, we help the world's largest operating and engineering companies improve facility uptime, manage process risk and comply with international safety standards.

Process Safety Solutions

Today, as one of the largest companies focused on providing process safety solutions, ACM runs a business that provides standards-driven risk management by offering education, risk consulting services and software solutions to individuals and organizations, over a wide variety of industries, that want to help make the world a safer place.

RISK CONSULTING SERVICES
ACM Facility Safety provides innovative safety solutions for all aspects of the design and facilitation of High Integrity and Critical Control systems.

EDUCATION
ACM Facility Safety is a recognized global provider of Process Safety education, tools and methodologies facilitated via the Institute of Hazard Prevention.

SOFTWARE
ACM Facility Safety provides software tools that make planning, operating, monitoring and trouble-shooting facility critical control systems safe, comprehensive and user-friendly.

ANALYTICS:
To gain further insight into process safety ACM has created specialized analytic offerings to stay safer.

PROCESS SAFETY TRAINING

Whether developing and rolling out a new initiative, on-boarding new personnel, transferring people to new roles or preparing for an upcoming project, ACM’s clients utilize our training programs to support them and their teams. ACM’s course developers build interactive, experiential adult training programs based on the 150 plus collective years of experience in our group. Using their real life experiences they create courses that add value and are relatable to participants. ACM offers our courses privately for specific client organizations and publicly through the Institute of Hazard Prevention. ACM offers a full course catalogue that features 12 public training courses as well as customizable private courses. Subject matter ranges from basic knowledge to certifications.

RISK CONSULTING SERVICES

Risk Management:

ACM offers consulting advice to clients throughout the full safety lifecycle, ranging from the conceptual phase through to operations. Risk management services include:
1. Facilitation Services
2. Safeguard Engineering
3. Procedure Development
4. Pre-Start-up Safety Review
5. Risk Matrix Building and Calibration
6. Safety Culture Self-Assessment
7. Strategic Planning (Process Safety)
8. Contingency Planning
9. Functional Safety Assessments

PHA Analytics:

In order to extract further value from the knowledge captured during PHA sessions, ACM’s team of experienced consultants have developed the following suite of PHA Analytics:

1. Safeguard Criticality Analysis
Do you know which safeguards in your facility are protecting you the most?
2. Recommendation Prioritization
Out of your list of HAZOP action items, which recommendations will provide maximum value and return for your facility?
3. Safeguard Operating Status (S.O.S.) Field Audit
What is the gap between the safeguards used for risk reduction in your risk assessments and the current operation?

Analytics Included with ACM PHA’s:

1. Stakeholder Review
The Stakeholder Review is a method to provide high level, target feedback on a Process Hazard Analysis (PHA) session to individuals who are not required to be in the PHA session, but have a vested interest in ensuring the high quality of process safety related activities.
2. Safeguard Summary
Safeguard Summary provides a way to further explore the diversity of current safeguards, and future safeguards (recommendations), in a facility.


SOFTWARE

SAFEGUARD PROFILER

Safeguard Profiler™ is a field proven, IEC 61511 compliant Safety Integrity Level (SIL) Life Cycle tool that gives high integrity and critical control systems designers, engineers, operators and maintainers the information and power to conduct SIL Determination, Verification/Validation and Optimization exercises. Developed by ACM Automation Inc. over several years and used successfully on onshore and offshore projects, Safeguard Profiler™ is truly unique in its ability to integrate all aspects of the Safety Life Cycle – from importing Risk Assessment (i.e. HAZOP) data to enable facility operators to manage the integrity of safety and critical control systems.

SAFEGUARD SENTINEL

SafeGuard Sentinel is innovative software that reveals danger in the plant in real time. SafeGuard Sentinel continuously measures, monitors and alerts you to process safety risks and delivers contingency plans that can be executed immediately. By presenting real time risk information and expert knowledge to make effective operational decisions, SafeGuard Sentinel helps prevent incidents, safety infractions and unexpected outages.


RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT – RISK ALIVE

Innovation in Process Safety relies heavily on analytics and big data in order to create leading metrics and provide usable insight in order to improve performance. Risk Alive is our research and development arm and this will aid in the vision and direction of the company. Risk Alive aims to analyse PHA data to benchmark, understand and predict process safety related topics. ACM has partnered with the University of Calgary and their computer science and statistics departments to create this vision. Our ongoing research has led to advancements in process safety including our current analytic offerings.
