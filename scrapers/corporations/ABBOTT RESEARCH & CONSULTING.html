Company Profile


We specialize in customer experience and customer innovation consulting for services organizations.

Services include:
- Qualitative research (focus groups, interviews, online forums, bulletin boards, ethnography, discovery labs)
- Customer experience and customer journey mapping
- Service strategy consulting
- Support to help you identify opportunities for innovation in customer experience
- Expert facilitation of war games workshops to help you get inside the heads of your competitors, customers, and regulators
- Training workshops in facilitation and creative thinking methods
- Keynote presentations on topics related to creative thinking, innovation, and customer experience
- Expert moderation of panels at conferences

We have deep expertise in financial services, both B2B and B2C (banking, insurance, investments, wealth management, trust services, corporate banking, FX, etc.)
We have significant expertise in professional services branding, employment branding, publishing, retail sales, major appliances, OTC pharmaceuticals, major media, and technology
Highly skilled at executive interviews and executive facilitation

We are the Canadian associates for Think Global Qualitative, an alliance of boutique consultancies with similar capabilities. We can provide you with global support for qualitative research and creative facilitation services in all major global markets. Please refer to http://www.thinkglobalqualitative.com for details.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Focus Groups
Focus groups are not dead -- in fact, they continue to be a solid methodology for obtaining customer insights.
We use a variety of proven approaches to help you gather the actionable insights you need.
We have experience in multi-lingual and multi-country studies, across many categories of consumers and businesses.

Project pricing starts at $9500 for two groups in one market, including all costs and a strategic report with recommendations.

Service Name:
Employee Research
Research with employees is always an intervention in your system, and needs to be carefully managed to lead only to positive outcomes.
Client challenges have included:
- understanding what is holding back employee engagement metrics on tracking studies
- how to recruit and retain the best
- how to empower employees to serve customers better
- identifying operational issues that are barriers to customer service
- tapping into employee knowledge of best practices to leverage success across the organization
- mobilizing employees around major change and transformation projects

We have used a wide variety of methods for this work, including online forums, facilitated workshops, large employee engagement events, focus groups, and individual interviews.
Our clients have included banks, municipalities, global professional services firms, telecommunications companies, and directory publishers.

Project pricing typically in the $25,000 to $50,000 range


Service Name:
Online Focus Groups
President Susan Abbott is co-author of the industry-leading book on online qualitative research: Qual-Online: The Essential Guide (Paramount Books, 2014).
We can assist you with a wide variety of approaches to online insight gathering:
- mobile ethnography
- online communities
- online discussion forums
- online diaries and extended studies
- webcam interviews and discussion groups
- global insights projects online
- ideation projects online

Please contact us to discuss the possibilities, and obtain an example report for a multi-country study on dog ownership.

Service Name:
Customer journey mapping
Map the customer experience through all touchpoints to support new experience design, improved customer outcomes, improved productivity.
Multiple methods are used, customized to meet your specific circumstances.
Deliverables include a complete journey map; identification of crucial touchpoints for customers; problems and opportunities; recommendations for action.
Approximate pricing: $25,000 to $60,000 depending on complexity.

Service Name:
Executive Interviews
You may need to interview internal executives to solicit their viewpoints.
Or you may want to interview executives in your customer/client organizations.
Or you may want to interview executives who have specialist expertise, for best practices, or industry trend studies.
We have done all of these kinds of projects, including international and multi-language studies.

Executives are among the most challenging individuals to interview successfully -- they are busy, impatient, and fast thinkers who need to be intellectually engaged in order to share their true ideas. We are experts in this area.

Service Name:
Customer laboratories and workshops
When you want insights to power innovation, a customer lab is the perfect solution.
Your project team interacts with a small group of customers in a carefully planned and facilitated session. This powerful methodology invariably leads to new ideas for achieving competitive advantage, clear prioritization of customer issues, and a project team that is aligned on priorities.

Individual half-day labs can be arranged from $6,000. Typical projects are $25,000 to $50,000 including strategic recommendations.
