Company Profile


As a worldwide leader in helicopter Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul (MRO), Vector Aerospace Helicopter Services – North America provides comprehensive worldwide support for Airbus Helicopters, Sikorsky and Bell helicopters. It also holds designations from four OEM’s for helicopter (turboshaft) engines: Pratt & Whitney, Rolls-Royce, Airbus Helicopters, Sikorsky, General Electric and Turbomeca. This division has facilities in Richmond BC, Langley BC and Andalusia AL, offering on-site and off-site MRO support, 24/7.
