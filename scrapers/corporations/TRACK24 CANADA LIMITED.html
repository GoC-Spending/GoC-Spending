Company Profile


Track24 was established in 2004 in response to the precarious situation faced by civilian contractors in Iraq. To provide an operational picture showing the location of personnel and vehicles on digital maps, Track24 introduced satellite tracking and miniaturised GSM technologies. A process was then established to identify who was at risk and pinpoint their exact location within seconds.

This system enables a swift, efficient response to emergencies. Panic alarms on Track24 devices proved fundamental to saving lives in the thousands of incidents our clients have been involved in.

Track24 has set the benchmark for the technological standards and quality of service expected in the highly demanding Security Tracking and Risk Management Sector (STRMS). Track24 now operates globally providing a wide range of products, tracking devices, location management systems, and situational command and control solutions.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
WHISPER
Satellite-based tracking with integral panic button and two-way text messaging.Whisper operates on the Iridium satellite network, which offers complete global coverage. Robust and versatile, the device can be hand-carried or deployed as a vehicle tracker using the optional vehicle kit. Track24 offers AES-256 bit encryption as standard across Iridium, ensuring the integrity of data.

The reporting rate can be changed as required, from one minute intervals upwards. Waypoint marking and 'checkin' messages are also supported, all controlled through the device's colour LCD screen.

Product Name:
ECHO
Echo operates on the Iridium satellite network, which offers complete global coverage and the option of AES-256-bit encryption. Robust and versatile, the device can be hand-carried or deployed as a vehicle tracker using the optional vehicle kit.
The reporting rate can be changed as required over the air, from one-minute intervals upwards. Waypoint marking and check-in messages are also supported, all controlled through the devices simple one touch buttons.

When the panic button is pushed, audible and visual alerts appear on the SCC Platform. Notification of the alarm is also automatically forwarded by SMS and e-mail to nominated recipients and SCC Tactical first responders.

The Echo provides 2-way chat messaging (free text) using an Android smart phone or tablet between the device and the SCC Platform, device to asset group and device to device is supported. Free text, predefined canned messages and asset groups are available.

Product Name:
Smart24
SMART24 provides a tracking and emergency alarm capability through iPhone, Android or BlackBerry handsets. The App allows for information such as positional data, messages and alerts to be presented in real time on the Track24 SCC Platform. This enables commanders to quickly identify the exact location & status of personnel and to make timely command & control decisions.

TRACKING

SMART24 tracker will send accurate, encrypted position updates, alarms and text messages back to the Track24 monitoring software. For personal privacy, tracking can be easily switched off when it is not required.

EMERGENCY ALERT

When the emergency alarm is activated a near real time notification will appear in the form of audible and visual alerts on the SCC Platform. An individuals location will update every 15 seconds and their status can be rapidly assessed and an effective response initiated.

Notification of an alarm is also automatically forwarded by SMS and e-mail to nominated recipients in your organisation.

ENCRYPTED TEXT MESSAGING

The application provides 2-way secure text messaging between the smartphone and the SCC Platform, and to other devices.

HEARTBEAT

If the user turns tracking off, they still have the option to send a Heartbeat message every day. This will allow the operations room to know the emergency button is still operational.

POLL

If the Apps tracking mode is switched off, the Network Operating Centre can still send a Poll command. This commands the App to send back its current position which can be useful for locating a users current location in an emergency. The poll feature can be turned off or locked if required.

Service Name:
M2M
M2M (Machine to Machine communications)

M2M refers to Machine to Machine communications  where machine stands for any device or sensor anywhere in the world. It is now possible to receive geospatially referenced data from machines and represent it on digital mapping. That information can be processed, manipulated and sent back to devices in the field. Devices can be interrogated or reconfigured in the field by pushing commands at them.

Track24 and M2M

To achieve M2M capability Track24 has invested heavily in the development of software and hardware platforms with the right characteristics. The bespoke hardware developed in-house acts as a communications bearer over satellite for the machines in the field. Exploiting the SBD capabilities of Iridium and Inmarsat coupled with our expertise in the design and build of gateways and Message Handling Systems has allowed us to rapidly come up with innovative improvements to existing capabilities. The sections that follow outline some examples in the M2M space where Track24 is already pioneering developments.

Sensors

Radiation & Atmospherics

Track24 has provided the Canadian Environmental services Agency the ability to collect data from their sensors in real time anywhere in the world.. We integrated their sensor into our satellite devices that acted as the communications network and provided a geospatial reference point thereby allowing the data to be passed back to the DEA platform in real time. As a result, data from sensors spread across Canada was retrieved in a more timely fashion.

CBRN (Chemical Biological Radiation Nuclear)

The Canadian DND (military) provide chemical and biological sensors to soldiers operating in some theatres. In the past, however, only the soldier wearing the device would be made aware of a threat if one was detected. By integrating a Whisper or Echo device to the sensor, allowing the threat data to be passed back to the platform, Track24 created a process whereby a message would be automatically generated to warn other soldiers within a geofenced area of the threat.

IVMS

Many industries, and particularly Oil and Gas companies, have a stipulated requirement for IVMS to enhance the safety of their personnel. IVMS allows data from a vehicle's CANBUS such as speed, harsh breaking, acceleration, lights on/off, ignition on/off and driver identification to be passed back to a platform for analysis. The main objective is to improve driver performance and reduce accidents.Track24 have produced an IVMS system that uses both satellites and GSM/GPRS to backhaul data. The data is then stored, processed and represented in a manner that provides actionable information for managers.

Electronic Counter Measures (ECM)

ECM equipment is used in vehicles to block the electronic signals from devices such as mobile phones which might be used to set off an Improvised Explosive Device (IED). Allen Vanguard produces Equinox, a leading ECM product. The problem was that the vehicle needed to return to base to have its settings changed. Furthermore, any threat data collected warned the occupants of the vehicle but no one else - the data remained on board the vehicle. Integrating a Track24 device to act as a satellite communication network and providing a gateway into a bespoke platform designed by Track24, meant the settings could be remotely configured in real time, and the threat data could be backhauled from the vehicles to base for analysis. Other assets within a geofenced area could then automatically be sent the threat information, enabling them to take appropriate action.

Service Name:
SCC Platform
The Track24 SCC Platform provides military and government organisations with a secure web-based application for full blue force tracking capability, including situational awareness, operational command and control, emergency response and the ability to provide comprehensive post operation debriefing. Track24 Defence also provides a turn-key server solution precisely tailored to the SCC Platform, allowing forces to operate situational command and control over 10,000 assets at any one time.

All communications use a secure virtual private network and AES256 encryption, and all satellite data is sent via a secure satellite network offering no-weak-link, point-to-point encryption.


Service Name:
SCC Tactical
Track24 Defences SCC Tactical is the mobile Blue Force Tracking situational awareness component of the SCC Titan solution. It is designed to be accessed on a tablet or laptop computer and is used in conjunction with the SCC Platform as a client-based standalone BFT solution. It utilises satellite communications infrastructure to ensure continuous secure operation, anywhere in the world.

SCC Tactical is a client-based software application that integrates the flexibility of the US military mission management software FalconView, with Track24 Defences situational command and control capability for the tactical user. Used with a portable computer, laptop or tablet, it is designed to connect and operate directly with the Track24 Iridium Whisper - the primary Track24 Defence personnel satellite tracking and command and control device.

Combining the Whisper with the SCC Tactical application allows field-personnel total, beyond-line-of-sight, blue force tracking and situational command and control, enabling tactical awareness, advanced communications capability and ultimate mission success.
