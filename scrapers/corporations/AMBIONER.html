Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Commissioning (Cx)
Comissioning is a quality-asssurance process that ensures the performance of a building’s systems and assemblies, as well as their conformity with the intentions of the designer and with the owner’s requirements. The main advantage of such a process is that it allows to control the early achievement of expected performance from the beginning to the end of the project, regarless of its nature (construction, renovation, redevelopment, expansion, etc.).

Ambioner is a pioneer of commissioning in Quebec. We have accomplished several large-scale projects in the institutional and commercial sectors, with clients such the Société Québécois des Infrastuctures ans Public Works and Government Service Canada. We leverage our strenght in our numerous expert resources, among which we count engineers which are certified CPMP (Commissioning Process Management Professional) by the AHSRAE, as well as expert technicians in building control and optimization.

Service Name:
Recommissioning (RCx)
Recommissioning consists in improving the operation modes of buildings or systems according to the current needs and condition of the building, without involving major investments for the replacement of main electromechanical equipment. According to Natural Resources Canada, recommissioning typically leads to a reduction of the overall energy consumption of a building by 5 to 15 %, not to mention that this process improves the value and lifecycle of the building, as well as the comfort of its occupants.

Ambioner was one of the first companies in Quebec to offer its building recommissioning(Rcx) services, independently from turnkey energy-efficiency projects. We have earned a strong reputation in the market, by helping customers like McGill University , the University of Quebec and the Grand Théâtre de Québec to achieve energy savings and major operation improvements, while increasing the service life of their equipement.


Service Name:
LEED, BOMA BESt, Green Globes and other Certifications- Application process and consulting services
The LEED® rating system is designed to measure a project’s compliance with particular criteria for performance and sustainable development that outperform typical standard practices. Following a LEED® certification process of the Green Building Concil of Canada, also means ensuring that the engineers, architects and contractors working on the project have to deliver a high quality building, while minimizing the environmental impact of its construction and operation. With a great number of achieved LEED® certification processes, from Certified to Platinum levels, Ambioner offers the expertise of its LEED® accredited professionals to help you successfully achieve your project’s certification process.
Our high-level expertise also extends to BOMA BESt, Green Globes and Novo Climat certification processes.


Service Name:
Energy Audits and Energy Master Plans
Energy audits are an excellent way to acquire a better knowledge of a building’s energy consumption. Ambioner’s experts first visit the installations and collect information on the operating modes, then analyze these data. They will then submit a detailed energy audit report with the conclusions of their analysis. A typical energy audit report contains the following information:
• Description of electromechanical installations;
• Assessment and analysis of the actual energy consumption (kW,kWh,GJ, GHGs);
• Analysis of the variations in energy consumption observed during the year;
• Distribution of the energy consumption by energy-consuming systems;
• Comparison of the building’s consumption with that of similar buildings;and
• Suggestions of energy-efficiency measures tailored to customer needs.
With an energy audit report in hand, we will be able to elaborate an energy-savings project solution or to produce an energy master plan that will help you make informed choices to reduce your energy costs efficiently.


Service Name:
Energy-Efficient Design
Once an energy audit report is completed, if customer wishes to implement energy-efficiency measures, each improvement opportunity will be carefully studied, in order to determine if they are feasible. Ambioner’s experts will evaluate the costs incurred and savings expectable, as well as the environmental impact (indoor and outdoor environment) and service life of each energy-savings measure, in order to choose the most profitable. We can then carry out the design of the selected measures and and supervise thir implementation.
Our professionals will also help you identify the type of investments most suitable for your building in order to maximize the cost efficiency of your infrastructures on a long-term basis. Here are a few examples of questions we can help you answer:
Should we undertake a qualification process for a turnkey energy efficiency project with an energy savings company, or rather work with a engineering consulting firm on a project under the traditional approach?
Our boilers are obsolete. Should we simply remplace them, explore new technologies, update our whole heating network… How can we get the right information?
Could we use a part of our budget dedicated to infrastructures to integrate new technologies?


Service Name:
Consulting Services
Our high-level expertise in energy efficiency makes us the best advisor in the choice of technologies, equipment and approaches that will allow you to optimize a building’s energy consumption and the comfort of its occupants. Whether it involves a commissioning plan, a LEED certification or simply assisting you in finding and evaluating your options, we can guide you at every step of your project.
A system or equipment reaching the end of its useful lifecycle is a good opportunity to improve your installations’ operational and energy costs. We will leverage our experience to help you select energy-efficient equipment that will best suit your actual and future needs.


Service Name:
Project Management
Ambioner can take over the management of your project, be it to construct a new building or implement energy-efficiency measures. From the beginning to the end of your project, we will ensure that every phase is successfully completed and respects all of your requirements along with respecting sustainable development and energy savings criteria. We will assist you in the following:
- Coordination between the professionals and contractors;
- Supervision of the design phase (plans and specifications);
- Budget and schedule planning management;
- Production of tender documents;
- Supervision of the equipment and material purchase;
- Supervision of the contractors on site;
- Start-up, commissioning and optimization of the operation;
- Post-construction service and management of warranty.



Service Name:
Energy Monitoring
After completing your project, you want to to know if the energy savings forecasted have been achieved. For a proper energy consumption monitoring of your building, it is necessary to consider several determining factors such as the weather conditions, the addition of any new equipment, schedule or occupancy changes. Ambioner’s certified energy managers (CEM) and certified measurement and verification professionals (CMVP) will analyse your energy data (bills, trend logs, etc.) while considering all these factors using the IPMVP for an accurate and energy monitoring process.


Service Name:
Grant Application Processes
Granting bodies invest to encourage durable construction and renovation projects across Canada in a perspective of sustainable development and energy savings. By carrying out preliminary studies and design phases of projects across Canada, we have developed a strong experience taking over grant research and application processes for our customers to obtain the maximum incentives applicable. We are official partners for all the incentive programs with Hydro-Québec, and we know very well the application processes with the main granting bodies.


Service Name:
Project Design - Plans and specifications
Ambioner’s high-end design team's expertise covers all the electromechanical building components : plumbing, heating, ventilation, control, electricity and fire protection. Our experts have achieved a great variety of projects in the commercial, industrial, institutional and residential sectors. Our experts have achieved numerous projects under different approaches, such as design-build, Integrated Design Process,and design with a commissioning team. We can also count on many of our experts that master both Revit design and the BIM approach.
Our attention to detail, as well as the high-quality and innovative nature of our designs unquestionably distinguishes Ambioner from the competition. But what stands out even more is our great care for each customer’s needs, and the full compliance of our design to those needs.

Service Name:
System Operation Optimization
Many customers have benefited from our high-end expertise in the optimization of the electromechanical systems operation parameters. Our projects result in the reduction of energy consumption, the improvement of occupant comfort, and the correction of operational problems.
After visiting your building and studying its operation parameters, equipment in place, and operational problems encountered, our experts will establish improvement hypotheses in order to implement a solution that will suit the installations’ specificity and occupation. They will then ensure a close follow-up to check and improve the results of the project, for optimized savings over a long-term period.


Service Name:
Measurement and Verification Plans
A Measurement and Verification Plan is the ideal tool to confirm and perpetuate the savings achieved by an energy efficiency project. Our Certified Measurement and Verification professionnals (CMVP) and Certified Energy Managers (CEM) are best positionned to accurately quantify savings generated by an energy efficiency project. Mindful of the requirements of this process, they are abilitated to design and implement any type of M&V plan.

Service Name:
ASSET MAINTENANCE PLANNING
Our experts have inspected over 2 million square feet of surface area in education, municipal and health care buildings, in order to identify the electromechanical systems, to analyze their life cycle, and to prioritize the required interventions. Our expertise, along with our interdisciplinary collaborative spirit, makes us a reference of choice for just about any asset maintenance planning project, whether it is only for recommendations or for the elaboration of a complete master plan.

Service Name:
Value Analysis
The objective of value analysis sessions is to increase the value of a project by sharing the optimization opportunities in each discipline involved (architecture, mechanical, electrical, civil and structure engineering, etc.), while making sure all the project’s needs are respected. Value analysis allows for a significant reduction in both implementation and operational costs of a project. Ambioner will support you by leading the value analysis workshops, in order to ensure that the project is optimized and progresses efficiently.

Service Name:
Training Programs and Awareness Raising Campaigns
Ambioner participated in numerous projects to train and raise the awareness of management, operation and maintenance personnel of buildings, to ensure that they maintain the positive impact of the projects implemented. We can offer training on energy management, LEED certification process, and efficient equipment O&M, measurement and verification, as well as the guaranteed energy savings project approach in Quebec. Ambioner also put up a personalized awareness raising campaign that will promote the rational use of energy among the building occupants.
