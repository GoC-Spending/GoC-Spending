Company Profile


The Centre for Imaging Technology Commercialization (CIMTEC) enables and accelerates the commercialization of medical imaging innovations, ensuring Canadas investment in imaging technology translates into economic growth and job creation.

CIMTEC helps researchers, and companies translate their medical imaging innovations into commercial products for clinical use.

We provide technology development in the broad areas of 3D visualization, image analysis and mechatronics design, as well as business development services to assess the potential of bringing an innovation to the marketplace, including opportunity evaluations, intellectual property and regulatory assessments and customer research. CIMTEC's clinical testing services provide customized support for the design and implementation of a range of clinical studies.

Start the conversation with CIMTEC. Visit www.cimtec-canada.ca

Key / Major clients:

Partners: Networks of Centres of Excellence; Ontario Institute for Cancer Research; Western University; Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre; WORLDiscoveries; MaRS Innovation

Success stories:

Densitas, Inc. - has used CIMTEC's business consulting services to understand design requirements, the commercialization pathway and how to access funding. CIMTEC has helped Densitas design and validate a robust algorithm to measure breast density and move it toward the first commercial prototype.

DQE Instruments - developed a novel device for quickly evaluating the performance of new x-ray imaging systems and their ability to produce high-quality images with very low levels of radiation. CIMTEC mechanical engineering team helped DQE solve a problematic vibration in the mechanical system in order to meet the deadline on a crucial overseas contract.

Perfint Healthcare - is working with CIMTEC and Canadian company Claron to develop hardware and software for a 3D ultrasound-guided focal liver tumour ablation system. The product has the potential to profoundly impact liver cancer treatment in developing countries such as India, North Asia and part of Europe where access to CT and MRI scanners is extremely limited.

KalGene Pharmaceuticals - is developing precision medicine tools to provide more effective cancer therapeutics that will also significantly alleviate patient suffering during treatment and improve overall quality of life. KalGene is benefitting from CIMTECs expert knowledge of the medical imaging landscape in Canada, its extensive technology development skills and its connections to clinician-scientists and academic institutions.

CIMTEC is providing project management and coordination across multiple sites, as well as developing an automated multiplexed immunohistochemistry (IHC) test to identify aggressive cancers, and creating an algorithm using digital pathology images that will drive the analysis for KalGenes new testing service.

Testimonial:

CIMTECs team was able to understand where we are coming from, what we require and how they could apply their significant knowledge and expertise toward our success.
Dr. T. Nathan Yoganathan, President & Chief Scientific Officer, KalGene Pharmaceuticals
