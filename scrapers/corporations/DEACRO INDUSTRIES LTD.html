Company Profile


Deacro is a leader in the development, manufacture, and service
of quality high performance slitting and rewinding machines. We
strive to provide the converting industry with innovative design
solutions to handle today's complex web constructions.

Deacro's new manufacturing facility provides a working
environment geared to quality control and optimized service. Our
computer-integrated manufacturing "CIM" facility provides a
"state-of-the-art" manufacturing environment, from design, to
parts production, to final assembly.

Our engineering staff's primary goal is to design ergonomic
machines that operators find easy to use, and maintenance
personnel to find easy to maintain. Our dedication to customer
service goes beyond successful installation and commissioning at
the customer's plant, to follow-up after sales training an
support by qualified technicians.

We believe that our prosperity is directly related to your
success. That's why, in all aspects of our operation, every
effort is made to achieve nothing less than your total
satisfaction.

For over 15 years Deacro Industries has been a leader in the
design and manufacture of quality high- performance slitting and
rewinding machines. We strive to provide the converting industry
with innovative design concepts to handle today's complex
laminates and thin gauge webs.

Worldwide demand for Deacro's industry leading slitters,
rewinders and roll handling systems has lead the company to
rapid expansion. Our brand new state-of-the-art 50,000 square
foot facility is located approximately 15 minutes from the
Toronto airport.

Deacro's recent move to its new manufacturing facility provides
a working environment geared to quality control and optimized
service. Our computer- integrate-manufacturing "CIM" facility
provides a state-of-the-art manufacturing environment, from
design, to parts production, to final assembly. Deacro's
complete control throughout the manufacturing process offers the
converting industry design solutions in an unprecedented "Time
to Market".

Product Name:
DA210A - ROLL HANDLING SYSTEM
The DA210A roll handling system is designed to be used in
conjunction with a conveyor system. When used with a roll
stripping system the roll unloading process becomes fully
automatic.

The sequence begins by placing the rolls onto the DA210's
indexable mandrels (automatically or manually). The unit then
sequentially lifts to its full height, indexes the rolls to 90
degrees and lowers the rolls to the conveyor. The rolls are
conveyed off of both mandrels independently with a conveyor
interlocked sequence.

Product Name:
DA220 - ROLL HANDLING & PALLETIZING SYSTEM
Never before have converters had the flexibility in one system
to pneumatically remove rolls from the slitter, then restart the
slitter while product rolls are on the receiving station, and
manipulate rolls from the receiving station to the required
position for packaging or palletizing, The system is the DA220.
The perfect complement to maximizing productivity for virtually
any slitting operation.

The system is capable of:
1. Stripping rewind shafts (with optional roll strippers)
2. Receiving finished rolls from the slitter onto the receiving
station.
3. Manipulating rolls as required (possible for weighing,
inspection or packaging)
4. Palletizing rolls as required for shipping (rolls can be
placed on their diameters or upended.)

The complete system maximizes slitting efficiency, and minimizes
operator fatigue, freeing up resources. The DA220's solid,
sturdy design is the reliable and safe solution to avoiding
roll-handling stress.

This package can be retrofitted to existing Deacro slitters or
your current in-house slitters.

Product Name:
MODEL DA400 - REVERSIBLE SALVAGE REWINDER
Deacro's model DA400 offers the printed web converter an
affordable and reliable salvage rewinder.

This machine is particularly well suited to printed web
converters in that the print direction can be maintained without
removing the roll. Conventional single direction rewinders often
require running the roll through twice in order to orient the
print direction. The DA400's reversible feature can reduce
rewinding time from 50 - 90%.

A position regulated dancer compensates for roll inertia
providing light web tension for extensible films. A user
selectable dance bypass mode can also be used for heavier web
constructions.

Web guiding is provided by a servo-actuated pivot steering guide.
Infrared or ultrasonic edge sensors are provide depending on
material type. The unit locks out on reverse since web steering
is unidirectional. A splice table is built into the web guide to
provide an ergonomic centralized location for web splicing.

STANDARD FEATURES
- reversible winding
- microprocessor controlled - data base management
- all electric DC drive system - auto tension control
- web dancer control - software configurable
- servo actuated web guide
- web splice table - pneumatic web clamping
- lay-on rider rollers - uniform roll density

OPTIONAL FEATURES
- hydraulic roll handling
- razor/shear slitting
- trim removal

ROLL HANDLING DEVICE
- in applications where large rolls must be rewound a roll
lifting arm can be provided, which is centrally located in the
machine and can be swung around to unload and unload a roll in
both the unwind and rewind position
- operator controls consist of a single toggle switch located to
its maximum lift capacity of 350 lbs.
- the arm conveniently parks into the baser of the machine when
not in use.

Product Name:
DA402 - REVERSIBLE REWINDER SERIES
Deacro's model DA402 offers the printed web converter an
affordable and reliable salvage rewinder.
This machine is particularly well suited to printed web
converters in that the print direction can be maintained without
removing the roll. Conventional single direction rewinders often
require running the roll through twice in order to orientate the
print direction. The DA402's reversible feature can reduce
rewinding time fro 50-90%.

A position regulated dancer compensates for roll inertia
providing light web tension for extensible films.

Web guiding is provided by a servo-actuated high speed side-lay
system. Infrared or ultrasonic edge sensors are provided
depending on material type. The web guide correct roll profile
in the left to right direction and locks out tin reverse.
Pneumatic clamping splice table is provided in an ergonomic
centralized location for web inspection and splicing.

The DA402 is equipped with Deacro's microprocessor-controller
interfaced with a touch screen.

The microprocessor is capable of storing and recalling set-ups
for repeat critical tension appplications. The touch screen
displays and edits all pertinent running parameters and provides
full control diagnostics.

STANDARD FEATURES
- reversible winding
- microprocessor controlled - data base management
- all electric AC Flux-Vector drive system
- servo actuated web guide
- web dancer control - software configurable
- web splice table - pneumatic web clamping
- lay-on rider rollers - uniform roll density

ROLL HANDLING DEVICE
- in applications were large rolls mus be rewound, a roll lifting
arm can be provided which is centrally located in the machine and
can be swung around and rewind position.
- operator controls consist of a single toggle switch located on
the lifting arm.
- the unit slides easily on a linear bearing system even when
loaded to its maximum lift capacity of 350 lbs.
- the arm conveniently parks into the base of the machine when
not in use.

VISUAL INSPECTION
- stroboscope with adjustable flash intensity is synchronized to
web speed by sensors reading "eye-spot" or matrix gap.
- offers excellent coverage of the inspection/splice area (20"
lamp width).
- high flash intensity provides good visibility for inspection
and long repeat lengths

Product Name:
MODEL DA503 - ADHESIVE TAPE SLITTER
Dacro's model DA503 is designed for the medical and specialty
tape converter. Its narrow web design provides precision tension
and roll profile control.

Product Name:
MODELS DA650 & DA680 - CANTILEVERED CENTER WINDERS
Deacro's DA650 & DA680 are the most popular in its center winder
line. These machines are controlled by a microprocessor capable
of storing and recalling machine set-ups by file name.
Repeatability of running parameters provides precise process
control.

Its ergonomic design allows operator comfort an conveniences,
common to all Dacro machines. Its cantilevered rewind mandrels,
combined with camlock tooling, reduce operator fatigue and set-up
time.

With options of razor, shear, or score slitting, and optional
rewind tooling tension ranges, these models will handle today's
extensible films. Combining these unique capabilities and
ergonomics results in increased productivity and quality.

DIFFERENTIAL REWIND/LAY-ON ROLLERS
- differential rewind system provides uniform roll density
regardless of caliper variation
- programable overspeed control provides constant "slip" speed
via ultrasonic roll measuring sensors
- individual pneumatic lay-on rollers provide constant contact
with the building roll to prevent trapped air between film
layers, eliminating side shift and weaving.

CAMLOCK TOOLING
- "camlocks" provide unmatched set-up and roll removal speeds
- twist in one direction to lock the core and in the opposite
direction to unlock the finished roll
- needle roller design allows easy unlocking even with large
diameter rolls
- multiple camlock core engagement can be used to multiply web
tension

CANTILEVERED SYSTEM
- cantilevered rewind mandrels are held by pneumatic positioning
and locking arms
- offers fast cor set-up and roll removal without the need for
overhead hoists
- designed for easy operation by one person with quick
change-over
- safety interlocks prevent the machine from running until arms
are in place.

Product Name:
MODEL DA900 - LIFT-OUT MANDREL SIMPLEX CENTER WINDERS
The DA900 is primarily a simplex "Locked core" slitter/rewinder.
Its large 50" diameter rewind capacity makes it ideal for
business form, paper, and laminate slitting and rewinding
applications.

The DA900 incorporates ether a single or dual "varibow" bow
roller arrangement to control web separation on the rewind. The
bow roller is mounted on the pneumatically counter-balanced
lay-on roller system to provide a constant wrap angle. The
knives are dovetail mounted and pneumatic in operation, designed
for quick set-up and removal.
The rewind air shaft is hydraulically held in the rewind arms and
can be lowered to floor level upon completion.

STANDARD FEATURES
REWIND ROLL OFF-LOADING
- rewind shaft is hydraulically clamped and positioned into
rewinding position
- safety interlocks prevent shaft from being released until
machine is stopped
- rewind spline driver pneumatically engages and withdraws from
rewind shaft
- rewound roll can be lowered to floor or off-loading trolley

"VARIBOW" BOW ROLLER
- variable bow roller separates slit webs to prevent interweaving
- full web width lay-on roller eliminates trapped air and ensures
proper roll density

UNWIND SYSTEMS
- standard unwind stand for DA900 is the separate unwind stand
model DA125
- optional shaftless unwind stand as shown above can be provided.
Shaftless unwind options include position regulated dancer and
splice table
- optional equipment for the shaft separate stand (DA125) include
a floor pickup, position-regulated dancer, and splice table.
A four-axis servo controller feeds the web, applies the tab stoc,
indexes the turrets, and transfers the strands onto the new
cores. The carriage system loads the cores and removes the
finished rolls from the rewind mandrels. The core feeding system
prefeeds, orientates, and loades the cores to the infeed system.

Product Name:
MODELS DA880 & DA950 SLITTER/REWINDERS
Deacro's DA880 and DA950 lift-out mandrel duplex
slitter/rewinders extend both the maximum rewind roll diameter
and web width that are available in the cantilevered models. The
lift-out mandrel style of centre winder is more practical when
large diameters and wide web are required.

These center winders are controlled by the same microprocessor
control system used in the cantilevered models. The capability
of storing and recalling machine set-ups by file name provides
precise process control.

The DA880 and DA950 use the proven system of alternating cores
and drive spacers to provide a differential rewind and, when used
in conjunction with four rewind mandrels, set-ups are simple and
quick.

A centre shaft support gives wider web width machines rewind
mandrel support. The floating inner race of the bearing does not
inhibit axial differential loading.

STANDARD FEATURES
SHAFT SUPPORT/LAY-ON ROLLERS
- shaft support provides rewind mandrel support for large roll
rewinding
- dovetail mounting of support arm accommodated placement on the
lay-on beam to adjust for rewind roll width
- individual pneumatic lay-on rolls provide constant contact with
the building roll to prevent trapped air between film layers,
eliminating side shift and weaving

DIFFERENTIAL REWIND
- alternating core and spacers provide constant contact with the
building roll to prevent trapped air between film layers,
eliminating side shift and weaving

DIFFERENTIAL REWIND
- alternating core and spacers provide a differential rewind
compensating for web caliper variation
- adjustable differential cylinder positions provide three rewind
tension rangers
- "Over-centre" safety clamps secure the rewind mandrel into the
cradle block, and interlocks prevent the machine from running
until clamps are locked into place.

UNWIND SYSTEMS
- standard unwind stand for DA880 and DA950 is the separate
unwind stand model DA125
- optional shaftless unwind stand as shown above can be provided.
Shaftless unwind options include position-regulated dancer and
splice table
- optional equipment for the shafted separate stand (DA125)
include a floor pickup, position regulated dancer, and splice
table.

Product Name:
MODELS DA520 & DA530 - INSPECTION SLITTER REWINDER
Deacro's DA520 & DA530 offer the label converting industry a
series of manually operated and fully automatic inspection,
slitting and rewinding machines.

Its unique ergonomic design concept allows the operator to
inspect and perform all web editiing funcitions from a single
position.

The DA530 is equipped with a two axis servo control system which
precisely positions the web for editing upon fault detection and
provides a smooth web advance/turret index function.

The touch screen provides the operator with a single location
for all machine control and web fualt desplay. Machine operating
parameters can be saved and recalled through the use of the
database.

The DA520 is the simplex Version of this series and can be
equipped with a "web advancing" or "auto-threadinging" option
for core or coreless rewinding.

OPTIONAL CORE FEEDER
- vibrating bowl orientates and discharges cores in an "end to
end" attitudde.
- prefeeder hopper maintains constant level in vibrating bowl.
- linear vibratory drive feeds cores to core magazine.
- automated magazine discharges cores tocore loader shuttle.
- preprogrammed no. of cores are loaded during winding cycle.

VISUAL INSPECTION & LABEL DETECTION
- stroboscope with selectable flash intensity is synchronized to
web speed by matrix or programmed repeat length.
- matrix present, label missing quality control system
- long infeed web span allows deceleration of web upon web fault
- combination web guide/splice table provides an adjustable
splicing station for web fault removal.

UNWIND FEATURES
- Unwind splice station for new master roll splicing.
- Flag and splice detector stops splice at central splice table.
- Electrically actuated roll lifeter enables handling of heavy
rolls.
