Company Profile


Greenland Corporation manufactures and markets the Greenplus
line of environmentally safe, vegetable oil based lubricants.
Made from a renewable resource, Greenplus lubricants are rapidly
biodegradable, are nontoxic to the environment and safe for
operators and mechanics. Greenplus products do not contain
petroleum based feed stocks, additives or carriers. Greenplus
products do not contain heavy metals or synthetics. Greenland
Corporation backs its products with technical support and can
provide equipment assessment for proper product choice and
on-going support.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Greenplus ES Grease
An environmentally safe, high temperature grease with a low
coefficient of friction. Contains no petroleum, heavy metals
or synthetics. Approved for use in registered food
establishments in Canada and the USA. An extreme pressure
grease.


Product Name:
Greenplus Gear Oil ES
An environmentally safe oil used in gear reducing applications,
formulated with rust and oxidation suppressing additives and
superior anit-foaming additives. Does not contain petroluem,
heavy metals or synthetics.


Product Name:
Greenplus Hydraulic Fluid ES
An environmentally safe non-toxic, rapidly biodegradable,
operator safe hydraulic fluid. Made without petroluem, heavy
metal or synthetic additives.

Product Name:
Greenplus Wire Rope Lube ES
An evironmentally safe liquid alternative to petroleum and
synthetic products used to lubricate cables and wire rope used
by draglines, hoists, elevators, etc. Made with vegetable oil
base stocks containing no mineral oil, heavy metals or
synthetics.


Product Name:
Greenplus Chain Saw Bar Oil ES
An environmentally safe lubricant designed to lubricate the chain
and bar of high speed chain saws. Reduces operator hazards
associated with the use of mineral oil, such as skin, eye and
lung irritations. Does not contain petroluem, heavy metals or
synthetics.


Product Name:
Greenplus Form Release Agent ES
An environmentally safe release agent for use in metal, wood,
plastic and fibreglass forms. Does not react with Portland
Cement or its common admixtures. Product does not contain heavy
metals, synthetics or petroleum.


Product Name:
Greenplus Line Shaft Turbine Fluid ES
For pollution free lubrication of vertical turbine pump equipment
with lubricated line shafts in either flanged or threaded and
coupled column construction. Does not contain petroleum, heavy
metals or synthetics.


Product Name:
Greenplus Air-Lube ES
An environmentally safe rock drill oil suitable for use in all
types of pneumatic equipment and tools. Also used to lubricate
pistons and packings on piston pumps. Does not contain
petroleum, heavy metals or synthetics.


Product Name:
Greenplus Saw Guide Oil ES
A high performance saw lubricant for the forestry and saw mill
industry that is rapidly biodegradable, operator safe, non-toxic
and environmentally safe.

Product Name:
Greenplus Dust Control Agent ES
A highly soluble concentrate designed to mix with hard or soft
water and form a dust control spray. Non-toxic, safe for
operators and completely environmentally safe.

Product Name:
Greenland Corporation's high performance, vegetable oil based lubricants.
Greenland Corporation is the manufacturer of Greenplus environmentally safe, vegetable oil based lubricants. Made from renewable resources, Greenplus lubricants are non-toxic to the environment and safe for operators and mechanics. Greenplus products are not just rapidly biodegradable but are environmentally safe. The Company's products do not contain petroleum, heavy metals or synthetics. Our products have high natural viscosity and stability - superior lubricants that can improve the performance levels of machinery, reduce costly downtime and ultimately increase productivity. Greenplus products include: hydraulic fluid, grease, industrial gear oils, chain saw bar lubricant, wire rope lubricant, air tool lubricant, line shaft turbine fluid and form release agent.
