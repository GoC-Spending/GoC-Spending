Company Profile


Ivey International Inc. in an award winning remediation technology company with active projects world-wide. We specialize in the development and application of innovative soil and groundwater remediation technology for treating; petroleum hydrocarbons, chlorinated solvents and heavy metals.

Among our more notable remediation technologies are the Ivey-sol® Surfactant Technology "products & processes" which can rapidly clean-up in-situ and ex-situ contaminated soil, groundwater, and vapours, in a fraction of the time and cost of conventional methods. We also have the new innovative Atomisol® Technology for treating heavy metal contamination in soils.

Ivey International Inc. has been honoured with several national and international awards. These include:
2006 GLOBE Award - Technology Innovation & Application (March 31, 2006);
2006 North American Frost & Sullivan Technology Innovation Award (February 7, 2007); and
2005 Environmental Business Journal Remediation Technology Merit Award (February 28, 2007)

Ivey International Inc. has overcome the challenges of rapid and cost effective site remediation by not only stepping out of the box, but redesigning it. With the dedicated support and innovative minds of our employees, research partners, and clients we are meeting our corporate mission to be recognized as having Todays Environmental Solutions For A Better Tomorrow

Through innovation, we believe 1 + 1 = 3. It is that simple!

Our commitment is to offer the best service through development and application of innovative environmental technology that will allow you to meet and/or exceed your site remediation requirements. We stand out from many other environmental service companies through our practical research and implementation. Simply put, we offer innovative solutions other companies do not. We understand the complex processes involved in areas such as:
In-situ contaminant recovery from groundwater using our SER Technology to improve contaminant mass recovery by >400%;
In-situ and ex-situ bioremediation using our SEB Technology reducing costs and duration by >40-60%;
In-situ and Ex-situ chemical oxidation using our SEO Technology to improve the effectiveness of chemical oxidation; and
Technology to improve oil recovery form oil sands, oil shale, and oil production fields.

We are also working on many innovative products and processes which are not detailed on this website. For more information, please contact our office at 1-800-246-2744 or visit our website at www.iveyinternational.com.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Atomisol
Technology for treatment of heavy metal contamination in soils.

Product Name:
Surfactant Ehanced Remediation (SER)
Surfactant Enhanced Remediation (SER) product and process include both in-situ and/or ex-situ soil and water treatment to rapidly liberate contamination (including F1, F2, F3, F4, PAH, PCB, TCE, PCE, dioxins, pesticides etc.) for recovery (if desired) or treatment (if required) to decontaminate the soils and/or groundwater.

Product Name:
Surfactant Enhanced Bioremediation (SEB)
Surfactant Enhanced Bioremediation (SEB) is a process which utilizes specially formulated surfactant mixtures to desorb higher molecular weight hydrocarbon contamination (including: F2, F3, F4, PAHs etc.) so they are more 'bioavailable' for in-situ or ex-situ microbial degradation.

The SEB technology can improve the bioremediation process by 60% or more.


Product Name:
Surfactant Enhanced Oxidation (SEO)
Surfactant Enhanced Oxidation (SEO) is a technology product and process which utilizes specifically formulated surfactants mixtures to desorb contaminants (including F1, F2, F3, F4, PAH, PCB, TCE, PCE, dioxins, pesticides etc.) so they are more 'chemically available' for oxidative chemical treatment be in-situ and/or ex-situ methods.

Service Name:
Environmental Site Assessments
Ivey International Inc. provides certified environmental
site assessment services. We are members of the Environmental
Assessment Association and the Association of Environmental Site
Assessors of Canada.

We provide Phase I (non-intrusive), Phase II and III
(intrusive) environmental site assessments (ESA).

Phase I ESAs primarily consist of a detailed records review, interviews and a site walk-over. Phase II and III assessments incorporate the information obtained from a Phase I assessment as well as the collection, analysis and interpretation of soil, air and/or groundwater samples.

We also provide risk based assessments using Tier 1
(look-up tables)and Tier 2 (computer modeling) methods. Risk
assessments provide a site specific set of remediation guidelines
often less stringent than generic tables provided by government
departments. These risk assessments are recognized by government agencies and financial institutions.

Technology profile

Ivey-sol removes petroleum hydrocarbon (gasoline, fuel oil,
diesel etc.) and PAH (Polycyclic Aromatic Hydrocarbon)
contaminants from soil, groundwater and bedrock, allowing you to
meet and achieve the highest possible environmental standards in
North America.

Ivey International Inc. is the only company in North America
to offer this new innovative technology, which dramatically
improves the ability to remove these contaminants from the
environment.

Ivey-sol is a phase transfer technology that works at the
molecular level, separating petroleum hydrocarbons and PAHs from
contaminated soils, bedrock and the groundwater table.
Proprietary biodegradable mixtures are used to increase the
solubility of the contaminants, making them easier to liberate,
recover and treat on-site.

Ivey-sol can be used as an addition to existing pump and
treatment facilities to reduce the decommissioning time or to
achieve lower concentrations of dissolved total petroleum
hydrocarbons (TPH) and PAHs. Cleanup time can be reduced by more than 75% and the result will exceed the strictest environmental standards in North America.

Ivey-sol technology incorporates designed phase transfer mixtures
(patent pending) that interact with a specific class or type of
organic (LNAPL) contaminants. The contaminant is liberated from
the soil, bedrock and/or free floating phase and encapsulated,
allowing for their rapid recovery at an on-site pumping well. The
encapsulated hydrocarbons called "micelles", vary in size and
composition and make the contaminants water soluble.

Ivey-sol guarantees an increase in the rate of contaminant
recovery. The clean-up times are shortened and project costs are
dramatically reduced. More of the contaminated material can be
recovered easily. This reduces the need for costly and extensive
soil excavation.

Ivey-sol reduces the inconvenience and liability of our clients.
Decommissioning time is shortened by years.
