Product Name:
Aqua-Guard line of oil spill response equipment and services for spills, chronic leakage and wastewater effluent.
Aqua-Guard Spill Response design and manufacture a range of oil spill response products worldwide for the containment, recovery and treatment of spilt hydrocarbons in water systems.
Applications are as follows: oil processing plants, refineries, production platforms; port authorities, harbours, marinas, spill response contractors; oil transport companies such as tanker owners, tug, barge, and railways; logging mining, and drilling; manufacturing plants, chemical plants, fish plants; government agencies etc.


Product Name:
Offshore oil skimming systems
Aqua-Guard design, manufacture and supply large offshore oil skimming systems specifically designed to mount on board large offshore support vessels. These vessels are typically on standby in the event of an offshore oil spill from E&P operations.

Product Name:
Oil water seperator skimming systems
Aqua-Guard manufacture a range of oil skimming systems to be placed in oil water seperators such as refinery API seperators to recover primary floating oil on the surface of these ponds, pits and other applications.

Service Name:
Recovery oil oil from pits
Aqua-Guard's service operators use state of the art Aqua-Guard RBS TRITON oil skimmers to recover oil from the surface of pits, ponds or in the event of oil spill from lagoons and other locations.

Service Name:
Environmental Audit
Aqua-Guard will audit your existing oil spill response equipment and recommend maintenance type, replacement spare parts or enhancement of existing equipment caches.

FSC Code:
1935-Barges and Lighters, Special Purpose
4330-Centrifugals, Separators, and Pressure and Vacuum Filters
