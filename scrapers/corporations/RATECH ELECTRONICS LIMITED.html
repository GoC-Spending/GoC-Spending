Company Profile


Ratech Electronics Ltd. is a Canadian owned company that has
been in the CCTV (closed circuit television) business for 30
years. We manufacture and service all our products. We deal
with clients on a national & international level. Most of our products are manufactured for sewer & pipeline inspection. Our most recent product called (SnapLock)
is a sewer repair module, directly related to the trenchless
technology and no-dig industries.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Sewer Rehabilitation Module\ SnapLock
Snap-Lock is a localised, no-dig system which renovates the
defective section of a sewer. It is the most economical,
efficient system for the quick repair of damaged areas in a
sewer whose overall good condition make excavation and whole
relining unnecessarily expensive and disruptive options.

Product Name:
Camera, Color,Sewer Pan N' Tilt
Specifically designed for the internal inspection of storm
and/or sanitary sewer lines with a minimum diameter of 6 in.
(150 mm). The camera is capable of providing a close-up view
of the sewer pipe walls and/or services, cracks and joints by
remotely tilting and rotating the camera lens which is
controlled by a remote control unit located in the operator's
studio/control center.

Product Name:
CCTV Sewer Portable Inspection Mini-Mainline System
Developed for the municipality or contractor that needs to
inspect larger mainline pipes, the Ratech mini-mainline
system extends the capability of the MC-2CA Lateral Service
Camera and control unit to be used for mainline inspections
in 8 inch(200mm)andlargerlines.

Product Name:
"The Plumber's Mate" Sewer Inspection System
Small pipe TV Inspection system colour or black & white. Also
includes a built in VCR and optional sonde transmitter/locator.
The two model types are PMV-C and PMV-B. Both models are
supplied with a hard cover case CCU (camera control unit) which
includes a high resolution 9 in. monitor, VHS format video tape
recorder and front mounted System controls.

Product Name:
Full Spectrum Color Pipeline Inspection System Truck Mount
The (Retis Visuvan), a quality, self contained, all weather
television inspection vehicle for sewer and pipe inspection.
The Retis Visuvan combines a low maintance, high performance and
dependable method of pinpointing sewer problems without costly
trial and error methods of excavation. It gives the owner a
high quality, state-of-the-art inspection tool with which he
can check specifications, workmanship, alignment, and grade
information before final acceptance on new or reconditioned
installations and projects.

Product Name:
Rear Vision Truck Camera Systems
Designed to enhance and to be used in addition to the rear view
mirrors on large vehicles and trucks. The Ratech Rear Vision
Camera System is a high quality rear viewing system that helps
drivers see vehicles and obstacles that would not normally be
within the driver's field of view (blind spots). The low cost
system can save thousands of dollars in potenial damage that
would occur when a vehicle is backing up.

Product Name:
Plumber's Helper Jr.
Key Benefits

Low cost entry level system
Self contained inspection unit comes complete ready to work
High sensitivity black and white camera
Perfect for plumber, drain cleaners and municipalities
State-of-the-art miniature camera
Used for pipe lines from 2 - 10"

DETAILS

Model PH Jr.

Model PH Jr. is an Ultra Miniature Black and White inspection
camera measuring just 1.2" in diameter by 1.7" in length.
Capable of inspecting pipes in diameters as small as 1.5"
(straight runs), the camera will also negotiate 90 degree bends
in 3" pipes and services. Lighting is controlled by high density
LED lights which reproduce an excellent black and white picture.

Monitor Control Unit

The PH Jr. monitor control unit has been designed with all
functions rear mounted with the exception of the Camera Light
Head control which is located on the front for easy access and
operation. Rear mounted functions include camera input, camera
test terminal, video I/O for recording or playback and fuses for
the camera, lights and AC line. The black and white 1000 line
resolution monitor includes a sun shield which doubles as a
screen protector when not in use. An optional carrying case is
also availalble.

Product Name:
Plumber's Elite Series of Pipe Inspection Cameras
Product Name:
Ultra Micro 3/4" Pipe Inspection System
Product Name:
Mini-Peek and Fast Peek All-In-One Cameras
Product Name:
RST Mainline Pipe Inspection Cameras
Based in California, RST, is a leader in mainline cameras for the pipe/sewer inspection industries

Product Name:
Electric Eel Drain Cleaning Equipment
Ratech is the Canadian distributor for Electric Eel products.

Service Name:
Repair Services For Pipe Cameras
Service and repair of pipe inspection cameras at our facility. Also available authorized repair facilties set up throughout the USA.
