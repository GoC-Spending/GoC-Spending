Company Profile


Seneca is an engineering firm specializing in industrial processes. We mainly serve the heavy industries in the field of mines and metallurgy, chemical and petrochemical. We also have a broad expertise in the energy and in the food industries. An important percentage of our projects is executed in existing plants, which has enabled us to hone a particular expertise in this area. We also are involved in the development and realization of large scale projects over the world.

We frequently contribute to initiatives involving the integration of new technologies into existing plants, aimed chiefly at reducing or controlling environmental emissions, increasing production capacity and reducing operating costs.

These projects involve providing some or all of the following services to our clients: research and comparative analysis of available technologies (theoretical analyses, on-site tests and piloting); implementation cost and operating cost estimates; process design and applications engineering (process, mechanical, electrical, instrumentation, civil and structural); and comprehensive project management, from the initial planning stages to facility commissioning.

Our client base is comprised primarily of large industrials for which we conduct analyses and produce the documentation required for their project management systems, such as Design for Six Sigma (DFSS), Stage-Gate Process and Front-End Loading.

Several of our staff members are qualified to conduct risk assessments and cost-reduction studies, and we are often called on to contribute to processes involving significant health and safety or environmental risks (extremely hazardous materials, strong acids and bases, highly toxic or highly unstable products).

Over the last 15 years we did realized mandates for projects in: North America, Central America, South America, Africa, Asia, Europe and Australia. We count on a team of highly skills professionals fully engaged in the success of our clients projects.

Seneca provides a full range of services to help clients pursue investment projects. The Seneca team works closely with clients to achieve their objectives  providing senior engineering with strategic solutions. We identify business and investment opportunities that allow our clients get results  quickly and effectively.
