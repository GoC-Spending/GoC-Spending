Company Profile


Aquarian Industries International has been transacting business on a global basis since 1968. Head office is in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. Aquarian maintains a well established network of affiliate offices throughout North America and in
most major trading country around the world. Aquarian Industries is a certified member of the Canadian Institute of Mar-
keting (International Institutes - worldwide) and has been the recipient of numerous business, community and hilanthropic
awards. It has owned nine other successful companies, and has had several of its world-class achievements widely published in a vast range of international journals & papers such as the Financial Post & Wall Street Journal and extensively in other world media. Aquarian has accomplished sales records, procurement strategies and many firsts in trade, technologies, environmental business and sustainable development on a global basis.

The company has four major divisions:
l. Environmental products and technologies
2. Commodity Trading and Sourcing
3. Sustainable development products, rehabilitation & remediation
4. 100% Environmental Fertilizer

Aquarian provides its certified strategic assistance to companies wishing to establish themselves internationally in the world's largest industry which is the environment. The importance of the products and technologies that Aquarian has developed is paramount to the state of the planet today and these environmental solutions are shipped worldwide. The most critical environmental products which Aquarian exports to world markets are as follows:

EarthZyme is an unparalleled enzyme soil treatment that creates infinitely better roads as well as a multitude of solutions to an endless list of other infrastructure problems, environmentally and economically.

Dust Stop has been proven as a 100% Environmentally Friendly dust suppressant to replace the toxic chemicals now used and that the earth's roads, lands, waters, wildlife, human health, agricultural and natural resources are now being devastatingly subjected to.

UltraZyme is the utmost in sustainable solutions possessing the ability to reduce and remove all waste and hydrocarbons from a number of industrial sources as well as water while at the same time being 100% environmentally friendly and providing instant odor control. UltraZyme is both an industrial as well as health solution.

GrowthRich Organics is a one-of safe and effective environmental product to fertilize all growing plants and it increases crop yields and ensures that all agricultural products are truly organic and free of all devastating toxins. Demand for this product as well as the other Aquarian products and technologies is immense and growing.
