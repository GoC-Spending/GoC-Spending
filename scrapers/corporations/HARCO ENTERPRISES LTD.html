Company Profile


Harco Enterprises is a diverse manufacturing and distribution company comprised of various divisions:

1: Plastic moulding division - manufacturing plastic items mainly for the toy and advertising industries such as frisbee's, citrus peelers, swizzle sticks (Beverage Stir Sticks and Picks), spoons, tokens, tops, small toys, puzzles and more.

2: Machine shop division - a fully equipped machine shop utilizing CAD/CAM and conventional machining methods. Moulds and tooling are produced in house for our plastics division. Specializing in stainless steel machining, welding and polishing for the food industry as well as general machining in all materials.

3: Process & Supply division - a supply house to the food, beverage, dairy, and pharmaceutical industry. We are a stocking distributor of sanitary pumps, valves, fittings, hose, lubricants, gaskets, brushes, thermometers, plate heat exchangers, separation and pasturization equipment, and other items to numerous to mention. Some brand names we distribute are: ALFA LAVAL, G& H PRODUCTS, TRI CLOVER, APV GAULIN, SPARTA BRUSH, ANDERSON INSTRUMENTS, STRAHMAN VALVES, WAUKESHA PUMPS, CHERRY BURRELL, HAYNES MANUFACTURING, MORTON LUBRICANTS, GATES, KUSSEL, DAMROW, 3M, PETROL GEL LUBRICANT, FRISTAM, BURKERT, AMPCO PUMPS.
