Company Profile


Consulting and software (S/W) development for land and ecosystem
management. GIS linked activity tracking, planning and
monitoring for forested lands are our main S/W products. These
products and our expertise have proven especially useful and
beneficial in complex, environmentally sensitive situations.
Our planning software has been used on over 200 million ha.,
worldwide, in the last 18 years.

Product Name:
Harvest Runner
Harvest Runner is a Microsoft mobile based product that allows users to view block information and update harvest obligations and block observations while in the field. The data collection program integrates with Phoenix Professional the D.R. systems forest activity tracking system.

Product Name:
Road Runner
Road Runner is a Microsoft mobile based data collection software product that allows users to manage their PhoenixPRO Road information in the field. The software is used for maintaining roads - inspections for roads, culverts, bridges and other structures. Tracks maintenance activities and contains risk assessments. Voice recording of notes capability.

Product Name:
Sylva Engineer
Sylva Engineer is a Microsoft Mobile based handheld system for recording traverses, site plans, rights-of-way, stem mapping, deflection line - slope profiles, and road surveys. It is a Windows CE program that is compatible with both Pocket PC and CE.NET and runs on handhelds having portrait or landscape display orientations, as well as handhelds with or without keyboards. It is integrated with RoadEng and Logger PC.

Product Name:
Sylva Scale
A Microsoft Windows CE/Mobile handheld data collection program for log scaling. Scribner and BC Metric scales are supported, other scale methods/rules customization available.

Product Name:
RTS - Referral Tracking System
RTS was designed and developed for First Nations by First Nations to track, plan activities
and respond to land referrals. It is a system to support and organize existing processes.
Referrals can now be streamlined and automated to provide a solution to support the
need for meaningful and timely consultation.
