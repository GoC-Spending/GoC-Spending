Company Profile


Canada City Lighting custom designs high powered energy efficient LED Street lights, Parking lot lamps, panel lighting that replaces fluorescent tube lights, moon lights that replaces incandescent bulbs etc... on a Municipal, Industrial and Commercial scale. Our research and development team develops innovated lighting solutions for all applications to meet our consumers demand, our current developments have received national and international recognition for the highest energy efficiency and highest quality light color rendering (CRI).

Canada City Lighting entered our high powered LED street lights, along with 10 top international manufacturers, who also entered their most prized street lights into a 90 day evaluation at the "International Demo Street Light Competition". Canada City Lighting's street lights were "Awarded First Place" for highest energy efficiency and highest quality light color rendering (CRI), our award winning lamps proved our state-of-the-art street lamps save municipalities 50% to 70% in energy savings.

Canada City Lighting has developed LED lamps for high bay applications that uses 300 watts of power and weighs under 15kg to replace the 1000 watt Halogen lamp.

Canada City Lighting developed street lights to replace the 100w, 150w, 200w, 250w, 400w, 600w, 1000w, 1500w, 2000w HID street lamps.

Canada City Lighting Corporation takes great pride being Canada's leader of energy efficient LED Lighting. Our company's main focus is to change-out old outdated mercury lighting technologies, known to pollute our homes, offices and environment, to our new non pollutant, energy efficient LED lighting solutions.

We are doing our part to eliminate harmful pollutants such as, mercury, UV rays, green house gas emissions and other carbon emissions that contribute to Climate Change. Our street lamps reduce carbon emissions by one ton per lamp per year.

Canada City Lighting uses Cree chips in all our lamp designs, our tightly sealed housing eliminates any chances of exterior elements interfering with internal modules, no moving parts, no glass envelopes (little chance of breakage) to give our fixtures a long lifespan of up to 17 years.

Canada City Lighting's LED lamps lifespan is rated for 100,000 hours, our lamps 70% efficiency point is between 50,000 to 70,000 hours based on 10 hours of usage per day.

Our Research and Development team continuously tests and evaluates our designs, continuously upgrading and perfecting our light designs to be competitive in the market place.

Canada City Lighting has competitive prices on all LED lighting products, we take great pride in saving our clients money, we invite you to contact us before purchasing LED lights to compare prices and quality, we guarantee you we will provide the highest quality at the best price in the markets.
