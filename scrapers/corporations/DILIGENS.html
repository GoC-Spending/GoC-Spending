Company Profile


Diligens was established in 2001 to assist organizations in designing and implementing IT solutions which included a specialized practice of application consulting on power quality and power management solutions. We provide system design, technical expertise, implementation, training and support services in 2 domain areas:

Enterprise-wide power quality management solutions for the IT infrastructure industry, and other applications -- wherever dependable power is essential to daily operations.
IT consulting services tailored to your business needs. We have the depth and the breadth of skills to address even the most complex IT problem. We have a proven methodology for delivering successful business and technology solutions for our customers.
Diligens Inc. is unique in its ability to bridge the gap between IT and the Facilities support infrastructure. As an established information technology solutions provider we are able to apply our expertise in network, network monitoring and systems integration to the power management industry. It is through this collective expertise that the benefits pay their biggest dividends for the client.

Today Diligens has grown to be a trusted advisor and provides data centre infrastructure solutions and services for organizations of all sizes.

Whatever your IT or data-centre related needs, Diligens' reputation of providing innovative solutions delivered with integrity will ensure a successful outcome for your project.

Municipal Web Site Software. Web based e-Government solutions for the municipal marketplace. www.qscend.ca

Product Name:
Power Management Solutions
Diligens Inc. is unique in its ability to provide customers a
complete offering of power management products and custom
solutions. While we are well established as ?Information
technology? network and software solutions provider, our name is
new to many in the power management and service provider
community. And yet it is here where the benefits of our expertise
in software solutions, system integration and manufacturing pay
their biggest dividends.

We offer a complete line of power management solutions for power
applications including, transient surge suppression (TVSS),
harmonic mitigation transformers, medical grade power
conditioners, UL 924 emergency lighting systems, power
distribution units, diesel generators, uninterruptible power
supplies (UPS) commercial, and industrial grade. Our Power
Management Group has over 20 years experience in quality power
management solutions.

At the core of our philosophy is a willingness to invest the
necessary resources to develop a custom solution that meets your
specific requirements. We will source the best solution for your
application and negotiate the best price on your behalf. Our team
is ready to address a wide range of AC or DC power problems: be
it, transient suppression (lightning protection), electrical line
noise, brownout conditions, harmonic filtering, redundant power
configurations, power distribution, and remote power management
or network power management. No application is too difficult for
us to handle.

Services
Our network power management consultants are available to customize:

* Network shutdown processes (individual workstations first,
hubs, e-mail and database servers last)
* Shutdown parameters (e.g. Oracle database shutdown scripts
* Configuration of enterprise-wide notification of critical
power events
* Network warnings
* Advanced notification features (e-mail, paging, execute
command)
* Custom queries and reports of digital and analog channel
data points
* Scheduled shutdown / reboot of the UPS and its load for
energy conservation
* Simultaneous network-wide testing of all UPS
* Power management network diagrams
* Network management applications (e.g. HP OpenView or other
SNMP based application) for monitoring and control of network
devices such as UPS.
