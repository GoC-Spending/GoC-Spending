Company Profile


Riddhima Inc is a Import & Export company with warehouse, recognized for its best selling product designs and distribution of home furnishings, Import & export of edible oil and industrial raw material for food industries. The company is divided into three main categories: Edible Oil, Industrial Raw Material and Home furnishing. We are particularly proud of the engineering and innovative design that have built our products' reputation. We are registered supplier to leading retailers in USA, Canada, Mexico Brazil and India and known for benchmark in home products with a contemporary design combining quality and simplicity.
Riddhima Inc. corporate headquarters and showroom, a 55000 square foot state-of-the-art facility, is located in Brampton (Greater Toronto). We have distribution and fulfillment distribution points: Toronto, Ontario; Los Angeles, California; and Ningbo, China.
