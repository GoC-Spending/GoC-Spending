Company Profile


Aqua is an international trading company aiming to provide high quality, reliable and effective natural products to global markets.

We specialize in unique natural Mediterranean, Anatolian & North American produces, organic cosmetics and oils. Aqua International Trade also listens to businesses with their needs and provides solutions through global sourcing of products and markets.

Aqua is the international sales office for SARU and OQON Organic Cosmetics. As well as our unique organic hair, skin and spa products, we provide custom product manufacturing and spa menu design services for our clients.

Product Name:
Monoi de Tahiti - Tiare body oil
The traditional beauty oil of Polynesia:
Monoi de Tahiti is an oily maceration of Tiare flowers obtained by "enfleurage" in coprah oil made in Tahiti. The "Tiare Tahiti", symbol of French Polynesia is cultivated in the remote areas of islands and has to be used at least the day after its gathering. Monoi de Tahiti, as a natural beauty care, allows to enjoy all Tiare virtues.
Properties: Progressive & Lasting moisturizing, Firming & smoothing, Tanning accelerator.


Product Name:
Monoi de Tahiti - Saffron body oil
The oceanian saffron particularly rich in derivates: curcuminoids, molecules with antioxidant properties and carotenoids, photo-protective molecules precursors of vitamin A. Titrated in carotenoids, these photo-protective extract and tanning stimulator is the guarantee for dark and harmonious tanning.
Propertes: Anti-oxidant, Free radical scavenger, Photo-protecting, moisturizing (progressive and lasting), Tanning accelerator, Firming & smooting effect on the skin, Protective effect against the external agressions, natural coloring.

Product Name:
Monoi de Tahiti - Frangipani body oil
"At 12th century, when an Italian man called Frangipani created a very appreciated fragrance. Four centuries later, European travellers discovered the Plumeria tree, which has a similar fragrance. Frangipani is one of the most beautiful tropical tree. Moreover, it is a perfuming and medicinal plant. It has the reputation of being immortal as it provides flowers and leaves even though it is torn.
Properties: Moisturizing (progressive and long-lasting), Free radical scavenger, Firming, stimulating, tonic, soothing, protective, aphrodisiac)

Product Name:
Monoi de Tahiti - Umuhei body oil
Natural Umuhei is a beauty oil coming from Marquesas tradition. Each morning, women make necklace with aromatic flowers, fruits and spices and men wear it all day long. In the evening, they make this necklace macerate in coconut oil in order to prepare an ointment containing aphrodisiac virtues for massage. Monoi Umuhei includes: Tiare, Ylang-Ylang, Blue Ageratum, Miri, Rea Tahiti, Pineapple and Sandalwood.
Properties : Moisturizing (progressive and long lasting), free radical scavenger, Firming, Stimulating, tonifying, soothing, protective, aphrodisiac.

Product Name:
Monoi de Tahiti - Tamanu body oil
Used by indigenous tribes in the Pacific area to shape dog outs or ritual objects, Ati sacred tree still plays a key role in local pharmacopoeia: its fruits, leaves and roots have exceptional curative properties. Scientific studies have highlighted for the time its healing , anti-bacterial, anti-parasitic and anti-inflammatory benefits.
Properties: Soothing and protective (sunburns, inflammations, erythemas...), Regenerating on the epidermis cells (burns, cracks...), Anti-bacterial and anti-septic, Anti-acneic, moisturizing, nourishing and repairing, increase the microcirculation - Heavy Legs.

Product Name:
Monoi de Tahiti - Roucou body oil
Body massage oil. Can be used for face and hair.
Roucou, the Scarler Seeds.
The Roucou Tree is native to South America. The Indians used it daily for protection against sun and insects, but also and primarily to color the skin, which is also the origin of the name <Red Skin>. Recent scientific studies reveal several interesting properties for the medical and cosmetic fields: sunscreen, antioxidant and photo protective, which makes these sunny seeds a perfect ingredient for sun care.
Ingredients properties: Moisturizing (progressive and lasting), Protecting against external aggressions, Firming & Smoothing, Photo-Protecting (Antiradical and antioxidant)< Tanning accelerator, Natural colouring.

Product Name:
Monoi de Tahiti - Argan body oil
Argan essential oil has many benefits. It could cure and provide protection on to the skin, contains anti-aging properties, can boost the immunity mechanism, is fantastic for skin care, and ideal for massage therapy. Due to Argan essential oil's anti-inflammatory and also calming properties on the skin, it can alleviate painful joints and is also soothing to painful muscle tissues. Furthermore, Argan essential oil is famous for providing comfort of varied arthritic and even rheumatic disorders.
Properties: Antioxydant, anti aging, nourish & moisturize, rejuvenate tired skin, reduce acne.
