Company Profile


Avenza is the producer of specialized geospatial software add-ons or plugins for Adobe Creative Suite that bridge the gap between Geographic Information Systems (GIS), CAD and graphics design to enable high quality map production and spatial image processing within the Adobe environment.
Markets include Geographic Information System (GIS)/desktop mapping; automobile navigation; electronic publishing; GPS.
The Company sells worldwide directly and through a growing list of distributors and value-added resellers.

The flagship product line consists of MAPublisher, a suite of GIS and cartographic design modules for use with Adobe Illustrator and Geographic Imager, a suite of geospatial tools and frameworks for Adobe Photoshop. Avenza's products are currently in
use worldwide by such major organizations as the United States Geological Survey, National Geographic, CIA, FBI, United Nations and UK Ordnance Survey.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
MAPublisher®
MAPublisher® is a suite of over 35 GIS and cartographic tools for Adobe® Illustrator® that provides GIS and cartographic
functionality to the high-end graphics environment. MAPublisher
imports raw digital data from ARC/INFO generate; ArcView Shape;
USGS DLG and SDTS; MapInfo mid/mif; and DXF and geo-referenced
JPEG and TIFF files with all attributes intact and editable. All
attribute management tools are included.
Import is directly into the high-end graphics environment of
Illustrator for easy, professional cartographic task
work. MAPublisher is the choice of the USGS and many other fine
sectors of government, academia and industry.


Product Name:
MAPublisher® LabelPro
MAPublisher® LabelPro offers advanced labelling capabilities beyond those available in the standard MAPublisher Feature Text Label and the MAP Tagger Tool, including a sophisticated and user-friendly rule-based and collision-free placement engine.
he MAP LabelPro engine contains sophisticated algorithms that solve many of the most common map labelling problems such as complex conflict resolution across multiple layers, the ability to specify data as obstacles and the ability to create complex labelling conventions using user defined rules.

MAPublisher LabelPro uses map attributes, rules and styles for labelling which provides a great level of sophistication and control. Map layers may be assigned an order of priority for labelling and set as label obstacles. Labels can be placed on any defined text layers, unplaceable layers can be ignored or placed. Placement rules and styles can be saved to a file and imported or shared over a network.

Product Name:
Geographic Imager®
Geographic Imager® transforms Adobe® Photoshop® into a powerful spatial imagery editing tool. Use native features such as transparencies, filters, image adjustments like brightness, contrast, and curve controls while maintaining spatial referencing.
Geographic Imager enhances the Adobe Photoshop imaging environment to make working with spatial imagery quick and efficient. It adds tools to import, edit, manipulate and export of geospatial images such as aerial and satellite imagery. Enjoy the use of native Adobe Photoshop functions such as transparencies, filters, pixel editing, and image adjustments like brightness, contrast, and curve controls while maintaining spatial referencing. Open and recognize GeoTIFF files and other industry standard raster formats, mosaic and tile images, change projections, transform and georeference images based on ground control points. Geographic Imager is a great companion to MAPublisher for Adobe Illustratorprepare images in Geographic Imager and use MAPublisher to directly import them.
