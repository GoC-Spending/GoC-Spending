Company Profile


Burwash Brand Horse Gear was established in 1995 as a
manufacturer and wholesaler of premium equine products.

Within Canada we supply over 200 stores and are currently
exporting into the United States.
We offer a high quality premium webbing and rope products at highly
competitive pricing with Rigid Quality Control Standards in each
department.

The owners, Mark and Shannon Peters, personally oversee production to and designs to ensure the best products on the market.

Dear Customers


Burwash Brand Horse Gear currently features six sizes of flat webbing and rope halters, breast collars, lunge lines, lead shanks, head stalls
and martingales in a variety of colors and designs. We have a full line of Professional Approved training gear, including halters, leads, training sticks, training flags, lariat ranch ropes with accessories such as stainless steel hondas and plastic speedy burners. If you want your horse gear to be customized, we can embroider to your specifications.

You will soon see that we have produced the finest horse products
at very competitive prices. We stand behind the quality of the product and I promise you the quality and service that you and your customer deserve.

We are constantly striving to improve products, and create new ones that offer an advantage to the end user.

Sincerely
Mark Peters

Product Name:
Equine Halters

small horse, average horse, large horse to heavy horse. All are
made form premium webbing, with triple ply webbing where strength
is needed, box stitching at the hardware. There is also an extra
strip of nylon in between the webbing to reinforce the holes ans
grommets and heavy duty hardware.
