Company Profile


Innovation Networks proud to be the winner of the 2008 Richmond Chamber of Commerce Business Excellence Award, 2001-2008 Consumers Choice Award, 2008 Richmond News Readers Choice Award, and the 2008 nominee of Volunteer Richmonds Shining Star Award. We are committed to customer service and it shows!
Innovation Networks is a leading provider of Managed Services, Outsourcing IT, Customer care, Technical support, Helpdesk, Internet services development and supply. Since our inception in 1996, our first priority is to provide our customers with complete solutions with the best resources and a guarantee that what we do works. Our knowledgeable and responsive support team is equipped and ready to answer all your questions, resolve your networking and systems issues and help you maintain your web presence or network computing technologies.
In todays world, your business is more dependent than ever on your network's availability, performance, and security and in todays world: cost effectiveness is key. Everyone knows that applications and network stability are business-critical, every moment that your employees can't send or receive e-mail, or access a database, your company is losing money and alienating customers. When your Network Application is down, you need it corrected quickly or your business suffers through productivity, revenue, or image loss. With our Real-time Managed Network Services, corrective action is already under way and the management of it is totally efficient and cost friendly. We work with you to develop a long-term strategy that defines your IT alternatives. We will even help you select the best IT Infrastructure for your business and create a network transition plan. Whether that infrastructure is E-Business or an enterprise system, our; services ensure that servers, applications, and network devices are stable platforms; for your people and business processes to flourish.
Our systems will provide detailed status information and automated critical notifications, allowing the fastest possible response to your networks needs. We prevent the crisis before IT happens.
