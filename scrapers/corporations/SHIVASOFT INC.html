Company Profile


ShivaSoft is the developer and implementer of Shiva a complete suite of solutions designed to help manufacturing enterprises improve their core competence  Manufacturing. As a perfect blend of web based technology and extensive manufacturing management experience, Shiva is a complete manufacturing centric enterprise resource management system that will revolutionize the way manufacturing management is perceived. Including modules for Order Entry, Real-time Available to Promise (ATP) and Capable to Promise (CTP), Collaborative Advanced Planning and Scheduling (CAPS), ShivaTrak -Shop Floor Tracking, Active Business Intelligence (BI), Web based Customer and Vendor Collaboration and much more, Shiva is the next generation of an integrated manufacturing management system. Our inside out deployment strategy ensures that the system can me deployed with minimal demand on internal resources, minimal disruption on day-to-day operation and being a true web based solution requires minimal training. Most importantly Shiva provides a demonstrated and guaranteed payback of at least $500,000 in less than one year.

Unique applications:

The Shiva system is non-traditional ERP system. It is non traditional by its basic design and by our approach to the implementation. Here is what makes us different:
A fully integrated demand management module that includes forecasting and capacity constrained planning.
Available-To-Promise and Capable-To-Promise giving you the ability to promise accurate and realistic delivery dates.
Ability to assess the impact of changing sales order priority before making the commitment in Real-Time from any location with an internet access.
Demand or plan driven work order generation in real time eliminates the need for traditional MRP.
Integrated FCS -Finite Capacity and Resource constrained scheduling that supports Lean or Just-in-Time scheduling, what-if analysis, option of maximizing asset utilization or minimizing queue times and a lot more.
Supports Make-to-Order, Make-to-Forecast and Mixed-Mode manufacturing environments.
Fully integrated real-time Shop Floor Data collection gives you the ability to monitor your shop from any location.
A fully integrated financial management system that is web-based.
A web based user interface that gives new meaning to the term ease-of-use.
Access to our team of manufacturing management experts and implementation services and methodology that ensures rapid and painless implementation.
In summary a fully integrated manufacturing management system that focuses on adding to your bottom line..
