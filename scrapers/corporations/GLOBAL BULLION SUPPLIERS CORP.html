Company Profile


Buy and Sell Gold & Silver straight from the source.
Call GBS at 1 (800) 427-4045.
Refining, Retail, Institutional and Private Investment, and Wholesale.

Global Bullion Suppliers Corp. is a direct source provider of Gold, Silver, Platinum, and Palladium Bullion.

GBS Clients include Institutional Investors such as Funds and Wealth Managers; Private Investors and collectors; and wholesale customers such as Jewellers, and Precious Metals Dealers, worldwide.

As an established international business, our values always come first. Proudly founded in Canada, our aim was to provide people the means to own precious metals on their own terms. No middleman, no hassle, and no hidden fees, just better quality at a better price. Globally.

Prices that wont be beat.

Pride is a commitment to philanthropy and sustainability.

Privacy is keeping you confidential and secure.

Service is freedom to do business face to face.

Security is multi-national shipping and vaulting insured by Lloyds of London.

Through a multi-national scale of operations and strategic partnerships, Global Bullion Suppliers has developed the infrastructure needed to instantly and securely supply and vault bullion for investors in 39 countries and growing.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Gold Bullion
All Gold Bars are individually poured, minted, numbered, and packaged in world renowned refineries using the latest technologies and brought to you at prices that won't be beat. GBS affiliated bars are renowned in quality of assay, fully customizable, contain only .9999+ Gold content and are available in certified .99999+ fine.

Some Brands Carried: Credit Suisse, Johnson Matthey, PAMP Suisse, Perth Mint (Australia), Rand Refinery (South Africa), Republic Metals Corporation, Royal Canadian Mint, Valcambi Suisse, and many more...


Product Name:
Silver Bullion
Silver Bars and Ingots refined in COMEX and LBMA certified facilities are one of the best ways to invest in Physical Silver Bullion. GBS carries only hallmarked .999+ and .9999+ fine silver. We make it easier to buy and sell Silver by offering honest prices that won't be beat.

Some Brands Carried: Credit Suisse, Johnson Matthey, PAMP Suisse, Perth Mint (Australia), Rand Refinery (South Africa), Republic Metals Corporation, Royal Canadian Mint, Valcambi Suisse, and many more...


Service Name:
Precious Metals Vaulting
All short and long-term vaulting is done in secure storage facilities wholly insured up to $1.2 billion by Lloyds of London. With cutting edge technology and software, you can track your bullion en route to and between vaults, such as our vault in Toronto, Canada. Our vaulting partner, is world renowned in shipping and vaulting abilities. All vaulting includes priority services such as the tracking of all bullion by serial number.

As an account holder with Global Bullion Suppliers, you also have access to international free zone vaults in:
- Hong Kong, China;
- London, United Kingdom;
- New York, United States of America;
- The Republic of Singapore;
- Zurich, Switzerland

Designed to save you duty, export, shipping, and transportation costs, these freezones have the added benefit of holding your assets in a secure location with instant international transportation available.

Each of these facilities is renowned for inventory management and security. For example, the London facility is the only commercial facility in the UK that meets Grade XII (EX) (CD) and EN 1143 levels of security.

Service Name:
Precious Metals Refining
Global Bullion Suppliers (GBS) incorporates a direct an active role in precious metals refining. Our industry-leading partner refining facility utilizes state-of-the art technology to offer larger, faster payouts. Incorporating advanced, proprietary processes, we strive to pay more than competitors by increasing standards of speed and cost effectiveness, while minimizing our environmental impact.

All refining is performed in ISO 14001 accredited facilities, awarded distinction by the US Department of Energy.

Focuses on innovation, and aiming to reduce the total refining process time, facility environmental policy has minimized scrubbing and chemical costs, further increasing client payouts.

Advanced technology is employed to avoid the need for pre-treatment measures, as utilized by the dangerous Miller Process, meaning a safer facility for both employees and the environment. Utra-efficiant refining circuits also remove reliance on the inefficient and Tim-consuming Wohlwill Process. This avoids unnecessary capital hold ups and enables rapid settlements.
Cumulatively, these practices enable the production of the highest grade precious metals in the world at the lower costs, including 99.999% gold.

Big or small, our promise to you, the client, is a bigger, faster payout.

GBS accepts the following materials containing precious metals:
- Mining Doré
- Impure Bars / Ingots
- Coins / Bars / Ingots
- Jewelry Scrap
- Alluvial Gold / Gold Dust (large volume)
- Manufacturing Scrap / By-Products
- Sweeps, Powders
- Polishing(s)
- Fillings, Sludge
- Crubicles

Precious Metals Accepted and Priced:
- Au (Gold)
- Ag (Silver)
- Pt (Platinum)
- Pd (Palladium)
- Rh (Rhodium)
- Ir (Iridium)
