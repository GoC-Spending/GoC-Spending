Company Profile


J.K. Engineering Ltd. is a civil engineering consulting company
specializing in environmental projects which J.K. Engineering Ltd. is a civil engineering consulting company, established in 1987 and specializing in civil and environmental projects which include water supply, wastewater disposal, solid and hazardous waste disposal and environmental impact studies.

J.K. Engineering Ltd. has performed aver 50 projects related to water supply and wastewater disposal for domestic and industrial uses.

The projects include a wide range of engineering services such as feasibility studies, preliminary and detailed design, construction supervision and project management, operating and maintenance manuals, staff training, operation monitoring, regulatory approvals, and complete turn key projects including construction.

The projects include small to large municipal, industrial and recreational, and individual residential wastewater treatment, disposal & re-use systems.

A major component of the projects executed was compliance with environmental and safety regulatory requirements, including field soils and hydrogeological testing for agricultural and environmental reasons. Also, each project includes detailed operation procedures and operation staff training.

The projects complied with very stringent Canadian Federal and Provincial Regulations and Guidelines for Design, Implementation and Operation Monitoring and Reporting.

Also, we are familiar with the European Union Environmental Regulations regarding Wastewater Treatment, Disposal and Re-use through our operation in Poland.

The projects completed include complex water and wastewater treatment processes such as removal of high concentration of inorganic and organic compounds from surface and groundwater sources, tertiary treatment of municipal wastewater with biological and chemical processes and disinfection with ozone and UV radiation and complex industrial wastewater treatment for BOD, COD, suspended solids, oil and grease, phenol and heavy metal removal and pH adjustment. New, but economical solutions are always employed to satisfy the water and wastewater quality standards.

Several projects involved benchscale testing and pilot plant studies for development of water and wastewater treatment processes. New processes such as ozonation, activated carbon adsorption, UV radiation and special chemical treatments have been tested for a variety of applications which include removal of heavy metals, dissolved organic carbon, suspended solids, colour, oil and grease, pH adjustment, and taste and odour control.

The wastewater (sewage) treatment processes include conventional lagoons, aerated lagoons, conventional activated sludge process and several novel activated sludge processes and rotating biological contactors, sludge digestion (anaerobic with bio-gas recovery and use, and aerobic), sludge drying beds and mechanical sludge dewatering, sewage effluent irrigation of agricultural land and recreational facilities (golf courses, parks) and treated effluent re-use for recreational, domestic, and industrial purposes.

Our services include surface and groundwater source development and water quality monitoring and evaluation. The groundwater development includes aquifer investigation and testing, wells installation, aquifer performance monitoring and aquifer contamination evaluation and remediation.

The environmental studies we perform include evaluation of impact of solid and liquid wastes on soils and surface and groundwater and surrounding developments.

The systems which we design include custom designed built-in place plants and shop-fabricated (packaged) smaller plants.

The custom design, built-in place plants include predominantly earth and cast-in-place concrete structures.

The shop fabricated systems which we design, fabricate and supply include steel, aluminium and pre-cast concrete plant components and complete plants including pumping stations, filter systems, clarifiers, aeration systems, chemical feed systems and complete treatment processes.

We also design and fabricate water level, flow and quality On-Site and Remote Monitoring Systems for process control and environmental applications.

We have been conducting research and development of new technologies for water and wastewater treatment with aim of reducing the residual waste and re-use of treated wastewater for land irrigation, recreational and industrial processes and domestic re-use for sanitary purposes and ground watering.

Our research and development conducted under the Canadian Scientific Research and Experimental Development Program led to development of several novel water and wastewater treatment technologies as follows:

 Air Aspirator-Mixer for aeration of water and wastewater and oxidation processes with Ozone and Ozone/H2O2.
 Versatile Biological Wastewater Treatment System.
 Aeration System for Wastewater Lagoons.
 Super Oxidation System for Water & Wastewater Treatment.
 Produced Water (Oily Water) Treatment System.
 Super Clarifier for Water Treatment.

The multipurpose Industrial Telemetry System (ITS) was designed for fixed position monitoring of signal-point flow, level, and temperature of fluid and gas media. The operation of sensing element is based upon the thermal dispersion principle. A low
power heater is used to produce a temperature differential between two resistance temperature detectors (RTD's). The RTD temperature differential varies as a function of forced convection for flow measurement. The measurement of the temperature is from the non-heated RTD. Heater power is field selectable for specific fluid or gas service requirements.

The instrument is factory calibrated to the applications as specified at the time of order. All alarm set points are programmable and send directly to your cell phone via built-in modem. A compact, fully automated design allows you to monitor multiple installations simultaneously when using a Personal Computer Base Station.

The unique built-in alarm capability ensures that you are immediately notified about the flow or temperature problems.

No moving parts and rugged housing ensures dependable, long service. The RTD sensor is easy to remove, clean and reinstall for maintenance.

APPLICATIONS

* continuous liquid or gas flow monitoring
* detecting increasing/decreasing flow rate
* alarming flow/no-flow, we/dry conditions
* dual flow and temperature applications

Water Well Wizard (WWW) was specifically designed to simplify the collection of water well pumping test data. The device is rugged, durable and fully automated to minimize the need for manual data gathering over long term. To obtain accurate changes in water level, there is a built-in compensation for atmospheric pressure changes.

Information is recorded in a timetable format meeting Government Agencies' requirements. All parameters are already preset to function as soon as the probe is submersed into the well.

Installation consists of placing a WWW unit in a pumping and / or observation well, lowering the probe into the well and starting the pumping cycle. WWW uses its own batteries to operate, and once set, will start working immediately, 24-hours per day, until the end of testing. It senses and automatically files time periods, water levels during each pumping and recovery cycles, as well as the power generator status. When finished testing the well, you are immediately ready to prepare a water well pumping test report from the accumulated data.

Based on the data collected from the well site, the WWW unit prepares the water well's performance results. To retrieve this information, you bring up the menu using a single toggle switch on the hand-held display unit. No need for programming - we did it for you!

Product Name:
VERSATILE BIOLOGICAL WASTEWATER TREATMENT SYSTEM
A versatile and efficient biological wastewater treatment system is provided which is adaptable to small, shop fabricated package plants and large, assembled in place plants, and which can be operated as a secondary treatment, activated sludge process, for suspended solids and Biochemical Oxygen Demand (BOD) removal, or a tertiary treatment, activated sludge process, for suspended solids, Biological Oxygen Demand (BOD), Nitrogen and Phosphorous removal. The system major equipment includes a wastewater pump and an air aspirator-mixer to provide aeration, sludge recirculation and waste, and wastewater recirculation for the secondary and tertiary treatment processes. The aeration system with the air aspirator-mixer is highly efficient in oxygen transfer to the wastewater, easy to maintain and versatile to use, as it is located outside aeration tanks. The wastewater pump and air aspirator-mixer system lends itself to continuous and cyclic aeration and oxic and anoxic treatment processes. The multiple use of the wastewater pump for aeration, recirculation and sludge waste minimizes the number of pumping systems required and eliminates air blowers.

Product Name:
WASTEWATER LAGOON AERATION SYSTEM (WLAS)
A novel, economical, efficient and versatile lagoon aeration system is provided which can be used with existing or new wastewater (sewage) lagoon to increase the lagoon treatment efficiency and capacity, and to allow for a continuous, year round discharge of treated wastewater to environment. The system can be operated in warm and cold climates for treatment of municipal and industrial wastewaters of varying strength and flow rates in a secondary activated sludge treatment process or in a tertiary activated sludge treatment process, or an extended aeration treatment process. The system employs patented (Patent No: US 6,969,052 B2) Air Aspirator-Mixer (AAM) which provides highly efficient wastewater aeration outside the lagoon which facilitates an easy and economical operation and maintenance of the system throughout the year.

Product Name:
SUPER OXIDATION, COAGULATION & FLOCCULATION SYSTEM
The super oxidation, coagulation and flocculation system is applicable to water and wastewater chemical treatment in which oxidation, coagulation and flocculation processes take place in a single vessel.

The apparatus and process can be used for treatment of domestic and industrial water and wastewater for removal of organic and inorganic contaminants.

The oxidation process can be provided by several oxidizing chemicals such as ozone, hydrogen peroxide, potassium permanganate, chlorine and the coagulation process can be accomplished with several coagulants such as aluminum sulphide, ferric chloride, ferric sulphate.

Selection of the chemicals depends on the quality of the water or wastewater to be treated and the treated water quality required, and it covers a wide range of the impurities to be removed and specifically difficult to oxidize hydrocarbons and chlorinated organics.

The apparatus and the process are economical and have a small installation footprint as several processes take place in a single vessel.

Product Name:
COMPACT WATER TREATMENT CLARIFIER
A compact and efficient clarifier is provided for domestic and industrial water and wastewater treatment processes. The clarifier does not have any moving internal parts and it ensures a plug flow water pattern, a high suspended solids (floc) settling rate, and an even sludge removal from the entire bottom of the clarifier. The clarifier has adjustable corrugated settling plates and sludge frequency and volume removal system, and a sludge recirculation system.

The clarifier is suitable for treatment of varying water and industrial wastewater quality and flow rates, and for fabrication of small and large water and wastewater treatment systems.

Product Name:
PRODUCED WATER (OILY WATER) TREATMENT SYSTEM (PWTS)
A complete treatment is provided in a single vessel to separate oil, water, gas and solids which are usually contained in produced water from oil and gas production and processing operations.

The apparatus and process provide for an automatic removal of sludge, oil and gas which separate from the produced water.

The apparatus and process have flexible and innovative designs to suit the produced water quality and treatment level required, and to provide an efficient, compact and complete treatment.

The apparatus and process can be operated under the atmospheric pressure or other pressure, higher or lower than the atmospheric pressure, and with or without a gas blanketing, to suit the sit conditions and to accomplish the required produced water treatment. The apparatus and process can operate as a pressure or gravity flow system.

The apparatus and process include demulsification, gravity separation, parallel plate separation, and diffused or induced gas or air flotation. The system components and the entire systems are novel and highly efficient.

Product Name:
AIR ASPIRATOR-MIXER
A single port venture device is provided for aeration of wastewaters containing suspended solids. The device has a non-obstructive design which prevents attachment or accumulation of suspended solids, and promotes even air distribution for efficient aspiration of air and mixing of air and wastewater. The device has provisions for adjustment of the venture throat size, for protection of the throat from wearing out, for enhancing air and wastewater mixing capabilities, and for flushing of the solids accumulated in the air entrance.

The device also has a wide application in water treatment for aeration and oxidation of water with ozone and other chemicals.

Product Name:
VERSATILE BIOLOGICAL WASTEWATER TREATMENT SYSTEM
A versatile and efficient biological wastewater treatment system is provided which is adaptable to small, shop fabricated package plants and large, assembled in place plants, and which can be operated as a secondary treatment, activated sludge process, for suspended solids and Biochemical Oxygen Demand (BOD) removal, or a tertiary treatment, activated sludge process, for suspended solids, Biological Oxygen Demand (BOD), Nitrogen and Phosphorous removal. The system major equipment includes a wastewater pump and an air aspirator-mixer to provide aeration, sludge recirculation and waste, and wastewater recirculation for the secondary and tertiary treatment processes. The aeration system with the air aspirator-mixer is highly efficient in oxygen transfer to the wastewater, easy to maintain and versatile to use, as it is located outside aeration tanks. The wastewater pump and air aspirator-mixer system lends itself to continuous and cyclic aeration and oxic and anoxic treatment processes. The multiple use of the wastewater pump for aeration, recirculation and sludge waste minimizes the number of pumping systems required and eliminates air blowers.

Product Name:
WASTEWATER LAGOON AERATION SYSTEM (WLAS)
A novel, economical, efficient and versatile lagoon aeration system is provided which can be used with existing or new wastewater (sewage) lagoon to increase the lagoon treatment efficiency and capacity, and to allow for a continuous, year round discharge of treated wastewater to environment. The system can be operated in warm and cold climates for treatment of municipal and industrial wastewaters of varying strength and flow rates in a secondary activated sludge treatment process or in a tertiary activated sludge treatment process, or an extended aeration treatment process. The system employs patented (Patent No: US 6,969,052 B2) Air Aspirator-Mixer (AAM) which provides highly efficient wastewater aeration outside the lagoon which facilitates an easy and economical operation and maintenance of the system throughout the year.

Product Name:
SUPER OXIDATION, COAGULATION & FLOCCULATION SYSTEM
The super oxidation, coagulation and flocculation system is applicable to water and wastewater chemical treatment in which oxidation, coagulation and flocculation processes take place in a single vessel.

The apparatus and process can be used for treatment of domestic and industrial water and wastewater for removal of organic and inorganic contaminants.

The oxidation process can be provided by several oxidizing chemicals such as ozone, hydrogen peroxide, potassium permanganate, chlorine and the coagulation process can be accomplished with several coagulants such as aluminum sulphide, ferric chloride, ferric sulphate.

Selection of the chemicals depends on the quality of the water or wastewater to be treated and the treated water quality required, and it covers a wide range of the impurities to be removed and specifically difficult to oxidize hydrocarbons and chlorinated organics.

The apparatus and the process are economical and have a small installation footprint as several processes take place in a single vessel.

Product Name:
COMPACT WATER TREATMENT CLARIFIER
A compact and efficient clarifier is provided for domestic and industrial water and wastewater treatment processes. The clarifier does not have any moving internal parts and it ensures a plug flow water pattern, a high suspended solids (floc) settling rate, and an even sludge removal from the entire bottom of the clarifier. The clarifier has adjustable corrugated settling plates and sludge frequency and volume removal system, and a sludge recirculation system.

The clarifier is suitable for treatment of varying water and industrial wastewater quality and flow rates, and for fabrication of small and large water and wastewater treatment systems.

Product Name:
PRODUCED WATER (OILY WATER) TREATMENT SYSTEM (PWTS)
A complete treatment is provided in a single vessel to separate oil, water, gas and solids which are usually contained in produced water from oil and gas production and processing operations.

The apparatus and process provide for an automatic removal of sludge, oil and gas which separate from the produced water.

The apparatus and process have flexible and innovative designs to suit the produced water quality and treatment level required, and to provide an efficient, compact and complete treatment.

The apparatus and process can be operated under the atmospheric pressure or other pressure, higher or lower than the atmospheric pressure, and with or without a gas blanketing, to suit the sit conditions and to accomplish the required produced water treatment. The apparatus and process can operate as a pressure or gravity flow system.

The apparatus and process include demulsification, gravity separation, parallel plate separation, and diffused or induced gas or air flotation. The system components and the entire systems are novel and highly efficient.

Product Name:
AIR ASPIRATOR-MIXER
A single port venture device is provided for aeration of wastewaters containing suspended solids. The device has a non-obstructive design which prevents attachment or accumulation of suspended solids, and promotes even air distribution for efficient aspiration of air and mixing of air and wastewater. The device has provisions for adjustment of the venture throat size, for protection of the throat from wearing out, for enhancing air and wastewater mixing capabilities, and for flushing of the solids accumulated in the air entrance.

The device also has a wide application in water treatment for aeration and oxidation of water with ozone and other chemicals.
