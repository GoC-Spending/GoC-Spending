Company Profile


Clevest provides software for mobile workforce automation and smart grid operations exclusively for electric, gas and water utilities. Over 150 utilities worldwide have chosen Clevest to transform their field operations by harnessing the power of our software and deep domain knowledge of mobile computing and utility operations.

Clevests award-winning platform powers a product suite that includes mobile workforce management, automatic vehicle location, meter reading, and a patent-pending graphical configuration tool, as well as the only complete solution for smart grid operations and maintenance activities.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Clevest Mobile Workforce Management
Connect field workers with office supervisors and dispatchers in real-time for more informed decision-making and more efficient field operations. Designed specifically for electric, water and gas utilities, Clevest Mobile Workforce Management (MWFM) unifies the field and the office on a single, next-generation mobile platform for wirelessly managing your mobile workers and virtually any type of work they performfrom meter service work, pole inspections and locates to restoring outages and a mass deployment of smart meters.

For even greater alignment between the field and office, leverage the fully integrated, utility-specific Clevest Automatic Vehicle Location (AVL) solution for a map-based view of the real-time, GPS-based location of vehicles and their relation to your asset infrastructure.

Product Name:
Clevest Automatic Vehicle Location
Connect your field and office operations with the most usable, functional and visually appealing browser-based AVL solution available to utilities today. Clevest AVL improves visibility into field activities with a map-based view using real-time, GPS-based location of your vehicles and their relation to your infrastructure. And you can significantly increase the value of your investment by leveraging the fully integrated Clevest Mobile Workforce Management (MWFM) solution to manage all your field work directly from the AVL WebMap. Highlights of this solution include:

- Integration with Esri ArcGIS  extends the value of existing technology investments

- MultiSpeak-certified interfaces  allows deployment of technology more reliably and quickly

- OMS standard integration  extends a critical utility function into the field for improved response and restoration

- Deployment flexibility  you decide which model is right for you, installed or hosted


Product Name:
Clevest AMI Operations
With Clevest AMI Operations, utilities have a single smart grid mobile software solution to manage deployment and post-deployment activities and help drive the expected return on AMI investments.

During deployment, the Clevest solution helps utilities install, program and provision new meters and network devices faster, more safely and with fewer resources. It also supports regulatory reporting with a comprehensive audit trail that documents and verifies all deployment activities.

Once deployed, the Clevest solution helps utilities complete ongoing operations and maintenance of AMI assets. Streamlined workflows and diagnostic tools integrated on a single mobile device enable field technicians to complete increasingly complex work in the field to service and maintain new technologies quickly and efficiently.

Product Name:
Clevest Meter Reading
Clevest Meter Reading software is a configurable enterprise solution that manages manual or automated meter reading, meter-related service orders and collection activities. Our solution includes a proven automated route control system for managing meter reading routes, a GIS mapping option and a real-time dispatching tool for work orders.

We provide you with choices in ruggedized collection devices, all of which can read Itron ERT, Neptune R900G and L+G Airpoint endpoints. Handheld and tablet devices also support optical probing for interval data capture as well as the ability to capture GPS coordinates.

Clevest works with your existing file layouts, eliminating the need for IT groups to re-write interfaces and providing significant IT resource savings. And we support hundreds of service order types, including exchanges, installs, collections, CTVT (current transformer/voltage transformer) work, investigates and meter installs, exchanges and removes.
