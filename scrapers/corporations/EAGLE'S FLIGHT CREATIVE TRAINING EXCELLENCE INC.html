Company Profile


Eagles Flight is an innovative leader in the development and
delivery of practical training programs for the global business
community. Through the use of experiential learning, we assist
organizations of all sizes to gain a competitive edge by
significantly strengthening their workforce.

Founded in 1988, our offerings now include skill-based program
development, conference training events, leadership initiatives
and custom development. Globally, our programs are offered in
17 languages and represented by international licensees in 28
countries. This global structure enables us to work with large
multinational companies to provide them with consistent
training messages and methods around the world.

Service Name:
Leadership Development
At Eagles Flight, we understand that training leaders is a
complex issue and we have developed a unique and highly
effective process that delivers results. Incorporating
experiential learning concepts, we combine solid leadership
principles with a hands-on style to help leaders at all levels
to learn, master and apply new skills. Our programs do more
than just teach the principles of leadership  they teach you
how to implement them and provide plenty of opportunity to
practice in the day-to-day realities of your world.

Topics include; Accelerating Performance; Beyond Time
Management; Leading in Transitional Times; Leading an Empowered
Workforce; Powering Team Performance; Executional Excellence;
Setting the Pace


Service Name:
Skill-Based Programs
Eagles Flight skill-based programs focus on knowledge
transfer, skill acquisition and behavior change. Using an
experiential framework, participants learn in an atmosphere of
relevance and fun, discovering practical skills and techniques
that can be applied directly on the job. Since the goal of
being trained on a new skill is to immediately be able to do
things differently, we deliver the content first and allow
plenty of opportunity for practice throughout.

Topics include: Supervisory Skills for First-Time Leaders;
Presentation Skills; Meeting Management; Communication Skills;
Tactical Execution Skills; Mastering the Skills of Process
Improvement


Service Name:
Conference Training Events
Eagles Flight conference programs are fast-paced, engaging and
packed with energy and excitement, making them an ideal choice
to kick off or wrap up company-wide meetings, conventions or
new product launches. Strong and interesting themes provide
great opportunities for linking events and giveaways. Our
program debrief is tailored specifically for your organization
and readily tied to your conference theme.

Topics include: Team Effectiveness; Partnerships for Long-term
Success; Developing Winning Ways; Driving Superior Results
Through Flawless Execution; Balancing Short-Term Results with
Long-Term Relationships


Service Name:
Custom Development
Eagles Flight provides a full range of customized training
solutions to meet your companys unique challenges. Our
abilities span the full spectrum from new program development 
beginning with concept through to delivery  to adding your
company logo to participant program materials. And anywhere in
between! We can incorporate your mission, vision and values
into the program and use language and case studiens specific to
your industry or company.

Custom initiatives include: Corporate University curriculum
development; large-scale training initiatives; employee
orientation programs; outsource training provider
