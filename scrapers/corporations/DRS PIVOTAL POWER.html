Company Profile


DRS Pivotal Power (“Pivotal Power Inc”) has been in business over 35 years, specializing in the design and manufacture of power conversion and distribution equipment in the 100W to 60 KW power range for naval applications. Its high-density power conversion products offer excellent electromagnetic compatibility, immunity to the harshest environments, and exceptional robust performance; all hallmark requirements for mission-critical defense applications. The company also offers build-to-print capabilities for a broad range of electro-mechanical assemblies, as well as engineering and logistical support services to meet a broad range of requirements.

Our business approach is focused on strong customer partnerships; the design and manufacture of reliable and affordable products, and product development support that ensures that our equipment fully integrates with other system components.

Our team includes experienced managers, professional mechanical and electronic design engineers, software/firmware developers, quality assurance specialists, manufacturing engineers, and skilled fabrication and assembly personnel. The company invests significantly in research and development initiatives and maintains a well-equipped in-house test facility, including a random axis vibration table, environmental chambers, and an EMI/EMC chamber.

Pivotal Power's integrated manufacturing operations support a wide range of fabrication and electronic assembly capabilities such as metal fabrication, plating and painting, and assembly. Manufacturing processes, including materials management and product configuration, are strictly controlled and have been developed to meet the stringent demands of our military customers.
Pivotal Power's products include uninterruptible power supplies, static frequency converters, helicopter starters, inverters, embedded power supplies, and intelligent battery management systems. Products are designed and qualified to survive extreme operating environments demanded of military or high-end commercial applications.

We have the privilege of participating in many major programs and providing equipment and services on an ongoing basis to customers such as the Canadian Department of National Defense, Naval Sea Systems Command (NAVSEA), Raytheon, L3, Computing Devices Canada, CAE Electronics and most of the major shipyards in North America.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Rectifiers
DRS Pivotal Power’s DC rectifier power supply (RPS) produces precise 28 VDC output power to comply with the stringent system requirements of MIL-P-15736 specification for critical naval applications.

The power conversion of the RPS is based on switch mode technology using high-frequency pulse-width modulation (PWM) which provides excellent DC output voltage regulation with low transients. The use of power MOSFETs as switching elements ensures high conversion efficiency.

The construction of the RPS is modular with draw-out type subassemblies for ease of maintenance. The systems reliability is an order of magnitude greater than other static conversion equipment due to the use of high reliability components and stringent quality screening standards.

The RPS was developed and fully qualified for military applications.

Product Name:
Uninterruptible Power Supplies
DRS Pivotal Power's uninterruptible power supplies (UPS) produce precise output power to comply with the stringent system requirements of STANAG 1008 specification for critical naval shipboard applications. The fully qualified, military UPS's are available in 1-6 kVA AC or 0.8-2.4 kW DC power ranges.

In the event of loss of ship service power, the UPS system continues to provide the output power for a specified time duration using rechargeable storage batteries. On restoration of ship service power, the UPS system automatically reverts to input power from the ship service power to provide the output power and to charge the storage batteries. The transition between ship service and battery power is achieved with less than 2% harmonic distortion. The UPS also functions as a power conditioner, blocking surges, spikes and transients, while maintaining stable voltage to the serviced equipment.

Each UPS system is made up of a power unit, which houses the power electronics, plus control and communication circuits and a battery unit, which house the storage batteries. The UPS system utilizes mil spec circular connectors for input, output and battery unit connection. Pivotal Power manufactures a full line of battery units to support the UPS system for various backup durations.

The UPS power units are fully Simple Network Management Protocol (SNMP) compatible, offering effective computer network management of the UPS either independently, or as part of a larger distributed power system. When enabled, the SNMP can remotely control various functions of the UPS and monitor a large number of status and system variables.

Product Name:
Battery Management Systems
Pivotal Power's intelligent battery management system for use in tactical environments. This new generation equipment incorporates a microprocessor which identifies the individual requirements for up to 8 batteries and automatically supplies each with the required charging current This intelligent system ensures that all batteries are charged quickly and safely with minimal operator intervention.

The BMS also incorporates analyzing and reconditioning capabilities which facilitate optimization of battery life.

Product Name:
Embedded Power Supplies
Pivotal Power's embedded power supplies provide conditioned power and/or multiple output rail voltages to supply critical electronic systems in military applications.

The circuit design of the EPS is based on state-of-the-art power conversion technology using a prudent mixture of switch-mode and linear techniques aimed at maximizing power conversion density, efficiency and electromagnetic compatibility.

Product Name:
Electronic systems- communication, control and accessories
Product Name:
Converters
The DRS Pivotal Power’s 138-01 power converter performs a number of functions. Primarily it takes 3-phase 440 VAC 60 Hz ships power as per MIL-STD-1399, Type 1 input power and provides two isolated output channels, which provide 20 to 35 VDC conditioned power. Each output channel has remote sensing capability and an internal current limiting algorithm optimized for charging 24 VDC batteries. The two output channels are identical except channel 1 is optimized for 100 A and channel 2 is optimized for 50 A charging currents.

A unique feature of the 138-01 power converter is the remote, in-field, programmable, charging profiles capability. From a remote computer, via a serial port, the charging profiles for either output channel can be reprogrammed. This allows for customization of the battery configuration or battery chemistries. The user can connect the 138-01 to an alternate battery tray and rapidly alter its charging characteristics to support the new requirements.

Product Name:
Frequency Converter
Product Name:
Inverters
DRS Technologies’ DC-AC export power inverters meet the need for increased power demands on vehicles and are designed to operate in the harshest of environmental conditions, including vibration, shock, high temperature and humidity. Our inverters contain safety protection such as output short circuit; input reverse polarity protection; and over temperature protection.

The design employs simple circuit topologies built from highly reliable components. Control of the inverter output is ensured by a high performance DSP running specialized algorithms within the embedded firmware. A low distortion, sinusoidal output voltage results which meets the steady state and transient requirements of MIL-STD-1332B Class 1.

Product Name:
Helicopter Starting System
DRS Pivotal Power’s DC rectifier power supply (RPS) is intended for use on board combat ships. Using the ship’s 440 V, 60 Hz,3-phase AC power conforming to Type 1 of MIL-STD-1399, Section 300, the RPS system provides output power at 28 VDC, adjustable from 26 V to 32 V, with a continuous current rating of 300 A. The RPS is capable of providing up to 1000 A short term high starting currents. The RPS also functions as a power conditioner of the ship service power by blocking the surges, spikes and the transients of the ship service power and maintaining stable voltage to the served equipment within specifications to assure their continued proper operation. Multiple RPS can be connected in parallel to increase overall output power.

With over 35 years of manufacturing experience, DRS Pivotal Power’s lean manufacturing lines are optimized for both sub and top assembly production. Our work cell production area is highly customizable and able to be quickly transformed to optimize for new product lines or build-to-print opportunities.

The Manufacturing Team is cross-trained in multiple operations, yielding a highly flexible, highly skilled workforce. Due to the nature of the business, rigorous standards are imposed which require specialized operator training and stringent control over product configuration and processes to ensure product repeatability, reliability and traceability. All operators are trained to meet the process and workmanship requirements of J-STD-001B and IPC-A-610 Class 3. DRS Pivotal Power also uses computerized systems to manage its planning, materials requirements and processes, giving us greater control over all aspects of manufacturing.

Our company has been ISO certified for over three decades. With a mature quality system, optimized operating procedures, frequent auditing, and thorough inspection, you can be confident in the systems we provide.
