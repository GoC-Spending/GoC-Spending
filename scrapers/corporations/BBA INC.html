Company Profile


BBA is a private Canadian consulting engineering firm that offers engineering services backed by advanced expertise. Its know-how is recognized in the energy, mining and metals, and oil, gas and chemicals sectors. BBA sets itself apart in the industry by providing unique integrated solutions that include expert advisory services for corporate executives, technical operations expertise, and commissioning.

BBA provides a wide range of services, from a projects initial definition to its full implementation: consulting, studies, design, traditional and multidisciplinary engineering, commissioning, and project delivery in EPCM mode (engineering, procurement, and construction management).

The BBA team consists of experts in several engineering disciplines (electrical, civil, mechanical, mining and metallurgical process engineering) as well as in related fields such as industrial IT, automation, project management and construction management. These specialties allow the firm to take on any multidisciplinary project from A to Z. The invaluable work of our shareholders in seeing projects through to final delivery and commissioning ensures exceptional service to our clients.

Since the firms inception in 1979, BBAs corporate culture has rested on the core values of respect, team spirit, commitment and accountability. A focus on knowledge sharing means that our experts are second to none when it comes to countering complex problems with cutting-edge solutions that are both practical and sustainablethereby maximizing our clients return on investment.

In the thirty-some years since BBA was founded, it has grown to a network of offices from coast to coast. Its head office is located in Mont-Saint-Hilaire, near Montréal. The firm also has offices in Vancouver, Calgary, Timmins, Toronto, Montréal, Brossard, Québec City and Labrador City. BBA has been named one of Canadas 50 Best Managed Companies and ranks among the Best Employers in Canada.
