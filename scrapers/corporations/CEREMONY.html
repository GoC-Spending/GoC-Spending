Company Profile


Ceremony is a multi-disciplinary Design firm based out of Toronto, Canada established by Samantha Coulombe. Drawing on over 10 years experience in the Interior Design industry, Ceremony’s mission is to ultimately create unique, personalized interiors for each client. Whether it is a Private Residence, Restaurant, or Retail environment, it is the strong belief that each design should be as forward thinking as the last.

As the Owner and Principal Designer, Samantha Coulombe established Ceremony to focus on Classic, Contemporary, Timeless Interiors. It is the belief that successful Interiors are those that stand the test of time which drive Ceremony’s creative thought process as a Design firm. With a strong foundation and well thought out, functionally planned Interior, Ceremony believes that any space can be designed to suit the aesthetics and requirements of the client.

Ceremony exists to work with clients to enhance all types of Interior environments. Whether it is a new project being built from the ground up, or a small renovation on a limited budget, the ultimate goal is to provide an exceptional service and impart the experience, and love for design which is the foundation of Ceremony, to the client and end user.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Interior Design
Initial Consultation and Project Evaluation

An Initial Meeting to understand the Client’s overall requirements and goals is the first step to a successful project. It is here where we aim to collect enough information to establish a concept, understand the Client’s timing and budgets, and make sure there are no loose ends in the services required by the Client and those that can be provided by Ceremony. After this information has been gathered and agreed upon, Ceremony can provide a more precise direction on how the project can be efficiently and creatively executed.



Design Concept & Schematic Planning

After the Initial Consultation, and enough Information has been gathered, a Conceptual direction and Schematic Floor Plans are presented. Plans may include proposed furniture placement, suggested architectural and decorative finishes (Flooring, Fabrics, etc.), and at this point any custom requirements can be identified. Kitchen and Bathroom Cabinetry, finishes, and appliance suggestions are also presented. A preliminary Furniture budget is developed including all loose furnishings, built in millwork, and all decorative finishes such as wall coverings and specialty finishes, plus any additional lighting or equipment required within the space.



Design Development and Construction Documents

Once the Client approves the Design Concept, Schematic Plans, and Furniture Budget, then all plans and elevations are refined and actual Furniture, Finishes, and Millwork pieces are selected and designed to work with the Client’s budget. Kitchen and Bathroom Cabinetry, finishes, and appliances are all refined. All Millwork pieces are designed, and drawn to be priced, and once completed everything is presented to the Client for final approval.



Purchasing Services

Ceremony is able to provide full scale Purchasing services for all Loose Furnishings, Custom Millwork, Decorative finishes, Lighting, and Draperies. All architectural finishes, Kitchen and Bathroom Cabinetry, Appliances, Equipment, and Architectural Lighting can also be sourced through Ceremony.



Project Administration and Construction Co-ordination

Ceremony is a hands-on design firm that works directly with clients from concept through to completion. As a full service Design firm, Ceremony provides working drawings, detailed finish specifications, and comprehensive purchasing documents to ensure the success and ongoing organization of a project. It is our belief that consistent, dedicated attention to the progress of each project ensures there is little to no room for error. Site measures and frequent site visits, on-going construction co-ordination with all contractors and sub-trades, maintaining consistent communication with suppliers and fabricators as well as being especially organized are all typical services provided in this execution phase of the project.



Installation and Project Completion

Ceremony will coordinate the delivery and installation of all loose Furnishings, Draperies, and Artwork once the Construction phase is complete. Additional services such as Accessorizing and Styling are also offered.
