Company Profile


Dominic Drolet Instrumentation inc. nevertheless take advantage of more then twenty years of experience in instrumentation sales and distribution. We markets specialized measuring instruments. Our main activities are designing, manufacturing, distribution, sale, and servicing measuring instruments such as temperature recorders, various gases, other environmental parameters, monitors and loggers, as well as parameters in energy consumption and HVAC in buildings. Here is the product line we offer:ACR Systems, Oceasoft, Greystone Energy Systems, YES, Vulcain, Belimo, DeltaTRAK, Summit technology, MTP, REED and many more.

Product Name:
Hanna Instruments
Hanna Instruments Canada Inc. is a member of the international Hanna family, established in Italy in 1978. Our company has proven that good customer service is the key to success in the field of electro-analytical instrumentation. Keeping close to your requirements, we pride ourselves in supporting many industries such as water analysis, agri-food and industrial. Sensitivity to our customers needs, timely response to market demands and a continuous commitment to expanding the product range have been the key factors of our success.

Product Name:
iButton
The iButton® is a computer chip enclosed in a 16mm thick stainless steel can. Because of this unique and durable container, up-to-date information can travel with a person or object anywhere they go. The steel iButton can be mounted virtually anywhere because it is rugged enough to withstand harsh environments, indoors or outdoors. It is small and portable enough to attach to a key fob, ring, watch, or other items for various data logging tasks.

Product Name:
Summit Technology/PowerSight
Summit Technology is the manufacturer of the PowerSight family of electric power analyzers, the "tools of first resort" for Energy Analysis. PowerSight analyzers provide extensive capabilities as demand analyzers, harmonic analyzers, power quality analyzers, power meters, and data loggers. They are the smallest, most cost effective instruments for the measurement and analysis of three phase electric power. Monitor, log, and analyze voltage, current, and power. Use them for three-phase, two-phase, single-phase, 4 wire delta, open delta, 2CT/2PT, DC, 50Hz, 60Hz, and 400Hz systems anywhere in the world. They come in 7 languages!

Product Name:
DeltaTRAK
Since 1989, DeltaTRAK, Inc. has been developing and manufacturing high quality portable test instruments such as chart recorders and data loggers that monitor / record temperature and humidity. We've subsequently developed affordable and dependable digital probe and infrared thermometers to provide better end-to-end cold chain management solutions. In addition, we have added state-of-the-art data download / management / analysis software to our data logger product line. Our products and services are depended on by businesses big and small around world.

Product Name:
Swema
Since 1944 Swema manufactures and calibrates measuring instruments and sensors for air velocity, air flow, temperature, humidity, force and resistance.

Product Name:
YES Environment Technologies/CETCI
YES Environment Technologies was founded in 1987 as Young Environmental Systems and has been developing and manufacturing gas detection products, air quality instruments, data loggers and related products ever since. YES has established itself as an industry leader and innovator in the Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) market. YES was the first company in the world to manufacture a miniature electronic recording IAQ Monitor. The YES brand name is well established and accepted around the world and is synonymous with quality products, competitive pricing and excellent service.

Product Name:
Reed
Reed Instruments is a leading supplier of test & measurement equipment.
Our mission is to supply First Quality products to the North American markets through our Coast-to-Coast distribution network. Our products are used in a wide cross-section of today's economy. A broad selection are available to meet the customers specifications and budget. The Reed Instruments can be calibrated, to NIST standards, in one of our two certified laboratories. Plus qualified service technicians offer full repair and/or warranty services. To Establish a Customer-Driven alliance, our distributors have the tools to reduce total procurement, inventory distribution costs, while improving service to the END-USER. Customers will benefit from our instruments by staying competitive through innovative products that will improve efficiency and productivity.

Product Name:
Greystone Energy Systems
Greystone Energy Systems Inc. is one of North America's largest ISO registered manufacturers of HVAC sensors and transducers for Building Automation Management Systems. We have conscientiously established a worldwide reputation as an industry leader by maintaining leading-edge design technology, prompt technical support, and a commitment to on-time deliveries. We take pride in our Quality Management System which is ISO 9001 certified, assuring our customers of consistent product reliability.

Service Name:
Calibration Certification of measuring instruments
For your instrument certification repair, verification or calibration just contacts us by phone or e-mail. Generally we recommend to obtain a Return Merchandise Authorisation (RMA) prior to ship any instrument. Upon reception of the instruments, we will contact you with price and service confirmation.
When you request calibration certificate, we perform verification and, if needed, the adjustment of instrument to the manufacturer's specifications. All measurements before adjustment are given with the certificate along with the calibration date and the due date in a written report with the comparison data.

All our calibration certificates are referenced since our standards are certified and referenced to N.R.C.C. or N.I.S.T. Some of our subcontractor laboratory are ISO 17025.
