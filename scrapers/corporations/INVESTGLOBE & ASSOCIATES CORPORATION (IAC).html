Company Profile


Investglobe & Associates Corporation (IAC) is the Canada based Consulting Management and Connections firm that provides Foreign Investment Facilitation and Project Management services to a diversified client base including domestic and foreign investors, developers, and partners. IAC bridges the gap in operational standards by acting as a strategic partner that effectively and efficiently identifies structures, executes, and manages foreign investments and projects on its clients' behalf.

IAC's first priority and prime objective is to help clients accomplish, endure, and recuperate control of their businesses. IAC works with public and private sectors to engage investors; to promote and acquire intelligence on new propositions from investors; to explore growth opportunities; to investigate entry into foreign markets; identify new revenue streams; attract FDI and capitalize on new technologies; evaluate strategies to minimize risk and stay ahead of competition.

In addition, IAC helps clients engage top C-level executives from world's top corporations, high level government officials, government institutions, and Public Organizations. These are just few among many solutions IAC offers its clients to achieve growth and hence competitive advantage.

IAC's Services Include:
*Trade Finance Facilitation;
*Global Supply Chain Finance Facilitation;
*Foreign Investment Promotions & Connections
*Foreign Investment Management & Facilitation
*Projects Management & Facilitation
*Trade Missions & Conferences
*B2B/ B2G/ B2C Promotions & Connections
*Contracts Negotiations
*Capacity Building Projects & Training


IAC's Sectors of Focus:

*Infrastructure

*Equipment and service supply chain

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Foreign Investments Promotion & Connection
InvestGlobe & Associates Corporation (IAC)'s role in the Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) services is to promote global economic direct investment by linking the potential foreign direct investors to wide open investment opportunities worldwide, hence facilitate the business transactions in a win-win situation.

This win-win situation is such that the potential investors get opportunity to explore the investment sectors of the target market and bring in Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) thus, strengthen the economy and create job opportunities in the investment country as well in the investor's country.

Strategic Promotion & Connection - IAC connects global business communities with potential reputable potential investors thus overlaying the way for each party to forward project proposals, profile and Balance of Quntity (BOQ) through IAC. Another valuable advantage is the corporate visibility that goes beyond investors and investment country to the rest of the world via IAC.

Strategic Relationship - IAC welcomes joint effort with institutions and business associations from the globe to maximize efficiency. For instance, IAC can collaborate with local and international investment promoting agencies, and business associations to co-organize trade missions, conferences etc. The method of relationship can be strategically customized to partnering institutions or business associations to go well with the objectives.

Hosting Delegates - IAC collaborates with its local associates and partners to host officials and assist them in promoting and securing investment projects in the target markets. These visits offer an excellent opportunity for officials to examine and analyze the investors, investments and the project opportunities while creating and cementing business and business contacts.


Service Name:
Foreign Investments Facilitation
Investglobe & Associates Corporation (IAC) offers the following range of Investments services and information for private and public sector business communities:

Investment Project Profiles - IAC provides Potential Investors with project profiles for joint ventures in industrial projects, as well as privatization opportunities in a target market investment sectors.

Distribution Analysis - IAC works with governmental Promoting Agencies, institutions, and partners to offer the prospective investors, the sector analysis reports that focus on specific investment sectors, and insights on market size, market players, manufacturing costs, distribution, government industrial policy and the like.

Investment Environment - IAC provides Investors with information on investment environments including investment incentives, policy on foreign investment, foreign exchange controls, tax systems, registration procedures, labour laws, visa requirements and other areas.


Service Name:
Projects Management
Project Management is not only about timelines and deliverable but also about firm's profits, reputation, stability and growth. IAC embarks on projects that are strategic in nature and that align with competitive business environment in order to enable clients to benefit successfully in a more cost-effective manner. With level of project management expertise, advanced and a higher degree of focus, IAC has created a methodology to enable its clients to successfully complete their strategic projects on-time, within budget and to the intended level of quality.

IAC's project management consulting services have an impressive track record of providing proven solutions that are both effective and cost-efficient. Not only do IAC's consultants and associates have the experience to roll-up their sleeves and help to drive clients' high-priority project to successful completion, but they also have the expertise to mentor their personnel through the process of adopting a more structured approach. If you require a customized approach, IAC's project management consulting personnel will work with your organization to deliver near-term successes on your projects while moving your desires in the right direction.


Service Name:
Business to Busines (B2B) promotions and Connections
With its experience of working with a numerous clients from top FTSE 500 organizations to start-ups, Investglobe & Associates Corporation (IAC) is exceptionally placed to help businesses to reach their competitive advantage. IAC's mandate is to establish a strong reputation by providing clients with services they need to grow their businesses; to make better business decisions; to create strategies that can grow their markets; to enter the new markets successfully; and to excel in competitive business environments. IAC's philosophy is based on the fact that each client`s need matters, each client is unique, each client is a long-term relationship; and believing that every business is a community of people and by helping people realize their vision, and but not least IAC makes a lasting impact that makes a difference. IAC's B2B promotions & Connections service centres on the following but not least to:

Strategic Business Partner Recruitment and Connection - IAC works with businesses to identify potential prioritized list of business counterparts and partners, then make initial contact and gauge their real level of interest. This list is evaluated against IAC's developed prime business/channel partner profile and from a pull of potential business counterparts while ensuring that the business transaction between both parties is of a win-win situation. We then schedule meetings for clients to present their business to the prospective business partner at the potential business partners' office.

Contract Negotiations - This is a another important matter that IAC takes it into serious considerations when pursuing international contracting. If You Are Looking For Contract Negotiator Who Can Help You Win Big Time In Your International Business, IAC is ready to help. IAC's expertise in Contract Negotiations is beyond comparison. Before IAC lets you agree to any particular deal, or agree to sign any contract, it will conduct a thorough evaluation of a potential counterpart in consequent negotiations while taking into account a realistic business counterpart assessment.


Service Name:
Capacity Building Training and Seminars
Investglobe & Associates Corporation (IAC) organizes tailored 'Assist Business Communities Understand The Know-How, and The Know-Why' training and seminars and for business communities around the globe in an interactive atmosphere by providing up-to-date and in-depth information on investment environments in a target market, as well as by giving insights of particular specific investment projects and sectors.

IAC offers these seminars through IAC's office locations, and various external partners on a request basis. If your group or association would like to have any of IAC's Training and Seminars delivered as part of your program or event, please contact us at info@investglobe.ca.


Service Name:
Trade Finance Facilitation
IAC has the convenience of co-working with international companies which specialize in initiating large-scale advanced projects in the world, and facilitating attractive financing packages based on leading well-known international banks and insurance companies.

IAC’s international partners enjoy a massive track record of successfully implementing & facilitating financing for infrastructures projects in Africa and worldwide, as well as enjoying deep and intimate networks with the most leading international financial institutes which provide long-term attractive financing for many projects in Africa and the world.


Service Name:
Global Supply Chain Finance Facilitation
IAC works in collaboration with international financiers, insurance companies and local banks to provide public and private banks, a set of solutions available for financing specific goods and/or products such as medical equipment, trucks and trailers, agricultural equipment, and general infrastructure equipment as they move from origin to destination along the supply chain.

Service Name:
Trade Missions & Conferences
IAC works with its domestic and international trade partners to organize and facilitate trade mission by giving businesses and institutions the first-hand insight and information on the investment opportunities in the target market, at the same time offers the foreign businesses and institutions the opportunities to meet counterparts in the target market. An important features of these trade missions and conferences are the fact that they provide an opportunities for one-on-one business meetings, initiate contacts and pursue partnerships such as joint ventures, licensing and dealerships. Investglobe & Associates Corporation (IAC)arranges these trade missions and conferences on the basis of specific interests identified in advance through investment project profiles.

Success stories:

In collaboration with clients/ partners IAC has successful managed to engage investors; to promote and acquire intelligence on new propositions from investors; to explore growth opportunities; to investigate entry into foreign markets; identify new revenue streams; attract FDI and capitalize on new technologies; evaluate strategies to minimize risk and stay ahead of competition.

International capital projects:

IAC works with international companies which enjoy a vast experience in offering comprehensive solutions International Capital Projects which include harnessing first class international EPC contractors & technologies companies, as well as facilitating attractive long-term foreign financing packages. Over the years, IAC's partners have been successfully involved in implementing turnkey projects at value of more than USD 4.5 Billion combined worldwide. Their financing structures are unique and bring a significant added- value factor. For example, IAC's partners have facilitated financing of about $60 Million for Water Project in Ghana; $205 Million for Gas Project in Turkmenistan; EUR 30 Million for Russia Non-woven Factory in Russia; $100 Million for Chemical plant in Vietnam; $38 Million for Water Desalination Project in Cyprus; $300 Million for Biomass Projects in USA; $40 Million for Metal Recovery Plant in Botswana; $20 Million for Agri-Industry Project in Belarus; $150 Million for Water Projects in Venezuela; and but not least $195 Million for Gas Pipeline Project in Uzbekistan.

IAC in collaboration with its international partners is currently in the initial stages of implementing international capital projects in other African countries such as Rwanda, Uganda, Tanzania, Ethiopia, and Ghana.
