Company Profile


Interfacing Technologies Corp.(ITC) is a Montreal-based Canadian
software company that provides world-class business process
management solutions. ITC's products and services are geared to
equip enterprises with the ability to successfully implement
processes that enable enterprise goals.
Interfacing Technologies Corporation provides business process
management tools and services to world-class enterprises around
the globe. Its award-winning state-of-the-art solutions for
modeling, analyzing and simulating current and future processes
have gained Interfacing Technologies a leadership position in
business-enabling tools and methods.
The FirstSTEP Tool Set developed and marketed by Interfacing
Technologies includes components that can operate in an
integrated fashion or independently to address a wide spectrum
of enterprise business process needs. FirstSTEP Designer is the
complete modeling, analysis, simulation and reporting tool; its
award-winning Unicenter TNG option adds process monitoring and
management. FirstSTEP Charter provides Visio® users the ability
to map processes in hierarchial and swim-lane fashion and
prepare them for analysis.
The EPC is a web-based portal that provides structured and
simplified access to the enterprise processes, process-related
content, projects and applications. The Enterprise Process
Center is designed to manage and promote collaboration within
the enterprise between employees, managers and process owners,
but also between the enterprise and its clients/suppliers.
Originally focused on consulting services and software solutions
to the manufacturing sector, ITC has evolved into a leading-edge
Business Process Management and Reengineering Software company.
ITC is interested in increasing its worldwide market share
through partnerships with value added resellers, distributors and
management consultants. The company would also entertain
strategic partnering arrangements with technology and service
providers.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
FirstSTEP Designer- Business Process Modeling, Simulation and Analysis
FirstSTEP Designer is the comprehensive business process
management tool giving business architects and managers the
power to design, document, stimulate, analyze, refine, improve
and reengineer business processes. Business professionals can
now create results by modeling their most important business
knowledge, information and hierarchically represented models
using FirstSTEP Designer 4.5.



Product Name:
Workflow Exchange
Evaluate and test your workflow with the FirstSTEP Workflow
exchange. Bi-directional import/export functionality through
PDL standard files (Compliant with Workflow Management Coalition
(WfMC)standards5 lets you define workflow models in FirstSTEP
and then transfer them to your workflow tools.

Product Name:
FirstSTEP Charter for Visio®
FirstSTEP Charter is the Microsoft Visio-integrated tool that
allows users across an enterprise to cooperatively create, view,
edit and publish structured process models. FirstSTEP Charter
incorporates such enhancements as new team-based enterprise
model creation and sharing, multi-language maps, model
validation, plus much more. Version 4.51, Charter's latest
version has even more enhanced installation features.

Product Name:
Enterprise Process Center - EPC
The EPC is a web-based portal that provides structured and
simplified access to the enterprise processes, process-related
content, projects and applications. From their EPC web browser
on the desktop, users can easily access their Enterprise Process
Framework and navigate in the process hierarchies to view;
the corresponding maps and process workflows; relevant data
(process and activity attributes as requested for specific
implementations); and related documentation (forms, procedures,
plans, work instructions, etc.). Users can also access
dedicated content areas for project, client and document
management.
The Enterprise Process Center is designed to manage and promote
collaboration within the enterprise between employees, managers
and process owners, but also between the enterprise and its
clients/suppliers.




Service Name:
COMPUTER SOFTWARE AND SERVICES - MANUFACTURING SIMULATION
FirstSTEP is a comprehensive and intuitive business process
management tool giving business architects the power to design,
document, analyze, refine, incrementally improve and reengineer
any business process. Developed using state of the art object
technology, FirstSTEP gives the user the power to create a
flexible working model of an enterprise that may be dynamically
simulated to evaluate performance, visualize current operations,
identify alternative process designs and opportunities for
improvement and test the impact of change.

FirstSTEP enables organizations to test changes and fine tune
processes through "what if" scenarios enabling quick reactions
to changing market conditions to maintain a competitive edge.
The organization can simulate the implementation of both new and
proven technologies, evaluate associated risks, determine
capacity requirements for changing market needs and make the
best process improvement decision.

Service Name:
Training & Professional services
ITC provides workshops and training courses in business process
improvement in reengineering using FirstSTEP.
ITC offers two and three-day workshops for business analysts and
users. The approach taken in te training provides a clear
understanding of FirstSTEP's role within an ongoing process
improvement strategy. Participants become familiar with all
important components and principles of FirstSTEP. Through
problem-solving techniques and hands-on modeling and exercises,
participants gain the initial experience necessary to create and
analyze their process and enterprise models upon completion of
the course. ITC offers project mentoring, facilitation and
consulting. FirstSTEP users and consultants can benefit from
these services in their initial projects to ensure successful
process modeling, simulation and analysis results. This service
is provided by ITC directly and through a network of consultants
and business partners world-wide

Service Name:
Process Management
ITC's family of FirstSTEP products are designed to fit into a
broad spectrum of enterprise consulting and project services
that include enterprise transformation, process management and
knowledge sharing. At ITC we refer to the methodology
underpinning these products and services as Enterprise and
Business Process Management (BPM). This methodology maintains a
clear focus on the value delivered to the client through a range
of value-added services; including:
- clarifying organizational vision from the enterprise level to
the process and task levels
- business process education and management
- capturing and sharing knowledge
- modeling and redesigning business processes
- mentoring and assistance for continuous process improvement
- implementing supporting technologies and procedures
- developing custom software applications, if required
The EBPM methodology is realized through a scaleable framework
in four phases: Focus, Describe, Innovate, and Implement.
Together, these four phases help you understand and control
process behavior and generate desired results from radical
reinvention to incremental improvement, through new procedures,
new technology and/or resource remaining. Whatever phase your
organization has reached in your renewal project, ITC's
experience with industry-leading enterprises brings clarity and
results.
In applying its approach, ITC's aim always to have its clients
become self-sufficient by mastering the competencies that are
key to their sucess.
